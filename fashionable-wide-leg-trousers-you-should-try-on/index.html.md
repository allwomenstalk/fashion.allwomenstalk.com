# 7 Fashionable Wide\-Leg Trousers You Should Try on ...

Wide-Leg Trousers are a chic new season look to try out. Pleated palazzo pants are a great way to work a 70s inspired silhouette, while tailored wide-leg trousers are elegantly stylish. Make sure your outfit is balanced by pairing your wide-leg trousers with a fitted top. Take a look at just some fashionable wide-leg trousers below.

## 1. Topshop Premium Wide-Leg Trousers

![Topshop Premium Wide-Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/ir/u7z3f2s354c92fdc7cc60775957390_334x488.jpg)

**Price:** $106.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/yv2Qq0)
What I like most about these voluminous trousers is that they have the appearance of a pleated maxi skirt. These trousers have a fitted waistband, pleating from the waist, and a zip fastening at the rear. The vibrant magenta colour is also on-trend for fall. Wear these with a simple white tank top, metallic sandals, and cropped denim jacket for a street smart look.

## 2. Diane Von Furstenberg Fimby Stretch-Wool Flared Pants

![Diane Von Furstenberg Fimby Stretch-Wool Flared Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/nz/rzaj32hf54c92fdd78f37444938121_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Gg8uNh)
Warmn, autumnal colours are key to your new season wardrobe. These wool pants would be perfect for wearing to the office. They are flared throughout the leg and sit high on the hips. They have a fitted waistband, concealed zip fly, and creases through the lefts. Team these trousers with a pussy-bow blouse and almond toe pumps.

## 3. Alexander Wang Wide Leg Pant

![Alexander Wang Wide Leg Pant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/ov/qhluczas54c92fde4b1e1666873137_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $450.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Black trousers are a wardrobe staple. Invest in a good quality pair, such as these Alexander Wang trousers. They have a straight wide-leg silhouette with zip fly, slit pockets at the sides, and buttoned welt pockets at the back. The relaxed fit is also an appealing feature. Black trousers are truly versatile and can be styled up or down, depending on the occasion.

## 4. J.Crew Maddie Linen and Cotton-Blend Wide-Leg Trousers

![J.Crew Maddie Linen and Cotton-Blend Wide-Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/i1/r0lexavx54c92fdf0dde4117817356_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Hf1TFC)
Bold, bright colours are a fun way to update your wardrobe. These orange wide-leg trousers are a guaranteed way of adding a pop of colour to your wardrobe. They have a high-waisted fit and feature seam detailing throughout the front of the waist. These trousers would look great paired with a geometric print top.

## 5. Rachel Comey Caucus Pant

![Rachel Comey Caucus Pant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/k5/a5qrzc6054c92fdfd59db297098542_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $472.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Work a boyish look with these Rachel Comey trousers. These trousers have a high-waisted, relaxed fit. The tonal silk twill material is a luxurious touch. Keep the slouchy look going by pairing these with a loose-fitting tank top and accessorise with bold, chunky necklaces.

## 6. Topshop Paper Bag Wide-Leg Trousers

![Topshop Paper Bag Wide-Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/rc/hmxn5sca54c92fe0abf77262569329_334x495.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vYBkXF)
These black wide-leg trousers are an affordable way to incorporate this look into your wardrobe. They have a paper-bag style waist and pleat details from the waistband. They also have front and back pockets. These black trousers can easily be styled in a myriad of ways.

## 7. Miu Miu Stretch Corduroy Wide-Leg Trousers

![Miu Miu Stretch Corduroy Wide-Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/z1/qkk8oxdu54c92fe17c356190498871_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fo0bco)
For a seriously luxe take on the trend, take a look at these red corduroy wide-leg trousers. They are made from stretch corduroy and have a concealed zip, front and back welt pockets, and turned-up cuffs. Team these with a crisp white shirt and metallic accessories for an elegant everyday look.

From basic black to bright punchy hues, there’s no doubt that wide-leg trousers are going to be big this season. Take a cue from the big name designers and pick out a pair of wide-leg trousers in rich, autumnal colours. What are your thoughts on wide-leg trousers this season?
