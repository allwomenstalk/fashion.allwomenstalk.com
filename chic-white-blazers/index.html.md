# 8 Chic White Blazers ...

White Blazers are a chic way to freshen up an outfit. While they are ideal for wearing during the spring/summer months, you can also make white blazers work as stylish transitional pieces. With rich autumnal colours set to dominate wardrobes this season, white blazers are perfect for anchoring your new season look. Take a look at a few chic [white blazers](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/celebrities-wearing-white-blazers-who-wore-it-best/) below.
