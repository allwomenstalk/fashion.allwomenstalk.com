# 8 Preppy and Plaid Pieces for Fall ...

The one trend that pops up every year and keeps returning season after season is preppy and plaid. The classic print gets a style remix every season and is incorporated into designer collections and retail stores in a variety of ways. Whether it be in a tartan print, large check, or classic preppy plaid, you can count on this print to complete your perfect fall wardrobe. You can be clad in plaid this season with these 8 preppy and plaid pieces for fall.

## 1. River Island Red Check Chelsea Girl Dress

![River Island Red Check Chelsea Girl Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/RI1_430x430.jpg)

**Price:** £25 at [riverisland.com](http://www.riverisland.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

A shirtdress is a must-have in every girl on the go’s wardrobe. The shirtdress is a simple and quick way to throw on something to start your busy day while still looking chic and put together. Adding a tall boot to this shirtdress instantly makes this the quintessential look for fall.

## 2. Romwe Plaid Check Blazer

![Romwe Plaid Check Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/romwe1_423x454.jpg)

**Price:** $139 at [romwe.com](https://us.romwe.com/check-one-button-orange-suitfont-stylecolorredcoming-soonfont-p-43863.html?ref=www&rep=dir&ret=us)

If you’re not big on bright reds and greens that most plaids come in, this subdued black and burnt orange blazer from Romwe is a great way to add a small touch of preppy plaid to your outfit. The great thing about a blazer is its ability to take you from office wear to casual chic when paired with a good skinny jean and boot.

## 3. Theory Testra Plaid Wool Pants

![Theory Testra Plaid Wool Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_theory-testra-plaid-wool-pants_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $295 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/byp1BL)

These Theory pants are a more traditional take on the plaid trend this season. The rich plum, navy blue, and deep green jewel tones make this pant perfect for fall, while a cropped, straight leg finish gives this pair a modern update. Let this piece standout on its own by choosing more subtle designs and solid shades to pair with.

## 4. Topshop Red Check Skater Skirt

![Topshop Red Check Skater Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_topshop-red-check-skater-skirt_600x615.jpg)

**Price:** $28 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/I1ekSE)

The cool grey and bold red tones in this Topshop skirt make it the ultimate versatile piece. You can wear this skirt in a number of ways while still keeping it fun and flirty. Pair it with a chunky knit sweater on top to create a cozy comfortable fall look.

## 5. Juicy Couture Regal Blue Plaid Eton Dress

![Juicy Couture Regal Blue Plaid Eton Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/juicydress_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $222 at [stylebop.com](http://www.stylebop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The shift dress gets a modern makeover with this prep-meets-rock number from Juicy Couture. The large plaid print in cool hues mixed with urban stud finishings makes this the perfect preppy piece fit for a city girl with edge. Pair with a moto jacket or leather blazer and buckle booties to complete this school of rock look.

## 6. Alice + Olivia Taylor Plaid Poncho

![Alice + Olivia Taylor Plaid Poncho](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/aliceolivia-368x560_368x560.jpg)

**Price:** $396 at [aliceandolivia.com](http://www.aliceandolivia.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Since fall is all about being cozy comfort, a poncho is the ideal alternative to your tried and true knit or wool sweaters. The simple white and black design is classic and will never go out of style. For an uber chic outfit style a poncho just like this Alice + Olivia one with a monochromatic look in all black.

## 7. Delia's Moto Plaid Wool Coat

![Delia's Moto Plaid Wool Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/delias_294x354.jpg)

**Price:** $69.50 at [store.delias.com](https://goo.gl/sDRXsG)

The classic red and black plaid design of this coat gives a modern update to your traditional lumberjack attire. With zips and buckle accents in a moto style, this coat is cool and edgy while still keeping that preppy punch.

## 8. River Island Green Check Zip Shorts

![River Island Green Check Zip Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/ri2_430x430.jpg)

**Price:** $25 at [riverisland.com](http://www.riverisland.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Fall shorts are a cool alternative to a skirt. The deep jewel tones in this plaid design from River Island make these shorts wearable with just about anything. Wear fun solid black or navy stockings with them, and you’ll be warm and stylish all at the same time. No one said you had to sacrifice style for weather!

Whatever your style, this popular preppy and plaid pattern is a must-have for the season. Which of these preppy plaid pieces is your fave, and how would you wear it? How else will you wear plaid this fall?
