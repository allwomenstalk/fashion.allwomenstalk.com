# 10 Make\-up Tips from Celebrities ...

Wouldn’t it be grand to have flawless skin! Most of us only dream of waking up to glowing, flawless skin. Celebrities seem to always look fantastic no matter the circumstance. Some celebrities have generously shared their beauty secrets to help us achieve beautiful skin.

## 1. Cameron Diaz

Cameron Diaz always washes her face with Evian water. She travels extensively; her skin benefits from the purity of Evian water.

## 2. Jessica Biel

Jessica mixes sugar with her face cleanser and uses it as a scrub every time she washes her face. She suggests adding oatmeal to your favorite cleanser as an alternate to sugar.

## 3. Teri Hatcher

Teri Hatcher adds red wine to her bath water. She credits the wine with softening her skin.

## 4. Katherine Heigl

This beautiful, talented actress uses peppermint extract to plump her lips. The peppermint draws blood into the lips to make them fuller.

## 5. Rihanna

This musician applies gold dust powder to accent cheeks and brow line for a rested, lively look. Using a bronzer will give similar results.

## 6. Gwyneth Paltrow

This radiant actress swears by organic foods and pre-natal vitamins. Vitamins are good for your body; furthermore they do wonders for your hair and nails.

## 7. Catherine Zeta Jones

This lovely actress brushes her teeth with mashed strawberries. Strawberries contain an acid that makes great toothpaste when mixed with baking soda. Castor oil is her secret to beautiful, shiny hair.

## 8. Julia Roberts

Julia Roberts soaks her nails in olive oil to prevent hangnails and keep her nails healthy.

## 9. Jennifer Love Hewitt

Jennifer treats acne blemishes with plain old toothpaste. She says that with an application of toothpaste her blemishes dry up and disappear. Her flawless skin is a real testament to the veracity of her beauty tip.

## 10. Brooke Shields

Since childhood Brooke Shields has been known for her beauty. She has gorgeous, long eyelashes made famous by her appearance in “Latisse” commercials. She promotes prescription “Latisse” for growing long, dark, healthy eyelashes.

Many of us consider celebrity caliber looks to be out of reach. Realistically however, celebrities face the same make-up challenges as the rest of us. Do you have any make-up tips you swear by?
