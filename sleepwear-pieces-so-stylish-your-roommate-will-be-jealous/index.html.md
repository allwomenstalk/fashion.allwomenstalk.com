# 9 Sleepwear Pieces so Stylish, Your Roommate Will Be Jealous ...

Women’s Sleepwear can sometimes be the items that we overlook in our wardrobes. Why splash out on women’s sleepwear when hardly anyone will see them, right? I know I have succumbed to slobbing around the house in sweatshirts and jersey shorts on occasion. This kind of women’s sleepwear isn’t the best way to impress the roommates! If your sleepwear section needs a bit of an update, why not take a look at a few bits of women’s sleepwear below.

## 1. Topshop Floral Meadow PJ Shorts

![Topshop Floral Meadow PJ Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/pn/sm5feimq54c935a1688da514214561_460x325.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/5ynZ9P)
These cute PJ shorts feature an all-over floral meadow print. They come in a grey colour with contrasting peach trims. They also have ruffle detailing along the leg openings. These shorts are a cute and stylish women’s sleepwear choice, and would look great worn with a simple white jersey t-shirt.

## 2. J.Crew Polka-Dot Sleep Shirt

![J.Crew Polka-Dot Sleep Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/i3/hx4t0dv854c935a2b59bc095760383_401x560.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bVLLth)
This J.Crew number comes in a classic navy colour and features an off-white polk-dot print. It also features offbeat yellow piping. Worn with shorts or matching pyjama bottoms around the house, this sleep shirt is perfect for those who prefer classic sleepwear.

## 3. Topshop Dalmation Fluffy PJ Robe

![Topshop Dalmation Fluffy PJ Robe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/q9/m74dk7ez54c935a3ec6aa669299896_373x558.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ouW2Ct)
Nothing says luxe lounging more than a fluffy robe. This fluffy robe from Topshop features a black and white Dalmatian print. It also has side pockets and a tie rope at the waist. It’s perfect for wearing on frosty mornings and snuggling up on the couch with a cup of tea.

## 4. Dorothy Perknis Oat Fairisle Print PJ Shorts

![Dorothy Perknis Oat Fairisle Print PJ Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/zi/d99towoq54c935a515134221898843_460x360.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A pair of sleep shorts is a must for both bed and wearing around the house. These feature a traditional Fairisle pattern in a sweet oat and coral colour combination. They also feature a delicate bow detail at the waist. These would look great paired with a slouchy grey sweater.

## 5. Eberjey Vintage Stripes Jersey Playsuit

![Eberjey Vintage Stripes Jersey Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fu/fo/it39hrno54c935a6103e0208624317_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/0AoG4E)
This jersey playsuit is a pretty and feminine piece of women’s sleepwear. It features a mauve and white striped pattern and has an elasticated waist, adjustable spaghetti straps, and lined briefs. It’s a playful piece perfect for a summer’s night.

## 6. Topshop Peach Floral Palazzo Trousers

![Topshop Peach Floral Palazzo Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/fi/lzuqaa9r54c935a734863414083553_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LYEx7E)
Palazzo pants were all the rage on the catwalks, so why can’t the look translate to the bedroom? These PJ trousers have a wide-legged style and feature an all-over peach floral print. They also have lace panels on the ankles. Worn with a fitted singlet, you’ll look perfectly stylish lounging around your room in these.

## 7. Aubin & Wills Winsford Pyjama Shorts

![Aubin & Wills Winsford Pyjama Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/u1/hr2cwi4l54c935a839427127704937_460x304.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/H9upSk)
Polka dot pieces of women’s sleepwear are a popular choice. These silk-blend pyjama shorts will add some serious style points to your bedtime wardrobe. At over $100 for the shorts alone, however, maybe these will just stay in our dreams rather than our wardrobes.

## 8. Juicy Couture Lace of Luxury Pyjama Shorts

![Juicy Couture Lace of Luxury Pyjama Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/wr/birm5hoo54c935a958f2e006424749_460x290.jpg)

**Price:** $26.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/nyD86t)
These pyjama shorts are a fun twist on usually racy lace sleepwear. They come in a bright blue colour and feature bow details at the sides. A matching singlet top is also available, if you’re interested in completing the set.

## 9. Dorothy Perkins Fairisle Slipper Socks

![Dorothy Perkins Fairisle Slipper Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fk/jc/qj9w6j1x54c935aa5deb7522301870_460x355.jpg)

**Price:** $12.50 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Don’t forget your feet when it comes to sleepwear this season. Keep your toes toasty warm in these cute slipper socks. They feature a traditional Fairisle print (which seems to be everywhere this season) as well as pom-pom details at the sides.

From chunky knit socks to fluffy robes, there’s a whole assortment of women’s sleepwear to lust over. You can either invest in classic pieces or try out some fun trend-based ones instead. The choice is up to you! Which of these pieces of women’s sleepwear do you like most?
