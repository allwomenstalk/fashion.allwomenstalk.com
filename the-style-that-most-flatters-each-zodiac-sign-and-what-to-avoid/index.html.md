# The Style That Most Flatters Each Zodiac Sign and What to Avoid ...

You have probably never thought about in this way before, but did you know that lots of astrology lovers out there believe there is a connection between your birthdate and the kinds of fashion styles that suit and don’t suit you? It seems like there should be any kind of link at all, but you never know, maybe the stars have it all planned out for us down to the very last detail of what shoes we should be wearing! Here is **the style that most flatters your zodiac sign** and the style that you should avoid.

## 1. Aries

![red, fashion model, fashion, vision care, eyewear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/v5/c5ruicj35bfff112d8d54111440806_600x741.jpg)

You look at your best in ravishing reds and even a touch of camo every now and then, two things that are big and bold. In the opposite fashion, you should avoid wishy-washy colours like millennial pinks and soft yellows.

## 2. Taurus

![vacation, summer, beach, neck, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/kq/lqaa37j35bffef4122074006049800_600x717.jpg)

Floral prints and boho chic are the styles that suits a Taurus the best; they enhance your care free, free spirit attitude. What you should be avoiding is anything too plain that doesn’t have an attractive colour or pattern.

## 3. Gemini

![footwear, pink, purple, leg, tights,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/kz/cs8gj7jj5bfff07a722ba425640294_600x750.jpg)

Your quirky personality suits neon colours and stand out styles really well; pretty much every day is a party day for you! What you should avoid at all costs is any kind of monochromatic look as it really dulls your spirit.

## 4. Cancer

![fashion model, photo shoot, shoulder, girl, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/v2/p60xq9gb5bffeeea200e4565877803_600x710.jpg)

White lace is something that really suits a Cancer. I’m not saying wear a wedding dress, but just think about incorporating more lace into your looks! Something that doesn’t suit you at all is corporate wear. You can’t stand fitting it and just being another worker bee!

## 5. Leo

![yellow, coat, fashion model, orange, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/a2/gcp2mvke5bfff16067a5c349752948_600x734.jpg)

Bright colours and sequins are exactly what a Leo needs to cement their status as the life and soul of the party! You want to stand out at all times, which means that any kind of muted colours are an absolute no-no.

## 6. Virgo

![coat, fashion, outerwear, joint, jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uf/wo/hb4bpb6x5bfff1aa99056711221819_600x600.jpg)

You like to put across a suave and sophisticated style, so demure and understated looks are your kind of bag. You always make sure to avoid anything overly flashy, because you think it comes across as tacky.

## 7. Libra

![shoulder, outerwear, jeans, road, street,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sa/x7/dn2kyz6g5bfff2c853a48794904733_600x750.jpg)

You like to keep things classy with a just a touch of trendy, balanced fashion is what you are most comfortable with. The one look that you just don’t seem to suit is the plain jeans and t-shirt look!

## 8. Scorpio

![clothing, purple, dress, shoulder, standing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xu/g1/puvl83gu5bfff58aa18d0716507250_600x750.jpg)

Sultry shades and crimson hues are a Scorpio’s best friend, basically, anything bold and vivid that makes you stand out amongst the crowd. Conversely, any kind of pastel shade tends to dull your personality.

## 9. Sagittarius

![clothing, footwear, shoulder, fashion accessory, shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uq/e3/clxphtb85bfff24d280ff990731289_600x703.jpg)

You always look amazing in sporty athleisure wear, making even the most casual outfits look high fashion and street at the same time. Minimalism isn’t usually something that you look good in.

## 10. Capricorn

![clothing, jeans, coat, outerwear, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z6/de/xst445lq5bfff4505daca600624605_600x751.jpg)

Unlike a Sagittarius, you look at your best when you are doing things really minimalist. You like clean lines and classic designs. To preserve this vibe you should stay away from campy fads that come in and out of style.

## 11. Aquarius

![fashion model, dress, little black dress, shoulder, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/om/w6t4mpur5bfff687d972c801703071_600x750.jpg)

Retro vintage is what you are most comfortable with as you can pull off any combination of eras. What you should be avoiding is the status quo! You have too much style to just go with the flow and wear what everyone is wearing.

## 12. Pisces

![fashion model, tights, coat, leg, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/gs/j4fez4m75bffedabd6f1d335382225_600x600.jpg)

You like to dress quite innocently, but always incorporating a touch of cool quirk into your outfits. Anything over the top tends to take the focus away from your natural beauty.
