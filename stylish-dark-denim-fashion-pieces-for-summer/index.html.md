# 9 Stylish Dark Denim Fashion Pieces for Summer ...

Dark denim fashion isn’t usually associated with the summer months. When summer hits, out wardrobes are usually bombarded with light or coloured denim. In general, summer wardrobes seem to be lighter and brighter than in winter. However, that’s not to say that dark denim fashions aren’t suited to summer weather. The following are just a few stylish dark denim fashions you might want to get your hands on this summer.

## 1. Denim Tie Front Shirt

![Denim Tie Front Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_denim-tie-front-shirt_214x400.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/GZTKhX)
A sleeveless collared shirt is a great way to work a preppy look. This one from Topshop comes in a dark denim colour and features snap buttons and a tie front detail. Wear it with some gingham shorts for a really retro inspired look.

## 2. Snap Button Denim Dress

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
Nothing says summer dressing like a dress that simply slips on. As far as dark denim fashion for summer goes, this dress from Forever 21 is a clear winner. It’d look perfectly chic worn with a compact leopard print side bag and black strappy sandals.

## 3. Indigo Skinny Jean

![Indigo Skinny Jean](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_indigo-skinny-jean_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Dark denim jeans are a must for any wardrobe and these come in a rich indigo colour. These ones have a super skinny design and classic five pocket detailing. Turn them up at the cuff, slip on a slouchy white tank, and pop on some tan sandals for a relaxed look.

## 4. Moto Stripe Skirt

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/mwyauW)
Here, dark denim fashion is given a twist in the form of black vertical stripes. This mini skirt has a classic design with five pocket detailing and authentic trims. Wear it with a silk blouse and ballet flats for a feminine look.

## 5. Self-Tie Floral Denim Top

**Price:** $17.80 at _forever21.com_
Cropped, tie front tops seem to be quite popular this season. What really sets this top apart is the pretty floral print that adorns it. It’d look extra sweet worn with a pastel maxi skirt this summer.

## 6. Moto Blue Black Denim Shorts

![Moto Blue Black Denim Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_moto-blue-black-denim-shorts_350x283.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/oeNVFO)
This dark denim fashion is a basic part of any summer wardrobe. Denim shorts are your go-to garment for casual summer dressing. These ones have authentic trims and rolled hems. Team them with anything from printed t-shirts to blouses to slouchy tank tops.

## 7. Blue Stitch Denim Shoulder Dress

![Blue Stitch Denim Shoulder Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_blue-stitch-denim-shoulder-dress_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This basic dress can easily be dressed up or down to create a number of looks. Cinch it in at the waist with a skinny leopard belt or push up the sleeves and layer on some colourful necklaces. The longer sleeves may get a bit hot in summer, so save this dress for slightly cooler days.

## 8. Gonsalves & Hall Denim Dress

![Gonsalves & Hall Denim Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/8_gonsalves-hall-denim-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $84.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This dark denim fashion dress has a bit of a vintage inspired design. It has a high neck, elastic waist, and full skirt. Cheeky touches include a cutout back and bold red fabric buttons. Incorporate some more red into the outfit in the form of red sandals and accessorise with a neutral handbag.

## 9. Blank NYC Denim Skirt

![Blank NYC Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/9_blank-nyc-denim-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $77.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If there’s any skirt you need in your wardrobe right now it’s a pencil skirt. This one comes in an inky black denim colour and is sure to last you a few fashion cycles, at least. For summer, team it with a loose tank top for an casual but polished look.

Dark denim fashion can look slick and sophisticated during the summer. Simply brighten up the mood by accessorising with bold colours. Which, if any, of these dark denim fashions would you sport this summer?
