# 7 Fun Playsuits for Summer ...

**Playsuits** are a fun choice for summer dressing. While they may divide critics, I think the success of this look always comes down to individual style and how you pull it off. If you choose a playsuit that is flattering and well fitting, then you really can’t go wrong. Get inspired for summer and take a look at the following cute playsuits.

## 1. Topshop Chiffon Tennis Playsuit

![Topshop Chiffon Tennis Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_topshop-chiffon-tennis-playsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ksVhw0)
Sporty silhouettes are on-trend for the summer and this **playsuit** has a classic tennis inspired feel to it. It has a button-up top and pleated bottom. Team this playsuit with cream lace-up shoes and a loose side braid for a chic and slightly sporty look.

## 2. Topshop Tropicana Playsuit

![Topshop Tropicana Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_topshop-tropicana-playsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/R0Rkal)
Loud prints are another popular trend for the upcoming summer season. This bright yellow **playsuit** features a tropical inspired floral print. It has a button up top, short collar, and turned up sleeves. It’s also a bit looser through the leg which allows for a much more flattering fit.

## 3. Alica + Olivia Floral-Print Playsuit

![Alica + Olivia Floral-Print Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_alica-olivia-floral-print-playsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $330.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/aR4PkG)
Summer dressing is all about fun, bright colours. This **playsuit** features a fun multicoloured floral print. It’s made a stretch-silk fabric and has a V-neckline, elastic waistband, and side slit pockets. Dressier playsuits like this one would look great teamed with some black patent accessories.

## 4. Topshop Grunge Ditsy Playsuit

![Topshop Grunge Ditsy Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_topshop-grunge-ditsy-playsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/DaJw7Q)
Another colourful **playsuit** to add to the list, this Topshop number has a bit more of a grunge edge to it. It features an all over ditsy floral print, ruffled collar, and exposed zip front fastening. Team with a black fedora and ankle boots for a stylish off-duty look.

## 5. Etoile Isabel Marant Floral Romper

![Etoile Isabel Marant Floral Romper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_etoile-isabel-marant-floral-romper_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $415.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You really can’t go wrong with a good floral print. This cotton-silk black **playsuit** features a lovely pinky-red floral print. It has a scooped neckline and sleeveless dropped armholes. The fit is quite relaxed, with flared skirt-like shorts. Relaxed playsuits like this are perfect for those who want a bit more give and freedom.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Mint Wrap Romper

![Dorothy Perkins Mint Wrap Romper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_dorothy-perkins-mint-wrap-romper_369x500.jpg)

**Price:** $21.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Dress to impress in this mint coloured romper. Mint is going to be one of the hottest colours this spring/summer season so get on board with this cute **playsuit**! It has a dressier design, with a wrap front and tie waist belt. It also has a puff hem detail. This playsuit would look great with a pair of wedge sandals and an embellished clutch.

## 7. Etoile Isabel Marant Denim Playsuit

**Price:** $545.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/j4VFFh)
Nothing says effortless summer dressing quite like faded denim. This light denim **playsuit** features plaid patches at the shoulders, front patch pockets, and button and zip fastenings to the front. It also has a fitted waist with belt loops. Wear one of these playsuits with tan shoes and a slouchy side bag.

As you can see, there are several different takes on the **playsuit** this season. Bright colours and prints are a favourite, but you also can’t go wrong with trusty denim. Are you a fan of the playsuit? What do you think of these particular playsuits?
