# 11 Gorgeous Shoes for Spring 2014 ...

Are you already shopping for shoes for spring 2014? Well, time to get those credit cards, ladies, because there are plenty of gorgeous shoes for spring! Here are some adorable shoes for spring 2014 that you should definitely consider buying:

## 1. Tory Burch ‘Miller’ Sandal

![Tory Burch ‘Miller’ Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/hb/ejek3j1y_600x263.jpg)

This is one of the cutest shoes for spring 2014! I like the metallic touch to it and this pair of shoes will help dress up any spring outfit! I would definitely wear these with my first dress this spring!

## 2. Vince Camuto ‘Evora’ Sandal

![Vince Camuto ‘Evora’ Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/et/0f/tcs3dgjm_300x145.jpg)

Another pretty metallic pair of fun shoes! I love how glam this pair looks. Whether you get them in gold or silver, or both, you can wear them with everything! I would wear these fun sandals with mint-colored shorts and a breezy boho top!

## 3. Tory Burch ‘Thora’ Sandal

![Tory Burch ‘Thora’ Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/g1/yfg8iedt_300x136.jpg)

I’m a Cali girl, so I love wearing flip flops. But as I’m getting older, I’ve realized that flip flops shouldn’t be worn everywhere and they aren’t too socially accepted. I love my Tory Burch flip flips because they are much dressier but equally as comfortable!

## 4. TOMS ‘Playa’ T-Strap Flat Sandal

![TOMS ‘Playa’ T-Strap Flat Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/bl/wj3dhiet_600x240.jpg)

Finally, the shoes that give back. I love the tribal print on this pair of sandals. Plus, I’m sure you all know that for each pair of TOMS shoes that you buy, TOMS will donate a pair to children in need! Get shopping, ladies!

## 5. ‘Bonsai’ Sandal

![‘Bonsai’ Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/pa/ruyx0cik_600x285.jpg)

I love the different colors that this pair comes in! Plus, the gold chain detailing adds an edge to these sandals! The zip in the back makes this pair of sandals much easier to put on once you’ve adjusted the buckle!

## 6. TOMS ‘Classic – Jonathan Adler’ Slip on

![TOMS ‘Classic – Jonathan Adler’ Slip on](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/at/fr0jbkd0_600x263.jpg)

Limited edition TOMS are my favorite! I love this fun pattern on these comfortable shoes. I would totally slip these on to run off to class during a nice spring day. Plus, they add so much color and fun to your outfit!

## 7. Kate Spade New York ‘tock’ Flat

![Kate Spade New York ‘tock’ Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bo/at/eupwaqz5_350x148.jpg)

I love Kate Spade shoes, and these flats do not disappoint! The cute and simple bow is super girly and the pink is too cute! I would wear these adorable flats with a plainer outfit so they stand out!

## 8. ‘Dali’ Slip-on

![‘Dali’ Slip-on](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/tl/feu6aza8_600x266.jpg)

Don’t feel like wearing espadrille wedges? Try these adorable pastel espadrille flats! They are super stylish and comfortable. Make sure to pack these when you head to the beach!

## 9. ‘Selina’ Flat

![‘Selina’ Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/ap/o0ydhrvl_600x233.jpg)

These fabulous flats are perfect for spring date nights! The crystals make these flats so elegant! These flats are airy and are very transclucent. I would wear these with some dark wash skinny jeans and a dressy top for a very J. Crew look.

## 10. Sperry Top-Sider ‘Authentic Original’ Leather Boat Shoe

![Sperry Top-Sider ‘Authentic Original’ Leather Boat Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/fi/xyojw4hs_600x246.jpg)

When I think spring, I always think of cruises. Sperry Topsiders are the perfect pair of spring shoes for you if you are going on a cruise! They are super comfortable and I love wearing them around campus on warmer days!

## 11. Kate Spade New York ‘elysee’ D’Orsay Flat

![Kate Spade New York ‘elysee’ D’Orsay Flat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/ka/wqs1vo4b_600x260.jpg)

I rarely give flats that have cut out sides a second look, but I just fell in love with these! These shoes would be so great for work or more formal events. I love the color options and the flower – so springy!

I love the shoes that come out during spring! They are all so pretty and the colors are bright. Plus, they are all much less bulky than those boots that we’ve been wearing for forever! Did you like any of these shoes? Where do you like to shop for your spring shoes?
