By Eliza

Summer has officially arrived, and whether you're lounging by the pool or heading to a tropical beach, finding the perfect swimsuit is a top priority. You're probably dreaming of margaritas on the beach, aren't you? I know I am! Even if your summer plans lean more toward the pool than the ocean, you are going to need a swimsuit for your time in the sun.

Blue is making a massive splash this season, offering everything from deep navy to vibrant turquoise. It is a magnificent color choice that suits every personality. Here are some of the best looking suits to inspire your next purchase.

Top Blue Swimsuit Trends to Watch:

  • Electric Cobalt: For those who want to stand out.
  • Soft Pastels: Perfect for a romantic, vintage vibe.
  • Sustainable Fabrics: Eco-friendly and stylish.
  • Asymmetric Cuts: For a modern, edgy look.
