# 7 New Perfumes for This Fall ...

New Perfumes can be pretty difficult to find if you don't know what you are looking for. This fall, I decided that I was going to go on a hunt for new perfumes that mixed with my body chemistry just right. Well ladies, below is some of the new perfumes that I absolutely love for autumn and that I found smelled delicious!

## 1. Too Too by Betsey Johnson

![Too Too by Betsey Johnson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p6/io/omellwnq54c932f1cc58f159142248_250x227.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 to $82.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Betsey Johnson is one of those designers that will never get old to me. Her style is so rebellious and she comes up with some really off-the-wall ideas … that work! Her Too Too perfume is one of the many new perfumes out there that I've fallen in deep addiction to. It's got a delicate smell of rose, sandalwood, vanilla and just a touch of pink musk. This is a scent that unfolds beautifully – and the bottle is awesome!

## 2. Diane Eau De Parfum by Diane Von Furstenberg

![Diane Eau De Parfum by Diane Von Furstenberg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ou/ig/ckr6lphq54c932f2b1e16621259464_250x242.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 to $110.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
What I was looking for in my search of new perfumes was something different than what I wear all of the time. Well ladies, Diane von Furstenberg did it for me! This incredible perfume is so unique, so awesome smelling – it's hard to describe! It's floral, light and is a signature scent. It's perfect for the office or for date night ladies, try it!

## 3. Shock for Her by CK One

![Shock for Her by CK One](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/qd/gzgg8b6z54c932f3563e1897045808_250x249.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 to $50.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love that CK is still making perfumes and still looking to excite and inspire the young at heart. CK Shock is actually one of the perfumes that knocked my socks off when I first smelled it. It's juicy, got a full floral scent and it stayed on my skin all day! It's the ideal perfume when you are looking to really inspire your man with your scent!

## 4. Someday by Justin Bieber

![Someday by Justin Bieber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/sz/d448cjhy54c932f41681b451105177_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 to $45.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I wasn't the girl that got the Bieber-fever, but I do have to say that the kid knows what he's doing with perfume. This scent is packed full of energy, is totally flirty and is anything but ordinary. It's a charmingly smell that has everything from vanilla scents to jasmine inside of it. It's a party in a bottle!

## 5. Wonderstruck by Taylor Swift

![Wonderstruck by Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s6/xo/i99ct5ej54c932f4dffdf332287291_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 to $59.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Taylor Swift is by far one of my favorite music artists, but when I heard she came out with her own scent? I had to have it. This is one of the new perfumes that is currently tucked away in my bathroom right now. It's full of fruit notes and soft petal flowers – it really does smell like romance in a bottle. The ideal perfume for any time of day!

## 6. Fan Di Fendi by Fendi

![Fan Di Fendi by Fendi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/d3/jctr820z54c932f5b84f1302997323_250x216.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 to $92.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whenever I am in the market for a new perfume – I have to say that it takes me forever to even try one. I want something that smells fresh, new and will leave a lasting impression. That's exactly what Fan di Fendi did for me! This awesome perfume is stuffed full of tons of different notes and smells so unique – I had to buy myself a bottle!

## 7. Verbena by PureDKNY

![Verbena by PureDKNY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/s8/j3yfngw454c932f6690f8641514945_250x244.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 to $85.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
When I am at the office, I want something that is going to be bright, fresh, clean and sophisticated. Something that smells beautiful but doesn't leave the guys wanting me. That is what Verbena does for me! This awesome honeysuckle-inspired scent smells crisp and clean without a whole lot of sex to it.

Well ladies, there you have it! If you are in the market for new perfumes this fall, you might want to give a check to some of these. Remember, whenever you are looking for new perfumes – think about who you are wearing the perfume for. Is it date night? The office? That should help you determine which scent works best in what situation. What are some of your favorite new perfumes this season? Share 'em!
