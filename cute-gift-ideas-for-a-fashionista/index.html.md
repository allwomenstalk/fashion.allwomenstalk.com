# 7 Cute Gift Ideas for a Fashionista ...

Gift Ideas can range from person to person. I often find that some people are quite easy to buy for while others are much, much harder. If you’ve got a [fashionista](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-become-a-fashionista/) friend, finding gift ideas that suit their personal style might prove difficult. To help you out, here are some cute gift ideas for that fashionista friend.

## 1. See by Chloe Nutter Squirrel Key Fob

![See by Chloe Nutter Squirrel Key Fob](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g0/la/xzoddfzi54c9342922ef7310561782_300x263.jpg)

**Price:** $115.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/KO1U3S)
This cute squirrel-shaped key fob is sure to put a smile on your dial. It has a black resin body, coated leather ears, and pale gold hardware. It also features stud detailing. This key fob would be a chic way to keep an assortment of keys organised.

## 2. Missoni Home Torta Four-wick Wax Candle

![Missoni Home Torta Four-wick Wax Candle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/u8/me6hmxso54c93429f1bd9224301671_221x293.jpg)

**Price:** $520.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/KpONMv)
This colourful candle would make a neat addition to any home setting. As far as gift ideas go, it’s very fashion-friendly. It features the distinctive zig-zag pattern of fashion house Missoni which is achieved through the layering of wax. This candle also features four extended wicks and a designer-engraved gunmetal plaque at the side.

## 3. Comme Des Garçons Polka Dot-Print Leather Pouch

![Comme Des Garçons Polka Dot-Print Leather Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/ne/hy3tuse354c9342ac428c817286334_300x248.jpg)

**Price:** $185.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/rXEQPG)
This polka dot leather pouch would make a neat gift for those who seemingly have it all. This compact pouch can fit cards and coins, and would be perfect for stashing inside this season’s must-have mini bags. The sleek design is visually appealing and the polka-dot print is a contemporary classic.

## 4. Topshop Contrast Frill Crook Umbrella

![Topshop Contrast Frill Crook Umbrella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/sm/b5d8lvx454c9342b9d308345150300_300x209.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/JNrGuU)
Umbrellas might be unlikely gift ideas, but this particular one is a beauty. It comes in a cream colour and features a contrast frill trim and binding. It also has a crook handle. The design is very feminine and flirty, and this umbrella would certainly brighten up a dreary day.

## 5. Modcloth Bow-tiful Belongings Pouch

![Modcloth Bow-tiful Belongings Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/t5/r9521got54c9342c4ed16696517603_307x216.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Black and white is a timeless colour combination, which makes such gift ideas perfect for the fashion-conscious person. This multipurpose pouch is adorned with an oversized bow detail, and has gold-tone hardware. It’s perfect for keeping an array of daily essentials in the one place.

## 6. ASOS Tassel Handle Mirror

![ASOS Tassel Handle Mirror](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/zc/okywtd7v54c9342d0fa24851025267_300x241.jpg)

**Price:** $26.96 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/71Gmxd).
This mirror has an antique look design and features a quirky tassel-shaped handle. It’s ideal for those last minute touch ups and close ups – perfect for the fashionista who appreciates a polished appearance. This dainty mirror would make a lovely addition to any dressing table.

## 7. DKNY Quilted Leather IPhone Case

![DKNY Quilted Leather IPhone Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/xj/co23v14a54c9342dbe05b989874824_232x297.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AbOZkW)
This leather phone pouch is a chic and stylish way of housing a piece of technology. It is made from black quilted leather and has a detachable chain shoulder strap. It also has gold hardware and comes with a designer tag and plaque. This is definitely one of those gift ideas intended for someone who seemingly already has it all.

These are just a few gift ideas that should be suitable for those fashion conscious friends. Hey, you might even be tempted to gift some to yourself! Do you have any gift ideas for the fussy fashionista?
