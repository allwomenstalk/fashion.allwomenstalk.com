# 9 Stylish Shorts for Spring/Summer Dressing ...

**Stylish shorts are where it’s** at this spring-summer season. While the weather is only just starting to warm up now, it’s time to get thinking about your warm weather wardrobe. This spring-summer is all about the stylish short. From slouchy runner styles to something more tailored, the short will be the hero of your wardrobe this season. Neutral colours are great for a capsule wardrobe, but trend based coloured and stylish printed shorts will really make your outfit pop. Take a look at a few examples of stylish shorts below.

## 1. Acne Crinkled Crepe Shorts

![Acne Crinkled Crepe Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_acne-crinkled-crepe-shorts_400x348.jpg)

**Price:** $210.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PBVFS2)
Mint is the colour for spring, and nobody is doing it better than Acne. These shorts have an elasticated waist, slit pockets at the front, and a patch pocket at the back. These lush silk shorts have a relaxed design, so dress them up with a white blazer and patent ballet flats.

## 2. Topshop High Waist Scarf Print Shorts

![Topshop High Waist Scarf Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_topshop-high-waist-scarf-print-shorts_400x387.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/UB6ipT)
Scarf prints are very on-trend for this season, thanks to luxury fashion houses like Celine. Topshop have taken inspiration from this trend and created a much more affordable take on the trend in the **form of these stylish printed shorts**. These shorts would look extremely chic paired with a simple black blouse and leather sandals.

## 3. Topshop Co-ord Fluid Shorts

![Topshop Co-ord Fluid Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_topshop-co-ord-fluid-shorts_400x384.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/yaUmbF)
Soft, pastel colours are always a popular choice in the warmer months. These blue tailored shorts would make a stylish addition to any wardrobe. Try your hand at pastel colour blocking and team these shorts with a faded orange singlet.

## 4. Juicy Couture Woven Cotton and Linen Blend Shorts

![Juicy Couture Woven Cotton and Linen Blend Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_juicy-couture-woven-cotton-and-linen-blend-shorts_400x315.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/0gl8Hx)
These cheeky shorts come are a great option for your summer wardrobe. They’re made from fuchsia and white woven linen-blend tweed, and have a fairly streamlined design. These **stylish shorts would look** great with a white t-shirts and tan sandals.

## 5. Topshop Co-ord Stripe Fluid Shorts

![Topshop Co-ord Stripe Fluid Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_topshop-co-ord-stripe-fluid-shorts_400x365.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/u7mHI9)
These striped shorts are perfect for adding a quirky touch to your look. They have a stylish tailored design and feature mustard and white horizontal stripes. Work a preppy look and team these shorts with a sleeveless collared shirt and lace-up brogues.

## 6. Thakoon Addition Volume Pocket Shorts

![Thakoon Addition Volume Pocket Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_thakoon-addition-volume-pocket-shorts_400x318.jpg)

## 7. Topshop Silk Runner Shorts

![Topshop Silk Runner Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_topshop-silk-runner-shorts_400x359.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vBfuh3)
These shorts are great for pulling off this season’s sporty silhouette. They come in a beautiful tangerine silk and feature an elasticated drawstring waist. Dress these up by pairing them with a cotton blouse and wedge shoes.

## 8. Topshop Tropical Mirror Print Shorts

![Topshop Tropical Mirror Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/8_topshop-tropical-mirror-print-shorts_400x365.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/gKalRP)
Florals are still as popular as ever, and these **stylish printed shorts are an** up to date take on the trend. They feature a mirrored tropical print to the sides and have a fitted waistband and fastening at the front. These shorts would look great worn with a reddish-pink top.

## 9. Anna Sui Floral Crochet Shorts

![Anna Sui Floral Crochet Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/9_anna-sui-floral-crochet-shorts_400x413.jpg)

Stylish shorts for the spring-summer seasons come in many forms. Whether you’re after a basic piece for your wardrobe or something more striking, you can’t go past a stylish short. Which of these stylish shorts do you like best?
