# 8 New Season Neon Jeans  Rad or Bad?

Neon jeans seem to be the culmination of two popular trends this season: coloured denim and neon. Stylish neon jeans can be found everywhere from the designer racks to high street stores. There’s a style to suit all budgets. If the neon jeans trend is something that you’re wanting to try out, take a look at a few fun options below.

## 1. Neon the Move Jeans

![Neon the Move Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_neon-the-move-jeans_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Jump head first into the neon trend with these neon green jeans. They have a fitted design but the fabric does allow for some stretch. They also have classic five pocket styling. These neon jeans would look great teamed with a light denim shirt and plain sandals.

## 2. Moto Neon Pink 7/8ths Jeans

![Moto Neon Pink 7/8ths Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_moto-neon-pink-7-8ths-jeans_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/29L9yB)
Bright pink colours are on-trend for the season so amp things up with a pair of neon pink jeans. These neon pink jeans have a cropped design and finish just above the ankle. Wear them with a basic t-shirt and chunky white sandals for a chic off-duty look.

## 3. Current/Elliott the Stiletto Neon Jeans

![Current/Elliott the Stiletto Neon Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_current-elliott-the-stiletto-neon-jeans_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/qVQYr0)
These highlighter green jeans will certainly send shockwaves through your wardrobe. They have a low-rise design and come in a skinny leg cut. You can play down the neon factor by teaming these jeans with neutral separates and accessories.

## 4. Moto Zip Hem Neon 7/8th Jeans

![Moto Zip Hem Neon 7/8th Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_moto-zip-hem-neon-7-8th-jeans_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zsFBnR)
These neon yellow jeans feature zip details at the ankles. They have a cropped design and come with authentic trims. Admittedly, the neon yellow colour isn’t for everyone so try your neon jeans in-store first to make sure they’re right for you. For a fresh look, team these jeans with a plain singlet and white blazer.

## 5. J.Crew Toothpick Cropped Neon Jeans

![J.Crew Toothpick Cropped Neon Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_j-crew-toothpick-cropped-neon-jeans_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $125.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/CCB7EQ)
These jeans come in a rich neon pink colour. They have a mid-rise design, which I prefer in a pair of jeans, and come in a skinny, cropped fit. Work a relaxed, sport luxe look by teaming these jeans with a grey marle top and fashionable low-cut trainers.

## 6. Christopher Kane X J Brand Martini Jean

_ **Price:** $109.00 at barneys.com_
You might have heard that flared jeans are making a comeback, and this particular pair embraces the flared silhouette and neon trend. They come in a neon orange colour and feature frayed hems. Personally, I’m not quite sure what to make of them. Are they cutting edge or just another fad?

## 7. Rag & Bone Leggings-Style Jeans

![Rag & Bone Leggings-Style Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_rag-bone-leggings-style-jeans_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $133.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/EnVmqW)
Another hot-pink option when it comes to neon jeans this season, this pair are marketed as being ‘leggings-style’ jeans. What this refers to is them being made from stretchy, lightweight, brushed-denim material. Team them with a chambray shirt and nude ballet flats for an easy weekend outfit.

## 8. J Brand 811 Mid-Rise Skinny Leg

_ **Price:** $188.00 at barneys.com_
These neon purple jeans will definitely have you standing out from the crowd. They’re made by cult denim label J Brand and come in a super skinny cut. Glam these neon jeans up by teaming them with some silver metallic heels.

Swap your basic blue denim for a neon pair to give your outfit a high impact twist. These are just a few examples that fit the neon jeans trend. So, what do you think of these neon jeans? Are they rad or just plain bad?
