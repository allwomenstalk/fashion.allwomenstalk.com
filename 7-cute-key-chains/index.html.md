# 7 Cute Key Chains ...

Key chains are both functional and decorative. Not only do they make it easy to recognise your set of keys, but they can also say a lot about your personality. Key chains come in a range of novel designs. Below are just a few of my favourite cute key chains.

This owl key chain is a cute and quirky way to house your keys. What I really like about this key chain is the locket feature. It’s perfect for storing a photo of loved ones. ## 3. Say Keys! Key Chains

![Say Keys! Key Chains](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j6/fu/z48zyw1254c8fb2c1374e322014634_300x281.jpg)

**Price:** $7.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This adorable key chain is a miniature replica of a Diana F+ camera. The retro-inspired design of this key chain is sure to set you apart from the rest. The miniature camera is attached to a small chain with a claw clasp. Attach these to your keys, handbag, or maybe even a camera bag!

## 4. Ukiyo5 Octopus Key Chain

**Price:** $7.20 at _shanalogic.com_
Cute key chains are a bit of a novelty item. Take this quirky key chain for example. It features a hot pink octopus character called ‘Yoshi’. It’s made from PVC materials so is flexible and durable. This key chain would make a lively addition to any set of keys.

## 5. Forever21 Coin Purse Key Chain

**Price:** $3.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This coin purse key chain is full of girlish charm. It features a polka dot pattern, twist front, and satin bow tie. It is fully lined and is available in a pink or black colour scheme. It also has a claw clasp enclosure for easy attachment.

## 6. Cath Kidston Enamel Key Ring

![Cath Kidston Enamel Key Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/zh/y2e7pb7y54c8fb2e904d8994810625_283x288.jpg)
