# Fashion Meets Art: Are You Ready to Be Blown Away?

Creativity and talent manifest in all forms. Russian artist Liliya Hudyakova has an incredible eye. Liliya has mastered the art of juxtapositioning art with fashion and design, the results of which are simply breathtaking. In this project I've picked to share with you, Liliya is demonstrating how fashion is so often inspired by nature. Take a look for yourself and then pop over to her site and tick her appreciation box - I think she deserves it - will you?
