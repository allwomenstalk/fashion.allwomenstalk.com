# Sexy on a Budget: 7 Stores That Sell Pretty Panties for under 10 ...

Pretty panties don’t have to cost you a fortune. There’re many different places where you can buy panties for under $10 and some of them are even less than that. Of course you want your underwear to make you feel attractive; that’s a natural desire for a woman. These’re some stores to check out for pretty panties under $10.

## 1. Rue 21

One of the best places to shop for panties under $10 is Rue 21. I didn’t realize how inexpensive their panties were until I was in the store a couple of weeks ago. They actually had panties at 5 for $7.50. You could also buy them individually for $1.99. I couldn’t believe the low price. They were unbelievably cute panties and came in a variety of colors and styles.

## 2. J.C. Penney

Not every pair of panties is under $10 at J.C. Penney but many are, especially if you buy in multiples. They have a brand called Flirtitude that has really cute panties. They’re colorful and perfect for you if you shop in juniors. A lot of their panties are around $7 but you save more if you buy in multiples. You can also buy a 5 pack for $25.

## 3. Charlotte Russe

I love Charlotte Russe for all of their clothing. They’re stylish, trendy and very economical. All of those apply to their panties, too. They have all the popular styles including boyshort, cheeky and thong. They also have several pair that are mostly or completely lace, making them very sexy choices. They sell for $3.50 each or can be caught on sale for 5 for $14.50.

## 4. Wet Seal

Wet Seal is a very unique clothing store. Their styles and fashions have their own brand of flair which is nice when you’d like a little something different. This shows in their panty selection, too. Most of their panties sell for $3.50 or $4.50 and can be caught on sale at 5 for $15.00.

## 5. Forever 21

Don’t you just love Forever 21? They have amazing stuff and a huge variety to choose from. You can spend a little and get a lot when you shop here. Their panties start at a very low price of $2.80. There’re plenty of [sexy styles](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-sexy-styles-to-try-this-weekend/) to choose from but they also have a lot of basic, casual but still pretty panties, too.

## 6. Aeropostale

Very few people have missed out on shopping at Aeropostale. It’s a fun, stylish store to shop at where you don’t have to fork out a fortune. Most of their panties are regularly priced at $8.50 but they’re almost always on sale for $3 or $4. You can also find them on clearance quite often. I’ve actually purchased several pairs for $1 each which is an absolutely amazing deal.

## 7. Urban Outfitters

Urban Outfitters isn’t a store I’ve had a lot of experience shopping at, mainly because there’s none in my area but it’s still a very popular store. Their panties seem to have just a bit more grown up feel to them but they’re still very sexy and not at all average. Most of them are priced at $8 but you can usually catch a sale on them at 5 for $25. They do have some pricier undies but you can choose to stick to the smaller price tags if you’re trying to be economical. When you do that, you have more money to shop for other things, right?

These’re 7 stores where you can find pretty, sexy panties for under $10. Where’s your favorite place to buy panties? What’s the best deal you’ve ever gotten on them?
