# 7 Helpful Tips to Starting a Career in Fashion as a Teenager ...

If you’re a [teenager](https://parenting.allwomenstalk.com/teenage-phases-your-daughter-may-go-through/) who wants a career in fashion, a lot of people are probably telling you that you need to wait until you’re older. However, in today’s world, that doesn’t need to be the case. With the evolution of the Internet, you could find a virtual job, or you could start your own business, or something else entirely. No matter what you choose to do in fashion, these tips are sure to help you start your career in fashion!

## 1. Don’t Let Distance Get in Your Way

If you don’t live in or around New York or Los Angeles, you may feel like you can’t start your fashion career just yet. That doesn’t have to be the case, though. If you look hard enough, you could find remote or virtual jobs in the fashion industry that will let you get career experience without living in a big city.

## 2. Don’t Let Age Get in Your Way

A lot of people will tell you to wait until college, or even after college, to start your career in fashion. However, don’t let that get in your way. Use your age as an advantage. Tell your potential employers your age, and make sure employers know just how passionate you are about your field. You’re sure to impress them.

## 3. Read

You need to know a lot about your industry, especially if you’re younger. Like I said, you want to impress your potential employers with your knowledge, and the more you know at a young age, the more you’ll impress them. Read. Read everything about fashion that you can get your hands on. You’ll upstage anyone who’s double your age if you’re well educated on the fashion industry.

## 4. Start Your Own Business

Just because you’re a teenager doesn’t mean you can’t start your own business. If you’ve ever had an idea for t-shirt designs, or you want to sell vintage clothes, do it. Don’t let your age stand in the way. You may have to work a job that you don’t love to fund your new business, but if you love what you’re doing and are passionate about it, you’re sure to be successful.

## 5. Start a Blog or YouTube Channel

You’re a teenager during a great time, because you could essentially create an entire brand and business about yourself. Start a blog if you love writing about fashion, or start a YouTube channel if you can better express your fashion tips in that medium. You may just build up a following, and you could become one of the biggest social media influencers on the Internet!

## 6. Find Something That Sets You Apart

The key to making a name for yourself in the fashion industry is to set yourself apart from everyone else. Make it whatever what you want, whether you have a unique sense of style, you make your own jewelry, or even just your age. If you’re a teenager starting a career in fashion, you’re already setting yourself apart from the crowd.

## 7. Look for Internships

There are actually a fair amount of fashion internships that will hire high school students. It can be tricky to find them, but if you really want it, you can find one that you love that will help you get your foot in the door of the fashion industry.

Are you looking to start a career in the fashion industry? If you are in the fashion industry, help some of the teenagers out in the comments!
