# 7 Hot Spring Fashion Trends for 2014 ...

Fashion is something I’ve always enjoyed keeping up with and the latest fashion trends for 2014 are already taking the runway by storm, along with fashion magazines everywhere. Vogue, Glamour Magazine, Lucky Magazine and others, have been showcasing the latest fashion trends for 2014 and showing women everywhere how to look their best and be in style at the same time. Want to know how to give your fashion routine a trendy makeover? Just check out these trends below and start the new year off in style!

## 1. Pretty Pastels

![Pretty Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15065331_6334_600x800.jpg)

While bold colors were a trend in 2013, some of the newest fashion trends for 2014 are pretty pastels. Whether for pants, skirts, blouses or dress attire, pretty pastels are all over the runway right now, as well as in magazines everywhere. Greens, blues, lavenders, pinks and yellow pastels will be everywhere in spring time, and the best part is, there is no boundary as how to wear them. They fit any silhouette, style, shape or form.

## 2. Wide-Legged Trousers

![Wide-Legged Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15065632_5401_480x720.jpg)

One of my favorite trends for 2014 is wide-legged trousers. I have always loved this style of pants, mainly because they flatter so many shapes and sizes, and they really add a wonderfully casual, yet glamorous look to any woman’s outfit. Wide-legged trousers are also incredibly comfortable and automatically lengthen legs, and give you a little extra height, which a short girl like me always appreciates!

## 3. Tea Length Skirts

![Tea Length Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15070317_0785_600x900.jpg)

Another one of my favorite looks for spring time is tea length skirts. This trend is already appearing in stores now, and was showcased on the runways in fall and winter fashion trends, but when spring hits, the impact will be even greater. Belling out, and hitting mid calf in length, these tea skirts add a romantic femininity to women’s outfits that we haven’t seen in quite some time. Paired with a cropped blouse and fitted jacket, these make a perfect outfit for the office, special events or even a day out shopping!

## 4. Cropped Jackets

![Cropped Jackets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15070405_0707_600x903.jpg)

Cropped jackets are one of my favorite trends of 2014 as well. They’re bold, yet really feminine, and even a little sassy at the same time. Unlike longer blazers, which I still happen to love, cropped jackets are a little trendier and can be worn anywhere, anytime, without taking on such a professional look.

## 5. Collarless Coats

![Collarless Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15070510_8671_600x1002.jpg)

Trends for coats in the past have usually involved bold collars that added a high fashion appearance to outfits, but this year, collars are taking a back seat in the fashion department. Long and short coats are both appearing without collars at all, and instead featuring stylish borders, buttons and hems.

## 6. Button Downs

![Button Downs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15070542_4930_500x750.jpg)

Whether it be a skirt, shirt, jacket or pair of pants, everything is about buttons this year. Button down shirts paired with a button down blouse are one of the most popular looks, as well as button down jackets and skirts.

## 7. Sporty Bomber Jackets

![Sporty Bomber Jackets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/15070618_4654_600x899.jpg)

I’ve always loved bomber jackets, but this year, they’re getting a much sportier look to them. The new jackets this year almost have an athletic look to them, and are much cooler than typical heavy bomber jackets we’ve been familiar with in past trends. The new jackets are light in weight, very casual and also easy to pair with any ensemble of your choosing.

You’ll be seeing these new fashion trends for 2014, along with many others, in stores everywhere soon. I’ve already got my eye on a few pairs of wide legged trousers, a crop jacket and a pastel pair of pants that I recently spotted. What’s your favorite fashion trend for 2014?
