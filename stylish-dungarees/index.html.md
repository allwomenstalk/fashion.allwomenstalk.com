# 7 Stylish Dungarees ...

Dungarees, also known as overalls, are enjoying a resurgence in popularity thanks to trend setters such as Alexa Chung. Take inspiration from your childhood days and add a mature twist to the faithful dungaree. You can opt for denim, or maybe materials that are a little bit more luxe. Take inspiration from a few of the stylish dungarees below.

## 1. A.P.C Denim Overall Dress

_ **Price:** $250.00 at net-a-porter.com_
These stylish dungarees are a sophisticated take on the trend. The indigo denim is a classic material and the dress design is a great way to work the utilitarian trend. These dungarees feature silver hardware, faux-fly stitching and slant pockets. Wear a striped t-shirt underneath and pair with loafers for that ‘it girl’ aesthetic.

## 2. Topshop USA Print Dungaree Dress

_ **Price:** $95.00 at us.topshop.com_
This dungaree dress is a little bit left of centre. It features a playful USA flag print design. It also features a front bib pocket and cross-over strap details at the back. These would look great layered with a basic t-shirt and slouchy blazer.

## 3. Miss Selfridge Navy Wideleg Dungaree Trousers

_ **Price:** $75.00 at missselfridge.com_
These navy dungaree trousers have a bit of a 70s retro feel about them. These stylish dungarees feature cross-over straps and a wide legged design. The high waist also helps to create a lengthened silhouette. These would be well suited for those after a more sophisticated take on the trend.

## 4. Topshop Moto Black Embroidered Denim Dungarees

_ **Price:** $100.00 at us.topshop.com_
These short dungarees are a little bit cheeky. The ‘hotpant’ design means that they would be well suited to a more youthful audience. They feature a button front, side pockets, and gold-tone hardware. They also feature rose embroidered details scattered throughout the material. Wear these with a floaty blouse to offset the skimpiness of the hotpant design.

## 5. Paul & Joe Sarment Cotton Playsuit

_ **Price:** $105.00 at theoutnet.com_
If you’re after something a little more sweet and playful, these stylish dungarees should do the trick. They are made from a beige cotton material and feature floral turn-up cuffs. The floral trim is what really stands out for me. Dress these up with contrasting warm accents, denim shirts, and strappy leather sandals.

## 6. A.P.C Indigo Overall

_ **Price:** $280.00 at lagarconne.com_
These dungarees feature a straight neckline, concealed zip at the side, and large patch pockets. They also feature overhanging straps at the front. They are a slightly relaxed fit, but would still create quite a lean silhouette. These would look great with a crisp white shirt and black patent pumps.

## 7. Y’s Salopette Pants

_ **Price:** $525.00 at lagarconne.com_
These Y’s by Yohji Yamamoto coveralls are slightly different take on the dungaree trend. They are made from 100% cotton and feature cotton twill straps. The legs feature snap closures to create a tapered silhouette. These dungarees are designed to be a relaxed fit, and would work well in a corporate setting worn with blouses and almond toed heels.

The dungaree can definitely be a dressier wardrobe option. Are you going to embrace the dungaree trend? Which of the above styles do you prefer most?
