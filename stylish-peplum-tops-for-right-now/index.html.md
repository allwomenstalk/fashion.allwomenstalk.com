# 7 Stylish Peplum Tops for Right Now ...

Peplum tops are an easy way to work the peplum trend right now. While peplum skirts can be tricky to master, you can easily slip a peplum top on over a plain skirt or pair of pants. Whether you go for a fitted style or something looser is entirely up to you. Get inspired by the following stylish peplum tops.

## 1. Peplum Frill Tee

![Peplum Frill Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/1_peplum-frill-tee_1008x1024.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/F8cLkK)
Colourful peplum tops are a guaranteed winner this season. This top has a looser style with a small peplum detail. This peplum frill tee comes in a bright blue colour and would look great paired with a structured white mini skirt or skinny jeans.

## 2. Bonbon Vivant Top

![Bonbon Vivant Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/2_bonbon-vivant-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This black and white striped top has a certain retro feel to it. The stripes are angled on the body to create a chevron effect. It also comes with a slim patent belt to help define the waist. Keep the retro feel going by teaming this peplum top with cropped black pants and cat eye sunglasses.

## 3. Textured Peplum Top

![Textured Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/3_textured-peplum-top_976x1232.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/JJM8Og)
This peplum top would look stunning worn at night. It has a black textured finish with an exposed zip detail at the back. Wear it with skinny jeans, killer heels, and a structured metallic bag for a chic and stylish night time look.

## 4. Sunny Attitude Top

![Sunny Attitude Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/4_sunny-attitude-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Brighten up your day and that of everyone around you by donning this sunny yellow top. Peplum tops are bound to garner attention anyway, so why not make the most of it in a vivid yellow hue? This top also features a cutout back. This top would look great paired with a navy patterned skirt and patent shoes.

## 5. Basic Peplum Top

![Basic Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_basic-peplum-top_1021x1530.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/D7FWjf)
Since peplum detailing can be quite dramatic in its own right, keep the rest of your look fairly simple. Choosing a peplum tops in basic colours and cuts is a great way of playing down the peplum effect. This top has a sleeveless design with a high neckline, and would look great worn underneath a cropped jacket or blazer.

## 6. As Cute as You Dot Top

![As Cute as You Dot Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/6_as-cute-as-you-dot-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a more relaxed take on the peplum top. It has a looser cut with a lightly ruffled hem. It’s perfect for someone wanting a peplum effect without the frilliness of some other designs. The polka dot pattern is also a cute touch. This top would be perfect worn in denim shorts in the summer time.

## 7. Maje Ruffled Peplum Top

![Maje Ruffled Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/7_maje-ruffled-peplum-top_460x528.jpg)

**Price:** $189.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pX9qXI)
Peplum tops are quite feminine and this white one is a decadent designer splurge. It’s made from a satin-twill and has a wide neck with ruffled hem. Team it with a fitted skirt and pointed heels for a modern, ladylike look.

It doesn’t matter what colour or pattern your top is, just make sure that it has a peplum silhouette! The style and size of the peplum detail may vary depending on what best suits your body shape. Which one of these stylish peplum tops do you like best?
