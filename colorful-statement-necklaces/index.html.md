# 7 Colorful Statement Necklaces ...

**Colorful statement necklaces** are a fun way to get into the swing of spring/summer dressing. Adding a colorful necklace to your outfit can instantly update your look. I’m a big fan of updating your look with trend-based accessories, and a colorful statement necklace is your best bet this season. Take a look at the following colorful necklaces that make a statement.

## 1. Topshop Regatta Multi Wrap Necklace

![Topshop Regatta Multi Wrap Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_topshop-regatta-multi-wrap-necklace_269x400.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/kXuv4Q)
Most colorful statement necklaces right now have a certain tribal feel to them. The tribal trend is still going strong so work it into your outfit with a beaded or fabric necklace. This necklace features multicoloured wrap strands and a clasp fastening at the back. Wear it with a white blouse and denim shorts for an easy weekend look.

## 2. Antik Batik Elias Tasseled Necklace

![Antik Batik Elias Tasseled Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_antik-batik-elias-tasseled-necklace_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hgoaMc)
If you really want to make a statement, try on a fringed or tasseled necklace. It features a short gold-tone brass chain with green and aqua suede tassels hanging off of it. This layered green number is also great for adding some interesting texture to your outfit. Work a really bright, summery look by wearing it with contrasting colours.

## 3. ASOS Silk Cord Statement Collar

![ASOS Silk Cord Statement Collar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_asos-silk-cord-statement-collar_307x400.jpg)

This colorful statement necklace combines a bit of both grunge and glamour. It features metallic cord pieces intertwined with silver-tone beading. The end result is one that is quite mesmerising. This necklace would look great worn with a neutral coloured top.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Heart Necklace

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Heart Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_marc-by-marc-jacobs-heart-necklace_332x400.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Uv7rHD)
Playful, novelty inspired jewellery is starting to take off as a trend. Take the lead from this Marc by Marc Jacobs heart necklace. It’s based on a classic padlock design but features neon pink acetate chains and details throughout. Wear it with a cream top and light denim jeans for a relaxed look.

## 5. Topshop Stripe Collar Necklace

![Topshop Stripe Collar Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_topshop-stripe-collar-necklace_284x400.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/4zYKD6)
Just because you’re after some color doesn’t mean you have to let go of the gold or silver finished. **This colorful statement necklace** combines blue, orange and gold stripes; all in a sleek collar design. Wear it at night with little black dress and gold heels.

## 6. Forever21 Color Blocked Fringe Necklace

_ **Price:** $8.80 at forever21.com_
Another example of fringing done well, this necklace also takes the lead from the color blocking trend. It features hot pink and gold fringing and chain details. Wear this necklace during the day or night - you’ll make a statement on both occasions!

## 7. Topshop Native Bead Collar Necklace

![Topshop Native Bead Collar Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_topshop-native-bead-collar-necklace_252x400.jpg)

Wear this colorful statement necklace with a plain singlet and colored jeans for a sunny spring-summer look.
