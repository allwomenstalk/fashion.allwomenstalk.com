# 8 Stylish Hats to Wear This Winter ...

Stylish hats are an impeccably fashionable way to top off your outfits this winter. Whether you prefer the eternally cool fedora or a vintage inspired cloche, a stylish hat is a must-have when it comes to accessorising your outfit. A hat is not only stylish, but it’s also perfect for concealing a bad hair day or for added warmth. Take a look at a few of these fashionable hat styles for the season and share with us your favorite pieces.

## 1. Felt Fedora

![Felt Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_felt-fedora_600x442.jpg)

Always a favourite, the fedora is a classic style of hat for you to wear this winter. No wardrobe is complete without one. This season try a fedora in an on-trend colour like a deep red or midnight blue. This one is by Rag & Bone but you could head to stores like Nasty Gal, Topshop, and Forever 21 for equally stylish hats that are easier on the wallet.

## 2. Cropped Cloche

![Cropped Cloche](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_cropped-cloche_300x261.jpg)

Embrace a retro revival in a chic cloche this winter. The style is extremely elegant and perfect for topping off a ladylike outfit. This black wool cloche is a steal at only $18 from Forever 21! You’ll also find similar hats at all your usual suspects like ASOS, Topshop, and Modcloth.

## 3. Leather Cap

![Leather Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_leather-cap_460x354.jpg)

When it comes to stylish hats this season, the leather cap is the cool kid in the crowd. Buoyed by the fashion world’s love of sport luxe trends, a leather baseball cap is definitely the ‘it’ item of the season. This leather cap by Shakuhachi will leave you close to $200 out of pocket, but you can find cheaper alternatives at stores like Nasty Gal and Free People.

## 4. Riding Hat

![Riding Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_riding-hat_592x496.jpg)

Add some equestrian inspired style to your outfit with a chic riding hat. For designer buys you can’t go past Ralph Lauren for this polo-inspired look, but you can definitely find more affordable alternatives at stores like ASOS and Topshop. The one pictured here is by Catarzi for ASOS and retails for around $60.

## 5. Roller Hat

![Roller Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_roller-hat_600x386.jpg)

Channel your inner hipster with a roller hat this winter. Wear them with your hair out and with a 90s inspired outfit of a denim shirt, leggings and Dr Martens boots. Topshop and ASOS both stock a range of these hat styles in different colors and most cost around the $50 mark.

## 6. Floppy Hat

![Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_floppy-hat_321x212.jpg)

Nothing says mysterious quite like a wide-brimmed floppy hat. Choose one in a wool-felt material for a winter appropriate take on this stylish hat. You can pick up these hats by brands like French Connection and Juicy Couture, or for under $20 from Forever 21 like the hat pictured above.

## 7. Studded Beret

![Studded Beret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_studded-beret_600x292.jpg)

The beret is given an update this winter with some studded embellishments. While this Gucci number costs a whopping $400, you can get much more budget friendly versions from Dorothy Perkins.

## 8. Fashion Turban

![Fashion Turban](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/8_fashion-turban_600x519.jpg)

More along the lines of a beanie, the turban is another stylish hat you could add to your repertoire this winter. Splash out on a resort-style Missoni turban or choose this more affordable knitted style from ASOS.

Embrace stylish hats this winter and update your look with one of these chic pieces of head gear. If you want a style that’ll last you year in and year out then a fedora or wide-brimmed floppy hat would be my pick of stylish hats. Which of these hat styles do you like best?
