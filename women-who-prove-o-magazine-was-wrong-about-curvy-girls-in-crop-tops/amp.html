Frequently Asked Questions

Why should chubby girls wear crop tops?
Why not? Crop tops are meant to be fun and stylish. Chubby girls can rock them just as well as anyone else! It's all about feeling confident and loving your body.
What are some tips for chubby girls wearing crop tops?
Start with high-waisted bottoms to balance the look, choose tops that hit just above the waist, and wear what makes you feel comfortable and fabulous.
Are crop tops only for skinny girls?
Absolutely not! Crop tops are for everyone, no matter what size. Fashion is about expressing yourself and feeling good in what you wear.
What if I don't feel confident in a crop top?
Confidence comes with time. Try wearing your crop top at home first to get used to it. Remember, it’s all about how you feel, not about what others think!
Where can I find crop tops for plus-size women?
Many brands carry gorgeous plus-size crop tops. Check out stores like Torrid, ASOS Curve, and Forever 21 Plus. You'll find plenty of options to choose from!
