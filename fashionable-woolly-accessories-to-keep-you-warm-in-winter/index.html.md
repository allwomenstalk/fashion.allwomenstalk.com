# 9 Fashionable Woolly Accessories to Keep You Warm in Winter ...

Stay warm in winter by kitting yourself out in woolly accessories. From hats to scarves to gloves, woolly accessories are the perfect accents to your cold weather outfits. Whether you prefer timeless or statement pieces, there’s a range of warm and woolly accessories for you to get your hands on this season. Check out these woolly accessories to keep you toasty warm in winter.

## 1. Wool Fedora Hat

![Wool Fedora Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210717_1931_599x303.jpg)

Top off your winter outfits with the ultimate off-duty woolly accessories. The fedora is a classic accessory and will give any outfit an effortlessly cool look. This wool fedora from ASOS comes in a chic grey colour and has a simple ribbon band. It’ll set you back around $80 but you can check out stores like Forever 21 for similar buys on a budget.

## 2. Wool Panel Gloves

Our hands usually bear the brunt of the elements in winter so cover them up in some woolly gloves. These basic grey marl gloves feature leather palms and would be a versatile addition to your winter wardrobe. They’re $50 at Topshop and you can also find similar gloves at Zara.

## 3. Chunky Knitted Snood

![Chunky Knitted Snood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210712_1648_599x743.jpg)

You can’t beat a chunky knit scarf for style, and this basic black ‘snood’ is sure to keep you cosy and warm. The double wrap design of this snood is perfect for making sure you’re all covered up. It’s $17 from Dorothy Perkins but you’re bound to find similar snoods at a range of fashion chain stores. Wear one with a classic camel trench, skinny jeans, ankle boots, and floppy fedora for a timeless winter look.

## 4. Bright Wool Mix Beanie

![Bright Wool Mix Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210715_8117_599x445.jpg)

Add a pop of colour to your winter outfits with a bright beanie. Work a sporty look and team one with a printed sweater, jeans, and sneakers. This bright red beanie is an affordable buy at under $20 from ASOS. It’s made from a chunky knit cable and is sure to keep your head nice and warm in winter. Check out Topshop and ASOS for more brightly coloured beanies.

## 5. Wool Baseball Cap

![Wool Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210716_8609_599x531.jpg)

Channel a sporty equestrian vibe and try on a chic wool baseball cap this winter. It’s a look that was popular on the runways with designers such as Stella McCartney. This particular cap will set you back a whopping $300 but you can try checking out J.Crew and Sole Society for similar caps under $40. This style of cap would look great worn with a wool coat, skinny jeans, and tall boots.

## 6. Embellished Beanie

Embellishments are still all the rage and this season even the humble beanie has been blinged up. You can buy embellished beanies, like this ASOS number, for around $20. You could also go for the DIY option and update an old beanie with your own design.

## 7. Wool-Blend Bow Gloves

![Wool-Blend Bow Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210713_2693_349x439.jpg)

Add a ladylike touch to your outfits with some bow detail gloves. These come in a striking red colour and feature a layered bow detail at the wrist. They’re around $50 from Modcloth and would look great worn with a ladylike dress, tailored coat, and ballet flats.

## 8. Wool-Blend Bag

![Wool-Blend Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210718_3090_599x558.jpg)

Wool-blend bags are an interesting way to add some woolly texture to your outfit. This doctor’s style bag features a woolly black and white check pattern and is roomy enough for all your daily essentials. You can purchase one from ASOS for $70 which is a fraction of the cost of similar designs by brands like Fendi and Marni.

## 9. Patterned Wool Tights

![Patterned Wool Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/26210718_8472_599x889.jpg)

Wool tights a great way to make your favourite dresses more winter-appropriate. These grey tights feature an all over pattern and would look great worn with vintage-style dresses. They cost around $20 from Topshop and you can find similar tights by Hansel from Basel and Modcloth.

These are just some woolly accessories you may want to add to your outfits this winter. What’s your favourite winter accessory? Which of these winter warmers do you like the look of best?
