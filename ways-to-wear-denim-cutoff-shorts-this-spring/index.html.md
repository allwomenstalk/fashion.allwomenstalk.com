# 19 Ways to Wear Denim Cutoff Shorts This Spring ...

Denim cutoff shorts are probably the most basic yet super trendy pieces ever. Finding the perfect pair of shorts that show off your killer legs (without putting everything on display) should pretty much be considered an art. I absolutely love pairing these sexy pieces with whatever I can find in my closet; it doesn't hurt that they're practically made for Spring. After seeing just how versatile they are, you'll have no choice but to grab or DIY a pair of your own!
