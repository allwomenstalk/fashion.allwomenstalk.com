# 8 Pretty Paisley Pieces for You to Wear ...

Paisley pieces tend to go in and out of fashion. Right now, with the popularity of all things retro, paisley print pieces are very much having a trending moment. Avoid anything too boho or hippie-ish by choosing a paisley piece in a minimal, contemporary design. Below are a few stylish paisley pieces that you may want to try out for yourself.

## 1. Sleeveless Paisley Shirt

_ **Price:** $16.90 at forever21.com_
This sleeveless collared shirt features a subtle paisley pattern and chambray trimming. This paisley piece is quite subtle and just looks like a large lace-like pattern. This shirt would look great tucked into a pair of tailored shorts or a full skirt for a cool summer look.

## 2. Gonsalves & Hall Paisley Dress

![Gonsalves & Hall Paisley Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_gonsalves-hall-paisley-dress_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $84.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This dress has a fairly obvious paisley print, but the design of the dress is quite clean and structured. It has a high round neckline, elastic waist, and cutout back. Work a feminine look by teaming it with neutral ballet flats and a compact side bag.

## 3. Paisley Lace Dress

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
Pretty paisley pieces like this lace dress are perfect for wearing to your next party. This one comes in a vivid shade of blue and has a paisley lace overlay. It has a strapless, fitted design and comes with a matching waist belt. Dress it up with silver heels and jewellery or toughen it up with a cream biker jacket.

## 4. Paisley Print Top Knot

![Paisley Print Top Knot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_paisley-print-top-knot_400x388.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/INmWMy)
If a touch of paisley is what you’re after, then work it into your outfit in the form of this top knot scarf. It comes in a retro brown paisley print and is perfect for tying back messy beach hair.

## 5. Ivory Paisley Lace Top

![Ivory Paisley Lace Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_ivory-paisley-lace-top_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Peplum is one of the big trends of the season, so work it wisely with paisley pieces such as this lace top. It features a stunning ivory lace overlay and peplum waist. This top has a bit of a vintage vibe, so if you want to keep things modern team it with black skinny jeans and ankle boots.

## 6. Orelia Paisley Ribbon Wrap Bracelet

![Orelia Paisley Ribbon Wrap Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_orelia-paisley-ribbon-wrap-bracelet_400x352.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/uPVpdm)
This ribbon bracelet is a quirky way to wear the paisley trend. It has a pretty green silk satin band and features an embellished paisley charm. Simply wrap it around your wrist for a sweet paisley hit.

## 7. Paisley Floral Top

**Price:** $32.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Paisley pieces like this top are perfect for working a breezy summer look. It comes in a striking yellow colour and features multicoloured paisley-shaped flower arrangements. Team this top with denim shorts, tan sandals, and a floppy hat for a fun and casual outfit.

## 8. Beaded Paisley Earrings

_ **Price:** $4.80 at forever21.com_
Glam up your look with these paisley earrings. These teardrop shaped earrings come in a gold-tone finish and feature cutout paisley shapes with beaded details. Wear these at night to glam up an LBD, or during the day with a crisp all-white outfit.

It’s never been easier to incorporate some paisley pieces into your wardrobe. Whether you like a bold paisley print or something more subtle, it will certainly add a bit of spark to an outfit. Will you try adding a paisley piece to your wardrobe this season?
