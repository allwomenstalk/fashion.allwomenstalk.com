# 8 Budget Friendly Fashion Buys for Right Now ...

Budget friendly fashion makes up the majority of my wardrobe. Like the majority of the population, decking myself out in designer threads each season is just not an option. Thankfully there are plenty of high street and budget fashion stores who keep up with seasonal trends, making it much easier to keep up with the latest fashions on a budget. Get the hottest looks for less with these budget friendly fashions.

## 1. Peplum Frill Tee

![Peplum Frill Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_peplum-frill-tee_600x633.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/yFJQOZ)
If you haven’t jumped on the peplum bandwagon, what are you waiting for? There’s no excuse with the amount of budget friendly fashion available at the moment. This black peplum tee is a relaxed way to work the look and a versatile option for your wardrobe.

## 2. Geometric Jacket

![Geometric Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_geometric-jacket_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $29.90 at [forever21.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.forever21.com/)
This jacket has a vintage inspired design which sits nicely with the 1960s inspired trends that are popular right now. It has a cropped design with a Peter Pan collar and three quarter sleeves. The geometric pattern is also quite eye-catching. Wear it open with a long line tee and skinny jeans for a casual look.

## 3. Silver Texture Pelmet Skirt

![Silver Texture Pelmet Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_silver-texture-pelmet-skirt_600x586.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wNO6N1)
Metallics are a big trend right now, with many designers creating metallic clothing. This skirt is a great example of budget friendly fashion that takes inspiration from runway looks. Work a contemporary look and team this skirt with a black biker jacket, plain singlet, and ankle boots.

## 4. Tribal Print Pants

_ **Price:** $17.80 at forever21.com_
Printed pants are a must for right now. These tribal printed pants are a stylish and affordable option. They come in a versatile navy blue colour and feature a subtle print. Wear them with a button down shirt and pumps for a polished everyday look.

## 5. Sheer Pocket Button up

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
This blue shirt would make a punchy addition to a basic wardrobe. It has a classic collared design and is made from sheer fabric. Work an on-trend look by tucking this budget friendly fashion buy into a coloured mini skirt or printed pants.

## 6. Gold Rush Hour Flats

_ **Price:** $34.99 at modcloth.com_
These loafer style shoes are given a glam update with a good dose of gold glitter. These shoes combine both the glitter and loafer trends, and would look effortlessly chic worn with a plain white tee and denim jeans.

## 7. Wine Cotton Ankle Skinny

![Wine Cotton Ankle Skinny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/7_wine-cotton-ankle-skinny_600x815.jpg)

**Price:** $39.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Get a jump on fall fashion by wearing these wine coloured pants right now. They have a skinny, ankle-length fit with pockets at the front and back. Wear them during the day with a striped Breton top, ballet flats, and satchel bag.

## 8. Double Zip Shoulder Bag

**Price:** $22.80 at _forever21.com_
This sporty looking shoulder bag is perfect for adding an athletic inspired touch to your look. In the vein of some designer bags, it has a slouchy shape and features multiple zip closures. Make it your casual, day to day bag and wear it slung across the body.

From glam glitter flats to tribal printed pants, these are just some budget friendly fashions to whet your appetite. What’s your best tip for buying budget friendly fashion? What are your favourite budget brands?
