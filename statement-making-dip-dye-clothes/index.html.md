# 7 Statement Making Dip Dye Clothes ...

Dip dye clothes are having a bit of a trending moment. Other buzz words that refer to dip dyed clothing include “ombré” and “degradé”. The general effect, however, is the same with clothing consisting of a change between one or more colours. Slight variations may depend on the desired effect, with the colour change either being dramatic and harsh or something that is more gradual. If this is a look you want to try out this season, take a look at the dip dye clothing featured below.

This luxe textured sweater features subtle dip dye detailing. It’s made from a cream knit and features silver flecks and a blue dip dye effect at the hem. Work a relaxed weekend look by teaming this sweater with jeans, and strappy sandals.

## 2. Modcloth Dip Dye Dress

![Modcloth Dip Dye Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_modcloth-dip-dye-dress_350x500.jpg)

This dress has a relaxed, slightly slouchy fit with an elasticised waist. Wear it with some strappy brown sandals and a slouchy side bag for an easy daytime look.

## 3. Topshop Knitted Crochet Dip Dye Shorts

![Topshop Knitted Crochet Dip Dye Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_topshop-knitted-crochet-dip-dye-shorts_350x303.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ZNqkwn)
A pair of crochet shorts would be the perfect addition to any summer festival wardrobe. Give the usually girly crochet short an updated edge by choosing one with dip dye detailing. The combination of blue dye on a white background is one that is extremely popular when it comes to dip dye clothes. These shorts would look great worn with a white blouse and neutral shoes.

## 4. Forever21 Ombré Trapeze Tank

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
For an on-trend and affordable dip dye hit, take a look at this minty blue tank top. It has a relaxed trapeze design and features a crocheted bib and dip dye hem. Wear this top with coloured denim shorts or jeans for a fun, fashionable look.

## 5. Reed Krakoff Printed Wrap Dress

![Reed Krakoff Printed Wrap Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_reed-krakoff-printed-wrap-dress_350x525.jpg)

## 6. Topshop Moto Dip Dyed Hotpants

![Topshop Moto Dip Dyed Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_topshop-moto-dip-dyed-hotpants_350x275.jpg)

## 7. Day Birger Et Mikkelson Maxi Dress

![Day Birger Et Mikkelson Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_day-birger-et-mikkelson-maxi-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $320.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/IZf6iZ)
When you’re wearing dip dye clothes, you really want the detail to be noticeable. A maxi dress is perfect for this as it allows you to see the change in colour. This particular maxi dress starts with a navy blue dyed top that gradually makes its way to a pale blue hem. Give it some shape by cinching it in with a skinny belt and pair with black sandals for an effortless warm weather outfit.
