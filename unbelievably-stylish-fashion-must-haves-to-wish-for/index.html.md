# 9 Unbelievably Stylish Fashion Must\-Haves to Wish for ...

Fashion Must-Haves vary from person to person. However, there are occasionally a few items that become so popular that they find themselves on many fashion savvy wish lists. From an oversized cocktail ring to the latest It-bag, fashion must-haves come in all shapes and sizes. Below are just a few fashion must-haves that are currently on my wish list.

## 1. Yves Saint Laurent Arty Gold-Plated Glass Ring

![Yves Saint Laurent Arty Gold-Plated Glass Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/na/im8zwtji54c9349ed934b999241897_400x329.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jSP94P)
This ring is a status symbol as much as it is an icon. The ring of choice for fashion bloggers, this ring is a modern classic. Originally available in coral and turquoise, this ring is now available in a range of colours and finishes.

## 2. Karen Walker Number One Sunglasses

![Karen Walker Number One Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/wa/l8legljd54c9349fecadc063847407_400x184.jpg)

**Price:** $180.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Tortoiseshell sunglasses are a classic choice. These Karen Walker ones, however, have a distinct contemporary design. They have acetate frames and feature a bold tortoiseshell pattern. They’re a killer way to update a plain outfit.

## 3. Alexander McQueen Velvet Britain Clutch

![Alexander McQueen Velvet Britain Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dd/h1/degfb4a954c934a0b1b77991828770_240x192.jpg)

**Price:** $1262.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This Alexander McQueen clutch features the iconic skull design clasp. It features a luxurious red velvet body with a Union Jack print on the front and back. The gold-tone skull clasp features pearl eyes and Swarovski teeth. This clutch would make a decadent addition to any nighttime look.

## 4. Mulberry Alexa Leather Bag

![Mulberry Alexa Leather Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/vz/idqfj5yr54c934a1901f3764503157_400x328.jpg)

**Price:** $1250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/F0kk4Q)
Named after fashion It-girl Alexa Chung, this bag is one of the newest fashion must-haves. Available in small to large sizes, this bag has a distinctly Mulberry design albeit with a modern twist. This bag is both versatile and wearable.

## 5. J Brand 811 Mid-Rise Twill Cropped Skinny Jeans

**Price:** $175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/kP3qPN)
Coloured jeans are all the rage at the moment. My top pick is this pair of red skinny jeans. They are made from lightweight Japanese twill and have a mid-rise design. Team them with a striped knit, black patent loafers, and satchel for a polished everyday look.

## 6. Miu Miu Gold Glitter round Toe Pump

_ **Price:** $630.00 at barneys.com_
Anything glittery is a must-have this season. It’s no wonder then that these Miu Miu gold glitter pumps have found their way onto this list. These pumps have a concealed platform and an exaggerated curved heel. They’d be a dazzling addition to any evening outfit.

## 7. Christian Louboutin Pigalle 85 Leather Pumps

![Christian Louboutin Pigalle 85 Leather Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/u1/or4df5u354c934a4a7907731859973_400x311.jpg)

**Price:** $595.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/8ES66j)
Nude pumps are a must for any wardrobe. This pair has a classic design and is sure to be a staple piece in any wardrobe. They have a mid heel, pointed toe, and feature the iconic Christian Louboutin red sole. Truly versatile, these shoes are worth the investment.

## 8. Proenza Schouler PS1 Pochette

**Price:** $895.00 at _barneys.com_
This bright red pochette features the distinctive PS1 design. It has a flat front face with leather straps and metal hardware. A pochette is slightly bigger than a wallet but smaller than a clutch, making it perfect for carrying the bare essentials. Travel light and use this pochette to carry your cards, keys, and phone during the day.

## 9. Yves Saint Laurent Skinny Y Belt

![Yves Saint Laurent Skinny Y Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zx/bp/sm0f5stp54c934a665402965939989_216x171.jpg)

**Price:** $237.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A neutral skinny belt is a wardrobe staple. This YSL belt features aged gold-tone hardware and a metal Y buckle. It is understated and elegant. Wear this belt with trousers, or use it to cinch in a dress.

These are just a few of my fashion must-haves this season. Of course, I can only dream of being able to own them all. What fashion must-haves are on the top of your wish list?
