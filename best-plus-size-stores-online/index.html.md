# 7 Best plus Size Stores Online ...

Nowadays if you are a fuller figured, curvy fashionista you don’t have to sacrifice your style, as the web features fashions from some of the best plus size stores online. Finally the industry has embraced different shapes and sizes with a new crop of plus size stores popping up everywhere and revamping curvy fashion with trendy styles and flattering clothing! So make the most out of your shape and shop these 7 best plus size stores online.

## 1. ASOS Curve

![ASOS Curve](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_asos-curve_600x515.jpg)

[us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/dQfFlA)
This UK based online retailer is a staple in plus size shopping for extremely on-trend focused styles. The ASOS Curve collection features directional designs right from their mainline collection so this way you can get what you want in a size that fits and flatters your body. They also offer selected styles exclusive to the Curve collection, which will have petite women teeming with envy.

## 2. Forever 21+

![Forever 21+](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_forever-21_600x515.jpg)

[forever21.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.forever21.com/)
Previously known as Faith 21, Forever 21+ offers sizes up to a 2x on most items at a reasonable price. Their super trendy and fun fashion forward designs are a hit among fashionistas worldwide and now full figured ladies can get in on the action too! With their affordable prices, Forever 21+ makes shopping a steal without compromising your style.

## 3. Torrid

![Torrid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_torrid_600x515.jpg)

[torrid.com](http://www.torrid.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For years fuller figured fashionistas have been shopping at this plus size chain. With its flair for design, Torrid caters to the edgy, fashion forward women who want to make a style statement regardless of shape or size. Although their pieces can run a little bit more on the expensive side the quality is top notch and will be a part of your wardrobe for years to come.

## 4. Dorothy Perkins

![Dorothy Perkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_dorothy-perkins_600x515.jpg)

[dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While this UK based site is not considered "plus size," it does offer larger range of sizes and women can get away with more from their generous cuts and designs. You can find pieces inspired from just about every decade, which makes Dorothy Perkins the perfect universal online shop no matter your taste or style.

## 5. Lane Bryant

![Lane Bryant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/5_lane-bryant_600x515.jpg)

[lanebryant.com](http://www.lanebryant.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With the launch of their e-commerce site in 2003, Lane Bryant is a leading plus size retailer that allows you to virtually "try on" your outfit, an important tool for finding the right fit and shape for curvier figures. Additionally the site also recently launched Fashion Genius, a virtual stylist that selects pieces for you that will compliment and flatter your shape. With stylishly smart designs, Lane Bryant offers classic silhouettes for the best basics needed in your wardrobe.

## 6. Sealed with a Kiss Designs

_ swakdesigns.com_
While the selection isn’t as large as the previous mentions, SWAK does boast free shipping worldwide, perfect for curvy girls around the world! Their styles blend a mix of style and sophistication with comfort and practicality to fit into the everyday lives of plus sized women. Another appeal is SWAK’s high quality items at affordable prices. This plus their free shipping worldwide offer makes SWAK a leading retailer for contemporary plus size fashion.

## 7. City Chic

_ [citychiconline.com](http://redirect.viglink.com/?key=a7330d1bba7bc7d258ecc1463a1b25b1&u=http://www.citychiconline.com/)
Also offering free shipping, City Chic provides a range of styles for the woman who is bold, sexy and glam and isn’t afraid to show it no matter her size. Founded in Australia, City Chic has opened its doors online across North America and has plans to open locations in the US and Canada. City Chic caters to all lifestyles and pushes the boundaries when it comes to what people think plus size shoppers should wear.

With a ride range of options available today, some of the best plus size shopping can be found online. What is your one stop shop for plus sized fashion? Do you have any other recommendations for plus size stores?
