# 7 Ways to Make Slip Dresses Winter Appropriate ...

Knowing how to wear slip dresses in winter is way trickier than during summer. A simple slip-style dress can be an easy, breezy outfit solution in the heat of the summer months but all too revealing (not to mention cold) in the winter months. If you’re fond of this underwear as outerwear look, there are still ways to make it winter appropriate. Check out the following tips on how to wear a slip dress in winter.

## 1. Layers

The most obvious tip for how to wear slip dresses in winter? Pile on the layers! Make the slip an accent rather than the focal point of your outfit. If it has lace or equally decorative trims, let them peek out from underneath a chunky cardigan, thick scarves, and cosy jackets.

## 2. Pullovers

In a similar vein to the previous tip, slip on a cosy pullover and let your slip dress peek out underneath. Oversized styles are on-trend right now and are perfect for working a casual, off-duty look. Depending on how cold your winters actually get, slip on some opaque tights underneath and finish the look off with stylish boots and a fashionable beanie.

## 3. Maxi Lengths

Another tip for how to wear slip dresses in winter is to go for a longer length dress. Most slip dresses are usually quite short, but winter is the perfect time to play with longer hemlines. Best of all? You can get away with wearing tights or thermals underneath and no one will be the wiser!

## 4. Leg-wear

When styling your dresses for winter, tights and knee high socks are a great layering option. A pair of opaque tights is a fool-proof option. Alternatively, work a girlish look by popping on a pair of knee-high cable knit socks with your slip dresses. Don’t forget a cosy cardigan over the top, and you’re good to go.

## 5. Thicker Fabrics

When you think of slip dresses, you probably think of slinky, silky fabrics. When looking at how to wear slip dresses in winter, try a one in a thicker knit fabric. It’ll be much more winter appropriate and less obvious than a silky slip dress.

## 6. Floor Length Outerwear

Floor skimming coats and cardigans have been a popular fixture on the designer runways, and they work well with the minimalist design of most slip dresses. It’s a balancing act of hemlines, but can look extremely fashionable when worn well with a basic slip dress.

## 7. Seasonal Colour Palettes

Nude coloured slip dresses can stick out like a sore thumb in the winter time, so try a darker colour palette instead. When it comes to how to wear a slip dress in winter, black, navy, and burgundy are all great colour options.

Depending on exactly how cold your winters get, you could get away with wearing your summer slip dresses during the colder months. When it comes to how to wear slip dresses for winter, it’s all about piling on the layers. Of course, if there’s sleet and snow then slip dresses are probably a no-go. Are you a fan of the slip dress? Do you tips for how to wear slip dresses in winter?
