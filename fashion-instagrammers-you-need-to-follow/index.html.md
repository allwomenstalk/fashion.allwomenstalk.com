# 12 Fashion Instagrammers You Need to Follow to Look Your Best Always ...

Remember the good old days when you needed to be subscribed to about three different major magazines in order to stay up to date with all of the latest fashions ins and outs? Well, the fashion landscape couldn’t look more different today! You can’t go two minutes on the internet without scrolling upon some sort of style guru, and places like Instagram have become a haven for girls who like to get their inspiration and advice in bitesize form. Here are 12 of the best **fashion Instagrammers** that you need to follow!

## 1. Alexa Chung

Alexa Chung certainly isn’t a fashion newcomer at this point, but what she does manage to do is keep her account and her style as up to date as anyone could imagine! She is clearly committed to fashion, and you can get great glimpses and inspiration from her posts.

**https://www.instagram.com/alexachung/**

## 2. Chiara Ferragni

Chiara was ranked the number one fashion influencer in Forbes in 2017, so you know that her account is going to be amazing! She has a whopping 15.4 million followers! Her partnerships with brands like Tods and Pantene are great inspiration.

[@chiaraferragni](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 3. Caroline Daur

Caroline has worked with brands like Fendi, Cartier, Dior, and Valentino, so you know that her posts are going to be dripping in fashion every single time. She has one of the most amazing overcoat collections I’ve ever seen!

[@carodaur](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 4. Danielle Bernstein

Danielle started a fashion blog in 2010 and today she makes millions of dollars per year! She has appeared in the Forbes 30 Under 30 list, and shares her fashion tips with nearly two million Insta followers.

[@weworewhat](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 5. Frédérique Harrel

This incredible London-based fashion blogger was Cosmo's Influencer of the Year in 2018. She's a mum and also the founder of She Unleashes. You'll lust after her street style and wonderful afro.

[@freddieharrel](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 6. Janni Olsson Deler

Janni mixes travel and fashion on her Instagram, enough to make anyone in the world jealous, but she has such a winning personality that you root for her rather than against her! Her 1.5 million followers love to see her adventures.

[@jannid](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 7. Miroslava Duma

If you are looking for inspiration in the realm of formal fashion, then Miroslava has you covered. She has a really professional style, and has blogged for numerous outlets from Vogue to Glamour to Forbes.

[@miraduma](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 8. Andy Torres

Andy is based in Amsterdam and is something of an Instagram rock star. She has nearly a million followers and shares her cutting edge, quirky fashion sense for everyone to see. She has even started her own fashion line.

[@stylescrapbook](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 9. Eva Chen

Eva was actually Instagram’s first ‘head of fashion’. She began her career as an intern at Harper’s Bazaar and moved to Insta from there to be in charge of all fashion related partnerships. Her account is a shining example of what fashion Instagram should be like!

[@evachen212](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 10. Natalie Live

A true advocate of minimalism, Natalie runs The Tiny Closet. She'll inspire you with her effortless streetwear and even give you tips on wear to buy the items to create a look of your own. If you want to adapt to different styles and how to wear the simplest clothing items with grace follow Natalie.

[@thetinycloset](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 10. Gabrielle Gregg

Gabrielle is one the most stunning and most followed plus size fashionistas on Instagram. She is a leading voice and figure in the plus size community, and her account is only set to continue growing!

[@gabifresh](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 11. Irene Lara

Another travel/fashion crossbreed, Irene’s account is the kind of account that can help you to indulge in a little escapism whilst at the same time giving you lots of style inspiration!

[@studio4lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
