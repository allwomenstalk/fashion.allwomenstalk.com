# 7 Affordable Alternatives to Designer Workout Gear ...

If working out is a part of your daily routine, why not look fabulous while you do it? But between gym membership, equipment, and workout plans, who has money lying around for designer workout gear? Shop at these places for amazing styles at affordable prices.

## 1. Fabletics

![Fabletics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/od/km97z1jd5747250e67fed295405688_577x866.jpg)

You've probably seen the ads on Facebook and Instagram by now for Kate Hudson's Fabletics. Have you given into their advertising tactics yet? If not, it’s time to! Fabletics has great quality, stylish workout gear, all at prices that won't leave you with an inkling of buyer's remorse.
**Shop this look at [fabletics.com](http://www.fabletics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com): Entire outfit: $29.97**

## 2. Victoria Secret

_ Victoria Secret can be your one-stop-shop for high-quality sports bras as well as cute tops and bottoms for all of your fitness needs.
**Shop this look at victoriassecret.com_: Sports Bra: $20.00**

## 3. MPG

![MPG](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/w9/ew6u0jtr5753dca32e5aa220545475_600x600.jpg)

MPG Sport sells all of the basics you need. Plus, they are super flattering for every body type and not too pricey!
**Shop this look at [mpgsport.com](http://mpgsport.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com): 1/4 Zip Pullover: $49.00**

## 4. Cory Vines

![Cory Vines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ut/e6/gz45t80j574727bd7e4df735237896_600x900.jpg)

Love these leggings? Cory Vines has lots of spunky leggings as well as more traditional ones-all for under $60.00!
**Shop this look at [usa.coryvines.com](https://goo.gl/CdlIlz): Leggings: $49.00**

## 5. Lorna Jane

![Lorna Jane](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zu/to/soy9qdhd5747288786ea6124776871_500x467.jpg)

From socks to headbands, and everything in between, Lorna Jane has you covered! Shop their sale section to find leggings for under $55.00 and cute tops like this one for $40.00 or less!
**Shop this look at [lornajane.com](http://www.lornajane.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com): Fit Chic Tank: $25.00**

## 6. Target

![Target](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/qm/zocg96i05747246cb87e8571238805_480x480.jpg)

I can't forget Target when talking about affordable workout gear! Their quality isn't always on par with designer brands, but their low prices mean you can stock up on workout gear all at once and be set for the year!
**Shop this look at [target.com](https://www.target.com/c/activewear-women-s-clothing/-/N-5xtcl): Tank top: $24.99**

## 7. Calia

![Calia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ca/zx/wjb8mhqf5747242c38dbd549000007_600x600.jpg)

Like Kate Hudson, Carrie Underwood has an affordable workout clothing company, too. Support your favorite country singer and fill your closet with stylish activewear all at once!
**Shop this look at [caliastudio.com](https://www.caliastudio.com/family/index.jsp?categoryId=89275896): Leggings: $38.97**

Looking good while you exercise doesn't have to mean spending hundreds of dollars on each outfit. Shopping at any of these stores will save you lots of money over time- and you'll still slay at the gym! Where else do you shop for fashionable activewear?
