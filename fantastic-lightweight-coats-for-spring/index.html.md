# 9 Fantastic Lightweight Coats for Spring ...

**Coats For Spring** can be a tricky look to pull off. The temperature is warming up but there are still plenty of cool days here and there. You want a coat for spring that will protect you from the elements but is still lightweight enough to carry around when you’re not wearing it. Light coats for spring are your solution. Take a look at the following stylish coats for spring dressing.

## 1. Burberry Brit Satin-Twill Trench Coat

![Burberry Brit Satin-Twill Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_burberry-brit-satin-twill-trench-coat_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/WXmIRb)
This lightweight coat comes in a fantastic kingfisher blue colour. It’s made from satin-twill and has a classic trench design. As an added bonus, this trench is also shower proof making it ideal for wearing on slightly overcast days.

## 2. Dorothy Perkins Black Lightweight Parka

![Dorothy Perkins Black Lightweight Parka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_dorothy-perkins-black-lightweight-parka_369x500.jpg)

**Price:** $79.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Every wardrobe needs a black coat, and this lightweight number is great casual option. It features a hood, gold trims, and a draw cord at the waist. Wear this coat with anything from jeans and a tee to a printed shift dress. It’s one of the more versatile lightweight **coats for spring**.

## 3. Acne Francis Coat

![Acne Francis Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_acne-francis-coat_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1100.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This oversized coat is ideal for creating a casual, off-duty look. It has a detachable hood, concealed front placket, and leather drawstring details. Now, this coat is very pricey but the look can be easily achieved on a much smaller budget. Keep an eye out for similar styles in chain and department stores.

## 4. Jil Sander Double-Breasted Coat

![Jil Sander Double-Breasted Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_jil-sander-double-breasted-coat_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $2060.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bZBxhN)
This coat is a dream. Pity that the price tag means that this **coat for spring** will only ever exist in my dreams! It has a clean, tailored design and comes in a lovely salmon colour. The lightweight fabric makes it perfect for wearing in between seasons.

## 5. Topshop Maxi Trench

![Topshop Maxi Trench](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_topshop-maxi-trench_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/RYnbAO)
Create a dramatic silhouette in a maxi length coat. This particular coat has an exaggerated large collar and tie waist belt. Experiment with lengths and wear this coat open over a mini dress.

## 6. Ian R.N. Brushed Hooded Parka

![Ian R.N. Brushed Hooded Parka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_ian-r-n-brushed-hooded-parka_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $247.50 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Another casual looking lightweight **coat for spring**, this one will definitely earn you street cred. It features a hood, drawstring waist, and four front patch pockets. It has a relaxed fit and would look great paired with this season’s must-have patterned pants.

## 7. Thakoon Addition Drawstring Trench

**Price:** $725.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This short trench coat is the perfect weekend piece. It has a zip up front with button-over flap, split hem at the front, and gun flaps at the shoulders. Offset the boyish design by teaming this coat with pastel or floral pieces.

## 8. Jack by BB Dakota Coat

![Jack by BB Dakota Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/8_jack-by-bb-dakota-coat_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $82.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you want something that’s a bit lighter in colour as well as fabric when it comes to **coats for spring**, then try on this fresh white coat. It features an asymmetrical hem, sash belt, and a flattering full skirt. Wear this cinched at the waist over your favourite vintage dress.

## 9. Tulle Clothing Coat

**Price:** $31.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For something a bit cosier when it comes to **stylish coats for spring**, try out this fantastic lightweight number. It’s made from sweatshirt style fabric but still has a classic trench design. It has four front patch pockets, a hood, and tie waist belt.

Choosing **coats for spring** all comes down to personal style and taste. There are a range of stylish coats for spring available, from casual parkas to more tailored dress coats. What do you think of these coats for spring?
