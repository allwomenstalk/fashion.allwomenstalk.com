# Stores to Help Re\-Vamp Your Wardrobe when Youre on a Budget ...

Re-vamping your wardrobe can be a tough task when you’re on a budget. There are a ton of stores you could shop at, but they’re not always very budget-friendly. When you’re completely re-hauling your wardrobe, you want a place where you can find a lot of clothes for a very good price. If you’re having issues finding a ton of stores that offer clothes for the girl on the budget, this is the place for you!

## 1. Forever 21

It’s not necessarily a secret that Forever 21 is the place to look if you’re trying to revamp your wardrobe on a budget. You’d be surprised by just how much you can get for your money at Forever 21. In fact, depending on your budget, you may be able to buy your entire new wardrobe from Forever 21!

## 2. Target

If you’re like me and get sucked into the aisles of Target fairly frequently, you might as well get your clothes at Target as well! One of my favorite parts about the Target clothing section is that although the clothes are changed out fairly often, providing a lot of variety, they also have sections dedicated to basics that they update frequently as well!

## 3. Old Navy

Old Navy is known for its crazy and amazing deals, so of course, this store is on the list of stores you must shop at when you’re revamping your wardrobe on a budget. There aren’t many stores where you can find a gorgeous pair of pants for around $10. Plus, doesn’t it seem like every mall in America has an Old Navy? They’re everywhere! Find one and get shopping!

## 4. Tobi

You may not have heard of Tobi yet. It’s not a brick-and-mortar store, but rather one that’s online and offers a ton of different styles! It’s one of my personal favorite online stores, especially if you’re looking to save money, because they offer 50% off of your first order and have different sales and promotions all of the time! If you’re looking for trendy clothes and good deals, Tobi is the first place to look!

## 5. ASOS

A lot of people tend to stay away from ASOS when you’re on a budget because it can be a little pricey. However, you’d be surprised by just how great some of the deals you can find on the website are. Search the sale section, or wait for good deals and discounts. If you’re a student, you could even get a student discount for 20% off!

## 6. Francesca’s

No matter what your style is, Francesca’s has something in their stores that will fit your personal style. While it’s a store that’s not nearly as cost-effective as a store like Forever 21, the quality speaks for itself, and they usually have a pretty large sale section where I’m sure you’ll find something that’s in your price range!

## 7. TJ Maxx

TJ Maxx is known as the store to find the best deals on everything; however what I really love to look for is the clothing selection. There’s always something different or new in store that you can’t find anywhere else. If you’re looking to find something that no one else has, TJ Maxx is the place to look!

Where do you shop when you’re looking to revamp your wardrobe on a budget? Give me some ideas and tips in the comments!
