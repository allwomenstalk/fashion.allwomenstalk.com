# 10 Pushing Daisies Inspired Dresses for Summer ...

With the warm weather upon us, the time is prime to pick a fresh batch of summer dresses. And if you were a fan of the short-lived "Pushing Daisies", June also marks the third anniversary of the last month ABC aired the quick-witted, fashion-forward show. So, if you've been waiting to step out in Chuck style, here are 10 dresses inspired by everyone’s favorite back-from-the-dead crime fighter!

## 1. Daisy Day in Dress

![Daisy Day in Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_daisy-day-in-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $87.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Charlotte «Chuck» Charles has some quirky summer dresses to go with her spirited personality. You’ll shine under the sun with this daisy patterned dress. The bow front just makes it all the more Chuck-like. I’d pair it with my cutest kitten heels and a clutch purse for an afternoon out.

## 2. What a Way to Bow Dress

![What a Way to Bow Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_what-a-way-to-bow-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $52.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One touch of this dress and you’ll fall in love! The sexy red shade is perfect for a night on the town. Even if you’re not fighting crime in your T-strap heels, you can still look ready to stop a few bad guys a la Chuck.

## 3. Sailor Shift Dress

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
Hello, sailor! The coral hue and sweet sailor collar is so Chuck. I’d don this one to a party on deck. It’s simply perfect to stow in your suitcase for your next cruise.

## 4. Floral Button Front Dress

![Floral Button Front Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_floral-button-front-dress_350x475.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This subdued summer dress features an artsy brushed floral print. And if you haven’t noticed already, Chuck can usually be spotted rocking a few flowers. I’d wear this with my favorite pair of bow flats and classic pearl earrings for a dinner party outdoors.

## 5. Polka Dot Shirtdress W/ Belt

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
Who knew vintage-inspired polka dots would make such an airy, effortless frock? So, pair it with white sandals, an oversized tote, and your usual sunnies. Or, use it as a cover-up as you’re strolling along the boardwalk.

## 6. Kain Cam Silk-blend Wrap-effect Dress

![Kain Cam Silk-blend Wrap-effect Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_kain-cam-silk-blend-wrap-effect-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $265.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/OmQlLY)
Chuck may be head over heels for Ned, but the color yellow is a close second to her beloved pie-maker. You’ll channel the free-spirited and feminine leading lady in this marigold wrap dress. Dress it up with neutral pumps or go bold and bright with a multicolored purse.

## 7. Glamour Power to You Dress in Flower Box

![Glamour Power to You Dress in Flower Box](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_glamour-power-to-you-dress-in-flower-box_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $74.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You’ll be red hot in this summer dress designed by Trollied Dolly, a pair of British sisters. Elegant and airy, you’ll be a style hit just like Chuck. I’d rock this with open toe wedges and a white crossbody bag for a look that’s feminine and flirty.

## 8. Pink Flared Dress

![Pink Flared Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_pink-flared-dress_350x475.jpg)

**Price:** $89.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You’ll be pretty in pink with a swingy frock Chuck would adore! The flared construction makes this little number the go-to dress for your next garden party. Or, pair it with contrasting heels for a look to remember.

## 9. Red Valentino Ruffled Stretch-Crepe Dress

![Red Valentino Ruffled Stretch-Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/9_red-valentino-ruffled-stretch-crepe-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/nSi57i)
The perfect combination of sexy and sweet, the spicy red color gets girly with the addition of ruffles and a bow. Wear it to your next big night out with your Ned.

## 10. Notched Lapel Shirtdress W/ Belt

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
This chic belted dress will keep your looking put together like Chuck. Pair it with your cutest flats and slip into it before heading to the office. Lightweight and bright, the cut and fit are subdued enough to impress your boss.

Though after two short seasons,"Pushing Daisies" came to a close, Chuck’s style will continue to live on in fresh summer dresses. Do you love Chuck’s style? Which dress is your favorite?
