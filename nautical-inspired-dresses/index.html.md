# 7 Nautical Inspired Dresses ...

**Nautical Inspired Dresses **are just too cute for words, and they're currently an amazingly popular trend right now -- especially if you're looking for a bit of a preppy look. They can be both timeless and fashionable. Think stripes and a colour combination of navy, red and white. If you’re not into wearing separates, nautical inspired dresses are an effortless way of wearing the entire look. Take a look at a few **nautical inspired dresses** below.

## 1. Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Sailor Dress

![Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Sailor Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/kt/wghefyh054c92c41247c4995481767_200x300.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/2ruDnZ)
This cute sailor dress definitely screams of nautical inspiration. The sailor collar and bow trim are the most notable nautical inspired features. Others include the blue and white colour combination. The dress features turned-up half sleeves, side pockets, and slightly gathered hem. Wear this nautical inspired dress alone, as is, or pair with a cream coloured boyfriend blazer.

## 2. Aubin & Wills Ledsham Striped Cotton Dress

![Aubin & Wills Ledsham Striped Cotton Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/vb/rkv1zn5z54c92c41caf7b796649130_200x300.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/nXuWhg)
This striped cotton dress is smart and sophisticated. The nautical inspiration comes from the thin horizontal stripes. This dress features a round neck, rolled sleeves, and side pockets. The elasticised waist gives the dress extra shape. The light grey colour makes this a stylish and casual option for everyday dressing.

## 3. Farhi by Nicole Farhi Cotton Dress

![Farhi by Nicole Farhi Cotton Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/lb/g4mmhbkt54c92c42ae170629108987_225x269.jpg)

**Price:** $251.00 at [coggles.com](http://www.coggles.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is one of the more subtle nautical inspired dresses out there. The dark navy colour is timeless. The addition of a sailor collar, however, is an interesting design detail. This dress a curved fixed waist, gathering at the back, and exposed zip at the side. Wear this with lighter accents and ballet flats for a sophisticated look.

## 4. Polka Dot Dress by Wal G

![Polka Dot Dress by Wal G](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r6/ru/l6qf83f654c92c4363210305449016_200x298.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ksr3FK)
This dress has a very retro feel about it. The navy and white colour combination is quite nautical, and the polka dot design is a playful touch. The contrast trim collar is reminiscent of a sailor style design. Wear this dress with some t-bar shoes and top the entire look off with a dash of red lipstick.

## 5. Theory Alesandra Striped Silk Dress

![Theory Alesandra Striped Silk Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/h8/rtixkozr54c92c442dd78303784696_200x300.jpg)

**Price:** $265.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/OLP0NZ)
If you’re after a relaxed style of nautical inspired dresses, try this one out. It’s made from silk and would make a chic addition to any wardrobe. It features a scoop neck, pouch pockets, and racer back design. Pair this with some leather sandals and colourful beaded accessories for a fresh look.

## 6. Wren Alexa Striped Dress

_ **Price:** $189.00 at shoplesnouvelles.com_
Nautical dresses can become a bit same-same after a while, but this one manages to spice things up a bit. Each shoulder is adorned with a crystal brooch, which adds a bit of sparkle to this nautical look. This dress, which was inspired by trendsetter Alexa Chung, also features a scoop neck, gathered waist, and pleating detail at the back. Channel the dress’s namesake and team it with a pair of black patent loafers.

## 7. Thread Social Striped Knitted Mini Dress

**Price:** $105.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This nautical inspired dress has three quarter length sleeves, a contrasting banded hem, and large bow detailing at the shoulder. The navy and white striped design is very nautical-chic. This knitted dress is a loosely fitted style, so be wary of any possible ‘lumps and bumps’. Pair it with tan accessories and maybe throw on a leather jacket on over the top to add a bit of edge.

The nautical trend is something that is always in style, so there are a lot of **nautical inspired dresses** available this season. Even if you can't make it to the ocean or don't have a yacht, you can still feel like you're headed out to see if you've got some nautical inspired dresses! You can incorporate this trend in varying degrees. Which of the above **nautical inspired dresses** do you like most?
