# 7 Chunky Heeled Shoes for Spring ...

Put your best foot forward this spring. Invest in a pair of chunky heels to take you through the warmer months. There are a range of styles available. Keep it natural with leather and wood, or go for something more interesting like perspex. Below are just a few to ponder over.

## 1. Chloe Sevigny for Opening Ceremony Mary-Janes

_ **Price:** $395.00 at openingceremony.us_
This one’s for the brave. Or extremely talented heel wearers. The height of this shoe is mind boggling. However, the overall aesthetic is one of sheer delight. The light tan leather is perfect paired with the light wood grain. I love the added bow detail on the toe of the shoe!

## 2. See by Chloe Suede Lace-up Platform Sandals

![See by Chloe Suede Lace-up Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m9/xo/d5ad1ekf54c8f2eaa8939246265583_300x250.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/dnegeX)
This sandal will freshen up any outfit. The blush suede is such a lovely material. I adore the lattice detail on the body of the shoe. This shoe manages to be edgy and sweet at the same time. Pair it with a printed skirt and blazer combination.

## 3. Rachel Comey Dekalb Clog Sandal

![Rachel Comey Dekalb Clog Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/g9/zw8vcwr554c8f2eba26b9812936554_300x215.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The clog trend from last spring is showing no signs of stopping. This design from Rachel Comey is a refined take on this trend. I love the combination of white leather and dark wooden heel. Wear these shoes with crisp denim and neutral colours.

## 4. Asos Party on Peep Toe Perspex Heel Shoe

![Asos Party on Peep Toe Perspex Heel Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m4/zy/u4eyvzxh54c8f2ec7c9a7405744451_300x338.jpg)

**Price:** $116.55 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/47WWQD)
This shoe is perfect for a night out on the town! I love the colour fading down the perspex heel. It’s such an interesting design feature. The taupe suede and light orange colour combination make this the perfect spring shoe.

## 5. Topshop Lark Dusky Pink Knotted Sandal

![Topshop Lark Dusky Pink Knotted Sandal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/d6/hu15zhvy54c8f2ed57e04530558920_300x332.jpg)

**Price:** $145.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/mHGTO1)
This coral coloured sandal is sure to be a big seller for high street store Topshop. I love the chunky heel combined with a petite ankle strap. The knotted detail is also an interesting design detail. These shoes will definitely have you putting your best foot forward!

## 6. Reed Krakoff Leather Platform Sandals

![Reed Krakoff Leather Platform Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/bv/w7iw2mjd54c8f2ee2478b661345439_300x292.jpg)

**Price:** $550.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jQOyIB)
I love the combination of tan leather and wood. This shoe combines this love in one sleek, fashionably designed package. The wide strap with contrasting white buckle looks sturdy and comfortable. These 70s inspired platform sandals would look great paired with a floaty sun dress or dressed up with more tailored clothes.

## 7. Aldo Krugh T-Strap Sandals

![Aldo Krugh T-Strap Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/pk/rqeplbr054c8f2ef134f5526176815_300x253.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [aldoshoes.com](http://www.aldoshoes.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is one stylish shoe. The T-strap design is on-trend for spring. My favourite part of this shoe has to be the crochet detail. The light brown colour of the shoe and wooden heel complements the cream crochet perfectly.
