# 7 Prom Dress Styles for 2013 ...

Prom is coming up quick for many teens and that means new prom dress styles for 2013! Prom dresses are a representation of you are and what your personality is all about. You have to decide between short or long, green or pink, and straps or no straps! Well I’m here to help! If you’re a teen who has the big dance this year, you definitely need to check out some of these prom dress styles for 2013!

## 1. Long and Slinky

![Long and Slinky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/1_long-and-lengthy_600x850.jpg)

Dresses don’t have to be short to be sexy! I absolutely love this option for prom dress styles for 2013 because it is a classic. You’ll look simply majestic with your dress spinning as you dance with your perfect date! And even if you don’t win prom queen, you’ll undoubtedly be the queen of the dance floor!

## 2. Short and Sassy

If you are a girl who is a little on the short side, you might want to find a dress with the same quality! Getting a short dress paired with a fabulous heel is a great way to add height without getting too swallowed up by your dress. Plus they are much easier to dance in!

## 3. Princess Ball Gown

![Princess Ball Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/3_princess-ball-gown_600x900.jpg)

Prom should be your day to feel like a princess, so why not dress like one too? Ball gowns are a perfect way to be extravagant while not being too over the top. You could have a skirt of ruffles, tulle, or even feathers! And don’t forget the tiara to top off your princess look!

## 4. High/Low

![High/Low](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/4_high-low_600x1004.jpg)

This new prom dress style is all the rage recently and I’m not surprised! It combines two amazing styles into one fab, new craze! This style is perfect for any height and build and shows the perfect amount of leg. It also solves the issue of not being able to see your fantastic heels! Wear your favorite pair and match them with your favorite necklace. It’ll be perfect!

## 5. Ruffles and Riches

![Ruffles and Riches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/5_ruffles-and-riches_600x800.jpg)

Why do I love ruffled prom dresses? Because they are a load of fun! Not only do they bounce around when you dance but they are such an attention grabber! How could you not stare at them? They are beautiful!

## 6. Neon

![Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/6_neon_420x604.jpg)

Looking to make a fashion statement? Why not go neon for a night? You will definitely shine like a star with this unique style! Pair it with some strappy silver heels and no one will be able to keep their eyes off of you!

## 7. Duck Tape

![Duck Tape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/7_duck-tape_299x447.jpg)

You think I’m joking, right? Well I’m not! This unique prom dress style is getting more and more popular every year and I’m starting to see why! There are so many cool designs for duck tape now, just think of how great they would look as a dress! If you’re crafty and are good with a pair of scissors, you should try this out! Let me know how it turns out!

Prom is such a special time for teens and finding the perfect dress is vital! Remember to find something that fits you and your personality perfectly and don’t be afraid to try a few different styles! I hope you loved these prom dress styles for 2013 as much as I did, and let me know if I missed any! Which prom dress style for 2013 is your favorite?
