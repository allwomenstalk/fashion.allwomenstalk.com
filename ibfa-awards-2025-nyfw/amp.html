IBFA Awards 2025 Celebrated Global Talent in Beauty and Fashion during New York Fashion Week

By Editor

New York, February 8, 2025 – A spectacular evening unfolded during New York Fashion Week as industry leaders, innovators, and rising stars gathered for the prestigious IBFA Awards. Held in an exclusive setting, the event celebrated outstanding achievements across fashion, beauty, and entrepreneurship, bringing together some of the most influential figures in the industry.

The ceremony and press conference welcomed distinguished guests including Hitoshi Sagaseki, Aliia Roza, Kristina Menissov, Katiely Kathissumi, and Rick Davy, alongside renowned designers, media representatives, and international experts.

The red ribbon-cutting ceremony symbolized a commitment to supporting talent worldwide, creating an atmosphere of inspiration and opportunity. The evening culminated in the highly anticipated IBFA Awards, recognizing individuals and organizations making a significant impact on the global fashion and beauty industries.

Anelya Alim – President of IBFA 

ANELYA Alim is the founder of IBFA , an association dedicated to supporting young talents in the beauty and fashion industry. She first developed this idea back in 2015 in Kazakhstan, where she founded a makeup school within her community. Over the years, she has taught more than 5,000 students in Paris, China, Ukraine, Russia, and Kazakhstan.

With her expertise and passion, she moved to the United States on a Talent Visa and EB-1, opening a makeup school in the heart of Manhattan. She later launched her own cosmetics line, Anelya Alim Cosmetics, and founded Anelya Alim Makeup School in New York. Additionally, she established the Anelya Alim School of Cosmetics in New York, further expanding her influence in the beauty industry.

Beyond business, Anelya dedicated two years as a volunteer at Fashion Week, gaining invaluable experience. Now, she is continuing her journey by creating an association that nurtures and supports emerging talents in beauty and fashion, ensuring they have the opportunities and resources to succeed.
