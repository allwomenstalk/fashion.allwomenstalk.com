# 7 Cute and Comfortable Pyjama Sets ...

Cute pajama sets are just one way to get your sleepwear sorted. Sometimes there’s no greater luxury than getting a good night’s sleep, so you should be resting in style. Adjust for comfort by choosing relaxed styles and pajamas made from comfortable fabrics. Take a look at a few cute PJ sets below.

## 1. Moonlit Owl PJ Set

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_
These knit pajamas feature a cute owl motif on the t-shirt and shorts. The t-shirt has a relaxed cut with a scooped neckline and short sleeves. The shorts have an elastic waistband with self tie feature. This set is definitely a contendor when it comes to cute pajama sets.

## 2. Diner in Your Dreams Sleep Set

![Diner in Your Dreams Sleep Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/2_diner-in-your-dreams-sleep-set_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $82.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These satiny pajamas would be a dream to wear. This set consists of a short-sleeved button up top and long pajama pants. A cute touch comes in the form of a heart-shaped pocket on the breast.

## 3. Sausage Dog Tee and Shorts

![Sausage Dog Tee and Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/3_sausage-dog-tee-and-shorts_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/2JPj7e)
Animal motifs seem to be a popular source of inspiration when it comes to designing cute pajama sets. These pajamas have a fairly basic design, consisting of a white t-shirt and grey marle shorts. The defining feature of these pajamas have to be the wrap around sausage dog print.

## 4. Life Motto PJ Set

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_
Pretty in pink? These pajamas will have you being just that! They’re made from a lightweight knit and come in a girly pink colour. They also feature an inspirational ‘life motto’ on the front. If pink’s not your colour, however, it’s also available in a soft mint shade.

## 5. Moustache Tee and Trouser Set

![Moustache Tee and Trouser Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_moustache-tee-and-trouser-set_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/VOGqqH)
This cute pajama set features a quirky moustache print on both the top and trousers. The top features a red striped pattern and ‘I heart moustache’ graphic. The trousers feature an all over moustache print with cuffs at the ankles. This is definitely a fun take on pajama dressing.

## 6. Beneath the Night Sky Sleepwear Set

![Beneath the Night Sky Sleepwear Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/6_beneath-the-night-sky-sleepwear-set_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This sleepwear set is just as sophisticated as it is cute. It consists of a cami and shorts combination and comes in a midnight blue colour. It also has lace and bow accents. Wear this around the house on the weekend with a luxe robe and slippers.

## 7. Scribbled Heart Motif PJ Set

_ **Price:** $14.80 at forever21.com_
Sometimes cute pajama sets are best kept fairly simple. This set consists of a plain scoop neck tee and pink pajama shorts. The shorts come in a fabulous pink colour and feature scribbled handwritten and heart motifs. Too cute!
