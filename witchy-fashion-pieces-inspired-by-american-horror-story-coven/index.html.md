# 7 Witchy Fashion Pieces Inspired by American Horror Story: Coven ...

If you’re like me, when you watch American Horror Story: Coven, you may find yourself wanting some witchy fashion pieces. I love each character’s unique style and did a little shopping to find clothes and accessories that match the distinct look of each witch. Here are my favorite witchy fashion pieces inspired by American Horror Story.

## 1. UNIF – Lore Hat

_ One of my favorite witchy fashion pieces is the Lore Hat by UNIF. Witches are known for their hats, after all! This hat is the perfect way to turn any of your existing ensembles into an outfit straight out of American Horror Story. Channel your inner Zoe Benson with the Lore Hat. You can order this witchy fashion accessory on unifclothing.com_.

## 2. Sisters of the Black Moon – Sudri Trouser

![Sisters of the Black Moon – Sudri Trouser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10205133_8634_600x900.jpg)

With a name like Sisters of the Black Moon, you know you’re getting something absolutely witchy! These pants will help you to look like everyone’s favorite witch turned voodoo girl, Queenie. On the show, Queenie is always wearing the cutest pants, which is where the inspiration for these trousers comes into play. The Sudri Trouser has a vegan leather harness that comes up the front of the pant and then turns into suspenders with adjustable straps in the back. You can purchase these trousers at [sistersoftheblackmoon.com](https://www.sistersoftheblackmoon.com/).

## 3. L’ecole Des Femmes – Pleated Belle De Jour Dress

![L’ecole Des Femmes – Pleated Belle De Jour Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10205133_9292_579x2000.jpg)

If Nan is your style inspiration on the show, then you will just adore this dress by L’ecole Des Femmes. Nan is hardly ever seen not wearing a dress on the show and loves sporting gowns that have a collar just like the Belle De Jour Dress. To purchase this Nan-inspired dress, visit [lecoledesfemmes.net](http://www.lecoledesfemmes.net/).

## 4. Chaser – Minx Faux Fur Moto Jacket

_ Madison Montgomery loves accessories, which is why for her I chose a jacket to represent her character. In the show, Madison has worn several fur pieces, so I decided this furry coat was the perfect piece for a Madison inspired outfit. Chaser Minx Faux Fur Moto Jacket will help you make a grand entrance – just like Madison! To add this coat to your closet, visit dollskill.com_.

## 5. Free People – Floral Embroidered Triangle Shawl

![Free People – Floral Embroidered Triangle Shawl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10205132_6622_356x475.jpg)

This list wouldn’t be complete without a shawl to represent Misty and her idol, the White Witch Stevie Nicks. How much does this shawl look like the one Stevie gave Misty in the show? If you want to dress like the “swamp witch” then this shawl is an absolute must! You can get this sheer chiffon shawl at [freepeople.com](http://www.freepeople.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. Bobeau – Ponte Peplum Top

![Bobeau – Ponte Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10205132_8896_231x506.jpg)

Is the Supreme more your style? If dressing like Fiona Goode is how you envision your witchy counterpart, than a black peplum top is an absolute must! This top by Bobeau is a basic black peplum top that will help you channel your inner Supreme. Simply pair this top with a pencil skirt and black pumps for an ensemble that is straight out of American Horror Story. You can get this top at [nordstrom.com](http://www.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 7. Forever 21 – Sheer Peter Pan Collared Shirt

![Forever 21 – Sheer Peter Pan Collared Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10205139_9412_600x731.jpg)

Cordelia Foxx wears less black than the other witches. In fact, she is more likely to wear lighter colors such as white, light blue, and tan. That’s why I thought this top from Forever 21 is absolutely perfect for a Cordelia inspired outfit. If you are looking for a witchy piece inspired by American Horror Story: Coven but don’t want to wear black, than this is the perfect option for you! To get this top, visit [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

These witchy fashion pieces will transform you into your favorite character from American Horror Story: Coven. All of these pieces look great alone and some of them make a beautiful statement when worn together. Where is your favorite place to shop when it comes to witchy fashion pieces?
