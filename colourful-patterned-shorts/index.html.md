# 8 Colourful Patterned Shorts ...

Patterned shorts are a fun and very weather appropriate way to add extra detail to an outfit this summer. A patterned short looks great with anything from a basic tank top to a more sophisticated button up shirt. You can choose a style of short to suit your body shape, and then pick a pattern accordingly. Get some outfit inspiration from the following stylish patterned shorts.

## 1. Confetti, Set, Go! Shorts

![Confetti, Set, Go! Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/1_confetti-set-go-shorts_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These patterned shorts are set to brighten up any outfit. They come in an eye-catching golden hue with a scattered white and pink geometric pattern. These shorts have a high waisted design and feature a front tie waist and hidden zip fastening at the back. Team these with a plain white tee for a cute summer look.

## 2. Aztec Print Running Shorts

![Aztec Print Running Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/2_aztec-print-running-shorts_600x443.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Cq2J6E)
‘Aztec’ has been a big buzz word this past season so get in on the trend with an inspired pattern. These shorts feature a zigzag ‘Aztec’ pattern in sunset hues. The style of these shorts is fairly relaxed so dress them up with a button-up shirt and patent shoes.

## 3. Striped Linen-Blend Shorts

_ **Price:** $15.80 at forever21.com_
These linen-blend shorts feature a fun striped pattern. They have a slightly tailored design and feature bright seasonal colours. Team these shorts with a slouchy top and brogues for a casual and relaxed look.

## 4. Ikat Print Shorts

_ **Price:** $17.80 at forever21.com_
These shorts feature a detailed ikat pattern. They have a fitted waist with button tab fastening and front pocket detailing. Choose one of the colours in the pattern to incorporate into your top half, either in the form of a shirt or accessories.

## 5. Tandem through Town Shorts

![Tandem through Town Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_tandem-through-town-shorts_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $32.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Floral prints are a favourite during the summer months, and these patterned shorts are a must have. They feature a multicoloured floral print and come in a loose, wide-leg design. Tuck in a light coloured top and slip on a pair of lace-up shoes for a day of adventuring!

## 6. Picnic Plaid Shorts

_ **Price:** $9.50 at forever21.com_
Plaid fabrics are trending right now, and these shorts feature a sweet pink and orange plaid pattern. They have a fairly fitted design with a button waist, zip fly, and front and back pockets. These shorts would look great worn with a chambray denim shirt and tan sandals.

## 7. Candy Hearts Short

_ **Price:** $9.99 at forever21.com_
These cute patterned shorts are covered in candy hearts. They have a high waisted design with a mock button placket, front pleats, and hidden side zippers. These shorts would look super sweet worn with a blush or neutral shirt and patent ballet flats.

## 8. Mata Traders Ikat Patterned Shorts

![Mata Traders Ikat Patterned Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/8_mata-traders-ikat-patterned-shorts_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Unlike the previous ikat patterned shorts on this list, these have a larger and more distinct pattern. The bright yellow of these shorts is balanced out by cooler cream and ecru hues. These shorts would look great worn with a white or yellow sleeveless top and strappy leather sandals.

If there’s ever a time to have fun with the shorts you’re wearing, it’s right now. A pair of fun, patterned shorts is a must for this season. What do you think of these stylish patterned shorts?
