# 7 Skirts for Fall ...

Skirts For Fall are key to your new season wardrobe. Fall colours tend to revolve around earthy browns, rich reds, and burnt oranges. However, when looking at skirts for fall, keep an eye out for jewel toned colours as well. Jewel toned colours are great for extending the colour blocking trend that was popular over the summer. The ideal skirts for fall can range from mini to maxi lengths – the choice is entirely up to you. Take a look at just some stylish skirts for fall below.

Maxi skirts are still a stylish choice. This Topshop maxi skirt comes in an on-trend maroon colour which is perfect for fall. It has a fitted waistband and comes with a side zip fastening. Wear it with a white tank top, faux fur vest, and lace-up boots for a contemporary boho-inspired look.

## 2. Topshop Waterfall Front Skirt

![Topshop Waterfall Front Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/rw/mo0zq6lu54c93098c84aa297580345_294x500.jpg)

This skirt has a fitted waistband, rear zip fastening, and waterfall pleats to the front. Wear this skirt with a chic striped top, cropped blazer, and ballet flats for a timeless look.

## 3. J.Crew Flair Wool Skirt

It also has button fastenings throughout the front and is fully lined. This skirt would look great paired with a long-sleeved burgundy knit and brown ankle boots.

## 4. Topshop Pleated Skater Skirt

## 5. Rachel Comey Pleaty Skirt

![Rachel Comey Pleaty Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/gy/xyqzrsdz54c9309bd888d035360722_310x500.jpg)

This skirt features a contrasting elastic waistband with shirred detailing and all around knife pleating. It sits at the hips and has a relaxed fit. Channel a preppy look and wear this with a crisp collared shirt, cardigan, and brogues.

## 6. J.Crew Stretch Cotton-Twill Skirt

![J.Crew Stretch Cotton-Twill Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/mw/z0hxpk0l54c9309cbfd89597322692_400x394.jpg)

This skirt features soft ruching from the waistband, side slit pockets, and a concealed zip fastening at the side. Team this skirt with a basic blouse, blazer, and tights. Complete the military-inspired theme by wearing a pair of combat-style boots.

## 7. Markus Lupfer Pleated Mini Skirt

![Markus Lupfer Pleated Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/xn/kmjy5eh454c9309dc5d80625804975_400x370.jpg)

When it comes to choosing skirts for fall, we really are spoilt for choice. From minis to midis to maxis, there’s a style to suit everyone. Skirts for fall look great in both the traditional autumnal colours as well as more on-trend brights. Play with texture and style your chiffon skirts with faux fur, or your leathers with silks.
