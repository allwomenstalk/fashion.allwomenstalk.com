# 7 Fashionable Takes on the Luxe Tee ...

Luxe tees are an on-trend take on the humble wardrobe staple. Right now the luxe tee is a must-have. Choose a tee in a bold, graphic print or one made from luxe fabrics like silk and lace. For a hit of glam, you can’t go past a luxe tee in a metallic finish. Take a look at a few fashionable luxe tees below.

## 1. Blue Scarf Print Tee

![Blue Scarf Print Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_blue-scarf-print-tee_348x400.jpg)

**Price:** $49.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Ever since debuting on the Celine runways, scarf print clothes have become this season’s must-have item. However, you don’t have to fork out a fortune to get in on this trend. This tee from Dorothy Perkins is bold and bright and would look casually on-trend paired with denim shorts this summer.

## 2. Floral Print Fishtail Tee

![Floral Print Fishtail Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_floral-print-fishtail-tee_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/f1hGFN)
Floral prints never really go out of fashion and they look great on this season’s luxe tees. This tee, while looking like it has an ordinary design from the front, has a dramatic v-shaped back and fishtail detail. Wear this tee with black cropped trousers and strappy heels for a dressy look.

## 3. Lace Front Tee

_ **Price:** $15.80 at forever21.com_
Luxe tees are especially stylish when they’re made from equally luxe fabrics. This tee has a lace front which gives the basic tee an updated twist. It comes in a rich mustard colour which would look great paired with denim bottoms.

## 4. Apricot Crinkle Tee

![Apricot Crinkle Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_apricot-crinkle-tee_342x400.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Metallic finishes just scream glamour, and a luxe tee is a great way to incorporate some glamour into your look. This crinkle effect tee comes in a stunning apricot gold colour and features rolled sleeves. Tuck it into a full leather skirt and pop on some ankle boots for a cool contemporary look.

## 5. Chain Metallic T-Shirt

![Chain Metallic T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_chain-metallic-t-shirt_307x400.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/cfDqbd)
Luxe tees can be set apart from ordinary tees simply by the fabric. This tee features all-over silver metallic chain detailing. It also has rolled sleeves that give it a bit of a sporty look. For a simple but stylish daytime look, team this tee with a pair of pastel jeans and neutral sandals.

## 6. Aubin & Wills Broderie Anglaise T-Shirt

![Aubin & Wills Broderie Anglaise T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_aubin-wills-broderie-anglaise-t-shirt_283x400.jpg)

**Price:** $85.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/12Bprw)
If you want your luxe tee to have a little bit more structure, this broderie anglaise-detailed tee is an excellent example. The black colour is quite versatile and would be a welcome addition to any wardrobe. For something quirky, wear a coloured slip underneath and let it peek out from the cutout details on the shirt.

## 7. Bird and Chain Print Tee

![Bird and Chain Print Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_bird-and-chain-print-tee_297x400.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This luxe tee features a sweet bird and chain print. The soft colours are perfect for making the most of the pastel trend. Wear it tucked into a light denim skirt and with coloured sandals for a breezy summertime look.

A luxe tee is a simple and effective way of sprucing up a basic outfit. Make a statement in a bold print or simply choose one from a luxurious fabric. What are your thoughts on these luxe tees?
