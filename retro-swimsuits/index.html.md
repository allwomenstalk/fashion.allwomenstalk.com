# 8 Retro Swimsuits ...

**Retro swimsuits** are becoming increasingly popular on the beach. While beach weather is still a long way off for those in the northern hemisphere, it still leaves plenty of time to search (and save) for summer beachwear inspiration. For those in the southern hemisphere, a retro swimsuit is perfect for enjoying the last rays of summer in style. Take a look at a few stylish retro inspired swimsuits below.

## 1. Topshop Stripe Retro Swimsuit

![Topshop Stripe Retro Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_topshop-stripe-retro-swimsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/m7VOOy)
A high waisted bikini brief is one of the clearest indicators of a **retro inspired swimsuit** design. This swimsuit from Topshop comes in a navy and white colourway and features a bold striped pattern. The stripes are arranged in a way which creates quite an eye-catching chevron pattern.

## 2. Topshop Red Textured Bikini

![Topshop Red Textured Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_topshop-red-textured-bikini_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/G4MMY2)
This red **retro swimsuit** is all about creating a va-va-voom factor. The material has a micro scallop texture, which creates quite an interesting finish. The bikini has a tie detail at the bust, as well as a self-tie halterneck strap. Complete the retro look by wearing a wide-brimmed floppy hat and cat eye sunglasses.

## 3. Norma Kamali Ruched Retro Swimsuit

![Norma Kamali Ruched Retro Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_norma-kamali-ruched-retro-swimsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1H6Vqt)
If you’re after one piece **retro swimsuits**, then take inspiration from this ruched number. It comes in a colourful jungle green, and has a sweetheart bust and halterneck strap. The overall design is quite flattering. The price is a bit steep, however, and if I was spending that much on a swimsuit I’d hardly dare introduce it to chlorine or salt water!

## 4. Topshop Dalmation Print Bikini

![Topshop Dalmation Print Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_topshop-dalmation-print-bikini_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/i8uOZd)
This bikini has a fun, playful design. It features a black and white Dalmatian print, with contrasting trims and a bow detail at the bust. The high waisted bikini brief is all about creating the effect of curvy silhouette, like that of a retro pin-up girl. Complete the pool-side look with a pair of oversized dark sunglasses.

## 5. Esther Williams Polka Dot Retro Swimsuit

![Esther Williams Polka Dot Retro Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_esther-williams-polka-dot-retro-swimsuit_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Polka dots are a timeless classic, and they look super cute when incorporated into a swimsuit design. This swimsuit has a typically retro design and comes in a striking red and colour. It has features ruching at the bust, a halterneck strap, and low-cut legs. This is definitely a swimsuit that will help show off your retro side.

## 6. Esther Williams Two Piece Swimsuit

![Esther Williams Two Piece Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_esther-williams-two-piece-swimsuit_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This swimsuit looks like it came from another era! It has a high waisted bikini bottom, thick halterneck straps, and comes in a retro gingham pattern. The bikini brief is ruched and slightly skirted, making it ideal for those who are a bit body conscious.

## 7. Miu Miu Retro Inspired Swimsuit

![Miu Miu Retro Inspired Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/7_miu-miu-retro-inspired-swimsuit_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $245.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/0a29ux)
This swimsuit is a bit more of a modern take on **retro swimsuits**. It features a more revealing cut than the others on this list, but is still probably quite conservative compared to modern standards. It has a feminine design with underwire cups, mid-rise briefs, scalloped trims, and a contrasting bow detail at the bust.

## 8. Bettie Page One Piece Swimsuit

![Bettie Page One Piece Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/8_bettie-page-one-piece-swimsuit_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This **retro swimsuit** has a quirky design. It comes in a bright yellow colour and features a white honeycomb pattern and bee print details throughout. It also has underwire cups and halterneck straps. Add a pair of white sunglasses and navy headscarf to really work the retro look.

There are certain themes and trends when it comes to **retro swimsuits**. Red, navy and black colours are popular, as are polka dot, gingham, and stripe patterns. What do you think of these retro inspired swimsuits?
