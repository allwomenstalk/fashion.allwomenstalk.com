# 8 New Takes on Animal Print Clothes ...

Animal print clothing has always been popular. Some might even call it a classic. Leopard print, zebra stripes and even snake skin – I’m sure we’re all familiar with these kinds of prints. However, this list will be exploring different kinds of animal print clothes. Instead of close ups of animal skin prints, right now it’s all about the whole animal. Cats, birds, horses… you name it, there’s probably a piece of animal printed clothing for it.

## 1. Mulberry Effie Printed Jersey T-Shirt

![Mulberry Effie Printed Jersey T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_mulberry-effie-printed-jersey-t-shirt_350x403.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/lUPOFz)

Pretend you’re on safari with this piece of animal print clothing. This basic jersey t-shirt features an array of wild animal figures from giraffes to zebras. Tuck it into a black skirt and team with flat sandals for a cool off-duty look.

## 2. Feline Fabulous Dress

![Feline Fabulous Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_feline-fabulous-dress_248x500.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Love cats? Then channel your inner cat lady and slip on this quirky cat print dress. It has a strapless sweetheart design with button details at the front and a full skirt. Work a polished look by teaming this dress with a cropped black blazer and patent shoes.

## 3. Horseplay for Keeps Top

![Horseplay for Keeps Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_horseplay-for-keeps-top_260x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Here, the classic button up blouse is given an animal inspired twist. It comes in a neutral cream colour and features horizontal rows of horse silhouettes. This piece of animal print clothing would no doubt make an interesting addition to an outfit. Try teaming it with a pair of cropped trousers and ballet flats.

## 4. Bird Print Tie Playsuit

![Bird Print Tie Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_bird-print-tie-playsuit_350x655.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Aw2OoV)

Playsuits are a fun option for summer dressing and this one features an all-over bird print design. Bird prints were a hot trend on the spring-summer designer runways so it’s definitely a trend worth trying out this season. Team this playsuit with flat sandals and a slouchy side bag for a casual weekend look.

## 5. Ivory Cat Print Blouse

![Ivory Cat Print Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_ivory-cat-print-blouse_350x726.jpg)

**Price:** $49.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This blouse features an all over cartoon-style cat print. It has a sleeveless design with a front patch pocket and dipped hem. Work a feminine look and tuck it into a full pleated skirt and pop on a pair of Mary Jane style shoes.

## 6. Paule & Joe Sister Elephant Print Shirt

![Paule & Joe Sister Elephant Print Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_paule-joe-sister-elephant-print-shirt_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $320.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/g6PnlG)

From afar the print on this shirt might seem a bit insignificant, but closer inspection reveals a quirky elephant print. It’s definitely a fun take on animal print clothing. This shirt would look great simply worn with skinny jeans and strappy sandals.

## 7. Owls Crop Pullover

_ **Price:** $11.99 at forever21.com_

There are some really quirky pieces of animal print clothing out there at the moment and this pullover is a prime example. The all over owl print is a hoot! It has a cropped design, so if you’re not keen on showing a bit of belly then team it with high waisted pants or skirts.

## 8. Marc by Marc Jacobs Night Bird Printed Skirt

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Night Bird Printed Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_marc-by-marc-jacobs-night-bird-printed-skirt_350x342.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/xUnF2r)

This skirt from Marc by Marc Jacobs features a sweet ‘night bird’ print. It’s made from silk taffeta and has a stiffened underskirt which gives the skirt structure. Team it with a basic t-shirt, compact side bag and court shoes for a fun and flirty look.

Take a different tack when it comes to animal print clothing this season by choosing something along the lines of the clothes on this list. Ditch the leopard prints and opt for animal silhouettes instead. What do you think of these animal print clothes?
