# 9 Must\-Have Vintage Inspired Intimates ...

Vintage inspired intimates are a luxe way to treat yourself. All things vintage are trending these days and it’s not just limited to what you wear on the outside. A vintage inspired intimate set or item is just one way to complete the entire vintage transition. From pretty petticoats to romantic sleep sets, bring a little bit of vintage glamour into your life with these beautiful vintage inspired intimates.

## 1. Skylight Romance Sleep Set

![Skylight Romance Sleep Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/1_skylight-romance-sleep-set_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Trade in your flannelette pyjamas for this sweet sleep set. This vintage inspired intimate set consists of a sleepwear tank and briefs. They both come in a sky blue colour and feature ruffle lace trims.

## 2. Let’s Turn up the Volume Petticoat

![Let’s Turn up the Volume Petticoat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/2_lets-turn-up-the-volume-petticoat_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $69.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Add a bit of volume to your mid-length skirts and dresses with this pretty petticoat. This one comes in a white colour with a bell shape and lace trims. Slip it on under a skirt and get ready to twirl!

## 3. Blush Lace Trim Teddy

![Blush Lace Trim Teddy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/3_blush-lace-trim-teddy_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/5ZO6T3)
These vintage inspired intimates are ideal for wearing to sleep or lazing about the house. It comes in a gorgeous blush colour and features lace trims and a keyhole back. It’s a bit more than I’d usually spend on sleepwear, but it is a bit of a luxe treat.

## 4. Chiffon Embellished Midi Slip

![Chiffon Embellished Midi Slip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/4_chiffon-embellished-midi-slip_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/M968eO)
This loose, mid-length slip just screams vintage. The angular shapes of the trims however, give it a much more modern edge. It’s made from black chiffon and features embroidered details and embellishments to the front. Channel your inner lady of leisure and wear this slip with a satin kimono at home.

## 5. Fräulein Annie Contouring Undies

![Fräulein Annie Contouring Undies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_fr-ulein-annie-contouring-undies_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $88.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These vintage inspired intimates will help contour your figure to create a screen siren silhouette. They have a high rise design and feature lace and bow embellishments. These are ideal for wearing underneath anything that requires a smooth silhouette.

## 6. Basking in Morning Light Robe

![Basking in Morning Light Robe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/6_basking-in-morning-light-robe_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Embrace a feminine look with this vintage look robe. While the high collared necklines and long sleeves can leave it feeling a bit dowdy, the short hem ensures that it stays youthful and modern. Slip this on over a nightgown, brew a pot of tea, and enjoy a lazy morning.

## 7. Pastel Gingham Bralet

![Pastel Gingham Bralet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/7_pastel-gingham-bralet_600x633.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/eL88FH)
Gingham fabrics are ideal for working a preppy, vintage inspired look. You could wear this cute bralet underneath a low cut or sheer top, or, alternatively, team it with a pair of daisy dukes for a fun summer look.

## 8. Starlet in Satin Slip Gown

![Starlet in Satin Slip Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/8_starlet-in-satin-slip-gown_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $110.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If there’s one thing about vintage inspired intimates it’s that they’re a lot more luxurious than their modern counterparts. This long satin slip gown features a V-cut bodice with lace trims and a cascading ruffled hem. Too good too keep hidden, you’d be forgiven for trying to wear this gown outside of the house.

## 9. Agent Provocateur Tulle Babydoll

![Agent Provocateur Tulle Babydoll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/9_agent-provocateur-tulle-babydoll_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $490.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pZwGuu)
The design of this babydoll slip is quite cutesy, but the sheer nature of it makes it much more risqué. It comes in a turquoise colour and features scalloped edges and pink satin trims.

While these vintage inspired intimates are at the pricier end of things, they do make for a luxe treat. And we all deserve to spoil ourselves every now and again! What to do thing of the vintage inspired intimate trend. Is it something that you’ll be embracing?
