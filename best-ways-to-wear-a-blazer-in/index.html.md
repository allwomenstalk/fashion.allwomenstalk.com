# Best Ways to Wear a Blazer in 2015 ...

It goes without saying that a blazer is the one of the top fashion essentials to have in your arsenal. But even though most of us usually whip them out for job interviews, there are so many other ways (not to mention places) to rock them. Whether you're heading to work or simply need an excuse to show off your classy style, rocking the right blazer can make the difference between a good time and a GREAT time!

## 1. Make It Pop!

![clothing,blue,jeans,footwear,denim,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/os/td/m9ae3_clothing_blue_jeans_footwear_denim_362x874.jpg)

Source: [Fashion Style Mag](https://goo.gl/9FGt8I)
If you end up owning just one blazer this year, let it be this one. This sultry and classy mix between a cropped blazer and tuxedo jacket is beyond epic.The fact that she paired this royal blue blazer with distressed jeans (and then paired her look off with a HOT gold watch) puts her way ahead of the style game. Keep slaying, girl!

## 2. Red Haute

_ Source: WorkShop_
I could definitely see myself wearing this around the office. If you plan on wearing it to work, it's imperative that you have a jack that fits your shape perfectly without being too tight or revealing. Ladies, don't be afraid to shake things up! Because red is a power color, that makes this the perfect contender to show off your prowess.

## 3. Photoshoot Ready

![clothing,footwear,outerwear,fashion,spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mp/ii/m0sg6_clothing_footwear_outerwear_fashion_spring_600x900.jpg)

Source: [Harper's Bazaar](https://www.harpersbazaar.com/beauty/makeup/advice/a3417/alma-jodorowsky-face-of-lancome/)
Heading to a last-minute event with no idea what to wear? When in doubt, a sleek blazer and a fierce pair of heels will do the trick. If you're not a fan of jeans, you can even finish the look off with tapered trousers or a flirty A-line skirt. French singer Alma Jodorowsky effortlessly pulls this look off. She deserves all of the awards.

## 4. Dressed up

![clothing,jacket,outerwear,suit,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/yf/u304b_clothing_jacket_outerwear_suit_fashion_504x757.jpg)

Source: [Dormify](http://inspo.dormify.com/)
Let's be real. Nothing comes close to this classy number. As a modern and trendy take on the professional interview look, this will never go out of style. This is one of my favorite looks by Emma Watson; she should just be crowned queen already.

## 5. Dressed down

_ Source: GURL.com_
Pairing this relaxed jacket with other neutrals makes this outfit even more of a win! I love how casual yet put together this look is. From the chambray shirt to her animal print shoes, this proves how important details are. This is the perfect blazer to head to brunch with your pals.

## 6. Laidback

![color,clothing,footwear,spring,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ya/vh/cts6g_color_clothing_footwear_spring_fashion_600x963.jpg)

Source: [PlogStyle](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=plogstyle.com)
Casual and flirty never looked so good. The dual tone of this military style blazer is hitting all of the right trends. You can wear this to a night on the town or even for a sweet coffee date with your beau. Now, this is one look that I can seriously get behind.

## 7. The Boyfriend Blazer

![clothing,footwear,boot,tights,outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z5/mg/dumo8_clothing_footwear_boot_tights_outerwear_529x750.jpg)

Source: [The Fashion Spot](https://www.thefashionspot.com/celebrity-fashion/358829-year-in-review-top-5-street-style-stars-of-2013/)
The boyfriend blazer is incredibly versatile, making it one of the best jackets to own this season (and in general). It doesn't matter what your style is; you can dress it up or down. The fact that the always stylish Jessica Alba paired a steel colored boyfriend blazer with leather boots blows my mind! Can you believe well how these subtle tones come together? You can't go wrong mix and matching "masculine" and metallic pieces.
