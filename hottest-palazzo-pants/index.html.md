# 7 Hottest Palazzo Pants ...

As much as I adore skirts and dresses, sometimes they’re not quite right for an occasion or event, and I find myself wishing I had a pair of hot palazzo pants to wear instead. They’re so comfortable, and so elegant! I especially love a few of the pairs I’ve stalked on ASOS lately… here are my picks for the 7 hottest palazzo pants…

![TFNC Wide Leg Palazzo Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/m5/dy53orsc54c9057bce754025307025_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/p8TjxD)
Available in taupe or coral, these palazzo pants feature a flattering high waist, side zip, and even hip pockets. They’re somewhat sheer, perfect for summer weddings and garden parties. The coral color is really hot, and easy to match with a stark white blouse.

## 2. French Connection Palazzo Pants with Tie Belt

![French Connection Palazzo Pants with Tie Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o6/qs/gt249gds54c9057cc3fc4369410144_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $170.34 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/czc1zC)
**French Connection has the basics covered:** a wide-leg palazzo pant available in cream and black, topped with a sweet tie belt at the side. They also have side slash pockets, and the cream pair in particular are so pretty!

## 3. TFNC Wide Leg Palazzo Pants

![TFNC Wide Leg Palazzo Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w5/xm/a85rgoq854c9057dc7b4e677497550_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $68.13 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/unYBtF)
These dove-grey palazzo pants are so hot! They look marvelous with yellow, black, white, red, or slate grey, and the slightly sheer fabric makes them floaty, soft and dreamy. These zip in the back, so there won’t be an odd bulges on the side and are smooth in front.

## 4. ASOS Zip Pleat Flare

![ASOS Zip Pleat Flare](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/v8/ei6qp26z54c9057edffd8038213093_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/WLDP2o)
So red hot! These gorgeous palazzo pants are a little more punk-sass and casual than most, done in a deep red color with zippers at the side pockets and a size zip. Wear them like the model with a black tank or body suit and your favorite black peep-toe pumps.

## 5. ASOS Gold Button Detail Wide Leg Pant

![ASOS Gold Button Detail Wide Leg Pant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q7/l7/wv1dayhz54c9058018e5a023129809_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $53.79 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/ryasPq)
Technically these aren’t palazzo pants, but they’re wide leg and gorgeous, with a row of brass buttons along each side, very nautical-inspired. I love the turned-up cuffs and the curvy side pockets. I’d wear these with a crisp white blouse and platforms.

## 6. ASOS Palazzo Pants

![ASOS Palazzo Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/qi/xbfk18wi54c90581821c2725631674_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $71.72 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/gnKZgL)
These classic palazzo pants come in three lush colors - olive green, faded pink, and bluish jade - with a button waist and wide, flowing legs. They’re pleated in front and have a booty-flattering pocket in back.

## 7. ASOS Premium Tailored High Waist Cropped Pants

![ASOS Premium Tailored High Waist Cropped Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/bl/rqib8b8f54c905827df67407107687_392x500.jpg)

**Price:** $46.62 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/HIY3Ws)
