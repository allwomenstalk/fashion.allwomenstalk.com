# 7 Ways to Wear the Blue Trend ...

Blue is my very favorite color, so I’m delighted that it’s such a popular color for the upcoming season. My closet and shoe rack is full of cobalts and cornflowers, but if you haven’t indulged yet, don’t fret. Here are 7 ways to wear the blue trend, so you can rock it, too.

## 1. Slate of the Art Dress

![Slate of the Art Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_slate-of-the-art-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $109.00 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This vintage-inspired dress is the perfect way to wear blue and the retro trend! It’s so Mad Men, begging to be worn to the office with a pair of very sheer black stockings and shiny patent heels, Wear an updo and simple earrings and your favorite red lippie, too.

## 2. Fifth Wheel Heels

![Fifth Wheel Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_fifth-wheel-heels_350x351.jpg)

**Price:** $99.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I don’t think Seychelles has ever made a pair of pumps I didn’t love, and these are no exception. It’s all about the details with these shoes - the faux snakeskin ankle strap, the perky bow at the toe, and of course, the lush blue color. These may well be my very favorite way to wear blue this season!

## 3. It’s Crunch Time Shoe

![It’s Crunch Time Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_its-crunch-time-shoe_350x227.jpg)

**Price:** $69.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Yes, you’re in a hurry, but there’s no reason to sacrifice style for time. Slip into these gorgeous loafer-styled flats and a pair of pin-striped trousers and a tie-neck blouse, and you’re all ready to go! I love the bright royal blue color and the softer-than-soft fabric.

## 4. Bona Fide Fetching Clutch

![Bona Fide Fetching Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_bona-fide-fetching-clutch_345x160.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
As the name implies, this little bag is adorable! It’s another easy way to wear blue this season. Add it to your LBD for a night out with the girls, or with a salmon-hues party dress for date night. Want to go hands-free? This clutch converts to a shoulder bag with a detachable gold-tone chain. Handy! Maybe the best way to wear blue is over your shoulder, yes?

## 5. Southwest Zest Earrings

![Southwest Zest Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_southwest-zest-earrings_350x399.jpg)

**Price:** $11.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Complete tyour southwest-inspired Boho look with these gorgeous turquoise-hued earrings, and they’ll quickly become your favorite way to wear blue! They’re so pretty and feminine, adding a hint of color peeping through your loose beach-y waves. Or wear your hair pulled back so everyone can enjoy the earrings themselves!

## 6. Bow to Stern Scarf

![Bow to Stern Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_bow-to-stern-scarf_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $7.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s a neck-scarf, it’s a belt, it’s a headband… it’s all three! No matter hw you wear it, this stripe-y scarf will add a touch of nautical flair… I’d wear it with a white blouse and red hair-waisted pants and canvas wedges for Fourth of July…

## 7. Skull of Style Ring

![Skull of Style Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/7_skull-of-style-ring_350x258.jpg)

**Price:** $16.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Maybe punk is more your thing than mod… no worries, you can still wear the blue trend! This two-finger ring, bearing three blue skulls, adds a little punk edge to even the sweetest outfit. Wear it with a cropped leather jacket and your favorite skinny jeans, or with a sweet floral-print dress and skimmer flats.

With so many gorgeous ways to wear the blue trend, you’ll rock the color like no one else, attracting al the right kinds of attention. Which of these ways to wear blue do you love best, and how would you wear it? Please share!
