# 8 Stunning Summer Kaftans ...

Summer kaftans are perfect for wearing as beach cover-ups or to channel a resort-inspired look. Summer kaftans typically look great in vivid colours with embellishments to boot, but neutral and monochrome colours are always stylish options. Whether you want a summer kaftan as something functional or fashionable (and, really, there’s no reason they can’t be both) the kaftan is an essential garment this summer season. Take some inspiration from the following stylish summer kaftans.

## 1. Embellished Kaftan Dress

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
This embellished kaftan comes in a cool cream colour and features antique-look beading along the neckline. It’s semi-sheer and has fluttery, three quarter length sleeves. Give summer kaftans such as this one a contemporary twist by teaming them with leather leggings or pants and ankle books.

## 2. Melissa Odabash Embroidered Cotton Kaftan

![Melissa Odabash Embroidered Cotton Kaftan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_melissa-odabash-embroidered-cotton-kaftan_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $185.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fTPj7M)
Hit the pool in style with this hot pink embroidered kaftan. It has pale pink embroidery with gold beading and tasselled ties at the front. Team it with a floppy straw hat, bejewelled sandals, and plenty of arm candy for a glamorous poolside look.

## 3. Fleur Maxi Beach Kaftan

![Fleur Maxi Beach Kaftan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_fleur-maxi-beach-kaftan_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $39.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Maxi length kaftans are ideal for working that whole beach to bar look. This one comes in a mottle multicolored pattern. It also has a deep v-neck and back, elasticised waist, and self tie straps at the back. Wear this summer kaftan to a beach or garden party and team it with metallic sandals and accessories.

## 4. Sheer Midi Cover up

![Sheer Midi Cover up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_sheer-midi-cover-up_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/oQksZd)
You can’t go past basic black, and this kaftan style cover up is perfect for those who prefer fuss-free dressing. It’s made from a sheer fabric and has short sleeves, covered buttons, and a braided belt. Wear it over your swimsuit for a basic poolside cover up or with a slip underneath for something more daytime appropriate.

## 5. Lime Kaftan Dress

![Lime Kaftan Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_lime-kaftan-dress_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $99.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Statement making summer kaftans like this lime green one are perfect for working a fun and fabulous look. This maxi length dress features a vintage-inspired paisley print and has a sheer overlay and is fully lined. Style it up with a pendant necklace and metallic sandals.

## 6. Embroidered Kaftan Dress

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
This kaftan comes in a brilliant blue colour and features an embroidered pattern along the front. It’s made from sheer fabric and simply slips on. Work a festival ready look by teaming this kaftan with coloured denim shorts and a bikini underneath.

## 7. Tie Dye Kimono Cover up

![Tie Dye Kimono Cover up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_tie-dye-kimono-cover-up_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/h3h3Wd)
Go retro by adding some tie dye madness to your summer look. This kimono-style summer kaftan has a soft melon and green tie dye print. Slip it on over your bikini, pop on some oversized shades, and tie a colourful headscarf on for a breezy beach look.

## 8. Antik Batik Silk Georgette Kaftan

![Antik Batik Silk Georgette Kaftan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/8_antik-batik-silk-georgette-kaftan_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $166.25 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you want to incorporate prints into your wardrobe, try doing so with summer kaftans. This one features a deep green print and has draped sleeves, beaded embellishments, and comes with a detachable slip. Wear it with gold sandals and a turban headband for a glamorous retro-inspired look.

The summer kaftan can be styled in a variety of ways to suit a range of occasions. Whether you’re by the pool or at a party, a kaftan can be a chic fashion choice. Which of these summer kaftans do you like best?
