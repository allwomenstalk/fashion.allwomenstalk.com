# The Easiest Way to Begin Mixing Prints Like a Pro

Remember when we had to worry about having everything matched perfectly? Well, those days are long gone. Now, it's cool to mix two different prints! It creates a unique sense of style and is a lot easier to create than you might think. This fall, celebrities have been embracing the trend, showing us that [fall fashion trends](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/fall-fashion-trends/) are all about being bold and expressive. Take some style inspo from these A-listers:

| Print 1 | Print 2 | Why it Works |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Polka Dots | Stripes | Classic and balanced |
| Leopard | Floral | Bold and modern |
| Plaid | Graphic Tee | Edgy and cool |

## 1. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hb/kk/y1wf421m_533x800.jpg)

Blake Lively, who you can follow on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/blakelively/) for more style tips, layered up her baby bump with a plaid shirt tucked into a pop-art skirt! Her pregnancy style has always been adorable, much like the effortless vibes we see from [Jessica Alba](#3). This mix of patterns is a great example of how to [mix patterns](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-mix-patterns/) effectively.

## 2. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/v2/ltq4srqr_600x855.jpg)

Beyonce recently debuted super short bangs (which look incredible on her) and has been taking such wonderful fashion risks. The singer, whose latest updates can be found on her [official website](https://www.beyonce.com/), paired a polka-dot skirt with a printed top. It's a masterclass in confidence and proves that [Blake Lively](#1) isn't the only one winning at the print game this season.

## 3. Jessica Alba

![Jessica Alba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/np/pm2d5468_435x580.jpg)

Jessica posed with her close pal, Rosario Dawson while wearing a polka-dot ombre skirt paired with a white floral top! You can see more of her looks on her [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/jessicaalba/). You'd think these prints wouldn't work together, but on her, they absolutely do, creating a look that is just as iconic as [Beyonce's](#2) bold choices.

**Quick Tips for Mixing Prints:**

- Start with one small print and one large print to avoid overwhelming the look.

- Stay within the same color family for a cohesive and polished feel.

- Use a neutral accessory, like a solid belt or bag, to break up the patterns.
