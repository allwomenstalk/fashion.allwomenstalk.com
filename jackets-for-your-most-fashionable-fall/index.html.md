# 7 Jackets for Your Most Fashionable Fall ...

## 1. Blush Pink Short Coat

![Blush Pink Short Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/in/chlx7rf25630e984a5a06851383773_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this blush [pink coat](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-wear-pink-coats-for-fall/). It’s such a classic staple that’s perfect for fall. Not only is the style absolutely classic, but so is the color. If you’re looking for a coat that’s classic but will catch people’s eyes during the fall season, this is definitely the coat for you. It’s a staple but it stands out!

## 2. Dark Plum Faux Fur Jacket

![Dark Plum Faux Fur Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gd/sg/kycxwvur5630e984bc683472540823_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Faux fur jackets are without a doubt the biggest trend of the fall! Dark plum hues are especially stylish this fall. If you’re looking to get in on the faux fur trend, this is a perfect gateway into that trend. It’s colorful and bold but a dark enough hue that it’s not too outlandish!

## 3. Black Jacket with Beaded Embroidery

![Black Jacket with Beaded Embroidery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/nk/hbnpqwte5630e98532681442907009_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this beaded jacket. It’s such a statement piece, which is really tough to find when it comes to outerwear! I just think it’s the perfect jacket for Thanksgiving. It looks like it would look amazing with peach or blush tones and dark contrasting colors. What a beautiful jacket, right?

## 4. Black Biker Jacket

![Black Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/nm/mg505uuc5630e9852c620024544031_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Biker jackets are notoriously edgy, which is why I love this jacket in particular. It’s still edgy, but it has a little bit of coziness to it, which is exactly what you want to wear in the fall! I love the fake leather contrasted against the comfortable fur. It’s edgy but still so polished and cute!

## 5. White Pile Jacket

![White Pile Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/iw/y9w6y2dn5630e985947ed511441816_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](https://goo.gl/rgCqAL)
Is anyone else absolutely obsessed with this jacket? It almost looks like a comfortable blanket that you want to wrap yourself into, but it looks incredibly chic and beautiful! It’s something that I want to get and wear to like all of my Thanksgiving parties this year! I feel like it’s so unique that it would get so many compliments!

## 6. Black Wool-Blend Pilot Jacket

![Black Wool-Blend Pilot Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/cb/d1lnti1f5630e985bb130700430856_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I think I speak for a lot of people when I say that pilot jackets are my absolute favorite fall jackets. I don’t know what it is about the jacket. I love the style, the color contrast, and the faux fur. It’s just such a chic look for your fall wardrobe!

## 7. Gray Wool-Blend Biker Jacket

![Gray Wool-Blend Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/dd/zmwlp4tv5630e985c8d38193157989_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Again, a biker jacket has stolen my heart. I love that this looks like a biker jacket but has the material of a comfortable wool jacket. It’s edgy but so perfect for fall! Aren’t you just obsessed?
