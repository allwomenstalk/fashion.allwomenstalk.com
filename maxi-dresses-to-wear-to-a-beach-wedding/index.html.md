# 6 Maxi Dresses to Wear to a Beach Wedding ...

Yesterday I found the perfect **maxi dress to wear to my sister's** beach wedding; it's long, has a summer, ocean feel to it, and just puts me in the mood to relax with my toes in the sand! I love the tropical blue and green colors and just can't wait to show if off. It was also on sale at [Kohl's](http://www.kohls.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)for $42.00 from Apt. 9, which is one of my favorite brands. Do you have a beach wedding to attend this summer, or maybe you're just looking for a dress to relax in...

## 6. Light & Flowy...

![Light & Flowy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/bluedress_431x643.jpg)

Price: $188.00 @ [Anthropologie](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

For a** low key wedding **on the **beach **with the bride and groom in relaxed attire and all the guests sipping on strawberry daiquiris, this ocean wave **maxi dress **will put you right in the perfect mood. The light tones of the blue color just feels right for walking along the tide. Pair this flowy dress with [gladiator flats](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and a [turquoise scarf](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-cool-ways-to-wear-a-scarf/)for a complete look.

## 5. Zig Zag Yellow...

_ Price: $148.00 by Jessica Simpson_via [Zappos](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you're looking for a** maxi dress **with a little more personality and unique style than this [Jessica Simpson](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)yellow zig zag print may be the one to wear to your friend's **beach wedding**. What I love most about this dress is how the belt around the waist ties into an adorable bow in the back. Have a fun and flirty time in this dress with matching [yellow sandals](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)and beautiful [drop earrings](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-color-your-wedding-bright-with-yellow/).

## 4. Tropical Florals...

![Tropical Florals...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/blueandbrown_424x495.jpg)

Price: $55.20 by [Christin Michaels](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)at [Zappos](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The tropical flowers, the bright blues against the rich brown; does this **maxi dress **not remind you of the colors of [Hawaii](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/a-honeymoon-cruise-to-hawaii-guest-blogger-bicoastal-bride/)! In this dress you are all set to enjoy an umbrella drink, a wedding reception on the beach, and good company! With a beautiful dress like this one what other accessories would you need than a blue flower in your hair.

## 3. Classy Beach Style...

_ Price: $178.00 by[Max and Cleo](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)at [Zappos](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Perhaps that **beach wedding is in the Hamptons_and you need something a bit more classy, but you still want that [beached](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-color-your-beach-wedding/), relaxed feeling. That's what I love about** maxi dresses, you can look casual and simple or classy and chic. The black and white pattern and the one-shoulder strap on this one by Max and Cleo is gorgeous and would wow the crowd and the bride!

## 2. Fuchsia Pink...

Price: $49.50 by [Rip Curl](https://www.ripcurl.com/us/)at [Zappos](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You'll be glowing in this **pink maxi dress **as the sun sets on the romantic beach wedding in [Mexico](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/reasons-to-celebrate-a-cinco-de-mayo-wedding/). This would also be a pretty dress to wear to the bridal shower if you already have your dress picked out for the big day.
