# 7 Slip Dresses ...

With current fashion trends revolving around sheer and silky fabrics, slips are becoming a must have wardrobe item. Simple slip dresses are also a fuss-free option for summer dressing. Slip dresses can be designed as undergarments or for outerwear. Either way, they both share an easy slip-on design. Take a look at a few stylish slip dresses below.

## 1. T by Alexander Wang Long Silk Slip Dress

![T by Alexander Wang Long Silk Slip Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/k0/d0kgs6or54c9058f833b4650746282_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $270.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This long silk slip dress is sleek and stylish. It is an elegant take on the maxi dress silhouette. It has a draped neckline, low arm holes, and twist seam detail at the side. It is designed to be a relaxed fit and would be a luxurious addition to any wardrobe. Experiment with textures and pair this silk dress with suede, faux fur, and sequins (although not necessarily all at once).

## 2. Tibi Sequined Slip Dress

![Tibi Sequined Slip Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/so/ii/yvnk40pq54c905906195f280157895_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $370.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tlbjnh)
This sequined slip is definitely a party dress. It is covered in black sequins and has spaghetti straps and a v-shaped neckline. It is also fully lined. This type of mini dress is reminiscent of a 90s supermodel style of dressing. When styling this dress, tame it with a structured blazer and patent pumps.

## 3. Topshop Ivory Lace Babydoll Slip

![Topshop Ivory Lace Babydoll Slip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/ru/p2gsrh7p54c9059179209006073305_334x498.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TL149Z)
Stylish slip dresses don’t always have to come in a straight ‘up and down’ design. This slip dress from Topshop comes in a babydoll silhouette. It has an ivory lace overlay, scalloped neckline, and satin empire line detailing. Wear this dress during the summer months with pastel blazers and nude-coloured shoes.

## 4. 3.1 Phillip Lim Initials Printed Chiffon Slip

![3.1 Phillip Lim Initials Printed Chiffon Slip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/dy/avwkr0cy54c9059274a3c717941227_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $185.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This indigo shift dress features a cute all over polka dot print. It has bralette-style top, keyhole opening at the back, and side vents at the hem. It has a semi-sheer appearance. This dress would look great paired with a colour palette of crisp white and tan.

## 5. Topshop Peach Slinky Knitted Slip

![Topshop Peach Slinky Knitted Slip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/y9/rpkpqvt454c9059370d65918162766_334x497.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/rh6vjy)
This knitted slip dress comes in a summer peach colour. It has spaghetti straps, a scoop neck, and low back. It is quite clingy, so the body conscious should remain wary of this dress. Slip dresses often make great basics and are a great starting point for working a layered look.

## 6. Rouland Mouret Powermesh Halterneck Slip Mini Dress

![Rouland Mouret Powermesh Halterneck Slip Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/rb/bv3hl4ba54c90594439ef769474916_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $470.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1JlE5u)
This expensive halterneck slip dress is designed to be worn as shapewear. It has a v-shaped neckline, seam detail under the bust, and zip fastening at the back. This dress is designed to smooth and sculpt your silhouette. This slip would be great worn underneath a range of stylish backless dresses.

## 7. Proenza Schouler Slip Dress with Chain Appliqués

![Proenza Schouler Slip Dress with Chain Appliqués](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/n5/nfm4i7z354c9059509828563910152_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1225.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While seemingly simple in design, stylish slip dresses can break the budget. Just take a look at this slip by Proenza Schouler. It features black and white layers of crinkled chiffon, chartreuse trims, and ruffled chain detailing. The design is quite modern and directional. This slip dress has a relaxed fit and sits just below the knee.

Slip dresses can come in a range of designs and silhouettes. Worn underneath garments, they provide modesty and shape. Worn as outerwear, they look chic and effortless. Do you like any of the slip dresses on this list?
