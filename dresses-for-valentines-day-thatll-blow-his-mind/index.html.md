# 7 Dresses for Valentine's Day That'll Blow His Mind ...

With so many options for dresses for Valentine’s Day, it can be hard to know where to begin. Therefore, I have compiled a list of some of my favorite dresses for the big day. Choose any of these dresses for Valentine’s Day and you will blow your date’s mind!

## 1. For Love & Lemons – Florence Dress

![For Love & Lemons – Florence Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120131_7868_472x656.jpg)

Dresses for Valentine’s Day should be both sweet and sexy. The Florence dress is the perfect combination of both! This deep red velvet dress channels what Valentine’s Day is all about. The sweetheart neckline is perfect for showing off one of your favorite short necklaces or chokers. Complete the look with a deep wine lipstick, your favorite ankle booties, and winged eyeliner.

## 2. Tempest – Lace Day Dream Dress

![Tempest – Lace Day Dream Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120131_6863_600x600.jpg)

This sweet dress comes in both cream and black, but for Valentine’s Day, I love the cream color! Tempest tailors their clothing to celebrate the female form. The Lace Day Dream dress is a perfect example of their mission. Accessorize this Valentine’s Day dress with nude high heels, pink lips, and a high bun.

## 3. Pins and Needles – Deep V-Back Fit & Flare Dress

![Pins and Needles – Deep V-Back Fit & Flare Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120132_3739_600x900.jpg)

This blush pink dress by Pins and Needles looks like it was made just for Valentine’s Day! The fitted bodice flares out to a circle skirt. The back of the dress is cut into a deep V down the middle. The fabric is a gorgeous lace pattern that is lined underneath. To complete the look, throw on your favorite bracelets, [midi rings](https://jewelry.allwomenstalk.com/majorly-stylish-midi-rings-for-fashionable-fingers/), and necklaces for a charming Valentine’s ensemble.

## 4. BB Dakota – Corella Black Velvet Dress

![BB Dakota – Corella Black Velvet Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120131_9480_560x840.jpg)

The burnout texture on the velvet on this gorgeous BB Dakota dress gives the bodice a leopard pattern. Animal print is one of my favorite patterns to wear on Valentine’s Day, making this one of my favorite options for the big night. The skirt on the dress gathers at the waist then flows out to create fullness. To complete the look, put on your favorite black booties, a black bra or undershirt, and gold jewelry.

## 5. Aryn K – Good Graces Strapless Red Maxi Dress

![Aryn K – Good Graces Strapless Red Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120131_3018_560x840.jpg)

Valentine’s Day is the perfect occasion to wear a floor length gown. One of my favorite maxi dresses for the holiday is Aryn K’s Good Graces dress. The bodice has a lace embroidery and sweetheart neckline. The red chiffon fabric flows from the waist to the floor and is adorned with a 27-inch slit. You can really play up the red with this dress by wearing red heels and lips to match, or let the red dress be the focus and opt for silver heels and nude lips.

## 6. Betsey Johnson – Pink Zip Front Party Dress

![Betsey Johnson – Pink Zip Front Party Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120132_7672_600x750.jpg)

If fun and flirty is your style, then you will love this cute dress from Betsey Johnson. Like most of her styles, this dress is loud and playful. This pink dress is adorned with black leopard print and a zipper in the front. The black tulle skirt pokes out at the bottom for an extra dimension. To accessorize this dress, step into your favorite black wedge heels and put on some red lipstick!

## 7. Lucca Couture – Iridescent Strapless Dress

![Lucca Couture – Iridescent Strapless Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/08120152_3907_600x900.jpg)

I love the shiny shimmery fabric on this dress from Lucca Couture. The iridescent fabric will make the colors go from purple to pink to blue and every color in-between. The bodice features a v-shaped cutout that is boned for extra support. The back of the dress is elastic to make this dress easy to put on. Your date won’t be able to take their eyes off of you in this dress! Since the dress is strapless, it is the perfect dress to show off your favorite necklaces and bracelets. Silver accents and shoes are a great choice for this type of fabric!

Each of these dresses for Valentine’s Day are sweet, sexy, and fun. Valentine’s Day is the perfect excuse to buy a new dress to celebrate in! Did you see something on this list you would wear on your date?
