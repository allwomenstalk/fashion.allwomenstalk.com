# Fab Tips to Finding the Most Flattering Sunglasses for Your Face ...

If you’re anything like me you may be thinking **what are the most flattering sunglasses for your face shape?**

I’ve always wanted to try the latest sunglasses trends, from the sleek cat eye shape to angular frames and of course, the fashionable aviator sunglasses, but I’ve always been hesitant and would think would they actually suit me?

Thanks to instyle.com they have come up with some useful tips on how to choose the best sunglasses for your face shape. Have a look at the guidelines below to help you pick the perfect pair of sunglasses for your face shape and complete your overall summer look.

## 1. Round

![eyewear, sun hat, sunglasses, vision care, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wb/xe/dyxj40ll595b5c3ceec1a539635078_400x400.jpg)

Wide ovals and sleek cat's-eye styles work to diminish fullness. Look for frame styles that are wider than they are tall and that have higher temples.

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, glasses, brown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/pk/dojv9fc5595e9cefbc3b6662382186_500x600.jpg)

Tory Burch, $150; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Oval

![eyewear, sunglasses, human hair color, vision care, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/ku/g47abt2e595b5c3d81853693717891_400x400.jpg)

Almost any eyeglass shape works on this face type. Generally, however, the frames should be as wide as or slightly wider than, the broadest part of your face.

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, glasses, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/gp/pn3y648k595eaa4b1e14b414368096_500x449.jpg)

H&M, $14.99; at [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Long

![eyewear, hair, blond, sunglasses, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/ap/u8430iwo595b5c3de4d16008816114_400x400.jpg)

Oval glasses or ones with strong horizontal lines shorten the appearance of the face and soften its angles. Details on the arms can also help to draw the focus outwards.

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, purple, goggles,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yi/e7/kscv1m9i595ea801f2125964118194_500x600.jpg)

Gucci, $350.88; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Narrow

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, hairstyle, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/iv/dq5h56lm595b5c3fc5ea1560785156_400x400.jpg)

Go with smaller frames as oversized versions can appear bulky. Rounded lenses flatter angular features plus embellished side pieces give the illusion of a width at your temples.

![eyewear, sunglasses, purple, vision care, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/ic/e5fh5lmw595ea8736d0e3693221596_500x600.jpg)

BP, $12; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Square

![eyewear, fashion model, sunglasses, vision care, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xs/gf/zbq88gqz595b5c406cb73715166506_400x400.jpg)

Curved shapes that are wider than they are tall will soften the angles of this face shape. Keep in mind, frames that are flat along the bottom can look boxy.

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, goggles, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/yq/rvt5pgku595ea801ec7ee372526744_500x600.jpg)

Prada, $260; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Wide

![eyewear, hair, sunglasses, blond, human hair color,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/ql/s19uisa9595b5c40ad55d629800034_400x400.jpg)

Choose larger, wider frames (too small glasses can look cartoonish), and look for side pieces with spring hinges that will sit snugly on your head without squeezing it.

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, product, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/v4/hzuiqwz6595ea80223ac7946423677_500x600.jpg)

Quay Australia, $55; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Inverted Triangle

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, glasses, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/xe/taknwrrn595b5c426a09c652914104_400x400.jpg)

Balance your face shape by choosing frames with an emphasis on the top-aviators or half-rim versions work best.

![eyewear, blue, sunglasses, purple, vision care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/do/x9j7y5v7595ea80307f92660088298_500x600.jpg)

BP, $12; at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Heart

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, eyebrow, chin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/m5/rr1zmvk7595b5c43105fc005554637_400x400.jpg)

Oval, slightly rounded or butterfly shapes help soften heart-shaped faces.
