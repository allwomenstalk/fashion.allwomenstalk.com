# 7 Fashionable Ways to Wear Faux Fur ...

The autumn and winter seasons are perfect times of year to have some fun with some chic ways to wear faux fur. With so many options in a variety of styles, faux fur can be utterly glamorous for a night on the town, or dressed down and comfortable for lunch. Play up your wardrobe this season with these seven fashionable ways to wear faux fur.

## 1. Faux Fur Collar

![Faux Fur Collar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25125625_6896_600x901.jpg)

Purchase a detachable faux fur collar to mix and match this piece for almost any outfit. A detachable faux fur collar can be worn with a cardigan or a winter coat to make it more elegant, a leather jacket to add texture, or with a dress for that finishing touch. The versatile ways to wear faux fur in a detachable collar make for an effortlessly cool look while staying trendy and stylish.

## 2. Faux Fur Vest

![Faux Fur Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25125737_4673_600x902.jpg)

One of the most popular trends that will never fail is the classic faux fur vest. Pair it with a printed dress and tights for a more professional look, or wear it on the weekend with a long sleeve tee and riding style boots. I love to travel in a faux fur vest because of the cozy and comfortable feel, while looking pulled together and glamorous when I arrive at my location.

## 3. Colorful Faux Fur

![Colorful Faux Fur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25125842_8788_432x648.jpg)

It can be fun to add color to your wardrobe so why not try colorful faux fur? Try wearing jewel tones like emerald, cobalt blue, and ruby red for some serious outfit appeal. Soft blush colors or even winter white can also be a good alternative rather than the usual brown or black. Sport a red lip with your colorful faux fur, and you will want to snap a selfie to remember this look to wear again!

## 4. Faux Fur Clutch

![Faux Fur Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25130037_3870_600x900.jpg)

If you are not up for wearing faux fur on your body, complete your winter look with a faux fur clutch or small cross body bag for a night out. You will feel winter-ready and chic, but not too over the top. The combination of leather with faux fur or a dainty chain on your bag is a plus!

## 5. Fur Hoodie

![Fur Hoodie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25130207_3583_600x797.jpg)

One of my favorite pieces is the faux fur hoodie. Stay warm and curl up by the fire in a casual and cute hoodie. With so many options in stores and in a variety of colors, find a hoodie that you can wear around the house or just to run around in.

## 6. Faux Fur Boots

![Faux Fur Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25130505_5523_600x903.jpg)

Faux fur boots can be warm and casual or dressed up in a stiletto! Whether your faux fur boots are a chic ankle wedge, or a flat boot with faux fur trim, find what speaks to your style or occasion. Boots look amazing with skinny jeans, leggings, dresses, and skirts! Try this trend for a movie night or for a holiday party.

## 7. Faux Fur Scarf

![Faux Fur Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/25130545_0773_576x864.jpg)

Scarves can be the finishing touch to an ensemble in any season. Chiffon scarves in spring, Linen scarves in summer, and faux fur scarves in winter! My favorite style is the faux fur infinity scarf. It’s easy to put on, looks beautiful and classy, and will keep you warm.

I can’t wait to break out my faux fur pieces this season! What is your favorite way to wear faux fur?
