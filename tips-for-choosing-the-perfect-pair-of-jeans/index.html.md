# 7 Tips for Choosing the Perfect Pair of Jeans ...

How To Choose Jeans that fit perfectly is something that we’d all like to achieve. The perfect pair of jeans are like the holy grail of the capsule wardrobe. When it comes to how to choose jeans, there are many different tricks of the trade. The following are just a few tips on how to choose jeans that should helpfully make buying your next pair all the more easier.

## 1. Know the Store

Choosing the right pair of jeans can be a daunting task in itself. You need to have your wits about you. Choose a store that you’re comfortable spending time in. There’s nothing worse than trying clothes on in a store where you find yourself intimidated by the staff or surroundings. You’ll find that choosing a relaxed setting will make the task at hand much more enjoyable.

## 2. Try Lots of Styles

Bring lots of different styles of jeans into the changing room with you. Don’t be afraid to bring in styles that you wouldn’t normally wear. Don’t judge a book by its cover, and this goes for jeans as well. The final verdict should come down to fit and feel. You might just be surprised at that pair of jeans you swore you would never wear.

## 3. Take Your Time

Finding the perfect pair of jeans should not be done in haste. Leave yourself plenty of time to go shopping. This tip for how to choose jeans also ties in with the first. The aim is to be as calm and at ease as possible. There’s no point in getting frustrated if you can’t find the right jeans at the first go.

## 4. Walk around in Them

Get an idea about how a pair of jeans will feel when you’re wearing them by walking around in them. Walk around, sit down, do a few squats even! When sitting, make sure that they don’t ride down too much. Ultimately, if the jeans feel like they’re a second skin – get them! If you show the slightest sign of hesitation in getting them – keep looking.

## 5. Think about Shoe Choices

When looking at how to choose jeans, think about what footwear you’ll be wearing with them. Jeans can look drastically different when worn with flats or heels. If you’ll be wearing them mostly with heels, you’ll need to make sure that the length will accommodate for them. Alternatively, if you’re going to be wearing them with flats, you don’t want the leg to be too long.

## 6. Pocket Positioning

Look at where the pockets are positioned on a pair of jeans. The right positioning can make all the difference. A pocket that is placed higher up can give the appearance of a perkier derriere. Also, pockets with bulky embellishments and buttons can add bulk to your behind. Slim, patch pockets are a good choice.

## 7. Price Range

Set a budget. A quality pair of jeans will no doubt cost extra. But don’t be put off by high price tags. You can still find a decent pair of jeans at a good price – just be prepared to look. Also, don’t turn your nose up at brand you wouldn’t normally try. It doesn’t matter if the jeans are J Brand or Target, if they fit well then they’re bound to look a million bucks anyway.

Everyone has a different method for how to choose jeans that fit perfectly. Finding the perfect pair of jeans is no easy task, so when you do find them I’d suggest stocking up on multiple pairs of the same style! What are your best tips when it comes to how to choose jeans?
