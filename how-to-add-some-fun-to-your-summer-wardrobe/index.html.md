# How to Add Some Fun to Your Summer Wardrobe ...

I always say that summer is the best season for style because it’s the time of the year when weather doesn’t get in the way of all of the different trends we’ve wanted to try out all year. If you feel the same but don’t really know where to start when it comes to adding some fun touches to your summer wardrobe, this is the list for you. Whether you want to add some bright colors or just some crazy patterns to your wardrobe, I’m sure that you’ll find something on this list that will add some fun touches to your summer wardrobe!

## 1. Add Neon

![Add Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gm/fx/o092x0zj557c8b4d150e1180119263_600x840.jpg)

Neon is such a big trend this summer that if you’re looking to spice things up in your wardrobe, adding some neon is the perfect way to do that. You don’t necessarily need to wear copious amounts of neon, but you could definitely add some splashes of the bright trend here and there in your outfit!

## 2. Pick a Style

![Pick a Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ou/sl/a1cvma2n557c8b921cca3602727143_407x610.jpg)

Pick a summer style to carry with you as the summer goes by. Try nautical, bright prints, or just a beach vibe, and stick with it for the whole summer. By the end of the summer, you’ll be known as the [insert your own awesome style here] girl!

## 3. Or Just Completely Go Crazy

![Or Just Completely Go Crazy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/qi/jrgbe3av557c8bb31ceb8254115100_600x878.jpg)

Or, if limiting yourself to one style isn’t really your thing, mix it up and go crazy with a different style every day. One day, dress like you’re going on a fancy boat trip, on another, dress like you’re going to Coachella. Changing it up just makes your wardrobe more fun!

## 4. Mix Prints

![Mix Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/br/pqpemgqi557c8c091a712135160286_600x890.jpg)

Mixing prints is a tricky game, but one of my summer style rules. It’s so much fun to take two completely different prints and make them look great together. If you’re looking to add some fun to your boring summer wardrobe, find two pieces with different prints and try to find a way to make them work together!

## 5. Add Tropical Touches

![Add Tropical Touches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/oa/sgk99ntv557c8eaad6ec3445678868_600x779.jpg)

Summer is all about being tropical, so let that show through in your style this summer. Add some tropical touches, whether that means wearing a flamingo on your shirt or palm tree earrings. It doesn’t matter what it is, but those tropical touches will make you feel like you’re on a Caribbean island at all times!

## 6. Dress up a Bathing Suit

![Dress up a Bathing Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/f2/nj2hsl4w557c8ee64973d409737662_424x572.jpg)

Just because it’s summer doesn’t mean that you can walk around in a bathing suit at all hours of the day. However, that doesn’t mean that you can’t dress up a bathing suit so that you can wear it at all hours of the day. Throw on a sarong or a tunic to completely change up your look and make your bathing suit look chic!

## 7. Don’t Be Afraid of Color

![Don’t Be Afraid of Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/hg/dkm6gv67557c8f0bd1c9d027464384_550x825.jpg)

Summer is one of the biggest seasons for color, so if you’re not wearing color during the summer, what are you wearing? If there is any season that you wear color, it should be summer, so don’t be afraid to rock color, in any capacity, this summer!

What is your favorite way to add some fun to your summer wardrobe? I love trying all of these ideas out (on different days, of course!). Let me know in the comments how you keep your summer wardrobe fresh!
