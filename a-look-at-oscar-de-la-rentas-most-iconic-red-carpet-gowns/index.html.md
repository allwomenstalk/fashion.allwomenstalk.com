# A Look at Oscar De La Rentas Most Iconic Red Carpet Gowns ...

With the legendary designer's recent death, we thought we'd share his most iconic red carpet gowns. Celebrities have all started to pay tribute to the late designer by posting photos of themselves in his creations. Taylor Swift led the tribute with a sweet Instagram post, calling it an honor to have known him and to have been able to wear his pieces. He's dressed every first lady since Jacqueline Kennedy and all of Hollywood's leading ladies. Here's a look at (some of) his most iconic gowns:

## 1. Oprah Winfrey

![Oprah Winfrey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/be/medxchbn_600x969.jpg)

At the 2010 Met Gala, Oprah wore a custom creation AND attended the red carpet on the arm of the designer.

## 2. Amy Adams

![Amy Adams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bo/em/kmh95wjb_466x700.jpg)

Amy's a vision to see in this gown at the 2013 Oscars.

## 3. Sarah Jessica Parker

![Sarah Jessica Parker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cp/lz/ezb8ze2x_600x901.jpg)

SJP made quite the fashion statement with this black and white gown at the MET Gala.

## 4. Cameron Diaz

![Cameron Diaz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/vf/yxr5ucyp_592x888.jpg)

Cameron Diaz glittered on the 2010 Oscar red carpet.

## 5. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wb/y1/nluvweh3_600x925.jpg)

For the last Harry Potter premiere back in 2011, Emma made a statement this stunning gown.

## 6. Jennifer Garner

![Jennifer Garner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/ce/znkudbh2_530x790.jpg)

At this year's Oscars, Jennifer Garner wowed in this fringed Oscar de la Renta gown.

## 7. Emily Blunt

![Emily Blunt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jd/ra/cls4knyw_470x689.jpg)

Emily Blunt wore this vibrant gown at the 2012 SAG Awards.

## 8. Kerry Washington

![Kerry Washington](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/ga/wyce3e7p_600x969.jpg)

Kerry was quite the sight in the designer's two-toned gown.

## 9. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/oo/rutr4tkj_600x900.jpg)

After Oscar's passing, Taylor took to Instagram to let everyone know that he was her "all time favorite designer."

## 10. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xd/tm/zd38ztnx_600x960.jpg)

Jennifer wore this bright gown at the 2011 SAG Awards.
