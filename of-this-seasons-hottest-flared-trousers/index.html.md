# 7 of This Seasons Hottest Flared Trousers ...

Flared Trousers are making a comeback. This is mainly due to the popularity of the Seventies-inspired trend that is sweeping the fashion world. It could also be a revolt to the dominance of the skinny jean over the past couple of years. This season, a pair of flared trousers or jeans is the way to go. From trusty denim to coloured corduroy, there are a variety of ways to wear the trend. Take a look at the following flared trousers for inspiration.

## 1. Rag & Bone Mid-Rise Flared Jeans

![Rag & Bone Mid-Rise Flared Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/oz/keruu7nz54c93303eec02958901113_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ov82ve)
These stretch-denim jeans are a casually chic way to work the flared trend. They come in a light ‘sand’ hue and are a great alternative to darker coloured flared trousers. They have a mid-rise design with five pocket detailing. Wear these with a long-sleeved striped shirt, lace-up flats, and wide-brimmed felt fedora for an on-trend look.

## 2. J Brand Cord Bellbottom Trousers

_ **Price:** $329.00 at mytheresa.com_
Cord fabrics are an on-trend choice. They are perfect for creating a lush, autumnal outfit. These flared trousers come in a rustic terracotta colour and have a high waisted design. They would look great paired with a slouchy top, chunky heels, and a crisp blazer.

## 3. Topshop Moto Jessie Jeans

![Topshop Moto Jessie Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/xl/q6hbfefc54c93305979f4895754411_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $85.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/KSXzOI)
A pair of black jeans is a wardrobe staple. Give your wardrobe the flared treatment with these affordable black jeans. They feature a crisp front crease, zip and button fastening, and five pocket detailing. Team these with a slouchy neutral knit, floppy felt hat, and stacks of bracelets for a casual off-duty look.

## 4. MIH Marrakesh Mid-Rise Flared Jeans

![MIH Marrakesh Mid-Rise Flared Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/cr/l66f4j4s54c933065b27a074863479_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $190.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/BWqZvp)
These flared jeans are a chic option. They come in an understated midnight-blue hue and are made from mid-weight stretch denim. They have pressed creases, tonal stitching, and four pocket detailing. The mid-rise design is ideal for balancing out the weight of the flares. Tuck in a white blouse and team with a blazer for a smart-casual look.

## 5. Adam Mid-Rise Flared Jeans

![Adam Mid-Rise Flared Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/cb/czg0urm354c9330746910151950034_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/TmdXca)
Coloured jeans are one of the biggest trends this year. Add a burst of colour to your outfit with these fuchsia jeans. They are made from light-weight stretch denim and have a mid-rise design. Team these flared trousers with a grey sweater and neutral accessories.

## 6. The Row Lassington Chantilly Lace Flared Trousers

![The Row Lassington Chantilly Lace Flared Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/ny/utc6igbp54c93308016eb343634942_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $1840.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4noP42)
The bohemian-luxe trend is a big favourite this season. These lace trousers are a sophisticated way to work the trend. They feature Chantilly lace, scalloped hems, and concealed side fastenings. Offset the intricate lace by pairing these flares with a black top, neutral pumps and structured bag.

## 7. Theysken’s Theory Silk-Blend Flared Trousers

![Theysken’s Theory Silk-Blend Flared Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/za/j1/n2df2gth54c93308bbd8b554642126_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $425.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/LB2Zhx)
These silk-blend flared trousers are an elegant take on the trend. They’re perfect for wearing to the office or on a night out. These trousers feature pressed creases, turned-up cuffs, and concealed side fastening. Team these trousers with a lacy top, white blazer, and statement-making cocktail jewellery.

Whatever your style, there’s bound to be a pair of flared trousers to suit your needs. The key to wearing flared trousers this season is creating a balanced outfit. If you think that this may be a passing trend, and don’t want to spend too much cash, then look to chain stores for affordable versions of your favourite styles. Will you be wearing flared trousers or jeans this season?
