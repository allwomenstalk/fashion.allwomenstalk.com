# 8 Pretty and Polished Peplum Jackets ...

Peplum jackets are a stylish and smart way to add a bit of drama to your outfit. The peplum silhouette is one that is quite striking and very fashionable right now. A peplum jacket thrown over a casual or work outfit is an easy way to work the trend. Take a look at a few fashionable peplum jackets below.

## 1. Peplum Ponte Jacket

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
This short sleeved black jacket would make a versatile addition to any wardrobe. It has a striking portrait neckline, three button placket, and peplum silhouette. Wear it over a neutral sleeveless dress and with court shoes for a corporate look.

## 2. Floral Lace Jacket

_ **Price:** $22.99 at forever21.com_
This floral lace peplum jacket is perfect for dressing up an outfit. It has a notched collar, long sleeves, and a hook and eye closure. It would look great paired with a white tank top, dark denim jeans, and strappy heels for a stylish night time look.

## 3. Geometric Print Peplum Jacket

![Geometric Print Peplum Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_geometric-print-peplum-jacket_354x400.jpg)

**Price:** $116.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IY9h2p)
Peplum jackets look especially on-trend in geometric prints. This one by Topshop features a blue geometric print and has a cute double layer Peter Pan collar. The fitted waist flares out into quite a striking peplum silhouette. Work a fresh look and team this jacket with some cream tailored trousers.

## 4. Étoile Isabel Marant Peplum Jacket

![Étoile Isabel Marant Peplum Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_toile-isabel-marant-peplum-jacket_332x400.jpg)

**Price:** $205.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You’ll be pretty in pink with this stylish jacket. It has a cropped design and features a distinctive peplum style hem. Wear it open over a black and white printed dress and with patent heels for a fun party look.

## 5. High-Low Peplum Hem Blazer

_ **Price:** $20.99 at forever21.com_
From the front this just looks like a cropped blazer, but the back reveals a long hem with subtle peplum detailing. Simply pop it on over a neutral work dress to give your look some extra polish.

## 6. Knitted Dove Jacket

![Knitted Dove Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_knitted-dove-jacket_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $99.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This military inspired jacket features the slightest peplum inspired detailing at the hem. It has a zip up front with four patch pockets and flared back tails. Wear this jacket open over and apricot shift dress for a quirky look.

## 7. Valentino Roma Peplum Jacket

![Valentino Roma Peplum Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_valentino-roma-peplum-jacket_348x400.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You can’t go past a good black jacket and this one just happens to come in with a peplum waist. It has a tailored fit with padded shoulders, a scooped neckline, and snap fastenings. Wear it as part of your work wardrobe or to add a bit of sophistication to more casual outfits.

## 8. Peplum Blazer

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
Colourful peplum jackets are an easy way to brighten up an outfit. This one has a notched collar, single button closure, and comes in a wonderful coral hue. Wear it over a playful printed dress or with a crisp white tank top and light denim jeans.

These are just a few peplum jackets that might take your fancy. Short sleeve styles are ideal for summer dressing, while longer sleeved styles are more substantial. What are your thoughts on the peplum jacket this season?
