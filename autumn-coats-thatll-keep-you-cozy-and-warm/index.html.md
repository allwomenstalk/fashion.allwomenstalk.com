# 7 Autumn Coats That'll Keep You Cozy and Warm ...

If there were one thing about autumn style that I absolutely love, it’s got to be the coats. They add another dimension to your overall look that you don’t get in many other seasons. I also think that they make it easier to look stylish, because they’re adding another layer to your overall look. You may just be wearing a t-shirt and jeans, but when worn with a wool coat and scarf, you suddenly have a “look.” If you’re looking for a coat to capture attention this fall, this list of H&M coats is sure to impress you!

## 1. Camel Wool-Blend Coat

![Camel Wool-Blend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/b5/a7qh9f0v561e4cbb1f413298542136_384x449.jpg)

I’m always here for a camel coat in the fall, and this coat is definitely no exception. I love how it looks like a chic coat you’d see walking down the runway, but it also looks cozy like a blanket you just can’t wait to wrap yourself into! I’m obsessed!

## 2. Wool-Blend Biker Jacket

![Wool-Blend Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/nf/lbor792x561e4cbb62b74786220684_384x449.jpg)

Biker jackets are always big in the fall, which is why I love how different this one is. It’s not your typical leather jacket that you’d wear on the back of a motorcycle. It is however, exactly the same style, it just has a gorgeous huge plaid pattern that looks just as chic as the style of the coat is!

## 3. Light Beige Wool-Blend Boucle Coat

![Light Beige Wool-Blend Boucle Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t1/vs/k1kk20dw561e4cbb8a7c6687891763_384x449.jpg)

This is a coat that should be a staple in all of our wardrobes. There are so many reasons to love it. First of all, the color of the coat is such a beautiful beige that it will never go out of style and it will pair with nearly anything in your closet. Next, the silhouette is gorgeous and will flatter any outfit you’re wearing underneath!

## 4. Salt and Pepper Boucle Coat

![Salt and Pepper Boucle Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/h2/bycw79al561e4cbbdbe5c489983934_384x449.jpg)

I absolutely love this salt and pepper gray trend that’s taken over the fashion world this fall. I especially love it when paired against these burgundy pants. What I really love about this coat, though, is the big, black lapels. They’re so trendy yet effortlessly stylish, and I’m a little obsessed.

## 5. Double-Breasted Wool Cape

![Double-Breasted Wool Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/gq/dz9ny22m561e4cbc0af51450219995_384x449.jpg)

Is anyone else seriously adoring this cape? I can’t get over it! It reminds me of something Olivia Pope would wear, but it also looks like something we could all just throw over any old outfit and look like we just spent months contemplating the outfit! It adds so much elegance so easily. I may just have to add this to my collection for Thanksgiving!

## 6. Dark Blue Cape

![Dark Blue Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g4/vl/qetczf5u561e4cbc2a95d213958051_384x449.jpg)

Capes are the biggest coat trend of the year, obviously, so you absolutely need to hope on the trend while you can! If you love to make a statement and neutral colors aren’t really your thing, this blue plaid cape is for you. I especially love the faux leather trim around the coat-it gives this preppy look a little more edge!

## 7. Teal Oversized Biker Coat

![Teal Oversized Biker Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qm/ac/cxxpx9nm561e4cbc63e81803816782_384x449.jpg)

Although H&M describes this coat as teal, I think we could all agree that it’s a darker green hue, which I absolutely love for fall. It’s perfect for any of your favorite fall holidays, and it will be just as cute for winter holidays as well. I also love the simple silhouette of the coat; it’s chic and effortless!

What is your favorite coat from this list? Will you add any of these to your wardrobe? Let me know in the comments!
