# 8 Chic and Stylish Tube Skirts ...

Tube skirts are trending this season. With fitted pencil skirts being quite popular lately, the progression to the tube skirt was only natural. This season, stick to longer length tube skirts for a more sophisticated take on this body-hugging style. Refresh your wardrobe with the following stylish tube skirts.

## 1. Self Stripe Tube Skirt

![Self Stripe Tube Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/1_self-stripe-tube-skirt_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HjSyWH)
The classic black skirt is given an updated twist in the form of a self stripe overlay. The effect is subtle but still adds an extra textural element to the skirt. Wear this skirt with a slouchy tank top and strappy sandals for a simple, summer-appropriate look.

## 2. For the Sun of It Skirt

![For the Sun of It Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/2_for-the-sun-of-it-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Add some high intensity colour to your new season wardrobe with brightly coloured tube skirts. This bandage-style skirt comes in a sunny yellow hue and is perfect for this season’s obsession with colour. Wear it with a light blue or pink top for an interesting take on colour blocking.

## 3. Pencils Downtown Skirt in Pink

![Pencils Downtown Skirt in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/3_pencils-downtown-skirt-in-pink_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This form fitting skirt comes in a gorgeous shade of watermelon pink. It has a panelled design with kick pleat detailing at the back. Team this skirt with a white silk blouse and patent shoes for a fresh, sophisticated look.

## 4. Black Double Layer Tube Skirt

![Black Double Layer Tube Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/4_black-double-layer-tube-skirt_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/if3mES)
Black tube skirts can be a wardrobe staple. This skirt has a double layer design and comes in a longer length. It’s quite form fitting, but the double layer design means it is a little more forgiving on the figure. Wear it with a neutral top and leg lengthening heels.

## 5. Rockin’ and a-reelin’ Skirt

![Rockin’ and a-reelin’ Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_rockin-and-a-reelin-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Work some stripes into your look by slipping on this long tube skirt. It features charcoal and white stripes and would be perfect for working a nautical inspired look. Team it with a collared blouse tied at the waist, colourful shoes, and cat eye sunglasses for a retro twist.

## 6. Waves of Style Skirt

![Waves of Style Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/6_waves-of-style-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $52.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Choose tube skirts with interesting embellishments to help conceal any body flaws. This skirt features wave-like pintuck detailing which adds an extra dimension to the skirt. It would look great worn with a plain white t-shirt and tan sandals.

## 7. Essential Pencil Skirt

![Essential Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/7_essential-pencil-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $32.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This dark blue skirt would be a great day time alternative to the classic black skirt. It is made from a stretchy fabric and features and contrasting coral waistband. You could easily dress this skirt up with a silk blouse, blazer, and patent pumps.

## 8. Diane Von Furstenberg Metallic Tube Skirt

![Diane Von Furstenberg Metallic Tube Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/8_diane-von-furstenberg-metallic-tube-skirt_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $275.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/yWCsgz)
The metallic trend is still going strong and this designer number is a stylish take on the trend. It has a silver textured finish and is made from a stretchy cotton-blend fabric. Wear it one a night out by teaming it with a slinky top and strappy heels.
