# 8 Luxe Black Leggings ...

Black Leggings have become a staple part of any wardrobe. They’re versatile pieces and are great for transeasonal dressing. Wear your favourite summer dress in fall simply by wearing a pair of black leggings underneath. Add a cool cardigan and a scarf and you’ve got a perfectly layered look. While you can buy black leggings at quite affordable prices, if the chance arises why not treat yourself and invest in a quality pair? Take a look at a few luxe black leggings below.

## 1. Topshop Velvet Black Leggings

![Topshop Velvet Black Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t8/qy/enz9w92854c9319da3811900285134_267x396.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/C9kV7F)
Velvet has been a popular choice of fabric the past few seasons. A pair of black velvet leggings is an easy way to incorporate this trend into your everyday look. Simply replace your normal leggings with a velvet pair. Velvet is also a great way to add a textural element to your outfit.

## 2. Spanx Look-at-Me Stretch Cotton-Blend Leggings

![Spanx Look-at-Me Stretch Cotton-Blend Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l5/d3/pa4b3rr554c9319e5d75c675875711_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $62.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/2ukyVL)
These black leggings are cut to flatter the figure. They are made from a black stretch-cotton blend, are high-waisted, and feature a wide waistband. Wear them with anything from shirt dresses to oversized sweaters. They can easily form the basis of a range of outfits.

## 3. DKNY Jersey and Leather Leggings

![DKNY Jersey and Leather Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/fw/ln65gyvg54c931a3b2bd3131003416_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $125.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Ei4FZR)
These leggings feature cool leather detailing. They are made from black jersey and feature diagonal leather trims to the front. While being quite ‘edgy’ the leather touches are still quite subtle. Wear these with oversized knits for a street-smart look.

## 4. Topshop Faux Leather Panel Black Leggings

![Topshop Faux Leather Panel Black Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/ni/lpeqfmsv54c931a471641023369409_267x398.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/kzeKjn)
These heavy weight leggings feature black faux leather panels at the sides. These leggings are high-waisted and hit at the ankle. Leather details are great for adding a contemporary touch to an outfit. Wear these with and collared denim shirts, biker boots, and studded handbag for a model-off-duty look.

## 5. By Malene Birger Luono Sheer Floral-Lace Leggings

![By Malene Birger Luono Sheer Floral-Lace Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/b6/lqo0nxll54c931a53526b694639327_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Ylm6gt)
These lace leggings are a delicate alternative to the more common stretch fabric pairs. These leggings are high-waisted and feature a fitted elastic waistband. Work a fashion-forward evening look and team these with your favourite LBD, a draped blazer, and heeled ankle boots.

## 6. Joseph Ribbed Knitted Silk Leggings

![Joseph Ribbed Knitted Silk Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/bk/y951bg4d54c931a609130119434329_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/R3nvFy)
These ribbed leggings would make a luxe addition to any wardrobe. They are made from knitted silk and have a high-waisted cut. The ribbed detailing provides a nice texture to the leggings. Wear these with oversized shirt dresses, ballet flats, and chunky knits for a chic fall look.

## 7. Topshop Black Matt Wetlook Leggings

![Topshop Black Matt Wetlook Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/qd/g5ufglrv54c931a6ca9fe746618997_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/dp6JjS)
These wet look leggings are part of Topshop’s Edited collection. These leggings have the appearance of leather and feature panelling throughout. They are high waisted and feature two wide panels at the waistband. Work a fashion forward look and wear these black leggings with draped jersey dresses, wedge boots, and cropped jackets.

## 8. Vince Corduroy Legging

_ **Price:** $195.00 at barneys.com_
Corduroy is making a comeback, so why not dip your toes into the trend with a pair of black leggings? They’re an easy and subtle way to incorporate some corduroy into your everyday look. These leggings are made from stretch fabrics and are high-waisted. They also feature a wide waistband. Wear these leggings with long-line shirts for a fashionably new season look.

While spending over $100 on plain black leggings may seem a bit extravagant, you can always take inspiration from these designs and look for cheaper alternatives. Black leggings are versatile pieces so even if you do spend a bit extra on them, the cost-per-wear will more than make up for it. How will you wear your black leggings this season?
