# 7 Ways to Reuse Summer Clothes during Winter ...

When the seasons change, you'll want to reuse summer clothes during winter instead of sticking them in the closet until next year. There’s no reason to waste money on a brand-new fall wardrobe when you can use what you already have. You’ll save money and won’t have to wait six months to wear your favorite tank tops and shorts. By layering and adding new accessories, you can reuse summer clothes during winter.

## 1. Lovely Leggings

![Lovely Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/1_lovely-leggings_600x902.jpg)

Leggings are an essential part of any woman’s wardrobe. They come in different colors, styles, and can have designs in them. Pair them with your favorite shorts or skirt. Depending on what you buy, they can be surprisingly warm. For extra coverage, wear them with long socks and boots. If you want to reuse summer clothes during winter, leggings are a must-have.

## 2. Cutesy Cardigans

![Cutesy Cardigans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_cutesy-cardigans_567x850.jpg)

If you have tons of tank tops, buy cardigans in various colors and styles. That way, you can wear spaghetti straps and short sleeves all year round. You can also make more outfits with the same few pieces. By wearing one shirt with a black cardigan one day and with a white cardigan on another, it’ll look like two completely different outfits. Plus, since cardigans are technically an accessory, you can wear them multiple times in a week.

## 3. Limitless Layering

![Limitless Layering](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_limitless-layering_600x900.jpg)

Buy some long sleeve shirts in solid colors to war beneath shorter shirts. You can also pile vests and sweaters on top of a skimpy top. Layering is easy and can be done with almost any article of clothing. Scarves come in handy when trying to make a light outfit warmer, and so do stylish hats. Accessories are a requirement during the winter season.

## 4. Casual Capris

![Casual Capris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/4_casual-capris_335x502.jpg)

If you have a pair of capris that fit just right, incorporate them into your winter look. As long as they aren’t too short, you can cover exposed skin with high boots. To keep warm, wear high socks or leggings beneath the boots. They won’t be seen, so it doesn’t matter how silly it looks. They’re only there to keep you from getting frostbite.

## 5. Fab Fabric Coverings

![Fab Fabric Coverings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_fab-fabric-coverings_470x705.jpg)

If you have a shirt that’s perfect for winter except for its low neckline, these come in handy. They cover up more of your chest so you’re not stuck freezing. They can also enhance your appearance if you get a decorative, frilly one or one with a color that mixes well with your outfit. Some stores sell them in a pack, so you can get a few at a time and swap out whenever you’d like. Check out [cleava.com](https://www.cleava.com/Default.asp) if you’re looking to purchase them.

## 6. Underwear underneath

![Underwear underneath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_underwear-underneath_600x901.jpg)

If the problem with your outfit is that the fabric is too light, put on thermal underwear to keep warm. Keep it disguised so you can still rock your summer look. As long as it doesn’t poke out of the outfit you’re wearing, there’s no harm in it. Buy some at any clothing store like PacSun or Walmart. You should be able to find them for relatively cheap.

## 7. Dreamy Dresses

![Dreamy Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_dreamy-dresses_470x705.jpg)

Why throw your dresses in the back of your closet just because it’s cold out? Some cute summer dresses look good with pants underneath. Or, if you prefer, you can bring out those useful leggings again. It'll gives you a fresh new look. The choice is all yours!

Don’t pack away all of your summer clothes when there’s still time to use them. Do you reuse your clothing in different ways in different months? Do you have any tips on how to do so?
