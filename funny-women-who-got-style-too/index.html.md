# 7 Funny Women Who Got Style Too ...

For so many years, it’s been assumed that if you’re a funny woman you can’t possibly be stylish (or else you won’t be taken seriously) and vice versa. In my opinion, that couldn’t be further from the truth, and I’m so excited that some of the funniest women in Hollywood are going against that, and aren’t afraid to display their love for style while also being absolutely hilarious. If you’re looking for funny women with style, these are definitely the women to pay attention to!

## 1. Mindy Kaling

![Mindy Kaling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/fc/nij37uy5569181bb280ec935659302_600x683.jpg)

Mindy Kaling’s show, The Mindy Project has become just as popular for its style as its hilarious jokes. Mindy Lahiri is always wearing crazy prints, patterns, and colors, and if something isn’t custom-made for her, it’s definitely a huge designer. I love that Mindy Kaling shows that you can still be stylish and funny!

## 2. Grace Helbig

![Grace Helbig](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/ob/akmqzbyp569181d075c60620219096_548x577.jpg)

Grace Helbig was never really known for her style at the beginning of the career, but these days, it’s almost become synonymous with her comedy career, and for good reason. She’s set to release a book soon that’s all about style, and she’s constantly doing parody fashion videos on her hilarious YouTube channel!

## 3. Amy Poehler

![Amy Poehler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jh/jh/mbyotxrj569181e4af68a444883274_600x600.jpg)

Is anyone else obsessed with Amy Poehler’s red carpet style? I love that she’s not afraid to take risks, like when she died her signature blonde hair red last year. When she’s not on the red carpet and she’s acting she’s just as stylish. I love all of the outfits she wore as Leslie Knope, especially during the last season!

## 4. Jenny Slate

![Jenny Slate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m4/ry/btrkwi8i569181fa3585a445235297_600x603.jpg)

I’m still thinking about Jenny Slate’s fashion choices in her hit movie Obvious Child, and I saw it almost a year ago. I love that she’s not afraid to be quirky with her style, but she’s still so stylish despite the fact that it’s not “conventionally fashionable.”

## 5. Melissa McCarthy

![Melissa McCarthy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/zp/yqtnweuw56918231c6459056782086_600x800.jpg)

Melissa McCarthy recently started her own fashion line, so she’s arguably one of the most stylish women on this list thus far. I’ve always loved that even though she’s plus-sized, and most people think that she should just be wearing a potato sack or something similar, she’s never let that compromise her style, which is why I think she’s one of the most stylish women on this list!

## 6. Blake Lively

![Blake Lively](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/zx/w75aehuq569182451584e282126412_600x600.jpg)

Blake Lively is one of the only women on this list who is better known for her taste in fashion than her comedy. She played a character on a hit TV show for years that was known for her style choices and she’s basically a model when it comes to red carpet looks, but a lot of people don’t know just how funny she is. She is hilarious, but a lot of people don’t know it because she doesn’t do conventionally funny movies. Believe me, a few scrolls through her Instagram account will change that perception of her!

## 7. Tina Fey

![Tina Fey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tf/ml/ah853ub85691825b1517b777968557_600x600.jpg)

Tina Fey always gets the attention of the Fashion Police for sticking with solid black dresses on the red carpet most of the time. I don’t understand why that’s such a big deal, though! I think that a big part of fashion is knowing what looks good on you, and if Tina Fey likes how she looks in black, why shouldn’t she wear it and still be considered stylish?

Who’s your favorite lady on this list? Were you surprised by any of these choices? Let me know what you think in the comments!
