# 7 Gorgeous Yoga Pants That Are Appropriate for Exercise and Lounging ...

If you haven’t gotten into the yoga pants trend yet, these gorgeous yoga pants will sure make you hop on board. Whether you wear yoga pants for actually doing yoga or relaxing around the house, these options are for you. Yoga pants are made from stretchy, comfortable fabric that moves with you no matter what activity you may do. These variations take it to another level with their fun prints, colors, and added perks. Your gorgeous yoga pants will make you stand out (in a good way) while being fashionable and relaxed.

## 3. Northern Lights Yoga Hot Pants by Teeki

![Northern Lights Yoga Hot Pants by Teeki](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_northern-lights-yoga-hot-pants-by-teeki_600x884.jpg)

If you have ever seen the Northern Lights before, you will love these pants! Even if you haven’t, these pants are beautifully made. They are so comfortable too, making them great for relaxing days. You can still wear them to yoga class, and they will move with your body no matter what. Maybe it’s because I’m from the North, but I absolutely love these! Would it be weird to wear these when actually watching the Northern Lights? I think not. Anyways, you can buy them on amazon.com.

## 4. Pisces Crop

![Pisces Crop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/4_pisces-crop_600x943.jpg)

The cropped design of these yoga pants will keep you feeling free and comfortable. You won’t have to worry about tight yoga pants that are uncomfortable with these! They are also perfect to wear to yoga class because of their stretchy fabric that moves with your body. The fabric is also sweat-wicking for those hot yoga sessions. The color is also very unique, because yoga pants are typically black instead of white! Find them on cozyorange.com.

## 5. Leggings with Side Tunnel and Contrast Back Yoke

![Leggings with Side Tunnel and Contrast Back Yoke](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_leggings-with-side-tunnel-and-contrast-back-yoke_566x850.jpg)

These leggings are so cute, and I love the “contrast back yoke” on the side of these pants. There is a tunnel drawstring on the bottom of them so you can adjust for efficiency and comfort! I love the contrasting color, but there are two other colors available as well if you don’t prefer this color. The legging-style of these yoga pants makes them great for not getting in the way when you are doing yoga poses. They are available on solowstyle.com.

## 6. Wunder under Pant

![Wunder under Pant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_wunder-under-pant_600x598.jpg)

## 7. Salutation Leggings

![Salutation Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_salutation-leggings_470x470.jpg)

These are probably my favorite yoga pants ever. They are skinny jean inspired, so they are fashionable in addition to comfortable. That means they are acceptable to wear out and about, to yoga class, and at home. There are two pockets in the front, which is very rare to find in a pair of yoga pants! I’m in love with all things red, but these are available in grey if this color isn’t for you. You can buy them at lolewomen.com.
