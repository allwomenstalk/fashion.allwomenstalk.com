# 8 of This Seasons Best Printed Jeans ...

**Printed jeans are a** trend to watch. Gone are the days or dark or light wash jeans – now there is a multitude of finishes to choose from. From polka dot patterns to tribal inspired designs, there are a range of print jeans on offer at the moment. Take some inspiration from the following stylish printed jeans.

## 1. Topshop Moto Baroque Print Jeans

![Topshop Moto Baroque Print Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-moto-baroque-print-jeans_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/tNVop4)
Baroque and scarf inspired prints were heavily featured in a range of designer collections this season. Work this trend on a budget with these **stylish printed jeans**. These jeans are made from a supersoft fabric and feature a bold baroque print. These jeans would look great worn at night with a silk tank top and strappy heels.

## 2. J Brand Denim Printed Skinny Jeans

![J Brand Denim Printed Skinny Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_j-brand-denim-printed-skinny-jeans_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $205.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XhS5VK)
Cut a feminine figure in these floral jeans. The combination of pale pink denim and deep red floral print make for quite an eye catching print. These jeans would be perfect for wearing on the weekends, catching up with friends.

## 3. Topshop Moto Printed Jeans

![Topshop Moto Printed Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_topshop-moto-printed-jeans_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zU3dqn)
Really stand out from the crowd in this rainbow striped jeans. They have a high waisted design and super skinny fit. Since these printed jeans are quite busy, pare them back with a basic t-shirt and minimal accessories.

## 4. Forever21 Ditsy Floral Skinnies

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
These floral print jeans are the perfect cheap and cheerful option. They’re available either in a rust or camel colour and feature a subtle all-over floral print. Wear them with a loose singlet and strappy sandals for a care-free spring look.

## 5. Current/Elliott Cropped Skinny Jeans

![Current/Elliott Cropped Skinny Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_current-elliott-cropped-skinny-jeans_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/qht6aw)
The classic stripe print has been reinvented with these cool cropped jeans. The come in the popular ‘stiletto’ style which is generally a skinnier fit. These printed jeans would look great worn with a neutral top and lace-up shoes. If you’re on the shorter end of the height spectrum, the vertical stripes could also give the illusion of an extra few inches!

## 6. Modcloth Plaid Jeans

![Modcloth Plaid Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_modcloth-plaid-jeans_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $79.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These plaid jeans are a great choice for adding a bit of punk style into your wardrobe. They have a mid-rise design and skinny fit. Dress them up by teaming them with a loose-fitting blouse and patent shoes, or grunge them up with a band t-shirt and lace-up boots.

## 7. Topshop Moto Wide Striped Jeans

![Topshop Moto Wide Striped Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_topshop-moto-wide-striped-jeans_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/jWMjcH)
These Topshop **printed jeans are an affordable** alternative to the Curren/Elliott ones previously mentioned. They feature wide vertical stripes and have authentic trims. For a fresh spring look, pair these jeans with a pastel tank top and neutral sandals.

## 8. Diane Von Furstenberg Mid-Rise Skinny Jeans

![Diane Von Furstenberg Mid-Rise Skinny Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/8_diane-von-furstenberg-mid-rise-skinny-jeans_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $255.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/5f5K1V)
The white and blue colour combination on these jeans gives them quite a crisp appearance. They have a mid-rise, skinny fit and feature a bold blue botanical print. This deep blue hue would look great teamed with orange, so incorporate an orange top or accessories into your look for instant chic.

When it comes to printed jeans this season, there’s just so much on offer. Floral denim is really big right now but your tribal, animal, and geometric prints are still a chic favourite. What do you think of these print jeans?
