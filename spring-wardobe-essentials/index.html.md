# 9 Spring Wardobe Essentials ...

Now that winter is coming to a close, it's time to get that spring in your step and into your wardrobe too. There are some things you need for spring and if you're a little stuck for inspiration, this list should help you to look your best as the weather begins to warm up.

## 1. Return of the Mac

![Return of the Mac](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/ix/u0g4crb554f60a4c198db078744649_400x600.jpg)

It's not super warm but it's not super cold either, which is where the light weight mac comes in. With a range of colors and styles, your lightweight mac really is a spring wardrobe essential as the weather gets a little warmer and the April showers start. Opt for a long mac and if you can, invest in a classic one which will keep you looking super sexy for years to come.

## 2. Neck Wear

![Neck Wear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nj/yo/f0hsnux054f60a6e5fed0729024256_400x600.jpg)

I have an array of scarfs and although woolly gloves and hats aren't usually required in the spring, a colorful scarf will brighten up any outfit as the weather begins to get warmer.

## 3. Beautiful Boots

![Beautiful Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xe/ra/qkruwrpf54f60aa282d6e242054306_600x900.jpg)

You probably don't need your Uggs anymore but some tan colored spring boots are still a spring essential. Spring is the time when the boots get a little lower down the leg and ankle boots are the perfect spring staple.

## 4. Sexy Sweater

![Sexy Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/w5/qwlxeb6354f60ac3401b1418433682_600x900.jpg)

Sexy spring sweaters are a great way to get you in the mood for the warmer months. The shops are awash with everything from pastels to bright colors. Choose your favorite spring sweater and combine with jeans or your favorite skirt and fabulous flats.

## 5. Cool Cardigans

![Cool Cardigans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/we/wy/f4umydaz54f60adc458f8583243345_600x901.jpg)

Cardigans are great for layering and spring is certainly the time to layer up. Sometimes the weather can vary throughout the day and having something you can take on and off during the day is essential. Invest in a cardigan to wear over dresses, skirts, pants, ANYTHING! The cardigan is one of those spring wardrobe essentials.

## 6. Fabulous Flats

![Fabulous Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s1/bl/odptjsyx54f60b06e050b881249415_600x900.jpg)

Now that the snow has hopefully cleared (although in my part of the world we have been known to get snow as late as April!) we can make way for some flats. It's not quite warm enough for flats sans tights but flats and tights are a great combination for when the weather starts to get a little warmer. Choose from am array of styles to lighten-up your look in the spring.

## 7. Mad Hatter

![Mad Hatter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/qv/kr0sqbie54f60b1fe9c6f825097164_470x705.jpg)

The woolly hats can be put to the back of the wardrobe for now and if you're looking for some head candy, you can get a spring hat to make you look fresh and sexy. I have a couple of spring hats that I like to wear when the weather warms up. There are so many styles to choose from so go crazy and get a beautiful spring hat with which to adorn your head in the coming weeks.

## 8. Awesome Accessories

![Awesome Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/b3/m9rkgzm254f60b3dc0b11239017148_500x700.jpg)

During the spring you can start to show a little more flesh. Not a lot but a little bit you understand - it's not THAT warm outside yet. Spring is the time to have some fun with some flora and fauna inspired accessories now that the flowers are in bloom. Invest in a statement necklace or earrings to pay homage to the birds, bees and the flowers.

## 9. Beautiful Blazer

![Beautiful Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/jj/aezuwf6u54f60b9f13b35114454123_600x900.jpg)

Spring is the time to ditch the heavy coat and enjoy the freedom of wearing something tailored and chic. With so many styles to choose from, it's a versatile look you can wear day and night.

These are just some of the spring wardrobe essentials to invest in this year. What will you be buying to spice up your spring wardrobe?
