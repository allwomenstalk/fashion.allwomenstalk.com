# 9 Stylish Dresses to Take You from Beach to Bar ...

Dresses from day to night need to have a sense of versatility to them. You want something that can look casual and dressed-down during the day but can also be styled up at night. Finding good day to night dresses can also be a key component in putting together a successful vacation wardrobe. These stylish dresses will take you from day to night and beach to bar.

## 1. Heart of Beach Glass Dress

![Heart of Beach Glass Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_heart-of-beach-glass-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $47.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A colourful beach dress is the way to go this season. This one comes in a sweet pink fabric and features flecks of colour throughout. Take this dress from day to night with the addition of metallic sandals and some colourful drop earrings.

## 2. Tie Dye Halter Dress

_ **Price:** $16.03 at forever21.com_
Nothing says beach dressing more than tie dye. This charcoal-grey tie dye print, however, is much more city chic than 70s hippie. Wear it out at night by teaming it with silver collar necklace, patent clutch, and wedge heels.

## 3. Lace Trapeze Tunic

![Lace Trapeze Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_lace-trapeze-tunic_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ATPrii)
This dress is perfect for cutting a romantic silhouette. The cream lace is stylish and sophisticated while the comfortable cut of the dress makes it perfect for wearing during the day. The addition of rose gold accessories will easily take this dress from day to night.

## 4. Bold in Bushwick Dress

![Bold in Bushwick Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_bold-in-bushwick-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Go for a nautical inspired look with a striped dress. This red one will definitely have you standing out from the crowds at the beach. Take this dress to the bar by swapping the waist tie for a slim metallic belt and adding some statement earrings.

## 5. Polka Dot Dress with Sash

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
Polka dots are a must have print this season. This polka dot dress is perfect for working a retro-inspired beach look. Team it with cat eye sunglasses during the day and style it up at night with the addition of a patent belt and heels.

## 6. Strappy Button Midi Dress

![Strappy Button Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_strappy-button-midi-dress_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TWJDmM)
Dresses from day to night are great in colour. This one comes in a summery sunshine yellow and has a relaxed design. Wear it with a floppy straw hat at the beach and add a colourful tribal-inspire necklace, embellished clutch, and wrap sandals for an eclectic night time look.

## 7. Gloria Satin-Jersey Maxi Dress

![Gloria Satin-Jersey Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_gloria-satin-jersey-maxi-dress_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/zUid3A)
If you’re feeling spendy, there are plenty of designer options when it comes to dresses from day to night. This luxe maxi dress comes in a hot pink colour with a relaxed design. Wear it with oversized sunglasses and a turban for a retro-inspired daytime look, and dress it up at night with bejewelled sandals and plenty of costume jewellery.

## 8. Crisscross Sweetheart Dress

_ **Price:** $15.50 at forever21.com_
This vintage inspired dress features a flattering sweetheart neckline and crisscross straps at the back. Wear it as is for a day at the beach and pop on a structured jacket and heels for a stylish night time look.

## 9. Jersey Tiered Maxi Dress

![Jersey Tiered Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/9_jersey-tiered-maxi-dress_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TRu8Qr)
This maxi dress is perfect for wearing during the day or night. The bandeau design means its perfect for showing of your beach glow. Add a drop necklace and embellished handbag for an effortlessly stylish night time look.

Dresses from day to night are a chic option to have in any wardrobe. Take a dress from day to night simply by adding some statement jewels or metallic accessories. What do you look for in a dress that takes you from beach to bar?
