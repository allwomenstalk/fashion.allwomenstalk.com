# 7 Awesome Denim Skirts That Will Become Staples in Your Wardrobe ...

Add some awesome denim skirts to your wardrobe this season. The humble denim skirt is enjoying a comeback of sorts, although some could argue that it never really went away. This season’s denim skirt comes in a range of different cuts and designs. Slip on a chic denim pencil skirt for a ladylike look or don a flippy skater skirt for a flirtier take on things. No matter what style you prefer, these are just a few awesome denim skirts that would make great wardrobe staples.

## 1. A-Line Denim Skirt

![A-Line Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/1_a-line-denim-skirt_599x536.jpg)

When stocking up on awesome denim skirts for your capsule wardrobe, stick to timeless and classic styles. This denim mini skirt has a classic A-line cut and would make a versatile addition to any wardrobe. Team it with a plain t-shirt, sandals, and small satchel for a crisp summertime look or pair it with a chunky knit and opaque stockings for fall. This particular skirt usually retails for around $60 from Topshop but if you’re lucky you can grab it on sale for $20.

## 2. Denim Midi Skirt

![Denim Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_denim-midi-skirt_599x738.jpg)

Stay on-trend this season in a midi length skirt. This midi skirt comes in a light vintage wash with an A-line, knee-length design. It costs $68 from Topshop and would look great teamed with a pastel top and tan sandals for a casual daytime look.

## 3. Bodycon Denim Skirt

![Bodycon Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_bodycon-denim-skirt_599x871.jpg)

Dark denim skirts are truly versatile. This bodycon stretch denim skirt could easily become one of those wardrobe pieces that you put on high rotation. Team it with a slinky camisole, biker jacket and ankle boots for cool downtown look. This skirt by MiH jeans is pretty pricey at $170 but you can also check out Dorothy Perkins and ASOS for similar, more affordable denim skirts.

## 4. Denim Skater Skirt

![Denim Skater Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/4_denim-skater-skirt_599x573.jpg)

Skater skirts are still a popular trend. They’re great for putting together a casual daytime like. This mid wash denim skater skirt from Dorothy Perkins would look effortlessly chic teamed with a strappy singlet and leather sandals. It usually retails for $44 but can be purchased on sale for $27. Also be sure to check out ASOS and Zara for similar styles.

## 5. Denim Pencil Skirt

![Denim Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_denim-pencil-skirt_599x792.jpg)

Work a polished look in a chic denim pencil skirt. This dark, almost black denim skirt from Dorothy Perkins would look great teamed with a silky blouse and heels for a sophisticated look. This particular skirt will set you back $44 which is a fraction of what you’d pay for similar skirts by fashion house Burberry. You can also try American Apparel for a denim pencil skirts in a range of colours.

## 6. Casual Denim Skirt

![Casual Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_casual-denim-skirt_350x500.jpg)

This denim skirt from Modcloth is perfect for working a laidback look. It has a fairly casual design with a gathered waist, button front, and front pockets. It costs $48 and would look great worn with a striped top and flats for a simple daytime look.

## 7. Leopard Print Denim Skirt

![Leopard Print Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_leopard-print-denim-skirt_336x596.jpg)

If you prefer to think of leopard print as a neutral, then this denim skirt might be right up your alley. It’s by Tibi and features a fun black on white leopard print. It has a flared design and would look great teamed with a colourful top and neutral accessories. It is pretty pricey though and will still cost you around $120 on sale.

The denim skirt can be dressed up or down to create a range of different looks. These are just some awesome denim skirts worth considering for your wardrobe. Which of these denim skirts do you like best?
