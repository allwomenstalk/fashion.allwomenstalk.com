# 8 Hottest New Season Hats ...

Hot Hats are a cool way of topping off your new season look. Black and grey hats are timeless, but this season is also about experimenting with colour. Hot hats in burnt orange and maroon colours will be quite popular this season. Wide brimmed fedoras and trilby hats are on-trend, but vintage-look cloche style hats are also stylish choices. Take a look at a few hot hats for this season below.

## 1. Topshop Blonde Trilby Hat

![Topshop Blonde Trilby Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/jh/dlr9kcxx54c930b9949c4413244008_400x308.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7INecW)
This neutral trilby hat can easily be worn with a range of outfits. It has a classic trilby shape but comes in an on-trend neutral colourway. It is made from a ‘blonde’ wool and features a contrasting band. It’s one of the more versatile hats out at the moment.

## 2. Eugenia Kim Corinna Rabbit Felt Fedora

![Eugenia Kim Corinna Rabbit Felt Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/b1/st03gmbu54c930ba678e0309593700_400x273.jpg)

**Price:** $285.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/P0SaE0)
When it comes to hot hats for your new season look, a black fedora is a classic choice. This hat is made from rabbit felt and features a waxed cord band. The cord also has gunmetal tips. While not everybody can afford to fork out $300 for a hat (myself included!) you can always keep an eye out for similar styles at a more affordable price point.

## 3. Pachacuti Cinnamon Panama Fedora

![Pachacuti Cinnamon Panama Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/cb/jw5s75er54c930bb3d8ec144616004_400x208.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hD3yOR)
Bring a little bit of summer styling into your new season look with this chic Panama hat. This hat is handmade from sustainable straw and has brown grosgrain trim. It also has an internal twill trim. Wear this hat with a denim shirt, coloured jeans, and lace-up shoes for a fashion-forward look this season.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Rabbit Felt Wide Brimmed Hat

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Rabbit Felt Wide Brimmed Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/p0/h5znbgw254c930bc1cb23323299638_400x209.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tOZ4B6)
This wide brimmed hat has a bit of a retro feel about it. It is made from dark brown rabbit fur and comes with a detachable fold-detailed band. The rabbit fur gives the hat a certain texture which ties in well with fall dressing. Team this hat with an outfit of wide legged pants, a silky blouse, and swing coat.

## 5. Lulu Flat Brim Fedora

![Lulu Flat Brim Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/z4/m6ga1dxt54c930bd098b5727782180_400x205.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This brown fedora is an on-trend and affordable choice. It has a wide, flat brim and comes with a matching bow-detailed band. As this hat is made from wool, keep it away from water and only spot clean when necessary. Work a masculine-inspired look and team this hat with a slouchy shirt, cuffed chinos, and brogue shoes.

## 6. Jessica Simpson Feathered Cloche

![Jessica Simpson Feathered Cloche](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/ee/q6ix9siv54c930bdf1b94371984891_400x309.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This season, look for hot hats in unexpected styles such as this cloche hat. This hat has a vintage-inspired design and features feather and button embellishments. It has a moulded design with an asymmetrical brim. Complete the vintage-inspired look and pair this hat with a ladylike dress.

## 7. Topshop Floppy Fedora

![Topshop Floppy Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/kw/hi7tr1fi54c930beee0e4213804261_400x270.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wtyJXi)
Like I mention earlier, the floppy fedora is a firm favourite. If you’re only looking to buy one or two hot hats this season, make sure one of them is a black felt fedora. It is versatile and can easily be styled. One look to try is the pleated skirt and oversized knit option. Complete the look with a pair of combat boots for a grungy twist.

## 8. Nordstrom Asymmetrical Cloche

![Nordstrom Asymmetrical Cloche](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/xg/de3arzk754c930bfd5e43853097364_400x313.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Cloche hats are definitely a more ladylike option. This particular cloche is made from a light speckled wool and features a contrasting grosgrain ribbon band. It also has an asymmetrical brim. It would look great teamed with a silky blouse, high waisted pencil skirt, and Mary-Jane pumps.

Admittedly, not everyone is a ‘hat person’. However, if you’re like me and think that hot hats are a nice way to top off an outfit, then these are just a few options you may want to look at. This season’s hot hats can be found in a range of styles from floppy fedoras to ladylike cloches. Which of these hot hats do you like best?
