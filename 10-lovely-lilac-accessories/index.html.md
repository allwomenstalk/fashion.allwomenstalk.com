# 10 Lovely Lilac Accessories ...

Lilac is such a lovely colour. It hasn’t been given much exposure in the past, but this year I think it’s going to have a fashion ‘moment’. It’s one of the most on-trend colours for the spring/summer months. Get ahead of the pack with these lovely lilac accessories.

## 1. Topshop Norma Lilac Twist Vamp Low Sandals

![Topshop Norma Lilac Twist Vamp Low Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/qi/f419kbhh54c8f53c903b9631063875_400x238.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/VRiFkO)
These leather sandals are the perfect way to incorporate a touch of lilac into your outfit. They feature a twisted front and ankle strap design. I really like the addition of the low wedge – it makes the shoe seem a bit classier. These would look great paired with a white, full-skirted dress.

## 2. Versace Leather Shoulder Bag

![Versace Leather Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ke/zq/hg9wy8uc54c8f53d9c0c1437066819_400x316.jpg)

**Price:** $1395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/c9WPM3)
If you’ve got the cash to burn, then this pretty little bag might be a worthwhile investment. The design is sleek and sophisticated. It features a chunky chain link strap, fold-over flap, and gold hardware. This bag will easily take you from day to night.

## 3. Elke Kramer Pastels Oversized Boulder Bracelet

![Elke Kramer Pastels Oversized Boulder Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/vc/jff9o92n54c8f53e9e494610659913_400x198.jpg)

**Price:** $105.79 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/v15W68)
This oversized statement bracelet is bold and punchy. It’s made from resin and features a ridged edge design. If you like bold accessories, then you should definitely try this one on for size. Wear it alone, or stacked with some clear Perspex bangles.

## 4. Modcloth Having a Heart Time Watch

![Modcloth Having a Heart Time Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/ce/fcbcyeeh54c8f53f5b3ef378702769_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $23.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This heart-shaped watch is so sweet! It is made from synthetic materials and features a stainless steel back. The pastel purple colour and silver watch face complement each other perfectly. It’s perfect for adding a quirky detail to an outfit.

## 5. Ippolita 18-Karat Gold Amethyst Drop Earrings

![Ippolita 18-Karat Gold Amethyst Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/xa/tlksbzfj54c8f540277ab993039826_304x392.jpg)

**Price:** $995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/P0gygd)
These drop earrings are a sophisticated take on the trend. They are made from 18-karat gold and contain teardrop-shaped amethyst stones. These colourful earrings could easily be dressed up or down.

## 6. Topshop Lilac Spot Bud Ankle Socks

![Topshop Lilac Spot Bud Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/a6/qsw67uvy54c8f541020f5767244928_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8jxty4)
These cotton blend socks feature a cute ‘spot bud’ pattern. The delicate scalloped edging is also a pretty design feature. If you’re tempted to try the socks and heels trend, why not pair these with some pretty grey wedges? If not, you can always just wear them around the house!

## 7. Forever21 Leatherette Matelasse Handbag

![Forever21 Leatherette Matelasse Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jp/ca/yj9tn7uo54c8f541da691709841337_328x384.jpg)

**Price:** $25.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This leatherette quilted bag is a modern take on a classic design. It features a fold-over flap, chain-link strap, and twist closure. It’s fully lined and features both interior and exterior zip pockets. Pair this with a slouchy blazer, jeans, and ballet flats for a chic everyday look.

## 8. Topshop Lilac Speckled Ankle Socks

![Topshop Lilac Speckled Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/fh/tzrr044a54c8f542c98ad200026547_267x381.jpg)

**Price:** $10.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HyxRMk)
These speckled knit socks are too good to hide underneath shoes! Their fuss-free design makes them ideal for a range of occasions. Wear them with patent cream brogues or shoes with cut-out details. I also think they’d look good against tan leather.

## 9. Modcloth Salon Sophisticate Necklace

![Modcloth Salon Sophisticate Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/pn/ls5v9prf54c8f543a5a2e346587513_280x384.jpg)

**Price:** $19.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bib necklace is very sophisticated indeed. This necklace is made up of lilac polished stones in a range of shapes and sizes. While most people would be tempted to dress this necklace up, I’d prefer pairing it with a grey marl sweater to create a casually sophisticated look.

## 10. Marie Hélène De Taillac Amethyst Gem Pendant

_ **Price:** $388.73 at brownsfashion.com_
This amethyst pendant is circular in shape and features a faceted face and circular hoop attachment. It’s a subtle and delicate way to add a touch of lilac to your outfit. Wear it alone on a necklace chain, or add it to a charm bracelet.

These are just a selection of lilac accessories out there. Step into any store in the next few months and you’ll surely be able to find a lot more. Will you be getting on the lilac bandwagon?
