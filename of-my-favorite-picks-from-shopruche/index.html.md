# 8 of My Favorite Picks from Shopruche ...

If you've never shopped at the vintage-inspired Ruche online store, then you're missing out! They have the cutest clothes and accessories on the web, and at very reasonable prices! To give you an idea of what you're missing, here are 8 of my favorite picks from shopruche.com.

## 1. Tribal Instincts Maxi Dress

![Tribal Instincts Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/maxi_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $61.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
I love maxi dresses, and this is my all-time favorite! It has such a whimsical carefree look to it, don't you think? You can wear this as you're transitioning from winter to spring pairing it with cardigans for the colder days and baring the shoulders on the warmer ones!

## 2. Lavender Fields Button-down Top

![Lavender Fields Button-down Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/lace_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $36.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html) I'm a girl who is in love with lace! You can dress this up for a night in town, or dress it down for a sweet, everyday look. Whatever you choose to do with it, you're sure to treasure this piece for many years! And hey, it pairs great with the next item I'm listing, except you can't see the back detail...

## 3. Details in the Fabric Cardigan

![Details in the Fabric Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/cardi1_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
As I said, it would make the perfect final layer to the lace top I just mentioned. Ruffles and lace, what more could a girl want...besides a diamond, right? This heather-colored cardi is very comfy, and can be worn with denim or lace - pretty much anything. A sure staple of your closet, I believe!

## 4. Film Noir Fashion Wool Blend Vest

![Film Noir Fashion Wool Blend Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/vest_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Whomever designed this beautiful fashion totally had me in mind! It was love at first sight! A vest like this is so versatile and can be mixed and matched in so many ways! And doesn't that black rose brooch just grab your attention?

## 5. A Touch of Vintage Lace Skirt

![A Touch of Vintage Lace Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/skirt1_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $48.99 at [shopruche.com](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Again, I love lace! This skirt is gorgeous, isn't it! Talk about being a date night sensation! With this skirt, you're sure to turn some heads! It has a zipper closure, but also elastic in the waist band to allow a little extra give-room for that delicious meal!

## 6. By the Docks Straw Hat

## 7. Peacock Trail Print Scarf

![Peacock Trail Print Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/07/8843-300x300_300x300.jpg)

## 8. Pewter Satin Rosey Heels
