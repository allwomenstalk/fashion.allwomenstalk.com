# 7 Unique Tights and ModCloth Tights to Elevate Your Style

Unique tights seem to be all the rage right now, and why shouldn’t they be? They’re bold, beautiful, and the perfect way to express your personality. From intricate floral patterns to whimsical designs, there are so many options to choose from. Many of our favorite picks come from **ModCloth**, a brand known for its quirky and vintage-inspired aesthetic. Whether you're looking for something subtle or a statement piece, these [modcloth tights](https://modcloth.com/collections/tights) are sure to stand out. Here are seven [unique tights](#1) you need in your wardrobe to elevate your style this season.

When styling these bold accessories, consider these quick tips:

- Pair busy patterns with solid-colored dresses or skirts.

- Use tights to add a pop of color to an all-black ensemble.

- Don't be afraid to mix textures, like wool skirts with sheer patterned tights.

## 1. Graceful Garden Tights

![Graceful Garden Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/0z/rsg1svga_600x804.jpg)

Unique tights are fantastic pieces to have in your rotation. Their distinct designs and vibrant colors are perfect for pairing with plain, basic items of clothing. ModCloth’s Graceful Garden Tights are ideal for spring, offering a refreshing floral touch to any outfit. I love the color palette of this item, featuring flowers in shades of pink, purple, orange, blue, and green against a soft tan background. For more inspiration on seasonal styling, check out our guide on [how to style tights for every season](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-tights/).

## 2. You’ve Got Character Tights

![You’ve Got Character Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0t/py/bezb0x1g_600x809.jpg)

Are you a fan of Lewis Carroll’s _Alice in Wonderland_? If so, these "You’ve Got Character" tights are perfect for you. These tights are patterned with iconic characters like the Cheshire Cat, the White Rabbit, card soldiers, and Alice herself. The design is also decorated with whimsical tea cups, tea pots, hearts, diamonds, stars, keys, and pocket watches. They are truly a conversation starter!

## 3. A Tale of New Tights

![A Tale of New Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ad/yj/g60gj1um_600x826.jpg)

ModCloth’s "A Tale of New Tights" is another great pair of unique tights. This item features detailed scenes inspired by the Victorian era. What’s great about these tights is the classic blue and white color scheme, making them easy to match with other blue and white clothing articles. Black would also provide a sharp contrast that highlights the intricate design.

## 4. Bloom of One’s Own Tights

![Bloom of One’s Own Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m7/e6/waby3laa_600x847.jpg)

If you are looking for something a little more subtle, the "Bloom of One’s Own" tights would be ideal. These tights are classy yet unique, keeping it traditional with a black base but spiced up with a bold floral print. Since these tights use neutral colors, you can wear them with almost anything. Keep it sophisticated by pairing them with a little black dress, or add a splash of color with a red, purple, or green skirt. They also look fantastic with high-waist shorts and flats.

## 5. Planetary Fairy Tights

![Planetary Fairy Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/f1/s89q4pwx_600x805.jpg)

These tights are literally out of this world! The Planetary Fairy tights will satisfy your craving for something out-of-the-ordinary. This item features the planets of our solar system in a black, white, and gray color palette. This neutral scheme makes them surprisingly versatile. Take your style to astronomical heights by pairing these with a sleek midi skirt or even denim shorts for a more casual look. Check out our latest [fall fashion trends](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/fall-fashion-trends/) for more ways to incorporate celestial prints.

## 6. Maple Grove Tights in Wine

![Maple Grove Tights in Wine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/zk/twml47ib_600x805.jpg)

This is another great pair of tights that maintains elegance while offering a unique twist. Paisley is a traditional pattern, but the deep maroon or "wine" color is what really sets this pair apart. These would be perfect for autumn, paired with a cozy knitted dress or an oversized sweater. The rich hue adds a layer of warmth and sophistication to your cold-weather wardrobe.

## 7. Riff You Please Tights

![Riff You Please Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c1/v0/d3vrhr96_600x852.jpg)

For those who love an edgy look, the "Riff You Please" tights are a must-have. These are definitely unique, featuring black and sheer horizontal stripes that aren't perfectly straight, giving them a rebellious feel. They would be the perfect companion for a black leather jacket and combat boots. It's a simple way to add some "riff" to your everyday style!
