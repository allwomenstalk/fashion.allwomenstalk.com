# 7 Perfect and Fashionable Winter Coats for the Coldest Weather ...

Fashionable Winter Coats that will keep you warm and look amazing don't have to cost you an arm and a leg! I've never had the money to spend anywhere over $75 on a coat, and so you can rest at ease-I'm a pro at sniffing out fashionable winter coats at affordable prices! I've put together a list of my favorite fashionable winter coats for you to look through today. I love all of these coats because they are cute and they will pull double duty to keep you warm this freezing winter. Keep on reading for the most fashionable winter coats ever!

## 1. Trench Coat W/ Contrast Piping

_ **Price:** $34.80 at forever21.com_
You can't get much more classic then a button down trench coat! I have always loved trench coats, and this one is in the perfect color. Its neutral, so it will go with everything, and it's appropriate for any occasion. This is one of the fashionable winter coats I'm going to stock up on, in different colors of course!

## 2. Fleece Pea Coat

_ **Price:** $27.50 at forever21.com_
This is one of the cutest fashionable winter coats ever! Perfect for adding that pop of color and for summing up your bright personality. I love colorful coats, they always seem to add a ray of sunshine to a drab winter day. What do you think of bright winter coats?

## 3. Faux Fur Collar Coat

_ **Price:** $49.80 at forever21.com_
Looking for something a little more dramatic and sleek? This is the coat for you! The fur collar will add just the right amount of sophistication, and the belt keeps it looking smooth and chic. Black is a great color choice too, since you can wear it with many different outfits.

## 4. Hooded Fleece Coat

_ **Price:** $27.50 at forever21.com_
This is one of the most unexpected colors for fashionable winter coats! Somewhere in between pink, orange, and red, it's the perfect blend of shades for a lovely, glowing, soft rust color. The hood is a nice bonus too, added warmth for those days when you're going to be out and about. Gotta keep warm out there!

## 5. Plaid Woven Coat

_ **Price:** $32.80 at forever21.com_
This is one of the most fashionable winter coats ever! I love the black and white plaid pattern. It adds contrast to any outfit! A red scarf and/or hat would look perfect with this coat too, don't you agree?

## 6. Toggle Knit Jacket

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
The style and cut of this fashionable winter coat almost reminds me of a bomber style jacket, which is high on my list of faves. Bomber jackets just aren't warm enough for the coldest months though! The gray color of this jacket is very sophisticated and will rock with any color you pair it with!

## 7. Plaid Jacket W/ Faux Fur Collar

_ **Price:** $37.80 at forever21.com_
The colors in this fashionable winter coat all go extremely well together! And the fur is simply the icing on the cake. This jacket would be appropriate for daytime wear, or possibly work. It has a very fun vibe and would work for a weekend getaway or shopping trip!

It's so hard for me to pick a favorite from this list of Fashionable Winter Coats! They are all so cute and since I live in a place where it stays cold almost year round, fashionable winter coats are always nice to have on hand. What do you think?
