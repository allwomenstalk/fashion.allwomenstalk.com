# How to Get What You Want by Wearing Mood Affecting Colors ...

You already know that the style of your outfit can change the way the world sees you, but did you know just how important the colors of that outfit are? If you choose the right shades, you'll have an easier time convincing your boss to give you a promotion or your crush to ask you out. That's why you need to fill your closet with as many different shades as you can. Here's how to get what you want by wearing mood affecting colors:

## 1. Blue

![Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f4/wz/cfk4uvlq5504d774e9e37893405927_600x886.jpg)

Blue is not only a calming color, but it's also a convincing color. It's used on most social media sites, like Tumblr and Facebook, as well as many billboards for banks, because it creates the sense of trust and security. So if you're attending an interview or working as a salesman, you should think about wearing blue in order to convince others of your reliability.

## 2. Red

![Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lx/rz/lxv0hpu15504d7a64e185044164310_600x725.jpg)

Red increases heart rate and creates urgency, which is why it's commonly used on discount and sale signs. However, it's also a color that men respond well to, which is why women should wear it whenever they go on a date. It's a great way to get your man's heart pumping.

## 3. Black

![Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/dx/eyutu6bk5504d7ce3b5d4112835944_600x900.jpg)

Black makes you appear powerful, and even aggressive. If you want to be taken seriously, then you should wear an outfit that mostly consists of black. That way, you'll look tougher, and will be taken more seriously than you would've been if you had chosen to wear a floral outfit.

## 4. Purple

![Purple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/tl/v69i3nk05504d814bb62d512773199_500x754.jpg)

Purple is meant to soothe and calm. That's why it's used on most anti-aging products, like wrinkle cream. It's perfect to wear when you're trying to make someone feel better about themselves, because it'll soothe them and help them trust you. So the next time you're helping out a friend in need, put on some purple.

## 5. Green

![Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hg/v9/k370g0c35504d85b168b0751601335_500x689.jpg)

Green is the easiest color for our eyes to process, which is why it does a great job of relaxing us (and is why you should paint your room the color). So if you want to appear casual and relaxed, slip into something green. That way, everyone will see you as someone that they can trust and go to for advice.

## 6. Pink

![Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/uk/tcm7qtpd5504d886a576f977840911_600x830.jpg)

Pink isn't only a feminine color, it's a young color. So if you're feeling self-conscious about your age and want to appear younger, the easiest way to do so is to add some pink into your wardrobe. It'll give you a youthful appearance, which is helpful when you're going out on a date with a younger man you want to impress.

## 7. Orange

![Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/o4/egyzo8nj5504d8ab6eba4794192836_600x900.jpg)

Orange has been proven to stimulate brain activity. That means you should wear it when you need your workers to be on their A game and come up with creative ideas. It's also great to wear when you're taking a test, because you can look down at it when you're stumped.

It's amazing what color can do, isn't it? Just remember that the length of your skirts and the style of your shirts are just as important as the colors of them. All together, they can help you create a look that'll help you get exactly what you want. Do you typically wear the same color all the time, or do you like to switch it up?
