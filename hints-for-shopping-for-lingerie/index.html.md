# 7 Wonderful Tips for Lingerie Shopping like a Star ...

I must confess, I have found it very difficult to shop for lingerie, that is, until I learned these 7 tips for lingerie shopping. If you find yourself dreading going shopping for new lingerie pieces, you will want to read these tips for lingerie shopping that are sure to make your trip more enjoyable, and maybe even fun!

## 1. Plan

All tips for lingerie shopping begin with planning. Know which places you would like to visit. Decide which price range you would like to stay within and which stores will offer you better merchandise for that price.

## 2. Don't Go over

If you have a tight budget, don't try on something over your budget. It's best to not got to Victoria's Secret when you have a Walmart budget. This only leads to tears and woe when you can't afford the beautiful item you have tried on. Don't put yourself through that!

## 3. Know Your Body

Every body is different, and every woman's curves are different. Knowing which styles complement your specific body type before going on your shopping trip will make your "job" easier. This will help to cut down on the try-on's that turn into tears and make every fitting something that will make you feel incredible to be you!

## 4. Know Your Color

Just as every body is different and is complemented with different styles, every body is complemented with certain colors as well. Some skin types look wonderful in red, others black, and still others are complemented better in pastel blues and pinks. By knowing which colors you will look best in, you will cut down on the time involved in finding the ones that are best for you.

## 5. Get a Fitting

Every lady should be professionally fitted for her correct size of bra. Once you know this, it will make sizing for negligee easier as well. You will probably be surprised to learn your correct bra size!

## 6. Relax!

Don't get all uptight and nervous when you're going to shop for new lingerie. Own it, girl! You've got womanly curves that need to be dressed sexy and beautifully! And each and every body is beautiful in its own way. Even if you don't have the "ideal body" (I don't, and who does?) you can still find lingerie that will enhance your femininity. So relax and enjoy!

## 7. Take Clothes

Of course, I want you to actually go shopping fully clothed. However, if you're shopping for a bra for a specific outfit, like, say, a wedding dress, you are going to want to see how your choice of bra fits under your dress.

## 8. Get Help

If you aren't sure what's going to look or fit best, get professional help! Most lingerie shops have staff that have been trained to help you find exactly the right piece for your body type and occasion. Why not use them?

So, there you go! Lingerie shopping will be a breeze with these tips for lingerie shopping. Are you ready to go shopping? I know I am! It's getting about time for some new bras...What about you? Have any tips to add? Please leave me a comment!
