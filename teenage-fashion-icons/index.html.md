# 7 Teenage Fashion Icons ...

Throughout the decades, there have been countless teenage fashion icons. Some last forever, others fade, and some are just on the rise now. While it’s up to you to determine your own style, you can always turn to these teenage fashion icons for a little stylistic inspiration every now and then.

## 1. Audrey Hepburn

![Audrey Hepburn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/f9/zdvl6cga_600x797.jpg)

In her time, Audrey Hepburn was among the most influential teenage fashion icons. But names like Audrey Hepburn rarely fade with time, and her style has become a classic look! You can’t go wrong with a fashion role-model like Audrey Hepburn during your adolescent -or adult- years!

## 2. Mary Kate and Ashley Olsen

![Mary Kate and Ashley Olsen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/kv/cidk89vu_600x742.jpg)

Everyone’s favorite twins! We all adored Mary Kate and Ashley when they starred on Full House as little girls, but now they’re all grown up and taking the world by storm! While their fashion line The Row is too high fashion for most teens’ everyday wear, the young women wear great street style selections that have certainly influenced teen culture! The twins are known for their keen eye for fabulous, boho-chic looks!

## 3. Leighton Meester

![Leighton Meester](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vq/c4/gb9287jt_600x856.jpg)

On Gossip Girl, Leighton’s outfits were always on point! But even off the screen, this fashion icon looks stunning day in and day out! Her look is a bit more put together than Mary Kate or Ashley’s, but is still casual and easy to pull off! It’s no wonder why Leighton has become a teenage fashion icon!

## 4. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/g9/mtghami6_600x827.jpg)

Personally, I think Selena is one of the most adorable celebrities out there! Not only that, but she’s also a great role model for young girls. And as far as her fashion decisions go, they are always spot on! She can pull off virtually anything, and has been inspiring teenage fashion since her days on the Disney Channel!

## 5. Dakota Fanning

![Dakota Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/fd/bqzw6iha_600x902.jpg)

Dakota’s look is a bit edgier than the rest. Like Mary Kate and Ashely, she has that boho look going for her, but she also pulls off a grungy, rock-and-roll style from time to time. What I love most about her style is that she can shift drastically from risky-street style to runway-ready outfits in a flash!

## 6. Kendall and Kylie Jenner

![Kendall and Kylie Jenner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d8/op/tgybtcn0_600x815.jpg)

Another pair of sisters who know how to rock the socks off of the fashion world is the Jenner sisters! Kendall and Kylie never leave the house without looking picture-perfect! While both girls have unique styles, both are modern, chic, and just the right amount of bold! Anyone can pull off their looks, which cause them to be leading fashion icons of this generation.

## 7. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/pm/aakgh36e_600x743.jpg)

Lately, the former Hannah Montana has been the subject of much controversy. But one thing is certain, Miley’s street style matches up with modern dress code almost perfectly! Crop tops, high waisted shorts, plaid shirts, and overalls... which one of these hasn’t been on the racks in the last six months? But Miley puts a unique spin on all of her ensembles, just like you should!

We look up to celebrities for a number of reasons, and just one of them is to pick and choose what we like about their fashion styles. Maybe one of these fashion icons describes your look perfectly, or maybe you’re a mixture of all of them! Which teenage fashion icons do you try to emulate?
