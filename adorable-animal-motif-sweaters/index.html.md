# 8 Adorable Animal Motif Sweaters ...

Animal motif sweaters are a fun and fearless way to wear your love for cute creatures on your sleeve! If you’ve rocked leopard print one too many times this season, consider going the whimsical route with kitty, mouse, bunny, and bear patterns (and more!). Whether you’re planning a shopping trip with the girls or spending a day at the internship, animal motif sweaters can be dressed up or down. Quirky-cool has never been so easy to achieve!
