# 7 Fashionable Sweaters That Will Keep You Comfortable in Air Conditioned Buildings ...

Even though it is hot outside in the summer, you still need fashionable sweaters on hand to keep from freezing in air conditioned buildings. Have you ever stepped into a building in the summer and been greeted with a blast of cold air? This happens to me all the time. To deal with the air conditioning, I always carry a sweater with me, which keeps me from freezing. If you need some fashionable sweaters so you can stay comfortable in air conditioned buildings, the following from Anthropologie will keep you from freezing while also keeping you stylish.

## 1. Moonrise Cardigan

![Moonrise Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f3/td/sduefe8k_410x615.jpg)

The Moonrise Cardigan from Angel of the North is just one of the many fashionable sweaters you will find at Anthropologie. This crocheted cardigan is lightweight and absolutely fabulous. The cutout detail on the back is perfect for summer because it gives the cardigan a light and airy feel.

## 2. Storystitch Cardigan

![Storystitch Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/c3/fvu5h5wu_410x615.jpg)

If you need a casual cardigan with a little attitude, this is the perfect cardigan for you. The loose fit and ruffled edges give this cardigan a laid back vibe. While this cardigan is casual, when you turn around there is a little edge to it. The back has an intricate cutout pattern that gives this cardigan some attitude. You will definitely want to wear this cardigan when you are shopping with your friends in a cold mall.

## 3. Visionary Backpleat Cardigan

![Visionary Backpleat Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/qp/lafz4ub4_410x615.jpg)

The Visionary Backpleat Cardigan has a classic feeling to it that I just love. I also love that it is a little longer, which makes it great for layering over summer dresses. This lovely cardigan comes in three colors, white, green, and gold, all of which are perfect colors for summer.

## 4. Tea Service Cardigan

![Tea Service Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/h6/zpum5m4e_410x615.jpg)

While this cardigan is called the Tea Service Cardigan, it has a more global feeling than an English tea. The black and white print gives this cardigan a global vibe that keeps it from being boring. You could layer this cardigan over just about anything, but a bright dress like the one pictured will make the pattern really pop.

## 5. Buttercup Circle-Stitch Shrug

![Buttercup Circle-Stitch Shrug](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/dk/0d21muvd_410x615.jpg)

The Buttercup Circle-Stitch Shrug is a fabulous cardigan that is very lightweight. The airy crochet will keep you from getting too hot, but it will also keep the air conditioning breeze from freezing you. The buttercup color is so beautiful and perfect for summer. Doesn’t it remind you of a summer flower? It certainly reminds me of a summer meadow. Although, the color of the sweater also has a rich undertone that makes it a great transitional piece when fall rolls around.

## 6. Deluna Cardigan

![Deluna Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/nu/zevhmov8_410x615.jpg)

If you are looking for a unique cardigan, look no further than the Deluna Cardigan. The flower cutouts in the front of this cardigan are stunning and original. Throwing this cardigan over a simple T-shirt elevates the entire look. This cardigan is so fantastic that you may be looking for air conditioned buildings just so you can wear it. Fortunately, you won't have to look too hard because just about every building has air conditioning.

## 7. Fleurs Des Champs Cardigan

![Fleurs Des Champs Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yz/ng/sobcfsx9_410x615.jpg)

Don’t you just adore this cardigan? The floral print is lovely; it gives the cardigan a soft, feminine feel. To keep the cardigan from seeming overly sweet, pair it with shorts. This will give your outfit a nice balance.

If the air conditioning this summer has you shivering, don’t despair. You can choose from any of these stylish cardigans to stay comfortable. Which of these cardigans is your favorite?
