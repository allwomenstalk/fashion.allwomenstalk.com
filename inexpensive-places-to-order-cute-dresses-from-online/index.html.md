# 7 Inexpensive Places to Order Cute Dresses from Online ...

I always find myself scouring the web for places to order cute dresses from, leading to the unfortunate emptying of my bank account. There is something about finding a good deal that leaves me with an adrenaline rush! It is sometimes time-consuming and frustrating to search the internet for inexpensive clothing sites. However, with these places to order cute dresses from on reserve, your luck is bound to change!

## 1. Romwe.com

If you pride yourself on having an eclectic taste in fashion, you will see that Romwe is the one of the best places to order cute dresses from. Equipped with dresses in every style and color, your collection is sure to expand! I love that Romwe designers are not afraid of pairing daring prints and contrasting colors together, proving that fashion is all about taking risks! Free shipping worldwide is also a perk that I plan on enjoying for a long time.

## 2. SimplyBe.com

One of my favorite things about SimplyBe is that this site carries dresses in a variety of plus sizes, a feat that is sadly hard to find in the fashion industry today. Not only does this online store have inexpensive clothing, but my friend (who can't help but shop here everyday) swears that their dresses are made from materials that help promote clothing longevity. There's nothing more horrifying than finally buying that dress that you have been eyeing for quite some time, only for it to lose its elasticity after a few washes!

## 3. BodyCentral.com

The best part about shopping at BodyCentral.com is the amount of sales happening on a weekly basis! Whenever I make a purchase, I always find myself going back to the dress section and coveting other pieces to buy. Shopping for items at such a cheap price is a habit that I am not ready to break!

## 4. GoJane.com

Ladies, trust me when I say that you will not be disappointed by shopping at this wonderful creation. Praised by Seventeen magazine, GoJane is a great place to go if you are looking for inexpensive yet fabulous finds! I always end up finding dresses that I have been pining after (on more expensive sites) for cheap at this fantastic shop.

## 5. UsTrendy.com

I am an absolute lover of indie clothing, which makes every dress offered here that much more appealing! These dresses are created by independent designers, increasing the value behind each article of clothing. There always seems to be some type of sale going on at this site, a fact that you are sure to appreciate!

## 6. Forever 21

Forever 21 is seen as one of the trendiest in-store/online stores around today. With affordable and lovely dresses, this title will not come as a surprise! Browse the flash deals section for incredible steals; almost everything in this store is under $30, a fact that makes me happy until I notice how void of cash my wallet has become!

## 7. Charlotte Russe

You will be amazed by the large selection that this store has to offer! Because their sales are widely known and loved, it is better to avoid the hectic lines by browsing online! I can't complain about their recurring BOGO deal and dresses that start at only $10!

You are certainly not alone in wanting to wear designer clothes without designer prices. With these sites, you won't have to break the bank to dress the way that you want. Are there any other inexpensive sites that you think people should be on the lookout for?
