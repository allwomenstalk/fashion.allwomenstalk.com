# The 7 Best Clothing Hauls for Fall ...

Fall isn't over yet, so you should keep on shopping for the season. After all, you can use most of your fall clothes in the winter, so they won't go to waste. If you're looking for outfit ideas, then you should check out some of the clothing that YouTube stars have bought. After all, they know a thing or two about fashion. Get ready, because here are some of the best clothing hauls for fall:

## 1. Meredith Foster

This video contains a bunch of gorgeous sweaters, long sleeved shirts, and flannel pieces for you to admire. They come from clothing stores we all love, like H&M and Forever 21. That means that everything in this video is actually affordable! So if you like any of the pieces, make sure you head to the store's website to try to find them.

## 2. Zoella

Zoella loves to layer up, so she has some amazing fall fashion ideas. Not only does she show you adorable sweaters and dresses, but she shows you some adorable scarves and socks, too. She knows the importance of the little things. Her items mainly come from places like Urban Outfitters and Top Shop. Those places can get a little pricy, but you never know when you'll find a great deal!

## 3. Bethany Mota

The clothing in this video comes from stores like H&M, Urban Outfitters, and Aeropostale. In it, Bethany shows you her favorite new beanies, skater skirts, and sweaters. She even bought a few crop tops to wear. Even though they aren't meant for fall, she suggests layering them with a few shirts and jackets to create a layered look. You should take her advice in order to reuse all of those gorgeous summer items that you haven't put away yet.

## 4. Ingrid Nilsen

Have you ever heard of Zara and DailyLook? That's where the clothing in this tutorial comes from. They have some adorable sweaters, some colorful tops, and some bold jackets. After all, you don't have to stick to wearing dark, bland colors this season. You can rock something cool and colorful. Don't you want to stand out from the crowd?

## 5. Carli Bybel

Here's one of the best fashion hauls you'll ever see. It contains adorable pieces from Forever 21, Missguided, ASOS, and a few other lesser known stores. The video will show you outfits that are perfect for wearing to school, while lounging around with friends, or while out at a party. They're super versatile! This video even has some coupon codes, so you might be able to get a good discount on a product you love!

## 6. Marzia

This clothing haul includes more than clothes. It also shows some adorable shoes. If you love conservative, minimalist clothing, then you're going to love Marzia's style. She knows how to rock that innocent look while still looking hot.

## 7. Bethany Mota

Bethany is everyone's favorite, so here's another clothing haul from her. She shows adorable items from Forever 21, H&M, and Urban Outfitters. That's why you know they're going to be cute!

If you steal ideas from these YouTubers, you'll have a closet to be proud of. So don't hold back on that shopping spree. You know you want to go on one! Which lady's style do you love the most?
