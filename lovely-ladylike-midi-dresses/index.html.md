# 7 Lovely Ladylike Midi Dresses ...

Midi Dresses are a stylish choice this season. Midi lengths are a demure and stylish alternative to short and maxi length hemlines. This ladylike look has been seen all over the catwalks and continues to dominate the clothing store racks. Ladylike dressing is a popular trend these days, and midi dresses are one easy way to achieve this look. Take inspiration from a few lovely midi dresses below.

## 1. Topshop Limited Edition Scattered Bead Maxi Dress

![Topshop Limited Edition Scattered Bead Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/el/yj2nqtxx54c932e20ccab640700880_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/D7pgP3)
This beaded maxi dress has a vintage appeal to it. It comes in a stylish peach colour and has a scattered embellished bodice. The demure cut of the dress balances out the boldness of the embellishments. Try this dress on with some neutral ballet flats and a framed cream handbag.

## 2. Topshop Cream Spot Midi Dress

![Topshop Cream Spot Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n3/oz/rri1a2xx54c932e2e38b4106877118_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/cR4Xcn)
Anything spotted is going to be a hit this season. So the combination of spots and midi dresses is bound to be a winning combination. This dress has a short sleeved design, gathered skirt, and navy polka dot pattern. This dress would look great worn with tights, Mary Jane heels, and a burgundy wool coat.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Galena Embroidered Silk-Chiffon Dress

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Galena Embroidered Silk-Chiffon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/eu/a66zx8ra54c932e3c1f46191788624_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $700.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Z3LgMT)
Straight from the runways, this Marc by Marc Jacobs dress has a very ladylike feel to it. The colourful leaf embroidery, however, is a fun little detail. This dress has a vintage-inspired pussy-bow neck tie, turn-up cuffs, and front chest pockets. Style this semi-sheer piece with a cute socks and heels combination, colourful side bag, and a cropped blazer.

## 4. Cacharel Long Wool Jersey Dress

![Cacharel Long Wool Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/xq/tp5yyoax54c932e48dc01980815703_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This wool jersey dress has a very clean, streamlined design. It is quite vintage-inspired but with a modern feel to it. It has a mock neck, slightly flared skirt, and seam detail at the waist. The neutral taupe grey colour is ideal for accessorising. Add a bejewelled collar necklace, patent clutch, and low pumps for a chic take on the trend.

## 5. Topshop Midi Chiffon Dress

![Topshop Midi Chiffon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/bz/gb7o1cdt54c932e559030311184363_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IZF897)
Sometimes less is more when it comes to midi dresses. This wine coloured dress is well suited to fall/winter dressing. It has a sleeveless design, high neckline, and gathered skirt. Dress it up with a cropped black blazer and pumps, or style it down with a chunky knit, brogues, and floppy fedora.

## 6. Steven Alan Cynthia Printed Silk-Georgette Dress

![Steven Alan Cynthia Printed Silk-Georgette Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/a6/szx47nmq54c932e65deaf488561528_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $385.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/CWYtKH)
Floral designs are always an option for ladylike dressing. This is also the case when it comes to midi dresses. This dress has a multi-coloured floral design, round neck, and gathered skirt. Team it with a neutral leather jacket, suede ankle boots, and slouchy bag for a casually cool look.

## 7. Roland Mouret Stretch-Crepe Midi Dress

![Roland Mouret Stretch-Crepe Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/q6/yw9xgq0y54c932e76f3ff585255444_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $2295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/a0zawu)
This dress has a timeless design and comes in a feminine lipstick-red colour. It has cap sleeves, artful pleat detailing, and a V-shaped back. This elegant number could easily be worn to work or for a classy night out. Take a cue from this elegant design when choosing midi dresses this season.

Midi dresses will still be in style for a while to come, so now is the perfect time to dip your toes into the trend. A basic style is a great first option, and can be styled up or down depending on the occasion. Will you be wearing midi dresses this season?
