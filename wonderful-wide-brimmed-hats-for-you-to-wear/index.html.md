# 7 Wonderful Wide Brimmed Hats for You to Wear ...

Wide brimmed hats can bring glamour and intrigue to your outfits. A stylish wide brimmed hat is must have in any summer wardrobe. It can be worn at the beach or in the city, and is a fashionable way to stay sun smart in the summertime. Make sure you’re covered this summer and take a look at the following fashionable hats with wide brims.

## 1. Polka Dot Ribbon Floppy Hat

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_
This sweet, vintage-inspired number is the perfect way to try your hand at the wide brimmed hats trend. It has a woven ‘natural’ finish and features a polka dot ribbon that ties into a bow at the back. This hat would look great worn with a drop waist navy dress and brogues for a truly vintage-inspired look.

## 2. Pinched Front Floppy Hat

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_
Channel your inner celebrity with this floppy black that. It has a pinched front, teardrop crown, and the wide brim is perfect for keeping the paparazzi out of your face. It also features a grosgrain ribbon trim with bow accent. Work a stylish off-duty look by teaming this hat with a black button-down shirt, dark denim jeans, and blush ballet flats.

## 3. Colour Block Floppy Hat

![Colour Block Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_colour-block-floppy-hat_600x395.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8y9s90)
This ginger and black wide brimmed hat is truly chic. The design is elegant and you could easily imagine it being worn by the likes of style icons such as Audrey Hepburn. For a polished summer look, pair this hat with a lightweight black sundress, patent sandals, and oversized sunglasses.

## 4. Saint-Tropez Hat

![Saint-Tropez Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_saint-tropez-hat_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sorbet colours are a favourite for the summer months, and you can make the most of them with this stylish hat. It has a loose weave design with pale pink, blue, and yellow interwoven strands. This hat would look super cute worn with a similarly pastel dress.

## 5. Striped Floppy Hat

_ **Price:** $13.80 at forever21.com_
You can’t go wrong with classic stripes and wide brimmed hats are an unusual yet stylish place to find them. Add a dose of colour to your outfit with this pink and cream striped hat. It’s perfect for packing away on vacation and wearing by the pool with your new retro-inspired bikini.

## 6. Natural Bow Lurex Floppy Hat

![Natural Bow Lurex Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_natural-bow-lurex-floppy-hat_600x353.jpg)

**Price:** $21.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you prefer classic, simple styles when it comes to your wide brimmed hats then take a look at this number. It has a natural straw-look finish and features a lurex stripe on the brim and a bow detail. This hat is versatile enough to be worn with a range of outfits, but I think it would look especially chic with a floaty maxi dress and embellished sandals.

## 7. Straw Band Floppy Hat

![Straw Band Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_straw-band-floppy-hat_600x381.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/XpXUTr)
This natural straw hat features a contrasting grosgrain band. The overall design is quite a classic style and you could easily imagine it being worn in a cottage garden. For a vintage schoolgirl inspired look, team this wide brimmed hat with a crisp white shirt dress and tan loafers.

These are just some ideas for wide brimmed hats this summer. Wear them with your hair out or in a low, loose braid for an easy and effortless outfit update. Will you be donning a wide brimmed hat this summer?
