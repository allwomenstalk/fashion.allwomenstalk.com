# 8 Crisp White Dresses for Summer ...

White dresses for summer are a sartorially chic choice. Warm weather trends call for fresh, light colour palettes and you can’t beat a nice white dress for summer. A crisp white dress is the perfect blank canvas for working a range of accessory trends from neon colour to shiny metallics to technicoloured tribal. Take a look at a few stylish white summer dresses below.

## 1. Topshop Crochet Sleeveless Shirt Dress

![Topshop Crochet Sleeveless Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-crochet-sleeveless-shirt-dress_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ZOxw7G)
This sleeveless shirt dress has a vintage-inspired design. It has a v-neck collar, gathered waist, and features all-over crochet detailing. This white dress for summer is perfect for working a feminine look. Team it with your everyday jewellery and maybe some glitter ballet pumps for a quirky touch.

## 2. J.Crew Eyelet Embellished Dress

![J.Crew Eyelet Embellished Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_j-crew-eyelet-embellished-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $230.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/u0C9iG)
Exaggerated eyelet detailing is an on-trend choice this season. It’s a great way of incorporating cutout-style detailing into a design, but in a much more demure way. This white dress is fun and flirty, and would look great worn with a colour popping bag.

## 3. Modcloth ‘Fame, Set, Match’ Dress

![Modcloth ‘Fame, Set, Match’ Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_modcloth-fame-set-match-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $147.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
White summer dresses that have a structured silhouette are a nice change from the typical free-flowing bohemian style dresses that tend to dominate the season’s fashion trends. This crisp white dress is perfect for working a more polished silhouette. It would look great worn with patent ballet flats or penny loafers and a framed handbag.

## 4. Topshop Strappy Split Maxi Dress

![Topshop Strappy Split Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_topshop-strappy-split-maxi-dress_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/5BgP7S)
Maxi silhouettes are still as popular as ever, so try working this trend for white summer dresses. This Topshop dress has a fairly minimal design and features daring side splits. It’s the kind of summer dress you’d slip on for a casual, carefree look. For that extra ‘oomph’ try styling it with a metallic collar necklace or cuff bracelets.

## 5. Forever21 Ponte Knit Dress

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
If you’re after a more basic style when it comes to white summer dresses, you can’t go past a fitted knit dress. This one has a round neckline, cap sleeves, and traditional waistline. The exposed zipper at the back also gives it a more contemporary touch. This dress also comes with a basic black belt, but I’d try switching it to a coloured one for something a bit more summery.

## 6. Vanessa Bruno Athé Cotton-Poplin Dress

![Vanessa Bruno Athé Cotton-Poplin Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_vanessa-bruno-ath-cotton-poplin-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $285.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/IQU4GD)
This white dress for summer could easily become a high rotation piece. It has a fitted top, flared skirt, and lace trims detailing. It’d look great worn with tan sandals and colourful beaded jewellery.

## 7. Modcloth ‘Billowing Beauty’ Dress

![Modcloth ‘Billowing Beauty’ Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_modcloth-billowing-beauty-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $62.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after a dress with a more relaxed silhouette, then try this one out. It has a simple slip on design and features delicately stitched embellishments across the front. The handkerchief hem also gives it a more free-flowing look.

## 8. Dress up by Topshop Skater Dress

![Dress up by Topshop Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/8_dress-up-by-topshop-skater-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pGYFjF)
White summer dresses can often appear quite angelic. If you want one with an edgier design, this Topshop number features daring sheer panels at the front and back. Top off the look by teaming this dress with ankle boots and bold jewellery.

There are an infinite number of ways to style a white dress for summer. They’re the perfect blank canvas for you to experiment with a range of trends and looks. Which of these white dresses for summer do you prefer?
