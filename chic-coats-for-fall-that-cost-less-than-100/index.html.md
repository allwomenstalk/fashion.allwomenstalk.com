# Chic Coats for Fall That Cost Less than $100 ...

As the cooler weather starts to draw in, we know that soon we'll need to start covering up - and what better way to keep the chill off than with a chic new coat? Buying some new items for your fall wardrobe is a lovely compensation for having to wave goodbye to summer. And what's more, you don't need to pay a fortune for your coat either. Here are some lovely coats for under $100 …

## 1. Mint Biker Jacket

![Mint Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/om/grb4i5ad55fa11ab72afe179389850_600x766.jpg)

The classic biker jacket comes in all sorts of colors and fabrics these days, so fashionistas can easily update their fall wardrobe with one like this lovely mint green jacket. It's a wool mix, which is ideal for keeping the chill off on cooler evenings. Wear with a dress and ankle boots for some edgy style.

## 2. Print Waterfall Jacket

![Print Waterfall Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/z0/ko02mgn855fa11ad5e927535728830_600x766.jpg)

If you're not ready to let go of that summer festival feel yet, this is the jacket for you. The Aztec print and fringed edging definitely has that carefree summer vibe to it. It's the perfect lightweight cover-up for those cooler days, and the waterfall front gives it a different look from the usual fall coats.

## 3. Boyfriend Blazer

![Boyfriend Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/i7/izqehfru55fa11add7ad5768122273_600x766.jpg)

Wondering what to wear with your skinny jeans? This long boyfriend blazer would look fabulous with them. It's given an extra edge with the contrast trim on the pockets and the matching buttons. The pale grey color is ideal if you want a neutral coat but don't want a dark shade like black or navy.

## 4. Duster Coat

![Duster Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sy/xz/tj50d6d255fa11ae0a46f266505980_600x766.jpg)

It seems like every other day Kim K is seen in another duster coat from her ample wardrobe. Maybe she's onto something with her fondness for this style. Duster coats are the perfect cover-up as the summer fades into fall. Blush pink is also very chic and a very flattering neutral shade.

## 5. Wrap Coat

![Wrap Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/uv/yqm7nk6855fa11ae4fc38532611813_600x766.jpg)

If fall where you live tends to be chillier, then you need a coat that's a bit thicker than a duster coat or boyfriend blazer. A wrap coat could be just right for you. And girls who take a larger size don't have to miss out on a chic cover-up; this particular coat comes from a plus-sized range. Whatever your size, there's a wrap coat out there with your name on it!

## 6. Checked Poncho

![Checked Poncho](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/zm/vkavomz255fa11ae3aff2845088095_600x766.jpg)

If you want a coat that's warm enough to keep out the chill, but light enough so that you don't overheat, consider treating yourself to a poncho. This particular one even has a turtle neck to keep out the chill. Styled with a pair of boots and a fedora, you'll look cool and stylish. It's the kind of thing an off-duty model would wear, so steal some of their style for yourself.

## 7. Textured Jacket

![Textured Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/fd/i14lt7h655fa11af81661737722097_600x766.jpg)

This is the kind of jacket that just cries out to be worn with a crisp white shirt. The marl knit fabric gives it a interesting look and means that it can be worn with different tones. It's smart enough to wear with a dress for work, and casual enough to dress down with jeans. This jacket would be a versatile and chic addition to your wardrobe, so look for a textured coat or jacket for fall.

So even on a budget of $100 you can find plenty of gorgeous, chic coats for fall!
