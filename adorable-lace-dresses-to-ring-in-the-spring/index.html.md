# 7 Adorable Lace Dresses to Ring in the Spring ...

I love lace dresses, they’re beautiful and feminine and a perfect way to ring in the spring! The best part is they come in a variety of styles, and they're perfect for any occasion! Since I’m so ready for the winter to be over, I’ve put together a list of 7 adorable lace dresses to ring in the spring!

## 1. Plunge-V Back Cutout Lace Dress from PGFANCY

![Plunge-V Back Cutout Lace Dress from PGFANCY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/om/ga/fxbta03j_500x500.jpg)

Subtle in the front, dramatic in the back, this is one of the sexier lace dresses for the spring! I love that it has long sleeves too, making it perfect for this time of year when it’s just warming up! Plus it’s at a reasonable price and comes in white too! This would be a perfect little number for date night!

## 2. Ernestina Lace Dress from Diane Von Furstenberg

![Ernestina Lace Dress from Diane Von Furstenberg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/p2/yd/mozcx66j_391x448.jpg)

This little number is such a perfect mixture of sweet and sexy. It’s one of the most unique lace dresses I’ve ever seen, and I love the dimensions of this dress. This is perfect for a formal occasion, and it’s really comfortable as well! The thing with lace is that you worry it may be too revealing in some spots, but this is perfectly proportioned!

## 3. Audrey's Top of the a-Line Dress in Lace from Modcloth

![Audrey's Top of the a-Line Dress in Lace from Modcloth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/mo/dt/hkfud385_600x857.jpg)

Perfectly understated, this is one of those dresses that is perfect for those of you who are just getting used to wearing this material. It’s classy, elegant, and totally channels your inner Audrey Hepburn!

## 4. One Shoulder Mini Dress from Donna Mizani

![One Shoulder Mini Dress from Donna Mizani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/br/t4/gmnynchx_599x890.jpg)

This is one of the prettiest lacy dresses I’ve seen, and I love that it's a little bit longer. The one sleeve and bare shoulder add a great dimension. I love that it’s slimming and flattering at the same time, and the off the shoulder collar is a wonderful addition!

## 5. The Standard Lace Dress from Suboo

![The Standard Lace Dress from Suboo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/r6/yc/iy6m8gev_599x890.jpg)

This is one of those lace dresses that is definitely for your inner sultry goddess. I love the fact that it's all covered up at the top, but gives a little peek-a-boo lace at the bottom. It's gorgeous!

## 6. Serenity Maxi Dress Karina Grimaldi

![Serenity Maxi Dress Karina Grimaldi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zj/xv/h7/abx29hqk_599x897.jpg)

A lot of people have weddings in the spring, and something tells me that that is a perfect number for such an occasion. I love long lace dresses, and the best thing about this one is that it’s elegant and sexy all at the same time. The thing with lace is that you need to find the right balance; too much and you’ll look like a doily, but with just the right amount, you’ll look stunning!

## 7. Kloss up Dress from Three Floor

![Kloss up Dress from Three Floor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/tm/hz/0uuxjtax_599x893.jpg)

Lace dresses are a great way to add a block of colour into your wardrobe, and this one is a perfect way to ring in the spring! Whether or not you’re just going out with the girls, or if you have a weekend date, this dress is sure to impress!

Come the warmer season, I say we should all celebrate with gorgeous lace dresses! Lace is fun, it’s floral, and best of all it comes it all shapes, sizes and colours! So far, it’s still al little cold out for me here in Toronto to wear a dress, but I can promise you that once it warms up, I’ll be in a little dress! Tell me, what are some of your favourite pieces to ring in the spring with?
