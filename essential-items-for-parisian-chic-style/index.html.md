# 7 Essential Items for Parisian Chic Style ...

Paris is one of the major fashion capitals of the world, so it’s no wonder this cool city has an abundance of fashion forward ladies wandering its streets. However, no need to worry if you don’t live in Paris or if you haven’t even visited, there is still a way to achieve your very own Parisian chic style. While Parisians are indeed superbly styled and put together, they are also timeless and elegant. Above all, Parisians have mastered the art of [effortless dressing](https://allwomenstalk.com/the-secrets-of-french-chic-fashion-and-materials/) with sophisticated and classic pieces that will last a lifetime when it comes to trends. To become as equally skilled in this art incorporate these essential items for Parisian chic style.

## 1. Classic Trench Coat

![Classic Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_classic-trench-coat_403x520.jpg)

A timelessly elegant trench coat is an essential in every girl’s wardrobe. A classic trench is not only an investment piece, but it is also the first basic item when trying to re-create that famous Parisian chic style. It provides structure and simple clean lines with a traditional tailored feel that defines Parisian style. Trench coats are functional and stylish, making them the ultimate utilitarian piece in a fashionista’s closet.

## 2. Striped Shirt

![Striped Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_striped-shirt_230x345.jpg)

Effortlessly cool is the striped long sleeve top that will not only keep you warm on cool Paris nights but also uber-chic. If you want to update this traditional black and white stripe design, you can always opt for pieces with detail that will make you stand out from the crowd. Try looking for striped shirts with a different colour stripe like red or even a bright blue and defining elements like this shirts exaggerated shoulders and high neckline.

## 3. Quilted Handbag

![Quilted Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_quilted-handbag_600x403.jpg)

What Parisian style would be complete without a quilted handbag a la Chanel. Parisian chic is all about minimal design but ultra-luxe pieces. Whether it’s rich material, lustrous hardware or classically tailored items, Parisian style aims for quality not quantity. A Chanel handbag, especially the vintage 2.55, includes all these details in one pretty package. I don’t condone fakes, but if you aren’t willing to break the bank for this famous bag, use the basic bone structure of this one for inspiration when picking one most suitable to your lifestyle. A square or rectangular shaped shoulder bag with sleek hardware and polished leather will add that Parisian finishing touch you’re looking for.

## 4. Cropped Denim

![Cropped Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_cropped-denim_600x800.jpg)

Denim is a simple and elementary way to stay cool and contemporary in any season. The right pair of cropped jeans that fits to your body is understatedly sexy while still remaining sophisticated. Darker wash denim is perfect when it comes to achieving Parisian chic style, as deeper denim tones tend to look more luxe and dressier than lighter wash jeans.

## 5. Neck Scarf

![Neck Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_neck-scarf_235x400.jpg)

Smart accessories like a neck scarf add a touch of refined ladylike appeal to any outfit, but Parisian style scarves are essential for feminine flair. Parisian style is not overly fussy or exuberant so a charming accessory like a silk scarf allows you to play with colour, pattern and print in a small way that won’t overpower the rest of your outfit. Opt for scarves that will pop against your minimalist look for a true standout statement.

## 6. Tortoise Shell Sunglasses

![Tortoise Shell Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_tortoise-shell-sunglasses_585x324.jpg)

Oversized sunglasses are always super chic (if they suit your face shape) and can be a simple way to work an accessory into your outfit if you are not a fan of accessories or the neck scarf. A tortoise shell print is vintage inspired especially if worn in a cat-eye shape. Round shaped sunglasses usually suit a variety of face shapes and a cat-eye design is flirty and feminine, defining the very essence of the coquettish city of Paris.

## 7. Pointed Pumps

![Pointed Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/7_pointed-pumps_449x400.jpg)

Sleek and sophisticated pointed pumps add an edge to your look when worn at sky-high heights. A true Parisian woman is never without her pumps, even adding the most basic of heels to your look can elevate your style immensely. A pair of patent leather pointed pumps offers high gloss shine for a refined polish yet sexy edged final touch.

Simple, effortless, classic and timeless are the defining elements of true Parisian chic style. Whether it is a tailored trench coat or a classic Chanel handbag, Parisians are well versed in the art of cool sophisticated dressing and all you need to do is mix in these essential items into your existing wardrobe to achieve this chic style. Which piece do you think defines Parisian style the most? How will you do Parisian chic?
