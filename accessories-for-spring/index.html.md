# 10 Accessories for Spring 2011 ...

Spring is almost here and it’s time to spiff up your accessories inventory. This season jewelry fashions are not conservative whereas apparel fashions are somewhat conservative. Accessorize fashionably with the 10 following tips.

## 1. Stretch Rings

![Stretch Rings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/cm/k42rq80k54c8f6624264e595341416_335x331.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stretch rings with gigantic stones and floral designs are huge this spring. This year it’s not about carats of gold but how much size and glam you can fit on your hand.

## 2. Jean Jackets/Vests

![Jean Jackets/Vests](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/if/f59mvia954c8f6635628e423195291_378x400.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Jean jackets are in again for the first time since the 80’s. Denim vests and jackets will be seen with skirts, slacks and even shorts this spring.

## 3. Sundresses

![Sundresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/zw/iut1vj9n54c8f66468500329712552_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sundresses range from classy to casual; they are cool on hot summer days and are a great way to show off a nice tan.

## 4. Cowboy Boots

![Cowboy Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/tq/ovxmn3ky54c8f6657069e959846832_445x398.jpg)

**Price:** $55.87 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Cowboy boots have traditionally been a winter fashion staple however this spring they are HOT! Jazz up your sundress with a pair of cowboy boots for the perfect trendy look.

## 5. Beaded Clothing

![Beaded Clothing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r6/fj/atreacrn54c8f66666a7b797978083_400x392.jpg)

**Price:** $19.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This spring beaded apparel is very popular for both women’s and men’s fashion. Stylish men’s clothing will be embellished with studded gems. Women’s clothing will be decorated with traditional style beads and gem studs.

## 6. Biker Gear

![Biker Gear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/bn/a458a39h54c8f6674df0e124363333_292x392.jpg)

**Price:** $89.95 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Leather and lace, biker chick sexy is in. Don satin, leather and lace for a glamorous yet edgy look.

## 7. Sunglasses

![Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/ea/jen2o9c754c8f6682a8be190583266_400x300.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This year accessories are big, literally. From jewelry to shoes accessories are super-sized. Pick large sized sunglasses in bright colors to coordinate with your handbag

## 8. Ankle Jewelry

![Ankle Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/ng/gtd4f1fa54c8f669149c9235074235_400x400.jpg)

**Price:** $10.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This year’s trendy ankle jewelry is bright and bold. Stretchy fabric of rubber band type materials that stretch or buckle are the best choices.

## 9. Coverage

![Coverage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/ns/eoqpkpdm54c8f669e4f59773964427_247x400.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Letting it all hang out is out, coverage is in. This year it is considered glamorous to cover up and leave something to the imagination.

## 10. Shoes

![Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/y1/twizho7y54c8f66ae66cb267742270_370x348.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Platform footwear is the thing this season; wearing any type from platform stilettos to strappy platforms will be the right stylish touch for your look.

It’s easy to be passionate about fashion; take time to care about how you look. Remember, everything you wear is making a statement about what and who you are. What does your look say about you?
