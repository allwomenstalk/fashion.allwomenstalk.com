# 7 Reasons Were Loving Kelly Brooks Lingerie Range ...

What do you think of Kelly Brook’s lingerie range? If you’re anything like me, you’ll be totally in love with it. There’s a whole range of fabrics, textures and designs, and for once it encompasses quite a good size range – Kelly Brook’s lingerie range goes from cup size A to cup size H, although the smaller sizes can be much harder to track down. So, here’s what we’re lusting after. What would you love?

## 1. Red Hot

![Red Hot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/wr/gf/v7sut45z_600x918.jpg)

Think Kelly Brook’s lingerie range is going to be expensive? Think again. The model teamed up with New Look to create luxurious pieces without the luxurious price tag – and she even claims to have worn the prototypes herself, to ensure comfort and great fit. This red set certainly does just that – the bra is supportive and comfortable, and the pants are designed to avoid VPL and be super comfy.

## 2. Classic Cool

![Classic Cool](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zx/mv/p3/vcfalaov_567x850.jpg)

Love stockings and suspenders? You’re not alone. Kelly always sneaks a touch of retro glam into her collections, and this is a wonderful example. Many sets consist of vintage-themed bras, pants, suspender belts and suspenders, so you are sure to feel a million dollars in them. These items aren't scratchy, either, which some of the cheaper pieces usually are.

## 3. Tasteful Teddies

![Tasteful Teddies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hi/xz/zr/pho9x4ku_600x801.jpg)

Want a bit more coverage for your tummy? Teddies are perfect for hiding stomachs whilst keeping you looking, and feeling, amazing. This vintage themed one is perfect – the lace is oh-so-retro, and the front paneling gives it a definite showgirl finish, too. For extra oomph, try teaming it with heels, and covering it with a silk dressing gown.

## 4. Dotty for Spots

![Dotty for Spots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/pi/qh/pphlbt7m_600x900.jpg)

If you prefer more casual lingerie collections, you’ll adore this two piece. Not only does the top feature real support, ensuring that it’s both flattering and comfortable, but it’s silky and luxurious, too. The spots and lace look amazing together, and who doesn’t look great in monochrome?! It’s perfect for wearing to cheer yourself up, as lounge wear, sleep wear, to impress someone special or just because.

## 5. LBD

![LBD](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/m6/ms/x62a89aa_600x899.jpg)

This is a really cute LBD, but I wouldn’t recommend making like Kelly and wearing it out in public! From the silky fabric to the lace top and short length, it’s uber sexy, and totally perfect for the bedroom. Outside, you’d be likely to catch a cold! Glam it up around the house by contrasting the black silk with red lipstick, and adding heels.

## 6. White Wonder

![White Wonder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/fa/m9/u1ifd4e3_590x848.jpg)

Want something subtle yet gorgeous to wear under work clothes, white shirts and cute sundresses? This is it. The bra is detailed enough to avoid being plain, and the lacy pants are super cute. It’s all very comfortable, too, even if you’ve got a large bust. It’s your go-to bra and pants set.

## 7. Sexy Swimwear

![Sexy Swimwear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/st/ji/qez7hiag_600x804.jpg)

Kelly Brook’s lingerie range isn’t the only collection worth noting. She’s got an amazing swimwear collection, too! From retro floral one-pieces to vintage frills bought up to date with neon colors and bikinis covered in bold prints, there’s something for everyone, and you can be sure your body will be looking its best. That’s not to mention this cute bikini, either. Definitely worth checking out.

So what do you think of Kelly Brook’s lingerie range? Have you tried anything from it? I’d love to know whether you're rating or hating it – and what you’d like to see her design next!
