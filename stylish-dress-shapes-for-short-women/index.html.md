# 7 Stylish Dress Shapes for Short Women ...

Dress shapes for short women can vary quite a lot when it comes to what’s flattering and what’s not. I’m sure we’ve all heard talks about what styles suit certain body shapes. But when it really comes down to it you’ll find it’s all about experimenting with what works for you. Take a look at the following dress shapes for shorter women and how to make them work for you.

## 1. Shift Dress

The shift dress is a popular and timeless style of dress. It has a generally universal appeal, making it suitable for most body shapes. When it comes to dress shapes for short women, the shift dress is perfect for showing off lean legs and a slim frame.

## 2. A-Line Dress

Dresses with A-line skirts tend to be more forgiving than figure hugging numbers. A dress that’s nipped in at the waist will also help give the illusion of longer legs and a taller silhouette. A short A-line dress is a flirty option, but longer hemlines that fall just above or below the knees are also flattering options.

## 3. Bodycon Dress

If you’ve got the figure for it, a body hugging dress can make you appear taller (if that’s what you’re after). When looking at dress shapes for short women, a body hugging dress can give the appearance of a tighter, leaner, and longer silhouette.

## 4. Shirt Dress

The shirt dress is a great style of dress for those on the shorter side of things. If you’ve got a slim frame you can easily get away with wearing it loose. However, if you want to create or accentuate curves, simply belt it in at the waist to create shape.

## 5. Empire Waist Dress

The empire waist dress can help create the illusion of height. Since it’s fitted right underneath the bust, it creates the appearance of a raised waist and longer legs. When wearing an empire waist dress, keep it fairly streamlined and avoid anything too puffy as this can overwhelm your silhouette.

## 6. Wrap Dress

The wrap dress is often hailed as a miracle dress and one that’s fit for all shapes and sizes. For short women, it’s the combination of the V-neckline and fitted waistline that makes it a winner.

## 7. Maxi Dress

Yes, you can wear that maxi dress! There seems to be this idea that maxi skirts and dresses aren’t for shorter women, but this couldn’t be further from the truth. When it comes to dress shapes for short women, avoid anything with too much volume and stick to maxis that are more fitted and streamlined. To get the right length, either shop petite styles or get them hemmed to fit.

Of course, there’s no real right or wrong answer when it comes to getting dressed. Hopefully this will help as an ideas guide when looking at dress shapes for short women. What dress shape for short women would you recommend, and how would you wear it?
