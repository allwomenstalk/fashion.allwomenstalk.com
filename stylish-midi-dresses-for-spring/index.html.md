# 9 Stylish Midi Dresses for Spring ...

Midi dresses are still as popular and as stylish as ever. They’re perfect if you want a longer dress but aren’t ready to commit to the floor-sweeping lengths of a maxi. Midi dresses can look anything from ladylike and feminine to daring and vampy, depending on the style of the dress you pick out. Hop on board the midi dress train and check out these stylish midi dresses for spring.

## 1. Striped Midi Dress

![Striped Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/1_striped-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Stripes and monochrome are two big trends for spring. A bold striped dress is a stylish option. Wear it during the day with a lightweight jacket and sneakers and dress it up at night with some metallic jewellery and stiletto heels. This Topshop dress will set you back a little over $50 but you can also find a range of striped midi dresses at Forever 21 and Dorothy Perkins.

## 2. Draped Shirt Dress

![Draped Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/2_draped-shirt-dress_600x900.jpg)

Spring is the perfect time to bust out the neutral and pastel colours. A relaxed shirt dress is perfect for creating a smart daytime look. This dress by OAK is pretty expensive at $275 but you can find similar, more affordable styles at places like ASOS. Team a shirt dress with strappy sandals and a floppy hat for a chic spring look.

## 3. Floral Midi Dress

![Floral Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/3_floral-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Pretty floral dresses and spring seem to go hand in hand. A light midi dress with a simple floral pattern can become a wardrobe staple. A floral dress teamed with a light denim jacket and tan shoes just screams spring dressing. This dress by Girl by Band of Outsiders will definitely burn a hole in your pocket at over $600 but you could always try Modcloth and ASOS for more affordable versions.

## 4. Strappy Beaded Midi Dress

![Strappy Beaded Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/4_strappy-beaded-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Shake off those winter blues by adding some colour to your wardrobe. A brightly coloured sun dress, like this Topshop number, will definitely put a spring in your step. Wear a bright dress with flat sandals and a neutral clutch for a cool, everyday look. Check out ASOS and Piperlime for similar strappy dresses.

## 5. Lace Midi Dress

![Lace Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/5_lace-midi-dress_600x732.jpg)

Lace dresses are always a popular option. This spring, go for a more bohemian inspired look with your lace dress. This lace midi dress costs around $30 at Forever 21 and is one of those dresses that you could just throw on and walk out the door in. Urban Outfitters and Motel are also places you could look for breezy lace dresses.

## 6. Polka Dot Midi Dress

![Polka Dot Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/6_polka-dot-midi-dress_600x899.jpg)

Anything polka dotted is in fashion right now. A simple polka dotted dress is a stylish way to stay on top of trends this spring. Team a monochrome spot dress with a bright red lip for a sassy, feminine look. This dress from Topshop is pretty pricey at $96 but you can find similar, more affordable styles at stores like Dorothy Perkins and Forever 21.

## 7. Printed Tank Midi Dress

![Printed Tank Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/7_printed-tank-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Work a cool and contemporary look with a graphic printed dress. Colourful abstract or geometric prints are your best bets this spring. This Topshop dress would look great worn with a bomber jacket, hi-top sneakers and a leather cap for a sport luxe inspired look. Also check out ASOS for a range of affordable printed midi dresses.

## 8. Bodycon Midi Dress

![Bodycon Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/8_bodycon-midi-dress_600x815.jpg)

Channel your inner glamazon in a bodycon midi dress. The look is very '90s supermodel. Wear a bodycon dress during the day by teaming it with slouchy cropped jackets and casual shoes. Dress it up at night with some statement-making earrings and stiletto heels. You don’t have to spend a fortune on a designer number by Herve Leger either, with stores like Dorothy Perkins and Topshop both stocking chic bodycon dresses.

## 9. Retro Midi Dress

![Retro Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/9_retro-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Vintage inspired fashion is still big business. A full-skirted midi dress is the perfect way to work a feminine, vintage-inspired look. This washed silk Donna Karan dress is ridiculously expensive at $2700. Luckily though, you can find equally stylish vintage-inspired dresses at stores like Modcloth for a fraction of the cost of a designer dress.

As you can see, there are a range of midi dresses worth checking out this spring. Are you a fan of the midi? How would you style these midi dresses?
