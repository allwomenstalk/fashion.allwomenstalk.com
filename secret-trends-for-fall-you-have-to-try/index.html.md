# Secret Trends for Fall You Have to Try ...

Don’t you absolutely love all of the different trends this fall? If you’re having a blast following all of the different trends that make up the autumn season but want a little variety too, follow these ideas. I love fall trends as much as the next person, but sometimes I want a little individuality, so I’ve compiled a list of my favorite fall trends that are a little less well known. Whether you decide to switch your plaid out for herringbone or you start wearing that outfit you thought didn’t look very flattering on you, these are the secret trends you should be following this fall!

## 1. Different Shades of Nude

![Different Shades of Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/fh/t3wd54do5623c8083c5cd137297648_580x978.jpg)

Nude and neutral colors are always so big during the fall months, but there’s actually a twist on it that’s become a huge hidden fall trend this year. Wear multiple different shades of nude for a look that’s perfectly fall and stylish! The similar colors will contrast and compliment each other perfectly!

## 2. Pleated Midi Skirts

![Pleated Midi Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/vs/z69kygi45623c8359d3ae060411437_600x900.jpg)

Midi skirts are a trend we’ve seen everywhere for the past year or so. However, they’re known for their big, dramatic silhouettes that catch everyone’s attention. These midi skirts are just as big of a trend, but a little-known secret. Wear a pleated midi skirt for a look that’s perfectly autumnal!

## 3. Herringbone

![Herringbone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/e7/wdmgpxod5623c8851c460023331584_560x735.jpg)

Every few years, herringbone makes its resurgence into the fall fashion world. This year is that year, but most people are jumping all over the plaid trend. Herringbone is just as cute, just a little more downplayed. If you haven’t worn herringbone yet this year, you definitely should!

## 4. Boxy Chunky Sweaters

![Boxy Chunky Sweaters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/hw/zhkm27595623c8e36f27a787350049_585x848.jpg)

A lot of people tend to stay away from this particular silhouette, making it a trend that many people haven’t jumped on this fall. Wear a sweater that’s big, chunky, and comfortable, but has a square-shaped silhouette. It’s still a pretty small trend this year because a lot of people worry about whether it’s flattering. Paired with high-waisted pants, this is one of the most chic and flattering looks of the year.

## 5. A Shift Mini Dress

![A Shift Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/dz/u4bmsx005623c8fd6fc1a294682020_600x899.jpg)

I’m all about mini dresses, like the rest of the world, but a lot of people put them away when the weather gets cold. The 70s are back in a big way this fall, so bring out your favorite shift mini dress for a look that’s so on trend but not yet over done

## 6. Big Glasses

![Big Glasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/oi/vqtp9nqr5623c910e2c62390997320_478x707.jpg)

While I don’t think this trend is necessarily a secret anymore, because it feels like nearly everyone rocks this trend these days, there are still a lot of people who worry about whether or not big glasses are flattering on their faces. I’m sure a lot of people are worried that the look is too 80s, but in reality, there are a pair of big glasses that looks good on everyone’s face shape!

## 7. Silk Scarves

![Silk Scarves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/sz/diq1gab45623c99ccbe18059579051_600x900.jpg)

It’s not secret that scarves are one of the biggest trends of the fall every year. If you love scarves but are looking to deviate just a little bit, try wearing a silk scarf instead. It’s just as fashionable as a wool scarf, if not more, plus you’re standing apart from the rest of the crowd, which is always a plus.

What are your favorite secret trends this fall? Will you wear any of these? Let me know in the comments.
