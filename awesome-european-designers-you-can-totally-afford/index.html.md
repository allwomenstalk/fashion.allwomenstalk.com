# 10 Awesome European Designers You Can Totally Afford ...

Designer clothing doesn't always have to cost a bomb. Here's the proof: an awesome guest post by [Mad Mama's](http://www.madmamanyc.com/) founder Alex Kogan (you might know her as [@madmamanyc](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) on Instagram), a New Yorker, an expert on healthy living, parenting and overall fabulosity. Read on!

Fashion doesn't actually have to be expensive. Classy? Yes, but pricey... well, not really. We, at Mad Mama NYC, created a list of 10 European Designers that you need to check out right now! Surprisingly, these talented emerging designers sell their items at very reasonable prices! Well, at least for now ;)

## 1. WeAnnaBe.com

They are a Ukrainian based Polish brand, that offers worldwide delivery. Their tweed suits are absolutely awesome and they cost about $101.

## 2. Martha Horovitz

She's a Polish designer who specializes in recycling and remaking vintage fur. Her fur coats are truly one of a kind. This website offers free shipping to the USA and the prices are a delight!

## 3. Hana Zarubova

Hana Zarubova is a Czech minimalistic hipster designer. She believes in simple materials and neutral colors along with interesting silhouettes.

## 4. PH Jewelry

This Czech girl makes jewelry that is out of this world! Some of the designs are so crazy and yet awesome! Check her out on [facebook.com](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

## 5. SABA Fashion

They are a men's apparel designer brand from Belarus. Two words to describe their style: urban and cool!

## 6. LEORGOFMAN

Here's an exquisite, elegant, and affordable brand from Minsk. How can you not love all that silk?! So lux.

## 7. Prototype23fcc

Here's a Bulgarian high fashion brand that is truly out there. Check out their coats and swimming suits. We love their creative combination of ordinary fabric and experimental cuts! This brand is definitely our favorite!

## 8. DVABUTIK

This is a Hungarian fashion designer who creates amazing modern bags and swimsuits if you are looking for one!

## 9. Kachorovska Atelier

Quality that is hard to believe at prices that you can afford. She's a second generation shoemaker from Ukraine, who offers made to measure, bespoke shoes that are beautiful and as comfortable as your ol' sneakers!

## 10. Ozerianko Bags

This brand offers trendy stylish leather bags from Ukraine. Think great leather, simple designs, and handcrafted patterns. Pure love!
Which of these brands you loved most? Tell us...
