## Comfort Is Queen: Why Fit Matters

One of the best things about Bluebella’s Naomi set is how much thought has gone into the fit. I’ve tried so many brands where the sizing just feels off, or the fabrics aren’t breathable. Not here. The Naomi set in cherry lacquer hugs in all the right places—it’s snug but never constricting. The bra’s underwire adds shape without poking, and the mesh is soft against the skin.

I’ve worn it on stay-at-home days and even under more fitted dresses, and it never fails me. There’s something about knowing your lingerie fits just right that gives you that extra bit of confidence, no matter what the day throws at you.
