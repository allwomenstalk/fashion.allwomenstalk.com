# 9 Comfy and Cute Sweaters to Add to Your Wardrobe This Winter ...

It is winter, and that means it is time to stock your closet full of comfy and [cute sweaters](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-cutest-womens-sweaters/). There is something for you on this list no matter what your style might be. I hope you’ll love these comfy and cute sweaters as much as I do!

## 1. UNIF - Cake Sweater

_ UNIF’s Cake Sweater really “takes the cake” when it comes to comfy and cute sweaters. The sweater is 100% acrylic and has a fabulous rainbow color scheme. Throw on your favorite jeans or leggings to for a cute and comfy winter look. You can get yours at unifclothing.com_.

## 2. Zara – Sweater with Loose Turtleneck

![Zara – Sweater with Loose Turtleneck](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/29131512_7738_400x496.jpg)

This acrylic, cotton, and wool sweater is guaranteed to keep you warm and looking stylish this winter. I love Zara for their high-end products at affordable prices. This sweater is adorable with skinny pants and ankle boots. You can shop for this sweater and other great finds at [zara.com](https://goo.gl/qxybAZ).

## 3. Wildfox Couture – Little Huskies

![Wildfox Couture – Little Huskies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/29131513_8053_600x950.jpg)

The Internet may be all about cats, but this sweater is all about huskies! Wildfox is known for cute prints and soft fabrics and the Little Huskies sweater is no exception. The sweater is a rayon, polyester, and spandex blend and comes in blush pink and sea foam green. I love to wear my Wildfox with a long black skirt with a side slit. Shop [wildfoxcouture.com](https://goo.gl/X8CNfL) for your very own!

## 4. The CULTLABEL – Crystal Skull

![The CULTLABEL – Crystal Skull](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/29131516_1687_499x755.jpg)

THE CULTLABEL is one of my favorite online shops. They have a fun and affordable collection. I love the design and color of the Crystal Skull Pullover. The skull design looks great against the purple/blue hue of the sweater. The sweater is made of cotton and is warm, but not too thick. Dress this sweater up with your favorite skirt for a stylish winter look. You can shop this sweater and other cute and comfy tops at [thecultlabel.com](http://www.thecultlabel.com/).

## 5. ASOS – Only Jacquard Sweat

This cotton-blend fabric is perfect for the holiday season. The silver and black print is a cute and festive for all your parties this season. You can dress the jacquard sweater up with your favorite skirt, or play it down with a pair of black denim pants. Pick up your sweater at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/NIOJ4Q)

## 6. Lazy Oaf – Dead Head Oversize Sweatshirt

_ This oversize sweatshirt from Lazy Oaf makes me want to curl up with a cup of hot chocolate and a book. The sweatshirt is made from 100% cotton so it is guaranteed to be soft to the touch. Throw on your favorite thick leggings and tall boots for a casual yet cute and comfy look for winter. Head over to dollskill.com_ to get your own Dead Head Sweatshirt.

## 7. Nasty Gal – Wild Light Quilted Sweatshirt

![Nasty Gal – Wild Light Quilted Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/29131513_8781_600x902.jpg)

This sweater is soft and made of 100% cotton and is ultra chic. Since animal print is always in style, this sweater is a great investment for your winter wardrobe. This versatile sweater looks great dressed up with dark denim and heels, or dressed down with your favorite shorts and hightops. Pick up your own at [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 8. Out of Print Clothing – Alice in Wonderland Fleece

_ If you are a book lover and haven’t heard of Out of Print Clothing, you have been missing out! Out of Print Clothing sells shirts and sweaters that features classic novels. You can sport your favorite book all while looking stylish. Since I am a fan of Alice in Wonderland, I chose to showcase this long sleeve fleece here, but there are so many options on their website. What’s best is that these sweaters look cute with sweat pants, but you can also wear them out with your favorite denim. Shop the collection at outofprintclothing.com_.

## 9. Anthropolgie – Paquerette Pullover

![Anthropolgie – Paquerette Pullover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/29131512_4999_410x615.jpg)

This sweater available at Anthropolgie is made from cotton, rayon, polyester, spandex, and cashmere. This dressy sweater is the perfect addition to your winter wardrobe. It looks great with slacks or a pencil skirt. Dress it up with your favorite black heels and accessories. Shop this sweater and other adorable sweaters at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

Getting to wear cute and comfy sweaters is one of my favorite things about winter. Sweaters don't have to be limited to grey zip-up hoodies; being warm can be chic and stylish. Where do you shop for your favorite winter sweaters? Share with us in the comments!
