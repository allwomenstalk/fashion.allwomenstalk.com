# 7 Fantastic Yet Simple Tech\-Savvy Gift Ideas ...

Tech Gift Ideas are becoming more and more popular these days. Technology-based gifts are no longer seen as impersonal. With more and more technological devices becoming a part of our daily lives, it’s only natural that tech gift ideas become a personal choice. Check out a few fantastic tech gift ideas below.

## 1. Alexander Wang Prisma Textured-Leather IPad Case

![Alexander Wang Prisma Textured-Leather IPad Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/b0/ovuwli3e54c935397c94d224762989_350x473.jpg)

**Price:** $325.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1I0qps)
This designer iPad case is a stylish way to house such a covetable piece of technology. The case itself is also quite covetable. It is made from black textured leather and features rhodium tips at the corners. This stylish case combines the best of both form and function.

## 2. Cell Phone Handset

![Cell Phone Handset](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ku/mt/hjsn3zjq54c9353a6859c423626206_344x468.jpg)

**Price:** $35.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Tech gift ideas like this have a certain retro charm to them. They’re for the person who doesn’t quite yet want to give in to the slick modernity of new technology. This handset extension simply plugs into cell phone so you can talk ‘like the good old days’. It’s compatible with the iPhone, iPad, computer, and a range of various other mobile devices as long as you have an appropriate adaptor.

## 3. Solar IPhone Charger

![Solar IPhone Charger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/jt/qvgnhho854c9353b4a407951119808_350x352.jpg)

**Price:** $37.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This solar powered charger is perfect for someone who is always on the go (and spends a lot of time on their iPhone and/or iPod while they’re at it). You can charge it by placing it in direct sunlight or by plugging it into the USB port of your computer. Attach it to your keychain and simply plug it in when a device needs charging.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Heart Shaped Acetate Earbuds

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Heart Shaped Acetate Earbuds](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/tw/cxuj6es654c9353c1b056765942329_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tRbMTa)
These heart shaped earbuds make for cute tech gift ideas. They feature slim white cords, white rubber earbuds, and designer-stamped plaques. These earbuds are compatible with a range of audio devices. They’re perfect for the music lover who also has a keen eye for designer fashion.

## 5. High Heel Speakers

![High Heel Speakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/wo/jlah7yff54c9353d0d6bc320707213_350x367.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These high heel speakers are perfect unleashing the inner fashionista. These shoe-shaped spakers are easy to use and come with a USB connector and 3.4mm phono jack. They’re compatible with a range of audio devices and are bound to liven up any mantle piece.

## 6. Jimmy Choo Trent Metallic IPhone Sleeve

![Jimmy Choo Trent Metallic IPhone Sleeve](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lu/yq/hvvne9b154c9353e566bf716370049_213x400.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DpglQI)
Metallics are all the rage this season and this trend has even filtered down to the world of tech gift ideas. This metallic iPhone sleeve is sure to impress. It is made from silver leather and has a gold-tipped grosgrain tab to remove the phone with. It’s a certainly a chic way to house an iPhone.

## 7. Urbanears Plattan White Headphones

![Urbanears Plattan White Headphones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/u3/k84mk8t654c9353f5126f217731734_350x176.jpg)

**Price:** $90.90 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/LvtRO1)
Another gift for the music-savvy recipient, these headphones are sure to impress. These full sized headphones are unisex and deliver crisp quality sound. They have a fold-down construction, tangle-resistant cord, and 3.5mm stereo plug. As far as tech gift ideas go, this one comes with lots of street cred.

Don’t be afraid of tech gift ideas. As you can see, there are a range of fun, stylish, and ingenious technology-based gifts on the market. Which of these tech gift ideas are your favourites?
