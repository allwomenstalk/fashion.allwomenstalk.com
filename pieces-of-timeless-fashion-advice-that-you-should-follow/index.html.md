# 7 Pieces of Timeless Fashion Advice That You Should Follow ...

Every woman wants to know some fabulous pieces of timeless fashion advice. After all, we want to look lovely at all times and in all situations, don’t we? And it is a lot easier to do that if we are armed with some good quality, timeless fashion advice. These little tidbits can help you to make the right choices when it comes to fashion now and for years to come.

## 1. Know when to Splurge

One of the best pieces of timeless fashion advice is to know when to splurge and when to save. Generally speaking, you want to save on items that seem as if they will only be here for a season or two. If it is something that you feel will be in your closet for years to come, that is the time to splurge. Usually, those are designer, classic pieces. They could also be basic pieces that you are relatively certain will not go out of style.

## 2. Own a LBD

This is a closet staple every woman needs. A LBD is a little black dress. This is a wardrobe staple that every woman should have. The reason is that a little black dress can be worn for many different occasions. It can be dressed up or dressed down. It is also a timeless piece that will endure the test of time.

## 3. Don’t Underestimate Accessories

Accessories can be a good choice in fashion that will be timeless if they are chosen carefully. For example, you can’t go wrong with a strand of pearls. Another timeless piece is a pair of diamond stud earrings. You can even choose costume jewelry in those pieces. The point is that there are some accessories that never go out of style and will be timeless pieces for you.

## 4. Solids Are Building Blocks

Solids are great building blocks. You can’t go wrong in purchasing solids because you can use them forever and always. They are great to use as basics with denim or to mix with a print. One especially great solid is a black t-shirt. You can wear it with denim and some simple accessories and you have a very elegant look.

## 5. Don’t Skimp on Jeans

A good pair of jeans is worth what they cost. If they look good on you and make you feel confident, they are worth purchasing. They can be a timeless addition to your closet. It is best to choose a shade of denim that is very common. It is also usually a good idea to stick with a brand that you know works for you.

## 6. Simplicity is the Key to Elegance

Remember what I said about the black t-shirt? That it could be a very elegant look with some simple accessories? That is because it is a simple combination and simplicity equals elegance. Elegance never says that you are trying too hard. Elegance is simplicity combined with a confidence in yourself.

## 7. Be True to You

We all have our own style and that is a good thing. Never get so wrapped up in trends and fashion that you forget your style. Your style is what feels right to you and what looks good on you. It is most likely the look you get the most compliments on. Your own unique style is what makes you, you.

These pieces of timeless fashion advice can take you far but they are only the tip of the iceberg. What are your best pieces of timeless fashion advice? I would love to learn from you!
