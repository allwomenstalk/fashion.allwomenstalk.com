# 8 Amazing Street Style Photographers ...

Street style photographers seem to be everywhere these days, but there are a few that have come to really stand out in the genre. Whether they’re shooting for their own websites or for various other fashion publications, their images often become the source inspiration for fashion savvy folk the world over. The following are a few street style photographers who have well and truly made their mark in the fashion world.

## 1. Tommy Ton

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/fashion/2012/09/1_730x450.jpeg)

Tommy Ton really does have an eye for detail. His blog, Jak & Jil regularly showcases the best of fashion weeks. It combines both street style and detailed shots, making it a never ending source of inspiration for any style savvy fashionista. As well as creating content for his own blog, he also contributes to Style.com during fashion week events.

## 2. Scott Schuman

![Scott Schuman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/21_500x750.jpg)

No list would be complete without one of the most recognisable names in the business, Scott Schuman aka The Sartorialist. He is undoubtedly one of the most popular street style photographers around. Whether he’s shooting the top editors or everyday folk, his photos are appealing and accessible.

## 3. Garance Doré

![Garance Doré](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/31_500x758.jpg)

While she started off as an illustrator, it’s quite clear that Garance Doré is quite handy with a camera as well. Her blog is a collection of stylish figures and is combined with plenty of appealing illustrated elements. Garance’s images are clean and light, and very appealing to the eye, which makes her a firm favourite as far as street style photographers go.

## 4. Candice Lake

![Candice Lake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/41_630x946.jpg)

A personal favourite of mine, Candice Lake is a London based photographer, blogger, and former model. She travels the globe and consistently manages to get stunning shots of the uber stylish. Not only does she shoot for her own website, but regularly contributes to various Vogue websites, Glamour UK, and Harper's Bazaar.

## 5. Vanessa Jackman

![Vanessa Jackman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/51_533x800.jpg)

Visit Vanessa Jackman’s eponymous blog and you’re transported to a visually beautiful world. Her images are well composed and make wonderful use of light. What I find most appealing about Vanessa Jackman, however, is her passion for film photography. In this digital age, I find this adds a certain rawness to her street style photography.

## 6. Eddie Newton

![Eddie Newton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/61_800x1200.jpg)

Eddie Newton’s blog, Mr. Newton, is regularly up there when it comes to popular sources of street style inspiration. His photos are refreshingly light, and his subjects are a mix between the big fashion week players and the everyday folk that catch his eye.

## 7. Ari Seth Cohen

![Ari Seth Cohen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/72_1065x1600.jpg)

Ari Seth Cohen is the creator of Advanced Style, a blog that showcases the fashion savvy and sometimes eccentric outfits of our elders. He has an eye for detail and his photos not only manage to showcase outfits but capture the personality of the people behind them as well.

## 8. Bill Cunningham

_ Bill Cunningham has been shooting street style looks for decades now. He’s a fashion photographer for the New York Times and is renowned for his candid street style photographs. He’s an icon of the fashion world and is undoubtedly one of the original and best street style photographers around.

These are just some of the most notable street style photographers around at the moment. Who’s your favourite street style photographer? Is there anyone else you’d like to add to the list?
