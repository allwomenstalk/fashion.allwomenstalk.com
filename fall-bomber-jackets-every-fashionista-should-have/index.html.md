# 8 Fall Bomber Jackets Every Fashionista Should Have ...

Cool cover-ups are essential for the chilly days of fall and bomber jackets are one of those essentials every [fashionista](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-become-a-fashionista/) should have. A bomber jacket is another tool in a fashionista’s arsenal of coats that can be used as a lightweight layering piece to throw on and off during the wavering temperatures of fall. This boyish basic is a forever favourite with its striking elegance that’s effortlessly chic and cool, making it a must-have for the season. The balance between classic and casual is created with these fall bomber jackets every fashionista should have.

## 1. Boucle Bomber Jacket

![Boucle Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/1_boucle-bomber-jacket_600x776.jpg)

For an elegant and ladylike alternative to traditional athletic bomber jackets, opt for a boucle finish a la Chanel. Boucle offers a refined edge to sport luxe bomber jackets that is classic and chic at the same time. Don a boucle bomber jacket with sophisticated flair in true feminine fashion by selecting one in interesting and pretty shades like rosy pinks or royal purples. A boucle bomber jacket is a great alternative to a traditional blazer or frayed cropped jacket and can gracefully take you from day to night with effortless ease.

## 2. Shearling Bomber Jacket

![Shearling Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/2_shearling-bomber-jacket_570x635.jpg)

Shearling is the perfect cool-weather material to bundle up to this season. The soft and plush element of shearling adds a textural edge to simple and minimal bomber jackets for fall. Opulent jewel tones and rich colors like chocolate brown or deep oxblood make a standout statement amongst grey and black tones this season.

## 3. Leather Bomber Jacket

![Leather Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/3_leather-bomber-jacket_300x300.jpg)

A cool cropped leather bomber jacket in classic black is the ultimate cool layering piece that’s essential to every fashionista. The layering element of a bomber jacket makes it so versatile and transitional it will be a piece in your wardrobe that never goes out of style.

## 4. Moto Bomber Jacket

![Moto Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/4_moto-bomber-jacket_570x605.jpg)

An edgy and strong moto-inspired bomber jacket is feminine with a tough finish. In a contemporary color combination like classic black and white, a moto bomber jacket is given a geometrical and futuristic vibe. Heavy metal detailing like buckles, zippers and snaps in a lustrous finish offer high-octane style that’s casual and cool for all occasions.

## 5. Leopard Print Bomber Jacket

![Leopard Print Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/5_leopard-print-bomber-jacket_344x344.jpg)

A traditional varsity or bomber jacket gets a modern update when re-created in the season’s chicest and trendiest prints like wild leopard print. A cool, neutral color palette of silvery grays gives this bomber jacket a cool, sophisticated edge. Pair with high top wedge sneakers and a set of leather leggings for an urban city-inspired way to wear.

## 6. Lace Bomber Jacket

![Lace Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/6_lace-bomber-jacket_288x360.jpg)

Although bomber jackets typically have a casual and laid back feel to them, you can sport a bomber jacket in a flirty and feminine fabric for a dressier edge to your evening-wear. Instead of selecting typical black blazers or coats, pair a lace bomber jacket over your little black dress for an instant shot of cool chic. You don’t have to reserve your bomber jackets for sporting events, as you can pair a elegant and luxe fabric with everything from wide leg trousers to a sleek pencil skirt.

## 7. Plaid Bomber Jacket

![Plaid Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/7_plaid-bomber-jacket_452x475.jpg)

Plaid is the quintessential print of fall. The festive checkered pattern is a simple and effortless way to inject color into your outfit this season. A traditional plaid print in red is a cool standout piece that you can revisit every fall season, as this classic is a favourite among the fashionista crowd.

## 8. Fur Bomber Jacket

_ For a chic and cozy way to wear bomber jackets for fall, opt for warm and plush materials like fur that add a fun and playful element to your outfit. Fur is a contemporary and luxe way to wear traditionally sport-inspired bomber jackets that are usually made of nylon or thick sweatshirt material. You can opt for a paneled fur bomber jacket with fur sleeves or select an all over fur jacket for a sophisticated and elegant style that can be worn for day or night.

Whether worn in a way that’s sport luxe and athletic cool or elegant and sophisticated, a chic bomber jacket is an edgy cool-weather cover-up that’s an essential layering piece for fashionista’s during the fall season. Do these fall bomber jackets top your must-have list this season? How will you sport a bomber jacket for fall?
