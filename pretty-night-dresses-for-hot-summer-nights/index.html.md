# 7 Pretty NightDresses for Hot Summer Nights ...

Summer is here, and with the days long and sunny and the nights hot and humid, every girl needs pretty nightdresses to keep her cool! There are some great pretty nightdresses around this season, whether you're into lace and florals or a more casual style. Here are my top 7 picks - I hope you like them as much as I do!

## 1. Paisley Print Nightdress

![Paisley Print Nightdress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/x3/cij55n1a_305x354.jpg)

This nightie by George at Asda is one of my favorite pretty night dresses for the season. You can even pick up a matching robe! The cool, silky light grey fabric, pretty paisley print and pink lace trim makes this perfect for the summer months and it's neither too sexy or too casual. I love it!

## 2. Flower Print Nightdress

![Flower Print Nightdress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/av/uvxojpwe_305x349.jpg)

Another nightdress from George at Asda (this may only be available in the UK), I love the light fabric, pretty floral print, ruched bust and ruffled hem. It's girlie without being overly so, and the black and white colorway ensures it looks stylish as well as keeping you cool.

## 3. Elle Macpherson Intimates Dentelle Chemise

![Elle Macpherson Intimates Dentelle Chemise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/o4/toolcz7z_290x370.jpg)

Head for the ASOS site to snap up this pretty apricot blush chemise. I love the color and the contrast between the soft, silky satin skirt and sheer lace top. This would be great for summer or even for special occasions!

## 4. ASOS Hit the Hay Longline Night Dress

![ASOS Hit the Hay Longline Night Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/xd/r3pnvnc0_290x370.jpg)

Perfect for slightly chillier summer evenings, this longline night dress works equally well for slouching around the house on a Saturday morning. The super-soft jersey finish and long sleeves add a snuggly finish and I love the nude color and dipped hem. This is one of my favorite nighties for lounging around in!

## 5. ASOS Pastel Cat Oversized Tee

![ASOS Pastel Cat Oversized Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/wt/zzw4jd6h_290x370.jpg)

For a relaxed weekend, slip into this oversized tee with its cute cat print. The turn-up cuffs and curved hem ensure a relaxed fit, whilst the multicolored cat print adds a fun touch. Ideal for lounging in front of Netflix or making brunch on a sunny weekend.

## 6. La Redoute Shoestring Strap Nightie

![La Redoute Shoestring Strap Nightie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/nm/pphegc7x_600x600.jpg)

I love the bold, bright blue color of this silky nightie from La Redoute - its delicate shoestring straps make it perfect for summer and its pretty tulle embroidered cups add a girlie touch. This is definitely one to wear when you want to impress, although I think the black and beige version is equally as pretty! This is one nightie that's definitely on my wishlist this summer!

## 7. Adjustable Strap Nightie

![Adjustable Strap Nightie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/u0/rsm9biww_600x599.jpg)

This is another of my favorite nighties from La Redoute. It comes in a choice of four colorways - the blue with white polkadots (pictured) is my personal favorite, but you can also choose from pink striped, light grey star print or grey with white polkadots. This is a gorgeous nightie for everyday wear and features a v-neckline with lace trim and delicate satin bow, super-pretty!

Whatever your style, hopefully you'll like at least one of these nightdresses! Of course, you don't have to stick to feminine nighties when it comes to sleepwear - there are plenty of other options out there too, including teddies, cute short and vest sets or sleep shirts as well as full length pajamas for colder weather. Choosing some cute nightwear for summer will leave you feeling cool even when the temperature rises, and looking cool too! Are you a separates girl or do you prefer wearing a slip?
