# Style Tips to Help You Show Your Individuality ...

You don't want to look the same as everybody else. I mean, how boring is that? It's better to stand out, which is why you should follow these bold tips from [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/5-style-tips-to-ensure-you-sta):

## 1. Don't Be Afraid of an over-the-knee Boot

![Don't Be Afraid of an over-the-knee Boot](https://img.allw.mn/content/bk/gd/i6u732aj561fa6e2ebf52359806325_500x217.gif)

Don't stick to small boots. Knee high boots can look sexier than any other shoe.

## 2. Always Have Five Black Items Always in Your Closet

![Always Have Five Black Items Always in Your Closet](https://img.allw.mn/content/og/lh/llbz4tqv561fa6e323b4a989403091_500x265.gif)

You can wear black with anything. That's why your closet should be filled with the color. It'll make it easy to put together outfits.

## 3. Make a Statement with a Bold Necklace

![Make a Statement with a Bold Necklace](https://img.allw.mn/content/c5/h4/cny2xcuj561fa6e3afbe9081205232_500x283.gif)

The bigger your necklace, the better. You want it to draw attention when the rest of your outfit is bland.

## 4. You Can Never Go Wrong with a Tone-on-tone Outfit

![You Can Never Go Wrong with a Tone-on-tone Outfit](https://img.allw.mn/content/h8/iz/c5ly1rjn561fa6e50269f056871629_500x280.gif)

There's nothing wrong with wearing all white. Sometimes, you'll look your best in one color and one color only.

## 5. Leather. Leather. Leather

![Leather. Leather. Leather](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/vd/pm5xb7sm561fa6e38f951162904919_500x281.gif)

Everyone looks sexy in leather. It doesn't matter if you buy a jacket, boots, or a skirt, because you'll look hot in it.

If you follow these tips, you'll always stand out from the crowd! What do you usually do to grab other's attention?
