# 7 Draped Jackets to Fall for ...

Draped jackets are a stylish way to soften up an outfit. A draped jacket usually has an open front and lacks the structure of many other jacket styles. They’re great for throwing on over a fitted dress or for balancing out more tailored outfits. Check out a few stylish draped jackets to wear this season.

## 1. Waterfall Collar Jacket

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
This draped jacket features a lovely waterfall collar. Combined with an open front, the jacket has quite a fluid, draped effect. This jacket features three quarter sleeves, is fully lined, and comes in an on-trend pastel pink shade. Work the pastel trend by wearing this jacket with light coloured cuffed pants, a plain tank top, and neutral heels.

## 2. Everyday Antwerp Jacket

![Everyday Antwerp Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_everyday-antwerp-jacket_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $69.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If your personal style gravitates more towards minimalism, this draped jacket might be just for you. It comes in basic black and features a diagonal zip front and shawl collar. Wear it open for the full draped effect or tuck the collar up to create draping around the neck.

## 3. Draped Front Jacket

_ **Price:** $10.99 at forever21.com_
Many draped jackets are made from lightweight fabrics so are perfect layering on during the warmer months. This jacket comes in an eye-catching rust colour and features a sporty elasticised hem at the back. Wear it with a long-line top, straight or skinny leg jeans, and strappy sandals for a more casual weekend look.

## 4. Drape Longline Blazer

![Drape Longline Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_drape-longline-blazer_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/2x16Tk)
This sophisticated white jacket features more subtle draped details. It has what seems to be a classic blazer design but is made from a drapey fabric. As a result, it sits much freer and looser on the body. The cost may break the budget but you can always pass it off as an ‘investment’ piece, right?

## 5. Angular Open Blazer

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
Stand out from the crowd in brightly coloured draped jackets. I love the bright blue hue of this particular jacket. It has a boxier cut and features a front draped construction. This jacket would look super stylish worn with a full skirted white dress, neutral pumps, and an eye-catching bejewelled necklace.

## 6. Metallic Drape Biker Jacket

![Metallic Drape Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/6_metallic-drape-biker-jacket_600x789.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wuoDre)
A metallic jacket may not seem like the most practical item, but it is one of the most on-trend for the season. This biker comes in a soft bronze colour with a subtle metallic sheen. Wear it open over a long black maxi dress and with ankle boots for dose of inner-city chic.

## 7. Lace Lined Cropped Jacket

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
In a similar style to the very first jacket on this list, this jacket features lace details at the collar and cuffs. Draped jackets like these can be styled for day or night. Team it with a structured silver cocktail dress and heels for night, or with a tank dress and flats for the day.

Draped jackets are a great alternative to more structured, boxy styles. The lightweight nature of most draped jackets also makes them a stylish option for creating a layered look during warmer weather. What do you think of these stylish draped jackets?
