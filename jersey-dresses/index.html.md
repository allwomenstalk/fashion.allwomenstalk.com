# 7 Jersey Dresses ...

In 1916, famed fashion designer Coco Chanel introduced jersey as the new ‘fashion fabric’. Once used only for undergarments, jersey is now used to make a range of garments. While viewed as a mostly casual fabric, it is versatile in that it can be dressed up or down, and can be designed to create a variety of looks. Take a look at a few jersey dresses below.

## 1. VPL Free Fall Dress

![VPL Free Fall Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/mo/p0e9yqo954c90676b7ef4885226095_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Jersey dresses are perfect creating a casual daytime look. This draped dress has an open back, low v-neck, and cascading hem. It also has a draped pocket on one side. Work a fashion forward aesthetic and pair this dress with black wedge heels and tribal-inspired silver cuffs.

## 2. T by Alexander Wang Jersey T-Shirt Dress

![T by Alexander Wang Jersey T-Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/gy/aug2qrg954c90677a925a581125889_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $85.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/BSVTIB)
This jersey dress has a basic t-shirt design. It would make an excellent layering piece. It has a cut-out hem at the front, patch pocket at the breast, and dropped shoulder seams. Add structure by pairing this dress with a fitted blazer and accessorize with a framed bag.

## 3. Black Jersey Triangle Maxi Dress

![Black Jersey Triangle Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/na/howeabwz54c906788ff12572387567_334x496.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/exogXB)
A black maxi dress can become a wardrobe staple. Wear it alone for a fuss-free look or dress it up with statement jewellery for a casual evening look. This particular dress has a v-neck, halter straps, and plaited ties. The triangle cups are flattering and the halter-neck design is perfect for summer.

## 4. Juicy Couture Flutter-Sleeved Cotton-Jersey Dress

![Juicy Couture Flutter-Sleeved Cotton-Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/x8/o39br78f54c9067969ea2103625186_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pTKc2s)
The flutter sleeves on this dress are a delicate addition. The dress has a round neck with twisted cotton trim, self-tie waist belt, and gathering at the bust. While some jersey dresses can be shapeless, the addition of the belt in this design accentuates the waist. Team this dress with some strappy metallic sandals and slim clutch for a chic everyday look.

## 5. Topshop Alexander Wang Jersey Draped Dress

![Topshop Alexander Wang Jersey Draped Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/ii/azvr1aso54c9067a58d03052118940_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While made from jersey, this dress has a certain evening-wear inspired design. It also has the price to match. This dress has a low asymmetric neckline, gathering at the front, and a handkerchief hem. Wear with a leather jacket and boots for a street smart look or dress it up with chandelier earrings and heels for a night out.

## 6. Topshop Multi Stripe Jersey Dress

![Topshop Multi Stripe Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lf/fn/c842y6ed54c9067b91fa7622802247_334x497.jpg)

**Price:** $66.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/NfU43p)
The striped jersey shirt is a classic. Take this trend one step further in this striped jersey dress. It has a classic t-shirt top and bodycon skirt. The bodice features red stripes while the skirt features navy stripes. Wear this dress with black ballet flats or loafers, and team it with a navy blazer for a timeless look.

## 7. Carven Draped Muscle Sleeve Dress

![Carven Draped Muscle Sleeve Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ar/w3/ias36o5m54c9067c9716b261016297_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $294.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This fitted t-shirt uses draping techniques to create a fashion-forward design. It has layered cuffs at the armholes and gathered layer details at the front. The rolls of gathered material run diagonally across the body. While jersey dress can be quite plain, this dress is anything but.

There is no doubt that jersey is one of the most used fabrics in the fashion industry. These jersey dresses take the fabric to another level of wearability. Which of these jersey dresses do you like best?
