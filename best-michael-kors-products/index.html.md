# 7 Best Michael Kors Products ...

If you're a fan of Micahel Kors (_psyche.co.uk_), you're going to love this list of some of his most fabulous products, shared by guest contributor Harry Stern!

Michael Kors has rapidly gained a reputation as one of the world’s most stylish designers, with a following that includes Jennifer Lopez, Angelina Jolie and even the First Lady herself, Michelle Obama. The brand is a force to be reckoned with, dominating the clothing, jewellery and accessories market. We’ve taken a look at the Michael Kors range available at Psyche and selected our top seven pieces. Here they are:

## 1. Michael Kors Bedford Tote Bag

![Michael Kors Bedford Tote Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074956_8952_599x436.jpg)

This gorgeous Bedford tote bag is a perfect standout accessory that will brighten up even the darkest of winter wardrobes. Made from super-soft genuine leather, the simple design is accentuated with minimalistic gold detailing. The large size and removable strap make this bag ideal for everyday use, as well as being glamorous enough for more formal occasions.

## 2. Michael Kors Cross over Dress

![Michael Kors Cross over Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074954_1257_599x582.jpg)

Every woman needs to own an LBD, and what better option is there than this fabulous cross over dress? With the wrap detailing disguising any imperfections, this dress is extremely flattering. The three-quarter-length sleeve adds a feminine touch, and the simple metal branded necklace adds an alternative edge to the classic black dress.

## 3. Michael Kors Black Quilted Leather Jacket

![Michael Kors Black Quilted Leather Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074954_0198_599x584.jpg)

The popularity of the leather jacket has continued to grow season after season, and it shows no signs of stopping. This black leather jacket is perfect for throwing on with jeans and a tee for a casual look, or toughening up a girly evening dress. Made from 100 per cent leather, the gold zip detailing makes this jacket a standout piece.

## 4. Michael Kors Studded Court Shoe

![Michael Kors Studded Court Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074956_6963_599x344.jpg)

Studs are another trend that is huge this season, and these studded court shoes will put you one step ahead of the fashion pack. With a 4.5-inch heel, these sky-scraping shoes are crafted from the finest suede and feature stud detailing to the front platform, as well as a branded plaque near the heel. Wear these to add an instant touch of glamour to any outfit.

## 5. Michael Kors Leopard Print Blouse

![Michael Kors Leopard Print Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074955_4151_599x576.jpg)

Nailing the leopard-print and bright-colour trends all in one, this blouse is a must-have for all fashionistas. You’ll really make a statement in this standout piece. Team with a tailored black blazer for a look that will help you make the transition from day to night.

## 6. Michael Kors Logo Purse

![Michael Kors Logo Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074958_9057_599x183.jpg)

A gorgeous bag simply isn’t complete without an equally gorgeous purse! This simple black purse is crafted from the finest leather, and is embellished with the classic Michael Kors logo. There’s plenty of room for your cash and cards inside too, so it’s practical and stylish.

## 7. Michael Kors Printed Ankle Boots

![Michael Kors Printed Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12074958_7200_599x366.jpg)

In the winter, every girl needs a pair of quality boots that will see them through the colder months. Made from leather and calf hair, these printed ankle boots with comfortable block heels will be the perfect addition to your wardrobe this season.

We may have to update this list soon though, as it has recently been announced that two of our fashion faves are joining forces – Michael Kors and Gwyneth Paltrow are designing a Christmas range together! We know what we’ll be asking Santa for!
