# 16 Curvy Girls Who Are Killing It on Instagram ...

No matter your personal style or look, you're bound to find fashion inspo on Instagram, and most of mine comes from a few particularly curvaceous bloggers who just slay, every day. Who are these curvy girls you need to be following, right now?Read on, and prepare to see exactly what "killing it" truly means.

## 1. Nicolette Mason

![Nicolette Mason](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/r9/zuj6rlvo5756bc882d42d128961059_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@nicolettemason](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
I adore Nicollette's style: very girly, very pink. She shares her outfits, natch, but she also posts lots of lifestyle photos, too.

## 2. Callie Thorpe

![Callie Thorpe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/h4/yepas7xv5756bc882e32c742139775_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@calliethorpe](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This Marie Claire columnist obviously leads a happy life, which you can clearly see in her OOTD shares, and especially in her shots with her friends.

## 3. Chastity Garner Valentine

![Chastity Garner Valentine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/sb/xobntozi5756bc8803363922734180_600x750.jpg)

Follow her at [@garnerstyle](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Co-creator of The Curvy Con, Chastity shares stellar fashion photos and lots of behind-the-scenes, having-fun-with-friends shots.

## 4. Georgina

![Georgina](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/s3/y3w6axbc5756bc887146a727311211_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@cupcakesloveme](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Georgina's style can best be summed up in one word: kawaii. While she does share lots of OOTD photos, Georgina also gives us an inside look at the cuteness overload that is her daily life.

## 5. Marie Denee

![Marie Denee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/sx/n0n41kk95756bc8a00572014232354_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@mariedenee](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Marie has all the sexy, all the gorgeous, and she doesn't mind sharing her style shots (and loads of lifestyle). This is how the curvy half lives.

## 6. Ragini Nag Rao

![Ragini Nag Rao](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/uw/moqy3wox5756bc8a75cbd753378645_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@kittehinfurs](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
From her love of color to her sweet pixie cut, Ragini is the girl to follow if you love all things vintage.

## 7. Allison Teng

![Allison Teng](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t6/a9/vbi5di515756bc8ae0c40900852007_600x749.jpg)

Follow her at [@curvygirlchic](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This curvy Cali girl has style to spare, but what I love most is that her wardrobe is totally attainable: she wears clothes you can buy just about anywhere. It's how she puts them all together I need to learn.

## 8. Gabi Gregg

![Gabi Gregg](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/n9/iing4jjd5756bc8ada798852676408_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@gabifresh](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
I wish I had even an ounce of Gabi's style. She's a risk taker, flawless, and she makes it look easy.

## 9. Tess Munster (Holliday)

![Tess Munster (Holliday)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/ua/x94zq4xi5756bc8b060d1065744738_600x750.jpg)

Follow her at [@tessholliday](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
You may know Tess already from her #EffYourBeautyStandards campaign, but if you're not following her for fashion inspo, do it now. You'll be so glad you did. Side note: her recent preggo pictures just kill me!

## 10. Teer Wayde

![Teer Wayde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/wx/raiy5yny5756bc8b49b54351224132_600x749.jpg)

Follow her at [@teerwayde](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Teer is the vintage vixen pin-up girl you've always wanted to be, with the clothes, style, bod, and makeup you'll covet forever.

## 11. Isha Reid

![Isha Reid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h4/tg/ihss34xj5756bc8c06beb139849574_600x749.jpg)

Follow her at [@pic_pixie](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
I just adore Isha's take on vintage style, and we have the same taste in books, so...

## 12. Nadia Aboulhosn

![Nadia Aboulhosn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/po/eemkwab15756bc8c8f7db601232936_600x709.jpg)

Follow her at [@nadiaaboulhosn](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Nadia's hair, makeup and OOTD are always on point, but she's also got a great sense of humor, so you'll want to give her a follow, too.

## 13. Kellie Brown

![Kellie Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/gq/jy2bxihx5756bc8d5e216720900769_600x749.jpg)

Follow her at [@itsmekellieb](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Am I more envious of Kellie's style (omg, her accessories!) or of her travel itinerary? Both. Definitely both.

## 14. Kobi Jae

![Kobi Jae](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f5/nx/sx5dk4iv5756bc8e35380053656560_600x600.jpg)

Follow her at [@kobi_jae](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Kobi touts herself as a "Fa(t)shion Blogger" who demonstrates what "fat girls ACTUALLY wear." Her style is inspired by maybe Edward Gorey, with a little punk thrown in for good measure.

## 15. Miz Liz

![Miz Liz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/s4/nhvtkf2o5756bc8f37daa180148137_600x526.jpg)

Follow her at [@bbwgeneration](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
I love that so many of the selfies she shares are just everyday, not styled or filtered to perfection. She's real... and I adore her!

## 16. Ashley Rose

![Ashley Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/c6/ifbe8tfn5756bc903ebaa590682601_600x600.jpg)

Ashley is always dressed to the nines, and it's the attention to the small details that make her outfits so enviable.
