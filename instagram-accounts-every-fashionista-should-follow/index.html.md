# 11 Instagram Accounts Every Fashionista Should Follow ...

There are SO many Instagram accounts for fashionistas to follow! It's such an easy and fun way to stay in the know about the fashion world. You can follow models, designers, brand stores, etc. Instagram is such a fantastic way to see what all of these people are up to, no matter where in the world they are! Check out some of my favorite Instagram accounts for fashionistas! Then, let me know which ways you like to stay in the fashion loop!

## 1. @caradelevingne

![@caradelevingne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/lj/arkxw2qw_490x490.jpg)

Cara Delevingne is easily one of the coolest models around. From her kick-ass street style to those awesome bold brows, Cara has a huge following. Her Instagram account has racked up nearly five million followers! How crazy is that? Follow her to see pictures as she travels the world and models for famous designers like Victoria’s Secret and Chanel. She's just one of the awesome Instagram accounts for fashionistas worth following!

## 2. @katcollings

![@katcollings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/cl/r0z7u4ob_490x490.jpg)

If you're a fan of the site WhoWhatWear.com, then following Kat Collings is a must! She's the associate editor of the site, a website that writes about the latest celebrity styles and runway trends. She's racked up nearly 8,000 followers and is always posting fun, quirky pictures!

## 3. @jacvanek

![@jacvanek](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/du/uk2fwvlc_490x490.jpg)

This is one of my favorite Instagram account to follow! Designer Jac Vanek makes fantastically witty clothing for stores like Urban Outfitters! The account has nearly 200,000 followers. Some of my favorite items in my closet are from Jac! My latest purchase was the "Music Is the Answer" jacket! It's so cute!

## 4. @victoriabeckham

![@victoriabeckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vt/vc/u7u2evim_490x490.jpg)

The queen of all fashionistas, Victoria Beckham is awesome to follow on Instagram! She brings her followers into her private world. You’ll see pics of her inside her office, on the runway, and if we're lucky, some pictures of David Beckham! LOL!

## 5. @nylonmag

![@nylonmag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/vk/taglc82m_490x490.jpg)

Nylon magazine’s Instagram is such a treat! It features photos of some of the editors' favorite items and they even post a bunch of behind-the-scenes photos of what it's like at the magazine! How cute are these socks?!

## 6. @georgiamayjagger

![@georgiamayjagger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/oz/hj7bsrbv_490x490.jpg)

Georgia May Jagger, daughter of the one and only Mick Jagger, is definitely worth following! She takes her followers around the world with her as she travels and models for tons of famous magazines!

## 7. @bergdorfs

![@bergdorfs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/vp/ghz09un2_490x490.jpg)

Every fashionista should be following Bergdorf Goodman. Its Instagram account shows you what’s being featured at the store so you're never out of the loop!

## 8. @badgalriri

![@badgalriri](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/0o/hlkp9x1i_490x446.jpg)

If you're a fashion lover, you NEED to be following Rihanna! She's always ahead of the game when it comes to fashion. We all know she's not afraid of taking risks - her photos are pretty awesome!

## 9. @chiaraferragni

![@chiaraferragni](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/r6/tldhfyqz_490x490.jpg)

Ever heard of TheBlondeSalad.com? You should have! Chiara Ferragni is the voice behind the site. She’s originally from Italy, but she travels the world and takes her Instagram followers with her. She even launched her own line! That makes her Instagram a very awesome mix of photos of herself, her travels, an exclusive looks at her line!

## 10. @nastygal

![@nastygal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0h/xt/o0gg3weg_490x490.jpg)

Nasty Gal is one of my favorite accounts to follow! It's also one of the most successful online clothing retailers for fashion clothing, shoes, and accessories for women. You'll fall in love with their items as soon as you check them out!

## 11. @justinliv

![@justinliv](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/cy/0u0lj6fh_490x490.jpg)

If you're looking for some eye candy, follow Justin Livingston! He's a writer and editor for his lifestyle blog, ScoutSixteen.com. He posts tons of photos of his travels and street style!

Well, there you have it! Who knew there were so many awesome fashion-related Instagram accounts?! These are just 11 of my favorites but there are so many more! Which Instagram accounts do you follow to make sure you're in the fashion loop?
