# 9 Affordable Fashion Websites ...

So if you're anything like me, you are always looking for affordable fashion websites. Being a student, I know how tight money can get, and sometimes that new top you've been dying to buy just isn't in your budget. But not to worry my loves, for I have put together this list of 9 affordable fashion websites that compromise prices, and NOT style.

## 1. Boohoo.com

This is one of the affordable fashion websites that I was recommended to by one of my good friends. It is based out of the UK, but they ship internationally. Not only does it have cute clothes, but the prices are reasonable. It has a style that is similar to Nasty Gal mixed with a little Forever 21, and is waaaay more affordable. There are so many things to choose from, and there is always new stuff to browse.

## 2. Pinkice.com

This is one of my favorite sites to shop. It has a style much like that of H&M and Forever 21. I love that it has everything from casual to dressy and party pieces. I can always find the hottest items of the season here at awesome prices. I can safely say that my shopping cart has A TON of things waiting for me.

## 3. Loveculture.com

I am in love with this site because it has something for everyone! Whether your style is girly, retro, indie, or grungy, it doesn’t matter. You are practically guaranteed to find something. Most of their jeans are under $25! Such a bargain, right?

## 4. Swaychic.com

I looove Urban Outfitters, but sometimes their prices are just a little too high. This site is perfect because it has tons of items that are similar ones sold by Urban Outfitters. So if you have a more indie or boho style, then I highly suggest you check this site out. They also have plenty of awesome sale items to choose from too.

## 5. Agacistore.com

This is another site with something for everyone, from the sweet girl next door to the punk rock chick. Don’t you just love that? I am always changing up my style, depending on how I am feeling that certain day, so this site is perfect. It has a lot of good basics and different styles that you would find at Forever 21 or H&M. The sale and clearance sections always have great pieces and hardly exceed $15, so go check it out!

## 6. Urbanog.com

Clothing, shoes, and beauty, oh my! This website has a very eclectic style, and has basics as well as chic fashion pieces. They also have a bunch of eye shadow palettes, and NYX cosmetic products - another bargain! I usually only buy tops from here, but there are plenty of things to choose from.

## 7. Etsy.com

Etsy is basically a bunch of little individual stores in one. You can get pretty much anything on here, not only fashion items. I am always on this site, and it is great for getting unique and one-of-a-kind products. You can even make an account and start selling your own creations (if you have any of course). Prices vary, and I always find stuff within my price range.

## 8. Forever21.com

Pretty much every site that I have already listed is similar to Forever 21. But if you are still a skeptic and are afraid to try the other sites, there is always this one. I can’t tell you how many things I have bought from this store, and it is still a go-to site for me. I love getting handbags from here because they actually last me a while and are reasonably priced.

## 9. Asos.com

This is a site that pretty much everyone already knows about, unless you've been living under a rock. Asos has a wide variety of things to buy and the prices are sure to be within the price range no matter what your budget is. It is kind of like an Amazon site, meaning it has all kinds of stuff for sale. They always have deals for shipping too!

So if you're on a budget, don't worry. There are plenty of sites out there that can give you the "in" styles of the season without breaking the bank! Have any of you come across other affordable sites? Have you had any experience with any of these sites? If so, how did you like them? If you are trying them for the first time, let me know if you like them!
