# Girls Guide on Wear a Red Dress like a Model ...

Every girl should know **how to wear a red dress like a model.**

It’s been said time and time again that every woman needs an LBD in her closet because you can dress it in many different ways, it’s beautiful, and you feel confident when wearing it.

![flower, red, dress, pink, gown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/pu/msrhc3i359f62bca9035b653300038_600x600.jpg)

However I believe that a woman also needs a red dress in her closet for a more sultry look that men find irresistible. Whether it’s a day look or an evening look, a red dress never fails. The red dress effect is something so powerful because men are drawn to the color. You’ll always get attention from the opposite sex in red. A red dress gives you a chance to show something a little more than a black dress. It’s sexy, classy and seductive, and you don’t have to say anything because the dress does all the work.

So, **how to wear a red dress like a model?**

![dress, fashion model, shoulder, joint, cocktail dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y9/l9/aah1af3u59f62bc9da627493752255_600x668.jpg)

One of the great things about red is the different shades within the color. Depending on the style of the red dress, the darker it is, the sexier the dress looks on you. A very flattering dark red in a peplum pencil style dress with leopard print shoes and your hair up in a French twist. This is a formal look that just screams classy and sexy. When you enter a room, be prepared for all eyes to be on you because you know how to make an entrance and stand out.

![orange, fashion model, shoulder, dress, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d0/sf/xx7gk8y959f62bc9633e7603816479_600x600.jpg)

'Tis the season for colder weather. Which means oversized sweater dresses, and who doesn’t love an oversized sweater dress? It can be worn different ways and it can be worn from day to night with a few simple accessories. For a more casual day look, you can wear it a pair of wedges, flats or flat thigh high boots and a floppy hat. For a night look, switch the flat thigh high boots for heeled knee high boots and sheer black tights with a little leg peaking through. Add a statement necklace, a red lip and loose waves and you’re ready for a night out on the town with your handsome arm candy.

A red lace dress is a bit of twist on the red dress effect. A red dress is sexy on its own, but a red lace dress kicks things up a notch because red lace is not often worn during the day. Wearing a body hugging red lace dress out on a date says a lot without you having to say much. In the dress you’ll feel seductive, playful and wicked. Combine this alluring red lace dress with black stilettos heels and vanilla scented perfume and don’t forget to stand tall, confident and command the room, and you’ll be the topic of conversation.
