# The Affordable New Fashion Line from a Victoria's Secret Model ...

Here's some exciting news about April 15th. It's the day when Behati Prinsloo, a sexy Victoria Secret's model, is launching her affordable new fashion line!

According to _Glamour Magazine,_ "The range will fuse Prinsloo's relaxed rock-inspired style with Juicy Couture signatures, resulting in bomber jackets, Bermuda shorts, velour, slogan t-shirts, denim lightweight midi coats and crop tops." Basically, she's selling clothing that looks sporty, but is meant to be worn outside and inside the gym.

That means that you can buy cute clothes without wasting your entire paycheck at once! Do you think this fashion line sounds cute?
