# 8 Glam Watches ...

I’m a huge fan of glam watches! Sparkly, studded, bright colors, you name it; I love it all! Plus, watches are not only for fashion, but practical as well! Many watches are on the expensive side, depending on the brand. Yet, even though the price tags can be on the higher side, glam watches are quite long-lasting; I’ve had mine for over four years now and it’s still running strong!

## 1. Fossil Watch, Women's Riley Rose Gold Plated Stainless Steel Bracelet 36mm ES2811

![Fossil Watch, Women's Riley Rose Gold Plated Stainless Steel Bracelet 36mm ES2811](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_fossil-watch-womens-riley-rose-gold-plated-stainless-steel-bracelet-36mm-es2811_212x369.jpg)

This timepiece tops my list of glam watches just because it’s so different. I mean, it’s not gold or silver. Instead, it’s rose gold, which means it’s a pink gold color. Plus, it has crystals around the face, which add a much needed sparkle! How cool is that?

## 2. BCBGeneration Love Charm Tan Leather Strap Watch

![BCBGeneration Love Charm Tan Leather Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_bcbgeneration-love-charm-tan-leather-sttrap-watch_195x400.jpg)

Ditch the traditional chain band for a leather strap! This unique watch is equipped with letters that spell out the word “LOVE.” The face of the watch is the O! Isn’t that a neat concept, ladies? This watch should definitely be worn as an everyday watch! I really want to pick one up for myself! Plus, it’s on the inexpensive side, compared to many other fashion watches on the market! Can you say 'bonus'?!

## 3. K&BROS 3 Hands Quartz Polyurethane Watch

![K&BROS 3 Hands Quartz Polyurethane Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_k-bros-3-hands-quartz-polyurethane-watch_188x300.jpg)

I love that this watch comes in bright colors! I especially love the teal! This watch is pretty inexpensive too, so you won't have to stretch your wallet on this accessory! Pick up one in every color to match various outfits if you'd like!

## 4. Marc Jacobs 'Henry' Chronograph & Crystal Topring Watch

![Marc Jacobs 'Henry' Chronograph & Crystal Topring Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_marc-jacobs-henry-chronograph-crystal-topring-watch_600x920.jpg)

How could you go wrong with this beautiful Marc Jacobs watch? The brand name takes the place of traditional numbers (and who really needs the numbers anyway?!). The combination of gold and silver will totally make this watch a fashion statement (and a conversation piece too!).

## 5. Studded Wrap Watch

![Studded Wrap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_studded-wrap-watch_200x296.jpg)

I fell in love with this wrap watch when I first saw it. Perhaps it's because I love studs! This watch either comes in black or a vibrant blue! I want both! What do you all think?

## 6. Anne Klein AK-1154

![Anne Klein AK-1154](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_anne-klein-ak-1154_240x312.jpg)

If you're going for a classy look, this Anne Klein watch is perfect for you! The sleek white and gold combination will add a bit of glamour to your business attire! With just a little bit of crystals around the face, you can wear this watch everywhere!

## 7. Julius Leopard Print

![Julius Leopard Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_julius-leopard-print_200x200.jpg)

Release your wild side with this watch! With leopard print, it's so bright and eye catching! Plus, if you're not a huge fan of pink, it comes in white, tan and brown too!

## 8. Sara Designs Floral Wrap Watch

![Sara Designs Floral Wrap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_sara-designs-floral-wrap-watch_304x398.jpg)

I couldn't help but fall in love with this flowery timepiece! It is so delicately feminine! This watch is perfect for the spring and summer seasons! Pair it with a casual summer dress or skirt for a pop for color!

That being said, how can you go wrong with any of these watches, ladies? Which one is your favorite? It's hard for me to pick just one! Or do you have another one to suggest that isn't on the list? Feel free to comment below!
