# Swimsuit Designers for Girls Who Want the Trendiest Beach Days ...

It can be hard to find a swimsuit designer that you absolutely love, especially one that suits your personal style. Whether you’re looking for something boho, something classic and refined, or something just trendy for this summer, finding the perfect bathing suit, or even just finding the perfect swimsuit designer, can often be such a struggle. If you often find yourself searching for the perfect swimsuit designer, it can feel like an uphill battle. Especially as the summer is nearly drawing to a close, if you haven’t found your favorite swimsuit designer yet, you may have to hold off the search until next summer. That doesn’t have to be the case anymore, though, because I’ve come up with the greatest swimsuit designers, of both the summer and of all-time, for your most fashionable days at the beach ever.

## 1. Mara Hoffman

![clothing, hair, photo shoot, model, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/fa/gtewvyxk57baec5c6d737847218337_600x600.jpg)

Mara Hoffman is one of the hottest swimsuit designers of the summer. Nearly every celebrity has worn her designs, from Mindy Kaling to Beyoncé. If you’re looking to make a splash in a bright swimsuit this summer, her patterned creations are perfect for you and are sure to turn heads!

## 2. Norma Kamali

![clothing, model, photograph, swimwear, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lx/qj/xg3qi16557baec98bda8d393987180_600x600.jpg)

Norma Kamali’s swimsuit designs were thrust into the spotlight when Farrah Fawcett took that iconic picture in her red one-piece. Her designs are still as gorgeous as ever, and if you’re looking for a classic piece, Norma Kamali is definitely the designer to choose from!

## 3. Melissa Odabash

![clothing, person, hair, lingerie, plaid,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ep/o4/les03nz457baecda00f0c243550633_600x600.jpg)

If you’re looking for a great bikini, look no further than Melissa Odabash. Her bikinis are classic numbers that our generation will be known for for years to come. If you cant that classic bikini, or even a crazier one-piece or monokini, she is definitely the designer to watch!

## 4. Ralph Lauren

![model, black hair, photo shoot, thigh, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/pm/ydandx5u57c09e4acc074282644333_600x600.jpg)

You can't find more classic Americana than Ralph Lauren, especially when it comes to swimwear. If you’re looking for a classic and American swimsuit, you simply can’t go wrong with a Ralph Lauren piece. No matter your age, they’re iconic and perfect for a day at the beach!

## 5. Kate Spade

![clothing, hair, hairstyle, blond, cocktail dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/u8/afh039qn57c09eb73b6de499904287_600x600.jpg)

Most people don’t even know that Kate Spade has a swimwear line, but it’s so adorable that it simply can’t be ignored or overlooked. From the usual bright colors and whimsical patterns to cool statement accents like giant bows, this is a swimwear line that I would personally live in if I could!

## 6. Tommy Hilfiger

![clothing, hair, image, black hair, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/g6/hehgf6ul57c09f2bc10a3512636908_600x600.jpg)

Tommy Hilfiger is another swimwear line that just screams classic and patriotic to me. Whether you’re going for a chic one-piece or something that shows a little more skin, everything in Tommy Hilfiger’s line is perfectly stylish and put-together for your most chic day at the beach ever!

## 7. Heidi Klein

![clothing, swimwear, supermodel, model, sun tanning,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/go/v6/xd63g7is57c09fa036f13775094612_426x426.jpg)

Heidi Klein may just be one of my favorite swimsuit designers ever. Somehow the looks simultaneously look new and trendy while also looking like a classic number we would’ve seen on the beach decades ago. If you want to go for a retro look but still look trendy, this is the line for you!
