# 8 Chic Leather Look Shorts ...

Leather look shorts are not only a fashionable choice for the season, but they are a much more animal-friendly option than their real leather counterparts. Usually made from PU or other synthetic materials, leather looking shorts can come in a range of colours from blacks and neutrals to brights. The following are just some examples of stylish leather look shorts that you may want to check out.

## 1. Goldie Leather Look Shorts

![Goldie Leather Look Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_goldie-leather-look-shorts_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $36.45 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/0rF9hz)
Brown leather look shorts are a great alternative to black. They’re also great for creating a range of subdued, daytime looks. These particular shorts are made from a cotton-mix fabric and have a mid-rise design with a ribbed waistband. To balance out the fitted style of these shorts, team them with looser or more voluminous tops.

## 2. Faux Leather Shorts

_ **Price:** $15.80 at forever21.com_
Show off your pins in these fitted leather look shorts. They have a banded waistline, hidden side zipper, and darted front and back. Wear these with a loose collared shirt, lazily tucked in, and with ballet flats for a simple yet stylish look.

## 3. Sister Jane PU High Waisted Shorts

![Sister Jane PU High Waisted Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_sister-jane-pu-high-waisted-shorts_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $91.14 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/nD2BZU).
If you prefer your shorts to be on the looser, more tailored end of the spectrum then take a look at these babies. They have a high waisted design with a wide cut to the legs. The light pink colour of these shorts is perfect for working a neutral or pastel colour palette. Just pair these shorts with a white singlet and sandals for an effortless summer look.

## 4. Stones Leather Look Short

![Stones Leather Look Short](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_stones-leather-look-short_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Channel your inner rock chic with these leather look shorts. They sit high on the waist and have cuffed hems. Wear these shorts with loose singlet, biker style jacket, and ankle boots for an outfit full of attitude.

## 5. Three Floor Dark Horse Faux Leather Shorts

![Three Floor Dark Horse Faux Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_three-floor-dark-horse-faux-leather-shorts_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $99.42 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/EZt8AB)
The quilted finish on these leather look shorts is unique and a bit unexpected. These shorts have a mini cut length, meaning they’re fairly short, but you can always wear tights underneath for more coverage.

## 6. A Wear PU Shorts

![A Wear PU Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_a-wear-pu-shorts_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $26.51 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/JPR3qs)
These leather look shorts have an earthy, distressed finish. They have a fitted waistband, pleating at the front, and cuffed hems. These would look great worn with a slouchy cream sweater and lace up ankle boots for a slightly rustic look.

## 7. Tripp NYC Vegan Leather Short

![Tripp NYC Vegan Leather Short](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_tripp-nyc-vegan-leather-short_600x900.jpg)

Leather look shorts can be quite edgy, and these particular ones feature skull rivet details at the front pockets. They have a high rise design with five pocket styling and angled legs. These shorts are fairly fitted so consult the size guide very carefully!

## 8. Beaded Faux Leather Shorts

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
These leather look shorts come in a light peach colour and feature beaded detailing to the front. They also have a hidden side zipper and finished hems. For a bit more of a girlish look, team these shorts with a Peter Pan collared top and blush shoes.

My tip for pulling off leather look shorts is to choose a looser, tailored style. However, if you’ve got the legs for it then definitely try a more fitted style if that’s what you’re comfortable with. Are you a fan of this look? Which of these leather looking shorts do you like best?
