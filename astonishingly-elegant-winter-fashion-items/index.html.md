# 7 Astonishingly Elegant Winter Fashion Items ...

Winter Fashion varies each year. Add a bit of pizzazz to your winter outfit this year with a few luxe items and accessories. Think an array of winter fashion overloaded with rich velvet, decadent faux fur details, and luxurious lace. A luxe winter accessory could be just what you need to take your winter outfit up a notch. Get your winter fashion sorted by taking some inspiration from the following goodies.

## 1. Gucci Wide-Brim Fedora

![Gucci Wide-Brim Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rb/i6/fsi4tkyq54c934bc70320242981135_460x320.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/6XZyUi)
A colourful fedora is just one way to incorporate some luxe winter fashion into your everyday look. Straight off the runway, this wide-brim fedora comes in a rich teal hue. It also features a contrasting grosgrain band with feather trims. Work a 70s inspired silhouette a la the Gucci runway, and pair this hat with some wide legged trousers and a coloured faux fur coat.

## 2. Burberry Embellished Wool-Blend Scarf

![Burberry Embellished Wool-Blend Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/za/yv/ghruoeeo54c934bd7d6a5918366877_372x498.jpg)

**Price:** $750.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/NUCq7n)
This embellished scarf is a great way of adding some subtle bling to your outfit. This grey wool-blend scarf has a pocket design and features crystal and bead embellishments at the ends. The pockets are also lined in check fabric. This scarf is perfect for throwing on over a casual outfit.

## 3. Topshop Velvet Halter Dress

![Topshop Velvet Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/ck/ua1l3eui54c934be9b9dc910153284_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8IMmnO)
Velvet is definitely a fabric that screams luxe dressing. This velvet dress has a halter design and comes in a vibrant gold colour. It also has cross-over detailing at the front and a flippy skirt. It would make the perfect party dress this season. Team it with jewel drop earrings, an embellished clutch, and ankle boots.

## 4. Topshop Faux Fur Tuck Stole

![Topshop Faux Fur Tuck Stole](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/mg/umeb0l0i54c934bfaaf5b157180939_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6ZbKNY)
Jewel-toned accessories are going to be a hit this season. This coloured faux fur stole would make a wonderful winter fashion accessory. It is made from teal faux fur material and features a slotted fastening. Stay warm this winter by wearing it with a grey wool coat.

## 5. Michael Michael Kors Faux Fur Coat

![Michael Michael Kors Faux Fur Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/a0/xp27p18r54c934c115ee2410858631_382x496.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wSbyc1)
Nothing says luxe winter fashion quite like a faux fur coat. This coat is made from dark brown and light brown faux fur, and has a shaggy appearance. It has a round neckline and concealed hook fastenings through the front. Work a dressy evening look by wearing this coat with silk trousers, stiletto heels, and jewel-encrusted earrings.

## 6. 3.1 Phillip Lim Long Fingerless Gloves

![3.1 Phillip Lim Long Fingerless Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cn/ff/kg9q7kt954c934c239565399666769_347x498.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These gloves are a statement making piece. They are made from merino wool and feature rabbit fur cuffs. They also have a fingerless design. Show off the cuffs by wearing them with elbow-length coats.

## 7. Topshop Knitted Sequin Sweater

![Topshop Knitted Sequin Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/hn/pshbrqjc54c934c357b94300624669_384x498.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/t0I0z6)
Glam up your winter fashion look with this sequin sweater. This knitted sweater features all over sequin detailing and contrasting ribbed trims. It has a relaxed fit, with a bit of a sporty feel to it. Wear this sweater with dark red jeans, ankle boots, and a felt fedora for a chic new season look.

You can easily create a luxe look on a budget. Take inspiration from these winter fashion items when choosing what to buy for your new season wardrobe. What winter fashion items do you have in mind for this season?
