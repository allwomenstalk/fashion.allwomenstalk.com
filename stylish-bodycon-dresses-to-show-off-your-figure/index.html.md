# 9 Stylish Bodycon Dresses to Show off Your Figure ...

Bodycon dresses can be tricky to pull off. Typically left to those with svelte physiques, many of us would be quite hesitant to work a bodycon dress. However, with summer just around the corner it’s the perfect time to show off some skin and your summer toned body (because we’ve all been getting ready for summer by exercising our butts off, right?) If you’ve been wanting to show off your figure then take a look at the following stylish bodycon dresses.

## 1. Modcloth Bodycon Dress

![Modcloth Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_modcloth-bodycon-dress_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Choose bodycon dresses in classic colours to make sure they last more than one fashion cycle. Black is timeless, but navy makes for a nice twist. This bandeau dress has a subtle sweetheart neckline with a panelled body. Wear it with a statement necklace for a night out or with a cream blazer and patent loafers for a cool daytime look.

## 2. Forever21 Ruched Knit Dress

_ **Price:** $13.80 at forever21.com_
Amp up your look with this vampy red dress. It has short cap sleeves, a sweetheart neckline, and ruching throughout. This red dress would look great with silver crystal-embellished necklace and neutral pumps. This dress is also available in other colours such as black, navy, and royal blue.

## 3. Dorothy Perkins Bodycon Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_dorothy-perkins-bodycon-dress_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $57.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Bodycon dresses can work in more corporate settings, just look for more conservative styles. This dress, for example, has a longer hem and has more structured panelling at the bodice. Wear it with enclosed heels and minimal jewellery for a classically chic look.

## 4. Forever21 Knit Dot Dress

_ **Price:** $13.80 at forever21.com_
Polka dot patterns are on-trend this season, and this dress is a fun and affordable way to incorporate the look into your wardrobe. It has a round neckline, short sleeves, and a darted waist. This dress would look great with nude ballet flats and a framed side bag.

## 5. Topshop Denim Cupped Bandeau Dress

![Topshop Denim Cupped Bandeau Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_topshop-denim-cupped-bandeau-dress_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zo0Jwi)
If you’re worried about bodycon dresses being too clingy, choose dresses made from thicker fabrics. This peach coloured dress is made from denim and has a structured design. Team it with a cropped jacket and strappy heels for a stylish look.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Cream Block Bodycon Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Cream Block Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_dorothy-perkins-cream-block-bodycon-dress_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This neutral dress has a cross-over top and bodycon bandage skirt. The top of the dress is made from chiffon and has a v-neckline. Dress it up by teaming it with a pastel plazer and oversized clutch.

## 7. Topshop Cutout Mesh Bodycon Tunic

![Topshop Cutout Mesh Bodycon Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_topshop-cutout-mesh-bodycon-tunic_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1AawpP)
Bodycon dresses are perfect for working an athletic inspired look. This black bodycon dress has mesh side panels and cutout details. If you’re fit and toned and not afraid of showing some skin then this dress must be for you!

## 8. Dorothy Perkins Ribbed Bodcyon Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Ribbed Bodcyon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/8_dorothy-perkins-ribbed-bodcyon-dress_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $75.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This dress is a much more ladylike take on the bodycon trend. It has a high neckline, capped sleeves, and exposed zip at the back. The fabric also has an interesting cross hatch pattern. Team this dress with a statement necklace, strappy heels and an embellished clutch for a simple cocktail look.

If you’re still feeling self conscious, you can always team your bodycon dress with a blazer or jacket to hide any lumps and bumps. What do you think of these bodycon dresses? Do you have a favourite style?
