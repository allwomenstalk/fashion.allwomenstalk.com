# 9 Office Friendly Dresses under $50 ...

As the holiday season ends, many of us prepare to return to daytime office friendly dress. Darn, just when I was getting into the Christmas sweater groove, too. Are you in search of a few new office friendly dress options? If you are interested in refreshing your closet in a budget friendly way then we offer nine (of many) options for an office friendly dress under $50 that are worth a glance.

## 1. V for Victory Belted Pencil Dress

![V for Victory Belted Pencil Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005003_3433_599x888.jpg)

When I first saw this dress I was struck by the color and the neckline details. If you want a lovely, comfortable, and professional office friendly dress, consider the V for Victory Belted Pencil Dress in burgundy from Lily Boutique. The wide neckline, simple cut and bold color are fantastic. The mini v-shaped neckline dip is a tasteful and unexpected touch. All that is nice, but the feature that tipped the scale and won my hear are the pockets! Pair this lovely frock with nice heels and minimal accessories. It can be yours for just $32.

## 2. 2 in 1 Pencil Dress

![2 in 1 Pencil Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005004_8991_599x806.jpg)

The 2 in 1 Pencil dress from Dorothy Perkins is a stylish take on the two-tone look. Not only is the dress two-tone but the bodice also has an interesting pattern. I like everything about this dress. The fit is great and the cap sleeves are comfy. The 2 in 1 Pencil Dress also features a knee length hem. If you like this style, try wearing it with a bold pop of color, like red – my favorite! At a mere $27, you can definitely afford some fun accessories too.

## 3. Luxe Sheath Dress

![Luxe Sheath Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005004_8081_600x881.jpg)

Forever 21 has a large selection of dresses in all sorts of styles and colors. Not all are office appropriate, but this one certainly is. The Luxe Sheath Dress is fun and pretty. It has a pretty cute pattern and simple color pallet that you can wear in the office. This dress features a round neckline, pleated skirt, and hidden zipper. If you want to add another cute patterned dress to your work options, consider this piece as a contender. The style is cute and at $24.80, the price is right.

## 4. S SLV Dress in Cherry Blossom

![S SLV Dress in Cherry Blossom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005007_2752_599x543.jpg)

The S SLV Dress in Cherry Blossom from Ally Fashion reminds me of one of those fantastic mod dresses of the early sixties that I love. When I first read the name, I thought S SLV was a rather strange abbreviation for “Short Sleeve.” I thought I’d mention that, just in case you are as puzzled as I was about those letters. Nevertheless, this dress is very cute and incredibly simple. The S SLV Dress in Cherry Blossom has a round neckline, fit and flare style, all over print, and exposed zipper with a mini cut length. Plus, it's just $34.99.

## 5. Nia Black 10 Chain Neck Shift Dress

![Nia Black 10 Chain Neck Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005005_2586_599x820.jpg)

Aside from the fantastic cut, this dress also has terrific sleeves. The midi Nia Black 10 Chain Neck Shift Dress from Fashion Union is classic comfy. This beauty has flared sleeves, rounded neckline, and hidden back zipper. It also features a removable attached half-necklace. The instant accessory is unexpected and complementary. Fashion Union is based in Manchester. The dress costs £15, or approximately $20.63, and international shipping is available.

## 6. Blouse Dress

![Blouse Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005003_9523_383x415.jpg)

The H&M Blouse Dress is another cute two-tone dress. This one has some pretty neat details, like long comfortable sleeves, hidden buttons, and collar with a little drop waist short skirt sass. It also has a removable tie that looks like a large, droopy bow. The H&M Blouse Dress is button-up professional without being stuffy. This would look lovely with a high bun and bright accessories. You can grab it for $34.95.

## 7. M.X.O. Dark Blue Shift Dress

![M.X.O. Dark Blue Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005008_6900_599x550.jpg)

When I first saw this dress, I immediately noticed the fantastic shape and black detail. I don’t know why, but this dress reminded me of the US television show: Commander In Chief (2005-2006). The M.X.O. Dark Blue Shift Dress from T.K. Maxx has a scoop neckline, above the knee hem, and back zipper. The shape and color are subtle and complementary. Wear this piece with statement jewelry and fabulous shoes. T.K. Maxx is based in the UK. This dress costs £16, or an estimated $22, and international shipping is available.

## 8. Amy Pleat Detail Shift Dress

![Amy Pleat Detail Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005007_7108_600x900.jpg)

Boohoo.com has a fantastic selection of dresses for all occasions. The Amy Pleat Detail Shift Dress is a lovely option that costs just $40. This dress is available in blue and black, but the blue color option really highlights the nice simple front pleat and flowing fabric. The Amy Pleat Detail Shift Dress also has an exposed gold zipper in back and ¾ length sleeve. This dress would look great with leggings, boots, and scarf or pretty necklace. Boohoo is a UK based company with international affiliates that include the US. As such, shipping costs are quite affordable.

## 9. Op Music Dress

![Op Music Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/17005006_8002_250x357.jpg)

Last on our short list is this fantastic optical patterned Op Music Dress from Modcloth. The black and white vertical zigzag pattern is cute and totally office appropriate. Paired with the right accessories, and of course a pop of bold color, this dress can take you from office duty to happy hour with friends. It can be yours for $33.99.

I live in pants; they are my go to style and comfort zone. But I also love a cute dress that can do double duty from work to cocktails. I especially love finding cute dresses that are also cost efficient. These nine dresses on our list are just a small sampling of the endless options of cute office friendly dresses under $50. Check them out. The stores are all pretty cool, too. You just might find a few other bargains to your liking there as well. What do you wear to work? Please, share.
