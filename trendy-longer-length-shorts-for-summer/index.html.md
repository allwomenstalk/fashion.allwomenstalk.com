# 8 Trendy Longer Length Shorts for Summer ...

Longer length shorts may be hard to find amongst a sea of hotpants and short shorts this season, but they are out there. A longer length short is an ideal alternative for those wanting to work a casual look but with a little more coverage when it comes to shorts. Whether you’re after dressed-down denim or tailored styles, there are a range of longer length shorts to choose from this season. Take a look at a few fashionable longer length shorts, below.

## 1. Topshop Moto Acid Pink Cycling Shorts

![Topshop Moto Acid Pink Cycling Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-moto-acid-pink-cycling-shorts_400x543.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/JeBHNg)
It’s all about coloured denim this year. Everywhere you look, designers are embracing a rainbow of denim colours. These ones come in a bright acid pink colour and have a slim leg with frayed trims. These longer length shorts from Topshop take their cue from the popular Christopher Kane ones, but are a much more affordable alternative.

## 2. J Brand Denim Stretch Denim Shorts

![J Brand Denim Stretch Denim Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_j-brand-denim-stretch-denim-shorts_400x473.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PkpjWs)
Finding the right pair of denim shorts can be quite a mission, and longer length styles are perfect for working a casual look. These ones are made from faded stretch denim and feature five pocket detailing and rolled cuffs. These shorts would look great worn with a basic white t-shirt and leather sandals.

## 3. Dorothy Perkins Aqua Chino Knee Shorts

![Dorothy Perkins Aqua Chino Knee Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_dorothy-perkins-aqua-chino-knee-shorts_400x516.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Summer is all about dressing in colour, and these longer length shorts are a stylish choice. They come in an on-trend aqua colour and have a fuss-free design. Wear them with a loose button-up blouse and bejewelled sandals for a chic daytime look.

## 4. Topshop Longline Bermuda Shorts

![Topshop Longline Bermuda Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_topshop-longline-bermuda-shorts_400x549.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wkCOEU)
Basic black shorts are a staple for any wardrobe. Choose one in a longer length style like these Topshop shorts. They are baggy throughout the leg and feature pleating at the front. Wear these with a basic singlet and wedge heels for an effortlessly stylish look.

## 5. Junya Watanabe Asymmetric Twill Shorts

![Junya Watanabe Asymmetric Twill Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_junya-watanabe-asymmetric-twill-shorts_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $445.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DSWo4P)
These longer length shorts have a very contemporary and fashion-forward design. I wouldn’t expect anything less from a designer such as Junya Watanabe. What really sets these black shorts apart is their asymmetric hem. Emphasise the design of these shorts with a statement top and shoes.

## 6. Topshop Moto Cyan Stripe Cycling Shorts

![Topshop Moto Cyan Stripe Cycling Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_topshop-moto-cyan-stripe-cycling-shorts_400x552.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ueOskK)
Stripes never go out of fashion. Incorporate them into your look this summer in new ways such as these striped shorts. These longer length shorts have a slim fitting design and feature a blue and white striped pattern. They’d look great teamed with a camel coloured t-shirt and strappy leather sandals.

## 7. 3.1 Phillip Lim Drawstring Leather Shorts

![3.1 Phillip Lim Drawstring Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_3-1-phillip-lim-drawstring-leather-shorts_400x471.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You wouldn’t usually expect leather to be used when making longer length shorts but these hit the mark. They have a lambskin waistband and have side seam zippers. They’d be perfect for working a relaxed, boyish silhouette. Pity these stylish longer length shorts are way out of my price range.

There are a range of different styles of longer length shorts to choose from this summer. Denim is always a popular choice, but other fabrics such as leather give the humble short a contemporary twist. What do you think of the longer length short and is it something you’ll be wearing this season?
