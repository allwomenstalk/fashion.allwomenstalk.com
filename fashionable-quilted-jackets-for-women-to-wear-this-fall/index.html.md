# 8 Fashionable Quilted Jackets for Women to Wear This Fall ...

Quilted jackets for women are a stylish option this fall. From leather jackets to more traditional trench coats, quilted details are taking centre stage this season. A quilted jacket can look like anything from on-trend to timeless, depending on the design and how you style it. Check out a few stylish women’s quilted jackets, below.

## 1. Quilted Leather Biker Jacket

![Quilted Leather Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_quilted-leather-biker-jacket_230x345.jpg)

Rock an edgy look in a quilted leather jacket this fall. Wear them with a foolproof uniform of skinny jeans and t-shirts, or something a bit more glam with a maxi dress and statement jewelry. Such quilted jackets for women can range anywhere in price from $30 for a faux leather Forever 21 number to $2000 for this J.W. Anderson quilted leather jacket from Net-A-Porter.

## 2. Quilted Fleece Jacket

![Quilted Fleece Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_quilted-fleece-jacket_350x500.jpg)

Work a more casual approach with quilted jackets this season and choose one in a fleecy fabric. It’d look great teamed with dark denim jeans, ankle boots, and a floppy fedora. This cozy looking jacket costs $95.00 at Modcloth but you can also find similar sporty styles at Urban Outfitters and ASOS.

## 3. Quilted Puffer Jacket

![Quilted Puffer Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_quilted-puffer-jacket_328x400.jpg)

Stay warm in style this winter with women’s quilted jackets. A puffer jacket like this chic belted number from Forever 21 will keep you toasty warm when the temperature drops. These styles of quilted jackets are quite common and you can pick up similar belted styles from stores like Zara and H&M.

## 4. Quilted Fur Collar Jacket

![Quilted Fur Collar Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_quilted-fur-collar-jacket_600x900.jpg)

A lot of winter coats feature fur (or faux fur) details. A basic quilted jacket looks a bit more luxe with a bit of fur detailing. This particular Topshop jacket comes with a detachable fur collar, which is great for having that added versatility. Other brands like River Island and Dorothy Perkins also offer affordable quilted jackets in this style.

## 5. Long Quilted Parka

![Long Quilted Parka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_long-quilted-parka_342x464.jpg)

A longer jacket can look much more flattering and chic. When it comes to quilted jackets for women this fall, a quilted parka like this one from Dorothy Perkins is a stylish option. There’s a similar Chloé quilted parka floating around right now, but I’m sure you’ll find this version a lot easier on the wallet.

## 6. Quilted Military Jacket

![Quilted Military Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_quilted-military-jacket_600x815.jpg)

A military inspired jacket is a must have this fall. It’s a look that dominated the runways and can easily be translated into your wardrobe. You don’t have to spend thousands of dollars on a regal looking Alexander McQueen or Lanvin design either, with this jacket clocking in at $69 from Dorothy Perkins’ online store.

## 7. Quilted Bomber Jacket

![Quilted Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_quilted-bomber-jacket_600x900.jpg)

Bomber jackets are another popular style right now. They easily play into this season’s sporty and utilitarian trends. Made popular by the trendsetting Alexander Wang, brands like Oasis at ASOS, Adidas, and Topshop all have stylish versions of the quilted bomber jacket in their current collections.

## 8. Quilted Metallic Jacket

_ Go all out glam in a metallic quilted jacket this season. A metallic jacket is great for sprucing up an evening outfit. You don’t have to spend a lot of money to pull off this trend either, with this particular jacket clocking in at $22.50 from Forever 21. Remember to keep the rest of your outfit fairly minimal to really let the jacket shine.

It could be an all over quilted look, or maybe quilted patches at the shoulders. What we do know is that quilted jackets for women are a fashionable choice for fall. What do you think of these women’s quilted jackets? How would you style them this fall?
