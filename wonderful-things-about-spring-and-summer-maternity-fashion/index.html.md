# 7 Wonderful Things about Spring and Summer Maternity Fashion ...

Spring and summer maternity fashion can be so much fun. It is definitely difficult to be pregnant in the summer because the heat feels so much more stifling, but you can absolutely look gorgeous while you glow. These are 7 wonderful and fun things about spring and summer maternity fashion.

## 1. Maxi Dresses and Skirts Are Perfect Maternity Wear

![Maxi Dresses and Skirts Are Perfect Maternity Wear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/rm/y2h19bki_600x913.jpg)

How I wish that maxi dresses and skirts had been in style when I needed spring and summer maternity fashion. These maxi pieces are perfect maternity wear. They are lovely and lend an air of femininity. They are also much cooler than some other clothing pieces you could choose, and I cannot neglect mentioning how comfortable they are.

## 2. Shoes Will Fit

![Shoes Will Fit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/lf/bnrixvzn_600x851.jpg)

Ladies, if you have not reached the last trimester of a pregnancy, you may not know this yet. But the truth is that most women suffer from swollen feet when they are at the end of their pregnancy. This can be a problem when it comes to what shoes you can comfortably wear. This is less of a problem in the summer. Sandals are the perfect shoe choice for swollen feet and there are so many adorable options.

## 3. Embrace the Cami/Cardigan Combo

![Embrace the Cami/Cardigan Combo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/fz/tunbjf7u_400x600.jpg)

The cami and cardigan combo is so attractive. It works for women whether they are expecting or not, but this look is really fashionable for pregnant women. I love that maternity fashion no longer tries to hide the bump, but instead focuses on showing it off. The cami and cardigan combo makes the most of this.

## 4. Warm Weather Fashions Are Simpler

![Warm Weather Fashions Are Simpler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/qm/flsk16y5_600x900.jpg)

Is it just me or do you feel this way, too? Warm weather fashions are much simpler. You can mix and match. A basic tank or t-shirt can take you a long way. This versatile advantage is very helpful to expectant moms.

## 5. Anything Will Pair with Maternity Jeans

![Anything Will Pair with Maternity Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uy/j6/wd197bcm_600x901.jpg)

Maternity jeans are one of the must have pieces when you are pregnant. You can do so much with them. You can dress them up or down, depending on the occasion. And in the spring and summer, all you really have to do is add a tank or a tee and sandals. Then you are good to go.

## 6. Accessories Are More Fun in the Spring and Summer

![Accessories Are More Fun in the Spring and Summer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/0o/nh5ofep7_600x900.jpg)

Maybe I feel this way because they're my favorite seasons, but accessories can make an outfit. And there are so many fun accessories to choose from in bright, bold colors in the spring and summer. You should never underestimate the power of accessories when you are pregnant. You can add them to a basic white t-shirt and skirt or jeans and have an adorable outfit. Accessories are also something you can enjoy now and after you have your baby.

## 7. Spring and Summer Clothing is Typically Cheaper

![Spring and Summer Clothing is Typically Cheaper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/09/ji/y2tap6qu_350x578.jpg)

I don’t know why this is, but it seems that spring and summer clothing is typically cheaper. Perhaps it is because it takes less fabric to make the pieces. Who knows? But cheaper prices allow you to buy more. That’s a win-win any way you go. You get to have more fun shopping for your maternity clothing and more fun wearing all your beautiful choices.

There are a lot of fun things about spring and summer maternity fashion. What is your favorite? Do you have any tips or tricks to share about a maternity wardrobe?
