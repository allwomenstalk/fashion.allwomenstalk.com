# 9 Current Designer Collaborations Worth Checking out ...

There are plenty of designer collaborations worth checking out right now. Whether it’s a well known designer teaming up with a chain store brand or designers joining forces, the designer collaboration can come in many forms. In some cases the goal is to create a more affordable line of clothing for buyers while in other cases the collaboration is more artistic. Whatever the case may be, these are just a few current designer collaborations worth checking out.

## 1. Derek Lam X Kohl’s DesigNation

![Derek Lam X Kohl’s DesigNation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/1_derek-lam-x-kohls-designation_600x800.jpg)

One of the most popular forms of designer collaborations is when a well known designer teams up with a more affordable retailer. This collaboration still carries the same kind of vibe as Derek Lam’s high end line, but at a more affordable price point. The collection is full of relaxed, tailored clothing and prices range from $36 to $88.

## 2. DVF X Roxy

![DVF X Roxy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/2_dvf-x-roxy_600x801.jpg)

Hit the beach in style with pieces from the collaboration between Diane von Furstenberg and Roxy. This limited edition collection features a mix of itsy bitsy bikinis, sporty rash guards, and relaxed cover ups. All the pieces are priced under $100, which is definitely music to our ears.

## 3. Keds X Kate Spade New York

![Keds X Kate Spade New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/3_keds-x-kate-spade-new-york_600x476.jpg)

Designer Kate Spade is well known for her colourful collections. She brings her trademark style to this latest collaboration with Keds. The shoes are all priced under $100 and come in a range of cute colours and patterns.

## 4. Prabal Gurung X Target

![Prabal Gurung X Target](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/4_prabal-gurung-x-target_600x900.jpg)

Target is pretty well known for their designer collaborations. Their latest collaboration with Prabal Gurung was probably one of their most anticipated yet. The collection consists of a range of colourful pieces and eye-catching prints. Prices range from $12.99 to $199.99, so there’s something for everyone.

## 5. Rihanna X River Island

![Rihanna X River Island](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/5_rihanna-x-river-island_600x400.jpg)

Rihanna can add ‘designer’ to her resume thanks to her recent collaboration with River Island. Her fashion tastes are always under the microscope and her fame means she’s a style icon of sorts. It was only a matter of time before she took the clothing collaboration route. The capsule collection is very much in keeping with Rihanna’s own personal style.

## 6. Preen X Aldo

![Preen X Aldo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/6_preen-x-aldo_600x900.jpg)

As part of their collaboration program, Aldo Rise, Aldo has teamed up with designer label Preen to create a capsule collection of fashion-forward shoes. The shoes from this collaboration are pretty pricey at around $150 a pop, but they’re still relatively affordable compared to how much you’d usually pay for Preen pieces.

## 7. Mary Katrantzou X Current/Elliott

![Mary Katrantzou X Current/Elliott](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/7_mary-katrantzou-x-current-elliott_600x554.jpg)

Mary Katrantzou and Current/Elliott were already popular brands in their own right. So we were always guaranteed good things when these two decided to join forces. This collaboration features Mary Katrantzou’s trademark colourful prints displayed on Current/Elliott’s cool denim pieces. If you’re looking for affordable pieces, however, look elsewhere because many of the clothes in this collection will set you back a couple of hundred dollars.

## 8. Kenzo X Vans

![Kenzo X Vans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/8_kenzo-x-vans_600x337.jpg)

Decorate your feet in style with this collaboration from Kenzo and Vans. This is actually the fourth collection between these two brands. The collaboration features classic Vans shoes decorated in a range of colourful Kenzo prints. You’ll still have to fork out $100+ dollars for a pair of these cool kicks though.

## 9. Milly X Banana Republic

![Milly X Banana Republic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/9_milly-x-banana-republic_600x544.jpg)

Milly teams up with Banana Republic to offer us a range of affordable clothes inspired by the designer brand. Girls aren’t the only ones to benefit from this collaboration either, with guys being catered to as well. Women’s pieces will be priced from $45 to $175, while pieces from the men’s range will cost between $25 and $65. You’ll have to wait a bit longer to get your hands on this collection as it won’t be available until May.

These are just a few designer collaborations worth checking out. Are you a fan of the designer collaboration? What designer collaborations are you excited about?
