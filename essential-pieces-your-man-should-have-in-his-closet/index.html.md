# 5 Essential Pieces Your Man  Should Have in His Closet ...

Look, I know that beauty is the eye of the beholder and then when you fall in love, you fall for the person regardless of what they look like or how they dress, but there is no getting away from the fact that a well-dressed man is a wonderful sight to behold! If you have a boyfriend who could do with a slight fashion makeover, and importantly doesn’t mind you getting involved with his choices, then here are five **essential pieces that your man should have in his closet** to stay looking on point!

## 1. White Shirt

You absolutely cannot go wrong with a classic white shirt. A nice, fitted shirt is an immediately solid base for all formal wear. It might not stand out compared to other things in the closet, but it’s something that your man will go back to time and time again.

## 2. Smart Suit

It’s really worth investing in a tailored suit that is fitted to his specific measurements. The difference between off the rack and fitted to measure is unbelievable.

## 3. Go-to Sweater

Every guy should have a great go-to sweater that he can pair with both casual and smart casual outfits. A sweater is a classically mature garment to own and you’ll find that he opts for it more often than not.

## 4. All Weather Jacket

An all weather jacket is an essential piece, because it combines the practical with fashionable unlike most other garments of its kind. Something like a slim line parka is perfect.

## 5. Quality Denim

Denim is something that is never, ever going to go out of fashion, so it is really worth investing in some great quality signature pieces that he will be able to bring out year after year whilst still being on trend.
