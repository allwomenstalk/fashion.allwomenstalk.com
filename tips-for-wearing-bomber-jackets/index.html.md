# 7 Tips for Wearing Bomber Jackets ...

How to wear bomber jacket styles well will get you ahead of the fashion pack this season. If you haven’t already noticed, bomber jackets are having a bit of a fashion moment. They’re perfect for pulling off the off-duty, sportswear inspired trends that are dominating the catwalks, shop fronts, and streets right now. Want to know how to wear the bomber jacket this season? Read on!

## 1. Stylish in Silk

![Stylish in Silk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/128_577x880.jpg)

Silk bomber jackets are oh so hot right now. They’re the perfect combination of slouch and sophistication. They’re also lightweight enough to be worn in the warmer months, making them perfect for transitional dressing. When thinking about how to wear bomber jacket styles like this, team a lightweight bomber with heavier, stiffer fabrics (like denim) for some balance.

## 2. Like Leather

![Like Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/220_612x918.jpg)

A leather bomber jacket is probably the bomber in its most original form. It’s perfect for creating a cool, aviator inspired look. Team a brown leather bomber jacket with denim jeans and a white t-shirt for an effortlessly cool look.

## 3. Stay Sporty

![Stay Sporty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/317_800x533.jpg)

Bomber jackets are so great because they give off a relaxed, ‘I didn’t even have to try’ kind of vibe. Perfect for working the sport luxe trend! When looking at how to wear a bomber jacket this way, work a look that’s been adopted by fashion editors the world over and team a more structured style with slouchy pants and heels.

## 4. Get Printed

![Get Printed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/418_1000x667.jpg)

Lately it’s all about prints, prints, and more prints! Bomber jackets are no exception to this trend. Plenty of places like Topshop, ASOS, and Forever 21 have printed bomber jackets in their collections. Try one in a cool floral or scarf print to jazz up a casual weekend outfit.

## 5. Crop It

![Crop It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/519_1300x1950.jpg)

Bomber jackets tend to be pretty short on the body, but take it to stylish new heights in a cropped style. When it comes to how to wear bomber jacket styles this way, a cropped style of jacket is perfect for wearing with [high waisted skirts](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-high-waisted-skirts/) and pants as it shows off your waistline.

## 6. Go Feminine

![Go Feminine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/618_486x741.jpg)

Since bomber jackets tend to be more relaxed and boxy, they can come across as slightly more masculine. Style them in a more feminine way either by wearing them over dresses and skirts or by choosing jackets in light, pastel shades.

## 7. Go Masculine

![Go Masculine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/719_660x924.jpg)

For a more masculine take on how to wear bomber jacket styles this season, there are plenty of preppy and athletic ways to work the look. Stick to classic styles of bomber jackets and them with cropped tuxedo style pants while playing up accessories like chunky watches and brogue shoes.

It pays to be creative when it comes to how to wear bomber jacket styles this season. Many of the styles available at the moment are fun and fresh, and perfect for updating your wardrobe. What’s your best style tip for how to wear the bomber jacket?
