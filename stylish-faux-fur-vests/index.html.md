# 7 Stylish Faux Fur Vests ...

A Faux Fur Vest is just one way to vamp up your new season look. Worn over a basic tee and jeans, the faux fur vest is a chic piece for fall. From cropped styles to longline versions, there’s a faux fur vest for all styles and budgets. The faux fur vest looks especially stylish when paired with this season’s hottest leather pieces - think leather skirts, pants, and leggings. Take a look at the following stylish faux fur vests.

## 7. Topshop Longline Chevron Faux Fur Vest

![Topshop Longline Chevron Faux Fur Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/my/avdh4jxt54c93041df244627129716_174x400.jpg)
