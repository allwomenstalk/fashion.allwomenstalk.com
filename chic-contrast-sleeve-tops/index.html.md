# 7 Chic Contrast Sleeve Tops ...

**Contrast sleeve top styles are popping** up all over the place. Classic styles like the raglan/baseball tee are ideal for working this season’s athletic aesthetic, but there are a range of other contrast sleeve tops available as well. They can range from knitwear to button-up blouses. Take some inspiration from the following stylish tops with contrast sleeves.

## 1. Topshop Knitted Mesh Sweater

![Topshop Knitted Mesh Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-knitted-mesh-sweater_366x400.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/H4aTqf)
This sweater resembles a classic baseball tee, but it comes in the form of a knitted mesh sweater. This contrast sleeve top has a fitted design and three quarter length sleeves. The colour combination of red and white is very athletic. It’d look great worn with a dark denim skirt or jeans.

## 2. Mulberry Contrast Sleeve Jersey Top

![Mulberry Contrast Sleeve Jersey Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_mulberry-contrast-sleeve-jersey-top_302x400.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/yYdBkA)
This jersey top has a raglan/baseball inspired design and comes in a blush and ecru colourway. The lighter colour palette and long-sleeved design makes it ideal for wearing during cooler spring days. When styling this top, be sure to incorporate bright bursts of colour into your outfit.

## 3. Zoe Karssen Contrast Sleeve Top

![Zoe Karssen Contrast Sleeve Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_zoe-karssen-contrast-sleeve-top_352x400.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Jg57qI)
This short sleeved top is simple and chic. It’s perfect for wearing on the weekends and your days off. Like the previous two shirts, it has a very athletic inspired design. It’s made from semi sheer cotton and features faded black sleeves and curved hems. This top would look great worn with coral colour jeans or a denim skirt.

## 4. Forever21 Whimsical Puffed Sleeve Top

_ **Price:** $18.90 at forever21.com_
The puffed sleeves on this top give it a much more feminine twist. It’s a bit of an updated take on the athletic baseball tee. Despite this, the navy and white colour combination is quite classic. Try working a quirkier angle and wear this top with this season’s striped or polka dot denim.

## 5. Juicy Couture Contrast Sweatshirt

![Juicy Couture Contrast Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_juicy-couture-contrast-sweatshirt_329x400.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/83dYUf)
If bling’s your thing then take a look at this contrast sleeve top. It’s a doozy! It features blue-grey sequinned tulle to the front and back, and has contrasting velour sleeves. It would look great worn it at night with a pair of tailored cropped pants or a fun mini skirt. Of course, all those sparkly embellishments come at a price so steer clear of this top if you’re on a budget.

## 6. Forever21 Soft Contrast Hooded Top

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
For a more **relaxed take on contrast sleeve tops** you can’t go past this lightweight top. It features a drawstring hood, kangaroo pocket at the front, contrast raglan sleeves, and banded hemlines and cuffs. You could wear it playing sport, or just if you want to look sporty.

## 7. Carven Contrast Sleeve Lace Shirt

![Carven Contrast Sleeve Lace Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_carven-contrast-sleeve-lace-shirt_310x400.jpg)

**Price:** $775.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZP6Ir6)
Many of the contrast sleeve tops on this list have had relaxed, slouchy designs. This shirt however, is very much the opposite. It has a button-up design with a white lace bodice and cobalt blue sleeves. It’s quite the statement-maker. Pity about the price tag, though.

A contrast sleeve top can look sporty or fashion-forward, depending on the style and design you’re after. Contrast sleeves are also a unique way of working the colour blocking trend. Which of these contrast sleeve tops do you like best?
