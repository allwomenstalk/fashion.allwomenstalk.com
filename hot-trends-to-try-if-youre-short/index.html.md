# 12 Hot Trends to Try if Youre Short ...

I’m not necessarily tall, but I’m not necessarily short, either. Because of that, I spend a lot of time envying trends on both ends of the spectrum. There are some trends that look amazing on tall girls, and other trends that look amazing on short girls. A lot of my friends are shorter, so I spend a lot of time wishing that I could rock trends that they do! If you’re a short girl looking for trends to rock this year, these are definitely the outfits you should check out!

## 1. Mini Skirts

![Mini Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p7/ee/ba0r8i4z5632b4e92a34e432547185_500x753.jpg)

If you are able to wear a mini skirt, you probably fairly short, and for that, I am so jealous. I love mini skirts so much, but my biggest issue is that they never fit quite right. They’re always just a little shorter than they’re supposed to be. If you’re short, rock mini skirts with pride, because you’re one of the few who can wear them perfectly!

## 2. The Shift Dress

![The Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/fs/ymw9xxex5632b8d40a099533198312_415x524.jpg)

I don’t know about you, but shift dresses are easily my favorite trend of the past few years. It’s not just a very flattering trend on me, though. I love the shift dress trend because it looks so mod, but out of all of the body types and sizes, short girls look the best in a shift dress!

## 3. That Deep-V Sweater Dress

![clothing, footwear, road, street, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lu/vu/kvnajhdz58768413cc4b4958252361_600x900.jpg)

I’m sure that you’ve seen that deep-v sweater dress that’s completely taken over the trends for the past few months, especially if you’re a teenager girl. It’s a trend that’s so cute and boho, but unfortunately for a lot of girls, it’s too short to be a dress and it turns into more of a sweater than a dress. Step into this sweater dress and rock it!

## 4. Ballet Flats

![clothing, dress, pink, lady, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gh/d0/ramjuybv587684470714e405201464_600x900.jpg)

I have a lot of short friends, and they all insist on wearing heels almost all of the time. Don’t get me wrong, I don’t blame if you like to wear heels, but I think that’s there nothing more stylish on a girl when she’s comfortable enough in the outfit she’s wearing to rock ballet flats instead of heels!

## 5. Ridiculously High Heels

![black, clothing, red, footwear, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x3/re/cdmnsd5k5876847b7a836828443258_600x896.jpg)

On the same note, short girls are the only few girls out there who can wear a pair of high heels that are so high it’s insane, but they still look fabulous and stylish. Most girls would look ridiculous, but when a short girl wears insanely high heels, it looks like it’s supposed to be a fashion statement!

## 6. Basic White T-Shirt

![Basic White T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/x9/chd8agdo5632ba320dc81283165653_600x900.jpg)

Okay, so most girls can rock a plain, white t-shirt, but don’t you agree that there’s something about the way it fits on a smaller torso, where it becomes a little oversized and looks so flattering. Like I said, everyone looks flattering in white t-shirts, but when they get a little oversized, they look so fashionable!

## 7. Crop Tops

![clothing, dress, spring, fashion, little black dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/z3/fx73c5un587684b31e66c703968739_527x790.jpg)

A lot of short girls are self-conscious about having a shorter torso. However, that just makes a crop top look amazing on you! Whether it’s loose or tight, crop tops are flattering on shorter frames!

## 8. A Mini Bag

![color, red, pink, clothing, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/dh/kcoj6lyj58778ec857387600913710_600x900.jpg)

The tiny purse won't overwhelm your petite frame and they're super fashionable with tons of different patterns.

## 9. Super High-Waisted Items

![clothing, blue, denim, jeans, footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r0/b3/v6f8n6ud58778ee523621254819506_500x751.jpg)

A thin high waist pencil skirt or glam high-rise jeans, the extra waist coverage will enlong your tiny frame making you appear taller.

## 10. A Line Skirt

![clothing, dress, spring, footwear, sleeve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/oc/tquzakoh58778f1c08a44956463805_600x800.jpg)

The cute and playful hemline of an A-line skirt paired with its pulled in waistline, will give your tiny gams a lengthy look. Pair with pointed toe pumps for even a few more inches in appearance.

## 11. Side Slit Skirt

![clothing, footwear, fashion, spring, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bt/nf/ljdvz39a58779107159b2725731866_600x900.jpg)

These are fab for short girls! They give you the length you may want without hiding your legs making them appear shorter by cutting off at a certain point.

## 12. Clean Lined Outerwear

![clothing, fashion, spring, season, fashion show,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/ds/d41ody1q587791311fa2e261047656_500x750.jpg)

Be sure your outerwear consists of a sleek structure in a neutral color for a lean and luxe look to your smaller body.

What is your favorite trend if you’re a short girl? If you’re not short, what do you always envy that short girls can wear? I think I envy all of these trends! They just wear them so well! Let me know what you think in the comments!
