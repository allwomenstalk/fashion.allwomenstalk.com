# 7 Cute and Cheap Accessories That Every Girl Should Own ...

If you’re on the hunt for some cheap and cute accessories to complement your wardrobe, have I got some eye candy for you! There are plenty of charming and stylish accessories that you can afford on just about any budget. So go ahead, spice up your wardrobe with some of these cheap and cute accessories. All of these accessories are under $15 and no one will ever know!

## 1. Lipstick Necklace by Hello Drama

![Lipstick Necklace by Hello Drama](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/1_lipstick-necklace-by-hello-drama_175x160.jpg)

These days we have so many more choices when it comes to cheap and cute accessories, so you don’t have to sacrifice style because you’re on a budget! This necklace is a fun way to punch up a casual outfit while you’re running errands or add some interest to a girls’ night out outfit. This is a really cute and unique piece of jewelry that shows your love of makeup!

## 2. Black Lace Style Silver Iced out Sideways Cross Macrame Bracelet by JOTW

![Black Lace Style Silver Iced out Sideways Cross Macrame Bracelet by JOTW](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/2_black-lace-style-silver-iced-out-sideways-cross-macrame-bracelet-by-jotw_512x336.jpg)

If you like to rock a lot of arm candy, this sparkly, thin cross bracelet will make a great addition to your collection! If you’re not into the arm party, this bracelet is also blingy enough to wear alone. I love the mix of black and silver but I think the best thing about this piece is that it’s under $2! At that price, you can’t go wrong!

## 3. Three on Braided HeadWrap by Wet Seal

![Three on Braided HeadWrap by Wet Seal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/3_three-on-braided-headwrap-by-wet-seal_600x340.jpg)

This is one of those cheap accessories that will come in handy when you least expect. You can easily wrap this cute braided headwrap around misbehaving hair and you look instantly polished. Or wear it when you don’t feel like rocking your bangs. Either way, it’s a stylish way to keep your hair out of your face and still look presentable!

## 4. Wrapped PU Turn-lock Bracelet by Charlotte Russe

![Wrapped PU Turn-lock Bracelet by Charlotte Russe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/4_wrapped-pu-turn-lock-bracelet-by-charlotte-russe_257x130.jpg)

This little baby reminds me of a pricey, designer inspired piece of accessories for women! It’s chic, understated and comes in red, black and taupe, so it’ll look great with pretty much everything you have! To me, one of the best things in life is finding a versatile accessory that’s under $10 that I’ll wear time and time again!

## 5. Holly GoNightly Sleep Mask by Fred Flare

![Holly GoNightly Sleep Mask by Fred Flare](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/5_holly-gonightly-sleep-mask-by-fred-flare_280x200.jpg)

So this isn’t exactly one of cute and cheap accessories that you’d wear out, but it’s still adorable nonetheless! Wear this Holly GoLightly inspired sleep mask to block out all the light and ensure that your eyes are not disturbed while you get your beauty sleep. Not only will you get a good night’s sleep, you’ll also look super glamorous while you do it!

## 6. ASOS Gold Pyramid Stud Ring

![ASOS Gold Pyramid Stud Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/6_asos-gold-pyramid-stud-ring_144x153.jpg)

I love wearing rings and one of my favorite styles is a simple, chunky ring. Accessories for women don’t have to be either tacky costume jewels or majorly high-end baubles. I think this ring strikes the perfect balance between the two worlds and can be worn with every day, casual wear or to toughen up a girly outfit.

## 7. LC Lauren Conrad Gold Tone Simulated Crystal Hoop Earrings

![LC Lauren Conrad Gold Tone Simulated Crystal Hoop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/7_lc-lauren-conrad-gold-tone-simulated-crystal-hoop-earrings_338x305.jpg)

Lauren Conrad makes some super ladylike yet cheap accessories that are perfect for dressing up both informal and more semi-formal outfits. These glitzy hoop earrings add the perfect touch of sparkle and gold that are much more flattering than just plain old hoop earrings!

These accessories for women are a fun and easy way to dress up your looks without breaking the bank. I’m a firm believer in and buyer of cheap accessories because I can’t imagine spending top dollar for super trendy jewelry that could be a big, fat no next week.
