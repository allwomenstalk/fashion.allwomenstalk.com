The Most Gorgeous Red Carpet Gowns of 2015 ...

Taylor Swift at the Grammy Awards • Fan Bingbing at the Cannes Film Festival • Aishwarya Rai at the Cannes Film Festival • Jennifer Lopez at the American Music Awards • Lena Headey at the Emmy Awards • More ...

By Sheila

2015 is coming to a close (seriously, can time slow down?!) and that means it's time to review the best and most gorgeous gowns that adorned the red carpet this year. Ready?

1. Taylor Swift at the Grammy Awards

Taylor was a breath of fresh air at an otherwise ho-hum Grammys this year. The teal ombre Elie Saab gown was absolutely gorgeous and fun at the same time! Of course those purple Giuseppe Zanotti sandals were the icing on the cake.

2. Fan Bingbing at the Cannes Film Festival

Fan Bingbing looked positively regal in this Ralph & Russo Couture gown at the Cannes Film Festival. The Chinese inspired motifs, the color, the fit, along with the hair, makeup, and accessories were pure perfection.

3. Aishwarya Rai at the Cannes Film Festival

Another Ralph & Russo creation that blew our minds was this dramatic black and white number. Of course, Aishwarya Rai completely owned this look! Isn't she gorgeous?

4. Jennifer Lopez at the American Music Awards

It is always refreshing to see Jennifer Lopez in something other than an illusion gown. This sparkly ombre Michael Cino dress was perfect for a modern day pop princess!

5. Lena Headey at the Emmy Awards

One word to describe Lena Headey in this dramatic Zuhair Murad Fall 2015 Couture gown? Showstopper!

6. Lady Gaga at the Emmy Awards

Gone are the days of meat dresses and bubble costumes. Lady Gaga looked understated yet sexy in this minimalist Brandon Maxwell gown at the Emmys this year.

7. Vanessa Hudgens at the Tony Awards

Statement floral prints can be a hit-or-miss, and in this case, it was a huge hit! Vanessa looked positively radiant in this voluminous Naeem Khan gown.

8. Jennifer Lopez at the Tony Awards

I wish JLo would leave the nude dresses behind and wear more beautiful gowns like this navy and gold Valentino creation.

9. Kate Hudson at the Glamour Woman of the Year Awards

This slinky J. Mendel gown is not easy to pull off but Kate looked divine!

10. Chanel Iman at the AmfAR Cinema against AIDS Gala

This is among my most favorite red carpet looks of 2015. Chanel looked absolutely ethereal in this breathtaking Zuhair Murad gown. Now that's an illusion dress done right!

11. Danielle Brookes at the SAG Awards

Seeing Danielle Brookes in this custom-made Christian Siriano gown made us all squeal with delight. Every little detail about her look was perfect!

12. Caitriona Balfe at the SAG Awards

Here's a delicious shade of orange that you don't see on the red carpet very often. Caitriona pulled off the Solace London gown beautifully!

13. Araya a. Hargate at the Cannes Film Festival

This Giambattista Valli Couture gown was unique, youthful, and screamed romance. The only thing I could have done without are those earrings.

14. Fan Bingbing at the Met Gala

Wow, that is one opulent gown by Christopher Bu! It was downright stunning and perfect for the ‘China: Through the Looking Glass’ theme this year and of course, Fan Bingbing looked like a vision in it. I'm truly in awe of her red carpet style!

15. Rosamund Pike at the Oscars

Am I the only one who found the red carpet at the Oscars pretty tame this year? Thank goodness for Rosamund Pike in this crimson Givenchy couture gown! It fit her like a dream and that color looked exquisite on her!

16. Emma Stone at the Oscars

Emma Stone in this chartreuse Elie Saab gown was the other saving grace at the Oscars. Love the color, love the hair, love Emma!

17. Leticia Dolera at the Goya Awards

Another statement floral gown that we loved was this Dolores Promesas gown on Leticia Dolera. Ravishing!

18. Léa Seydoux at the BAFTAs

I'm a HUGE fan of color on the red carpet, especially when it's a happy shade of canary yellow. Léa Seydoux looked radiant in this bold Prada gown with cut-outs. LOVE!

19. Diane Kruger at the CFDA Fashion Awards

You've got to love Diane and the fashion risks she takes. This divine red and black gown by Prabal Gurung looked delicious on the German fashionista.

Did your red carpet favorites make it to the list? Tell us below!
