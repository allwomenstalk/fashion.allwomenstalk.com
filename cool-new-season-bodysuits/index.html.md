# 8 Cool New Season Bodysuits ...

Bodysuits can be an easy fix when it comes to relaxed summer dressing. A fitted bodysuit can look super stylish when worn with denim shorts or a high waisted skirt. This season, keep an eye out for bodysuits in eye-catching prints and colours. The following are just a few trendy bodysuits to inspire your new season style.

## 1. Motel Hillary Candy Stripe Bodysuit

![Motel Hillary Candy Stripe Bodysuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/1_motel-hillary-candy-stripe-bodysuit_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $18.23 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/B0Vgaw)
Brightly coloured bodysuits just scream summer dressing. This one has a strapless design with a sweetheart neckline, stitched cups, and bold stripe print. Work a fun summer look by teaming this bodysuit with denim shorts and a floppy straw hat.

## 2. Ruched Polka Dot Bodysuit

**Price:** $6.80 at _forever21.com_
Polka dot patterns are extremely on-trend right now. This bodysuit features an all over polka dot pattern comes in a bandeau design with ruching at the bust. Team this polka dot bodysuit with a full black skirt, Mary Jane shoes, and cat eye sunglasses for a retro inspired outfit.

## 3. American Apparel Mesh Panel Bodysuit

![American Apparel Mesh Panel Bodysuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/3_american-apparel-mesh-panel-bodysuit_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $66.28 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/1BjPMa)
Bodysuits in pastel colours are just another way to make your mark this season. This mesh bodysuit comes in a light blue colour and features a white mesh insert to the front. Make this mesh bodysuit work by teaming it with a flowing blush coloured midi skirt.

## 4. Staring at Stars Printed Bodysuit

![Staring at Stars Printed Bodysuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/4_staring-at-stars-printed-bodysuit_600x902.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bodysuit features a multicoloured zigzag pattern that is very reminiscent of the signature Missoni pattern. It has a scoop neck design and comes in a semi sheer knit. This bodysuit would look great teamed with a red or blue skirt, strappy leather sandals, and oversized sunglasses.

## 5. Motel Hillary Fiji Print Bodysuit

![Motel Hillary Fiji Print Bodysuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/5_motel-hillary-fiji-print-bodysuit_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $36.45 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/szhZ6b)
This bodysuit features a striking tropical print. It has a flattering sweetheart neckline and stitched cups. Work a luxe kind of look by teaming this black bodysuit with wide legged white pants and strappy stilettos.

## 6. Spaghetti Strap Bodysuit

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_
Keep it simple this season in this strappy bodysuit. It comes in a gingery red colour and has a scoop neck line with adjustable shoulder straps. Since this bodysuit is quite simple, you can easily play up the bottom half with printed trousers, shorts, or skirt. I think this bodysuit would look quite chic teamed with loose tailored shorts and patent flats.

## 7. One Teaspoon Goldrush Embellished Bodysuit

![One Teaspoon Goldrush Embellished Bodysuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/07/7_one-teaspoon-goldrush-embellished-bodysuit_600x766.jpg)

**Price:** $188.90 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/dVhJin).
For more glam bodysuits, you can’t go past metallics and embellishments. This bodysuit features stunning sequin embellishments to the front that really give it an edgy yet glamorous touch. Dress this bodysuit up by teaming it with a leather skirt, ankle boots, and smokey eye make up.

## 8. Buttoned Tie Neck Bodysuit

_ **Price:** $15.80 at forever21.com_
This bodysuit is a bit more of a sophisticated offering. It has a self tie collar which is a sweet and feminine touch. This bodysuit would look great teamed with pastel trousers or a knee-length skirt for a sophisticated daytime look.

When it comes to bodysuits there are quite a few options to look at this season. Bright and bold patterns dominate the mix but you can’t go past classic, basic designs either. What are your thoughts on the bodysuit? How would you style yours?
