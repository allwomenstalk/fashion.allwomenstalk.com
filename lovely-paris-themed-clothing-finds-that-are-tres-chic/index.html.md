# 9 Lovely Paris Themed Clothing Finds That Are Très Chic ...

When it comes to Paris themed clothing, I must admit that I am deeply influenced by the timeless glamour of this magnificent capital. There is something about the very essence of this beautiful city that makes it so easy to fall in love with its culture. By observing French fashion trends, I have been able to learn about diverse wardrobe elements and the best way to incorporate them into my own eclectic style. These lovely Paris themed clothing finds will give you the perfect excuse to find your inner Frenchie.

## 1. Hot Topic Hot Pink Knit Beret

![Hot Topic Hot Pink Knit Beret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23084530_3963_599x883.jpg)

The whimsical nature of a bright beret is completely swoon-worthy, making it one of the loveliest Paris themed clothing finds. Knit berets are definitely my latest obsession. Despite being around for a substantial amount of time (leading to being included in many clichés surrounding French fashion), it still manages to maintain its stylish qualities. The fact that the beret is seen as a classic French icon alone makes it a staple for the fall season.

## 2. Modcloth Postcard from Paris Top

![Modcloth Postcard from Paris Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23003755_6845_350x500.jpg)

Am I the only one who finds this postage stamp print utterly fantastic? I have been eying this gem for quite some time now; with its thin but sturdy material, the loose fit makes it really comfortable to wear. The best part about this top is that its charcoal grey color is extremely flattering! To make this look pop, pair this shirt with a flirty skirt and cherry red belt.

## 3. Urban Outfitters DV by Dolce Vita Alishia Polka Dot Dress

![Urban Outfitters DV by Dolce Vita Alishia Polka Dot Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23084122_7378_600x900.jpg)

The classic A-line dress is definitely another trend that is popular in Paris. In a post providing tips on dressing appropriately for a Paris theme party, eHow contributor Kat Consador stated that the "words 'French' and 'fashion' share more than the same first letter [...] For French women, it's not what they wear, but how they wear it. They never let the dress wear them." I feel that this beautiful dress directly embodies this message.

## 4. Modcloth Salut Your Shirt! Top

![Modcloth Salut Your Shirt! Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23003756_9233_349x498.jpg)

This marvelous blouse features two elements that I hold very dear to my heart: Paris and the peter pan collar. As most people are cognizant of the vitality behind this historical landmark, the shirt's design finds a way to draw in a trendy audience.

## 5. Corilynn Red Awning Striped Skirt

![Corilynn Red Awning Striped Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23084122_1403_570x762.jpg)

I absolutely love the Parisian feel to this next item. Contrasting stripes can be considered one of the biggest trends in France due to their ability to accent features. Even if you are not a fan of this particular piece, you can get the full effect by rocking any nautical striped skirt. There's no better way to promote patriotism than to rock the vibrant colors of the French flag!

## 6. Modcloth when Eiffel in Love

![Modcloth when Eiffel in Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23003756_2698_599x841.jpg)

While you may start thinking of prints featuring the Eiffel Tower as a bit of a cliché at this point, you have to admit that this top is still pretty fabulous! The high-low cut of this shirt is definitely in tune with the styles currently on the runway.

## 7. Material Girl Front Zip Satchel Handbag

![Material Girl Front Zip Satchel Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23092102_1162_459x533.jpg)

I am a huge fan of the versatility in this next item. Having a trendy satchel bag is very convenient as well as fashion forward. This sleek design and innovative style will be sure to turn heads.

## 8. ASOS Sexy Pencil Dress

![ASOS Sexy Pencil Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23091614_1784_460x587.jpg)

How could I make a list based on Paris' unmistakable influence on the fashion industry without mentioning the little black dress? According to Forbes contributor Cecilia Rodriguez, the little black dress is "[a]n everlasting creation. A legendary garment. A must-have for all women. A unique fashion phenomenon. A wardrobe essential that stands proud in the dressing closet of fashion cognoscenti around the world." This idea can be applied to any dress that allows you to confidently show off your amazing figure.

## 9. ASOS Premium Skater Dress with Embroidery

![ASOS Premium Skater Dress with Embroidery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23091614_6598_460x587.jpg)

The cut, fit and shape of this final piece will be sure to both caress and lightly skim over your curves. You can take the classic LBD a step further by rocking a black dress featuring a bold print! I love that this particular style comes in a variety of sizes, making it more accessible to fabulous women everywhere; you must check out the Asos curve line.

As a fashionista, I've never been prouder of my French heritage. Fashion is all about taking risks. It doesn't matter whether your outfit is conventional; you just have to make sure that your confidence shines through. How would you define French fashion? As a trendsetter, what are some of your favorite pieces to wear in general?
