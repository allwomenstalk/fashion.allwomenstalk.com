# How to Look Stylish Even when Youre Not Feeling It ...

It takes a lot of effort to look great on a daily basis, no matter how naturally beautiful you are! Thanks to the ridiculously high standards that us women have to face at the hands of society, the option to go out with having no effort in terms of your aesthetic is something that is frowned upon. I’m not saying that you should be dressing up to the nines every day, in fact there are a few little things that you do that don’t take much effort, but that can have a big impact. Here is how to look stylish even when you’re not feeling it!

## 1. Add Colour

![vacation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/qc/fbpn2ma75bb524cf41b72689372227_600x715.jpg)

Adding colour to your outfit is a simple and instant way to add character and style, even when you are feeling uninspired. Pops of colour in both your clothing and your accessories can liven up any outfit, and you never know, the vibes might motivate you and lift your mood anyway!

## 2. Catchy glasses

![](https://img.allw.mn/3bb3fec8e151076218d705069a554e_1024x999.jpg)

If you’re not in the mood to fuss over your outfit, let your eyewear steal the show! A pair of catchy glasses can completely transform your look, adding instant charm and personality. Whether you go for bold frames, bright colors, or a quirky design, glasses can become your secret weapon on days when your style spark is a little dim. [Rectangle glasses](https://www.eyebuydirect.com/eyeglasses/rectangle), in particular, have that chic-meets-smart vibe that works with just about anything. Pop them on, and suddenly you look polished—even if you’re rocking your comfiest hoodie underneath!

## 3. Statement Bag

![basket, wicker, product, lace, crochet,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/x6/vc0p478u5bb525024a941149606848_600x600.jpg)

You can hide a lot of enthusiasm in fashion by carrying around a big statement bag! I’m talking about the kind of bag that takes the attention away from your outfit, which means that you can get away with wearing something a little plainer. If you have an in-your-face designer bag, it’s time to bust it out and have it draw everyone’s eyes away from the less inspired parts of your look!

## 4. Jewellery

![brassiere, shoulder, beauty, fashion model, neck,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/rv/g7fdqm2x5bb5251a3a87c368648628_600x750.jpg)

You can instantly elevate the look of simple outfits by pairing them with eye-catching statement jewellery. I’m talking big earrings, chunky chains, flashy rings, bracelets ... In fact, jewellery tends to look even better when it is worn with an outfit that isn’t trying to steal the limelight, so it’s a win/win situation for you really.

## 5. Messy Bun

![hair, hairstyle, long hair, hair coloring, bun,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/ws/nf62nmy45bb5253940e21733661813_600x750.jpg)

Thankfully for you, messy buns are still very much on trend in 2018, so you can get away with turning a bad hair day into a stylish one with a simple flick of the wrist. Messy buns look great with both casual and more formal outfits, so you have plenty of options.

## 6. Baggy

![clothing, shoulder, joint, fashion model, jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yj/rx/mdnju76v5bb52555913e6819010150_600x648.jpg)

If you are having one of those days when you don’t feel comfortable in your own body, then going oversized is always a good option. Bagginess is definitely in this year, with plenty of oversized shirts and a move away from skinny jeans, so make the most of it and put together something that looks good but that you can also hide in for a while!
