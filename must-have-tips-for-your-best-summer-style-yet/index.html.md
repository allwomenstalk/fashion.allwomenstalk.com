# Must\-Have Tips for Your Best Summer Style Yet ...

Summer is a great time of the year for style. Without the restrictions of outerwear and needing to stay warm, you can really dress however you want to. If you’re looking for the most important style tips this summer, this list has got you covered. For everything from staying cool to staying trendy, this list is made up of only the most important style tips and tricks for your most stylish summer yet.

## 1. Stay Cool

![Stay Cool](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/zz/e5c8slq25557b4c36b324304648031_499x750.jpg)

As much as we all summer to be the peak of fashion and style, we have to be honest: it gets hot in the summer. While it is the one season of the year where the weather isn’t as big of an issue, it can also quickly turn into an issue when humidity and heat become too much. Take that into consideration when you’re getting dressed this summer. I don’t know about you, but being cool is always so important to me in the summer!

## 2. Don’t Be Afraid to Get Trendy

![Don’t Be Afraid to Get Trendy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/vd/k8ujt0ky5557b4da7b9c9600342821_500x749.jpg)

If there’s any time of the year to just completely follow the trends, it’s the summer. Because the weather’s so great, you don’t have to worry about bulky coats or other outwear getting in the way. It makes following trends so much easier!

## 3. Don’t Invest as Much Money

![Don’t Invest as Much Money](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/rd/ehpfs9575557b7433e31e552082141_489x750.jpg)

Because summer is the time to get trendy, it’s also the time of the year when you shouldn’t invest as much money in big pieces. Think about it: summer is all about bathing suits, sundresses and crop tops. Obviously those pieces cost much less than, say, a pea coat that you’ll wear every fall for the next decade.

## 4. Go Crazy with Color

![Go Crazy with Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/xt/kem37ajk5557b64ad70b9378076294_450x675.jpg)

If there’s any time of the year to start infusing color into your wardrobe, summer is the time. Once you get used to wearing color in your everyday wardrobe, it’ll be easier to carry that over into the other seasons as well!

## 5. Dress like You’re Going to the Beach

![Dress like You’re Going to the Beach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/ue/fk4ay5525557b6119779d600751487_500x750.jpg)

I think we can all agree that the best part of summer is going to the beach. Why not dress like you’re going to the beach all of the time? Wear a bandeau under a tank top, spray your hair with salt spray or wear a flowy sundress. Even when you’re not going to the beach, it can feel like a fun day at the shore!

## 6. Think about Your Fabrics

![Think about Your Fabrics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/hb/yunesn0x5557b5b4b7a92686889374_500x750.jpg)

If there were any time to spend a little more time thinking about the fabrics you’re wearing, it’s the summertime. Stay away from poly-blends and instead wear linen, one of the most breathable fabrics. You’ll instantly notice a difference in how much you sweat when you switch a more breathable fabric!

## 7. Wear White

![Wear White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d8/gy/iaz351nh5557b5cd0da34339226436_500x718.jpg)

Do you remember that old rule, “don’t wear white before Memorial Day or after Labor Day”? Even though we don’t necessarily follow that rule, summer is that magical time of the year when wearing all white is more socially accepted, so wear as much white as you can during the summertime!

What are your most important summer style tips? Summer is such a fun time for style for so many different reasons. Whether you’re spending your summer at the beach or at work, let me know the tips you follow to stay fashionable in the summertime! I want to hear what you always follow, so let me know in the comments!
