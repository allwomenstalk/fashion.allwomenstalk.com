# Carrie Underwood's Launching a Fitness Clothing Line! Here's Your First Look ...

Looking fashionable while working out is about to become a whole lot easier! American Idol winner Carrie Underwood has partnered up with Dick's Sporting Goods to launch CALIA by Carrie, a trendy fitness line!

"The inspiration for this line came from my desire to have clothes that seamlessly take me from workouts, to errands, to rehearsals and beyond," she shared in a press release. "As someone who is passionate about health and fitness, I'm excited to offer women a wardrobe that will move with them and encourage them to stay the path of leading an active lifestyle."

When speaking of the line's future retailer, she said: "It has been such a rewarding experience working with Dick's Sporting Goods on all aspects of the design process for CALIA, from choosing fabrics to making sure the look, feel and fit is right for active women!"
