# Stylish Ways to Wear a Tie While Still Looking Feminine ...

There's nothing wrong with looking masculine, but if you're the girly type, then there are different ways to wear a tie without sacrificing your femininity. All you have to do is watch what you pair with the tie, how you actually tie it, and how you style your hair for the day. If you're looking for ideas, here are a few fashionable ways to wear a tie while still looking feminine:

## 1. Wear a Vest Instead of a Blazer

It's easier to see your feminine shape when you replace your blazer with a vest. That way, everyone will be able to see your shapely shoulders and they'll also have a better view of your waist. So if you want to stay stylish, try buying a vest that goes perfectly with your favorite tie.

## 2. Leave Your Hair down

A tie will make you look professional all on its own, so you don't have to wear your hair up in a bun in order to seem like a businesswoman. You're allowed to wear your hair down, as long as you've washed it and brushed it.

## 3. Leave Your Tie Loose around Your Neck

You don't actually need to tie your tie in the correct way. You've seen how sexy men look when their tie is loose, haven't you? Well, you'll look just as fabulous when you do the same, so don't tighten that tie all the way up.

## 4. Wear a Skirt Instead of Slacks

The best way to feel like a lady is to wear a skirt. You could even wear heels with your outfit. That way, you'll feel as feminine as always. The best part is that you have a lot of options. You can wear a school girl skirt if you're trying to look sexy for your crush, or you could wear a pencil skirt if you're trying to look professional for work.

## 5. Wear a "Feminine" Colored Tie

We all know that men can wear pink, too, but it's traditionally a feminine color. That's why it's the perfect shade to wear when you're worried about losing your feminine look. Another great idea is to wear a color that matches your eyes, so that it'll bring out your beautiful, natural features.

## 6. Test out the Rocker Look

You don't have to save your ties for important business meetings. You can wear them when you want to look like a rocker. Follow in Avril Lavigne's footsteps and wear it with a skirt and some fishnets in order to seem edgy. It's not a look that you see very often nowadays, so you're sure to stand out in a crowd.

## 7. Don't Wear Anything underneath Your Suit

You don't actually need to wear anything at all under your suit and tie. Of course, you need to be careful with this one, because if you don't have enough buttons toward the top of your shirt, you're going to show off more of your chest than you intended. If you're going to a party, that's fine. But if you're on your way to work, you don't want to show off too much cleavage.

Ties aren't meant for men. You're allowed to wear them whenever you want, whether you're trying to achieve the businesswoman look or the rocker look. Have you ever worn a tie before? How do you usually style the rest of your outfit when you do so?
