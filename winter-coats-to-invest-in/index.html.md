# 10 Winter Coats to Invest in ...

Winter Coats are worthy investment pieces. The right coat will last you year in and year out. When choosing winter coats take care to invest in ones with timeless, classic cuts. These are the ones that will go with any outfit and be suitable for a range of occasions. Once you’ve got your investment coat sorted, then you can start worrying about those trend-based ones! Take a look at a few classic winter coats below.

## 1. Diane Von Furstenberg Loretta Wool-Blend Peacoat

![Diane Von Furstenberg Loretta Wool-Blend Peacoat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/ao/jx5a87d354c9302861ae7900321741_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/E6RlL4)
The classic peacoat is a smart choice. This particular coat comes in a light-camel colour in mid-weight wool-blend. It has cropped bell sleeves, a wind vent, and is fully lined. This kind of coat is perfect for wearing during the day.

## 2. Topshop Black Double Breasted Hooded Coat

![Topshop Black Double Breasted Hooded Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wg/s2/ppqzzzcv54c9302946c74755583311_334x493.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/KCFWEJ)
Double breasted winter coats are a classic. This simple black coat has a double breasted design and comes with a hood. It also hits at about mid-thigh. This would make an excellent everyday coat.

## 3. Pringle of Scotland Swing Coat

![Pringle of Scotland Swing Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/jr/z01yufp954c9302a329a5782010548_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1795.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This black swing coat is perfect for layering over your winter outfits. It has a stand collar, double breasted front, and an A-line cut. The hem is also slightly longer at the back. Layer it over an outfit of warm knits and denim for a street-smart look.

## 4. Etoile Isabel Marant Azuli Oversized Wool-Blend Coat

![Etoile Isabel Marant Azuli Oversized Wool-Blend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/fk/uzjprfu954c9302b789b6052980078_333x487.jpg)

**Price:** $670.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/yvNbVw)
Oversized winter coats are perfect for accommodating all those extra layers. This wool-blend coat comes in an on-trend midnight blue colour. It has wide collar lapels, bracelet sleeves, and oversized patch pockets. This coat would look great teamed with a colour palette of rich reds and browns.

## 5. Topshop Boucle Faux Fur Collar Coat

![Topshop Boucle Faux Fur Collar Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/p7/jk2j1eix54c9302c8c0a4580704625_334x496.jpg)

**Price:** $190.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/trixKK)
The faux fur touches on this coat give it a slight retro feel. This thigh length coat is made form a wool-blend and features faux fur detailing and scalloping at the shoulders. Keep the retro look going by pairing it with accessories such as a framed handbag and cat-eye sunglasses.

## 6. Rachel Comey Cabot Coat

![Rachel Comey Cabot Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/c3/bhlnjso554c9302da39d7885428424_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $760.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This slightly oversized coat comes in a wonderful olive colour. It has a double breasted design, notched lapel collar, and front slit pockets. The A-line cut of this coat is quite accommodating. Team it with knit dresses, tights, and boots for a chic off-duty look.

## 7. Miu Miu Wool Princess Coat

![Miu Miu Wool Princess Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/wl/n9ok4lao54c9302eb6f38041538084_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1795.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Xb5ZLk)
This coat has a very ladylike design. It has a rounded collar, gold button fastenings, and front flap pockets. It comes in a slate grey colour which makes it a great staple piece to have in your wardrobe. Winter coats such as this one are great for teaming with your everyday outfits.

## 8. Ralph Lauren Black Label Houndstooth Trench Coat

![Ralph Lauren Black Label Houndstooth Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qz/mb/julw51vm54c9302fc7e4b635375730_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $4000.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/eD2wCU)
The houndstooth design is a timeless favourite. This cashmere trench coat has a double breasted design and comes with a detachable waist belt. It also has button fastening epaulets, wind flap, and front slant pockets. Wear it with slim pants and riding boots for a chic look.

## 9. Viktor & Rolf Wool Coat with Knit Sleeves

_ **Price:** $2395.00 at lagarconne.com_
Longer length winter coats are perfect for providing extra coverage. This brushed wool coat has thick knit sleeves, an overlapping funnel neck, and self tie belt. Throw it over any outfit to keep you warm in the winter months.

## 10. Rick Owens Asymmetric Wool-Blend Melange Coat

![Rick Owens Asymmetric Wool-Blend Melange Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y4/ei/eg7avyle54c93031ab845083708115_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $2735.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YNcKEt)
For those who prefer more contemporary cuts, this coat manages to incorporate both contemporary and classic designs. It has a funnel neck, long sleeves, and ribbed inserts at the inner arms. Keep the contemporary look going by teaming this coat with an all black outfit and knee high boots.

A coat in a classic cut is a great part of any capsule wardrobe. The right coat can complement a range of outfits. When choosing winter coats, keep an eye out for those in thick wool or wool-blends. Which of these winter coats do you prefer?
