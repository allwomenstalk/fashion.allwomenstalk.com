# Conservative Trends to Wear to Your Sunday Brunch ...

Sunday brunch is basically a fashion show in and of itself. If you’re someone who loves going to brunch with friends, you probably put a lot of pressure to look great at brunch. Even if you’re going out with your best friends, you still want to look perfectly put-together at Sunday brunch! If you’ve hit a mental roadblock and can’t come up with any new brunch outfits, I’ve got you covered! And, who knows, a few of these may just surprise you!

## 1. A Graphic Tee and Jean Jacket

![A Graphic Tee and Jean Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/mc/ruo0fb9p5674c97808994962786899_546x795.jpg)

When you’ve woken up on a weekend to go hang out with your friends, you want to be comfortable first and foremost. Is there anything more effortlessly cool but also super comfortable than a graphic tee with a jean jacket? You’ll look so stylish but you’ll also be comfortable which is what everyone wants on a Sunday morning!

## 2. Midi Skirts

![Midi Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/oq/f5kk3l5g5674c989efb52297147598_600x737.jpg)

If you and your best girlfriends get a little more dressed up for Sunday brunch, you’ll probably want to rock a trend like a midi skirt. Midi skirts are perfect for the girl who loves classic, fresh style, especially when it comes to brunch. Imagine wearing a crisp, white midi skirt with a pinstripe button-down. It doesn’t get much more classic than that!

## 3. Cat Eye Sunglasses

![Cat Eye Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/nn/tc4s3r045674c9b3399b2033096827_500x617.jpg)

Let’s be real here, ladies. Sometimes brunch is just a little too early on Sundays and we want to stay in the dark, so to speak. If you’re trying to cover up your eyes at your next brunch, you really can’t go wrong with cay eye sunglasses. The big glasses will hide last night’s mistakes on your eyes and the dramatic look will completely look like you’re trying to look stylish! It’s the perfect brunch accessory!

## 4. Bright, Fresh Colors

![Bright, Fresh Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/eh/pwzoausi5674c9cdb63fc927832214_500x629.jpg)

No matter the time of the year, there’s something about brunch that calls for bright, fresh colors. Whether you want to wear neon, pastels, or varying shades of white, you can always wearing bright or fresh colors when it comes to brunch! Don’t shy away from those beautiful colors just because it’s not spring or summer! They’re still perfect for Sunday brunch!

## 5. Skinny Jeans and Booties

![Skinny Jeans and Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/h5/id1dlxwt5674c9e102676312248552_600x868.jpg)

If you’re going for a look that’s effortless, comfortable, and super stylish, make it easy on yourself. Throw on your favorite pair of skinny jeans and wear a cute pair of booties with them. It’s perfect no matter the season. It can easily be dressed with dark or bright colors depending on the season, making it such a versatile brunch outfit!

## 6. A Peasant Top

![A Peasant Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/ja/hqeg3mpg5674c9f29e460459932897_500x750.jpg)

When you’re hanging out with your best friends on a weekend morning, you want to be comfortable. You don’t always want to be super dressed up and uncomfortable. Wear a loose, peasant top for a look that looks like you’re trying to be stylish but you’ll know how comfortable you are!

## 7. A Jumpsuit

![A Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/x1/puqlof6l5674ca03a4fc1859400646_500x632.jpg)

Jumpsuits are an instant way to look chic with almost no effort or planning whatsoever. If you’re running late for brunch, throw on a jumpsuit to look so stylish in no time at all! It’s such a good staple to keep in your closet!

What’s your favorite trend to wear to brunch? Will you wear any of these trends to your next Sunday brunch? Let me know what you think in the comments!
