# 7 Flirty Accessories for the Perfect First Date ...

If you’re going on a first date soon, you’re probably on the hunt for the perfect first date outfit. Once you’ve got that covered, it’s time to find the perfect accessories for your first date. It can be difficult to find the perfect outfit and accessories, because you don’t want to be too sexy on a first date, but you also don’t want your look to be too romantic. If you’re looking for the perfect, flirty accessories for your first date, look no further than this list of hair accessories, jewelry, and purses. You’re sure to find something you’d love to wear on your next first date.

## 1. A Red Bow

_ forever21.com_
Red doesn’t need to be solely associated with Valentine’s Day! It’s a romantic color that can be worn all year around, especially if you’re going on a first date, and this bow headband is the perfect way to make your look a little flirtier!

## 2. A Romantic Necklace

_ forever21.com_
I just think that there’s something so romantic about wearing this ring on a necklace. It may send off a different vibe if you’re on a first date, but personally, it reminds me of an heirloom or something you would’ve seen in The Notebook or another love story.

## 3. Love You like XO

_ forever21.com_
This one’s for all of the Beyoncé fans out there. Whether you’re on a first date, simply want to give a shout-out to Queen Bey, or you’re somewhere in between, this necklace is perfect for you. It’s flirty without being too in your date’s face.

## 4. Dainty Rings

_ forever21.com_
Something about this set of rings is just so romantic to me. Just like the ring necklace, they look like antique finds, that they’re older than they are, or that they have a story to them. I think that they’re the perfect, flirty accessories for a first date!

## 5. Baby Blue Mini Maise

![Baby Blue Mini Maise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ga/vc/zb6si5ha54f0908c8f597775220205_600x593.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Everyone loves miniatures. I’m sure that there are scientific findings proving it, but just take my word on it. This miniature Kate Spade purse is the perfect accessory for your first date. Plus, its light blue hue is just as flirty as the red bow, but it’s a little more muted if you’re looking to tone things down.

## 6. A Parisian Beret with a Twist

![A Parisian Beret with a Twist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ki/yp/zow3kume54fdb92cde49f579129663_301x203.jpg)

[katespade.com](https://www.katespade.com/sale/)
Paris is the city of love, of course, and this beret will remind you of being in Paris. Plus, it has a little bit of twist. It’s covered in sequins, making it perfect if you’re looking for a classic, romantic look with a bit of edge. It’s romantic but flirty, the perfect combination.

## 7. A Statement Bag

![A Statement Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/su/elape43j54fdb9378bc00791555818_301x136.jpg)

[katespade.com](https://www.katespade.com/sale/)
If you’re a little bit worried about conversation on your first date, a statement bag is the perfect conversation starter, and this bag will definitely start a conversation. Not only is it cute, but it also looks like an old-school radio, which is sure to bring up some questions. Plus it’s pink, which is always a flirtier choice when it comes to a date!
