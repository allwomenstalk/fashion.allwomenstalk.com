# 7 Fun Fashion Hats for Fall That Are Worth Trying on This Year ...

I have to tell you, I’m not normally one who is into fashion hats for fall, but suddenly I’ve found myself drawn to them in magazines, fashion ads, or wherever else I see them. I’ll just be honest; I’ve got a ton of hair, and trying to put a hat on my head is almost a joke, however I like the idea of wearing one if nothing else. I think some people can pull them off marvelously, while others like me are left to admire from afar. Either way, fashion hats can be a great addition to an outfit, add a touch of flair, or just be a great way to cover up a horrid hair day. We’ve all had those from time to time, right? Try on a few of these fashion hats for fall if you see them out in stores. Hats are one of the hottest trends for 2013, so try on some of my favorites or find one of your own to love!

## 1. Felt Wool Cap

![Felt Wool Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30140506_5696_600x980.jpg)

One of my favorite fashion hats for fall is this little red one by Haute Design. It’s small, petite, and so feminine, yet makes such a great statement. It looks so classic and old school, and I totally love it.

## 2. The Rider Hat

![The Rider Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30140506_2904_600x825.jpg)

I adore the rider hat, especially with that cute coat and those boots. A rider hat makes a bold, brilliant statement, and is great for dressing up your look. These hats come in different colors, but I’m partial to the classic black most of all.

## 3. Cabbie Hat

![Cabbie Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/18133500_4350_600x600.jpg)

The cabbie hat from Novae brand is so stylish, and practically perfect for this time of year. Cabbie hats are great for your more casual outfits and they really work well on almost anyone. I think pairing them with jeans and a pair of boots is a great way to take advantage of them in the fall, and they go with just about anything.

## 4. Cadet Hat

![Cadet Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/18133537_4522_600x750.jpg)

Another hat I’m loving for fall is this cadet style hat made by Novae as well. It would also pair great with jeans and a more casual style outfit. Plus, the cute embellishment on the sides adds just the right touch of feminine flair to keep things girly and trendy.

## 5. The Classic Floppy Hat

![The Classic Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30140505_9362_500x692.jpg)

Always in style is a classic floppy hat, which doesn't just look great, but also keeps the sun out of your face. I love this tan color, which is perfect for fall and would match most anything.

## 6. The Beanie Hat

![The Beanie Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/18133606_9147_600x857.jpg)

Can you believe the beanie hat has made a comeback? I always liked these as a kid, but by the time I was a teen, they were out of style. Well, I guess I’m in luck because they’re one of the major trends for 2013 and one of the best fall fashion hats to own. Nordstrom has some amazing cute styles of beanie hats, but you can find them just about anywhere this year since they’re a hot classic comeback.

## 7. The Crochet Hat

![The Crochet Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30140507_6620_600x857.jpg)

Here’s a cozy hat that is fashionable, versatile, and comes in many different variations and colors. If you’re crafty, you can even make one of these hats for yourself! The crochet hat goes great with jeans, boots, pea coats, sweaters, or casual dresses and skirts. I especially love this fall fashion hat since it is casual, yet also really girly at the same time.

Do you have any favorite fashion hats for fall? If so, feel free to share them!
