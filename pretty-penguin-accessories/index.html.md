# 8 Adorable Penguin Accessories to Brighten Up Your Winter Wardrobe

As it turns out, you don’t have to take a trip to Antarctica to pick up a few pretty penguin accessories. From socks you’ll want to wear everywhere to ultra-cozy gloves, penguin-themed items are all the rage this winter. And with good reason! These eight arctic bird-inspired finds will keep you fashion-forward and fabulous all season long.

## 1. Juicy Couture Penguin Mini Critter Necklace

![Juicy Couture Penguin Mini Critter Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/1_juicy-couture-penguin-mini-critter-necklace_300x300.jpg)

One of my favorite penguin accessories is a bejeweled Juicy Couture necklace. You can’t go wrong with a sassy little penguin pendant around your neck, especially when it adds a touch of sparkle to your favorite [jewelry pieces](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/jewelry/). This sparkler will go with almost anything in your closet! It’ll look especially cool with a pleated leather skirt, booties, and a satchel bag. Check out the latest collections at [Juicy Couture](https://www.juicycouture.com) to find your next statement piece.

## 2. Kindred Soles Socks in Penguin

![Kindred Soles Socks in Penguin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/2_kindred-soles-socks-in-penguin_600x857.jpg)

Cool and cozy, a cute pair of animal-print socks is a drawer essential. You’ll make your friends giggle when they see your kitschy penguin socks—complete with bright red bowties, of course! They’re perfect to keep your toes warm during a sleepover or to wear out underneath your favorite [winter booties](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/shoes/). You can often find similar whimsical styles at retailers like [ModCloth](https://modcloth.com).

## 3. Penguin Shower Mitt

![Penguin Shower Mitt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/3_penguin-shower-mitt_306x290.jpg)

Forget your boring old washcloths! Rub and scrub with an undeniably cute penguin shower mitt. And how awesome would this little guy look on your shower caddy? It is a simple way to add some fun to your self-care routine. Keep an eye out for these budget-friendly finds at [Forever 21](https://www.forever21.com).

## 4. Retractable Penguin Earbuds

![Retractable Penguin Earbuds](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/4_retractable-penguin-earbuds_300x300.jpg)

If you and your smartphone are practically inseparable, treat yourself to a charming pair of penguin earbuds. Best of all, many modern versions feature retractable wires, meaning you won’t have to spend half your morning commute getting them untangled! They make for a great conversation starter while you are listening to your favorite podcasts.

## 5. Penguin Earrings

![Penguin Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/5_penguin-earrings_600x587.jpg)

Spruce up your neutral garb with a polished pair of penguin earrings. These tuxedo birds will go with anything and everything in your winter wardrobe. I’d pair them with a chunky knit sweater, skinny jeans, and heeled booties for a casual lunch date. You can find unique, artisan-made jewelry like this at [Uncommon Goods](https://www.uncommongoods.com).

## 6. Bungalow 360 Large Penguin Wallet

![Bungalow 360 Large Penguin Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/6_bungalow-360-large-penguin-wallet_500x257.jpg)

If you heart penguins, a canvas wallet with a fun print will soon be your purse’s new BFF. Featuring adorable penguin pairs, it’s a simple way to add a touch of playful flair to your style without going overboard. Plus, a pop of pattern makes any black pea coat stand out! Explore the latest eco-friendly designs from Bungalow 360 for more [fashion accessories](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/accessories/) that make a statement.

## 7. Penguin Convertible Gloves

![Penguin Convertible Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/7_penguin-convertible-gloves_310x310.jpg)

When the weather gets frigid, these toasty fingerless gloves that convert into mittens are a lifesaver. Luckily for us penguin lovers, they’ll keep you texting without your fingers freezing. It’s the perfect blend of functionality and cuteness for the winter season. You can often find these affordable winter staples at [Claire’s](https://www.claires.com).

## 8. So Fleece Penguin Bootie Slippers

![So Fleece Penguin Bootie Slippers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/8_so-fleece-penguin-bootie-slippers_300x300.jpg)

If you’ve been walking across those hardwood floors without slippers, it’s time to treat your toes to a pair of warm booties. They’ll keep your feet and calves toasty as you’re whipping up breakfast or lounging in front of the TV. Check out the cozy selection at [Kohl’s](https://www.kohls.com) to find your perfect fit.
