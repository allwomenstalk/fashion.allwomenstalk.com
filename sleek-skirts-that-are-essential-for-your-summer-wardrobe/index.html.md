# 9 Sleek Skirts That Are Essential for Your Summer Wardrobe ...

Refresh your wardrobe with some sleek skirts for summer. This season, pencil and A-line skirts are at the forefront of fashion. They’re perfect for working a streamlined summer look. Short A-line skirts are great for working casual summer looks while pencil skirts are ideal for pulling off ladylike and retro-inspired trends. Check out our selection of stylishly sleek skirts for summer.

## 1. Colourful Embroidered Skirt

![Colourful Embroidered Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_colourful-embroidered-skirt_600x700.jpg)

Nothing says summer dressing better than a bit of colour. Embroidered skirts for summer can typically be a bit busy, but this J.Crew skirt hits all the right notes. It comes in a high voltage hue and features all over floral embroidery. It has a sleek A-line design and would look great teamed with a slouchy grey t-shirt and neutral sandals. It is pretty pricey at around $150 but you’re bound to find similar, more affordable styles at stores like ASOS.

## 2. Linen Blend Skirt

![Linen Blend Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_linen-blend-skirt_600x732.jpg)

A simple skirt in a block colour is the perfect wardrobe updater. This navy blue linen blend skirt will become a summer wardrobe essential. Work a polished look by wearing it with a button up blouse or dress it down with a t-shirt and sneakers. This skirt is a steal at around $16 from Forever 21 and it also comes in a bright red version.

## 3. Leather a-line Skirt

![Leather a-line Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_leather-a-line-skirt_600x706.jpg)

The leather skirt is quickly becoming a wardrobe essential. Stick to streamlined, A-line skirts if you want to cut a sleek figure this summer. Wear them during the day with striped t-shirts and ballet flats or style them up at night with a slinky camisole and heels. This Topshop leather skirt will burn a hole in your pocket at $170 but you can always look to faux leather varieties for more budget-friendly buys.

## 4. Lace Pencil Skirt

![Lace Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_lace-pencil-skirt_600x900.jpg)

Work a ladylike look in this white lace pencil skirt. This skirt has a knee-length, fitted design and features a jagged lace hem. It’s a splurge at $100 from Topshop but the style is simple enough to last you more than one season. Try working an on-trend monochrome look and team the skirt with a white top and accessories.

## 5. Scalloped Blush Skirt

![Scalloped Blush Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_scalloped-blush-skirt_600x732.jpg)

Lighten up your wardrobe with blush colours. Instead of a full pleated skirt or a sheer maxi, try a sleek mini skirt. This blush skirt from Forever 21 is a bargain at around $18. Team it with a similarly light coloured top and accessories to work a fresh summertime look.

## 6. Floral Print Skirt

![Floral Print Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_floral-print-skirt_600x732.jpg)

Really embrace the season with a summery floral skirt. This floral skirt from Forever 21 is a versatile number and can be dressed up or down. Wear it during the day with a crop top and sandals or at night with a silky blouse and heels.

## 7. Mixed Print Skirt

![Mixed Print Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_mixed-print-skirt_600x900.jpg)

Stay on top of trends this summer with a mixed print skirt. This Topshop skirt features a patchwork oriental-inspired print. Draw from the colours in the print and team it with either a red, white or blue top. This skirt will set you back around $76 from Topshop and you can find a great range of printed skirts at Banana Republic and J.C. Penney.

## 8. Denim-Look Skirt

![Denim-Look Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/8_denim-look-skirt_161x337.jpg)

A knee-length denim skirt is great for working a relaxed look this summer. This affordable skirt from New Look comes in faded denim and would look perfectly summery teamed with a white singlet and embellished sandals. Also be sure to check out Topshop and Anthropologie for similar sleek denim skirts.

## 9. Maxi Tube Skirt

![Maxi Tube Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/9_maxi-tube-skirt_600x815.jpg)

If you’re a fan of maxi-lengths, then create a streamlined look in a tube-style skirt. Stay on top of trends and choose one with split details at the sides. This khaki maxi skirt costs $28 from Dorothy Perkins and would look great teamed with a tank top and denim vest for a cool off-duty outfit.

Update your summer wardrobe by adding one of these skirts into the mix. Are you a fan of sleek skirts or do you prefer fuller styles? Which of these skirts would you wear this summer?
