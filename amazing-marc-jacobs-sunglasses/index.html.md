# 7 Amazing Marc Jacobs Sunglasses ...

I adore sunglasses, and I seem to have a pair for every occasion, in every color and style you can think of… but of course I want more of them! And I’ve seen a few new pairs by Marc Jacobs that currently top my must0have list… so I thought I’d share! Here are 7 amazing sunglasses by Marc Jacobs.

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs round Tortoise Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs round Tortoise Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/j5/s1chd59x54c92d2d39d32911888843_350x314.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These retro-inspired round frames feature a brown tortoiseshell print called “Havana” with green tinted lenses and would look so pretty with a vintage sundress and cute sandals. The round shape will work with just about any face shape… I love them!

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Medium Rounded Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Medium Rounded Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/pe/jzyynxxq54c92d2e19bfb225196623_350x472.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Is “rounded square” a shape? It is if you’re talking about these sunglasses, with square-ish frames in violet, smoke grey, or tortoiseshell. They feature a gradient lens in grey or brown with the Marc Jacobs signature on each arm and they offer 100% UV protection for your peepers.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Exclusive Shield Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Exclusive Shield Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n6/n7/uoagacd954c92d2ee9691171783100_350x354.jpg)

**Price:** $85.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These hot sunglasses are available exclusively at Saks, and are available in either brown tortoiseshell with brown shield lenses or black with grey lenses. The arms feature a metal Marc Jacobs nameplate and the signature print.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Aviator Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Aviator Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/tq/i7sw3oom54c92d2fb2c2a167677334_350x448.jpg)

**Price:** $85.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I do love a good pair of classic aviators, and these ones are so pretty! They come in electric blue, white, red, or tortoiseshell, with various gradient lens colors, and offer 100% UV protection. They also have the Marc Jacobs signature on the arms, and would look so cute for summer!

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs Metal Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Metal Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/a7/jox9cxc454c92d30a1cc2063950731_350x345.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the shape of these sunglasses! They’re almost like butterflies, only more squared off. I love them! They come with gold or brown frames and gradient brown 100% UV-protection lenses. Each arm is decorated with a metal Marc Jacobs nameplate and a signature.

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Metal Shield Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Metal Shield Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/mx/jol5f7eo54c92d316f29f831392165_350x343.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Choose from black and white or brown and white, either way, you’ll love these cute leopard-print large shield sunglasses. When you’re not using them as sunglasses, use them to push your long tresses out of your eyes. I love the print… it’s so unique!

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Medium Square Sunglasses

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Medium Square Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/te/w0hj54jm54c92d32420be040812895_350x444.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Oh, I love these in that light brown color! They’re great in the tortoiseshell or black, too. All of them feature a metal Marc Jacobs medallion at the sides and a semi-wrap style. If I had to choose one of these, this would be my favorite pair…

With so many sexy sunglasses to choose from, how will I ever choose just one? I really love that last pair, especially in the light brown, and the aviators in turquoise, but they’re all gorgeous! Which do you like best, and why? Please let me know!
