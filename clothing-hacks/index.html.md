# Clothing Hacks That Will Totally save Your Life ...

Every woman in the world knows how stressful it is to choose the right outfit for every occasion and that's why we all need some great **clothing hacks**. But if there is something even more stressful, it is the number of problems that arise while we choose those outfits. So here are some **clothing hacks** that will undoubtedly get you out the door in a hurry.

## 1. Hang Multiple Garments in One Spot

Hang multiple garments in one spot with soda can tabs. You can also use chains and hang the hangers on the links. This is one of my favorite **clothing hacks** because it maximizes space in your closet.

## 2. Organize Your Scarves

Use bath curtain rings to organize your scarves. Just use the rings to hang them.

## 3. Keep Loose Zippers up

Keep loose zippers up attaching a small key ring to your zipper. Then put your pants on, and zip up the loose zipper, looping the ring around the button. Fasten the button, concealing the ring. You can also use rubber bands or a piece of elastic.

## 4. Get Rid of Deodorant Stains

Do you have deodorant stains on your clothes? Clean them using wet wipes or make-up remover wipes.

## 5. De-pill Your Clothing Using a Razor

Just roll the razor over the cloth and then use tape to remove any residue.

## 6. Make Your Shoes Waterproof

This is a trick that has saved my life more than once. Just heat a candle and let some of the wax fall on your sneakers. Do it until you cover your shoes with a thin layer of wax. Once you have done it you will get some waterproof tennis shoes and the wax of the candle, being transparent, will not even be noticed. You can also use beeswax, which is much better.

## 7. Use Talcum Powder to Remove Oil Stains

You just have to apply it on the stain and leave it overnight.

## 8. Remove Stains from Leather

Mix cold water and vinegar to remove stains from leather without causing any damage to your garment.

## 9. Get Rid of Makeup Stains

Did you wear your favorite white shirt and it got stained with your lipstick? Put some hairspray on the stain and leave it for a few minutes. Then put it in the washing machine.

## 10. Remove the Chewing Gum from Your Jeans

Remove the chewing gum from your jeans by freezing the gum first. You can also roll an ice cube over the chewing gum. This will make removing it much easier.

## 11. Your Shoes Slip

You have a party and you wear that beautiful new pair of shoes that you bought recently. The thing is that you realize that you can't walk comfortably because the sole slips. Well, you can solve that problem easily. Just take a knife and make some small cuts in the sole. That will act as anti-slip and you can walk and dance all night without worrying about falling.

## 12. Prevent Your Nylon Stockings from Keeling

Just put some transparent nail polish on the borders of the keel.

## 13. Get Rid of Odors in Your Shoes

This is my favorite trick by far. Remove any odor from your shoes with this simple trick. You just have to peel an orange and put the peel on your shoes. Leave it overnight and the next day the unpleasant odors will have disappeared.
