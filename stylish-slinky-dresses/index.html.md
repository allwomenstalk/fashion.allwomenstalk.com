# 9 Stylish Slinky Dresses ...

Slinky Dresses are great for wearing on a night out. They are perfect for slipping on during balmy summer nights, and in winter they look great team with faux fur coats and an array of layers and jewels. Most slinky dresses are made from satin or equally clingy fabrics, so try artfully draped pieces that are tucked and gathered in all the right places if you are a tad body conscious. Take inspiration from the following stylish slinky dresses.

## 1. Topshop Satin Skater Slip Dress

![Topshop Satin Skater Slip Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y5/i2/srrvv28054c9332e8902d813994433_333x499.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/z8fCx1)
This satin slip dress comes in a flirty skater style. It has spaghetti straps, a V-neck, and zip fastening at the back. It comes in a midnight blue colour and can be styled up or down. Team this dress with a faux fur coat, tights, ankle boots, and plenty of molten metallic jewellery for an artfully eclectic look.

## 2. Vivienne Westwood Gold Label Silk-Satin Dress

![Vivienne Westwood Gold Label Silk-Satin Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/yn/o7lezzh054c9332f79b78944270909_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1265.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/RVWBoM)
This is a more sophisticated take on the slinky dresses trend. It features a draped scooped neck, twisted shoulder straps, and short back hem with draped and tie details. It is ideal for creating an hourglass effect. This dress would look great with a pair of metallic heels and a colourfully embellished clutch.

![Topshop Satin Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/bt/vra83gsv54c933320fb4a412301987_334x497.jpg)
