# 15 TV Couples Halloween Costume Ideas for Couples Dressing up Together ...

Halloween is quickly approaching and there are so many great TV couples Halloween costumes to choose from! Do you and your partner love dressing up as a couple for Halloween? Well why not consider a TV couple theme this year? There are so many great TV couples Halloween costumes to choose from! Here is just a brief list of some of my favorites.

## 5. Lois and Clark from Smallville

## 8. Homer and Marge from the Simpsons

![face, yellow, facial expression, smile, head,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/o9/lo5ggbmh59e4d291823ef036900838_600x600.jpg)

## 9. Spencer and Ashley from South of Nowhere

![clothing, suit, formal wear, standing, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/b1/rhyt71kn59e4c7c7e8268820441883_600x600.jpg)

## 12. April and Andy from Parks and Recreation

![clothing, outerwear, product, eyewear, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/ms/y17s5ap359e4c8539e770149474707_600x600.jpg)

## 13. Jeannie and Major Nelson from I Dream of Jeannie

![profession, official, staff, girl, uniform,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sp/uf/d04dp7hl59e4d13d8d08d934203344_600x596.jpg)

## 14. Glenn and Maggie from the Walking Dead

![product, shoulder, fashion, outerwear, socialite,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ie/nn/jbag2xw959e4cd117b9d6314999771_600x600.jpg)

## 15. The Doctor and Rose

![clothing, costume, fashion, cosplay, socialite,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/x8/jc2gzyh559e4cb3019286734831554_600x600.jpg)

Well there it is, my top 15 television couples to dress up as for this Halloween!

Dressing up is one of the best parts of this holiday and being able to do it with someone you really care about makes it even more enjoyable. I hope everyone enjoys their Halloween and finds the perfect costume for themselves!
