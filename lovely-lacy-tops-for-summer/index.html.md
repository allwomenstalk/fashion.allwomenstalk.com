# 9 Lovely Lacy Tops for Summer ...

Lacy tops are perfect for working a feminine aesthetic. Whether you go for lace embellishments or a full lace look, you just can’t go past a lacy top for some summertime chic. This season, play up a sheer lace top by wearing a coloured slip underneath. Take a look at some lovely lacy tops, below.

## 1. Topshop Scallop Lace Embellished Top

![Topshop Scallop Lace Embellished Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-scallop-lace-embellished-top_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/XCsFTf)
This summer, you can’t go past lacy tops in pastel shades. This one comes in an ultra feminine blush colour and features sweet scallop detailing at the hem. It also features tiny pearl details for that extra hint of glamour. This top would look great teamed with a white blazer, slim fit pants, and metallic jewellery.

## 2. Modcloth Lavender Lacy Top

![Modcloth Lavender Lacy Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_modcloth-lavender-lacy-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This sheer lace top is perfect for throwing on over a pair of faded denim shorts or jeans (and a slip perhaps, seeing as it is very see through). It has a basic t-shirt design with a relaxed fit. It’s ideal for working a casually [feminine look](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/super-simple-tips-to-look-more-feminine/).

## 3. Topshop Lace Mix Sleeveless Shell

![Topshop Lace Mix Sleeveless Shell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_topshop-lace-mix-sleeveless-shell_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1Nlkp3)
This sleeveless top features mixed lace detailing which gives off an interesting effect. It comes in a lovely light blue colour and has a scooped neckline at the front and back. This top would look great with some tailored white shorts and rose gold jewellery.

## 4. J.Crew Raindrop Lace Top

![J.Crew Raindrop Lace Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_j-crew-raindrop-lace-top_434x500.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vjoNMM)
Don’t be afraid of brightly coloured lacy tops this summer. This J.Crew top comes in a eye-catching pink shade and features sheer sleeves and scallop detailing. Team it with prints for a statement summer look.

## 5. Dorothy Perkins Blush Lacy Top

![Dorothy Perkins Blush Lacy Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_dorothy-perkins-blush-lacy-top_368x500.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This top has a simple sleeveless design and the lace has a slight metallic sheen to it. A neutral lace top like this one can be styled in a variety of ways. You can dress it down with denim for a casual weekend look or style it up with tailored pants and stiletto heels for a night out.

## 6. Modcloth Letter to Mum Top

![Modcloth Letter to Mum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_modcloth-letter-to-mum-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the larger lace detailing on this top. Sometimes the lace pattern can be lost on lighter colour tops but this crocheted lace pattern really stands out. Wear this top with some light denim boyfriend jeans and strappy sandals for a cool off-duty look.

## 7. Tibi Cotton Blend Lace Top

![Tibi Cotton Blend Lace Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_tibi-cotton-blend-lace-top_460x474.jpg)

**Price:** $265.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wT68f6)
Crisp white clothes are synonymous with summer. The look is clean and fresh and perfect for working a palette of colourful accessories. This Tibi top is a bit of a splurge, but comes in a loose t-shirt design which is perfect for styling up or down. If basic lacy tops are what you’re after then this one is worth the investment.

## 8. Topshop Lace Peplum Top

![Topshop Lace Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/8_topshop-lace-peplum-top_412x500.jpg)

**Price:** $52.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pV3FWO)
Peplum silhouettes are a popular choice this season. This black lace top is partially lined with a sheer back. It has a cropped design, so team it with high waisted skirts and pants for a polished look.

## 9. Dorothy Perkins Ivory Eyelash Top

![Dorothy Perkins Ivory Eyelash Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/9_dorothy-perkins-ivory-eyelash-top_401x500.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after more subtle lace detailing when it comes to lacy tops, take a look at this one. It has a sheer body with a white underlay and features an eyelash lace trim. Team it with jeans and ballet flats for a classic feminine look.

Lace is a firm favourite on the fashion scene. Make it work for you this summer in the form of a lacy top. Which of these lacy tops do you like best?
