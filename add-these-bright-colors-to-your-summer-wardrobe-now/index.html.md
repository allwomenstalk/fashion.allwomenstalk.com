# Add These Bright Colors to Your Summer Wardrobe Now ...

Summer is all about bright colors, but sometimes it can be hard to find colors that you love and want to add to your wardrobe. Even more than that, it can be hard to figure out which colors are in style every summer. If you’re at a loss for your own style, you want to add color but aren’t really sure just how you should do that, these colors are sure to give you some great ideas for your perfectly colorful summer style. Whether you love and embrace color or tend to shy away from it but want to go big this summer, these colors are sure to inspire your summer wardrobe!

## 1. Coral

![Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/p0/cfx8u20i559ed6e448820679851764_600x932.jpg)

Coral may seem like an overplayed color at this point. I feel like it’s been on every single “Hot Color of the Spring/Summer” list for a few years now. However, I’m still having a love affair with it, and you should too. The coral color is just a tad different than both pink and orange, and it goes with a lot of neutrals, making it a fun and different choice for you to wear this summer!

## 2. Turquoise

![Turquoise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/lb/q7tds5nn559ed72069029821872127_400x600.jpg)

Turquoise is one of my personal favorite colors no matter the time of the year, but I especially love it during the summer. If you’re someone who wants to pull outfit inspiration from Mediterranean countries, turquoise is the only way to go. The color is the same shade as the sea, reminding everyone, including you, of relaxed days at the beach!

## 3. Neon Yellow

![Neon Yellow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/gl/ejusjo2h559ed73380aa7209639994_585x883.jpg)

Neon yellow is seriously having a moment right now. It’s everywhere, from dresses, to separates, and even bathing suits. If you’re looking for neon to wear this summer, your first go-to color should be yellow. It will especially look amazing once you have your summer tan!

## 4. Anything else That’s Neon

![Anything else That’s Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/ce/pnu774y7559ed74e7f27f592695133_600x900.jpg)

However, just because yellow is the hot neon of the summer does not mean that you have to avoid all neon colors this summer. All neon colors are so popular too this summer. Whether you want something green or pink or something else entirely, if it’s neon, you should add it to your summer wardrobe!

## 5. Go Pink

![Go Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/xc/hdvrvdxv559ed7a7a5329256610274_427x640.jpg)

No matter the shades of pink, I always think that pink is a summer wardrobe staple. It always adds such a nice pop, especially if you’re wearing something monochromatic. It doesn’t necessarily need to be neon, but a little touch of bright pink can go a long way in your summer wardrobe!

## 6. Electric Blue

![Electric Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sj/u6/yigi1ezl559ed7bebaa7e218973505_600x900.jpg)

Blue is already such a hot color this year and this summer, but electric blue is truly having a moment. If you want to experiment with a big, bold, and bright color, blue must be your choice. It is sure to stand out and make a statement no matter the event you’re going to!

## 7. Orange

![Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/rk/dm5awu7k559ed7d324b23231756569_600x900.jpg)

If pink and coral aren’t really your style but you want something in that family, try orange. Orange is a great color for the summer. It will look great no matter how you wear it, whether it’s as a dress, a crop top, or even a blazer. If you incorporate a little bit of orange into your look you’ll be surprised how far it goes in terms of color in your wardrobe!

What colors are you incorporating into your summer wardrobe? Let me know in the comments!
