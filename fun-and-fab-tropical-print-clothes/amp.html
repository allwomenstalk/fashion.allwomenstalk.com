8 Fun and Fab Tropical Print Clothes ...

Topshop Tropical Floral Print Shorts • Topshop Bright Floral Bow Back Top • Sandro Printed Silk Blouse • Dorothy Perkins Tropical Maxi Dress • Stella McCartney Hawaiian Print T-shirt • More ...

By Sophia

Tropical print clothing is making a bit of a comeback. Designers such as Stella McCartney, Altuzarra, and Proenza Schouler all featured tropical inspired prints in their Spring 2012 collections. To work tropical printed clothing this season, make sure to choose prints in bold, vivid colours and stick to contemporary designs. Take inspiration from the following list of stylish tropical print clothes.

1. Topshop Tropical Floral Print Shorts

Price: $56.00 at us.topshop.com
These printed running shorts are a fun take on the tropical print clothing trend. The print is bright and quirky but not too overpowering. These shorts have a relaxed design and would be perfect for working a casual summer look. They’d look great teamed with a sleeveless white blouse and coloured sandals.

2. Topshop Bright Floral Bow Back Top

Price: $56.00 at us.topshop.com
The tropical-inspired floral print on this blouse is sure to get you noticed. From the front the blouse looks like it has a fairly simple design but the back features a distinguishing bow detail. This top would look super stylish teamed with denim shorts for a casual weekend look or with cropped tuxedo trousers and heels for something dressier.

3. Sandro Printed Silk Blouse

Price: $340.00 at net-a-porter.com
For a luxe take on the tropical print clothing trend, you can’t go past a silk blouse. This white silk blouse features a striking tropical print on the front with three-quarter length sleeves and a split detail at the back. It would look great worn tucked into a full skirt and with strappy heels.

4. Dorothy Perkins Tropical Maxi Dress

Price: $59.00 at us.dorothyperkins.com
Tropical prints often evoke images of beaches and vacations. If you want a piece of tropical printed clothing that is more relaxed, then this maxi dress is a great choice. It has a basic bandeau design and has an elastic waist. Wear it with metallic sandals and eye-catching jewellery for a luxe resort-inspired look.

5. Stella McCartney Hawaiian Print T-shirt

Price: $315.00 at net-a-porter.com
Price: $300 for a t-shirt does seem exorbitant, but I guess that’s the going price of designer goods these days. This shirt is made from white semi-sheer cotton and features a blue-based Hawaiian print on the front. Create a fun look by teaming this shirt with blue or green coloured jeans.

6. ASOS Tropical Slipper Shoes

Price: $29.09 at asos.com
A quirky way to wear your tropical print clothing this season is on your feet. These shoes feature a tropical palm print and come in a casual slipper style. Wear them with a red or orange dress for a clash-tastic summer look.

7. Topshop Hawaiian Print Bralet

Price: $50.00 at us.topshop.com
It’s time to bare some skin with a cropped top this season. Work two trends at once with this Hawaiian print bralet. Style it with a high-waisted black maxi skirt for a contemporary look that’s bound to get you noticed.

8. Gianvito Rossi for Altuzarra Ankle Cuff Pump

Price: $795.00 at barneys.com
Like mentioned previously, footwear is a fun way to work the tropical print clothing trend. These stiletto heels were designed by Gianvito Rossi for Altuzarra and feature a tropical parrot print. If you’re after a statement heel, this is it.

Tropical print is a bold look and one that can be tricky to pull off. It’s a very trend-based look so I’d probably stick to the more affordable styles of tropical print clothing. What’s your take on tropical printed clothing this season?
