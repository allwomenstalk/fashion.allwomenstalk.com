9 Cool Winter Accessories ...

Eugenia Kim Leopard Beret • Missoni Striped Cashmere Gloves • Topshop Oxblood Floppy Fedora • Gucci Patent and Nappa Leather Gloves • Topshop Glossy Faux Fur Cossack • More ...

By Sophia

Winter Accessories can be both fashionable and functional. While keeping warm is a priority, we still want our winter accessories to look good too. From leopard print berets to faux fur trims, there are a range of ways to accessorise your winter outfit. Choosing the right winter accessories is all down to your personal style and preferences. Take a look at a few funky winter accessories below.

1. Eugenia Kim Leopard Beret

Price: $195.00 at barneys.com
This alpaca knit beret features a cute leopard print pattern. It is available in a range of colours and could easily liven up your winter look. This beret would look great worn with a grey swing coat and knee-high leather boots. While this version is fairly expensive, there are bound to be cheaper alternatives in similar designs available in high street stores.

2. Missoni Striped Cashmere Gloves

Price: $245.00 at net-a-porter.com
As far as winter accessories goes, gloves are almost a necessity. Keep your hands toasty warm this winter in these cashmere knit gloves. They come in a coral and nude colourway and feature a striped pattern throughout. A matching scarf is also available.

3. Topshop Oxblood Floppy Fedora

Price: $50.00 at us.topshop.com
The floppy fedora is a classic. This wool fedora comes in a rustic reddish-brown colour and is the perfect addition to your daytime winter look. It features a contrasting grosgrain bow ribbon and floppy brim. Wear this fedora with a knit sweater and flared jeans for an on-trend look.

4. Gucci Patent and Nappa Leather Gloves

Price: $565.00 at net-a-porter.com
Add a ‘grown-up’ touch to your look with these patent leather gloves. The patent leather is visible on the outside while the underside features nappa leather. These gloves also feature a designer plaque and are lined in silk. Wear these with a pussy-bow blouse and flared trousers for a retro-inspired look.

5. Topshop Glossy Faux Fur Cossack

Price: $45.00 at us.topshop.com
This faux fur cossack hat would make an interesting alternative to the common beanie. It is made from synthetic materials and comes in a rich brown colour. Winter accessories such as this cossack hat have a certain vintage feel to them. Team it with a swing coat, pencil skirt, and almond toe pumps for a ladylike look.

6. Eugenia Kim Lula Wool Headband

Price: $80.00 at net-a-porter.com
Wool knits are synonymous with winter dressing. This maroon cable-knit headband features a cool cross-over detail at the front. This wide headband is fairly chunky and looks best when worn low over the ears. Wear your hair out and slip this headband on for a casual winter look.

7. Limi Few Shawl Coat

Price: $1240.00 at lagarconne.com
This oversized shawl is a stylish way to keep warm when the temperature drops. It features tonal fringe detailing and comes with a silver safety pin fastening. Work a fashion forward look and team this shawl with leather-look leggings and a draped dress.

8. Marc by Marc Jacobs Wool Snood

Price: $180.00 at net-a-porter.com
This soft wool snood is perfect for throwing on over a casual outfit. The snood – a hybrid between a scarf and hood – is one of the most fail-safe winter accessories around. This snood is made from ribbed wool and comes in a cinnamon brown colour. For an off-duty look, pair it with a grey sweater, coloured jeans, and lace-up boots.

9. Topshop Slouchy over the Knee Socks

Price: $15.00 at us.topshop.com
If the weather allows, a pair of over the knee socks is a great option for fall/winter dressing. These basic black socks are ribbed and have a slouchy design. Wear them with short dresses or skirts, and team them with T-bar shoes.

There are a range of winter accessories available. Whether you want them as mere embellishments to an outfit or for more functional reasons, these winter accessories are sure to take you through the season in style. What kind of winter accessories will you be rocking this year?
