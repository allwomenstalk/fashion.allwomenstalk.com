# 7 Ways to Wear Leopard Print Right Now ...

When it comes to how to wear leopard print, there seems to be a fine line between fashion and faux pas. Printed separates are great for incorporating this trend into your wardrobes, and leopard print accessories are pretty fool-proof when it comes to how to wear leopard print in a sophisticated and stylish manner. Take a look at some new season ways for how to wear your leopard print pieces, right now.

## 1. Printed Denim

![Printed Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/113_600x752.jpg)

Printed denim has been really popular lately. So why not try a leopard print pair of denim jeans this season? When it comes to how to wear leopard print this way, keep the rest of your look fairly minimal. A plain t-shirt, blazer, and black pumps look effortlessly chic when teamed with leopard print jeans.

## 2. Shoes

![Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/27_500x527.jpg)

Is it just me or do leopard print shoes seem to be everywhere right now? Whether it’s a pair of leopard print loafers from Zara, sneakers by Vans, or platform heels by Charlotte Olympia it seems like leopard print shoes are definitely the way to go this season. Again, just remember to keep the rest of your ensemble minimal but chic.

## 3. Clutches

![Clutches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/37_750x502.jpg)

Leopard print accents are always a stylish option. Right now, you can’t go past a chic leopard print clutch. An oversized leopard print clutch is perfect for wearing during the daytime while a compact clutch is a stylish evening option.

## 4. Head to Toe

![Head to Toe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/46_378x650.jpg)

OK, so usually we might spruik the less is more idea when it comes to how to wear leopard print but there’s always the exception! Usually I’d cringe at the idea but after seeing matching leopard print separates work for designers such as 3.1 Phillip Lim and Karla Spetic, I’m convinced that it can look stylish when done right! However it does take practise so spend plenty of time in front of the mirror.

## 5. Sunglasses

![Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/55_750x502.jpg)

A subtle way for incorporating the leopard print trend into your outfit is with a pair of chic sunglasses. They’ll resemble a timeless tortoiseshell print, but with a bit more punch. The best part about how to wear leopard print this way is that you can wear it with all of your outfits!

## 6. Nail Art

![Nail Art](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/63_500x375.jpg)

A fun way to wear leopard print right now is with some quirky nail art. If you’re not adept enough to design your own leopard print looks with nail polish, try an easy nail wrap or sticker instead. Consider incorporating different colour palettes as well.

## 7. Colour

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/fashion/2012/12/7_300x578.jpeg)

Your normal black and yellow/tan leopard print might be timeless, but right now we’re seeing a trend toward more colourful prints. When it comes to how to wear leopard print this season, take a cue from the House of Holland and embrace an unusually colourful print instead.

When it comes to how to wear leopard print, there are plenty of ways to make this look work for you. These are just a few tips for you to take note of right now. When it comes to how wear your leopard print, what tips do you have to share?
