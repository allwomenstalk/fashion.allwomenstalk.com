# 7 Ways to Wear Yoga Pants in the Office ...

Is there anything in the world more comfortable than yoga pants? I think not! Too bad you can't wear them to work... or can you? Let's check out these ways to wear yoga pants in the office, and still look stylish.

## 1. With a Long Sweater and Flats

![With a Long Sweater and Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/g6/rchjj1r756078d9c8bc92984034037_465x700.jpg)

If you have a long, butt-covering sweater, try wearing that over your yoga pants, like you would with leggings, but instead of boots, wear flats or TOMS. Yoga pants at work? Done!

## 2. With a Blouse and Blazer

![With a Blouse and Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/nk/rzhk8iwd56078dd045c53102599820_600x900.jpg)

Will anyone know those are black or dark grey yoga pants with that crisp white blouse and super-cute blazer? Probably not. You'll be stylish and comfortable in equal measure.

## 3. With an off-the-shoulder Tunic Top

![With an off-the-shoulder Tunic Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/r0/d1pr4sdk56078e4f2b20a171664801_600x808.jpg)

Again, wear your yoga pants instead of leggings with your tunic top, and slouch it off one shoulder to make your outfit extra-cute. If you want a little more modesty, wear a tank underneath, too.

## 4. With a Sporty Top

![With a Sporty Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/lt/oqebscht56078e63f1e04979766123_520x693.jpg)

I mean, they are yoga pants, so if you have a nice, not grubby, sports top you can wear, give it a go! Just wear casual shoes, like slip-ons, instead of your trainers.

## 5. With a Chambray Shirt

![With a Chambray Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/lr/bszbk2g356078e857e058154512105_600x600.jpg)

Picture this: dark grey yoga pants, soft light grey vee-neck tee, and a long-sleeved blue chambray button-down with rolled sleeves and a dainty gold necklace. Aaand... your work outfit is complete!

## 6. With a Vest

![With a Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/ly/pqq97byx56078eae49188500118051_600x901.jpg)

I love how this looks! Pair your yoga pants with a long-sleeved tee and a cute vest, then a pair of low-heel booties. Darling and totes office-appropriate!

## 7. Invest in Yoga Slacks

![Invest in Yoga Slacks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/tm/ehi7lfzn56078ee4a1d65699669367_600x900.jpg)

If you haven't heard of Betabrand yoga pants for the office, you need to check them out, stat! These pants truly are as comfy as yoga pants, but they look like office attire, all the way. Swoon!

What other ways can you think of wearing your Saturday morning yoga pants to the office Monday through Friday?
