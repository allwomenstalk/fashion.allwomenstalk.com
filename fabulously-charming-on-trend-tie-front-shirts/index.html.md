# 7 Fabulously Charming on\-Trend Tie\-Front Shirts ...

Tie Front Shirts are an on-trend choice this season. A shirt tied at the bottom can look effortlessly stylish paired with a casual outfit of skinny jeans and pumps. While tie front shirts may be a reminder of less-than-tasteful 80s and 90s fashion, this year’s look is all about creating a relaxed yet polished look. Take a look at just a few tie front shirts below.

## 1. Dorothy Perkins Green Tie Front Blouse

![Dorothy Perkins Green Tie Front Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/a5/jaj527ic54c9348b054aa053558765_350x472.jpg)

**Price:** $46.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Jewel colours are a popular choice this season. This shirt is an easy way to wear the trend. It has a button-down front, fitted cuffs, and front patch pocket. Tie front shirts like this would look great paired with dark denim jeans and patent pumps. Finish off with a pair of drop earrings for added sparkle.

## 2. Theory Silk-Chiffon Tie Front Shirt

![Theory Silk-Chiffon Tie Front Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/uz/qzelqfrv54c9348bee2b0722713006_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $235.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hNXDC9)
For a subtler approach, try out a black tie front shirt. This one has a loose fit, button-fastening cuffs, and elasticated hem. This shirt is perfect for someone who prefers a minimalist aesthetic. Team it with a pair of high-wasted shorts for a polished take on casual dressing.

## 3. Topshop Gingham Tie Front Shirt

![Topshop Gingham Tie Front Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/bw/b0sfpu5l54c9348d21df0358920806_350x510.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1ezEXM)
Gingham fabrics are a great way of incorporating some retro chic into your wardrobe. It’s a look that was popular on the runways, and is slowly drifting into the mainstream. This black and white sleeveless top features a contrasting collar, button-down front, and single breast pocket. For a seriously preppy look, team it with a full skirt, wide headband, and T-bar pumps.

## 4. Carven Silk Blouse with Self Tie Front

_ **Price:** $355.00 at mytheresa.com_
Rather than tie front detailing at the hem, this Carven shirt features it at the bust. The look has a refined preppy feel to it. This blouse has a button-up design and features a contrasting pointed collar. Wear this shirt tucked into a high-waisted skirt and with classic ballet flats for a polished look.

## 5. Topshop Mini Spot Tie Front Shirt

![Topshop Mini Spot Tie Front Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/cp/pkoa54bv54c9348f27fde717760531_350x522.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/9z4FF5)
This season is all about spots and polka dots. This sleeveless navy top has an all over spot print, pointed collar, and tie front hem. All over prints are a fun variation on tie front shirts. Wear this shirt with burgundy jeans and a mustard coloured cardigan for an effortlessly on-trend look.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Blue Tie Hem Top

**Price:** $24.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This royal blue top features pleating throughout the front and a tie-front hem. It also has draped butterfly-style sleeves. Wear this shirt with a printed high-waisted skirt and nude pumps. Add come colourful accessories for good measure, too.

## 7. Topshop Knot Front Tank

![Topshop Knot Front Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q7/ex/upq9riz754c9349156863731004965_350x513.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/RuWJC2)
Tie front shirts such as this one are great for creating an athletic aesthetic. This tank top is made from a marl fabric and features a knot detail at the hem. It has a sleeveless design and a round necklince. Wear this top with dark denim jeans, and an oversized knit cardigan for a cool everyday look.

These are just a few tie front shirts that are currently available. Whether you prefer a preppy or athletic look, there’s no denying that this trend is one that is in favour at the moment. What are your thoughts on tie front shirts?
