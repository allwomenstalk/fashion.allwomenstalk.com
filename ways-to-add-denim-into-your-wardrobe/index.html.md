# 7 Ways to Add Denim into Your Wardrobe ...

Of course, denim is one of those trends that just never go out of style. However, for some reason, it always becomes a much bigger trend during the spring. Something about the weather warming up truly brings out the denim trend every year. As we get closer and closer to spring, denim is going to become a bigger part of your wardrobe again. If you’re looking for new and fresh ways to add denim to your wardrobe, this list will help you come up with new, trendy ideas for this season!

## 1. Denim Jacket

![Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/cg/xvmtw4bk54ef7653849ba677220828_600x857.jpg)

What is it about denim jackets that make them seem so carefree and perfect for spring? Maybe it’s the idea of throwing one over your favorite sundress when the cold, winter air finally gives you a break that just reminds me of spring. No matter what, it’s the perfect way to incorporate a little bit of denim into your favorite springtime looks!

## 2. Denim Vest

![Denim Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qb/ml/lmhgqiem54ef76a1b9cda880486212_432x627.jpg)

No matter what look you’re going for, a denim vest can add a little something else to your look. If you’re trying to go for a grunge look, a roughed up denim vest would look great with your favorite tank top. If you’re going for a more preppy look, a lighter denim vest would pair perfectly with your favorite statement necklace.

## 3. Denim Shirt a La Rachel Greene

![Denim Shirt a La Rachel Greene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/is/pw/skpuwsdv54ef76cadff39964667154_600x900.jpg)

More than ten years after the finale of Friends, and we’re all still drooling over Rachel Greene’s fashion choices. If you loved her work uniform from her Central Perk days, bring it back. Find a sleeveless denim shirt and tie it up. The 90s are back, girls!

## 4. Colored Denim

![Colored Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/h4/mte79dpr54ef771ea22fe122546136_600x908.jpg)

I think we can all agree that the best part of spring is the color. Not only does it feel like the entire world starts to bloom again, but we can also finally add some much-needed color to our wardrobes. Include that into your denim jeans, by wearing pastel jeans, bright, statement jeans, or other colors entirely. It’s a fun way to switch things up from the norm!

## 5. A Denim Dress

![A Denim Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/ig/jv1a2t1254ef7750ae51a225185005_396x594.jpg)

Okay, so hear me out. A denim dress may seem too 90s for you to even consider wearing, but it’s actually a versatile choice! If you’re going for a grunge look, try an acid wash sleeveless dress. If you want to wear denim with a preppy edge, wear a denim shirtdress, belted and with ¾ length sleeves to complete the look.

## 6. Cropped Denim

![Cropped Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/q3/zabkr3an54ef798ddf8dd479667948_600x900.jpg)

I just think that there’s something so fresh about denim that’s cropped just above the ankle. Whether it’s white denim pants or simply a good pair of denim jeans, cropped denim is one of the easiest ways to look cute and pulled together when you’re in a hurry!

## 7. Classic Boyfriend Jeans

![Classic Boyfriend Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/r2/rxxtzs9s54ef79aa0436b004270833_600x900.jpg)

Last, but certainly not least, are the classic boyfriend jeans. Everyone has her favorite pair of comfortable jeans sitting in her closet, waiting to be worn with the perfect outfit. You may reserve your favorite pair of jeans for more relaxed events, but with a little playing around, you may be surprised by just how quickly you can put together an outfit for an important event!

How are you adding denim to your wardrobe this spring? Give me some ideas and tips in the comments!
