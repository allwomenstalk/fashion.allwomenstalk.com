# 7 Beach Fashion Looks to Steal ...

Beach fashion is something you should start thinking about if you’ll be hitting the beaches this summer. Pretty sundresses and fun rompers are all great alternatives to the tried and true combination of denim shorts and singlets. The best part about a stylish beach inspired fashion look is that you don’t always have to wear it at the beach. Take inspiration from the following beach fashion trends.

## 1. Printed Coverup

![Printed Coverup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_printed-coverup_553x775.jpg)

Cover up in a bold printed dress when you hit the beach this season. A bright, colourful print is the perfect beach fashion for the summer season. Choose a dress in a relaxed style so you can simply slip it on over your swimming gear. Off the beach, a printed coverup would look chic teamed with strappy sandals, a compact clutch, and wavy beach hair.

## 2. All in White

![All in White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_all-in-white_521x793.jpg)

An all white outfit is one of the hottest looks this season and one that can easily be translated to the beach. Whether you choose white separates or a one-piece is entirely up to you. Striped accessories are great for adding a nautical touch while neons are perfect for a more contemporary look.

## 3. Denim Shorts

![Denim Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_denim-shorts_554x831.jpg)

Denim shorts are a firm favourite when it comes to beach fashion. A distressed pair of denim shorts is great for working a casual look. If you’re style is a little bit more refined, however, you can always opt for looser and more tailored designs. Wear them with a fitted top and strappy sandals for a bit more style.

## 4. Sheer Maxi

![Sheer Maxi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_sheer-maxi_553x812.jpg)

A sheer maxi is a luxe way to cover up at the beach. It’s perfect for creating a bohemian inspired look. Adding a crochet or lace top is also a great way of creating another textural element to the look.

## 5. Chambray Shirt Dress

![Chambray Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_chambray-shirt-dress_554x831.jpg)

For relaxed beach fashion, you can’t go past a casual shirt dress. This simple chambray number is relaxed beach fashion at its best. Simply slip it on over your swimming gear and accessorise with a straw trilby and aviator sunglasses.

## 6. Little Black Dress

![Little Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_little-black-dress_383x576.jpg)

Make the little black dress work at the beach by choosing one in a relaxed cut. Complete the dark and mysterious look by teaming a beach-friendly LBD with a floppy hat and oversized sunglasses.

## 7. Neon Neon

![Neon Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_neon-neon_500x750.jpg)

Neon is definitely trending right now, so be sure to incorporate it into your beach fashion. You don’t have to go to town with this trend, just add a few neon pieces here and there to update your look. For bolder beach fashion looks, try a surf-inspired neon dress for a cool and contemporary take on the trend.

When it comes to beach fashion, there are a number of paths that you could go down. From luxe looks to more casual outfits, these are just a few beach trends to take inspiration from. Which of these beach inspired fashion looks do you like best?
