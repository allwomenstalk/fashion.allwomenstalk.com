# 16 Easy Fashion and Beauty Resolutions for the New Year ...

Happy 2019 darling AWS readers! We hope you had a splendid 2018 and aren't too hungover after all the festivities. Of course, you can't start the new year without a few resolutions, so here are our **fashion and beauty resolutions for 2019** ...

## 1. Experiment with Styles That You Usually Steer Away from...

![Experiment with Styles That You Usually Steer Away from...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/tt/og0ttize56878f1f98780840569542_600x901.jpg)

Fashion is supposed to be fun, so don't focus too much on the "rules". You'll be surprised to discover what you can pull off despite what the rules say.

## 2. Spend Wisely

![Spend Wisely](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/gt/v5i494j456878f1f15b29375275737_480x720.jpg)

You don't have to buy something just because it's on sale. How many times have you ended up with clothes that still have tags on them years later? Ask yourself whether it really looks good on you and if you're actually going to wear it more than once.

## 3. Clean out Your Closet

![Clean out Your Closet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/a8/ybieilmn56878f1fb8248485547887_500x750.jpg)

This would be the perfect time to go through your closet and get rid of everything you haven't seen or worn in a while. Donate them, give them away, or resell them to make some money.

## 4. Get a Professional Bra Fitting

![Get a Professional Bra Fitting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/so/hg1d4l1z56878f24b14dc564962782_500x750.jpg)

I cannot stress the importance of good fitting lingerie!

## 5. Invest in Staple Pieces

![Invest in Staple Pieces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/au/aa8l6shr56878f2581907995070706_499x750.jpg)

Quality staple pieces will never go out of style and they'll last a long, long time.

## 6. Accept That Some Styles Will Simply Not Work on Your Body Type

![Accept That Some Styles Will Simply Not Work on Your Body Type](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/xw/bnxcathq56878f275d096913815819_600x900.jpg)

Yes you should be fearless when it comes to fashion but at the same time, you have to accept that some styles will just not look good on you, no matter how much you try.

## 7. Get a Pair of Statement Shoes

![Get a Pair of Statement Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/c6/dxc0k88756878f2606571369103458_500x667.jpg)

Every girl needs a pair of "Omg-where-did-you-get-those-shoes-from?" shoes!

## 8. Wear Bold Lips

![Wear Bold Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/kk/v16l95bz56878f279e45f374092777_600x809.jpg)

Bring out the reds, maroons, purples, browns, and blacks!

## 9. Have Fun with Makeup

![Have Fun with Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/lv/tb6hwomt56878f2885c64367594035_600x798.jpg)

Break out of your makeup rut in 2016 and vow to take risks.

## 10. Be Good to Your Skin

![Be Good to Your Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/x8/y03394ly56878f26e2732743945580_500x625.jpg)

That means no sleeping with makeup on, plenty of sunscreen and moisturizer, and less sugar, more water.

## 11. Give Thrift Shopping a Try

![Give Thrift Shopping a Try](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/u1/y7lyc35v56878f279365b672210067_500x667.jpg)

You never know what gems you may end up finding.

## 12. Spend Time Accessorizing

![Spend Time Accessorizing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/ic/oamwedj756878f28e2883286840144_600x900.jpg)

Accessories maketh the outfit.

## 13. Go Makeup Free Sometimes

![Go Makeup Free Sometimes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/ww/qnkz8cfo56878f28cd904296395058_500x667.jpg)

Your skin will thank you!

## 14. Master How to do Your Brows and Winged Liner

![Master How to do Your Brows and Winged Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/co/jl/vxfsxn8z56878f29243d0252022699_493x750.jpg)

Winged liner is the perfect go-to for when you want to look glamorous with minimum effort.

## 15. Say No to Anything Tested on Animals

![Say No to Anything Tested on Animals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/z2/ytmcvden56878f29ac863491906037_600x1067.jpg)

When there are plenty of cruelty-free options to choose from, why would you try anything else?

And finally...

## 16. Love Your Body

![Love Your Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ju/u1/l8agyoso56879981d2452560799401_500x748.jpg)

...and if you don't, start making changes. When you love your body, fashion automatically becomes more fun! I'm not saying fitness isn't important, but perhaps it's time we realize that "fit" comes in all shapes and sizes.

What are your fashion and beauty resolutions for 2019?
