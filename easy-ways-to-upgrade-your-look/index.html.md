# 7 Easy Ways to Upgrade Your Look ...

Are you looking for some **easy ways to upgrade your look?** You are not alone.

On top of having 3 kids, I have little time to devote to my appearance. I've never been one to spend time on makeup and clothing, however, I have learned a few things over the years that help to make me look and feel more attractive.

These tips are so simple that anyone can pull them off! So try these **easy ways to upgrade your look** and rock your day, everyday.

## 1. Earrings

![eyebrow, beauty, human hair color, chin, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a2/xs/z1ec68x159bf626b77e75833117904_600x597.jpg)

I am an earring fiend! Any and all kinds. I absolutely love earrings! Even a simple crystal stud can add just a bit of glimmer to your smile. This is my favorite of the many **easy ways to upgrade your look.**

## 2. Other Pieces of Jewelry

![fashion model, fashion, outerwear, neck, socialite,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/h7/fcpam1o459bf62ef6e002435824786_600x722.jpg)

Beyond earrings, almost any piece of jewelry can make you feel special! I particularly like anklets and bracelets, but go for what you like! Necklaces can totally make an outfit!

## 3. Mascara

![eyebrow, face, beauty, eyelash, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/xo/igspavb359bf631924bbe713169145_600x600.jpg)

My favorite cosmetic is mascara. The impact is not as huge as making up your whole face, but it does have a subtle brightening effect on the eyes and maybe that's all you want! Plus it's incredibly quick to apply.

## 4. Scarves

![pink, beauty, shoulder, vision care, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/vn/zw33zh0s59bf6430a60e6861125625_600x900.jpg)

Scarves are awesome because there are so many options to choose from. Thin and colorful for summer, and thicker and cozy for fall. You can wear one on your head in a Rosie the Riveter fashion, or loop it around your neck and shoulders. Fabric and length are virtually limitless!

## 5. Sexy Underwear

![lingerie, undergarment, model, girl, brassiere,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sa/w1/jgzpa61o59c10217c416d417846434_500x500.jpg)

This MAY seem like a counterproductive tip but trust me! Even if no one sees it, wearing sexy underwear can make you feel like a million bucks! Plus, don't forget what a good bra can do for you cleavage!

## 6. Boots

![footwear, fashion model, joint, tights, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/h3/lk6nkbai59bf65765a48b790476567_600x735.jpg)

Who doesn't love a good boot? Like scarves, there are a variety of boots for every shape and size. When I wear boots, I feel 1,000x more confident. My only regret is that I can't wear boots in the summertime.

## 7. Eyebrows

![eyebrow, beauty, human hair color, chin, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/u1/wogtwol859bf653fc55bc658157780_600x739.jpg)

Whether you pluck and trim yourself or have someone do them professionally, I believe keeping up on your eyebrows is a small way to make a big impact.

When I was a kid, I asked my mom why jewelry makes you look so much better. She told me it was like a Christmas tree. An evergreen is beautiful and as nature intended all on its own. Decorations simply help it sparkle and shine.
