# 9 Retro Looking Fashion Accessories ...

Retro looking fashion accessories are the perfect addition to your outfit this season. Fashion is quite cyclical and we often see past fashions making a comeback every few decades. A retro look fashion accessory can make all the difference when styling your outfit this season. Channel bygone eras and be inspired by the following retro look fashion accessories.

Add some retro glamour to your look with these cat eye sunglasses. The glossy black frame is stylish and versatile. These retro looking fashion accessories would look great with anything from the most vintage of outfits to the most modern.

## 2. Poodle Print Bow Headband

![Poodle Print Bow Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_poodle-print-bow-headband_600x562.jpg)

## 3. Leatherette Braid Wool Hat

_ **Price:** $14.85 at forever21.com_
Work a free spirited 1970s inspired look with this burnt orange hat. It has a floppy wide brim with contrasting leatherette braid. Wear it with a floaty maxi dress or bell-sleeved crochet dress to complete the boho look.

## 4. Uptown and Country Bag

## 5. Anna Sui Printed Silk Chiffon Turban

![Anna Sui Printed Silk Chiffon Turban](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_anna-sui-printed-silk-chiffon-turban_460x474.jpg)

## 6. Retro Glam Clip on Earrings

_ **Price:** $4.80 at forever21.com_
These clip on earrings look like they could be vintage! They have a square design and come in a chic black and gold colour combination. They also feature a rhinestone centre. Wear them with a cocktail dress for a glamorous night time look.

## 7. Thierry Lasry round-Frame Acetate Sunglasses

![Thierry Lasry round-Frame Acetate Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_thierry-lasry-round-frame-acetate-sunglasses_460x240.jpg)

If the full retro look isn’t for you then you can always just pop these retro looking fashion accessories on with your everyday outfit for some added glamour!

## 8. First in Glasses Case in Deco Diamond

## 9. Marni Contrast Trimmed Leather Shoulder Bag

Whether you’re channelling a glamorous 1950s look or 1970s bohemia, retro looking fashion accessories can be found to suit a range of styles and tastes. What kind of retro looking accessory, if any, will you be toting this season?
