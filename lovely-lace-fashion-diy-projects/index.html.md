# 10 Lovely Lace Fashion DIY Projects ...

Lace Fashion DIY Projects are refashion ideas that range from fairly simple to somewhat complicated. Whatever your craft skill level is, I'm sure you will find something fun to do from this list of** lace fashion DIY projects**. Some of these will require sewing but there are also some that can be constructed using simpler steps. Let's check out these **pretty lace fashion craft projects**!

## 1. Removable Lace Collar

_ Using lace and ribbon, you can make this pretty **lace fashion DIY project** this weekend. The great thing about this necklace idea is that it can be made in many versions. You can use lace of other colors or designs. You can even sew this directly to a top to give it a girlier vibe.

## 3. Lace Collar

## 5. Easy Lace Skirt

![Easy Lace Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/52_553x403.jpg)

This is a simple lace skirt that you can wear with tights. If you want to catch up with the sheer trend, this is the** lace fashion DIY project** for you. Don't worry if you can't sew; Maegan hand stitched hers.

[Tutorial](https://lovemaegan.com/)

## 6. Lace Pencil Skirt

![Lace Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/62_553x403.jpg)

Gorgeous! This is a project for those who love to sew. This is going to my to-sew list, no doubt about it. Check out the link to see how Leanne made this beautiful lace pencil skirt.

[Tutorial](http://elleapparel.blogspot.com/2010/11/lace-for-days-skirt.html)

## 7. Lace Earrings

![Lace Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/72_384x274.jpg)

Cut pretty pattern from lace, harden it using clear nail polish, add beads and wires, and you're done! Simple but the earrings you end up making will be very lovely.

[Tutorial](http://thecupcakediary.blogspot.com/2008/11/diy-lace-earrings.html)

## 8. Lace Pocket Shorts

![Lace Pocket Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/82_553x403.jpg)

If you think that a pair of shorts covered in lace is much too girly, perhaps you can refashion the pockets only? This is a fantastic** lace fashion DIY project** for those who are new at fashion-related craft projects.

[Tutorial](http://www.chictopia.com/photo/show/622778-DIY+Lace+Pocket+Shorts-coral-diy-lace-american-apparel-shorts-ivory-cropped-lace-motel-rocks-top)

## 9. Lace Choker

![Lace Choker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/9_600x402.jpg)

A little bit Goth, 100% lovely. From Cathy: It’s a great statement piece that works with either a T shirt and jeans or an evening look. Very true. This will look lovely even without the brooch.

## 10. Lace Crown

![Lace Crown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/101_1024x702.jpg)
