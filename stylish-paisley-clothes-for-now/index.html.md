# 8 Stylish Paisley Clothes for Now ...

Paisley clothes are enjoying a bit of a revival at the moment. When you first think of paisley, your mind might instantly conjure up images of retro hippie prints. However, this season’s take on paisley is much more contemporary and, dare we say it, fashion forward. Not convinced yet? Then check out all these paisley clothes to wear right now.

## 1. Floral Paisley Dress

![Floral Paisley Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_floral-paisley-dress_600x732.jpg)

Work a playful summertime look in a brightly coloured paisley dress. Since paisley clothes can be quite busy, stick to dresses in simple cuts like this sleeveless dress from Forever 21. Team a colourful paisley dress with tan sandals and a simple side bag for a casual weekend look. Be sure to check out ASOS and J.Crew for more colourful paisley dresses.

## 2. Paisley Shorts

![Paisley Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_paisley-shorts_600x566.jpg)

Printed shorts are a hot trend right now. Pick out a paisley pair for a cute look this season. Team them with a slouchy t-shirt and sandals for a cool summertime look, or dress them up with a blouse, blazer, and pumps for something more sophisticated. These tailored paisley shorts will set you back around $76 from Topshop and you can find similar styles from Charlotte Russe and Lauren Ralph Lauren.

## 3. Paisley Blazer

![Paisley Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_paisley-blazer_600x809.jpg)

Instead of flowing, seventies-inspired maxi dresses, keep your paisley clothes on-trend by sticking to tailored styles. One way to incorporate a bit of paisley into your wardrobe is with a paisley print blazer. Team it with jeans and a plain tank top or pop it on over a plain white dress. This blazer is pretty expensive at $130 from Topshop but you can find more affordable styles from ASOS and Macy’s.

## 4. Paisley Peplum Top

![Paisley Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_paisley-peplum-top_600x732.jpg)

A paisley print top can instantly add some character to your outfit. Wear one with leather-look trousers and heels for a stylish night time look. This blue and white paisley print top is from Forever 21 and, at under $20, is a steal compared to similar designer versions by MSGM.

## 5. Paisley Print Jeans

![Paisley Print Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_paisley-print-jeans_600x900.jpg)

Work a rock star inspired vibe in a pair of paisley print jeans. These Topshop jeans feature bold paisley details to the sides and would look great teamed with a chambray shirt and short ankle boots. They’ll set you back $96 but you can find more budget-friendly buys at Forever 21 and Kohl’s.

## 6. Paisley Denim Skater Skirt

![Paisley Denim Skater Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_paisley-denim-skater-skirt_600x581.jpg)

Denim skirts are a summer classic. This season, try a paisley denim skirt on for size. This skater skirt from Dorothy Perkins costs around $30 and would look great worn with a simple white blouse and ballet flats. You can also check out ASOS and Forever 21 for more paisley skirts this season.

## 7. Paisley Overalls

![Paisley Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_paisley-overalls_350x500.jpg)

Overalls are having a big moment right now. These paisley overalls from Modcloth are great for working a relaxed summertime look. Pop on a pastel t-shirt underneath, slip on some canvas sneakers, and hit the park in style!

## 8. Paisley Shoes

![Paisley Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_paisley-shoes_304x432.jpg)

Paisley shoes would make a quirky addition to any outfit. Designers like Givenchy and Carven have included paisley shoes in their collections but you don’t have to spend big to be able to wear the trend. These paisley shoes are from Modcloth and will set you back around $50.

These paisley clothes are some of the more stylish options available right now. What do you think of the paisley trend? Which of these paisley clothes do you like best?
