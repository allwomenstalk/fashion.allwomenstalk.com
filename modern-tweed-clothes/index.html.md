# 7 Modern Tweed Clothes ...

**Tweed Clothes** are a surprisingly chic choice this season. While the idea of tweed fabric may conjure up images of dowdy or less than stylish outfits, this season the look lends toward more contemporary aesthetics. There are a range of stylish tweed clothes out there fit for any modern wardrobe. Take a look at some contemporary tweed clothes below.

## 1. Michael Michael Kors Tweed Jacket

![Michael Michael Kors Tweed Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_michael-michael-kors-tweed-jacket_409x500.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wj3J97)
Nothing says modern style more than metallics. This particular jacket is made from black and gold metallic silk-blend tweed. It has a slightly military inspired design with a round neck, epaulets, and four tab pockets. This jacket is a contemporary take on classic **tweed clothes**. It would look great worn with black skinny jeans, patent pumps and a sleek envelope clutch.

## 2. Lanvin Neon Tweed Mini Skirt

![Lanvin Neon Tweed Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_lanvin-neon-tweed-mini-skirt_382x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1190.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/NXFwAf)
Neon clothes will be well and truly making their mark this season. This neon green tweed mini skirt by Lanvin embraces the neon trend in a unique way. The cut is quite modern and so is the choice of colour. Luckily, the neon green is mixed in with black so it’s not too confronting.

## 3. Zero + Maria Cornejo Tweed Shorts

![Zero + Maria Cornejo Tweed Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_zero-maria-cornejo-tweed-shorts_360x344.jpg)

**Price:** $199.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These shorts are a great take on **contemporary tweed clothes**. They have a curved hem and feature diagonal front pleats and side pockets. These shorts are designed to be a relaxed fit and they also have an elastic waistband. They’d look great worn with a blush silk blouse and tan sandals.

## 4. Giambattista Valli Tweed Dress

![Giambattista Valli Tweed Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_giambattista-valli-tweed-dress_210x500.jpg)

**Price:** $3135.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/dKWtU6)
Despite the fact you’d have to be seriously cashed up to buy this dress, you can’t deny its modern credentials. It is made from multicoloured tweed and features a very on-trend tribal inspired pattern on the skirt. The red silk-grosgrain waist does an excellent job of breaking up the bright yellow tweed. This is a fun and feminine take on tweed dressing.

## 5. Erdem Tweed Mini Skirt

_ **Price:** $355.00 at mytheresa.com_
A tweed mini skirt is one of the easiest ways to wear **tweed clothes**. This particular skirt comes in a striking blue colour and has a slim silhouette. It would look great worn with a slouchy sweater and ankle boots for a funky, off-beat look.

## 6. Oscar De La Renta Tweed Shorts

![Oscar De La Renta Tweed Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_oscar-de-la-renta-tweed-shorts_460x404.jpg)

**Price:** $590.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YyWPob)
These luxe tweed shorts are a modern and stylish take on uptown dressing. These particular **tweed clothes** feature a metallic thread throughout and have fringe details at the cuffs. They’re quite a tailored cut and would look fantastic teamed with a white silk blouse and patent ballet flats.

## 7. Proenza Schouler Button Front Dress

_ **Price:** $615.00 at mytheresa.com_
This wrap-front dress has a delightfully modern appeal. It features a multicoloured metallic bodice with lurex trims and a tweed patterned skirt. This would make a great party dress, especially teamed with some metallic pumps and a sleek clutch. If **contemporary tweed clothes** are what you’re after then you can’t go past this dress for inspiration.

When incorporating tweed into your wardrobe, take inspiration from these modern pieces. If you’re afraid of anything too vintage or dowdy, there are plenty of **contemporary tweed clothes** out there. What do you think of these tweed clothes?
