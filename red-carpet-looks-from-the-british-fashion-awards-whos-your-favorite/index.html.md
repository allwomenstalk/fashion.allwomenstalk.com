# Red Carpet Looks from the British Fashion Awards \- Whos Your Favorite?

The 2014 British Fashion Awards took place on Monday night, December 1st, and honored some of the biggest fashion-forward designers, models, and good dressers. Some of the winners included Cara Delevingne for Best Model and Emma Watson for the British Style Award! Here's a look at the best red carpet fashion:

## 1. Kendall Jenner

![Kendall Jenner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/w8/g11f8irg_600x969.jpg)

Kendall stunned in a white jumpsuit that proved the girl has legs for days!

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/em/s7d9v6fc_600x969.jpg)

## 3. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/um/lp19mulc_600x969.jpg)

## 4. Victoria Beckham

![Victoria Beckham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/pt/dadvrg7n_600x969.jpg)
