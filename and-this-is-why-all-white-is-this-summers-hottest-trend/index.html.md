# And This is Why All\-White is This Summers Hottest Trend ...

Remember that old fashion rule about waiting for the unofficial start of summer to wear white? Well, according to a survey by [TJ Maxx](http://tjmaxx.tjx.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and [Marshalls,](https://www.marshalls.com/us/store/index.jsp) 63% of women agree that it's time to abolish that rule for good. After all, it's not about the season. It's about the temperature!

61% of women start to wear white when the temperature is between 55° and 76° or higher, and 39% of women wear white year-round, no matter what the temperature is. So don't keep your white clothes in your closet when you could start wearing them today!

The top three white clothing items and accessories women are most excited to wear are tank tops, dresses, and shoes. If you can't wait to wear white, you should check out these clothing items that are available in [TJ Maxx](http://tjmaxx.tjx.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and [Marshalls](https://www.marshalls.com/us/store/index.jsp) stores nationwide:
