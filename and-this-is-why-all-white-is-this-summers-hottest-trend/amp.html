White Shoe • Marshalls Sandal • Marshalls White Bag • Marshalls White Dress • Marshalls White Jacket • More ...

By Holly

Remember that old fashion rule about waiting for the unofficial start of summer to wear white? Well, according to a survey by TJ Maxx and Marshalls, 63% of women agree that it's time to abolish that rule for good. After all, it's not about the season. It's about the temperature!

61% of women start to wear white when the temperature is between 55° and 76° or higher, and 39% of women wear white year-round, no matter what the temperature is. So don't keep your white clothes in your closet when you could start wearing them today!
