# 8 Eco Friendly Fashions to Rock on Earth Day ...

When was the last time you checked out eco friendly fashions? While fashion that is kinder to the environment, and to the people who make it, has been a hot topic lately, there’s still some confusion over which brands really are creating the styles we love, and which are missing the mark. Fancy updating your wardrobe with fashion that really helps the earth? Here’s some of the top eco friendly fashions and brands to choose.

## 1. People Tree…

![People Tree…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_people-tree_352x449.jpg)

People Tree create some of my favorite eco friendly fashions, and they’ve certainly grown in popularity since their launch in the UK in 2001. ASOS made the brand available worldwide, and their stylish designs have been snapped up everywhere since. From floral rompers to polka-dot trousers and printed tops, you’ll find high fashion items that you really want to wear, but in great eco friendly materials. They’ve even got collections designed by actress Emma Watson. I love them, and they are super easy to clean, too!

## 2. Nepali by TDM, Design…

![Nepali by TDM, Design…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2-nepali-by-TM-design_724x361.jpg)

Fancy making an eco-day statement without buying a whole new outfit? Check out these scarves. Founder Michelle Baldwin found a whole host of inspiration for amazing scarves on a 2007 visit to Nepal, and now works directly with female weavers who use bamboo, silk, wool and cashmere to create scarves that look great, and love the Earth. It’s no surprise that celebrities like Anne Hathaway and Kim Kardashian are big fans!

## 3. Urban Renewals…

![Urban Renewals…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_urban-renewals_352x528.jpg)

I love this playsuit, and it comes with plenty of feel-good vibes, too. Urban Renewals is the eco friendly collection by Urban Outfitters, containing the same style that they are known for in a host of earth-friendly dyes and materials. There’s everything from unique one-of-a-kind pieces to vintage inspired designs and off-the-hanger outfits, and there’s a host of accessories on offer, too.

## 4. Sprout…

![Sprout…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_sprout_597x361.jpg)

How cute is this nautical watch?! It’d be the perfect finishing touch for a whole variety of outfits, and it’s sure to attract compliments- and you can wear it knowing you have done the world a favor, too. Sprout uses cork, bamboo, conflict-free diamonds and corn resin to make jewelry that you’ll fall in love with, and it’s designed to be affordable, too. Most of their watches cost less than $75! That’s my summer style sorted.

## 5. Maheya…

![Maheya…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_maheya_352x527.jpg)

Maheya by Free People has a definite carefree vibe, with their collections containing tie-dye tops, silk dresses, kaftans and shorts…items that look great in the sun, and will keep you cool, too . Everything is made using natural dyes from herbs, and natural materials, too.

## 6. The Andean Collection…

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/fashion/2013/05/6-the-andean-collection_250x370.png)

Looking for the perfect statement necklace to show off your style? Look no further. The Andean Collection is a jewelry brand that works with natural materials found in the Rainforests of South America, along with recycled materials. Think bullhorn, alpaca fibers and coconut shells… The pieces are dyed with herbs to create bold, bright and eye-catching bracelets, necklaces and earrings, and the hand-crafted finish means you’ll always get something unique. Perfect for those big occasions!

## 7. Study NY…

![Study NY…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_study-ny_300x375.jpg)

Tara St James, the designer who used to work for Covet, launched Study NY as a selection of city-friendly pieces that look great and are good for the environment. The pieces are made from organic cotton, linen, recycled materials and hand-dyed fabrics, and the company strives for a no-waste manufacturing process. And the clothes? I love this shirt dress, and there’s everything from jeans to high-waisted shorts and blouses. Fill your wardrobe, people!

## 8. Alabama Chanin…

![Alabama Chanin…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_alabama-chanin_300x511.jpg)

Alabama Chanin sells pieces that are individually handcrafted by artisans living in Florence, Alabama. They create everything from clothes to quilts, all with that unique blend of vintage and earthy, and the pieces are to die for. This wedding gown is just beautiful… This is eco friendly fashion that is perfect for anything, from everyday wear to big events.

It seems eco friendly fashion can only get bigger from here, with designers branching out to create sustainable collections using recycled or eco friendly materials- some of the ideas are just crazy! Have you got a favorite eco fashion brand? I’d love to hear about it!
