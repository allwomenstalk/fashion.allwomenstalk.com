# 7 Stylish Neon Accents ...

After falling out of favour a few seasons ago, the neon trend is enjoying a revival of sorts. This time it’s all about off-beat neon accents. Think splashes of neon on your shoes or giving a classic wardrobe staple a neon twist. Take inspiration from the following stylish neon accents.

## 1. Jimmy Choo Candy Acrylic Clutch

![Jimmy Choo Candy Acrylic Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/du/lw/sqgg5o8o54c92c751027d320292794_350x310.jpg)

**Price:** $550.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/e2e8mU)
This candy-coloured clutch makes for one stylish neon accent. This hard acrylic clutch features ridged details, asymmetric fold-over front flap, and comes with a long silver chain handle. Internal features include tonal leather and satin lining, and an internal pouch pocket. Style this clutch with black jeans, white t-shirt, and faded denim jacket for a casual look.

## 2. Splendid Vintage Whisper Cotton Tank

![Splendid Vintage Whisper Cotton Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/sd/gc9mc7vg54c92c75bf0c3434148632_233x350.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/LrXZsw)
Layer this cotton tank top underneath jackets and cardigans to add a pop of neon to your outfit. It has a scoop neck, racer back design, and a curved hem. This bright neon tank top would work well with a range of summer-inspired outfits. Try a fresh approach and dabble in a bit of colour blocking – an orange full skirt would make a great companion.

## 3. Bex Rox Golden Fluoro Friendship Bracelet

![Bex Rox Golden Fluoro Friendship Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/neon-bracelet_500x278.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [stylebop.com](https://www.stylebop.com/?utm_source=linkshare_us&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=je6NUbpObpQ&utm_content=1)
This discreet bangle is an ingenious way of incorporating the neon trend into an outfit. This gold-plated bracelet features a coil chain with interwoven multicoloured satin bands. The approach is subtle, yet still very on-trend. If you wear gold jewellery, this bracelet could easily work into your daily outfit.

## 4. Issy Salamon Neon Squiggle Necklace

![Issy Salamon Neon Squiggle Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/py/s0e65pm854c92c76acced240481763_350x272.jpg)

**Price:** $318.00 at [charmandchain.com](https://charmandchain.com/necklaces/)
This necklace is a playful creation. It is made from neon Lucite, glass beads, and rhinestones. The neon squiggles are by far the focal point of this creation. This necklace would work well worn with a cream shift dress and coloured pumps.

## 5. Forever21 Neon Yellow Nail Polish

![Forever21 Neon Yellow Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/r2/lmbntybu54c92c7786958527411063_287x329.jpg)

**Price:** $2.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Try using a coat of neon polish on your nails to add a bit of the neon trend to your look. This neon yellow nail polish from Forever21 is a cheap and cheerful way to work the look. This bright yellow shade may not suit all skin tones, so choose your colours wisely. Wear this on the weekend for a fun and frivolous look.

## 6. Ralph Lauren Black Label Cashmere Sweater

![Ralph Lauren Black Label Cashmere Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/kk/fps5xb4154c92c7877f3b176163025_261x350.jpg)

**Price:** $400.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/mXGsxB)
Ralph Lauren has reworked the classic cashmere sweater and given it a neon twist. This modern version of the cable knit sweater comes in an array of bright neon colours. This lime coloured sweater has a round neck, ribbed trims, and cable knit design. I think this sweater would look great paired with contrasting pink tailored shorts and leather sandals.

## 7. Miu Miu Fluorescent Patent Ballerina Flats

_ **Price:** $349.00 at mytheresa.com_
An easy way to add stylish neon accents to your outfit is with a new pair of shoes. Just take these Miu Miu flats, for example. These neon ballet flats have an elasticised mouth, round cap, and sash detailing on the toe. They’d work well with a subdued colour palette of navy blue and white.
