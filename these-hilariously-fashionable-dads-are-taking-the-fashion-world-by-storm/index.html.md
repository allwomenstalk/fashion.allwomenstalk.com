# These Hilariously Fashionable Dads Are Taking the Fashion World by Storm!

If you haven't been following [@fashiondads_](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser), what have you been doing with your life?! Inspired by the many, many fashion bloggers out there, this parody account is dedicated to "fashionable" dads everywhere. Both the pictures and the descriptions are HILARIOUS and of course, hashtags are EVERYTHING.

I wish my helmet was green but you can't always have everything you want in this industry! #OOTD #StayHumble #AllGreenErrythang #HolidayFashion"
