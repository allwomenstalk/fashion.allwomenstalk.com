# Say Yes to the Print on Print Trend!

Gone are the days when fashion rules stated that a printed piece of clothing can only be worn with something plain. Head to toe prints are taking the fashion world by storm and I could not be happier! Sure, it's a little intimidating at first but these tips will help you wear the print on print trend with confidence and style!
