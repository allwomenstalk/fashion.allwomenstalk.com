# 7 Cute and Comfy Shoes to Check out ...

I’m all about having plenty of cute shoes, not to mention tops, jeans and of course, accessories to match! The thing is, I also want my shoes to be comfortable. You don’t have to sacrifice style for comfort when it comes to choosing cute shoes. Check out these amazingly cute shoes that are also comfy! Now you can still look cute without having to deal with nasty blisters or other less-than-pretty alternatives.

## 1. Black and Canvas Toms

![Black and Canvas Toms](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/1_black-and-canvas-toms1_600x426.jpg)

Toms are one of my favorite brands of cute shoes that give you comfort along with classic style. These Toms are some of my favorites and they are so versatile. You could wear them in winter or summer, spring or fall, and they could be worn with so many things. These would even make cute work shoes paired with the right skirt or pair of pants. I’m personally loving the simplicity behind these cute shoes, what about you?

## 2. Tiffany Blue Vans

![Tiffany Blue Vans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/2_tiffany-blue-vans1_500x371.jpg)

If you’re a fan of Vans shoes, you’re going to love these super chic, yet cute Tiffany blue Vans! If you’re not a fan of Vans, I bet these might just convert you! These are fresh and so versatile. They could be paired with a casual summer dress or skirt to kick things up a notch, or paired with some cute little shorts to tone down your look a bit. I think a classic white shirt would really set these off too.

## 3. Clarks Salon Spirit

![Clarks Salon Spirit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/3_clarks-salon-spirit_480x192.jpg)

If you’re a fan of flip flops, this style of Clarks is definitely a pair you want to check out. They are incredibly simple, yet stylish too, and are perfect for summer, spring and fall. Try them out anytime you want a simple, yet cute and of course, comfy flip flop. I can just envision them with a laid back pair of denim jeans and a classic white blouse for a totally simple, yet stylish look. You could also pair these with a sundress for a casual outing out with friends.

## 4. Nine West Faycie

![Nine West Faycie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/4_nine-west-faycie_456x200.jpg)

Who doesn’t love a shoe by Nine West, right? If you’re looking for a really cute ballet flat to wear to work, church, or even to an outdoor wedding, try these shoes on for size! While I don’t think they would have enough support for walking around a city all day, they are great for wearing to work instead of heels.

## 5. Blowfish Glo

![Blowfish Glo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/5_blowfish-glo_480x192.jpg)

I really like to add some versatility to my shoe collection and these shoes have it! They make a simple, yet stylish statement that can be worn with shorts or jeans. I also love the inside color, though it doesn’t show. These shoes have a high comfort rating and would be excellent if you’re new to wearing flats.

## 6. Lucky Brand Emmie

![Lucky Brand Emmie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/6_lucky-brand-emmie_464x215.jpg)

I rarely tire of ballet flats and they make up a majority of my comfy shoe selection. I love wearing ballet flats to work, church, or while running errands, yet I am always looking for something a little more up to date. I also want something with good support, which makes these flats by Lucky Brand so awesome. These are incredibly feminine, yet also flexible, so they bend with your feet and offer maximum comfort.

## 7. Clarks Amelia Drift

![Clarks Amelia Drift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/7_clarks-amelia-drift_416x312.jpg)

Clarks are well known for their high-comfort rating, but some styles are less than cute, in my opinion, with a few exceptions. These cute little wedges are a fabulous exception, however, and I’m totally in love with them! These would look so cute with some skinny jeans, capris, a skirt, or even shorts! I love their versatile color and the fact that they give me a little boost in the height department, which I always appreciate!

Wearing a comfy shoe is important for preventing foot injuries. I love putting on super high heels like any girly-girl out there, but my feet sure do repay me later! If you want something cute and comfy, give these shoes a try. Do you have any brands of comfy, cute shoes to share with me?
