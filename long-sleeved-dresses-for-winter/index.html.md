# 8 Long Sleeved Dresses for Winter ...

Long Sleeved Dresses are a chic choice for winter. The longer arm length provides extra warmth, and can easily be incorporated into a range of dress designs. When working long sleeved dresses for winter, ideally keep the sleeve skin-tight so it can easily be layered under cardigans and jackets. Dresses made from jerseys and heavy wools are also a smart choice for winter dressing. Take a look at a range of long sleeved dresses below.
