# 7 Ways to Wear a Statement Necklace ...

If you’re looking to add a major dose of flair to your fall ensembles, check out these fabulous ways to wear statement necklaces. The secret’s out: you don’t need a whole new wardrobe to look like a fashionista. With these 7 foolproof tips, you’ll be getting the most bling for your buck –no popped collars in sight!

## 1. Over a Little Black Dress

![Over a Little Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/156_600x900.jpg)

One of the first –and easiest -ways to wear a statement necklace is to throw it on over your favorite little black dress. You’ll be the star of your next soirée when you strut out in a black mini and chunky neon collar. Who knows? You might even catch your crush’s eye –and his number!

## 2. With Your Classic White Button down

![With Your Classic White Button down](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/225_612x943.jpg)

If your standard office garb needs a little work, pair your white button down with a glistening gold statement necklace. Let it peek out under the collar of your shirt for a look that’s daring yet meeting-appropriate.

## 3. Invest in Chambray

![Invest in Chambray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/327_1064x1600.jpg)

This season, a chambray shirt and multi-hued necklace is a fashion-forward dream team. Ladies, if you don’t have chambray in your closet, now’s the time to stock up. Just don’t act surprised when you get mistaken for a runway model.

## 4. Dress It up

![Dress It up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/427_500x750.jpg)

My favorite way to wear a statement necklace is to pair it with sleek satin pumps, a cool clutch, and sheer tights. My Peter Pan necklace always adds a bit of magic to my go-to party pieces. You’ll thank me when the compliments come in!

## 5. Dress It down

![Dress It down](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/526_600x858.jpg)

If you’re a T-shirt and jeans kind of gal, I’m about to deliver some great style news! Go ahead and don your skinny jeans and neutral fitted tee –just top your outfit off with a standout necklace. And don’t forget to swap out your Converse for cute flats or wedges.

## 6. Let It Shine

![Let It Shine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/627_589x880.jpg)

Another way to wear a statement necklace is with a subdued, non-patterned frock and little to no jewelry. Your statement necklace should actually have the chance to make a statement, right? So, let your necklace do the talking by keeping the rest of your look easy, breezy.

## 7. Show Your Lips Some Love

![Show Your Lips Some Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/726_1024x683.jpg)

One final way to get the most out of your statement necklace is to pretty up your pucker. My go-to color is an electric neon pink. Not only will a bright lipstick shade look oh-so-sexy, but it’ll also make your necklace a few watts brighter. Yum!

Now that you know my top ways to wear a statement necklace, which tips will you try? Do you have any of your own to add?
