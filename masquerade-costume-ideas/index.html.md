# Cleopatra Costume and Other Stunning Masquerade Ideas ...

We love masquerade parties because they're so lively, energetic, and fun. Guests have the chance to dress in opulent costumes, wear extravagant masks, and be whoever they want to be for one night. Everyone has a favorite character, from Cleopatra to any style inspired by Audrey Hepburn; while kids want to be superheroes and save the world, adults are more attracted to mature characters from books and movies. Get your inspiration for a masquerade costume from the following innovative ideas.

## 1. Cleopatra Costume

![Cleopatra Costume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/ko/fsdgv97q_600x755.jpg)

The Cleopatra masquerade costume is classy, stylish, and interesting. Although you're not wearing any type of mask, the make-up is so strong that it almost looks like a disguise. Exclusively meant for women, the Cleopatra costume is made of a long golden dress, Egyptian wig, golden collar, and gold belt. The wig must be black and have a straight fringe. As for the make-up, your eyes should be accentuated with black and golden nuances. Keep your lips nude though so your look won't be overpowering.

## 2. Phantom of the Opera

![Phantom of the Opera](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qt/mj/drmcv1az_519x766.jpg)

A Phantom of the Opera costume is suitable for men only. The key piece is the masquerade mask, which has to be white and cover half of the face. To get the full effect, men should also wear a black cape. Underneath, the attire is formal, which means men should wear black trousers with a white shirt and a vest.

## 3. Cat Woman

![Cat Woman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/bg/nshu0hg4_600x906.jpg)

A Cat Woman costume is appropriate for women with beautiful bodies. The outfit is extremely tight because it is made of latex or leather. The mask only covers the eyes, and thus the wearer will be able to talk freely. The costume comes in black, but if you want to set yourself apart, add bloody-red lipstick on the lips.

## 4. Marie Antoinette

![Marie Antoinette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/0n/lbgxnhh8_600x900.jpg)

Marie Antoinette was the Queen of France in the 18th century. Therefore, your costume should look vintage. You can always borrow your grandma's wedding dress if you don't want to spend money on a new costume. The mask is really discreet (made of black lace) and the hair must be curly and big. You can always add feather clips in your hair just to make your outfit even more striking.

## 5. Black/White Swan

![Black/White Swan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/nx/gdlgp8md_463x499.jpg)

At some point in life, every little girl wants to be a ballerina. The Black/White Swan masquerade costume can make your dream come true. Invest in a tutu skirt and a tight t-shirt, and opt for white or black colors. Your hair should be bound in a bun and the masquerade mask has to be laser cut. The beautifully crafted design will emphasize your eye make-up and stunning feminine features.

## 6. The Audrey Hepburn Costume

![The Audrey Hepburn Costume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l0/gk/sed8bf1j_600x761.jpg)

Audrey Hepburn is best known for her classic little black dress, pearl necklace and black sunglasses. If you're going to a classy, elegant masquerade event, the Audrey Hepburn costume is an excellent choice. It's not flashy but rather chic and stylish, perfect for a black-tie event where a costume is mandatory. Although you're not wearing an actual masquerade mask, those sunglasses will do the trick.

## 7. Batman/Batgirl Costume

![Batman/Batgirl Costume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/f4/y5wtw99a_580x871.jpg)

Suitable for men and women of all ages, the Batman/Batgirl costume is excellent for a masquerade party. The superhero outfit is fun, innovative and original. If you want to make it even more interesting, quit the black color and go for red, green, or yellow instead. Don't forget the mask, it's the most important piece of your entire costume!

How much do you love masquerade parties? There are tens of original ideas you can get inspired by when it comes to costume ideas. Be whoever you want to be for one night, and dazzle the audience with a original, unique masquerade costume that everyone will remember.
