# What to Wear in 2019 According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Here’s the thing about fashion and style, if you don’t change and adapt with the times, you end up getting left behind! There are always a bunch of new trends that come along with the start of a new year and fashion season, so it’s up to use to make sure that we keep up! If you want to stay on trend this year, here is **what to wear in 2019 according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

![Clothing, Sportswear, Active pants, Waist, Leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m8/h3/gltnzbu25c64771892131510465364_600x750.jpg)

You are always the one ahead of the curve and leading the pack, so only the strongest trends are the ones that you adopt. Athleisure is going to continue to be big in 2019, so stock up on your clean white sneakers!

## 2. Taurus

![Clothing, Shoulder, Leg, Eyewear, Footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/xa/r94rgmne5c64773703d65310508087_600x750.jpg)

Both comfort and style are equally important to you, so luscious fabrics like velvet and cashmere should be a big part of your wardrobe this year.

## 3. Gemini

![Beauty, Skin, Fashion model, Bangle, Bracelet,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fu/mx/e4twqkes5c648aba51543464248044_600x750.jpg)

You like everything you wear to be a conversation starter, so make sure that your accessories are on point this year: must-haves are statement heels, tinted sunglasses, and chunky jewellery!

## 4. Cancer

![Clothing, Orange, Red, Street fashion, Peach,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/x3/q6zn36hw5c6477d486dfe603640754_600x750.jpg)

Consistency is your middle name, so your main style inspiration is going to based on the same tried and tested shapes that you love, but introducing new colours and patterns.

## 5. Leo

![Clothing, Dress, Shoulder, Cocktail dress, Joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/hj/odaocoj85c6477e187a67508804145_600x750.jpg)

Metallics, metallic, metallic! That is your mantra for 2019. All of those metallic tones and textures are going to be the thing that helps your vibrant personality shine even brighter in 2019.

## 6. Virgo

![White, Clothing, Fashion, Hat, Footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/ly/zaayre9w5c6478104e9a0167012431_600x750.jpg)

It’s time to add some more interesting texture to your outfits, and one of the best ways to achieve that is through experimenting with embroidery.

## 7. Libra

![White, Clothing, Photograph, Street fashion, Fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/dt/fnzjtygo5c648b1bda4e6938409583_600x600.jpg)

Monochrome is going to be big for you this year. It should be all about streamlining your look and singling out colour schemes that accentuate your natural beauty.

## 8. Scorpio

![Photograph, Clothing, Street fashion, Footwear, Tights,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/fu/qjabvuxj5c64783bd7244590663115_600x750.jpg)

For you, it is always a case of black is the new black! The dark colour suits you perfectly, and if it isn’t broke, then there is no need to fix it!

## 9. Sagittarius

![Sitting, Yellow, Sari, Cool, Formal wear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/u4/lmaqyp465c64789a4bdab095095282_600x750.jpg)

Keep doing what you were doing in 2018, with one key rule change: before you leave the house, commit to taking one thing off in order to curb your ‘over the top’ habit!

## 10. Capricorn

![Clothing, Street fashion, Black, Fashion, Outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/rv/t4eki6sn5c6478b80e585389576426_600x750.jpg)

Wide-legged trousers are something that you need to wear this year. You have been stuck in the same skinny jean phase for so long that it is time to enjoy the complete opposite.

## 11. Aquarius

![Clothing, Shoulder, Pink, Sweater, Street fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/bi/a5llepho5c648a09c5e9b147395121_600x750.jpg)

Fluid silhouettes and lots of flowing layers are what you should be focusing in this year. These will help to emphasise the calmer, more tranquil side to your personality.

## 12. Pisces

Vivid colours and problem patterns should be your signature look in 2019! For a trailblazing Pisces it is definitely a case of going big or going home, so make sure that you dress as vibrantly as possible!
