# 8 Sheer and Stylish Garments ...

**Sheer and Stylish Garments** are extraordinarily trendy this season, and with good reason. Anything light and sheer is bound to be both fashionable and comfortable in this kind of heat. When you wear **sheer and stylish garments** for the summer months, you should opt for dark navy and black for an edgy take on the trend, or choose light pastel colours for something a little more whimsical. Sheer garments have the potential to be see-through, so layer a slip or camisole underneath for modesty. Below are just a few **sheer and stylish garments** that you may want to check out.
