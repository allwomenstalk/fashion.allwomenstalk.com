# 7 Chic and Stylish Maxi Skirts ...

Chic maxi skirts are the way to go this upcoming season. A chic maxi skirt is perfect for working a layered look come the cooler months and can easily be styled up or down. Either choose one in a seasonal colour, on-trend pattern, or go for one in a stylish slim cut. Take inspiration from the following chic maxi skirts and update your new season wardrobe.

## 1. Black Maxi Skirt

![Black Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_black-maxi-skirt_600x815.jpg)

**Price:** $29.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
When it comes to versatility and style, you can’t go past chic maxi skirts in basic black. This one comes in a stylish slim cut and features a split to one side. Split skirts are having a bit of a moment right now and this number is an affordable way to work the trend. You could easily dress this skirt up or down, depending on the occasion.

## 2. Urban Romance Skirt

![Urban Romance Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_urban-romance-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Rich, burgundy colours will be a popular choice for the upcoming fall/winter season. Wear the look right now with this lush crinkled skirt. Work a glam angle and wear it with a black top, gold heels, and gold statement jewels.

## 3. Khaki Pleated Maxi Skirt

![Khaki Pleated Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_khaki-pleated-maxi-skirt_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Yz5H82)
Another on-trend colour for the season is khaki. It’s perfect for working a military inspired look. In the form of chic maxi skirts, however, khaki comes across as much more feminine. Dress this skirt up with a plain tank top, cream biker jacket, and low slung bag for a stylish off-duty look.

## 4. Rockin’ and a-reelin’ Skirt

![Rockin’ and a-reelin’ Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_rockin-and-a-reelin-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Stripes never go out of favour, and this skirt is a sleek way to wear this pattern. It features grey and white horizontal stripes and has a slim, column-like design. Work a nautical inspired look by teaming this skirt with a red shirt, tied at the waist, and oversized sunglasses.

## 5. Sheer Abstract Maxi Skirt

_ **Price:** $13.50 at forever21.com_
Add a little bit of intrigue to your look with chic maxi skirts in eye-catching patterns. This sheer skirt features an abstract print that would look great teamed with an array of basics. Wear this skirt with a v-neck t-shirt, cropped denim jacket, and tan shoes for a cool everyday look.

## 6. Pleating Moments Skirt

![Pleating Moments Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/6_pleating-moments-skirt_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $82.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This chic maxi skirt will inject some subtle colour into your look. It has a feminine pleated design and features an all-over mottled pattern. Wear it during the day with a tucked in white shirt, strappy flat sandals, and floppy felt hat.

## 7. Sheer Hem Maxi Skirt

_ **Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/hyc0CT)
The sheer trend is still going strong and this skirt is a stylish option to try out. It has a sheer hem and is partially lined. It also has a drawstring waist that gives it a casual feel. Work an athletic inspired look by pairing this skirt with slouchy tops and sporty looking accessories.

Whether you’re dressing for the day or a night out on the town, these are just some chic maxi skirts for you to consider. A chic maxi skirt can be a versatile addition to any wardrobe. Which of these styles do you like best?
