# 7 Pieces of Clothing That Will Last You Year in and Year out ...

There are some pieces of clothing that will last you year in and year out. They’re the reliable wardrobe workhorses that you’ll find yourself reaching for no matter what current trends dictate. These pieces of clothing can include the perfect fitted jeans, a classic collared shirt or the basic black dress, to name a few. When you think about it, they’re the kind of items that no wardrobe should be without. Check out these pieces of clothing that will last.

## 1. Basic Black Dress

![Basic Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01055517_8561_600x756.jpg)

When it comes to clothing that will last, you can’t beat a basic black dress. It’s versatile and can be dressed up or down to suit a range of occasions. It’s perfect for throwing on when you seem to have nothing else to wear. To make sure it will stand the test of time, choose one in a simple design that suits your body shape and personal style. For example, if wrap dresses are your thing then by all means opt for a sleek black wrap dress. If you prefer shorter hemlines then maybe a short shift dress is more suited to your style. Add heels and jewellery at night or dress it down with casual separates for the day.

## 2. Denim Jeans

![Denim Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01055819_5074_503x640.jpg)

Jeans are a wardrobe staple that just seem to stick around no matter what. The cut may vary every now and then but a good pair of jeans will last you year in and year out. Darker washes are great for dressier outfits while light and distressed washes are great for more casual looks. It might take you a while to find the perfect pair of jeans for you but once you’ve found them it’s like finding the Holy Grail.

## 3. White Collared Shirt

![White Collared Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01055925_7880_569x790.jpg)

The white shirt is another great wardrobe staple. A white shirt is a great basic that can be styled in a range of different ways. Wear it buttoned up for a prim and proper look or dress it down in a slouchy style. The key to choosing a great white shirt lies in the cut and fabric. Try and stick to shirts made from natural fibres and look for flattering designs.

## 4. Black Blazer

![Black Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01060035_3060_600x900.jpg)

The black blazer is a classic. Wear it to work over shift dresses or use it to dress up a casual outfit. My tip would be to wear your black blazer with low key pieces like jeans and t-shirts for an effortlessly chic every day look.

## 5. Striped Top

![Striped Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01060202_8646_600x902.jpg)

Made popular by Coco Chanel, the striped top is not only a classic wardrobe piece but it’s great for breaking up a basic outfit. It’s the perfect way to add some subtle prints to your look. Wear it with jeans and a leather jacket for a typical model-off-duty inspired outfit or team it with cropped pants and ballet flats for a classic Audrey Hepburn inspired look.

## 6. Black Skirt

![Black Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01060755_3398_600x900.jpg)

A good black skirt is another essential wardrobe item. Choose one in a flattering design that suits your figure. Generally, styles that hit just above the knee will be the most versatile. Wear them with bare legs and slinky tops in summer or with tights and plenty of layers during winter.

## 7. Classic Trench Coat

![Classic Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/01060714_2812_600x1067.jpg)

When it comes to outerwear, the classic trench coat is a staple. It’s great for wearing in between seasons and you can throw it on over almost any kind of outfit. You don’t have to splash out on an iconic Burberry trench, either, with plenty of more affordable versions available at high street stores.

These are just some pieces of clothing that are sure to stand the test of time. They’re the building blocks of any timeless wardrobe. Are there any other must-have timeless pieces of clothing you’d like to add to the list?
