# 7 Pretty Pastel Coats to Wear in Winter ...

Pastel coats are one of winter’s top trends. Pastels aren’t just for spring and summer anymore, with pastel coats and knits proving to be the perfect remedy for dreary winter weather. Work a full ladylike look in subtle pastel shades or wear a pastel coat to offset a grungy outfit. If you’re a fan of sorbet shades this season, then take a look at these pretty pastel coats.

## 1. Light Blue Boyfriend Coat

![Light Blue Boyfriend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201032_4805_599x796.jpg)

There seems to be quite a number of pastel coats to choose from this season, and a pale blue coat is just one on-trend option. Wear it with black skinny jeans and tan lace-up shoes for a smart everyday outfit. This particular coat comes in a slightly oversized, ‘boyfriend’ design and costs $99 from Dorothy Perkins. You can also check out Oasis and Topshop for more pastel blue coats.

## 2. Retro Mint Coat

![Retro Mint Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201030_6724_600x900.jpg)

Look out for coats with a vintage feel this season. A retro-inspired coat in a soft pastel shade is perfect for topping off a ladylike outfit. This coat comes in a fresh mint colour and has a retro boxy fit, cropped sleeves, and flared hem. Stick to the retro theme and wear it buttoned up over a mini dress, tights, and T-bar shoes. Beehive hair and winged eyeliner are optional! This coat is a really expensive number by RED Valentino but you can find a more affordable range of retro-inspired coats at Modcloth.

## 3. Pastel Pink Coat

![Pastel Pink Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201032_4565_599x788.jpg)

It seems like the fashion world can’t get enough of pastel pink right now. A pastel [pink coat](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-wear-pink-coats-for-fall/) is a must-have item of clothing for winter. Wear it with jeans and a t-shirt for a cool and casual look or try teaming it with leather-look pants and ankle boots for something edgier. This wool mix boyfriend-style coat costs $150 from Miss Selfridge and you can also check out Topshop for similar pastel pink coats this season.

## 4. Lemon Yellow Coat

![Lemon Yellow Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201030_4719_391x477.jpg)

Inject a bit of sunshine into your day with a lemon coloured coat. This Stella McCartney coat comes in an oversized design with a collarless silhouette. It has a quite padded appearance but you could balance out the shape by teaming it with slim-leg trousers and ballet flats. For more budget-friendly yellow coats, try looking at Topshop and ASOS.

## 5. Pastel Purple Coat

![Pastel Purple Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201032_4626_599x874.jpg)

Stand out from the crowd in a pastel purple coat. This Topshop coat has a boxy cocoon-like design and would look great worn with check pants and patent loafers. Since it is made from 100% wool, this coat does come with a whopping $390 price tag. You could also work this pastel shade into your outfit in the form of a knit sweater or cardigan.

## 6. Pale Blue Trench Coat

![Pale Blue Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201029_2580_349x495.jpg)

The trench coat is a timeless wardrobe piece. Here it’s been given an on-trend update in a pale blue colour. A pastel trench coat would be a great alternative to your usual beige and khaki numbers. This particular coat is from Modcloth and will set you back around $140. Wear it buttoned up to accentuate the ladylike flared skirt or open over skinny jeans and ankle boots for a cool off-duty look.

## 7. Pink Fit and Flare Coat

![Pink Fit and Flare Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/22201032_3607_599x802.jpg)

Oversized coats may be popular this season but they’re not always flattering for all. If you prefer your coats with a bit more structure and shape then keep an eye out for coats with fitted designs. This pastel pink coat from Dorothy Perkins has a nipped waist and flared hem. It’s a perfectly ladylike coat and would look great worn over dresses and tights in winter.

These are just a few coats that embrace the trend for all things pastel. What do you think of the pastel coat trend? How would you style these pastel coats for winter?
