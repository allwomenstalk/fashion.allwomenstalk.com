# How to Develop Your Own Sense of Style for Girls Who Want to Stand out ...

Anyone who wants to stand out from the crowd should love the idea of having their own sense of style, right? Keep reading for everything you need to know about developing your own and anything you should consider along the way! 😃 ✌️ 👠 👛

## 1. Have a Signature Look

One of the best things about having your own sense of style is that you can incorporate a signature look if you so choose! This can be anything you love to have in your outfits and wear a lot such as a certain style of sunglasses or silver chunky necklaces! Get creative and think of a signature look that you've not seen many people (if any) have so that yours will be totally unique and personal to you!

## 2. Personalise Your Pieces

Personalising your pieces might not be for everyone - but if this is something you're interested in you should definitely consider it when developing your own sense of style! So, consider any pieces that you think are suited to being customised/personalised such as your favourite handbag, a purse, pyjamas, shoes or even your suitcase if you travel a lot! The actual personalisation is completely up to you, but one idea could be to have your initials sewn into these pieces, which many people love to do!

## 3. Consider Your Ideal Style

This might sound super obvious if you want to create your own style, but in order to achieve this you first need to think about what kind of style you actually want to have! Your own sense of style is a form of [self expression](https://teen.allwomenstalk.com/forms-of-self-expression-to-try-in-high-school/), so what do you want your style to say about you? Do you want it to say that you're daring and love to try bold pieces, never one to shy away from trying new trends? Or do you want it to say that you're a more of a girly-girl who adores pastel pinks, frills and lace hems? These a just a couple of examples to get you thinking!

## 4. Make a Fashion Board

You might want to consider making your own fashion board and fill it with fashion ideas and inspiration! Being able to look at different styles in photos at a glance can help you to figure out what you want your own personal style to look like while keeping you super inspired as you think about this!

## 5. Prepare for All Seasons

Remember when you start to develop your own sense of style it needs to be fairly flexible so that you can dress according to your style no matter the temperature, weather, season, month or any other time of the year! It won't be any good to only plan for the hotter months if you live somewhere which has a pretty cold winter, right? Consider pieces for all four seasons which reflect your own sense of style!

## 6. Consider Your Budget

Budget is something that's going to be different for everyone, but you're the best person for knowing your own budget so this is definitely something to consider when developing your own sense of style. Think carefully about how much of your budget you generally want to put towards creating and maintaining your sense of style and try your best to stay within this limit or guideline when it comes to actually buying your pieces to create your style!

## 7. Take Your Time Experimenting

One of the most important things to remember is that developing your own sense of style probably won't happen overnight! If you think about it, it's kind of like a process which you have to go through in order to come out with a customised wardrobe suited to your own style, so it's only natural that it will take time to do. It also takes a lot of thought on your behalf so be patient and try to enjoy the process instead of rushing it!
