# Check out Anthropologies Girliest Accessories ...

Online shopping really is easier once I found accessories from Anthropologie. I have been frustrated for quite some time now because a couple of the products that I need to buy can’t be found in this part of the world. Not that I will die because I don’t have them but … a pair of earrings or even a bag can make a huge difference to your fashion statement; and I consider those as top essentials. I have always loved accessories so when I find really great ones, I make sure that I take care of them so I can make them last longer. Here are seven of my recent fabulous finds among the list of accessories from Anthropologie:

## 1. Pampas Backpack

![Pampas Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/g5/j49mgrg4_410x615.jpg)

I am crazy about backpacks so this product is my most favorite among all accessories from Anthropologie. It’s amazing how the designers were able to conveniently and artfully combine modern functionality with its ethnic design. This is just a visual showcase of color and style and one that backpack lovers should have.

## 2. Jeweled Fresco Posts

![Jeweled Fresco Posts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tq/di/n3qkvbaq_410x615.jpg)

This adorable pair of earrings is a darling mix of colors that can be worn on that exciting but nerve-wracking first date. It’s a very feminine pair that is made of metal, glass and acrylic. The angel is in the details in this product. I especially love the four flowers that bring more attention to the rose-colored center.

## 3. Dual-Tone Convertible Backpack

![Dual-Tone Convertible Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/u3/ahcg6b1g_410x615.jpg)

Best for days when you are running errands in the morning and attending meetings and briefings during lunch and dinner. This is going to be my go-to-bag in all kinds of weather and seasons. It is true when they say that this leather bag is “timeless and endlessly functional”.

## 4. Popolo Bow Beret

![Popolo Bow Beret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wk/h8/wqmus4ou_410x615.jpg)

An adorable head accessory, this piece is a great addition to my growing family of berets with bows. I found one in an underground mall in Guangzhou (China). I fell so in love with it that it became my best friend the entire time I was in a winter trip around France and Germany early this year. With the arrival of this beret, looks like I need to travel some place cold again.

## 5. Valdivia Infinity Scarf

![Valdivia Infinity Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d3/ks/n6h067mp_410x615.jpg)

The perfect scarf for plain tops. I always look for colorful neck scarfs because they pump up my energy especially when I am facing a challenging day. This one is sure to be my ally during those difficult days. I admit that when the going gets tough, I dress up. It makes my day just great!

## 6. Varanasi Sunglasses

![Varanasi Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/m8/za83c608_410x615.jpg)

Fall is fast approaching but I am visiting the Philippines (in October), where there is perpetual summer, so this pair of sunglasses will come in handy. I stopped buying sunglasses with plain colors recently because I feel that the more colorful ones add more spunk to an office outfit that normally falls within the black, white and gray category.

## 7. Arusha Beaded Turban

![Arusha Beaded Turban](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/nq/ph5vcjqy_410x615.jpg)

This is my hair accessory of the month! It’s a great match to those pants-and-shirt days as it adds drama to the look because of its retro feel and beaded design. It tames stubborn hair strands and gives that laidback and (very) pretty vibe.

What are your favorite accessories these days? Where did you get them? Do you shop at Anthropologie?
