# 7 Great Graduation Dresses to Get Your Diploma in ...

Graduation is a special time to celebrate ending your time as a student, and you need some great graduation dresses to help you do it in style. The perfect graduation dress shows off your fashion sense and style, while making you feel like a million bucks. You want to feel confident as you walk across the stage and enter the real world, and here are some great graduation dresses to help you get there.
.

## 1. Floral & Flare

![Floral & Flare](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/bn/ciyw5ofy_495x755.jpg)

The best graduation dresses are the ones that make you feel celebratory and confident. With this Lilly Pulitzer Ollie Fit & Flare Dress, you are sure to feel like a million bucks as you walk across that stage. Its bold and fun print is perfect for a spring graduation, while the fit and flare style is flattering on all body types.

## 2. White Pencil Dress

![White Pencil Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/ts/p4yhb1ak_290x370.jpg)

The best thing about a white graduation dress is that it will never clash with your cap and gown, and you can bring in color with your accessories. This white dress from ASOS is perfect if you want to be more conservative, but also has the flare at the bottom to spice it up a bit. Choose heels that match the color of your gown and add in a statement necklace to bring this outfit full circle.

## 3. The LBD

![The LBD](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/jo/jpb7e8gn_330x484.jpg)

Every event is a good excuse for a little black dress. This dress from Forever 21 is simple enough to be worn at graduation, yet the belted waist helps you keep your form under a massive gown. If you want to go simple, keep accessories to a minimum. Or you can go glam and pair it with bright heels and a bold metallic necklace.

## 4. Bold Prints

![Bold Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wu/pr/lv14adss_290x370.jpg)

Graduation is your day of celebration, so stand out in this ASOS printed skater dress. It will go great with any color cap and gown, and will be a surefire way to stand out in graduation pictures with your friends. Not to mention, skater dresses flatter all types of figures.

## 5. Pastel Overlay

![Pastel Overlay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gw/mz/cg2s99wa_290x370.jpg)

If you want to embrace your spring wardrobe for graduation, this pastel overlay dress from ASOS is one of the best graduation dresses to get your diploma in. It looks great on a variety of skin tones and can easily be dressed up with a statement necklace. Throw on a pair of wedges and go take some beautiful graduation pictures around campus.

## 6. Glam BodyCon

![Glam BodyCon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vd/iq/lqjdg2by_330x484.jpg)

If you want to go glam for graduation, look no further than this bodycon dress from Forever 21. It is bold but doesn’t show too much skin, so it is still commencement appropriate. Add in some over the top accessories and you are ready to rock that graduation stage.

## 7. Off White Elegance

![Off White Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/sf/hjhor7r3_290x370.jpg)

This off white dress from ASOS is perfect if you want a vintage-esque look for graduation. This dress would look great against a dark colored cap and gown, and just as good by itself for pictures. It would look perfect with black heels and a vintage black necklace.
