# 7 Ways to Wear Red Denim ...

**Red Denim** is all the rage right now. A pair of **red denim** jeans or a **red denim** pencil skirt is your key to recreating this season’s must-have look. If tomato **red denim** is too brash for your tastes, you can always opt for a more subdued burgundy colour. The following are just a few ideas for how to style your **red denim** pieces this season.

## 1. Red Denim and a Black Blazer

Team your **red denim** jeans with a simple white t-shirt and black blazer for a casually cool look. Keep it casual with some sandals or ballet flats and a slouchy messenger bag, or dress it up a bit with heels and a sleek clutch.

## 2. Red Denim and a White Button-down Shirt

Tone down the harshness of some **red denim** pieces by teaming them with neutral clothes. A white button down shirt is a must have in any wardrobe and they look great when worn with **red denim** jeans or skirts. Whether you choose a silky blouse or crisp collared shirt, both are on-trend choices.

## 3. Red Denim and a Leather Jacket

Work a rock chic look and team your **red denim** jeans with a black leather jacket. Wear a band t-shirt for extra rock star vibes, or keep it simple with a slouchy tank top. Grungy jewellery and ankle boots complete the rock-inspired look.

## 4. Red Denim and a Striped Shirt

Keep it classic and team your **red denim** pieces with a striped t-shirt. Navy and white stripes are timeless and look great when teamed with black ballet flats. This is an effortlessly stylish way to wear your **red denim**.

## 5. Red Denim and Blue Denim

While double denim is questionable, if the denims are two different colours then you can likely get away with this fashion ‘crime’. Wear your **red denim** with a faded denim shirt for a cool down-town look. Add some leather sandals and a floppy fedora for that extra chic touch.

## 6. Red Denim and Leopard Print

**Red denim** is a great backdrop for leopard print pieces, Add touches of leopard print in the form of accessories such as belts, scarves, and bags. Get even more daring by teaming your **red denim** pieces with leopard print shoes.

## 7. Red Denim with Brights

Colour blocking is still a popular trend, and **red denim** jeans are an excellent way to get in on the trend. Red and pink is a trending colour combination, so team your **red denim** pieces with a fuchsia button-down shirt or t-shirt for a stylish look. Complete this look with nude or neutral accessories.

There are a range of ways to style **red denim** this season, and hopefully these have given you a few ideas if you were to take up the trend. **Red denim** is a refreshing alternative to classic blue denim and a look that is going to enjoy much more popularity in the future. How would you wear **red denim** this season?
