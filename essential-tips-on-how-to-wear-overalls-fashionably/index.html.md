# 7 Modern Ways to Style Overalls Fashionably as an Adult

How to wear overalls fashionably might seem like an oxymoron to some, but this versatile piece has come a long way from the playground. While overalls can be divisive—with some embracing the nostalgia and others feeling they should stay in kindergarten—modern styling has transformed them into a chic wardrobe staple. Whether you're looking for a casual weekend vibe or something more sophisticated, there are plenty of ways to make the humble overall work for your adult years. Here are seven essential tips to help you style your overalls with confidence and flair.

## 1. Get the Right Fit

![Get the Right Fit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_get-the-right-fit_553x822.jpg)

To work a high-fashion look with longer length overalls, opt for a slim cut rather than baggier styles. This creates a streamlined silhouette and prevents you from looking swallowed up by denim from head to toe. For shorter overalls, you want a pair that allows room to move but remains in proportion to your body. If you're looking for more denim inspiration, check out our guide on [fashionable ways to wear denim shorts](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/fashionable-ways-to-wear-denim-shorts/).

## 2. Layer like a Pro

![Layer like a Pro](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_layer-like-a-pro_333x500.jpg)

When it comes to styling overalls, layering is your secret weapon. Instead of a basic t-shirt, pop on a crisp silk blouse or a button-down shirt for a more sophisticated feel. Adding a tailored jacket or a structured blazer over the top is another excellent way to give your look a sense of intentionality and polish.

## 3. Add Adult Accessories

![Add Adult Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_add-adult-accessories_527x790.jpg)

Give your overalls a grown-up makeover by teaming them with elevated accessories. Think along the lines of a luxe faux fur coat, a sleek leather jacket, or bold statement shoes. Embellished jewelry and a high-end handbag can also instantly transform the look from casual to runway-ready. You can find beautiful designer options at [Net-a-Porter](https://www.net-a-porter.com) or [Farfetch](https://www.farfetch.com).

## 4. Ditch the Denim

![Ditch the Denim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_ditch-the-denim_428x640.jpg)

Another way to make overalls look more fashion-forward is to choose fabrics other than denim. Leather, silk, or even linen overalls offer a fresh take on the trend and are often easier to dress up for evening events. These materials provide a different texture and weight that can feel more aligned with a modern, minimalist aesthetic.

## 5. Play with Proportions

![Play with Proportions](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_play-with-proportions_333x500.jpg)

Experimenting with proportions can lead to some truly unique outfits. Try pairing oversized overalls with a fitted crop top or a slim-fitting turtleneck. Conversely, you can wear more tailored overalls with a chunky knit sweater for a cozy yet stylish autumn look. Balancing the volume of your pieces is key to maintaining a flattering shape. For more tips on balancing outfits, read about [ways to look stylish every day](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-look-stylish-every-day/).

## 6. Choose Rich Shades

![Choose Rich Shades](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_choose-rich-shades_411x617.jpg)

While classic blue denim is always a safe bet, exploring overalls in rich, deep shades can elevate your style. Deep burgundy, forest green, or even a classic black can make your overalls look more expensive and intentional. These colors also tend to transition better from day to night compared to lighter washes.

## 7. Confidence is Key

![Confidence is Key](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_confidence-is-key_428x640.jpg)

Ultimately, the most important accessory you can wear with your overalls is confidence. Since they are a bold fashion choice, own the look and wear it with pride. If you feel good in what you're wearing, it will show, and you'll pull off the trend effortlessly.
