# 8 Vibrant Colorful Statement Blazers ...

Colorful blazers are one of the easiest ways to freshen up an outfit. Bright shades of orange, pink, and blue are ideal this season. Despite what you may think, a colorful blazer can be quite versatile and worn either for work or play. So, ditch the dreary black and grey blazers for a pop of colour instead. Take a look at the following colorful statement blazers for some inspiration.

## 1. Fresh to Impress

![Fresh to Impress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_fresh-to-impress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Freshen up your wardrobe with this colorful blazer. It comes in a bright lime green shade and has a single breasted design. Pop it on over a white sundress and accessorise with some colorful stud earrings and tan shoes.

## 2. Co-Ord Ombre Fluid Blazer

![Co-Ord Ombre Fluid Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_co-ord-ombre-fluid-blazer_350x465.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6ubGct)

Really make a statement with this ombre blazer. The hem of the blazer is bright pink, but this gradually fades into a soft lemon color on top. This blazer also comes with matching shorts, so if you’re daring you could coordinate with the matching set. Otherwise, team it with a plain white tee and jeans for something more casual.

## 3. Single Button Blazer

_ **Price:** $24.90 at forever21.com_

All shades of blue are in this season and colorful blazers are an ideal way to work the trend. This single button blazer has a short design and features notched lapels and decorative welt pockets. Wear it with a cream colored dress and pumps for a fresh take on workwear.

## 4. Belted Open Front Blazer

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_

This blazer has an exclusive Forever21 design. It has a retro-inspired design with a notched collar, open front, and padded shoulders. To top it all off, it comes in a stunning shade of pink. I’d team it with a crisp white shift dress and metallic shoes and accessories.

## 5. Ponte Boyfriend Blazer

![Ponte Boyfriend Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_ponte-boyfriend-blazer_350x458.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/A62qYl)

Colorful blazers in warmer hues like this amber colored one tends to be much more versatile than their bright pink, green, or orange counterparts. Team this blazer with a white shirt and denim skirt for a casual daytime look.

## 6. Don’t Pink Twice Blazer

![Don’t Pink Twice Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_dont-pink-twice-blazer_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $67.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This bright pink blazer has a cropped design with half sleeves and a pointed hem. Try your hand at color blocking by wearing this blazer with a turquoise dress or skirt. If you’re wearing lots of color, then remember to keep the rest of your accessories fairly minimal.

## 7. Watermelon Boyfriend Blazer

![Watermelon Boyfriend Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_watermelon-boyfriend-blazer_350x499.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/twrrUa)

This boyfriend style blazer comes in the loveliest shade of watermelon red. It features contrast nude lining and has no button fastening. With colorful blazers like this, push the sleeves up and wear it open over a printed dress.

## 8. Rag & Bone Scuba-Sateen Blazer

![Rag & Bone Scuba-Sateen Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_rag-bone-scuba-sateen-blazer_350x426.jpg)

**Price:** $450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oJ05ay)

If you’ve got a bit of extra cash to spare, take a look at this bright orange blazer. It has a sharp tailored design and is perfect for making a statement. Wear it over a black and white geometric print dress and with silver heels for a fashion-forward look.

These are just a few colorful blazers that are bound to update your work or play wardrobe. Wear them with neutrals to really make them stand out or team them with prints for more of an statement look. Will you be working a colorful blazer into your look this season?
