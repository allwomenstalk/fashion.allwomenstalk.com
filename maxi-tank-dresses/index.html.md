# 7 Maxi Tank Dresses ...

Maxi dresses are here to stay for another season. This time around, look for column-like tank dresses. The look is relaxed, effortless cool. Wear alone for a fuss-free summer look, or layer with vests and jackets for something more street-smart. Take a look at the following maxi tank dresses below.

## 1. Topshop Yellow Easy Vest Maxi Dress

![Topshop Yellow Easy Vest Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/b0/huu4ovc654c8ff0a1fc26183715598_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/nRRAUY)
Bright colours are a popular choice for the summer months. This bold yellow maxi dress is an easy way to work the colour. It has a sporty racerback and split side detail. Wear this to a pool party and top it off with a floppy straw hat and strappy leather sandals.

## 2. T by Alexander Wang Asymmetric Long Dress

![T by Alexander Wang Asymmetric Long Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/bl/uf8buavq54c8ff0acc9b9032307552_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For those worried about clingy maxi tank dresses, take a look at this one from T by Alexander Wang. It has a scoop neck, low scoop back, and asymmetric hem. The design of this dress creates a draped effect. Dress it up with a statement bib necklace and tribal inspired pieces of jewellery.

## 3. Kain Vera Cross-Back Jersey Maxi Dress

![Kain Vera Cross-Back Jersey Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/ch/f1o8ohai54c8ff0b83d50749662824_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/6J0gVt)
This maxi dress features a unique-looking cut out detail at the back. It comes in an on-trend dusky rose colour and has a simple scoop neck at the front. The cross-over straps at the back create a keyhole detail at the centre. Worn alone, you can team it with metallic accents and a slouchy bag for an effortlessly chic look.

## 4. Jeremy Laing Wind Tunnel Tank Dress

![Jeremy Laing Wind Tunnel Tank Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i1/nb/bai26ceu54c8ff0c3d0f3401265922_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $1150.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The colour block aspect of this dress is a great twist on the basic tank design. It has an off-white top layer that is split at the side to reveal the lower layer. It is designed to be a relaxed fit and has a scoop neck and bubble hem. This dress already has a lot going on so keep it simple with some flat black shoes and minimal accessories.

## 5. Stella McCartney Thigh-Split Jersey Tank Dress

![Stella McCartney Thigh-Split Jersey Tank Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/fa/a1e6qs1z54c8ff0d00979355732947_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $785.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/lhMHhE)
This dress does well to break up the column-like design of most maxi tank dresses. This Stella McCartney number has a self-tie waist which creates a gathered effect. It also has two thigh-high splits at the front. Work a neutral colour pallet and pair this dress with dove grey and cream colours.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Neutral Stripe Maxi Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Neutral Stripe Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/sg/dv453i9d54c8ff0dcbf36529613160_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Neutral colours are still going strong. This tank dress is an interesting way to work the trend. The all-over neutral stripe pattern is a great twist on the classic navy/red/white combination. Pair this dress with a structured black blazer and strappy leather flats for a more sophisticated look.

## 7. Mara Hoffman Aztec-Print Jersey Maxi Tank Dress

![Mara Hoffman Aztec-Print Jersey Maxi Tank Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j6/qq/nhutt7d354c8ff0eaac57114719638_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $240.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/F9uzgo)
This printed maxi dress is bound to be a head-turner. It features a contemporary Aztec print and has a sporty racerback design. This dress is designed to be figure hugging. As the print is so bold, keep the rest of your accessories fairly simple.

Most maxi tank dresses come in clingy jersey fabrics. Take this into account when purchasing online, especially if you are a tad body conscious. Which of these dresses do you like best?
