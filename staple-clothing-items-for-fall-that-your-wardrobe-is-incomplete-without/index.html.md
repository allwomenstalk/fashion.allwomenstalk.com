# 9 Staple Clothing Items for Fall That Your Wardrobe is Incomplete without ...

There are a few staple clothing items for fall that you should not be without. Some are classic items, while others can become signature pieces. These pieces are not just meant to keep you warm and cozy, but to keep you looking amazing! Here are 9 staple clothing items for fall you don’t want to be without.

## 1. Scarf

![Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18102942_1091_400x600.jpg)

The first of all the staple clothing items for fall is of course the scarf! For me, fall is synonymous with the scarf. There are so many different ways to style your wardrobe with it. Thick scarves, patterned scarves, single colored scarves, oh my! There are countless varieties to fit your needs! Choose one scarf that can go with anything, and pick another that will add an extra edge to your wardrobe.

## 2. Boots

![Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103016_9732_400x600.jpg)

Can you say fall without boots? No! Colder weathers warrant boots, not just for style, but as a necessity. When the rain (or snow!) starts kicking in, wearing flats or tennis shoes isn’t practical anymore. Boots can also make an outfit outstanding. I love wearing brown knee length riding boots, but I also have a pair of short black biker boots. Two different boots for two different occasions! See what works for you.

## 3. Coat

![Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103220_6338_355x600.jpg)

Oh, the coat! Not only does a good coat keep you warm during the fall and winter, but it can be a statement piece as well. My bright yellow coat stands out in a crowd and makes me feel cheery on a gloomy day. Fall just isn’t complete without a warm coat!

## 4. Leather Jacket

![Leather Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103333_2851_400x600.jpg)

On the days you want to feel fierce and tough, leather jackets are amazing. Paired with jeans and biker boots, a scarf and a beanie, you’ve got a whole look complete! If real leather isn’t your thing, check out pleather. It’s usually more affordable and animal-friendly! I find my pleather jacket great on days I don’t need a full on coat.

## 5. Dresses

![Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103433_3204_400x600.jpg)

It may seem that dresses aren’t a good idea when the weather turns cold, but trust me, they are! I love wearing dresses to holiday parties. Find a really great black dress as a staple, but play with the reds and dark greens. Be the belle at the ball wherever you go!

## 6. Pullover Sweater

![Pullover Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103521_9699_400x600.jpg)

Some days, I just feel like staying in on nice, cold day to watch a movie and drink a cup of hot cocoa. Or sometimes, I find myself feeling a little under weather and don’t feel like dressing up. On these such occasions, I know I can easily slip on a pullover sweater and a scarf. Sweaters keep you warm and cozy, but also give a very relaxed and calm vibe. Even if you're the type who loves taking her time to get ready, having a pullover is great if you're running late.

## 7. Tights

![Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103614_0333_400x600.jpg)

What cozy outfit would be complete without tights? Tights are not restricted to plain black tights anymore, because there are many different colors and patterned variations. They can be paired with dresses, shirts or sweatshirts! On especially cold days, I wear my tights under my jeans for added warmth!

## 8. Beanie

![Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103704_6019_400x600.jpg)

I get really cold during the fall and winter, and a beanie is a cute way to keep my body heat in. Most of the time, I wear beanies because I don’t want to do my hair! Beanies make it perfectly okay to not do your hair that day, but still look cute! An alternative to beanies are head wraps, especially the crochet ones. Fall wouldn’t be complete without these!

## 9. Gloves

![Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18103732_8266_400x600.jpg)

Gloves are such a necessity to keep warm hands during the fall! My hands get really cold and two winters ago received so many pairs of gloves. I was thrilled! I had gloves for every occasion, even had the gloves with the mitten flap. If you need accessibility for your touch screen device, there are gloves for that, too.

Fall is just not complete without these items of clothing! There is no need to stop having a great style just because colder temperatures are here. Each of these items are staple clothing items, but remember to choose what works best for you! What are your favorite fall clothing items?
