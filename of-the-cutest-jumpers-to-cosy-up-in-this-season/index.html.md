# 9 of the Cutest Jumpers to Cosy up in This Season ...

Spring might be just around the corner, but there's still a chill in the air, and you'll need the cutest jumpers to keep warm when it's frosty in the morning! I love gorgeous winter knits and there are some super-cute jumpers around at the moment. I thought I would share 9 of the cutest jumpers I've found with you!

## 1. Knitted Clean Rib Jumper

![Knitted Clean Rib Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/0c/t89oytkr_600x772.jpg)

This primrose yellow Topshop jumper is simply gorgeous - it's one of the cutest jumpers around this season! Its slouchy, boyfriend style knit makes it ideal for relaxing in and it can be worn with skinny jeans, leggings or even over a dress if you're feeling chilly. I love the bright colour and chunky knit!

## 2. Knitted Curve Hem Crop Jumper

![Knitted Curve Hem Crop Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ks/se/be/ndwci8p2_279x351.jpg)

Topshop's range of jumpers is great this season and this cropped jumper is so on-trend. I love the neutral pale pink colour and the cropped style would look great with jeans or a skirt. Cosy!

## 3. Scot on Cardigan in Grey

![Scot on Cardigan in Grey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/db/na/v4vfpuxc_350x500.jpg)

This Modcloth cardigan isn't technically a jumper - but it's such a cute piece of knitwear I had to include it! I love the Scotty dog pattern and quirky appeal; it would look great teamed with a blouse and skirt or trousers at work, or over a pretty tea dress. It's definitely on my wish list!

## 4. Louche Peter Jumper

![Louche Peter Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/c8/uh/erq4rks6_599x737.jpg)

Peter the Puffin looks awesome on this cute jumper from Louche. Available to purchase on the Joy website, this stripy, nautical knit is lightweight and would look great with your favorite jeans!

## 5. Louche Babianne Deer Intarsia Jumper

![Louche Babianne Deer Intarsia Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/ou/of/q3jwc02z_599x737.jpg)

Teal is one of this season's hottest hues and this lightweight jumper is ideal for spring - plus it has an adorable deer print that looks so cute! I have this jumper and love teaming it with Joy's range of cute skirts and some opaque tights.

## 6. Louche Top Hat Badger Jumper

![Louche Top Hat Badger Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/i8/rj/guqh12pu_599x750.jpg)

Louche is one of my favorite brands and they're available to buy at Joy! This badger jumper features a quirky print of a badger in a top hat and the bright cherry red color makes this a bold choice, which is sure to brighten up even the greyest Monday morning. This is one of my favorites!

## 7. Louche Bee Embroidered Cardigan

![Louche Bee Embroidered Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wu/en/we/fj30hj5p_599x756.jpg)

This cute as a button cardigan features a delicate bee print so you'll bee-happy all day long! (Sorry, couldn't resist.) I love the pretty ornamental buttons and feminine print, and think this makes a great addition to any spring wardrobe.

## 8. Wrangler Dip Dye Jumper

![Wrangler Dip Dye Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/r7/cc/mcrxsfic_290x370.jpg)

I love the dip-dye effect of this cool chunky knit jumper. It's 100% cotton so it won't be itchy or irritate sensitive skin and its chunky knit will keep you warm all winter long and into spring - plus its cool blue tones are on-trend for spring 2014!

## 9. Louche Kat Intarsia Jumper

![Louche Kat Intarsia Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j0/vn/s8/zlknn2uj_599x724.jpg)

This jumper just makes me laugh - I love the quirky cat print and the bright cobalt blue color, plus it's lightweight enough to wear over your favorite dress! A definite must have from Joy and one of the cutest animal print jumpers around.

So there you have it - 9 of my favorite cute jumpers for this season. What's your favorite jumper style? Are you a lightweight or chunky cable knit type of girl? I'd love to know!
