# 11 Best Pencil Skirts ...

A list of the best pencil skirts is not an easy one to put together. There are so many different designs and colors out there that it’s hard to choose the best, but it is possible. If you’ve never owned a pencil skirt before, don’t wait any longer. Just choose one from this list and give it a try. Here are some of the best pencil skirts that you should get your hands on.

## 1. Theory Golda II Pencil Skirt

![Theory Golda II Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10045323_5229_521x517.jpg)

This is a classic high waisted pencil skirt! What screams “best pencil skirts” more than a classic one like this? Theory has brought a classic lined silhouette that will definitely make you look put together and professional, while still keeping a hint of sexy. The double-vented back hem is a simple detail that sticks with the classic lines of the skirt, but keeps it a little more exciting than a plain skirt.

**Price:** $200 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. J.Crew TELEGRAPH PENCIL SKIRT

![J.Crew TELEGRAPH PENCIL SKIRT](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/10045323_1911_521x471.jpg)

Don’t feel like wearing a black skirt? Well J.Crew to the rescue! This skirt, while gray, sits slightly below your natural waist and is made with an extra-long silhouette. The fabric and design come together to help create a vintage look that you can help bring into modern days. It’s easy to wear, easy to match, and easy to love!

**Price:** $138 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/NAHwMQ)

## 3. Halogen Seamed Pencil Skirt

Not only does it come in this black and purple combination, but it also comes with designs such as checker print. Users love this skirt because of its comfort and wearability. The hidden back-zip closures are just an added bonus! Check it out and add your name to the list of customers who love this skirt.

## 4. 5th & Mercer Pencil Skirt

This skirt also has a subtle woven textured pattern that is a simple yet nice touch that is sure to make people look again. Extra bonus points: this skirt is machine washable!

## 6. ASOS Pencil Skirt in Jersey

This pencil skirt is made out of a soft and stretchy cotton jersey and elastane that will fit like a glove and give an added sexy look to your outfit. Don’t worry about the skirt being too much. Although it’s form fitting, the length keeps it respectable for the workplace or anywhere else you may want to wear it.

## 7. T Tahari Bellona Pencil Skirt

That simple notch helps to make this skirt a little different than all other pencil skirts you might see. This skirt is is easy, reliable, and will definitely get people turning their heads.

The fabric is stretchy and flattering, especially if you have an hourglass body shape, but don’t worry if you don’t. It also has a hidden back zipper to seal you into this gorgeous skirt.

**Price:** $79 at [anntaylor.com](https://goo.gl/zPGghh)
