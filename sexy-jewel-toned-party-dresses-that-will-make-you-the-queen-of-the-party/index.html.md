# 7 Sexy Jewel\-Toned Party Dresses That Will Make You the Queen of the Party ...

Party Dresses come in a myriad of forms. Jewel-toned party dresses have quite a classic, regal feel to them. They’re also a great way of incorporating colour into your evening look. While past seasons have seen an emphasis on acid and neon bright colours, jewel colours are much less overpowering. Take a look at a few jewel-toned party dresses below.

## 1. Topshop Split Neck Maxi

![Topshop Split Neck Maxi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/az/m9iu0xqb54c935c9206d1352444932_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LN0RP3)
Those around you will be green with envy when you step out in this dress. It has a flowing goddess-like design and comes in an eye-catching green colour. It features a gathered neckline, split neck design, and gathered waist detail. This dress would look utterly stunning paired with gold drop earrings and cocktail rings.

## 2. Tibi Open Back Dress

![Tibi Open Back Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/x3/ce80ohaf54c935ca01431963578190_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oG1HsO)
While it may not look like much from the front, this dress has a daring low-cut back. It comes in a rich ruby hue and is made from silk crepe de chine. The design features puffed cap sleeves, a fitted waist, and is fully lined. Show off the open back partry dresses such as this by wearing your hair up or gathered to one side.

## 3. Haute Hippie Wrap-Effect Dress

![Haute Hippie Wrap-Effect Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/ra/bh7vp59m54c935cae7eae555540145_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $475.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/k5QDkG)
Teal is a statement jewel tone. This fun party dress features cascading ruffle details and has a décolletage-baring design. It has halterneck straps, a tiered skirt, and a dipped hem. Stick with gold metallic accessories and team this dress with a cuff bangle, embellished clutch, and strappy heels.

## 4. Topshop Velvet Halter Dress

![Topshop Velvet Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ra/eo/n9gy2h2854c935cc16daf455846180_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/mf8fVp)
This richly coloured velvet dress almost resembles liquid amber. It has a fun, sexy design with a cross-over front and flippy skirt. Velvet has a rich texture with a really regal feel to it. This dress would look great paired with turquoise accessories and toughened up with a pair of wedge ankle boots.

## 5. Dorothy Perkins Twist Cowl Neck Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Twist Cowl Neck Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/ez/rglfnt7854c935cd57e1c020948340_412x560.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This dress has a slinky, body-hugging design. It features a distinctive knot detail at the waist, as well as a cowl neck design. Body-hugging party dresses in clingy fabrics can sometimes be a it terrifying, so investing in quality shapewear is a must. Wear this dress with chandelier drop earrings, chunky bracelets, and neutral pumps.

## 6. Topshop Cut out Back Maxi Dress

![Topshop Cut out Back Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/ov/ogde14qs54c935ce30cad660063918_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/MHVDuW)
Maxi-length party dresses cut a striking figure. This magenta dress has a figure-hugging design with a revealing cut-out back. It also has thigh-high splits at the sides. Wear this dress with a gold collar necklace, cuff bangles, and bejewelled strappy sandals.

## 7. Roberto Cavalli Ruched Silk-Jersey Gown

![Roberto Cavalli Ruched Silk-Jersey Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/am/qd9diyis54c935cf21716031511039_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $1665.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UEAUZu)
If you want to feel like a queen in this dress, you’re going to need a queen-sized budget. This silk-jersey gown comes in a dark fuchsia colour and features elegant ruching at the waist. It also features a daring thigh-high front split. Perfect for black-tie functions, this dress would look heavenly paired with chandelier earrings, a framed clutch, and suede strappy sandals.

Party dresses in jewel tones are a chic choice. They look great with gold and silver metallic accessories, and the combination is one that’s sure to make these party dresses pop. Which of these jewel-toned party dresses do you like best?
