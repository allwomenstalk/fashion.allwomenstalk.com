# 8 Sporty and Stylish Baseball Caps ...

Baseball caps are perfect for working a sport-inspired look. Popping on one of this season’s must-have baseball caps is an easy way to update your look in an instant. Leather-look baseball caps seem to be the most popular option at the moment, but there are plenty of other fashionable designs to check out as well. Take a look at our pick of stylish baseball caps for the season.

## 1. Leather Baseball Cap

![Leather Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_leather-baseball-cap_592x511.jpg)

Leather-look baseball caps seem to be a very popular choice at the moment. They’re a great way of adding a sport-luxe touch to your outfit. Black leather-look baseball caps can look good with anything from t-shirts and shorts to mini dresses and bomber jackets. You can grab this leather baseball cap for $40 from ASOS but check out faux leather versions if you’re after something more affordable.

## 2. Gem Embellished Baseball Cap

![Gem Embellished Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_gem-embellished-baseball-cap_584x512.jpg)

Go glam in a gem embellished baseball cap. It’s an interesting twist on a fairly basic piece of head gear. Wear one with a slouchy top and denim shorts for a cool and casual weekend look. This gem embellished cap is by Pieces and will cost you around $25. Alternatively, you could always glue some plastic gemstones onto a plain baseball cap for an easy DIY option.

## 3. Floral Baseball Cap

![Floral Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_floral-baseball-cap_416x320.jpg)

Get into the swing of spring and summer with a floral baseball cap. It’s a quirky way to wear your florals this season. Wear your floral baseball cap with a white shift dress and colourful cropped jacket for a fresh summertime look. Designer caps by MSGM will set you back up to $80 to but you can find more affordable floral caps at ASOS and Forever 21 for only $10 - $15.

## 4. Leopard Print Baseball Cap

![Leopard Print Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_leopard-print-baseball-cap_600x488.jpg)

A little bit of leopard print goes a long way in spicing up a fairly basic outfit. Add some flavour to your look with a fun leopard print cap. This cap by Pieces will set you back around $15, which is a fraction of what you’d be paying for similar caps by designers like Eugenia Kim.

## 5. Mesh Baseball Cap

![Mesh Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_mesh-baseball-cap_460x308.jpg)

An understated black cap is undoubtedly the most timeless choice when it comes to baseball caps. However, they can seem a bit plain. That’s why it pays to look out for caps with some unexpected details. This Cheap Monday cap features a mesh overlay which adds an interesting texture to the overall design. Wear it with a plain white blouse, black waxy jeans and heels for a chic off-duty inspired look.

## 6. Studded Baseball Cap

![Studded Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_studded-baseball-cap_600x358.jpg)

A studded baseball cap would make an edgy addition to your outfits this season. Wear one with an outfit of a slouchy tank top and boyfriend-style jeans for a casual everyday look. This studded baseball cap is from Topshop but you can also find a range of studded styles at ASOS.

## 7. Denim Baseball Cap

![Denim Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_denim-baseball-cap_600x324.jpg)

Denim baseball caps are great for working a casual look this season. Faded denim caps look coolly relaxed while darker denim styles can look much smarter. This denim baseball cap from Topshop features a patterned panel to the front but you can find plain denim styles by Acne and Urban Outfitters.

## 8. Geometric Print Baseball Cap

![Geometric Print Baseball Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_geometric-print-baseball-cap_460x450.jpg)

Make your mark in a bold geometric print. This geometric print baseball cap is a stylish find and will cost you around $25 from Need Supply Co. It would look great worn with a white skater dress and canvas sneakers.

These are just a few baseball caps that could easily top off your outfits this season. Are you a fan of this sport-inspired fashion trend? Which of these caps do you like best?
