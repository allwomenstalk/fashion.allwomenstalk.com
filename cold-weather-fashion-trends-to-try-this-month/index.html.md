# 7 Cold Weather Fashion Trends to Try This Month ...

You may not be excited to dress for the cold weather, but there are so many cold weather fashion trends to try this year that it’s not so bad! Sometimes when it’s cold, the last thing you want to do is put together a cute outfit. We’ve all had those mornings. However, you don’t have to sacrifice comfort for style! Hopefully, the next time you’re in a rut, these cold weather fashion trends will come in handy!

## 1. Hats and Scarves

![Hats and Scarves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18104926_4596_600x771.jpg)

Hats and scarves, while essential, are one of the most perfect cold weather fashion trends to try out. They add something new and fresh to an outfit, all while keeping you warm in the cold! Hats and scarves are seemingly endless in choices, which is what makes them so perfect for winter! Options are slim in the winter, when you’re always trying to stay warm, so hats and scarves are the easiest way to stay warm while looking chic!

## 2. Statement Coats

![Statement Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18105043_7516_600x780.jpg)

Even if you have a somewhat drab outfit on, a statement coat can change that around completely. There are so many different statement coats out there, especially this winter, when bright colors are definitely on trend. Whether you go for a chic print or a bright color, you can’t go wrong with statement coats.

## 3. Oversized Sweaters

![Oversized Sweaters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18104837_5820_600x841.jpg)

Oversized sweaters are a winter essential. Whether you’re going out or staying in, you can’t go wrong with an oversized sweater. They’re comfortable, they’re cute, and they’re so in for winter. You can dress them up or simply wear them with a pair of leggings, and you’ll always look stylish.

## 4. Convert Warm Weather Clothes

![Convert Warm Weather Clothes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18104909_3770_600x864.jpg)

You definitely don’t need to buy an entirely new wardrobe for every new season. Save yourself some money and convert some of your favorite summer and fall outfits to winter. Throw a sweater over your favorite dress or maybe add a cardigan and tights. It requires a little bit of thinking outside the box, but it’s such an easy way to put together a cute outfit without spending a lot of money on a new wardrobe!

## 5. Layering

![Layering](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18104951_0975_600x780.jpg)

Layering is another easy way to save money while creating a diverse wardrobe. Not only will layering keep you warm, but with just a few basics, such as sweaters, dresses, and blouses, you can create a bunch of different outfits. If you’re looking to create a cute new outfit on a budget, layering is the perfect cold weather trend for you!

## 6. Faux Fur

![Faux Fur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18105117_0142_600x800.jpg)

Faux fur is a great way to stay comfortable yet chic in the winter. Whether you wear a faux fur coat, sweater, or maybe even a vest, faux fur is so in for winter. Faux fur will help you stay warm while adding style to any outfit. If you’re looking for a chic cold weather trend, you’ve found it in faux fur, especially this year. It’s the perfect way to keep warm on a cold day while looking perfectly put-together!

## 7. Ankle Boots

![Ankle Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18105134_8954_571x794.jpg)

While all boots in general are so perfect for cold weather, ankle boots are really on trend this winter. Whether you’re looking for an ankle boot that will keep you warm on a long commute, or ankle boot heels to wear on a night out, there are so many options to choose from that will keep you warm and stylish on the coldest days!

What’s your favorite cold weather trend? Have you tried any of these? Let me know in the comments!
