# 7 Chic Color Block Dresses for Summer ...

Color block dresses are a fun addition to any summer wardrobe. Color blocking is still a popular trend and will be just as relevant this summer as it was last year. Working a head-to-toe color blocked trend can be tricky, so an easy cheat is to buy something like a color block dress. That way, all the hard work’s already been done for you! Have a look at a few chic color blocked dresses, below.

## 1. Modcloth Color Block Dress

![Modcloth Color Block Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_modcloth-color-block-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $57.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Ladylike color block dresses are made more summer appropriate when they come in vibrant colors. This dress has a coral top and yellow skirt. The design is quite vintage inspired with a bow neckline and pleated skirt. This dress would look fantastic styled with neutral sandals and accessories.

## 2. Topshop Two Tone Silk Midi Dress

![Topshop Two Tone Silk Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_topshop-two-tone-silk-midi-dress_350x524.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/uh1tfN)
If you’re after something a bit more toned down when it comes to color blocked dresses, try out this midi dress. It comes in a cream and navy colorway and would be perfect for wearing at the office or for a polished off-duty look. Pair this dress with glossy ballet flats and a framed handbag.

## 3. Diane Von Furstenberg Color Block Dress

![Diane Von Furstenberg Color Block Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_diane-von-furstenberg-color-block-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AkGQ8H)
The splash of pink on this dress adds some much needed colour to an otherwise basic blocked dress. It has a high neck, side slit pockets, and a racy cutout back. This dress would look great teamed with a black patent clutch and strappy heels.

## 4. Modcloth Color Block Dress

![Modcloth Color Block Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_modcloth-color-block-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $54.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The all-over pleating on this dress gives it plenty of volume. Luckily, the sash belt at the waist allows for a cinched silhouette. The combination of pale colors on this dress makes it perfect for wearing during the day. Pair it with nude shoes and a compact side bag for a simple summer look.

## 5. Topshop Limited Edition Twin Frill Dress

![Topshop Limited Edition Twin Frill Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_topshop-limited-edition-twin-frill-dress_350x524.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zszroM)
You can work color block dresses in black and white. Just take this pretty frill dress, for example. It has a deep V-neck front and frilling at a low waist. Team this dress with strappy black heels and a small clutch for a stylish party look.

## 6. Versus Color Block Dress

![Versus Color Block Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_versus-color-block-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/xlMR7x)
This dress has a simple shift design which is accentuated by a color blocked design. It comes in a sweet pink hue and features a lime green panel to one side. This dress would look great worn with nude shoes and neutral accessories.

## 7. Modcloth Color Block Dress

![Modcloth Color Block Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_modcloth-color-block-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $137.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you want to work some color into your look but aren’t a fan of this season’s pastels or neons, try rustic colors instead. Color block dresses that feature warm reds and tans are still a chic option. This dress features a red skirt with waterfall pleating, a mustard top, and beige draped sash at the front. Style this dress up with gold accessories and nude pumps for a night out on the town.

A color block dress is a great way to work a stylish summer look. As a general rule, the brighter your color block dress is, the more minimal the accessories. What do you think of these color block dresses?
