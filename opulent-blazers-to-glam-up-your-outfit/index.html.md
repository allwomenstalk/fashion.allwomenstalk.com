# 8 Opulent Blazers to Glam up Your Outfit ...

Opulent blazers are perfect for working this season’s Baroque inspired trends. Simply throw them on over your outfit and walk out the door. An opulent blazer can transform your entire look. Right now it’s all about excess, so think beautiful brocade fabrics, intricate embroidery, and even a few embellishments! If this is a look you’re interesting in, take a look at some stylish opulent blazers below.

## 1. Sequin Blazer

![Sequin Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_sequin-blazer_600x810.jpg)

Opulent blazers are perfect for glamming up an outfit. Try a shimmery sequin blazer for a high voltage take on the trend. A sequin blazer would look great thrown over a simple outfit of a plain white tee and jeans. Right now stores like Dorothy Perkins stock some amazingly affordable sequin blazers in a range of colours from basic black to electric blue.

## 2. Jacquard Blazer

![Jacquard Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_jacquard-blazer_600x816.jpg)

A lot of designers have been incorporating Orient inspired touches into their ranges lately. A contemporary way to get on board this trend would be to pick up a blazer in a Chinoiserie inspired jacquard fabric. Stores like Topshop tend to cater for this style of high-end blazer but at a more affordable price point.

## 3. Lace Blazer

![Lace Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_lace-blazer_600x720.jpg)

A bit of lace goes a long way when it comes to adding a glamorous touch to an outfit. An opulent blazer made entirely out of lace would look amazing teamed with a simple dress and heels. DKNY did a great black lace blazer that you can currently grab for a fraction of the cost on The Outnet, or try places like Topshop and Charlotte Russe for more budget friendly options!

## 4. Floral Blazer

![Floral Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_floral-blazer_600x833.jpg)

Printed blazers are a firm favourite right now. Pick one up in a floral print to add some extra interest to your look. Choose a blazer in a silky, draped fabric to give it that luxe touch. Hit up online stores like ASOS and Nasty Gal for a great range of floral blazers.

## 5. Metallic Blazer

![Metallic Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_metallic-blazer_600x678.jpg)

Nothing says glam more than a hint of metallic. Or, in the case of this opulent blazer, a lot of metallic! A lot of designers are jumping on board this trend, so be prepared to fork out a lot more cash if this is a look you’re keen to try.

## 6. Botanic Print Blazer

![Botanic Print Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_botanic-print-blazer_270x500.jpg)

Big, lush, botanic prints are seriously on-trend right now. They’re like an exotic version of the common ditsy floral print. These are the kinds of opulent blazers that you could easily get away with wearing during the day with a sundress and flats.

## 7. Brocade Blazer

![Brocade Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/7_brocade-blazer_384x368.jpg)

Heavy brocade blazers are perfect for working this season’s popular Baroque inspired looks. With designers such as Balmain and Alexander McQueen working the trend, these opulent blazers can be quite pricey. However, with a bit of bargain hunting, you might be able to find affordable alternatives in places like ASOS.

## 8. Coloured Lamé Blazer

![Coloured Lamé Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/8_coloured-lam-blazer_230x304.jpg)

Metallics are a tried and tested favourite when it comes to glamming up an outfit. Why not take it to the next level and give your outfit a fun twist with coloured metallics this season? Lush sea greens and electric blues are great alternatives to your typical golds and silvers.

When it comes to opulent blazers, these are just some styles to consider. Hit stores like The Outnet to grab a designer bargain, or go easy on the wallet by sticking to stores like Forever 21 and Topshop. How would you style an opulent blazer this season?
