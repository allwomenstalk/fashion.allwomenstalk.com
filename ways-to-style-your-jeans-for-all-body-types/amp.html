17 Ways to Style Your Jeans (for All Body Types) ...

Plaid Blanket Scarves and Beanies Will Keep You Warm and Cozy • Fitted Jeans and Loose Tees Are about to Become Your Biggest Fashion OTP (original Top Pairing) Ever • Peplum Shirts Compliment Skinny Jeans Really Well • Chambray on Denim is Every Denim Lover's Dream • Your Hips Definitely Won't Lie when You Wear High Waisted Pants and a Crop Top • More ...

By Cassandra

Full disclosure: For the past five years, my wardrobe has mainly consisted of dresses, leggings and sweatpants. I spent a lot of time in the dance studio, which meant that wearing things that didn't let me change quickly wasn't an option; the habit ended up sticking. It wasn't until a few months ago that I started wearing jeans again. My mind was BLOWN at how they made me feel! Jeans can be a total confidence booster, regardless of your size or height. You just have to find the right pair and style them correctly.

1. Plaid Blanket Scarves and Beanies Will Keep You Warm and Cozy

Source: 7 ways to style a blanket scarf

It's a given that scarves are a great way to accessorize a casual outfit. Blanket scarves give your look that much more depth and layer; wearing one will totally make you look like you put a lot of thought into what you're wearing (even if you just grabbed it before heading out that door).

2. Fitted Jeans and Loose Tees Are about to Become Your Biggest Fashion OTP (original Top Pairing) Ever

Source: Distressed Low Rise Skinny Jean

Here goes the endless list of reasons why fitted jeans are truly the best. First, they will perfectly accentuate your shape. Next, they can actually make you look slimmer. It's all about making sure that the fit is right for you.

3. Peplum Shirts Compliment Skinny Jeans Really Well

Source: Trendy Plus Size Clothing: Fashion

The best thing about peplum shirts is that they're ridiculously trendy and pretty much flatter all shapes and sizes. Another perk is that they are the easiest way to dress up your outfit. Just throw on a pair of heels and you're good to go!

4. Chambray on Denim is Every Denim Lover's Dream

Source: Find That Perfect Pair of

I never thought that I could pull off wearing denim on denim until I saw just how well the contrasting shades of blue looked on other people. Now, chambray shirts have essentially become a part of my uniform!

5. Your Hips Definitely Won't Lie when You Wear High Waisted Pants and a Crop Top

Source: Love Your Humps: Dressing Tips

It doesn't matter how skinny, curvy, tall or short you are. High waisted pants will always be in style. There's something incredibly timeless about the silhouette that they give off.

6. Speaking of the High-waist Trend, Flared Pants Are Making a Huge Comeback

Source: Flared Pants Are Spring 2015’s

Give off some major 70s vibes with these trendy jeans. These are not your mom's bell bottoms. While finding the perfect outfit to compliment them sounds easier said than done, I've found that the best move is to balance out your look with heels and a flowy crop.

7. Obsessed with Boyfriend Jeans? It's All about the Heels

Source: Naja Diamond: Happy New Year!

Whether you're going out on a date or heading to brunch with your girls, you can never go wrong with this combo.

8. Hello Layers, Layers, Layers!

Source: 3 outfits for the curvy girl

Everything about this look is perfect. From the bold scarf to the leather jacket, the added layers bring her casual jeans to the next level.

9. Blazers Will Make You Look Office Ready

Source: Skinny Jeans on Curvy Girls

One of the reasons why this is my go-to look at work is because of the delightful contrast it gives off. The blazer softens the roughness of the jeans and the accessorizes make it even more chic.

10. Don't Be Afraid to Play around with Different Lengths

Source: Button Back Striped Tops ~

Wearing knee highs make your legs look more defined. When you pair them with a long tunic, both pieces actually work together to make you look slimmer and taller. If that isn't a win win situation, I don't know what is!

11. Here's Further Proof Why You Should Rock a Long Tunic over Your Jeans

Source: 10 Reasons why the tunic

Because I'm only 5'2, I was a little skeptical at first about how short a tunic would make my legs look. I''m also not too into wearing heels, especially at work. Luckily, I've found that for my body type, skinny or cuffed jeans look amazing with long shirts.

12. Wear Open Cardigans Often!

Source: Curvy Girl Fashion: 40 Plus

I love how effortless open cardigans look, especially when they are layered over a simple shirt. This blogger made a great choice by pairing her already cool look with that awesome bag!

13. Throw on Some Dual Tone Shoes, a Casual Tee and a Leather Bag to Show the World You Mean Business

Source: Denim And Navy Outfit Idea

Sometimes, keeping it simple is your best bet. It doesn't get any better than this.

14. Rock Dark Denim and Knee Highs on Your Next Date. Trust Us!

Source: Tops Trendy & Affordable The

I'm a sucker for dark denim. Everything about this outfit screams perfection. If you're not a fan of knee high boots, booties are a great substitute.

15. Experiment with Prints and Neutrals

Source: girlwithcurves.tumblr.com

From her printed scarf to her beige blazer, this look is totally spot on. I love that her jeans flare out a little and she doesn't shy away from pairing different neutrals together.

16. Always Have at Least One Statement Piece (just Don't Go Overboard!)

Source: Sweater & Jeans + Forever

Having just one statement piece, either in the form of jewelry or a designer bag, can make all of the difference. It shows that you don't mind letting your style speak for itself.

17. Make Sure Your Shirt Compliments Your Bag

Source: Kendi Everyday: The Fall Uniform

This is probably one of my favorite looks. The burgundy color of her shirt really makes her neutral toned bag and booties stand out.

How do you style your jeans? Give us a shout out in the comments below!
