# 13 Free People Fashions and Accessories for Spring ...

The Free People brand is one of my favorite clothing brands. I love the flowy and boho clothes that Free People sells. With spring and summer coming up, I have been shopping at Free People pretty frequently. Here are some Free People brand clothes that you should definitely check out for this season:

## 1. Maya off Shoulder Mini

![Maya off Shoulder Mini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xm/rd/d36sihzp_356x475.jpg)

This is one of my favorite Free People brand dresses! Music festival season is coming up, and so are those fun outdoor summer concerts. This dress would be a perfect option to wear to a music festival! It’s so carefree and feminine, not to mention how appropriate this is for spring and summer!

## 2. Pop Pier Embroidered Dress

![Pop Pier Embroidered Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/aj/fpxn2l9k_356x475.jpg)

Speaking of music festivals, I will be wearing this gorgeous dress to Coachella! I love the colorful embroidered pattern on this dress. If a dress like this is a little too fancy for your taste, you could wear this to a fun dinner party! It’s sure to turn heads!

## 3. Olivia Crossbody

![Olivia Crossbody](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/aj/yf8mcutr_356x475.jpg)

This handmade leather crossbody is the perfect accessory for this season! It is small enough to complement any outfit, but large enough to carry your essentials in. The strap is removable, too, so you can wear this bag as a clutch. I really like the fun woven pattern!

## 4. Something like Olivia Top

![Something like Olivia Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uc/c1/t55lw7a7_600x801.jpg)

This summery crop top is one of my favorite Free People things this season! I like the delicate lace trim and tie at the front! I would wear this adorable top with some cutoff shorts and fun sandals.

## 5. Parting Sun Top

![Parting Sun Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/01/l9j3dcpo_356x475.jpg)

This oversized sheer top with tribal embroidery is so springy! I think that the little keyhole at the bust is so cute! It's such a boho inspired look, it's ideal for spring and summer.

## 6. Cutwork Oversized Tunic

This top is too cute for words! I really like this oversized shirt with mesh floral details! I would wear this gorgeous top either with leggings or maybe just some shorts. You can definitely pull off the effortless look with this top!

## 7. Harpoon Flat Sandal

These strappy lace-up sandals are just screaming spring! The unconventional style will add a fun twist to your outfits. These versatile shoes can take you from a day on the beach to a dressier evening with family!

## 8. Textured High Rise Short

![Textured High Rise Short](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/m2/z9imicjz_356x475.jpg)

Channel your inner Taylor Swift with these gorgeous textured high rise shorts! The stretchy material makes these shorts fitting and slimming. I would wear them just like the model, with a crochet crop top. So cute!

## 9. Full of Grace Dress

![Full of Grace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/nb/07kom83w_356x475.jpg)

I am drooling over this gorgeous dress! Going to a beach wedding? This dress has beautiful and intricate embroidery and sequins and so much glam! The handkerchief skirt adds a lot of dimension to this work of art.

## 10. Eyelet Shapeless V Neck

Imagine yourself frolicking through long blades of grass in the countryside in this dress… I love how sweet and simple this dress is. Whether you’re spending the day with your significant other or just strolling around downtown, this is such a great dress for spring!

## 11. Something Sweet Slip Dress

I absolutely love eyelet and lace clothing items, and this sweet slip dress is absolutely precious! It has eyelet detailing on the bodice, and the gauzy hem adds a sweet detail to the dress. This lightweight dress is perfect for a warm spring afternoon.

## 12. Fitted with Daisies Dress

![Fitted with Daisies Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/lq/ph0z8603_600x801.jpg)

I love everything about this dress: the color, the style, and the details! This fit and flare style is incredibly flattering. The cutout around the waist is my favorite because it makes this dress extra flirty and springy!

## 13. FP Foiled Annabella Dress

![FP Foiled Annabella Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bu/ip/fa3jx1he_600x801.jpg)

This minidress with lace accents is a classic spring dress. It’s flowy and dressy enough for fun spring events! And I love how that red color just pops!

These are some Free People fashions and accessories for spring. Are you going to wear any of these this spring? Where do you like to shop for your spring wardrobe?
