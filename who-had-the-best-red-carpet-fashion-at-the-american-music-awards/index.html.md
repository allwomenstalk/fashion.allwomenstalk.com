# Who Had the Best Red Carpet Fashion at the American Music Awards?

The American Music Awards was the star-studded place to be on Sunday night, November 23rd. Music's biggest names like One Direction, Sam Smith, Jennifer Lopez, and Mary J. Blige (just to name a few) took to the stage to belt out their hit singles. Aside from the performances, the red carpet fashion has to be my favorite thing about award season.
