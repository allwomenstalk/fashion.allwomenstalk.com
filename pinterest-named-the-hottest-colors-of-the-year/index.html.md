# Pinterest Named the Hottest Colors of the Year ...

According to Pinterest, the top colors for women fashion are Blush Pink (which is similar to Pantone’s Rose Quartz) and Mint Green. If you want to wear those colors, look no further than [TJ Maxx](http://tjmaxx.tjx.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and [Marshalls.](https://www.marshalls.com/us/store/index.jsp) Both stores carry the latest on-trend designer merchandise, in the latest on-trend colors – Blush Pink and Mint Green. Take a look at how beautiful they are:
