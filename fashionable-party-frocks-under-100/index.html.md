# 9 Fashionable Party Frocks under 100 ...

Finding fashionable party dresses under $100 is easy! When dressing for your next party, there’s no need to drop the big bucks on an outfit. With a bit of searching and comparing, you’ll find that you can work some of the most fashionable looks with party frocks under $100. Check out the following list to find your perfect party dress for under $100.

## 1. The Brocade Dress

![The Brocade Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_the-brocade-dress_350x500.jpg)

Brocade fabrics are great for working this season’s Baroque inspired trends. This dress from Modcloth comes in at $89.99 and comes in a rich metallic bronze colour. For similar party dresses under $100, you could also try stores like ASOS and Forever 21.

## 2. The Little Red Dress

![The Little Red Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_the-little-red-dress_600x900.jpg)

Move over little black dress, we need you to make way for the little red dress. A fiery crimson hue is perfect for making a statement and can look extremely stylish with some metallic jewellery. Hunt around at your favourite chain stores, like this one from Topshop, for a fun little red dress to wear to your next party.

## 3. The Lace Dress

![The Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_the-lace-dress_328x400.jpg)

Lace dresses are a firm favourite, and luckily for us it’s easy to find these styles of party dresses under $100. This season look out for more modern takes on the lace dress, like this cool cut-out dress from Forever 21. Best of all? It’ll cost you less than $30!

## 4. The Metallic Dress

![The Metallic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_the-metallic-dress_600x899.jpg)

Metallic dresses might look like they cost a fortune, but you can easily get these party dresses under $100. You can pick up a shimmery silver dress like this number for $60 from Topshop. Popular online stores like Modcloth and ASOS also currently stock a range of stylish metallic dresses in plenty of different colours.

## 5. The Retro Dress

![The Retro Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_the-retro-dress_350x500.jpg)

Embrace the decades past with a stylish retro inspired dress. Check out retro-inspired fashion sites like Modcloth and Ruche for a stylish party dress under $100. This cute polka dot creation comes from Modcloth and will cost you just under $50. You could probably get away with wearing this number during the daytime as well!

## 6. The Little Black Dress

![The Little Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_the-little-black-dress_328x400.jpg)

Every wardrobe needs a little black dress. They can be the solution to nearly every outfit dilemma. You can pick up a feminine LBD like this one from Forever 21 for a little over $20. Depending on the style of your LBD, dress it up with accessories accordingly.

## 7. The Peplum Dress

![The Peplum Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_the-peplum-dress_328x400.jpg)

Peplum silhouettes are still enjoying their time in the limelight. You can also find plenty of peplum party dresses under $100. Try stores like ASOS, Nasty Gal, and Forever 21 for plenty of affordable and stylish peplum dresses. This one from Forever 21 is sweet and feminine, and would look great teamed with nude pumps and dainty jewels.

## 8. The Printed Dress

![The Printed Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/8_the-printed-dress_350x500.jpg)

It seems like you can’t go into a store these days without laying your eyes on printed clothes. A printed party dress is perfect for making a statement, like this contemporary looking number from Modcloth. Team your printed dresses with basic accessories to really let the print do the talking.

## 9. The Maxi Dress

![The Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/9_the-maxi-dress_350x500.jpg)

Floor-skimming hemlines are perfect for creating a luxurious look. This pretty purple gown from Modcloth will only set you back $80. When it comes to party dresses under $100, longer hemlines will really give the feel of extra glamour.

These are a few examples of fashionable party dresses under $100. Whether you buy them online or in-store, there’s a type of dress to suit a range of styles and occasions. Which of these party frocks under $100 do you like best?
