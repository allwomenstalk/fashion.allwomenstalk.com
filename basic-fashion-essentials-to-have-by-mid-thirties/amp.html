7 Basic Fashion Essentials to Have by Mid Thirties ...

By Jelena • 8 Comments

Basic fashion essentials are those great pieces that are a bit more classic, a bit more neutral, and a bit finer looking. They some how accidentally or on purpose find a way into our closet and end up spending a few amazing years in it (or, usually out of it), helping us look great on daily basis! These fashionable staple pieces are worth collecting and spending a bit more cash on as well. They are more than just a current fashion craze and usually end up being loved until they get too threadbare to wear! Well, I’m not sure how far you have gotten with your list of must-haves, but here’s a list you simply must check out to make sure no basic fashion essential gets forgotten!

Table of contents:

  1. Figure Flattering Chinos
  2. Trench
  3. Black and/or Nude Pumps
  4. Your Own Equivalent of a Little Black Dress

1 Figure Flattering Chinos

First on my list of basic fashion essentials you’re sure to discover and fall in love with sooner or later are, of course, chinos. Most classic, but at the same time most versatile pair of pants a woman can have! No need to make stocking up on these your personal goal - one really great pair will do. When purchased wisely and selected with care they will literally last you YEARS and help you put together tons of different outfits! Wear them belted (go for a fancy belt) with a classy top, great pumps, clutch, and a cardigan for a professional chic look. Or roll the legs a bit, throw on a bit more casual yet girly top, and hop into your favorite ballet flats or loafers in case you need to look casual but have no interest in losing your trademarked “well put together” look. You can also experiment with blazers of different colors and styles, as there are tons of looks you can go for once you obtain a pair of these wonderfully versatile babies!

Shopping Tip: Invest in a pair of By Malene Birger chino style pants and you’ll be covered for a decade! They do cost quite a pretty penny in season, but you can always choose a last season’s model and take advantage of both fantastic quality and fantastic price!

2 Trench

Classic trench and trench-style coats are definitely one of those fashionable staple pieces that last a lifetime and should be on your list of must-buys. That is, once you’ve finished building your personal style and become independent enough to finance it, of course. Autumns and springs just wouldn’t be the same without this basic fashion essential, now, would they? And the best thing about it is that it never goes out of style nor does it need to be too tight and therefore it won't become unusable in case you go up or down a few pounds.

Shopping Tip: Burberry trench knock offs are a huge no-no! If this classic, always trendy trench is what you want, give yourself a bit more time to collect the funds you’ll need. You can also visit a Burberry Outlet and take your pick amongst the discounted models they have in store or invest in a similar, good quality non-Burberry piece that will look just as pricy without actually being just as pricy!

3 Black and/or Nude Pumps

They don’t have to be designer but they have to be really awesome! And by that, I mean excellent quality and super comfy! Most fashion guides and pros recommend black pumps and, although I totally see the practical side of it, I must admit classic blacks kind of remind me of flight attendants and lawyers. Go for them if you like corporate style fashion or express your creativity by choosing a non-standard cut or low key yet interesting details a classic black pump often lacks. Nude pumps are another cool option to go for if you already have a lot of black shoes and no intention of investing in another pair anytime soon, regardless of how great and timeless they are. Wear these babies with neutrals if you’re going for that simple, dressy casual feel. You can also play with colors for a fantastically trendy look or slip them on for work simply because a well matched neutral pump makes legs seem longer!

Shopping Tip: Prada classic platform pumps are a textbook example of what a low key, timeless pump is supposed to look like so give them a look before you decide to go shopping for your next most favorite pair of shoes!

4 Your Own Equivalent of a Little Black Dress

A little black dress is more than a piece of clothing nowadays! It’s a term that basically describes a fantastic outfit that fits one perfectly, yet is hassle free enough to allow that person to be completely ready in less than an hour! It can still be a dress, of course, but it doesn’t have to be an evening dress nor does it need to be black anymore! Your equivalent of a little black dress can even include a few of your most favorite basic fashion essentials that look extremely well together and are always clean and pressed. These are the essentials that are convenient to pull out, throw on, and be out the door in no time yet look like you’ve really spent some serious time contemplating your wardrobe choices for the day.

Shopping Tip: Diane Von Furstenberg wrap dresses are the definition of hassle free femininity! Invest in a boldly printed one in case you are a petite lady with an astonishing collection of neutral-colored shoes, go for a monochromatic one in case you love to play with your accessories or take a peek at my personal favorites – dresses that combine the best of both worlds!

5 Ballet Flats to Die for

Super comfy, super chic, and super nice-looking ballet pumps are definitely one of those fashionable staple pieces to keep a look out for! Leave your desire to opt for something neutral aside and kick up the cool factor a notch by opting for a really amazing color such as burnt orange, jade green, turquoise, vine red, or mustard yellow! These colors may seem impossible or at least very hard to color coordinate with the rest of your clothes, but are actually quite versatile, easy to wear not to mention ideal for keeping things interesting if your closet contains mostly neutrals.

Shopping Tip: If you’re looking for great quality ballet flats to love for years to come, pay special attention to the sole and insole. Both designer and non designer ballet flats are often made to look pretty only and are completely flat, too inflexible and provide no arch support whatsoever.

6 Chanel Style Blazer

Okay, now tell me this isn't one of your favorite pieces ever! I know, I know… nothing spells classic and timeless better than a Chanel blazer and I’m sure this fantastic item will make it onto your must-have list sooner or later! No need to spend too many words on it, I believe, as the popularity of this style speaks for itself.

Shopping Tip: This coveted basic fashion essential makes its appearance at Zara shops very often where you’ll be able to find many different textile, leather and faux leather variations of this amazing style. They still cost a pretty penny, mind you, but way less then you’d have to shell out for a real Chanel piece!

7 Mini Dress

A bold, sexy piece like this one will put you under the spotlight at any age and is one of those basic fashion essentials you definitely should keep your eyes opened for even if you’re past your twenties! Don’t let the stereotypes dictate your fashion choices – a refined, classic cut you can accessorize for the occasion needs no ID, just a pair of great legs or a few smart tricks to help a pair of normal legs seem positively flawless! Opt for a round neckline, ¾ sleeves and a figure flattering A line in case you don’t feel comfortable showing too much. Or say yes to a small V, sleeveless style and a fit that accents your curves without being too tight if you’re looking for a dress you can wear alone or over a shirt, and a style that looks positively breathtaking with just any type of blazer!

Shopping Tip: In case you’re a fan of classic and neutral colors, your fantastic piece might be waiting for you at your local Ralph Lauren boutique! Not exactly what you’re hoping for? No problem – visit Mango or ASOS website and take your pick amongst tons of both trendy and classic styles that won’t burn a huge hole in your wallet!

Which of these basic fashion essentials do you own, and is there a piece or two you’re hoping to acquire but have no idea where to look for? Do share as there are plenty of super stylish girls here ready to help you find those fashionable staple pieces in no time and for the best price!
