# 8 Stylish Cut out Shoulder Tops ...

Cut out shoulder tops are a fun trend to try out this upcoming summer season. A cut out shoulder top is perfect for showing off some extra skin, plus it doubles as added ventilation! If you think a shoulder cut out top is on your list of must-haves, take a look at a few on-trend options below.

## 1. Shoulder Cut out Top

_ **Price:** $17.80 at forever21.com_
This slouchy top is a perfect example of stylish cut out shoulder tops. It comes in the softest pink shade and has a round neck, button-up front, and roomy three quarter length sleeves. The striking feature of this top is, of course, the cut out detailing at the shoulders. Simply team this top with coloured shorts and sandals for a fresh look.

## 2. Lattice Cut out Top

![Lattice Cut out Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_lattice-cut-out-top_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This top features cut out detailing of a different kind in the form of a lattice pattern at the shoulders. This short sleeved top has a traditional button-up design and has a loose fit throughout. Team it with some cropped check pants and Oxford shoes for a bit of a preppy twist.

## 3. White Cut out Shoulder Top

![White Cut out Shoulder Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_white-cut-out-shoulder-top_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/AK3vp5)
A simple white top can be given a basic makeover with the addition of cut out shoulders. This top is made from a silky fabric and has three quarter length sleeves. In the summertime, fresh white clothes look great set against bronzy skin so make sure yours is prepped prior to wearing.

## 4. Lace Cut Outs Top

_ **Price:** $15.80 at forever21.com_
Coloured lace is a stylish option this season. This orange lace top has cut out shoulder detailing and a slightly dipped hem. It would look great teamed with a full white skirt and tan sandals.

## 5. Chain Trim Cut out Top

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
A basic white top is given a new lease on life with cut out shoulders and chain trim detailing. It’s made from a soft knit fabric and has a scooped neckline with faceted bead, rhinestone, and cable chain detailing. Keep the approach edgy by teaming this top with leather look pants and ankle boots.

## 6. Pink Cut out Shoulder Top

![Pink Cut out Shoulder Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_pink-cut-out-shoulder-top_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s all about light, neutral colours right now, so embrace the trend with this pink cut out top. It has a relaxed design with a draped hemline and features a subtle shell print. Work an on-trend look by pairing this top with pastel coloured jeans.

## 7. Dotted Cut out Top

_ **Price:** $13.80 at forever21.com_
You’ve got to love a good polka dot pattern. Well, I know I do, at least. This top features a bold black and white spotted pattern and has cut out shoulders. Team cut out shoulder tops such as this one with red jeans and patent shoes for a fun, youthful look.

## 8. A.L.C. Snakeskin Trimmed Top

![A.L.C. Snakeskin Trimmed Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/8_a-l-c-snakeskin-trimmed-top_288x400.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If light and bright colours aren’t your thing, stick to basic black. This top features small cut out details near the shoulders and snakeskin trims. Work a cool night time look by teaming this top with printed trousers and metallic heels.

Cut out shoulder tops are a fun way to update your summer look. If you don’t want to splurge on this trend, you could always take to an old shirt with scissors instead! Are you a fan of the cut out shoulder top? Which of these styles do you like best?
