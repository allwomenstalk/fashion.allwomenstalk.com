# 9 Trendy Pale Dresses to Own ...

**Pale dresses** just scream spring dressing. This season, there’s a wealth of pretty pale dresses for you to choose from. The most delightful dresses for spring revolve around a pastel colour palette. When choosing a pale dress, you can easily take your own personal style into account. If your style is feminine then you could go for a dress that’s a bit more froufrou. If you’re a bit edgier and prefer minimalism, opt for a tank or column style dress in a pale hue. Take a look at some pretty pale dresses below.

## 1. Topshop Chevron Metallic Tunic

![Topshop Chevron Metallic Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_topshop-chevron-metallic-tunic_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Hnsrfn)
This pale blue dress features a stunning chevron pattern. The fabric also has silver metallic accents which add a bit of shimmer to the overall look. This pale dress would look delightful paired with some patent nude sandals a funky clutch.

## 2. Topshop Crepe Shirt Dress

![Topshop Crepe Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_topshop-crepe-shirt-dress_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $92.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/PSo7Ds)

## 3. Dorothy Perkins Pink One Shoulder Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Pink One Shoulder Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_dorothy-perkins-pink-one-shoulder-dress_412x560.jpg)

## 4. Vanesssa Bruno Athé Silk Crepe De Chine

## 5. Topshop Lace Strappy Dress

![Topshop Lace Strappy Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_topshop-lace-strappy-dress_373x560.jpg)

A little bit of lace never goes out of style. This flirty **pale dress has a** lace bodice and flippy skirt. Wear it with a cropped denim jacket and leather sandals for a cool and casual look.

## 6. Jack by BB Dakota Peach Dress

![Jack by BB Dakota Peach Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_jack-by-bb-dakota-peach-dress_350x500.jpg)

I love the intricate criss-cross neckline on this dress. It comes in a lovely pale peach colour and would be perfect for wearing during the day. Pop a cardigan or jacket over the top during cooler moments, and team with a pair of glitter shoes for a fun and on-trend look.

## 7. Betsey Johnson by the Beautiful Sea Dress

![Betsey Johnson by the Beautiful Sea Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_betsey-johnson-by-the-beautiful-sea-dress_350x500.jpg)

This pale dress is made from a wonderful blue seersucker fabric. It has a sweetheart neckline, fitted bodice, and full skirt. Team it with dainty accessories and white bow shoes for simply sweet look.

## 8. Girl by Band of Outsiders Chiffon Dress

![Girl by Band of Outsiders Chiffon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/8_girl-by-band-of-outsiders-chiffon-dress_373x560.jpg)

This tiered chiffon dress is a whimsical little number. It features pale pink and cream stripes and has a grey all-over floral print. Work a flirty springtime look by teaming this dress with neutral shoes and accessories.

## 9. Topshop Fleck Vest Midi Dress

![Topshop Fleck Vest Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/9_topshop-fleck-vest-midi-dress_373x560.jpg)

If you’re after a more laidback and contemporary look when it comes to your **pale dresses, try** this midi-length vest dress. Toughen up the pastel pink by teaming it with ankle boots and a cropped biker jacket.

There’s a variety of ways for you to style pale dresses. Metallics and embellishments are great for adding some glitz and glamour, while neutral accessories are perfect for during the day. Which of these pretty pale dresses do you like best?
