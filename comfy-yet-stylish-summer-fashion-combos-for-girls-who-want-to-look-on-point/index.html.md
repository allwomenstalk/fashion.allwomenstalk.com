# 5 Comfy Yet Stylish Summer Fashion Combos for Girls Who Want to Look on Point ...

Summer is here and there is nothing that would stop us from wearing all those light pieces of clothing that we love, when it comes to warm days and romantic summer nights. Have you prepared your wardrobe for these days? If not, here are some ideas on how to advance and devise your summer fashion combinations, while always looking perfectly and completely to enjoy the free, comfortable feeling that they will give to you.

## 1. COMBINE YOUR COLORFUL DRESS with FLATS

![clothing, footwear, fashion model, shoe, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/rb/ck5qjd5659618957aaf0d799277299_600x600.jpg)

We all know that dresses and flats are a blend that is always in the trend. Replace the dress with more elegant strap that will be tied around your waist and a purse that will lift the entire look to a higher level. This is a great everyday combination that you can carry anywhere.

## 2. CHOOSE a FLOWER DRESS, with a DARKER BASE, FLAT CUT, over the KNEE LENGTH

![fashion model, catwalk, dress, supermodel, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c1/l1/aqnzfjiv59618ab3295ea336810073_600x750.jpg)

Rock this outfit with high heels and a purse for a more elegant look. You are ready for another successful day.

## 3. WEAR BLACK FASHION COMBINATIONS in the SUMMER without ANY PROBLEM

![eyewear, girl, glasses, socialite, sunglasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/sv/umwarin059618b10304b5903353716_600x692.jpg)

It is important to choose more wider models, but also lighter materials, and of course you will look great. In combination with sneakers or espadrillos, you will get a blend in which with no doubt you will enjoy the next few days.

## 4. DON’T MISS the PANTS with the LINES THIS SEASON

![clothing, shoulder, fashion model, dress, shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/r9/y251dj1e59618b919407c618640338_600x750.jpg)

They are quite comfortable, and the design makes you look slimmer. Combined with a casual blouse or a shirt, flats for the day, heels for the evening and elegant bag will make you look like a real fashionista.

## 5. LITTLE BLACK DRESS

![fashion model, shoulder, handbag, joint, neck,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ed/nu/nukvp90j59618b5e288c2288893315_600x600.jpg)

If there is a summer celebration, such as wedding, in the warm nights a little black dress always wins, but this time with length to the sleeves. The model with A cut, will give you an elegant, but above all unpretentious look and attitude which will make all heads turn.
