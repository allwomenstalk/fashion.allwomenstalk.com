# 7 Chic Jumpsuits to Rock This Season ...

Chic jumpsuits are your answer to effortless dressing this season. The key to making them work is finding the right style to suit you. From sleek tailored jumpsuits to floaty floral styles, there’s something for everybody this summer. Unlike rompers and playsuits, which are fairly casual, jumpsuits can come off as much more polished if you style them right. Check out these chic jumpsuits for the season ahead.

## 1. Black Silk Jumpsuit

![Black Silk Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_black-silk-jumpsuit_600x899.jpg)

Jumpsuits can be tricky to style but it’s hard to go wrong with a basic black version. Chic jumpsuits like this Topshop one can be dressed up or down and styled in all sorts of different ways. Team it with a tailored blazer and heels for a polished nighttime look or with colour pop accessories for a more trend based look. While this silk jumpsuit is pretty expensive at $160, you can find similar, budget-friendly buys at Forever 21 and ASOS.

## 2. Ornate Printed Jumpsuit

![Ornate Printed Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_ornate-printed-jumpsuit_600x732.jpg)

Wow crowds in a funky printed jumpsuit this season. This one from Forever 21 features an eye-catching geometric print and will only set you back around $30. The collared, button-down top also gives it a more polished feel. This jumpsuit would look great teamed with strappy sandals and a small side bag for a stylish everyday look.

## 3. Floral Strap Jumpsuit

![Floral Strap Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_floral-strap-jumpsuit_600x900.jpg)

For a truly summery feel, you can’t go past a floaty floral jumpsuit. Wear one with a floppy straw hat, fringed bag, and espadrilles for a bohemian-inspired look. This Topshop jumpsuit will set you back around $120 but you can find equally stylish and more affordable buys at Modcloth, Nasty Gal, and ASOS.

## 4. Faded Denim Jumpsuit

![Faded Denim Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_faded-denim-jumpsuit_600x732.jpg)

If there’s one way to describe summer dressing this season, it’s all denim everything! Seriously, it’s like the fashion world can’t get enough of denim at the moment. Rock a fashion forward look in a denim jumpsuit like this one from Forever 21. Avoid looking like a mechanic by teaming your denim jumpsuit with slick shoes and accessories. Check out Nasty Gal and Bebe for more denim jumpsuits.

## 5. Tailored White Jumpsuit

![Tailored White Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_tailored-white-jumpsuit_600x900.jpg)

Another big trend this season is for all-white outfits. Work an all-white look this season in a chic white jumpsuit. Team it with neutral accessories during the day or dress it up with big statement jewellery at night. This jumpsuit is from Topshop and you can find more styles by Bebe and Victoria’s Secret.

## 6. Colour Block Jumpsuit

![Colour Block Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_colour-block-jumpsuit_600x900.jpg)

For a bolder look, try a colour blocked jumpsuit. This one by Tibi features sorbet shades, making it the perfect piece of clothing for summer parties and outdoor events. This particular jumpsuit would look great worn with a crisp white blazer and nude pointed pumps. It is expensive at a little over $400, but you can find more budget-friendly buys from Forever 21 and ASOS.

## 7. Paisley Print Jumpsuit

![Paisley Print Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_paisley-print-jumpsuit_407x569.jpg)

Paisley print is coming back in a big way this season. It can be a pretty full on print, so a black and white printed jumpsuit like this one from Mango is a great way to make the look work. When wearing paisley, keep the rest of your look fairly minimal and contemporary rather than opting for a boho-glam look. Also check out River Island and Etoile Isabel Marant for more paisley jumpsuit inspiration.

These are just a few styles of jumpsuits to try out this season. Are you a fan of the jumpsuit? Which of these jumpsuits do you like best?
