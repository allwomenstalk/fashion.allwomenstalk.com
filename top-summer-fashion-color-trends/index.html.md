# Top 8 Summer 2012 Fashion Color Trends ...

Summer fashion 2012 fashion color trends cover almost the entire rainbow. When it comes to 2012 summer fashion color trends, there’s something to suit everyone. Color trends are usually determined by what features prominently in the corresponding seasons’ designer runway collections. If you’ve seen ‘The Devil Wears Prada’ you’ll probably remember the scene where Meryl Streep’s character explains the trickle down effect of color trends from runways to department stores. Take look at some summer fashion color trends for 2012, below.

## 1. Mint

One of the most on-trend colors for summer is mint. Designer brand Acne released a range of mint colored clothing that quickly became lusted after by fashionistas the world over. Mint is fresh and fun, and looks great paired with neutrals and faded denim.

## 2. Orange

Shades of orange from cool corals to vibrant tangerines are where it’s at this summer. This summer fashion 2012 fashion color trend was seen all over the runways from Marc by Marc Jacobs to Bottega Veneta.

## 3. Ocean Blues

All things aquatic are trending for summer, so incorporate this into your wardrobe by wearing shades of blue. From striking cobalt to aquamarine, shades of blue featured prominently on the runways of designers such as Vera Wang, Elie Saab, and Proenza Schouler.

## 4. Yellow

Citrus colors are a popular choice this summer. If orange just doesn’t do it for you, you could always try yellow. From pale yellows to bright lemon colors, this is one of the cheerier summer fashion 2012 fashion color trends to look out for.

## 5. Hot Pink

Channel your inner Barbie girl by adding a dash of hot pink to your wardrobe this summer. From floor-length gowns at Salvatore Ferragamo to playful separates at Badgley Mischka, pink was a prominent player on the runways.

## 6. White

White is a mainstay color when it comes to summer fashion color tends for 2012. It’s great for teaming with both pastels and bright colors. Alternatively, take a cue from the runways of Fendi and Preen and cut a striking figure in an all-[white outfit](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-an-all-white-outfit/).

## 7. Caramel

With all this color around, you need neutrals to anchor your look. White is one on-trend choice, as mentioned previously, but another neutral you should take into consideration is caramel. Caramel tones look great teamed with pastel color palettes as well as colors such as hot pink and red.

## 8. Red

Stick to primary colors and go for a bright red hue this summer. As far as summer fashion 2012 fashion color trends go, this one has longevity and is more of a timeless choice. Red hues were popular with designers such as Marc by Marc Jacobs, Nina Ricci, and Emilio Pucci.

These are just a few summer fashion 2012 fashion color trends for you to consider. When you choose a color to add to your wardrobe, it’s always important to first and foremost consider what colors suit you best. Out of these summer fashion color trends for 2012, what colors will you be wearing?
