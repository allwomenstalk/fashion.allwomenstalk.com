# 9 Stunning Designer Clothing Sale Bargains You Wont Want to Miss

**Designer Clothing Sales** give fashion lovers the chance to bag amazing bargains. Sure, you might think that high-end clothes are overpriced in the first place, especially if they can be reduced by hundreds of dollars. But if you want to invest in quality pieces, a [designer clothing sale](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-shopping-at-designer-sales/) can give you the opportunity to do so at a much more affordable price. Here are some gorgeous finds from recent designer sales …

## 1. Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Dress

![Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/1_vivienne-westwood-anglomania-dress_400x600.jpg)

Price: £109.50 (approx $170) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tflFjz)

If you’re a fan of [Vivienne Westwood’s](https://www.viviennewestwood.com/) signature tartan garments, snap up these designer bargains when you find them. This smart grey piece is more subtle than her usual bold colors and incredibly practical since it's hand-washable. You’d get so much wear out of a piece like this, making it a great addition to your collection of [high street fashion finds](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/high-street-fashion-finds/) and designer steals.

## 2. Diane Von Furstenberg Cape

![Diane Von Furstenberg Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/dvf-b-s3209277t11_san_1-765x1024_765x1024.jpg)

Price: £260 (approx $400) at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Best known for her iconic wrap dresses, [Diane von Furstenberg](https://www.dvf.com/) has created a stunning twist on the classic raincoat. This cape would see you through the changing seasons for years, offering that timeless style reminiscent of film stars like Grace Kelly, but with a modern slant.

## 3. Yves St Laurent Sunglasses

![Yves St Laurent Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/3_yves-st-laurent-sunglasses_400x185.jpg)

Price: £126 (approx $195) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Ux3Uu2)

Even in the designer clothing sales, [Yves St Laurent](https://www.ysl.com/) pieces can sometimes feel out of reach. These cool sunglasses, however, are the perfect gateway treat. Available in classic black or ruby red, they'll make you feel like an absolute star every time you wear them. Just be careful not to leave them behind!

## 4. Nina Ricci Taffeta Dress

![Nina Ricci Taffeta Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/4_nina-ricci-taffeta-dress_400x600.jpg)

Price: £1,552 (approx $2,400) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tEIOw5)

At a whopping £1,500 (even at its designer clothing sale price) this sparkly dress from [Nina Ricci](https://www.ninaricci.com/) may not strictly qualify as a "bargain" for everyone, but isn’t it just gorgeous? It’s the kind of dress that’s so beautiful it feels like a work of art.

## 5. Vanessa Bruno Athé Dress

![Vanessa Bruno Athé Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/5_vanessa-bruno-ath-eacute-dress_400x600.jpg)

Price: £85.50 (approx $133) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/gjyZac)

If the Nina Ricci dress is out of reach, consider a less showy but equally lovely option. This Vanessa Bruno dress is a similar color but skips the sequins for a more versatile look. It features beautiful detailing and is a great example of how to [save money on clothes](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-save-money-on-clothes/) without sacrificing style.

## 6. Theory Open-Knit Sweater

![Theory Open-Knit Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/6_theory-open-knit-sweater_400x600.jpg)

Price: £106 (approx $165) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/HtywFP)

Open-knit sweaters look fabulous with a plain vest underneath in a contrasting color. They're also perfect for when you need a light cover-up during those transitional weather days. This teal shade adds a unique splash of color to your wardrobe without being too overwhelming.

## 7. Lulu Frost Turquoise & Diamante Bracelet

![Lulu Frost Turquoise & Diamante Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/7_lulu-frost-turquoise-diamante-bracelet_400x426.jpg)

Price: £104 (approx $160) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tjLxxa)

Adding a lovely piece of jewelry to your wardrobe can instantly elevate your look. Just one or two special items can make your other garments pop, and this turquoise and diamante bracelet will go wonderfully with a summer wardrobe or even a little black dress.

## 8. Lisa Maree Crochet Bikini

![Lisa Maree Crochet Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/8_lisa-maree-crochet-bikini_400x600.jpg)

Price: £42 (approx $65) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/z7083O)

Have you ever wanted one of those gorgeous crochet bikinis that celebrities are always spotted wearing? This sale find is a great way to get the look without spending a fortune. Just imagine how amazing you’ll look lounging by the pool in this!

## 9. Lanvin Pumps

![Lanvin Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/9_lanvin-pumps_400x376.jpg)

Price: £318 (approx $495) at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ivUvuD)

Every woman should have at least one pair of utterly fabulous shoes, and these Lanvin pumps fit the bill perfectly. They are mostly classic black but are finished with a large bow at the back to add that signature designer touch. Plus, they’re designed for comfort with a built-in platform, so you won't wear your feet out!

A **designer clothing sale** can offer some wonderful bargains if you choose carefully. As the saying goes, one high-quality piece can often be a better investment than several cheaper items, especially if you get more wear out of it. Do you love a good designer sale? What’s the best bargain you’ve ever snapped up?
