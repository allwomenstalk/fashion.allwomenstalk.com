# 10 Trendy Ways to Wear Belts ...

How to Wear Belts isn't something most women think about when putting together an outfit. However, since a trendy belt is the accessory du jour, it pays to learn how to wear belts not just properly, but prettily. I know what you're thinking: it just goes around your waist, right? Nuh uh. That's not the case at all. On the contrary, by changing things up, you can breathe new life into any ensemble. So if you've ever wanted to learn how to wear belts a little differently, read on!

## 1. With a Dress

The best piece of advice on how to wear belts right now is to wear it with a dress – any dress. Retro 1950s dresses look great with belts. In fact, any kind of fitted dress looks stunning with this simple little accessory. Some look better with thin belts, some look better with thick ones; it all depends on what you like best.

## 2. Over a Sweater

Wearing a belt over your clothing, not just your pants, has actually become quite popular. If you're wearing an oversized sweater or even a flowing cardigan over a tee shirt, you can hold it all together with a snazzy belt. The look is absolutely fab and quite unique as well – it's not something you would usually think about doing.

## 3. With a Sweater Dress

Back to the subject of dresses, one great tip on how to wear belts is to pair one with a sweater dress. This look is incredibly trendy right now. Generally, you can get away with wearing really cool belts. Try something with a lot of detailing, or else go with something simple but wide.

## 4. Over a Long Shirt

Button down shirts also look fantastic with belts. Make sure the shirt comes down past your hips, though. It can be fitted, or voluminous. In that case, you can arrange your shirt so that it bells out above or beneath the belt.

## 5. Beneath Your Breasts

This has to be my absolute favorite tip on how to wear belts. Do you like wearing A-line clothing? I love it! A-lines are so flattering, and they hide a multitude of sins. By wearing your belt up beneath your breasts, you can instantly transform any outfit, whether it's a shirt, sweater, or dress, into an adorable A-line masterpiece!

## 6. Use a Scarf

And who says you have to wear a proper belt anyway? Instead, try a scarf. Lots of women are doing this now, and for good reason. Scarves are comfortable, colorful, and you can arrange them around your body in any number of inventive ways.

## 7. Knotted

If you're looking to learn about how to wear belts in a trendier style, this is it. Knotted belts are popping up all over the place. You just have to remember buy one several sizes larger than you actually need, so there's room for the actual tying.

## 8. Doubled up

A lot of people are also wearing their belts doubled up. You can do this in a couple of ways. For instance, you can actually wear two belts, perhaps the same or in complementary styles. You can also, again, buy a belt that is much too big for you, and actually wrap it twice around your waist or hips.

## 9. Low on Your Hips

Most women wear their belts around their waist, but since hip huggers are so popular, why do that? If you're looking for a fashionable tip on how to wear belts, rearrange the norm. Wear yours low on your hips, highlight your curves, and employ an original style!

## 10. Wear a Ribbon

Just as scarves make great belts, ribbons do as well. Thin or thick, braided, twined, or multi-colored, ribbons work great as belts. You can really highlight your individuality this way, and add some color and a touch of femininity to your outfit.

I hope these tips on how to wear belts has been helpful! Who knew that belts could be so versatile? There are so many ways you can fashion these fun accessories that it really pays to know how to wear belts in the trendiest, most stylish ways. Do you have any other fun tips on how to wear belts?
