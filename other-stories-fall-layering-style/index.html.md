# Elevate Your Autumn Wardrobe with Colorful Knits and Leather ...

If fall is about transformation, then my closet is ready for a major shift. There’s something about this season that feels like an invitation to experiment—think playful color-blocks, tactile knits, and a little attitude. That’s exactly why I found myself scrolling the [@andotherstories](https://www.instagram.com/andotherstories/) feed late last Sunday, swooning over their latest drop. Autumn brings out my inner stylist—I want to feel cozy, but also look like I could wander into an art gallery at any moment.

You know that feeling when you slip into something that just gets you? That’s what & Other Stories does best: they blend unexpected fabric combos and colors that shouldn’t work together… but totally do! The result is a closet that’s energized, dimensional, and anything but basic. I’m already dreaming about mixing lightweight jersey with textured knits for my next coffee run or impromptu dinner. There’s a freshness—a kind of bold, almost Parisian nonchalance—in every piece. Let’s dive into my favorite looks and styling ideas for this fall, inspired by the latest from & Other Stories.

## Color Play and Fabric Contrasts: Where Fall Begins

![](https://pub-f8777d80e41b4c19bf8050f85c3ea0f1.r2.dev/ig/andotherstories/DO8oQNEiKHA/img_02.jpg)

Is there anything more satisfying than a pop of unexpected color? The goldenrod yellow jersey top instantly grabs attention, especially when paired with a high-waisted black leather skirt. The skirt’s embossed texture catches the light with every step, making the whole outfit feel dynamic and a little daring. Carrying a chocolate brown leather tote, topped with a silky printed scarf, this look is pure autumn attitude. It’s easy to imagine heading from your day job straight into cocktail hour—no wardrobe change required.

What I love about this combo is the subtle rebellion: yellow isn’t a ‘typical’ fall color, but here it’s a declaration. The skirt’s structure keeps things polished, while the slouchy bag and scarf add a touch of softness. If you want to channel cool-girl vibes, this is it.

## Jersey Basics with a Twist

![](https://pub-f8777d80e41b4c19bf8050f85c3ea0f1.r2.dev/ig/andotherstories/DO8oQNEiKHA/img_01.jpg)

Lightweight jersey is the unsung hero of transitional weather. This burnt sienna long-sleeve top isn’t just practical—it’s flattering, hugging all the right places without feeling clingy. Tucked into a deep burgundy corduroy mini, it’s the kind of outfit that’s equal parts retro and now. The black belt with a gold oval buckle adds just enough shine, while a chunky beaded necklace brings a little edge.

For under $60, this jersey top is my new layering must-have. I’d throw it under a slip dress or even an oversized blazer when the air turns crisp. The skirt? It’s a perfect $79 investment for anyone who loves a little ‘90s nostalgia with their PSLs.

## The Art of Oversized Knits

![](https://pub-f8777d80e41b4c19bf8050f85c3ea0f1.r2.dev/ig/andotherstories/DO8oQNEiKHA/img_03.jpg)

Honestly, nothing makes me happier than an oversized sweater. Especially when it’s a dreamy lavender shade, slouchy in all the right places. Pairing it with a black faux-croc midi skirt is pure genius. The textures play off each other—softness meets structure, cozy meets sleek. Add in a bright red suede tote for a dash of drama, and you’ve got a look that’s ready for anything.

This is the outfit I want for lazy brunches, gallery strolls, or just curling up with a book at my favorite café. & Other Stories really nails that balance between comfort and style—you feel totally put-together, even if you’re just running errands. The loafers and grey socks add a preppy twist, grounding the whimsy of the lavender knit.

## Bold Red Knits and Corduroy Confidence

![](https://pub-f8777d80e41b4c19bf8050f85c3ea0f1.r2.dev/ig/andotherstories/DO8oQNEiKHA/img_04.jpg)

Red is one of those colors that never really goes out of style, but this season it feels especially powerful. The ribbed red knit, with its subtle diagonal pattern, pairs perfectly with the same burgundy corduroy mini seen earlier. It’s proof that you don’t need a ton of pieces—you just need the right ones that mix and match seamlessly.

What makes this outfit pop is the accessories. A chocolate brown faux-fur tote (big enough to fit my entire life), black tassel loafers, and ankle socks—it’s preppy, a little quirky, and 100% fall-ready. For $45, the ribbed top feels luxe, and the skirt continues to be my MVP.

## Mixing It Up: How to Style & Other Stories Pieces for Every Occasion

Here’s the secret: every piece from & Other Stories’ fall collection feels intentionally designed for mixing and matching. Try a jersey top under a chunky cardigan, or layer a textured knit over a silk slip dress. The color palette—burnt oranges, soft lilacs, bold reds, and classic blacks—makes building outfits genuinely fun.

Want to add a little extra dimension? Play with accessories. Scarves, chunky jewelry, and unexpected socks can transform basics into statement looks. And don’t be afraid to experiment with color pairings you might not have tried before—sometimes, a combo like lavender and red is exactly the twist your wardrobe needs.
