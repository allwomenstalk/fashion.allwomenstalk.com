# 8 Luxe Sweat Shirts ...

Sweat Shirts may not necessarily be at the forefront of cutting-edge fashion, but they’re an old favourite. There’s never been a better time for comfort dressing, and this season you can get away with layering your sweats for a cool, street-smart look. Put away the faded and worn sweat shirts and treat yourself to a luxe version of this wardrobe staple. Take a look at some very luxe sweat shirts below.

## 1. Jason Wu Lace Trimmed Wool Sweater

![Jason Wu Lace Trimmed Wool Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/zi/urdxln5554c92ff7ae343919908791_400x527.jpg)

**Price:** $1195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pAXuLv)
This luxury sweat shirt has the luxury price tag to match. The plain grey sweater has been given an upmarket twist in the form of baroque-inspired lace-trimmed sleeves. It is made from 100% wool and has a round neck and ribbed trims. Dress this sweat shirt up by teaming it with a pair of sleek leather leggings and a pair of classic pumps.

## 2. Clu Satin-Trimmed Cotton-Terry Sweat Shirt

![Clu Satin-Trimmed Cotton-Terry Sweat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/rx/a3z3a46z54c92ff8e6397765271707_400x557.jpg)

**Price:** $260.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/WPwfNI)
When looking for dressy sweat shirts, keep an eye out for luxe trims. This taupe marl sweat shirt features brushed satin trims at the hem. It also has a V-shaped insert at the neck and cutout detailing at the hem. Team this chic sweat shirt with some denim shorts, brogues, and a fedora for a chic weekend look.

## 3. Zucca Draped Pullover Sweat Shirt

![Zucca Draped Pullover Sweat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/bs/m6djcu4r54c92ffa36222781666353_400x616.jpg)

**Price:** $398.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The draping on this sweat shirt adds interest to a basic design. This lightweight sweat shirt has batwing sleeves, dropped shoulders, and a U-neck with unfinished edge. It also has a contrasting unfinished edge across the centre at the reverse. This draped sweat shirt would look great with pastel denim.

## 4. Acne Doli Cropped Pullover

![Acne Doli Cropped Pullover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/rd/qapo999q54c92ffb4e374167762964_400x439.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Cropped garments have been all the rage lately. Work this look into your fall outfit with this Acne pullover. This pullover has a boatneck design with ¾ length sleeves. It sits at the hips and has a relaxed fit. Wear it with high waisted jeans, lace-up boots, and a circle scarf for a street-smart look.

## 5. T by Alexander Wang Cotton-Fleece Sweat Shirt

![T by Alexander Wang Cotton-Fleece Sweat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ej/io/tamrpmvy54c92ffc6d7b9112493616_400x522.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/7db3uG)
Alexander Wang is well known for creating luxe staples out of basic garments such as sweat shirts. This version from his diffusion line features subtle zip detailing at the shoulders. It also has a rolled hem and seam detailing. It would look great teamed with a camel coloured suede mini skirt and wedge ankle boots.

## 6. 3.1 Phillip Lim Silk Contrast Sweat Shirt

_ **Price:** $375.00 at barneys.com_
This sweat shirt has a sheer body. As such, it may not be the most ideal garment for keeping you warm. At least it still looks stylish! It is made from silk blend fabric and has a high round neck, ruche detailing on the sleeves, and a cotton pocket square. Layer it over a neutral tank top and team it with black jeans for a fuss-free look.

## 7. Topshop Grey Marl Sequin Heart Embellished Sweat Shirt

![Topshop Grey Marl Sequin Heart Embellished Sweat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ji/ws/mvsuhs4d54c92ffebc248314964086_400x556.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/jzASJu)
This sweat shirt features all-over embellished sequin heart detailing. It has a round neck, grey marl body, and ribbed trims. The look effect of the sequin hearts is quirky and cute. These kinds of sweat shirts would look great paired with coloured denim and bold accessories.

## 8. T by Alexander Wang Cropped Sweat Shirt

![T by Alexander Wang Cropped Sweat Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/my/gjuotieo54c92fffd62f8394551076_400x597.jpg)

**Price:** $145.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cropped sweat shirt also has a funnel neck design and buttoned front placket. It is made from a cotton terry fabric and comes in a light heather grey colour. It has a relaxed fit and sits just above the hips. Channel a sporty chic look and team it with some burgundy coloured slouchy trousers and tan leather accessories.

Sweat shirts don’t always have to be one of those things you wear around the house. These sweat shirts can easily be incorporated into a range of stylish outfits. Team them with luxe leathers or keep the look entirely utilitarian. Which of these sweat shirts do you like best?
