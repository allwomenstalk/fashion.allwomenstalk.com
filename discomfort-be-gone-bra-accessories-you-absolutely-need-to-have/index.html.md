# Discomfort Be Gone! Bra Accessories You Absolutely Need to Have ...

Whether you like it or not, all of us women are stuck wearing bras--at least until the work day is over and we can toss them onto our floors. Even though bras aren't always the most comfortable item of clothing, there are things you can do to make them more bearable. So if you've been having some issues with your wardrobe, here are some lifesaving bra accessories you absolutely need to buy for yourself:

## 1. Racer Back Bra Clips

![Racer Back Bra Clips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/g7/nljkhn8955311316bc91c299804472_600x585.jpg)

Don't you hate when those pesky bra straps don't line up with the straps on your tank top? Well, a racer back bra clip will pull both sides of your bra together to keep the straps out of your way. So if you're wearing a certain style of shirt that requires the straps to rest in the center, this clip will save you from having to buy a bra with an entirely different design.

## 2. Silicone Bra-strap Pads

![Silicone Bra-strap Pads](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/sh/uc0cam1i553113155f161442950407_500x500.jpg)

Do your bra straps dig into your skin? You won't have to worry about that anymore, because a bra-strap pad will make sure that you don't feel any pain. They're easy to apply, and they don't come off easily, so they're exactly what you need when you don't feel like buying a new bra with a better fit.

## 3. Nipple Covers

![Nipple Covers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/oe/pbu5bc4s55311318da831668856309_600x659.jpg)

When you wear a super tight shirt, sometimes it's possible for the world to see your nipples through the fabric. That's why you should buy these nipple covers. They're easy to put on, and they'll assure that no one can see through your shirt, no matter how sheer it is.

## 4. Bra Extender

![Bra Extender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/s5/jsksibj65531131862fe7069473360_600x472.jpg)

If you've outgrown your bra, but don't want to toss it away just yet, you can buy a bra extender. All it does is give you a few extra row of clips, so that your bra won't be as tight against your skin. It's a great solution for when you don't want to part with your favorite bra.

## 5. Bra Wash Bag

![Bra Wash Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/ta/jursw2mj553113191ae6d976316902_560x457.jpg)

If your sick of your bras getting tangled up with all your other clothing items, you should invest in a bra wash bag. That way, you can keep your bras separate from everything else. It's a great way to reduce tangles and stretching.

## 6. Bra Liner

![Bra Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uy/p4/iistyzgt5531131678965595917243_500x500.jpg)

Bra liners have multiple purposes. If the bottom of your bra digs into your skin, the liner will help protect you from pain. However, it will also collect whatever moisture and sweat your body builds up. That way, you won't have to worry about any embarrassing boob sweat soaking through your shirt.

## 7. Fashion Tape

![Fashion Tape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/a4/vfyxqya65531131a540b1539463853_600x391.jpg)

If you don't want your loose shirt to reveal your bra, you can use fashion tape to secure your blouse in the right spot. Of course, fashion tape is useful for all types of issues. You can use it when the edge of your belt won't stay down, when you want your scarf to stay in a certain position, or when there are gaps between the buttons on your shirt.

You don't want your skin to be irritated all day long, which is why you should buy whatever products will help you feel more comfortable in your clothes and more confident in your appearance. Do you own any bra accessories that you would suggest to other women?
