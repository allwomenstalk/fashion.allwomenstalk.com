# 7 Easy\-to\-Wear Printed Tunic Dresses Youll Want to Slip into This Summer ...

Printed tunic dresses are your go-to pieces for effortless summer dressing. Sometimes getting dressed in the summer heat doesn’t always leave much room to experiment with your style. Luckily, this season’s slew of printed tunic dresses will be the solution to your summer outfit woes. The easy-to-wear design of tunic dresses makes them perfect for relaxed summer dressing, while a statement print can trick people into thinking you’ve actually made an effort. Check out these breezy printed tunic dresses to slip into this summer.

## 1. Topshop Scarf Print Tunic Dress

![Topshop Scarf Print Tunic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mk/hp/ohfkfnc6_600x875.jpg)

Printed tunic dresses like this daring Topshop number really make a statement. Featuring a bold yellow and green scarf print, this tunic dress is instantly eye catching. It has a really sporty feel to it, making it ideal for teaming with sneakers for a relaxed street smart look. Alternatively, you could pair it with strappy slides and oversized sunglasses for a vacation-ready outfit.

## 2. Equipment Leopard Print Mini Dress

![Equipment Leopard Print Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fi/ta/r5hocp42_600x900.jpg)

The neutral leopard print on this Equipment tunic dress makes it the perfect understated everyday dress. It has a round neck, elbow length sleeves, and two patch pockets. This dress could easily be styled up or down, depending on the occasion. Wear it with a floppy hat and sandals for a dressed-down summertime look or style it up with block-heeled sandals and metallic jewellery.

## 3. Dorothy Perkins Stripe Denim Tunic Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Stripe Denim Tunic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/i5/h7x7yprj_600x793.jpg)

If you like your dresses with a little bit more structure, choose one made from a thicker fabric. Denim is always a favourite and this tunic dress features a striking navy and white striped pattern. It’s a classic style of dress that could easily become a wardrobe staple. Work a polished look and team it with ballet flats and a compact, ladylike handbag.

## 4. Topshop Geo Print Tunic Dress

![Topshop Geo Print Tunic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/np/ko/j6xj99hh_600x876.jpg)

This Topshop tunic dress comes in an on-trend monochrome geo print. It also comes in a summer-friendly sleeveless design. The busy print is eye-catching but not overpowering thanks to the simple black and white colour scheme. Work an athletic-inspired look by teaming this dress with sporty pool slides and a baseball cap.

## 5. Etro Printed Silk-Crepe Mini Dress

The print on this Etro dress is just a little bit psychedelic. It features summery coral and mint hues and has a relaxed, loose fit. It’s a dress for all occasions and would look just as stylish teamed with flats as it would with a pair of heels.

## 6. McQ Alexander McQueen Leopard Print Dress

![McQ Alexander McQueen Leopard Print Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/jn/t78an58v_600x882.jpg)

This McQ Alexander McQueen tunic dress takes leopard print to the next level. It features a bold red, white, and black digital leopard print. Dress it down during the day with flats and an oversized tote and style it up at night with heels and a compact clutch.

## 7. Topshop Folk Panel Print Tunic Dress

![Topshop Folk Panel Print Tunic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/v5/nwxftno6_600x880.jpg)

The print on this Topshop dress offers up a bit of an optical illusion. The boho-inspired folk print gives the illusion of peeling away from the middle of the dress. But it’s really just the magic of an eye-catching print. Since this dress is quite busy, keep the rest of your accessories to a minimum.

Summer dressing would be a breeze with these printed tunic dresses. Just slip one on and you’re well on your way to one striking summer outfit. How would you style these printed tunic dresses?
