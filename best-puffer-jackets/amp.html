11 Best Puffer Jackets ...

Alice + Olivia Hooded Puffer Jacket • J.Crew Wintress Puffer • Canada Goose Kensington Parka • The North Face Miss Metro Parka • Mackage Eileen Coat • More ...

By Lisa

Now that fall is in full swing, I bet you’re wondering where the best puffer jackets are. I love puffer jackets because I’ve never worn one that wasn’t extremely warm, comfy and just screamed fall. If you think all puffer jackets are big, bulky and not very stylish, think again! I’ll prove to you that there are such things as stylish and comfortable puffer jackets with my picks for the best puffer jackets this season.

1. Alice + Olivia Hooded Puffer Jacket

Alice + Olivia makes one the chicest and best puffer jackets around. This jacket is perfect for the lady who doesn’t like to sacrifice style for comfort and knows the beauty is in the details. This hooded puffer jacket features an uneven hem and quilting that take this cold weather staple to the next level.

Price: $698 at shopbop.com

2. J.Crew Wintress Puffer

J. Crew’s Wintress Puffer features luxurious goose down that provides tons of warmth with a sleek silhouette. Made with a removable hood , it features welt pockets and a belt. It hits at mid-thigh for a chic and fitted look that’s perfect for keeping warm during outdoor activities.

Price: $268 at jcrew.com

3. Canada Goose Kensington Parka

Here’s another goose down jacket that’s big on comfort and style. The Kensington Parka has lots of fun details to it such as a removable fur-trimmed hood with fleece lining, side vents with snap closure and recessed knit cuffs. This lightweight jacket is sure to keep you toasty without the dreaded puffy marshmallow look.

Price: $695 at bloomingdales.com

4. The North Face Miss Metro Parka

The North Face is one of the first brands that come to mind when I think of puffer jackets. The Miss Metro Parka combines function and flair with the lightweight look and 600-fill down. You can face the cold weather with confidence in this sleek and chic jacket that’s fully insulated to keep you warm and dry.

Price: $320 at thenorthface.com

5. Mackage Eileen Coat

Mackage's Eileen Coat is military-inspired and made to withstand the coldest of weather without skimping on style. Keeping with the military trend, you will find epaulets, buttons and officer stripes on this cozy coat that will totally elevate your outerwear look.

Price: $690 at bloomingdales.com

6. Soia & Kyo Kisha Hooded down Jacket

You can count on Soia & Kyo’s coats to always have extraordinary details and styling. Take the Kisha down jacket for instance. It’s lightweight, has a front zip closure, ribbed cuffs, an oversized hood and is water repellant so Mother Nature can’t put a damper on your day or your clothes.

Price: $275 at soiakyo.com

7. Burberry Brit Metcalfe Puffer Coat

The Metcalfe Puffer Coat adds an elegant feel to the hardworking puffer jacket. The stitched panels give this quilted coat a contoured look so you’re always sleek and sophisticated. With this breathable down coat, you stay warm and snug without feeling heavy or weighed down.

Price: $795 at bloomingdales.com

8. Uniqlo Ultra Light down Coat

Uniqlo’s Ultra Light Down Coat is not only cozy and comfy, it’s super lightweight so you can fold it up and take it with you in the small carry pouch that comes with it. The flexibility of the coat makes it ideal for travel and the variety of colors and designs makes it easy to coordinate with your clothes.

Price: $89.90-$99.90 at uniqlo.com

9. Moncler Moka Lacquer Quilted Mid Length

Sleek, shiny and sophisticated are just a few words that come to mind when you see Moncler’s Moka Lacquer Quilted Mid Length. The streamlined silhouette makes it easy to stay cozy and chic while you face the harsh winter weather.

Price: $1095 at bloomingdales.com

10. Add down Belted down Coat

ADD Down's Belted Down Coat is a classic with a twist. It feature an seatbelt style belt, pillow collar, two zippered pockets and elasticized cuffs for maximum warmth. This coat is super light and fitted so you’ll keep warm without the bulky look or feel.

Price: $460 at bloomingdales.com

11. DKNY down Hooded Maxi Coat

DKNY welcomes winter with a sassy and stylish puffer. This long coat will keep you warm from your head to your knees. The removable faux fur trimmed hood, zip pockets and snap plackets lend a fashionable and streamlined touch to the versatile coat.

Price: $198.60 at bloomingdales.com

Although these coats have similarities, all of them have their own distinct personality with its various details and accents. Treat yourself to a warm, cozy puffer jacket this winter so you can stay fashionable while you walk in your very own winter wonderland!

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes puffer jackets a great choice for winter?
Puffer jackets provide excellent insulation by trapping heat, making them warm and cozy for cold weather.
Are puffer jackets suitable for everyday wear?
Yes, puffer jackets are versatile and can be styled casually or dressed up for various occasions.
How do I choose the right size for a puffer jacket?
Pick a size that allows layering underneath without feeling too bulky or restrictive.
What materials should I look for in a quality puffer jacket?
Look for jackets with down or synthetic insulation and a water-resistant outer shell for durability.
Can puffer jackets be flattering for all body types?
Absolutely! Choose styles and lengths that complement your figure, like fitted waists or longer cuts.
How do I care for and clean my puffer jacket?
Most puffer jackets can be machine washed on a gentle cycle; always check the care label for specific instructions.
Are lightweight puffer jackets warm enough for freezing temperatures?
Lightweight puffers provide warmth for mild to moderately cold days, but thicker options are better for extreme cold.
Can puffer jackets be worn in wet weather?
Many puffer jackets are water-resistant, but for heavy rain, it's best to pair them with a waterproof shell.
What colors are popular for women’s puffer jackets this season?
Neutrals like black and beige are timeless, while bold colors like red and metallics add a trendy touch.
Are puffer jackets eco-friendly?
Some brands offer puffer jackets made with recycled materials and ethical down, so look for those if sustainability matters to you.
