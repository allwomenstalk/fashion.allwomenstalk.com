# 8 Affordable Spring Trends ...

**Spring Trends** come in all shapes and sizes this year. From drop waist silhouettes to colour-pop accessories, knowing what spring trends are in this season can make or break an outfit. Keeping up with the trends, however, can be a costly exercise. If you’re on a budget, the easiest way to work spring trends into your wardrobe is through the use of accessories. You can also pick a range of on-trend clothes thanks to on-the-pulse fashion store chains. Take a look at some affordable spring trends below.

## 1. White Midi Dress - Topshop Button Front Midi Dress

![White Midi Dress - Topshop Button Front Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/1_white-midi-dress-topshop-button-front-midi-dress_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.asos.com/us/topshop/?ctaref=ts%7Cww%7Cus%7Cweb)
Spring fashion is heavily focused on light colours. A white dress is the perfect canvas for experimenting with a range of **spring trends**. It can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

## 2. Coloured Bag - ASOS Slot through Portfolio Clutch

![Coloured Bag - ASOS Slot through Portfolio Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/2_coloured-bag-asos-slot-through-portfolio-clutch_560x375.jpg)

**Price:** $35.81 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/K7BV1B).
A coloured bag is a must-have accessory for spring. This oversized clutch by ASOS comes in a cheery yellow colour and would be an excellent way to wear this **spring trend**. Team it with a fresh white dress or cropped pants for a chic spring look.

## 3. Minimalist Sandals - Topshop Houpla Leather Sandals

![Minimalist Sandals - Topshop Houpla Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/3_minimalist-sandals-topshop-houpla-leather-sandals_560x237.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://www.asos.com/us/topshop/?ctaref=ts%7Cww%7Cus%7Cweb)
Lately there’s been a focus on minimalist footwear. Update your spring footwear collection with these simple strap sandals. A basic pair of tan sandals will see you through a range of occasions.

## 4. Full Skirt - Topshop Pleated Skater Skirt

![Full Skirt - Topshop Pleated Skater Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/4_full-skirt-topshop-pleated-skater-skirt_569x560.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://www.asos.com/us/topshop/?ctaref=ts%7Cww%7Cus%7Cweb)
Full, skater style skirts are a chic choice for spring. This one comes in a light tan colour and is perfect for teaming with spring’s key colour palettes of pastels and neutrals. This skirt would look great with a plain white t-shirt and mint coloured accessories.

## 5. Statement Sunglasses - Pieces Oblong Sunglasses

![Statement Sunglasses - Pieces Oblong Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/5_statement-sunglasses-pieces-oblong-sunglasses_560x272.jpg)

**Price:** $25.07 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/bbv0L1).
A pair of statement making sunglasses is one of the hottest **spring trends**. It’s also one of the easiest to work into your wardrobe. These Pieces sunglasses have an oversized opaque frame with a slight cat-eye silhouette.

## 6. Denim - Topshop Moto Fitted Denim Shirt

![Denim - Topshop Moto Fitted Denim Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/6_denim-topshop-moto-fitted-denim-shirt_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.asos.com/us/topshop/?ctaref=ts%7Cww%7Cus%7Cweb)
You can’t go wrong with a classic styled denim shirt. A light denim shirt is a great pairing for spring’s fresh colour palettes. Work a stylish denim on denim look by teaming a blue denim shirt with coloured cut-offs or jeans.

## 7. Cuff Bracelets - ASOS Smooth Double Cuffs

![Cuff Bracelets - ASOS Smooth Double Cuffs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/7_cuff-bracelets-asos-smooth-double-cuffs_560x302.jpg)

**Price:** $32.32 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/JRRwNQ)
Adorn your wrists with some sleek metal cuffs this spring. This **spring trend** is a quick and effective way to update your everyday jewellery. The look is quite warrior inspired. Wear a cuff on each wrist or one at a time.

## 8. Pastel Pants – Topshop High Waisted Chinos

![Pastel Pants – Topshop High Waisted Chinos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/8_pastel-pants-topshop-high-waisted-chinos4_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://www.asos.com/us/topshop/?ctaref=ts%7Cww%7Cus%7Cweb)
Pastel colours are a spring staple. Pastel was a big feature of many spring designer collections and this is set to filter into most fashion stores. An easy way to wear this **spring trend** is with a pair of pastel pants.

You don’t have to spend a fortune to keep up with covetable **spring trends**. By knowing the key colour palettes and using accessories to update your outfits, you can easily recreate some of the hottest spring trends. What spring trends are you excited for this year?
