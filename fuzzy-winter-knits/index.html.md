# 7 Fuzzy Winter Knits ...

Winter Knits come in an array of styles, colours, and price points. This season is all about playing around with different textures, and winter knits provide a great contrast to heavier materials such as leather and tweed. This season, keep an eye out for winter knits that have fuzzy textures. Wool, mohair and cashmere-blends are perfect for creating that warm, fuzzy appearance.

## 1. Topshop Knitted Fluffy Stripe Jumper

![Topshop Knitted Fluffy Stripe Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/ds/vx6z4hnq54c9329dd5ae4762581139_261x350.jpg)

**Price:** $92.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/BEnyvX)
This knitted jumper features a number of contrasting fluffy panels. The contrast of loose and tight knits creates the striped effect. This jumper is made from an alpaca and wool blend and features a high neckline and ribbed hem. Wear this jumper with burgundy coloured jeans and brogues for a cool, off-duty look.

## 2. J.Crew Betsy Wool and Cashmere-Blend Sweater

![J.Crew Betsy Wool and Cashmere-Blend Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/r8/s9zl826e54c9329eadb96389328943_257x350.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Fbcfi9)
Basic winter knits are the perfect pieces to fall back on. This cashmere-blend sweater comes in a red-brick hue and has a soft textured finish. It has a crew neck design and ribbed trims. This sweater would look great layered with a trench coat and chunky knit scarves.

## 3. Topshop Knitted Stitched Tie Back Top

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/QaUZdh)
A navy knit is one way to work a stylish look this winter. The colour is a classic, and now is the time to be making the most of the trend. This knitted sweater has a relaxed fit and features all over raised spot details. It also has a bow neck fastening at the back. Wear this with black jeans and patent shoes for a chic everyday look.

## 4. Rag & Bone Montgomery Cable Knit Pullover

_ **Price:** $355.00 at mytheresa.com_
Winter knits look great when they have a textured appearance. This cable-knit pullover has a rounded neckline and cropped silhouette. It has a heathered finish which gives it a warm, fuzzy appearance. This pullover would look great worn with a brown leather mini skirt, tights, and lace up ankle boots.

## 5. Closed Alpaca Mix Ribbed Knit Cardigan

_ **Price:** $415.00 at mytheresa.com_
A black and white striped knit is timeless. This ribbed cardigan has a very contemporary design. It has a V-shaped neck and patch pockets on the front. Wear this cardigan with this season’s must have red jeans and slip-on ankle boots.

## 6. Vanessa Bruno Athé Scoop Neck Mohair Sweater

![Vanessa Bruno Athé Scoop Neck Mohair Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/wa/auxbyvfe54c932a28297d304197276_256x350.jpg)

**Price:** $219.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This mohair-blend sweater has a multicoloured design. It has a scooped neck, raglan sleeves, and ribbed trims. It has a relaxed fit and hits below the hips. This cozy sweater would look great worn with skinny jeans, knee-length boots, and a contrasting knit scarf.

## 7. VPL Shifted Knit Pullover

![VPL Shifted Knit Pullover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/pi/bvi7gi2554c932a392c63412162237_245x337.jpg)

**Price:** $385.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This knit pullover has an interesting cropped design. It has a cropped hem at the front and a longer hem at the back. It also has a boatneck design and dropped shoulders. Like most of the winter knits on this list, the mohair blend gives this pullover a soft textured appearance. This pullover would look great teamed with [high waisted skirts](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-high-waisted-skirts/) and pants.

There are a variety of winter knits available at the moment. To create an artfully textured outfit, combine your cozy winter knits with slick leather pieces or heavy tweed fabrics. Alternatively, sheer chiffon and silk fabrics also provide an interesting contrast. Which of these winter knits do you like best?
