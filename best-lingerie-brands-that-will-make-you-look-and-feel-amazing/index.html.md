# 7 Best Lingerie Brands That Will Make You Look and Feel Amazing ...

Finding the perfect lingerie for any occasion or mood that will make you look and feel amazing can be a bit tricky when you don’t know where to start. The normal stores that carry lingerie at any local mall might satisfy your craving for bras and panties at that moment, but what about the lingerie brands that are exclusive, unique, luxurious, elegant, comfortable, and sexy? Lingerie that will make you look and feel amazing in a variety of colors, fabrics, and shapes is only a click away. With amazing reviews by product consumers and my own lingerie shopping, you will want to start your very own intimates collection.

## 1. Bordelle

![Bordelle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005631_2604_600x900.jpg)

Bordelle is easily my top favorite lingerie designer because of the intense design and construction incorporated into each garment. The details and fit are seductive with an elegant bad girl attitude, all while remaining ultra luxe and superior. The Angela dress is their signature piece that put the U.K. based brand on top of the luxury lingerie world. Giving the illusion of a perfect hourglass figure, the dress is structured from customized elastic ribbons and finished with 18k gold plated details. Bordelle has an impeccable reputation for having unique pieces that have a dominating look to them, while remaining stunningly beautiful and feminine. There is nothing better than having a unique garment that will make you look and feel amazing. Purchase Bordelle on their website or contact them at customerservice@bordelle.co.uk to find a specialized boutique that will carry the collections.

## 2. Stella McCartney

![Stella McCartney](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005700_5350_600x901.jpg)

Like her innovative and eco-friendly fashion house empire, Stella McCartney created her own lingerie brand just recently in 2012. Paying attention to detail and fit, her designs are modern and fresh with pieces made to be mixed and matched and bra straps designed to be seen. Each garment is made of organic cotton and recycled metal hardware for a beautiful lightweight feeling. Bras also come in five different bra shapes for a custom fit. The Stella McCartney lingerie line offers amazing support with slimming technology for everyday wear and comfort. You can find Stella McCartney intimates at her website, Nordstroms, Barneys, or at net-a-porter.com.

## 3. Mimi Holliday

![Mimi Holliday](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005722_2479_600x901.jpg)

Another budding U.K. brand, the Mimi Holliday range is sweet, charming, fun, and sexy. With bra sizing from AA to GG cups, this is a brand that has mastered an incredible fit with gorgeous color and print combinations. Delicate lace, silk chiffon, and adorned with the bow trio details, Mimi Holliday has a certain signature flair in their styles and designs. One of the best sellers is the silk chiffon balcony bra that offers padding in 100 percent cotton for a perfect, natural looking lift. I adore this brand because of the comfort and beautifully colored lace. Purchase Mimi Holliday at their website at [damaris.co.uk](http://www.damaris.co.uk/) or at Nordstroms, Asos.com and net-a-porter.com.

## 4. Chantelle

![Chantelle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005749_8681_600x799.jpg)

Chantelle has been in business for over 50 years, launching the "girdle that never rides up" as their first best selling product. With so much experience in lingerie and bra fitting, this french lingerie house has a large selection of bras and panties that offer incredible comfort and attractive designs. Bra sizes go up to a G cup for a more customized fit. Each collection is designed in France using super soft stretch lace, lace edging for invisibility under clothes, and specialized materials for the skin to breathe. Find Chantelle lingerie at their website, and at high-end department stores, like Nordstroms, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Bloomingdales.

## 5. Von Follies by Dita Von Teese

![Von Follies by Dita Von Teese](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005814_7117_600x900.jpg)

After much anticipation, the leading lady of burlesque has finally introduced her lingerie line! With a modern twist on vintage styles, the Von Follies line is reminiscent of a 1950's pin up girl with sophisticated touches. Velvet ribbons, eyelash lace, and control mesh are some of the qualities the garments possess, to channel your inner vixen. Von Follies offers fitted chemises, gloves, bustiers, corsets, and stretch garter belts for a unique selection. This line is fairly new with her most popular styles selling out in hours! Treat yourself to this glamorous collection at stylebop.com and asos.com.

## 6. L'Agent by Agent Provocateur

![L'Agent by Agent Provocateur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005843_0201_600x896.jpg)

Designed in collaboration with Penelope and Monica Cruz, L'Agent by Agent Provocateur is reminiscent of the signature line with a youthful attitude at more affordable prices. Launching just this year, L' Agent focuses on creating high quality garments in an array of fabrics, bright colors, and gorgeous silhouettes. Agent Provocateur has a shining reputation for being thoroughly feminine and seriously sexy designs. Now with a more accessible audience, the L'Agent girl is confident and bold. Find this line at Nordstroms and Bloomingdales, and net-a-porter.com.

## 7. Marlies Dekkers

![Marlies Dekkers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/20005910_8758_600x992.jpg)

Marlies Dekkers is a dutch lingerie brand that has famously started the "innerwear as outerwear" concept. One of the most popular designs that has been famously imitated, is the Dame de Paris plunge bra. With padded push-up cups and underwire support, this bra is also fashion forward with a triple strap detail across the bust made to be seen. Her other bra and panty ranges come in yummy colors with the signature strap details in thongs, briefs, and corsets. Marlies Dekkers designs have proven to be trendy, innovative, and legendary. Find Marlies Dekkers at marliesdekkers.com or shopbop.com.

These lingerie brands are worth a splurge because of their story, unique quality, and va va voom appeal. Do you own any of these brands?
