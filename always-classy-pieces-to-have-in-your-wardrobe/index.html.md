# 7 Always Classy Pieces to Have in Your Wardrobe ...

There’re some classy pieces that you always want to have in your wardrobe. These are pieces that will never go out of style. You can wear them for a variety of occasions. These classy pieces will really stretch your wardrobe and help you to be prepared for whatever event you need to dress for.

## 1. The Little Black Dress

![The Little Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/ni/safmj6es_433x650.jpg)

The little black dress is something every woman needs to have in her closet. You can wear this for so many different occasions. It’s perfect for funerals, interviews, parties and even a fancier date night. It’s one of the classy pieces you’ll find yourself reaching for the most. A little black dress looks elegant and makes every woman look attractive. The simplicity of a little black dress allows your beauty to shine.

## 2. Dark Wash Jeans

![Dark Wash Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/yf/h4vhwkyn_600x900.jpg)

Dark wash jeans are a staple that you need in your closet. They’re more slimming than light wash jeans. They’re also very versatile and can be dressed up or down. They’re perfect for Saturday morning errands, a casual day at the office or a date with your sweetie. The most important thing about purchasing a pair of dark wash jeans is making sure they fit you well and are flattering to your figure.

## 3. A Black Top

![A Black Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/is/p1np7xo4_600x900.jpg)

A black top is a classy piece to have in your wardrobe for similar reasons to the little black dress. It’s simple, elegant and never goes out of style. You can dress it up or down and wear it for a variety of events. Your black top can be a t shirt, a turtleneck or a nice blouse with a bit of detailing. It’s actually good to have several different versions of a black top.

## 4. Pearl Jewelry

![Pearl Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/sx/e87yt1k2_404x515.jpg)

Pearl jewelry is always classy. I love the quote by Jackie Kennedy that says this, “Pearls are always appropriate.” You can wear pearls with many different things from a little black dress to a simple top and a pair of jeans. It’s good to have more than just your basic short strand of pearls, too. Keep your eyes open for longer strands of pearls you can layer as well as pearl earrings and bracelets..

## 5. A Black Purse

![A Black Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/vv/oxj77mbb_500x720.jpg)

A black purse always looks classy. The options are practically limitless when it comes to choosing a black purse. There’re so many different styles and brands to choose from. Usually one that has clean lines and structure will stand up to the changing world of fashion. A black purse never looks out of place.

## 6. Simple Hoop Earrings

![Simple Hoop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/sh/l2jl4qyd_600x900.jpg)

Simple hoop earrings are a classy choice to have in your wardrobe. The great thing about hoop earrings is that you can wear them whatever the occasion is. They are fine to wear with a pair of jeans and a t shirt and work just as well with a suit for a job interview. Hoops in gold, silver, hematite and rose gold are all very versatile pieces of jewelry and are all classy. Hoops are actually the item in this list that I find myself wearing most often.

## 7. Nude Heels

![Nude Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/gb/l46frqmc_600x900.jpg)

Nude heels are very classy. They don’t go out of style and can be paired with many different choices of clothing. Nude heels can go with a simple sundress or be the right choice for a more formal occasion. You may find that having more than one style of them is helpful. A pump will tend to be dressier while a wedge heel lends a casual air.

Do you own any of these classy pieces? What can you add to this list? I can’t wait to be inspired by your wardrobes!
