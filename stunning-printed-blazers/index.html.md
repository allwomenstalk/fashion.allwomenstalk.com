# 7 Stunning Printed Blazers ...

**Printed Blazers** are a fantastic way to give your outfit a new season twist. A printed blazer makes for a functional and stylish outfit option. A blazer also works as a great transitional piece. You simply can’t for wrong with a blazer! Take it to the next level this season by adding a printed blazer to the mix. Take inspiration from the following stylish printed blazers.

## 1. Alexander Mcqueen Printed Blazer

![Alexander Mcqueen Printed Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_alexander-mcqueen-printed-blazer_263x350.jpg)

**Price:** $1493.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This opulent looking **printed blazer** has the price tag to match. Regardless, it’s still a striking piece of art. Or fashion as art. It has a single breasted design with a point collar and structured shoulders. This blazer would look great teamed with a cream dress and strappy sandals.

## 2. Tibi Printed Cotton-Blend Sateen Blazer

![Tibi Printed Cotton-Blend Sateen Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_tibi-printed-cotton-blend-sateen-blazer_276x350.jpg)

**Price:** $420.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9PX6fR)
Take a walk on the wild side with this cheetah print Tibi blazer. It’s made from a stretch cotton-blend sateen fabric and has a fairly fitted design. It features shawl lapels, button-fastening cuffs, and front flap pockets. Wear this blazer to jazz up a simple outfit of blue jeans and a white t-shirt.

## 3. Etro Paisley Printed Blazer

_ **Price:** $2190.00 at mytheresa.com_
Another exorbitantly priced blazer, another extraordinary print. This Etro blazer features a fresh and funky paisley print with contrasting boucle trims. This is a slim fitting blazer and falls at the hip. For those who have the money, it would be a fairly stylish way of updating a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/).

## 4. 3.1 Phillip Lim Leopard Print Blazer

![3.1 Phillip Lim Leopard Print Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_3-1-phillip-lim-leopard-print-blazer_277x350.jpg)

**Price:** $750.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jLdWZQ)
Animal prints are always a popular choice. This leopard print blazer has a contemporary deconstructed silhouette. It is collarless with a draped, open fround. This blazer would look great with a tank top, cigarette style pants and black heels for a chic night time look.

## 5. Co-Ord Stripe Fluid Blazer

![Co-Ord Stripe Fluid Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_co-ord-stripe-fluid-blazer_274x350.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/F97tx8)
For those wanting something simpler in terms of **printed blazers**, then this striped blazer is a smart option. It comes in a fresh yellow stripe print and has a distinctly preppy feel to it. Keep the preppy vibe going by wearing this blazer with tailored shorts, a t-shirt, and lace-up brogues.

## 6. Diane Von Furstenberg Barke Jacket

![Diane Von Furstenberg Barke Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_diane-von-furstenberg-barke-jacket_271x350.jpg)

**Price:** $279.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Don’t be intimated by boldly **printed blazers**. This particular blazer has a certain 80s feel to the design, but can look quite modern when styled right. Balance out the boxy, oversized look of this blazer by teaming it with slim-fitting pants and fitted tops.

## 7. Marc Jacobs Small Flower Blazer

![Marc Jacobs Small Flower Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/7_marc-jacobs-small-flower-blazer_266x350.jpg)

**Price:** $458.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This floral **printed blazer** is well suited for spring dressing. It comes in a delightful pink and orange colourway, and has a fuss-free cut. Matching shorts for this jacket are also available, but if you think that would be too busy then keep it simple by teaming this blazer with a contrasting tank top and block coloured shorts.

While many of these **printed blazers** would be out of many of our budgets, they’re still great for inspiration. Next time you’re out shopping keep them in mind when you’re browsing for blazers. What do you think of these stylish printed blazers?
