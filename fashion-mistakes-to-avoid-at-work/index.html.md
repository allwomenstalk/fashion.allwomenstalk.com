# Fashion Mistakes to Completely Avoid at Work ...

There are definitely some **fashion mistakes to avoid at work** and know what they are can really help you make the best impression at the office.

What is needed in order to look extremely stunning at your workplace? Are there any tips to use when combining your outfits every day? Do you look confident and satisfied with your choice every time you look in the mirror? What are the most common mistakes that people make when dressing for the office? There are some outfits that are considered to be ideal at the workplace, and there are some that should definitely be avoided. If you love fashion and want to look your best, here are some **fashion mistakes to avoid at work**.

## 1. Make Fashionable Garment Combinations

![clothing, red, jeans, fashion, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/gg/hg5lzx1e59ace961c4c34937457769_600x750.jpg)

Many women struggle with how to choose the best combination of clothing, which accessories to combine with what, what type of shoes to wear and what kind of purse to carry. All of this can be time consuming and often women make some mistakes that can be instantly noticed at the work place. If you're ever in doubt about a combination, it's probably best to skip it and try it out another time when you won't be at work. That way if it doesn't end up looking that good, you won't worry about the impact it made at the office.

## 2. No Workout Clothes

![clothing, footwear, leggings, tights, shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/es/o4/x6jg2ewr59acecd890d08944684862_600x721.jpg)

It is definitely not recommended to wear clothes that are for exercise to work (unless you're a personal trainer). Spandex yoga pants, leggings, tank tops and t-shirts that are used for the gym are definitely not the right choice and are one of the biggest fashion mistakes you can make when you're headed to work.

## 3. Wearing Tight Clothes

![fashion model, photo shoot, shoulder, joint, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/m3/aa8wj6ow59ace663a424f211484848_600x665.jpg)

Tight clothes have no place at work. Those clothes are intended for going out on the town. Tight clothes can be very distracting and they look extremely unprofessional. Save them for the weekend.

## 4. No Flip Flops

![clothing, footwear, shoulder, joint, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/cy/lrkxe6cg59acec81c5de5499531319_600x687.jpg)

It doesn’t matter how casual the company is, flip flops probably don't belong. You need to respect the dress code of your work and be properly dressed every time you arrive. You can wear flip flops on the weekends.

## 5. Too Much Jewelry

![hair, hair accessory, blond, human hair color, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/hq/sra2pfoo59acebac46822873816283_600x749.jpg)

Most women want to combine and complement their outfits with some pieces of jewelry. But putting too much on or wearing something big and extreme looks totally unprofessional. Instead, wear one or two understated pieces.

## 6. Dressing Too Casually

![footwear, pink, shoulder, sitting, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/cm/gihim1mp59aceb79a5b33173542183_600x750.jpg)

The office is not the place for dressing casually. Leave that for home, a picnic or shopping. Stick to business casual pieces like tailored pants, skirts and tops. If it's a little less formal where you work, dark wash jeans can sometimes be appropriate.

## 7. Forget about Trendy Pieces

![clothing, pink, jeans, fashion model, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/uz/u3ddtnfu59aced4fa60e6709890829_600x750.jpg)

You can definitely wear trendy clothing to work, but try not to go all out. That image isn't always a professional one and can give off the wrong impression.

## 8. Pay Attention to the Overall Look

![clothing, fashion model, outerwear, shoulder, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/re/x0ul5rln59aced349b062768813668_600x723.jpg)

It is important to make sure your clothes to match, but the most important thing is that when you see yourself in the mirror, the overall look is perfect. Tiny details make great looks.
