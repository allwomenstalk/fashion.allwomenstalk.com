# 7 Fashion Pieces from Target to Complete Your Wardrobe for a Fraction of the Price ...

As a Target addict, I’m constantly looking for affordable fashion pieces that can help me complete my wardrobe. I want to look cute, but I don’t want to spend a fortune on designer labels, and that’s where Target comes in. When you shop for clothes at Target, you can find pieces that are not only classic, but also well made, which isn’t always easy when you’re shopping for clothes are affordable!

## 1. Embellished Long Sleeve Sweatshirt

![Embellished Long Sleeve Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/s5/yxh05h51_410x387.jpg)

I’ve been a fan of these embellished sweatshirts since they first hit the scene last winter. Not only do they add some spice to a usually simple winter staple, but they also help you look cute while staying comfortable. They dress up something that would be considered “loungewear” so that you can wear it out and about!

## 2. Shadow Stripe Organza Skirt

![Shadow Stripe Organza Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/qg/i1aoqcyh_410x403.jpg)

From the moment I saw this skirt, I instantly started having visions of everything you could pair with it. It’s perfect no matter the time of the year, and if I owned it, (I might just have to go on a hunt for it at my local Target) I would definitely wear it year-round. It can be paired with a sweater, tights, or both in the summer, and your normal basic shirts in the spring, summer, and fall. Plus, it can be dressed up or down. You could wear it out with friends, or even at your new internship!

## 3. Slim Jogger Pants

![Slim Jogger Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/mo/ituqwgh9_410x404.jpg)

I’m not going to lie; I was hesitant to jump on this trend. I got what the appeal was-you can wear sweatpants and pass them off as sophisticated attire. However, I didn’t think they would look good on me. These pair of pants completely converted me, however. Maybe it’s the fact that the model is wearing heels in the picture, but something clicked for me, and I’ve finally accepted that these pants are the future. They look comfortable, yet chic, which seems to be a recurring trend this season!

## 4. Pullover Sweater

![Pullover Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/jg/u9sopipw_410x402.jpg)

As someone who lives in an area with extremely cold weather, it’s necessary for me to find sweaters that will keep me warm without compromising my style. From the moment I saw this sweater, I knew it was one that I wanted to add my wardrobe. The stripes and geometric shapes are so modern, and the monochromatic colors make it easy to pair with a number of different outfits!

## 5. Jacquard Sweater

![Jacquard Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/ip/crhw4qj1_410x400.jpg)

Even if the Ombré hair trend may not be here to stay any longer, Ombré is definitely still in style when it comes to your wardrobe, and this sweater is evidence of that! This is another sweater that I love for the winter months, because it doesn’t force you to compromise your style for warmth, and it’s super versatile!

## 6. Women’s Faux Fur Collar Coat

![Women’s Faux Fur Collar Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ad/d7/spsyf036_410x403.jpg)

Faux fur coats are back with a vengeance this year, so if you haven’t jumped on this trend yet, this coat will give you the perfect opportunity to do so! I think that the perfect coat is always the missing piece to a perfectly put-together outfit, so if you can’t figure out what exactly is missing from your wardrobe, a coat might be it!

## 7. Women’s Sweater Dress

![Women’s Sweater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rg/ko/d5136954_410x391.jpg)

As someone who’s constantly looking for a dress that can be dressed up or down, this dress was like a beacon of light in a dark room. This dress can easily be dressed up for a night out, perfect for the office, or just for a day of shopping!

What are your favorite affordable fashion pieces from Target? Let me know in the comments!
