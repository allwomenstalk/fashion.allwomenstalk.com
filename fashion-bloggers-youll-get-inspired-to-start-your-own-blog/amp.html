17 Fashion Bloggers Youll Get Inspired to Start Your Own Blog ...

Kyrzayda • MYFASHDIARY • Hello Fashion • Atlantic Pacific • Harper &amp; Harley • More ...

By Lucy

Maybe you adore the idea of having your own blog but keep asking yourself the same question: how do you actually start?! Well, these super successful and creative fashion bloggers will not only give you the inspo you need to turn your dream blog into a reality, but you'll also discover how a lot of them started and where it all began! Sound interesting? Then just read on...

1. Kyrzayda

This super successful 37-year-old full-time mom and blogger is known for her fierce haircut, fit physique and impeccable sartorial sense!
Instagram: @kyrzayda_
kyrzayda.com

2. MYFASHDIARY

Who is Tala Samman, you might ask? Well, for starters she was born in Chicago, originally Syrian, and grew up in Dubai. She's a proud graduate of The London College of Fashion, having edited the first blog to come out of the UAE back in 2009. Her style blog, Myfashdiary.com has been nominated in Ahlan!
*Fashion is what others declare is in. Style is what you declare is in.
Instagram: @myfashdiary

3. Hello Fashion

Back in December 2011, Christine Andrew founded Hello Fashion as an exciting way to showcase current trends in fresh, inspiring ways. Hello Fashion allows Christine to communicate directly with “the everyday woman” and offer style inspiration and advice along the way!
Instagram: @hellofashionblog
hellofashionblog.com

4. Atlantic Pacific

Ever since launching her fashion career in 2007 at Gap Inc. as a merchandiser, Blair Eadie has come to the realization of how street style is shaping up and the influence it has on its customers. So, in 2010, as a hobby, she created Atlantic-Pacific to add perspective to the growing conversation!

Flash forward to today, she's working with an amazing list of brand partners including CoverGirl, Gucci, Nordstrom, Target, Tory Burch, Sephora, and many, more.
Instagram: @blaireadiebee
the-atlantic-pacific.com

5. Harper & Harley

If you're a minimalist and obsessed with black, white and grey style, Harper & Harley is the ultimate blog for you! Just to top it all off, Aussie style star Sara Donaldson has worked with Uniqlo, Gucci, Swarovski, Nike and Harper's Bazaar.
Instagram: @harperandharley
harperandharley.com

6. Rach Parcell

Pink Peonies is an amazing blog dedicated to being a source of inspiration for women and young girls who love all things fashion, beauty, home decor and family life. Here she gives ideas and tips on how to bring beauty into your everyday life!
Instagram: @rachparcell
pinkpeonies.com

‌﻿

7. Gabi Fresh

Gabi started her blog in September 2008, right after graduating college with an interest in fashion journalism. Here Gabi inspires women to take risks and have fun with clothing, regardless of their size!
Instagram: @gabifresh
gabifresh.com

8. Hanna Stefansson

Introducing Hanna: a blogger and influencer based in Stockholm! Hanna enjoys blogging about her days with a focus on style, travels and yummy breakfasts. Seriously, you have to check out her Instagram feed - you'll love it!
Instagram: @hannastefansson
hanna.elle.se

9. Aleali May

The super successful Aleali May is an image consultant, model, and fashion blogger! While studying Business Marketing at Columbia College Chicago in 2010, her career began in the fast-paced world of fashion with Louis Vuitton and RSVP Gallery Chicago. Aleali enjoys the opportunity to create with others - including world renowned brands such as Audi USA, Nike, and Adidas!
Instagram: @alealimay
alealimay.com

10. Jeanne Grey

GreyLayers is one of the most influential blogs in the East Coast and now soaring throughout the world with subscribers from Asia, Europe, North America and South America! Jeanne is highly regarded in the world of fashion, digital and social media as she collaborates with brands as a stylist, model, brand ambassador and product influencer. Not only this, but she's notorious for breaking the rules of fashion and blurring lines between fashion for both men and women!
Instagram: @thegreylayers
greylayers.com

11. Song of Style

This is a super stylish fashion and interior design blog written by Los Angeles based interior designer Aimee Song. It features daily fashion photos, design inspiration and heaps more!
Instagram: @songofstyle
songofstyle.com

12. Annabelle Fleur

Annabelle was inspired with this quote "Dare to dream and all you wish for will come true” to start a fashion blog - a long-time dream of hers. She lives, breathes and dreams fashion so any other fashion lovers out there should check out her blog ASAP!
Instagram: @vivaluxuryblog
thevivaluxury.com

13. Kenza Zouiten

Kenza's 25 year old, Swedish/Moroccan (although raised in Stockholm) with a huge love for friends, family, fashion and travel! Can you believe she started her blog when she was just 15?! Now she's a blogger, model, influencer, designer, TV host and entrepreneur! Follow her to see more of her journey!
Instagram: @kenzas
kenzas.se

14. Janni Delér

Janni Delér is 26 year old and originally from Stockholm, Sweden, although currently lives in Monaco. Janni is a great lover of everything fashion, travel and fitness related! She started her blog hoping to document her life, to give her the ability to reflect later on and see all the things her younger self was up to. Now, however, her blog is a huge part of her life! Join her in following her adventures with her boyfriend!
Instagram: @jannid
jannideler.com

15. Lisa Olsson

Lisa is a lifestyle, fashion, travel and interior design lover who began blogging at the early age of 13, after developing her brand in all channels! She has progressively grown to become one of Sweden’s biggest influencers. Lisa did a design collaboration with Nelly.com in early 2016 and has worked with major brands such as Nikon, Coca-Cola, Bik Bok, Zalando and British e-commerce giant River Island, just to name a few!
Instagram: @lisa.olssons
lisaplace.se

16. Molly Rustas

Another blonde beauty from Sweden conquered the Instagram world with her natural beauty! With her lifestyle and fashion blog she has attracted more than 145K followers...maybe it's time to hop on the bandwagon and make sure you're the next one!
Instagram: @mollyrustas
mollyrustas.se

17. Tatjana Mariposa

Tatjana is a fashion blogger and social media influencer who rose to fame posting on her self-titled blog! Not only this, but she has become an Instagram sensation, earning more than 1 million dedicated fans posting fashion and travel photos to the picture sharing app! How exciting!
Instagram:
Instagram: @tatjanamariposa
tatjanacatic.com
