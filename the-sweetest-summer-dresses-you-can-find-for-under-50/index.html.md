# The Sweetest Summer Dresses You Can Find for under $50 ...

I personally believe that the best time to wear dresses is in the summer. There’s just something so fun about the breeziness of wearing a dress when the weather’s nice and you’re not freezing to death! If you agree with me, you need to check out this list of affordable summer dresses I compiled. I picked from all different styles to find something that I’m sure everyone will love. This list will surprise you with just how cheap some of these dresses are. Believe me, a few of these price tags shocked me, so get them while you can!

## 1. Watercolor Tie-Dye

![Watercolor Tie-Dye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/mj/immje7xw552e5ae651676483961933_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I think that this watercolor number is meant to look like an abstract floral print, which is why it’s so perfect for the summer. Plus, the body-con look paired with the bright, fun colors make it easy to dress up or down depending on where you’re wearing the dress!

## 2. White Lace

![White Lace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/tk/osewhnuo552e5ae68f835472441289_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Even though some may argue that this dress is simple, I think it’s stunning. The lace detailing that shows just the littlest bit of skin is so subtle but so pretty. Plus, it’s from H&M! It’s rare that you find a dress that’s so cheap and yet has this much attention to detail.

## 3. Comic Book Floral

![Comic Book Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/o7/p7ogbcuh552e5b5223e39243290611_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Are you not really feeling the classic floral trend this year? Go for this graphic art version instead. I don’t know what it is exactly, but it almost reminds me of something you’d see on Mad Men. It just has that mod look about it. If you’re looking to shake things up, this is a great dress to do it with.

## 4. Collared Shirt Dress

![Collared Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/qm/qwia07ix554cc22dea56a857012800_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you're a product of the late '90s (or even the early 2000s), you probably remember when these dresses were everywhere! This collared shirt was one of the biggest trends of those years, even when you weren't on the golf course, and it's back in a big way this summer!

## 5. Coral Fitted Dress

![Coral Fitted Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/ry/azmzkaye554cc22e37352960391409_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Do you prefer wearing a more form-fitting dress, especially in the summer? You'll probably agree that it can be tough to find a dress that's form-fitting but isn't a body-con dress. This dress is the answer to all of your problems. It has a gorgeous coral color for summer, but even more than that, the fit is probably exactly what you were looking for!

## 6. Summer Music Festival Look

![Summer Music Festival Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/m1/bktjlo8a554cc22e52d2c603887572_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Don't you think that summer brings out the boho chic chick in all of us? This dress is the answer to all of your boho-inspired Pins on Pinterest! Whether or not you're going to a music festival, this dress is the perfect addition to your summer wardrobe. Even better, Taylor Swift was seen wearing an almost-exact replica of this dress a few months ago! Who doesn't want to dress like Taylor Swift?

## 7. Mamma Mia

![Mamma Mia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/ic/scvn0o12554cc22e7757f118705062_384x449.jpg)

[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Did this dress conjure up images of Mamma Mia for anyone else? If you’ve spent years trying to recreate that Grecian style, this dress is the perfect choice for you. Everything from the neckline to the detailing on the torso and even the tassels are so beautiful and perfect for any event you’re going to this summer, whether it’s a first date or a big event!
