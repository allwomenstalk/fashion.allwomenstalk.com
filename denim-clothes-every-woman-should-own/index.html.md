# 8 Adorable  Denim Clothes Every Woman Should Own ...

Denim is a cute and fun way to spice up your outfit! Here are the denim clothes every girl should own! The great thing about these pieces is that they match just about anything you own so you can pair them with stuff you already have. Have fun rocking denim all year long!

## 1. DENIM SKIRT

![clothing, shoulder, fashion model, joint, jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bf/l0/h2b7mcfg598377fe5573e781633172_600x728.jpg)

Whether it's a mini skirt, straight skirt, or a pencil skirt you can pair any cute crop top or blouse with a denim skirt.

## 2. DENIM PANTS

![jeans, clothing, denim, shoulder, joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/rx/kprz5eb9598379344830b823470199_600x750.jpg)

Let's be honest, we're all obsessed with jeans! Whether they are skinny, straight-legged, or bell-bottoms, denim jeans come in so many shades and they go with just about everything!

## 3. DENIM JACKET

![denim, jeans, shoulder, girl, joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/aq/uhve7rhf598378205728f983356492_600x749.jpg)

Jean jackets are an absolute necessity in every girl's closet! Whether you get a tight, form fitting one or a loose, baggy one, a jean jacket could be just what you need to top off your plain outfit!

## 4. DENIM DRESS

![blue, fashion model, shoulder, jeans, vision care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/u6/vq6ph0dh598379743f741952042521_600x698.jpg)

A jean dress is an adorable and unique way to rock denim! You can dress it up with a nice pair of tights and your favorite booties, or you can dress it down with a simple pair of Keds!

## 5. DENIM OVERALLS

![blue, denim, clothing, jeans, electric blue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ni/i0/zzlbkffg59838b008207f261231274_600x749.jpg)

Jean overalls were in just about everyone's closet during the 90's and we're brining them back! Pair an adorable pair of jean overalls with a cute graphic tee or your favorite crop top!

## 6. DENIM SHIRTS

![clothing, denim, footwear, fashion model, snapshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/uq/f4frh1zo59838b2cf1fd6307681196_600x600.jpg)

Jean shirts can be paired with almost any bottom and give any outfit a nice, unique flair!

## 7. DENIM SHORTS

![shoulder, fashion accessory, joint, leg, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f9/ml/jsowcwb359838b44cfdb2810726134_600x750.jpg)

Jean shorts are great for the summertime! Whether you're going to the mall, to an amusement park, or even out for lunch, there are so many different ways you can rock a pair of these!

## 8. DENIM JUMPER DRESS

![clothing, denim, jeans, shoulder, shorts,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v8/ns/sxce1vq759838b6299984156999274_600x698.jpg)

Jumper dresses are a really fun and girly way to rock denim! These can be dressed up or dressed down! The great thing about denim is that it is such a simple fabric that you can have fun and do just about anything with it! Be creative!
