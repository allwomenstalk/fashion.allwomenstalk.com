# 8 Marvelous Tips on Accessorizing a Pretty Little Black Dress ...

**How To Accessorize A Black Dress:** when it comes to how to accessorize a black dress, there is no right or wrong answer. The little black dress is the fashion world’s equivalent of a blank canvas. You can style it up or down, depending on mood, budget, and seasonal trends. Below are just a few timeless and trend-based tips on how to accessorize a black dress.

## 1. Keep It Simple

When it doubt, keep it simple. If you are unsure of how to dress for a particular occasion then stick to the basics – gold or silver jewellery, pearls, and neutral bags and shoes. The type of accessories you choose may also be based upon the style of your dress. A more basic dress may call for more interesting accessories, whereas a dress with a fussier design may need only the basics.

## 2. Add Embellishments

A feather cape here, a lace collar there – get creative when thinking about how to accessorize a black dress. A few embellishments can really update your basic black dress. Antique-looking brooches are also an option for dressing up a ladylike LBD. Embellishments can be both classic and trend-based.

## 3. Go Tribal

Tribal is a big trend right now. From Navaho prints to heavily beaded accessories, this look is perfect for updating your basic black dress. While coloured tribal accessories are great for creating a carefree and casual vibe, look for metallic tribal-inspired jewellery for a more polished take on the trend.

## 4. Just Add Colour

Nothing livens up a black dress more than a splash of colour. A coloured clutch, some costume jewellery, and even adding a slick of brightly coloured lipstick are a few ways to brighten up your basic black dress.

## 5. Toughen up

Get a little moody and give your LBD a rock-chic edge. This look is great for giving a pretty party dress an edgy twist. Team it with a beaten up biker jacket, ankle boots, and a chain-link bag. Layer on some necklaces, and you’re good to go.

## 6. Tone It down

Make your LBD suitable for everyday wear by teaming it with a knit cardigan or slouchy blazer. If you are looking for how to accessorize a black dress this way, look for accessories that create a relaxed vibe. Team it with a large canvas tote bag, ballet flats or loafers, and a printed scarf for a casually chic look.

## 7. Belt It

A belt is a great way to simply update your LBD. Depending on your style of dress, a belt could act to add shape or simply as an adornment. A simple tan leather belt is great for cinching in a shift dress, while a metallic or jewelled belt would be great for accessorising a cocktail dress.

## 8. Get Leggy

Since black dresses can be quite plain, add a bit of texture with a pair of patterned tights. Lace styles are a popular choice, as are polka dot or heart prints tights. When looking at how to accessorize a black dress this way, make sure to keep the rest of your accessories fairly simple.

When looking at how to accessorize a black dress, it’s important to keep the style of dress in mind. Simple shift style dresses are better for day wear, while lace and body hugging dresses might be more suitable for night. What are your best tips on how to accessorize a black dress?
