# 16 Fabulous Outfit Ideas for the Sagittarius Woman ...

If you're a Sagittarius woman, chances are you're optimistic, cheerful, adventurous, and a traveler at heart and these qualities really reflect in the way you dress. When it comes to fashion, you prefer comfort over trends, but you also love to experiment and you'll try any trend at least once. Purple is your power color, but you also look fabulous in reds, blues, and neutral colors. You love statement accessories that you usually acquire from your travels. All in all, your style is the perfect blend of relaxed elegance and quirky elements. Ready to take a look at some outfit ideas for you? Here we go:

## 10. All-White

## 12. Gorgeous Neutrals
