# 8 Edgy Lace Pieces to Update Your Look ...

Edgy lace pieces are a great alternative to the usually feminine lace styles you see around. If you’re a big fan of the lace trend, but want to update your look with something a bit different, then an edgy lace piece just might be what you’re after. It could be a structured jacket, a cool little bag, or a fun lace accessory – just something that’s a little bit more risqué and unexpected than usual. Interested? Then take a look at the following lace pieces with edge.

## 1. Lace Biker Jacket

![Lace Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_lace-biker-jacket_960x1448.jpg)

A lace biker jacket? Yep, you read correctly. When it comes to edgy lace pieces, a lace biker jacket may just top the list. A lighter coloured lace can be more subtle and suitable for the day time, while black jackets are perfect for a dark and dangerous night time look.

## 2. Metal Lace Collar

![Metal Lace Collar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_metal-lace-collar_920x976.jpg)

Lace doesn’t always have to be confined to fabric. Lace-look jewellery is a great way to update your everyday look. A statement collar necklace is one of the easiest and on-trend options for now. A lot of affordable fashion chains like Topshop and Forever21 do a great range of collar necklaces so keep an eye out for lace styles!

## 3. Lace Skinny Trousers

It’s all about prints these days, and lace print pants would definitely give your outfit the X-factor. Brightly coloured and busy prints tend to be harder to style, so choosing a pair of pants in a neutral colourway will make them much more versatile in the long run. Team them with a silky blouse, patent shoes and a framed handbag for a timeless look.

## 4. Lace Bag

![Lace Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_lace-bag_705x408.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to update a plain old outfit is with a brand spanking new handbag. Edgy lace pieces like a clutch can add some gritty glamour to your look, as long as you choose the right kind of style. Steer clear of feminine designs and instead opt for contemporary silhouettes and unexpected embellishments. Check out online sites like ASOS for an affordable range, or something like Net-A-Porter if you’re feeling spendy.

## 5. Lace Insert Top

![Lace Insert Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_lace-insert-top_1008x1288.jpg)

Peplum and Peter Pan style collars aren’t what you’d usually call edgy, but with a few risqué lace touches they can become exactly that! Tops with some carefully placed sheer lace panels are perfect for showing a hint of skin.

## 6. Lace Booties

![Lace Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_lace-booties_264x278.jpg)

Killer heels can top off a killer outfit. Black booties are a no-brainer, but can look extra edgy when they come with lace detailing. The key to making them look more frisky than feminine? Stick to stiletto heels and cut back on the bow details.

## 7. Lace Cross Sweater

![Lace Cross Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/7_lace-cross-sweater_1008x1128.jpg)

The most common lace fabrics tend to have floral patterns on them. However, keep an eye out for other less-feminine patterns if you want to give your look an update. Things like crosses are the extreme opposite to florals. Larger lace patterns can also look more contemporary.

## 8. Lace Bralet

_ Nothing says risqué more than a bit of bra peeking through your outfit. When it comes to edgy lace pieces, work a rock’n’roll look by wearing a lace bralet under low-cut or sheer styles of clothing.

These edgy lace pieces could easily update an old outfit. When it comes to lace, what kind of look do you prefer – edgy or feminine? How would you style these edgy lace pieces?
