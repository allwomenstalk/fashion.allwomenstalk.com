# 7 Summer Sleepwear Styles Thatll Rejuvenate Your Wardrobe ...

Do you have some gorgeous summer sleepwear? There’s nothing nicer than a cold shower and something light and cool to slip into after a hot day, and it’s an essential for slumber parties or holidays, too. If your pyjama drawer is looking pretty bare, check out these pretty summer sleepwear numbers…

## 1. Chiffon Shorts…

![Chiffon Shorts…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_chiffon-shorts_290x370.jpg)

These chiffon shorts make great summer sleepwear – the material is light and cool, and they are flexible enough for you to move around in. I love the nude color and applique detailing, too, it’s so feminine and pretty! ASOS sells a matching bra, or you could team them with any light tank. You’ll look and feel relaxed and pretty.

## 2. Onesie Wonder…

![Onesie Wonder…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_onesie-wonder_290x370.jpg)

Don’t want to give up your Onesie? River Island has you covered with these Summery short onesies. Made from the same easy-care jersey material, they feel exactly the same, but will keep you much cooler. There’s a whole variety of designs, too! My favorite is this cute pastel pink and Aztec design.

## 3. Delicate Diva…

![Delicate Diva…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_delicate-diva_290x370.jpg)

Elle Macpherson lingerie never disappoints, and this stunning sleepwear set from ASOS is no different. Made from cool silk with a scalloped edge and lace detail, it’s soft and pretty, with subtle vintage influences. Cute sleepwear that is comfortable and easy to wash? Win.

## 4. Pretty Pastels…

![Pretty Pastels…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_pretty-pastels_290x370.jpg)

I have a wardrobe full of chemises! They are so comfortable and stylish, and I love the ease of pulling one on when you jump out of the shower. This Elle Macpherson design from ASOS is right on trend with its subtle pastel colors, scalloped trims and lace cups, and it’ll make anyone feel amazing. Definitely check a chemise out!

## 5. Casual Comfort…

![Casual Comfort…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_casual-comfort_290x370.jpg)

Want something more comfortable and casual than silk and lace? Check out Calvin Klein sleepwear. It’s designed to be flattering and supportive, and is made from a comfortable breathable fabric that is sure to keep you cool. I love the mix-and-match styles, too – you can get shorts, capris, long trousers and knee-length shorts, making them perfect for any weather.

## 6. Cute Kimonos…

![Cute Kimonos…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_cute-kimonos_290x370.jpg)

Kimonos got a revival after the gorgeous Blake Lively admitted to wearing one to bed, and it’s no surprise when they look this good! This authentic kimono was made in Japan, and the fabric is like strong, cool silk. I love the cute cherry blossom print, and the wrap front and wide sleeves are super-flattering. If you don’t already have a kimono, it’s definitely time to grab one! Find this one at ASOS.

## 7. Sports Luxe…

![Sports Luxe…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_sports-luxe_290x370.jpg)

This Bjorn Borg hoodie from ASOS is one of the most unique summer sleepwear items I’ve ever seen, but it’s comfortable and casual, too. The peach color will flatter any skin tone, and the loose fit makes it very unrestricted. You could wear it over shorts or a chemise for a warmer look, or on its own on hotter days - or simply wear it to lounge around, and take it off when you get into bed.

I’m definitely going to stock up on summer sleepwear this year. The shops seem full of delicate designs, pastel colors and cute chemises, and who doesn’t want to feel like a princess before going to bed?! Have you found some great summer sleepwear? Share it with me in the comments, I’d love to see.
