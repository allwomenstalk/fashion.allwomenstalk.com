# 8 Stylish New Season Overalls ...

Take a leaf out of your childhood wardrobe and don a pair of new season overalls. They’re becoming increasingly popular with fashionistas and celebrities the world over. Short denim styles are perfect for working a casual summer look while slimmer, sleeker styles are great for creating a more grown-up, polished look. Take a look at the following new season overalls.

## 1. Bleach Long Leg Overalls

![Bleach Long Leg Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_bleach-long-leg-overalls_600x900.jpg)

When it comes to new season overalls you can’t get more traditional than denim long leg styles. Wear them cuffed at the ankles with a white tee and espadrilles for a relaxed summer look or dress them up with wedge heels and a crisp white blazer. These overalls will set you back around $110 Topshop but you could always try ASOS and Urban Outfitters for more affordable buys.

## 2. Paisley Printed Overalls

![Paisley Printed Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_paisley-printed-overalls_350x500.jpg)

Prints are all the rage right now. Not even overalls can escape the statement print this season. These Motel overalls come in a cute paisley print and would look cute teamed with a silky blouse and brogue shoes for a vintage-inspired look. Also check out Free People for more styles of printed overalls.

## 3. Check Overalls

![Check Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_check-overalls_600x900.jpg)

Overalls don’t always have to be of the denim variety, as you can see here. These Topshop overalls have a tailored design and feature an on-trend check pattern. They’d look great teamed with a black tank top, strappy flat sandals, and oversized sunglasses for a casual weekend look.

## 4. White Denim Overalls

![White Denim Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_white-denim-overalls_600x900.jpg)

White denim can be a bit hit and miss, but it really just comes down to how you style it. Make a pair of white denim overalls work for you by pairing them with classic pieces like a striped Breton top and black patent loafers or ballet pumps. These white denim overalls will cost you $76 at Topshop and you can also try Free People or Need Supply Co. for more white denim overalls.

## 5. Vintage Denim Overalls

![Vintage Denim Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_vintage-denim-overalls_600x900.jpg)

Dark denim is a classic. Whether you go for short or long overalls, you can’t go wrong with this classic dark denim wash. Wear them with t-shirts and sneakers for a relaxed look, or dress them up with a printed blouse and heels. These denim overalls will set you back $76 at Topshop but try snapping up a bargain buy from thrift and consignment stores.

## 6. Khaki Washed Utility Overalls

![Khaki Washed Utility Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_khaki-washed-utility-overalls_600x900.jpg)

Work a different angle in a pair of coloured overalls. These khaki overalls from Topshop are ideal for working a military-inspired look. Roll them up at the ankles and wear them with a white tank top and leather-look sandals for a relaxed weekend outfit. Be sure to check out River Island and ASOS for more coloured overalls.

## 7. Black Overalls

![Black Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_black-overalls_340x511.jpg)

Dress up a pair of black overalls for a sleek and sophisticated look. Team them with a structured blazer, killer heels, and a compact clutch for a cool evening look. Keep your hair and makeup polished to really make the outfit work. From designer pairs by Maison Martin Margiela to more affordable versions at Need Supply Co. and Nasty Gal, black overalls would make a chic addition to any wardrobe this season.

## 8. Patchwork Overalls

![Patchwork Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_patchwork-overalls_336x596.jpg)

Patchwork denim has its moments every now and then. This season, try a pair of patchwork overalls a la 3.1 Phillip Lim. The key to making them look more high fashion than hick is to stick to sleek, clean lines. Keep the patchwork theme going by teaming them with a geometric printed top and two-tone shoes.

Overalls can be pretty divisive, but there’s no denying that they’re having a fashion moment right now. What do you think of the overall trend? Will you be wearing a pair this season?
