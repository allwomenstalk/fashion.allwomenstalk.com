# 7 Tips for Wearing Pencil Skirts ...

**How to wear pencil skirt** more than one way can be a tad tricky. Knowing how to wear pencil skirts to suit different trends and occasions is the key to getting the most wear out of them. The pencil skirt is quite timeless and refined; however you can easily dress it down for a more casual take on things. Take a look at the following tips on how to wear a pencil skirt below.

## 1. How to Wear Pencil Skirts as Workwear

When thinking about **how to wear a pencil skirt**, once of the first images that comes to mind is workwear. When wearing a pencil skirt to work, choose a high waisted style and make sure that it is not too body hugging. Neutral colours are best for a work environment, however if your dress code allows for colour then go for it! A tucked in blouse, cropped blazer and pumps teamed with a pencil skirt is a classic workwear look.

## 2. How to Wear Pencil Skirts as Weekend Wear

Dress down your pencils skirts and wear them on the weekend. Since pencil skirts are quite fitted, you could balance this out by teaming them with a slouchy knit. Tuck in any baggy tops to maintain that appearance of an accentuated waist. Team them with sneakers or slipper style shoes for a casual off-duty look.

## 3. How to Wear Pencil Skirts as Evening Wear

If you want to know **how to wear pencil skirt** as evening wear, the key is to choose a dressy fabric. Metallic fabrics are quite on-trend right now, so a pencil skirt in a metallic finish would be a stylish option. Wear a pencil skirt in a high waisted style, and with a sleeveless blouse tucked in. Top off the look with cocktail jewellery and a pair of stunning stiletto heels.

## 4. How to Wear Pencil Skirts for Summer

In summer I like my clothes to be a bit looser and free-flowing, so pencil skirts aren’t really at the top of my summer hit list. However, a shorter pencil skirt that sits lower on the waist would be a great option. Also look out for more breathable fabrics. A neutral pencil skirt teamed with a coloured tank top and strappy sandals would be a simple yet stylish example of summer dressing.

## 5. How to Wear Pencil Skirts for Winter

When winter hits, stick to pencil skirts in thicker fabrics. Winter is a great time to incorporate a tweed pencil skirt into your wardrobe. Team your pencil skirt with a long sleeved knit and layer with a coat and tights. Choosing the right footwear can be quite tricky as it’s all about achieving balance. When wearing knee to thigh-high boots, stick to pencil skirts that hit at the knee or just above.

## 6. How to Wear Pencil Skirts with a Coat

When wearing a coat, make sure that you can see the bottom of your pencil skirt from underneath your coat. Stick to coats that can be cinched in at the waist as this will help keep a shapely silhouette. Wearing an A-line coat with your pencil skirt is also a smart way of recreating this season’s must-have peplum silhouette.

## 7. How to Wear Pencil Skirts in Denim

Nothing says preppy, Americana inspired dressing quite like a denim pencil skirt. Look for a mid-rise to high waisted style that sits just above the knee. When looking at **how to wear pencil skirt** this way, remember that denim can be quite rigid so stick to skirts that allow for more give. A dark denim skirt teamed with a red striped t-shirt is timeless.

These are just a few tips for **how to wear pencil skirt**. A plain pencil skirt can be dressed up or down, and is a great piece to have in your capsule wardrobe. Do you have any handy tips on how to wear a pencil skirt that you’d like to share?
