# 8 Ways to Wear Your Summer Dresses for Fall ...

Wearing summer dresses in fall might sound a bit strange. Won´t it be too cold, you wonder? Yet it does seem a shame to have to put away your lovely dresses as the weather gets colder. Well, there are some great ways to adapt your wardrobe so you can wear summer dresses in fall. So here are some great tips on how to wear summer dresses for fall …

## 1. Lovely Leggings

![Lovely Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/122_470x705.jpg)

Leggings are a good way of extending your summer dresses into the fall season. This is a look that suits younger women well, although it can also work for older women. The trick is to pick the right dress – if you´re going to wear one with leggings, make sure that the two pieces work well together.

## 2. Tights

![Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/213_448x700.jpg)

Another way to wear your summer dresses in fall is to pop on a pair of tights. In fact, this can give you the opportunity to glam up a plain dress and give it a completely different look. For example, a plain green dress that goes well with tanned legs would also look amazing with some leopard-print tights.

## 3. Over Jeans

![Over Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/311_586x880.jpg)

Summer dresses can have a new lease of life worn as a long top over jeans. This look works best with dresses that have sleeves, rather than strappy dresses, and that are above the knee. A maxi dress over jeans would make you feel like you were wearing a tent!

## 4. Cute Cardi

![Cute Cardi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/412_706x940.jpg)

Sometimes as the weather cools down you find it´s your shoulders that get cold rather than your legs. If this is the case, try throwing a cute cardigan over your summer dress. Carrying a cardi is really useful when the days are still warm, but the evenings tend to get a bit chilly.

## 5. Topping a T-shirt

![Topping a T-shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/513_540x767.jpg)

Another example of how to wear summer dresses for fall is to slip on a t-shirt underneath. This kind of look tends to suit young women better. Make sure that the t-shirt is fitted, or the dress will not sit well on top. T-shirts with lycra or elastane are best.

## 6. Boots

![Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/612_593x941.jpg)

You can also get some extra wear out of your summer dresses in fall by wearing them with boots. Once the weather is cool enough, boots can actually look pretty good worn with a summer dress. It makes an interesting contrast, especially if you´re bold enough to pair a floaty dress with biker boots.

## 7. Turn into a Top

![Turn into a Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/713_931x1400.jpg)

If you enjoy sewing, your summer dress can have a second life as a top. It can then be worn with jeans, pants or a skirt. You could even use some of the excess material to make sleeves if you´re clever. But before you cut the dress up, be sure that you don´t want to wear it as a dress next summer!

## 8. Wrap up

Covering up with a wrap or a huge scarf is a brilliant way of wearing your summer dress on cooler days. It also gives you the chance to add more color or contrast. Plus you can be creative with how you style the wrap – try tying and folding it different ways.

So, if you´ve been wondering how to wear summer dresses for fall, here are plenty of ideas! It´s always a shame to have to put away those pretty dresses until next year, so with these tips you can enjoy wearing them for longer! Have you found any creative ways of wearing summer dresses in fall?
