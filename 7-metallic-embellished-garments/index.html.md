# 7 Metallic Embellished Garments ...

A little bit of metallic embellishment is a guaranteed way of adding glitz and glam to an outfit. A few sequins will suffice for a day-friendly look, and heavy embellishments can really add the ‘wow factor’ to your night time look. From sequinned tops to embellished shorts, there’s a plethora of garments to choose from. Here is just a selection of metallic embellished garments.

## 1. Alice + Olivia Sequin-Embellished Silk-Blend Top

![Alice + Olivia Sequin-Embellished Silk-Blend Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/m0/kaohyfaa54c8f96997d2e781246218_400x472.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This striped top is a great example of a heavily metallic embellished garment. This black silk top features a scoop neck, short sleeves, and is fully lined. This top is a terrific way to liven up an otherwise basic outfit. Slip this on over a pair of dark jeans, slip on some patent pumps, and your outfit for the night is sorted!

## 2. J.Crew Melanie Pailette-Embellished Merino Wool Skirt

![J.Crew Melanie Pailette-Embellished Merino Wool Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/h7/nskute3w54c8f96acd414171540940_400x470.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/z0RXUc)
This glitzy skirt features an all-over silver, copper, and gold, pailette embellishment. This combination of metallic colours gives the skirt a pale, shimmering appearance. It’s glitzy, but without being too over the top. Tone it down for a daytime look by pairing it with a loose-fitting t-shirt and some flat shoes.

## 3. Topshop Rust Gold Sleeve Cross Back Blouse

![Topshop Rust Gold Sleeve Cross Back Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/x4/u4npni2y54c8f96bdf8fd789344652_400x494.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ez5Wrb)
This rust coloured blouse features gold embellishments along its sleeves. The front of the blouse is demure with its high neck and long sheer sleeves. The back of the blouse, however, reveals an open cross-back design. This blouse would look great tucked into a fitted leather skirt.

## 4. Topshop Grey Embellished Tee

![Topshop Grey Embellished Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/pk/sr148otd54c8f96d099fb285144533_400x459.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/5T32ek)
Metallic embellished garments can at times be a bit overplayed. This grey tee, however, balances the heaviness of the embellishments with a soft, sheer body. This tee features an embellished panel along the front of the neckline. It also features an exposed zip at the back. The grey colour would work well with a range of bright and pastel colours.

## 5. Tara Matthews Embellished Bandeau Bikini Top

![Tara Matthews Embellished Bandeau Bikini Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/x9/vhefaeh854c8f96e0179d432241257_400x167.jpg)

**Price:** $230.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wFKtOD)
This embellished bandeau top is exclusive to net-a-porter.com. It is made from a combination of polyester and lycra, and features metallic bead embellishments along the front. While it is quite pricey for a bikini top, it can also be worn as an eye-catching undergarment. Wear it under loose fitting and plunging tops to add extra detail to an outfit.

## 6. Walter Sequin Embellished Silk Tank Top

![Walter Sequin Embellished Silk Tank Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/mq/f67qe0n154c8f96f10236186893528_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The olive-green colour of this tank top is unusual, but enticing. The rich colour works well with the metallic embellishment along the shoulders of the tank top. It has a scoop neck and gathering at the back. Pair this with cream coloured bottoms to really make the olive-green colour stand out.

## 7. Dorothy Perkins Purple Embellished Maxi Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Purple Embellished Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/jc/xfvwmc1854c8f96fcce09100809182_342x464.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Metallic embellished garments don’t always have to consist of separates. This purple maxi trend incorporates metallic embellishments with square studs lining the next and waist. The muted purple colour is on trend for spring, and the maxi design is still as popular as ever. Channel a Grecian goddess and wear this dress with lace up sandals and wrist adornments.

Metallic embellished garments look fantastic when done right. They are an easy and chic option for those wanting a fuss-free glamorous look. Which of the above are your favourites?
