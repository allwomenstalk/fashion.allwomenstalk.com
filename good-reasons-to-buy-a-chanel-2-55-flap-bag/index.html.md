# 8 Good Reasons to Buy a Chanel 2.55 Flap Bag ...

**Why buy Chanel bags**? For the people who love the brand, there are plenty of reasons! For those wanting to buy a designer handbag but not sure which particular brand or style, it may take a little more persuasion. If you’re going to take the plunge and buy a designer handbag, why not make it the Chanel 2.55? It’s an iconic style and one that won’t date. Take a look at a few reasons as to why buy a Chanel bag below.

## 1. It’s an Iconic Style

The Chanel handbag is probably one of the most recognisable designs. We’re all well acquainted with the image of quilted leather, the interlocking C’s, and the chain link straps. When you think of designer handbags, I’ll bet that the image of the Chanel 2.55 would be one of the first to come to mind. So why buy a Chanel bag? It’s simply iconic.

## 2. It’s Forever Fashionable

Bags like the Chanel 2.55 are timeless. This Chanel handbag has withstood many decades of fashion trends and fads, and it’s still one of the most coveted designer items around. A simple black or beige Chanel 2.55 handbag is truly versatile and will last a lifetime.

## 3. Make It an Investment Piece

About to splurge on an item? Thinking of a reason when it comes to asking why buy a Chanel bag? Call it an investment piece! Especially when it comes to classic items like the Chanel 2.55. It will never go out of style and is versatile enough to be worn with a range of outfits. And when you think about it, the cost per wear will make it all worthwhile.

## 4. You Can Pass It on

I’ve always imagined what it would be like to have one of those mothers or grandmothers with a wardrobe full of designer pieces. Imagine the things I could inherit or borrow! Why not aim to become that awesome mother or grandmother? Once you’ve had your run of things, you can pass it on and share the Chanel joy.

## 5. The ‘It Girl’ Factor

A celebrity endorsement always seems to make certain items that little bit more popular. The Chanel 2.55 is a favourite of many an It Girl and style icon. Owning one is enough to make you feel like you’re in an elite club of sorts. It’s perfectly fine to want to emulate the style of someone you admire, as long as you make it your own.

## 6. Treat Yourself

If you’ve recently reached a milestone or achieved a goal, then think about treating yourself. And who doesn’t love a designer handbag? If you spend too much time trying to justify reasons for buying a Chanel bag and why, you’ll never get around to it. Sometimes you’ve just got to be selfish and that’s okay, as long as you’re not disadvantaging yourself or others by doing so.

## 7. Resell Value

One thing about investing in a popular style like the Chanel 2.55 is that if you need to sell it later on for whatever reason, you’ll get a good resale price on it. You might not recoup all your money on it but, depending on how well you’ve looked after it, you’ll get a fair chunk back of it back.

## 8. Stop Counterfeiting

By purchasing a real Chanel handbag, you’re taking a stand against the counterfeit industry. While it’s perfectly understandable that we all can’t afford to buy luxury items, buying counterfeit is not the answer. You can always buy designer inspired items, but blatant rip-offs hurt the industry. If you’re willing to spend the cash, then buying the real thing is the way to go.

So **why buy Chanel bags**? For some, Chanel bags like the 2.55 are the holy grail of designer handbags. There are also a range of other reasons that could factor into buying a Chanel handbag and why. Would you ever buy a Chanel handbag? What would be your reason for buying it?
