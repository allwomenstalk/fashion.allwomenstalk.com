# The Cutest Color Combos to Wear in Autumn ...

Fall is the best season for fashion. It's a time when you get to wear your favorite boots, scarves, and layers. Of course, if you want to look your best, you need to buy clothing in colors that fit the theme of the season. You'll look strange if you walk around in neon pink or white when autumn arrives. If you're not sure which colors you should be buying, here are the cutest shades that you could ever wear in autumn:

## 1. Chestnut Brown

![Chestnut Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/wr/o9wdp5do560e8cbb7e174725638331_600x901.jpg)

It's almost time to roast chestnuts on the fire, which is why it's the perfect time to wear a chestnut color. If you have brown eyes, a chestnut shirt could make them pop. Even if you have light eyes, you don't want to let the season pass by without buying a few chestnut colored clothing items. After all, they'll look amazing on any skin tone.

## 2. Olive Green

![Olive Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/gl/qkt64gmn560e8cbb3744b136838264_585x876.jpg)

Even though the leaves aren't going to be green in fall, it doesn't mean you should shy away from wearing the color. It's actually the perfect season for wearing an olive green. Just make sure that you don't wear greens that are too bright, because you should save those shades for Christmas.

## 3. Dark Blue

![Dark Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/mu/cxchhlsh560e8cbd55d37990139837_600x898.jpg)

Dark blue shirts can look amazing with jeans. Plus, the darker the shade of your shirt, the dirtier it can get before others notice. That's why you should be happy to know that dark blue is one of the hottest colors to wear during the fall.

## 4. Gold

![Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/be/hm8fnwnz560e8cbdc81c4313180831_600x900.jpg)

Gold can make any woman look elegant. While it's recommended that you buy golden shoes, shirts, and skirts, you can settle for wearing gold jewelry. Even though we all love silver, autumn is the best time to break out your gold bangles and earrings.

## 5. Grey

![Grey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t4/ue/gumf2xua560e8cbb8f10a578860092_524x804.jpg)

We all love to wear black, but you should wait until winter to bust out your black coats. For now, you should stick to wearing a slightly lighter shade. Grey can look just as slimming and elegant as black does, so you shouldn't mind swapping out your black boots for grey ones--at least until it starts to snow.

## 6. Teal

![Teal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/zg/kqr473ej560e8cbded21f682672969_600x901.jpg)

You can pair a teal shirt with almost anything. That's why it's not only a fashionable color to wear during the season--it's also a convenient color. Plus, it'll give you a break from wearing super dark shades. Compared to the rest of the colors you see during fall, you'll feel bright while walking around in teal.

## 7. Cherry Red

![Cherry Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/ve/rg0b9qfp560e8cbc93f41315262738_482x724.jpg)

This is the time of year when you should be daring. Go ahead. Grab that cherry red lipstick and paint your mouth with it. You'll be thankful you did. If you're not ready to take such a big leap with your makeup, then you should at least do so with your clothing. Buy a cherry red skirt (or even just a bag), so that you stand out from the crowd. Don't worry: You're bound to get compliments.

You don't want to wear the wrong colors during the fall season. If you do, then your outfit could end up looking odd. That's why it's best for you to fill your closet with these adorable fall colors. What's your favorite shade to wear in autumn?
