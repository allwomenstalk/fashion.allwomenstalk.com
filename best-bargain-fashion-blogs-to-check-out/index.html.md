# 7 Best Bargain Fashion Blogs to Check out ...

If you’re looking for some fab bargain fashion blogs, you’ve gotta check out my list! I love following fashion blogs period, but sometimes it’s nice to be able to actually afford some of the fashions you see and not just lust after them from afar! If you’d like to check out some of the best in bargain fashion blogs, keep reading below to get the scoop!

## 1. The Budget Babe by Dianna Baros

![The Budget Babe by Dianna Baros](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_the-budget-babe-by-dianna-baros_400x309.jpg)

One of my favorite bargain fashion blogs has to be The Budget Babe! You can find tons of information on this blog about how to wear specific pieces, celebrity looks for less, lower cost versions of designer looks and previews on designer collabs with department stores. I love seeing blog founder Dianna Baros recreate high end designer looks for so much less and looking at her bargain fashion gems from Target and Marshalls!

## 2. The Budget Fashionista by Kathryn Finney

![The Budget Fashionista by Kathryn Finney](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_the-budget-fashionista-by-kathryn-finney_545x389.jpg)

Just as the name of her fashion blog implies, you can expect to see the hottest in fashion trends for the girl on a budget. Founder Kathryn Finney started her fashion blog in 2003 when there weren’t tons of bargain fashion blogs out there and helped create a movement to shop for value bargains, and not just shopping cheap. Check out her blog to see the best in bargain fashion trends, plus-size fashion finds and fashion advice.

## 3. Penny Chic by Shauna Miller

![Penny Chic by Shauna Miller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_penny-chic-by-shauna-miller_600x522.jpg)

Another expert on budget fashion is Shauna Miller and you can see all of her handiwork on PennyChic.com. Are you interested in DIY projects? Want to rock the latest fashion trend without the hefty price tag? Or maybe you want to learn how to create a certain look for under $100. You can get all that and more when you stop by the blog or check out her videos on YouTube!

## 4. Fab Finds under $50 by Kimberly Brinkman

![Fab Finds under $50 by Kimberly Brinkman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_fab-finds-under-50-by-kimberly-brinkman_600x534.jpg)

One of the newer blogs I’ve been reading lately is Fab Finds Under $50. I really love reading about bloggers who come up with stylish items for under $100, but under $50 sounded like a real challenge so I had to check it out! You can find tons of stylish and affordable looks as well as a daily outfit diary and outfit inspiration calendars so you can get ideas for new looks!

## 5. The Cheap Chica’s Guide to Style by Lilliana Vazquez

![The Cheap Chica’s Guide to Style by Lilliana Vazquez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_the-cheap-chica-s-guide-to-style-by-lilliana-vazquez_600x450.jpg)

I’ve been following The Cheap Chica's Guide for years now and I never get tired of browsing this bargain fashion blog! You might have seen founder Lilliana Vazquez on the Today Show, news shows or various magazines because this lady knows her stuff! Check out her blog to find the latest trends on the cheap, celebrity and runway looks for less and beauty recommendations.

## 6. Frugal Fashionista by Sally Glover

![Frugal Fashionista by Sally Glover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_frugal-fashionista-by-sally-glover_400x275.jpg)

Frugal Fashionista is another amazing fashion blog where you will primarily find recreations of celebrity looks for less. This blog proves that you can easily recreate a celebrity’s wardrobe with a little creativity and a little shopping savvy! You’ll find loads of various celeb styles ranging from comfy chic to trendy and edgy for so much less than you’d expect!

## 7. Madison Avenue Spy by Lila Delilah

![Madison Avenue Spy by Lila Delilah](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_madison-avenue-spy-by-lila-delilah_600x551.jpg)

If you’re obsessed with designer fashions and don’t want to settle for anything less, you’ve got to check out Madison Avenue Spy. This fashion blog specializes in helping us get the best in designer fashion for less. Are you curious to know about the latest sale or sample sale for a certain brand? The Madison Avenue Spy will help you spot the best sales and deals for all your designer fashion finds!

If you don’t follow any of these fashion blogs, I highly recommend you start! Who doesn’t love to score a designer piece on sale or find a carbon copy of a celeb’s highly sought after bag for less?! If you love the challenge of finding a good sale or recreating looks, these seven ladies know how it’s done! What’s your favorite budget fashion blog?
