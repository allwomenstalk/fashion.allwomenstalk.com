# The Top Fashion Essentials Every Woman Needs in Her Life ...

There comes a time in every woman’s life when she looks into her closest and realizes there are something’s missing and there are **fashion essentials every woman needs**. That includes basic pieces that help complete an outfit or basic pieces you can build an outfit around. There are **fashion essentials every woman needs** in order to complete her wardrobe.

## 1. Little Black Dress

One of the most popular and timeless **fashion essentials every woman needs** is the LBD (the little black dress). The LBD is good for being the basic piece to build an outfit around or being the piece to make an outfit stand out. One dress can go from day to night with little effort in between. You can do no wrong with the little black dress. It’s just a must have in any wardrobe.

## 2. Basic White Tee

The basic white t-shirt is a must in any wardrobe. It is perfect for all year round and for building an outfit around. Throw on a cardigan and a pair of jeans with a cute pair of flats, and there is your outfit for the day. For work it’s also easy to transform a plain white tee into a more professional look by adding black ankle pants, a blazer and nice pumps to finish off the look.

## 3. Flats

Flats are a major essential. I know that many woman love wearing heels and don’t feel like a real feminine woman without them, but a few pairs of flats never hurt either. If flats aren’t really your thing, keep in mind that heels aren’t meant to be worn all day and you should lets your feet rest a little in between. The flats you should look for are colors that can go with any outfit such as black, red, nude, metallic and a pair with a design on them, to give the outfit a bit of flair.

## 4. Dark Wash Jeans

A pair of dark wash jeans is an essential part of any wardrobe because it can be dressed casual or a little more on business casual side with a nice blouse and a blazer or jacket. One of the benefits of dark wash jeans is that they never go out of style. They are a classic piece that can be worn in many different ways.

## 5. Trench Coat

A trench coat is another classic clothing staple that needs to be in every woman’s closet. It’s been said that shoes make the outfit. While this is true, once you put on the trench coat you’ll find that is gives the entire outfit a classic and chic look and you will always feel beautiful and confident, no matter your mood that day.

## 6. Blazer

A structured and tailored blazer is a must have, because when styled right, it can go from day to night. It can also be used as a jacket when it’s cool outside pr over a dress for a more fashion blogger, chic look.
