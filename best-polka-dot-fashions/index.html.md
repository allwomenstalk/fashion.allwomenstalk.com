# 9 Best Polka Dot Fashions ...

Polka dot fashion just keeps on trending! Move over stripes, polka dots are where it’s at this season. From classic navy and white patterns to more outrageous colour mixes, there’s a polka dot pattern for everyone. Get on board the polka dot trend and take a look at the following polka dot fashions.

## 1. Polka Dot Fashion Blazer

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
Here, the traditional black blazer is given an on-trend update in the form of a cute polka dot print. It has a relaxed, lightweight design and can be dressed up or down. Style it up with a blouse and cropped trousers or go for something more casual in jeans and a tee.

## 2. Khaki Flock Spot Trousers

![Khaki Flock Spot Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_khaki-flock-spot-trousers_600x899.jpg)

Polka dot pants are everywhere right now! Inject some polka dot loving into your wardrobe with these tailored cigarette trousers. They come in a cool khaki colour with a black polka dot print. These would look great worn with a loose cream blouse and ballet flats for a polished daytime look.

## 3. Polka Dot Pullover

_ **Price:** $16.90 at forever21.com_
This cute pullover is probably my pick of the bunch when it comes to polka dot fashion. It’s perfect for working a casual look, and would look great worn with anything from boyfriend jeans to full vintage-inspired skirts. It’s available in three different colours, and at such a great price I’m seriously considering buying one of each!

## 4. Bettie Page Polka Dot Skirt

![Bettie Page Polka Dot Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_bettie-page-polka-dot-skirt_350x500.jpg)

Channel your inner glamour girl and team it with a fitted top, heel court shoes, and don’t forget to apply that winged eyeliner and bold red lip.

## 5. Polka Dot Chambray Shirt

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
The polka dot fashion trend is even taking the denim world by storm. Whether it’s a denim shirt or jeans, if it’s not covered in polka dots this season then it’s just not worth wearing! This sweet chambray shirt would look great worn with coloured pants or tucked into a tan skirt.

## 6. Endless Options Skirt

![Endless Options Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/6_endless-options-skirt_350x406.jpg)

## 7. Miss KG Lola Spot Ballet Pumps

![Miss KG Lola Spot Ballet Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/7_miss-kg-lola-spot-ballet-pumps_600x293.jpg)

## 8. On the Polka Dot Bag

## 9. Dash of Dots Tights
