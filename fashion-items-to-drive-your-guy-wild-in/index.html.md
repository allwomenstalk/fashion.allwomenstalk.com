# 8 Fashion Items to Drive Your Guy Wild in 2017 ...

If you're looking for ways to drive your guy wild, you should consider investing in some super cute (and affordable) fashion items that will make his jaw hit the ground when he sees you in them! From edgy cut out designs, modest midi lengths, luxurious lace and a flattering fluted style, we've thought of plenty of ways you can drive your guy wild in 2017! Keep reading to discover the top items you can rely on to get the job done!

## 1. Lace Cami Crop Top

_ $9.99 at forever21.com_

Your man will love this sexy knit crop top featuring crochet lace with floral embroidery, eyelash lace with a scalloped trim and a flattering V-neckline!

## 2. Shoe Republic Ruffle Strap Heel

_ $28 at forever21.com_

A pair of faux suede heels is sure to make your man wild! Opt for a super sexy pair like these, featuring a ruffled toe strap, ruffle accent on the back, buckled ankle strap, and a stiletto heel!

## 3. Fluted High-Low Lace Skirt

_ $35 at forever21.com_

This gorgeously feminine yet sexy skirt like this one is bound to put a smile on your man's face. You can't go wrong with a semi-sheer crochet lace skirt featuring a fluted silhouette and a high-low ruffle hem!

## 4. Metallic Lace-up Tank Top

_ $35 at forever21.com_

This super sparkly metallic top is everything you need to drive him wild! He will love to see you in this semi-sheer lurex tank top featuring a plunging lace-up front in a sexy sleeveless cut.

## 5. Girl in Mind Lace Slip Dress

_ $58 at forever21.com_

For all the girly-girls out there, this dress was made for you! It features a lace overlay, padded cups with underwire, and a bottom slit to subtlety show off a little more leg that you're guy will love!

## 6. Lace-up Chain Bralette

_ $14.90 at forever21.com_

If you're a little more daring with your tops then you're going to want to wear this one for your man for sure! This super flattering bralette features a plunging front with a lace-up chain design and sparkly chain cami straps.

## 7. Cuffed High-Waist Denim Shorts

_ $17.90 at forever21.com_

Every girl should be rocking a pair of these shorts this year! Opt for a high-waist denim style of shorts as they're known for being flattering, plus slanted front pockets and a cuffed hem to be ultimately trendy.

Guys love the look of bodysuits, so why not show this one off for him as well? It's a ribbed knit bodysuit featuring a plunging wired V-neckline and a sexy cut out, open back!
