# 7 Super Trendy Skirts for Summer 2012 ...

**Trendy skirts 2012:** from prints to pastels and short to long hemlines, there’s a skirt trend to suit most styles this season. Pencil skirts are enjoying popularity, as are bold graphic prints and eye-catching colors. When it comes to skirt trends for 2012, you’re spoilt for choice. Get some summer wardrobe inspiration from a few trendy skirts for 2012, below.

## 1. Jack by BB Dakota Lace Skirt

![Jack by BB Dakota Lace Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_jack-by-bb-dakota-lace-skirt_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Colored lace is a fun trend to try out this summer when it comes to trendy skirts 2012. This skirt comes in a striking sea green color and features a large lace overlay. Wear it with a loose fitting shirt and neutral ballet flats for a casually chic look.

## 2. Pleated Slit Maxi Skirt

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_

The maxi skirt is forever a favourite, and this one features on-trend slit details to the sides. It also comes in a pastel mint color, which ticks another trend box for the season. Tuck in a crisp oxford top and team this skirt with strappy flat sandals.

## 3. Longer Length Bodycon Pencil Skirt

![Longer Length Bodycon Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_longer-length-bodycon-pencil-skirt_350x447.jpg)

**Price:** $49.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/U9xFoJ)

When it comes to trendy skirts 2012, the pencil skirt is a must-have. The look is ladylike but also great for showing off your physique. This skirt comes in a hot orange color and would be perfect for working an off-beat summer vibe.

## 4. Electric Vines Skirt

![Electric Vines Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_electric-vines-skirt_350x522.jpg)

**Price:** $118.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This skirt incorporates the contemporary neon trend into a delicate and feminine style of skirt. It has a fitted waistband with gauzy cotton overlay. Neon blooms decorate the hem of the skirt. Wear it with a silk blouse and delicate sandals for a stunning summertime look.

## 5. Vero Moda Stretch Denim Skirt

![Vero Moda Stretch Denim Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_vero-moda-stretch-denim-skirt_350x447.jpg)

**Price:** $39.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/yMB9pQ)

Colored denim is everywhere right now. Jeans may not be the most summer appropriate clothing so work this trend with a skirt instead. When it comes to trendy skirts for 2012, this has got to be one of the funner styles. Team this skirt with a patterned singlet and block-heeled sandals for a fresh look.

## 6. Oasis Scarf Print Skirt

![Oasis Scarf Print Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_oasis-scarf-print-skirt_350x447.jpg)

**Price:** $83.50 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/YCjRtf)

Scarf prints are having a trending moment right now. From shirts to pants to skirts, it’s something that can drastically change the look of your outfit. This skirt features an ornate, almost vintage inspired scarf print. It would look super cute worn with a light pink t-shirt and nude ballet flats.

## 7. Matelassé Bodycon Skirt

_ **Price:** $14.80 at forever21.com_

When looking at trendy skirts 2012, keep an eye out for those with interesting finishes or textures. This one has a matelasse finish and comes in a sleek pencil cut. Dress it up with a white singlet, metallic waist belt, and patent pumps.

As you can see, for trendy skirts 2012, most styles are covered. It’s simply up to you to choose what style and trend you like best. What are your top skirt trends for 2012?
