# 7 Full Midi Skirts That Will Have You Working This Seasons Ladylike Trend ...

With the ladylike trend in full swing, full midi skirts are taking over wardrobes this season. Wearing a full midi skirt exudes a sense of prim and proper elegance, but don’t let that fool you. You can just as easily toughen up the demure midi skirt for a very contemporary take on the trend. Try working this season’s ladylike trend with these lovely full midi skirts.

## 1. Textured Pleat Midi Skirt

![Textured Pleat Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213920_9837_599x687.jpg)

The black skirt is a versatile wardrobe choice. When it comes to full midi skirts, choosing one in basic black will guarantee that it stands the test of time. For a more polished affair, choose one in a tailored design. This Topshop skirt is a bit of an investment at $110 but you can always check out H&M and ASOS for more affordable buys. Wear your black midi skirt with a crisp white shirt and pointed pumps for a sophisticated look or dress it down with a printed sweater and ankle boots for a casual weekend look.

## 2. Denim Midi Skirt

![Denim Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213919_2091_599x806.jpg)

Add a ladylike touch to a casual outfit with a full denim midi skirt. This Topshop skirt comes in a crisp dark blue denim and will set you back around $70. You can also find similar denim skirts at Zara. A denim skirt like this would look great teamed with a crew neck t-shirt, ballet flats, and structured side bag.

## 3. White Full Midi Skirt

![White Full Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213917_4498_406x425.jpg)

Work a fresh look in a clean white midi skirt. Choose one with a bit of structure to it, like this textured skirt from Mango. Wear it with all-white pieces to work this season’s must-have monochrome trend or team it with a striped top and loafers for a nautical inspired look.

## 4. Check Midi Skirt

![Check Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213919_7383_599x787.jpg)

You’ll be looking positively preppy this season in a check midi skirt. Oversized check prints are the real statement makers and look great when worn in the form of a full, pleated skirt. This particular skirt costs $49 from Dorothy Perkins and you can also check out ASOS and Modcloth for similar check skirts. Wear it with a tucked-in tank top, biker jacket, and ankle boots for a tougher take on preppy dressing.

## 5. Leather Midi Skirt

![Leather Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213917_3183_464x583.jpg)

Talking about toughening up outfits, how about this leather midi skirt? A leather-look midi skirt is a contemporary and edgy way of wearing the full midi trend. Wear it with a fitted sweater and ballet flats for a smart and stylish look. This particular skirt by Tibi costs close to a whopping $1000 but you can find similar, MUCH more affordable versions at Topshop and ASOS.

## 6. Twill Circle Skirt

![Twill Circle Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213919_8032_599x804.jpg)

Embrace fall tones in a dark red coloured skirt. If you prefer full skirts that flow a bit more, choose ones in softer fabrics. This skirt comes from Dorothy Perkins and will set you back $49. Work a casual, street smart look by wearing it with a fitted sweater, cropped denim jacket, ankle boots, and a slouchy beanie.

## 7. Floral Printed Skirt

![Floral Printed Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/29213919_3877_459x648.jpg)

Really do ladylike glamour in a full floral midi skirt. It’s a guaranteed way of adding some fifties-inspired charm to your look. This MSGM skirt is ridiculously expensive at around $1000 but if you want to add a full floral skirt to your wardrobe then check out ASOS and Modcloth for more budget-friendly buys

Whether you prefer something edgy or elegant, these full midi skirts will have you covered. It’s just a matter of styling them as you see fit! What do you think of these midi skirts? How would you style them?
