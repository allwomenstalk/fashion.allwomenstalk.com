# The Best Ways to Tie a Scarf in Fall ...

You don't have to wait until winter to start wearing scarves. You can wear them right here and now, as long as you follow [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/how-to-tie-a-scarf) tips. Here are their suggestions for how to tie a scarf during fall:

## 1. Bow Tie

![Bow Tie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hu/l6/gi46a5qt56210423d90b0252752461_482x724.jpg)

All you have to do is tie a loose bow at the nape of your neck. Then you should turn it slightly and allow the edges to hang down from the knot.

## 2. Long and Skinny

![Long and Skinny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/w1/nlli4lmt56210423e671b524830018_482x724.jpg)

You should wrap your scarf around your neck once and drape the ends up front. Then you should tie it in a loose knot right below your clavicle.

## 3. Neckerchief

![Neckerchief](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/un/egib7m6k5621042438c8f130905970_482x724.jpg)

You should roll it it into a rope shape. Then you should double knot it around the midpoint of your neck.

## 4. Blanket Scarf

![Blanket Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ud/f3/vhy5a8vi562104245318a448346200_483x724.jpg)

You should drape it around your shoulders, like a cape. Then you should fold it in half and wrap it around your neck.

## 5. Turtleneck

![Turtleneck](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ub/qy/zcgi4m9d562104244987f174870218_482x724.jpg)

You don't actually need to tie this one at all. Isn't that great?
