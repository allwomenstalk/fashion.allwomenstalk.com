# 7 Fashion Choices Thatll Draw the Eye Away from Flaws ...

There are plenty of fashion choices that'll [draw](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/drawing-websites-to-visit-when-youre-bored/) the eye away from flaws, in order to make you feel more comfortable with your appearance. Of course, that doesn't mean that you should constantly be worried about your 'flaws.' You're beautiful just the way you are, so you shouldn't be ashamed of showing off your beautiful body. But we all have insecurities, so here are some fashion choices that'll draw the eye away from flaws:

## 1. Belts

![Belts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i9/u4/ivupenkz_600x861.jpg)

Fashion belts can do more than add some sparkle to your outfit. Wear them a bit higher than your natural waistline for a slimmer look that'll draw the eye away from flaws. You should also invest in something with a shiny buckle, so that it draws attention to the center of your body, creating the illusion that you're slimmer. Of course, the only way to know which way your outfit looks best is by trying out every option. Step in front of that mirror and strut your stuff.

## 2. Color Blocking

![Color Blocking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/ar/q7mqxdau_600x798.jpg)

It's common knowledge that black makes you appear slimmer, but it's not the only color that should exist in your wardrobe. Buy clothes with big blocks of color in order to draw the eye to certain areas on your body, and away from the 'flaws.' If you're crazy about black, then you can buy outfits that contain vivid colors, and some black. You don't have to choose one over the other.

## 3. Heels

![Heels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/ir/aj53pnxq_600x781.jpg)

If you want your booty to look beautiful, and your legs to look lean, strap on your heels. Your choice of footwear can do a lot for you. When wearing heels, the way you walk changes, making you swing your hips more, drawing attention toward your gorgeous body. As long as you're able to walk in them properly, you'll look fabulous wherever you go.

## 4. Jewelry

![Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/0i/w134re1t_500x502.jpg)

Just like a little lipstick will draw attention toward your mouth and away from your facial 'flaws,' jewelry can lead the eye away from imperfections. When you're donning a sparkling necklace, everyone will be looking at it instead of the birthmark on your shoulder.

## 5. Leggings

![Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/qm/vmm4vdie_600x846.jpg)

If you're self-conscious about your legs, you can still show them off. Buy a pair of leggings with a cute design. When you wear them, everyone will be too busy complimenting your style to examine the size of your thighs.

## 6. Cover up

![Cover up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/c8/bm2eqjfr_600x776.jpg)

If you're uncomfortable showing your shoulders, then you don't have to. Remember that you're free to show as much or as little skin as you'd like. If you don't want the world to see your stomach, buy a cut out bathing suit that hides the parts you're not willing to share.

## 7. Pockets

![Pockets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/vf/03bio8ln_600x840.jpg)

The positioning of pockets is more important than you'd think. Whether they're on a shirt or a pair of pants, they can make certain body parts, such as your butt or breasts, appear larger or smaller. So choose your outfits carefully.

Use these tips in order to feel more comfortable in your body. Of course, one day you should be able to be fully happy with yourself, and won't want to hide any mark on your skin or flab that you have. You're beautiful, whether you think so or not. What outfit do you love to wear, because it always makes you feel gorgeous?
