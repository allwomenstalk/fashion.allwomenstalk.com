# 9 Functional Wardrobe Items Every Woman Needs ...

When it comes to assembling a functional wardrobe without accumulating clutter, versatility is everything. Clothing and shoes that can be worn in a variety of situations and circumstances are essential. That even includes undergarments.

The following are night examples of versatile clothing and footwear options for women:

## 1. Denim

Everyone needs a good pair of denim jeans. That’s because they can be worn in almost any casual setting. And when properly cared for, denim jeans can last for decades without going out of style.

## 2. Racerback

When it comes to bra styles, the racerback is by far the most versatile. A [racerback bra](https://www.spanx.com/bras/racerback) can be worn with any outfit, including strapless dresses. It’s also a suitable option to wear during workouts.

## 3. Flats

A pair of flats are suitable footwear for many different types of outfits. They can be worn with skinny jeans, skirts, dresses, and even pantsuits. The versatility of flats is owed to their minimal design. However, those who like to put a lot of emphasis on shoes when putting together outfits might be underwhelmed by the simple look of flats.

## 4. Cardigan

While sweaters are associated with cold weather, that doesn’t account for cardigans. These thin cotton or [cashmere](https://www.businessinsider.com/cashmere-factory-why-so-expensive-how-its-made-2019-3) sweaters are also perfect for cool summer nights. A series of three or four cardigans can be worn in all seasons, making them a very versatile style of sweater.

## 5. Black Pumps

Whether going on a date or meeting friends for drinks, a pair of black pumps is a universal footwear option for women. They’re a simple way to add a subtle sexiness to almost any outfit, as well as a timeless classic that will never go out of style.

## 6. Black Dress

Almost every woman has a [little black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-a-little-black-dress-for-all-occasions/) in her closet. That’s because it’s one of the most versatile garment options of all time. It’s a go-to in those situations where nothing else seems to look right and time is running out to pick your outfit.

## 7. Black Blazer

A black blazer is another go-to piece of clothing for any number of occasions. It’s ideal for business settings as well as formal functions. What’s more, a black blazer goes with almost any style of blouse.

## 8. Trench Coat

A reporter’s style tan trench coat is a timeless option to wear in cold and rainy weather. It’s a sleek and stylish way to conceal an otherwise less-than-ideal outfit underneath. Best of all, trench coats look great on everyone, making them appear taller and leaner.

## 9. Canvas Sneakers

A pair of Converse Chuck Taylors are yet another classic footwear option every woman needs to have in her closet. While black is the go-to, other colors can be equally practical for all sorts of situations. Whether high-tops or low, Converse will never stop looking good on feet around the world.

Anyone can acquire hundreds of different articles of clothing and pairs of shoes, but that’s [wasteful](https://seekbamboo.com/blogs/sustainable-lifestyle/zero-waste-lifestyle) and expensive. A better choice is to find a handful of versatile options to serve as the backbone of your wardrobe. These garments and shoes can be worn almost anytime, anywhere, and always look great. They’re safe bets and sure things. When it comes to fashion, you can’t ask for anything more.
