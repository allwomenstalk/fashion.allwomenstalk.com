# 5 Easy Ways to Transition Your Wardrobe from Winter to Spring ...

We're nearly there guys! Winter is almost gone! Of course, for the past few months, you have probably been comfortable in your seasonal clothing - the big coats, scarves, and knits - that come together to create a really sophisticated and functional winter wardrobe. But as the temperature starts to rise a little, and the sun starts to make more of an appearance, so too must you adapt your clothing to keep up with the trends! Here are some simple **ways to transition your wardrobe from winter to spring**.

## 1. Trench Coat

![Clothing, Street fashion, Red, Fashion, Orange,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/lg/ovxdy5on5c8c0fb500fec505333422_600x600.jpg)

Put away that heavy puffer jacket that saw you through the winter and find a stunning trench coat instead. Trench coats are perfect for spring fashion because they evoke that oversized nature that is still so hot on all of the runways from Paris to New York, and they are thin enough to allow a little seasonal heat but also robust enough to withstand any cheeky April showers that might come your way.

## 2. Floaty Midi Dress

![Clothing, Street fashion, Dress, Fashion, Shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bx/ce/q7w2vffi5c8c110df1d03925868360_600x600.jpg)

Nothing says the joys of spring quite like a floaty midi dress and if you can find something in a wonderful floral pattern, even better! You have probably been relying on a jumper dress to get you through the winter, but the changing of the seasons calls for something lighter and brighter, and a cute little midi dress fits the bill perfectly.

## 3. Mules

![Footwear, Shoe, Leg, Ankle, Human leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/ld/wqaye4a15c8c1235a7524485690554_600x600.jpg)

Reintroduce your legs and feet to the joys of warmer weather! Your leather knee highs and other kinds of boots will have been your go to footwear in the cold, so now that the weather is turning a corner, it is time to take full advantage with a lovely pair of mules. It’s the perfect time of the year to start showing off some ankle again!

## 4. Tonal Dressing

![Clothing, Street fashion, White, Photograph, Green,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/qv/g8kgt1h45c8c125f1fcc1718321090_600x744.jpg)

The winter is much more about colour blocking to stand out in the dark light and dreary weather, but the spring is much more suited to the concept of tonal dressing. You don’t need to go over the top with your colours to compensate for the horrible conditions at this time of the year, so make the most of it and explore what you can do with things like beige tones.

## 5. Light Wash Denim

![White, Clothing, Jeans, Beauty, Street fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/r6/r9bk98tr5c8c1290abb88865985670_600x743.jpg)

Winter is the perfect time for protective black jeans, but the lightness of springs calls for lightness in clothing, so light wash denims is what you should be focusing on in your everyday looks. Don’t be scared to rock a little double denim either, that signature look from the 90s is very much back in style!
