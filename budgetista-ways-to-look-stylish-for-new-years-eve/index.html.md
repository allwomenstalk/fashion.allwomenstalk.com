# 7 Budgetista Ways to Look Stylish for New Years Eve ...

There are tons of ways to look stylish for New Year’s Eve and being on a tight budget is no reason to feel like a Cinderella sans the fairy godmother! Now, I know this year’s trends might seem a bit hard to pull off but if you keep waiting for a wand-wielding Prabal Gurung to show up at your doorstep, you’ll end up wearing a pumpkin as the clock strikes midnight. No need to go to the extremes, though, because these ways to look stylish for New Year’s Eve promise to make you the belle of the ball:

## 1. Hot Makeup Look

![Hot Makeup Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ep/bx/ozvl1syq_600x821.jpg)

Going for a full glam, ultra-trendy vampy look is one of those foolproof ways to look stylish for New Year’s Eve without having to shop for new clothes. Pick any outfit that works for the occasion and fret not – glam hair and makeup as well as some smart styling on your part will definitely make you the belle of the ball.

## 2. Blitz & Glitz

![Blitz & Glitz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/dm/w9onmv1s_460x690.jpg)

Gold is the way to go this winter and you can do it as little or a lot as you want to. Best thing about it? This metal has been a rather popular choice for quite a few seasons which means you won’t have to shop much to pull off your very own runway-inspired look.

## 3. Vintage- Inspired

![Vintage- Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/kl/zraq65cs_500x768.jpg)

Sixties prints and silhouettes are no longer considered theme-party wear and now is the perfect time to pay your most flamboyant aunt a visit and ask if you can shop in her closet. Girls used to primp and prime quite a bit back in the day and I’m sure you’ll have no problem finding a gorgeous dress. Pay H&M as well as your favorite thrift store a visit too, because a girl without a backup plan is a stressed girl indeed!

## 4. Geometric

![Geometric](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/bc/iuvlhmk5_600x776.jpg)

Dazzle the crowd with a unique combination of runway-inspired black and white prints and keep them wondering how you did it. All you have to do is pick out two different geometric prints and top it off with some chunky gold jewelry. Balmain, Bottega Veneta and Dries Van Noten runway looks are a perfect example of what to look for so check them out before you start mixing and matching.

## 5. Maxi Length

![Maxi Length](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/sv/p4ii51r8_600x901.jpg)

Floor-length skirts and dresses are still going strong, allowing you to recycle that gorgeously ethereal summer outfit and actually spend very little time worrying about your New Year’s Eve outfit. Thank Valentino, Dolce & Gabbana and McQueen for it and do check out their ideas for the season while you’re at it.

## 6. Don’t Forget the Details

![Don’t Forget the Details](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/h8/ovwt2yf2_600x863.jpg)

Velvety black or rich plum nails will make your hands look absolutely gorgeous and these small details do add much to the look. Choose your shade on time, making sure it not only complements your outfit but makes your hands look glam as well.

## 7. Right Attitude

![Right Attitude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/fo/fgeh7b12_600x899.jpg)

Last but not least – it’s attitude that counts, girl, and this is the time to be happy, confident and celebrate the coming of a brand new year and all the wonderful opportunities it may bring. Be the life of the party, smile and enjoy yourself because that’s what the celebration is all about.

But what will you be wearing, my beauties? Are you all set for the big party or still browsing for that perfect match?
