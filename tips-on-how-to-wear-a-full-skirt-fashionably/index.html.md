# 7 Tips on How to Wear a Full Skirt Fashionably ...

Knowing how to wear a full skirt this season will keep you ahead of the style set. Full, voluminous skirts are more popular than ever. They’re flattering to most silhouettes and can be dressed up or down. Embrace this feminine silhouette and take a look at the following tips on how to wear your full skirt this season

## 1. Hem Length

![Hem Length](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_hem-length_600x900.jpg)

Choosing a full skirt that’s the right length for you can make all the difference when it comes to looking stylish. When looking at how to wear a full skirt, choose a skirt that finishes at the slimmest part of your leg. This is usually above or below the knee. Avoid full skirts that are too long as they can overwhelm your figure.

## 2. Material Girl

![Material Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_material-girl_590x923.jpg)

The type of material your full skirt is made of can drastically alter your overall look. Silky, swishy skirts can be quite girly while stiffer fabrics can look more modern. For fall, say hello to heavier fabrics such as leather (or leather look fabrics) and wool.

## 3. Fit and Flare

![Fit and Flare](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_fit-and-flare_500x749.jpg)

Balance out the volume of a full skirt by keeping the top half of your outfit fairly fitted. Full skirts traditionally sit high on the waist, so tuck in a t-shirt or slim fitting blouse to work a well balanced look. When it comes how to wear a full skirt with jackets, stick to cropped or close fitting styles.

## 4. Vintage Inspired

![Vintage Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_vintage-inspired_553x830.jpg)

The full skirt fits well into this season’s retro trends. Work a vintage inspired angle and team your full skirt with a feminine blouse and pretty pumps. Top off the look with a framed handbag and cat eye sunglasses for a truly glamorous look.

## 5. Modern Dressing

![Modern Dressing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_modern-dressing_600x920.jpg)

When it comes to how to wear a full skirt, make it look more contemporary by teaming it with up-to-date clothing and accessories. For an off-duty look, try teaming a full skirt with a plain white t-shirt, denim jacket, and wayfarer sunglasses. Crop tops are also a popular choice at the moment and look great when teamed with high waisted, full skirts.

## 6. Shoe Styles

![Shoe Styles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_shoe-styles_600x595.jpg)

One of the hottest looks right now is a full skirt teamed with a pointed court shoe. It’s a flattering look that is quite feminine. As for flats, you can’t go past ballet flats or low-heeled pumps.

## 7. PetticoatS

_ Give your full skirt that extra bit of volume by wearing a petticoat underneath. This tip for how to wear a full skirt will add fullness to your skirt as well as helping it keep its shape. A peek of lace or tulle underneath can also give your outfit a slyly seductive touch.

When looking at how to wear a full skirt, the look can be modern or vintage inspired. Either way, it’s incredibly on-trend. What’s your best tip for how to wear full skirts this season?
