# 7 Daring Digital Print Dresses ...

**Digital Print Dresses** would make a cutting edge addition to any wardrobe. The technique of digitally printing a pattern or photograph onto fabric is one that has been popular with many designers recently. The end result is one that is quite dramatic. The process is quite expensive, and as a result many **digital print dresses** are out of the price range of the ordinary consumer. However, there’s no harm in looking, right? Check out a few daring **digital print dresses** below.

## 1. Peter Pilotto AKS Dress

_ **Price:** $825.00 at mytheresa.com_
This dress features a vibrant watercolour-effect print. It has a sleeveless, fitted design and features a structured bustier bodice. This dress also features drape detailing on the skirt. Wear this dress with wedge booties, a patent clutch, and cropped jacket for a fashion-forward look.

## 2. Roberto Cavalli Printed Silk-Jersey Dress

![Roberto Cavalli Printed Silk-Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vc/c6/esbrl0m354c9330c6457a853477532_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $1450.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oFKUSY)
Digital print dresses often feature bold photographic images. This figure hugging dress features a scenic-inspired print. It has a round neck, long sleeves, and ruching at the sides. The drawstring ties at the side allow for the length to be adjusted. This dress would look great teamed with a leather jacket and heeled ankle boots.

## 3. Zero+ MariaCornejo Digital Print Dress

![Zero+ MariaCornejo Digital Print Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/za/p3/jf9e55si54c9330d3b1c0407544056_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/c6umVi)
This dress has a very contemporary design. It has a fairly streamlined cut, which showcases the striking digital print. It has a round neck, singled draped sleeve, and simply slips on. Wear dress with tights and knee-high boots, and accessories with bold black jewellery.

## 4. Marni Printed Gauze Dress

![Marni Printed Gauze Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/Marni-Printed-Gauze-Dress_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $321.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The abstract digital print on this dress is certainly eye-catching. It is made from multicoloured gauze and has a wide round neck, short sleeves, and unfinished edges. It is designed to be a loose fit and runs true to size. Digital print dresses like this look great teamed with some neutral sandals and Lucite accessories.

## 5. Mary Katrantzou

![Mary Katrantzou](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pn/hz/g6gx8n1n54c9330e21efe409297470_330x384.jpg)

**Price:** $996.00 at [brownsfashion.com](http://www.brownsfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Digital print dresses don’t get much more daring than this one. This dress features an all-over digital zebra print. It has a strapless design with a large fan detail that sits across the bust. This dress would be ideal for cocktail functions or other evening events. Pair it with some strappy sandals, a compact clutch, and chandelier earrings.

## 6. Zero+ MariaCornejo Pia Digital Print Stretch-Silk Dress

![Zero+ MariaCornejo Pia Digital Print Stretch-Silk Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g3/vt/rv1lsyie54c9330eedc24391541129_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/55h6IX)
This stretch-silk gown would make a directional choice of eveningwear. This dress has one cap sleeve and one shoulder strap, slits at the front and sides, and an asymmetric hem. It features a scenic dusk print on the front. Wear this dress with a pair of black sandals and a patent clutch.

## 7. Lanvin Printed Silk-Gazar Dress

![Lanvin Printed Silk-Gazar Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/z9/up6id03j54c9330fac739120125735_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $6800.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/H7s6Js)
Floral digital print dresses aren’t typically thought of as ‘daring,' but this one features a bold black and white digital print. This dress has a round neck, long sleeves, and an uneven hem. It features sculptural pleating throughout. This dress would be great for a night out, and worn with silver heels and a large boxy clutch.

While **digital print dresses** are quite pricey, hopefully once the practice becomes more common we might see a drop in prices. Be sure to keep an eye out for more affordable limited edition **digital print dresses** in chain stores as well. What are your thoughts on these **digital print dresses**?
