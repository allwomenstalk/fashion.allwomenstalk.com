# 8 Skinny Statement Belts ...

A belt is not only functional; it can also act as a stylish addition to an outfit. There are a range of different styles out there. At the moment, it’s all about the skinny belt. Pick one in a bright colour or interesting pattern and make it the stand-out piece of your outfit. Why not try these skinny statement belts on for size?

## 1. Zara Leather Belt

_ **Price:** $39.00 at zara.com_
This sleek green belt is quite the attention-grabber. Jewel tones are an exciting trend for this season. The colour is fresh and is perfect for accenting an on-trend neutral colour palette. Use it to nip in a tan military style shirt dress.

## 2. Topshop Tan Scotty Dog Clasp Leather Skinny Belt

![Topshop Tan Scotty Dog Clasp Leather Skinny Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/xc/plq8g5x054c8f7627cf58876775847_400x184.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/NdJCkP)
If you’re a canine lover then you simply can’t go past this cute belt. It’s made from tan leather and features a Scotty dog clasp. The clasps are finished in an antique-looking burnished metal. Wear this belt over layered cardigans and sweaters.

## 3. Topshop Gold Metal Bar Belt

![Topshop Gold Metal Bar Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/ov/br1lw7jf54c8f76367287630768492_400x48.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8KwF0d)
This gold belt is all about refined glamour. The design is simple and sleek, yet still exudes volumes of style. The body of the belt is cord-like in design, and it features a thick bar detail clasp at the front. Worn at the waist, this would work well with a draped Grecian-style dress.

## 4. Asos Polka Dot Skinny Belt

![Asos Polka Dot Skinny Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/f8/ph84oo8z54c8f7643ed7f692764802_400x179.jpg)

**Price:** $14.34 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Mp4W6d)
I do love a good polka dot number. This red and white polka-dotted belt is no exception. It is adjustable and features a silver-tone buckle. I could imagine this belt being worn at the waist of a full-skirted 50s style black dress.

## 5. Asos Fantasy Leather Leopard Belt

![Asos Fantasy Leather Leopard Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/t8/v1cfg3fv54c8f7651fcd8313120084_400x184.jpg)

**Price:** $21.52 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/UtVzl2)
Subtle leopard print accessories are a terrific way to spice up an outfit. If you’re after a stylish statement belt, look no further than this one from Asos. It’s a leather-look belt and features a gold-tone buckle and classic notch fastenings. This belt is quite versatile and would suit a wide range of colour palettes. Personally, I’d team it with navy pieces.

## 6. J.Crew Teenie Glitter Leather Belt

![J.Crew Teenie Glitter Leather Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/fz/junr6oh554c8f765f1784836020930_400x161.jpg)

**Price:** $24.50 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ubJPLx)
This shimmering skinny leather belt is a sure fire way to add a bit of bling to your wardrobe. It has a gunmetal buckle fastening and silver glitter finish. Dress it down during the day and pair it with slouchy knits. At night, use it to cinch in a silky dress.

## 7. Asos Twist Bow Waist Belt

![Asos Twist Bow Waist Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/xv/bwo8526654c8f766c98c8583710461_400x136.jpg)

**Price:** $21.52 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/w0Evd0)
Bold, bright colours will always make great statement pieces. This leather-look waist belt features a twisted bow detail at the centre. It’s an ingenious, yet simple design feature. This look can easily be recreated at home with a thin strip of leather or material.

## 8. Topshop Pale Pink Leather Flower Cutout Skinny Belt

![Topshop Pale Pink Leather Flower Cutout Skinny Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h9/pt/uwkxb4kq54c8f767c49d0978608452_400x206.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/S944np)
The flower cut-out details on this belt are what really stand out for me. The pale pink leather is a nice touch. The overall design is soft and sweet. This belt is 100% leather and comes in three sizes ranging from small to large.

Skinny belts can be worn a myriad of ways – to cinch in a loose dress or simply looped through the waistband of your pants. Which of these skinny statement belts would you most like to be wearing?
