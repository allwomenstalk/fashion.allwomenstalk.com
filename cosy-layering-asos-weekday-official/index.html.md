## The Ultimate Layering Formula

So what’s the secret to nailing that effortless, thrown-together look? It’s all about mixing textures and silhouettes. Start with a crisp shirt, add a slim vest, and top it all off with a big, fluffy bomber or a sweeping trench. Don’t forget those little touches—rings, a playful bag charm, or peekaboo socks. The result? An outfit that looks like you spent hours planning, but really just came together in a flash.
