# 7 Great Looks from the New Victoria Beckham Collection ...

Have you seen the new Victoria Beckham collection? She’s just released her Autumn/Winter 2013 clothing online, and I’ve been totally blown away. It seems VB is still embracing the androgynous style that she’s been rocking for the past few months, and her new collection features plenty of sharp tailoring and simple shapes, too. Here are my first thoughts on the new Victoria Beckham collection…

## 1. Space Style

![Space Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013659_2790_166x439.jpg)

I have totally fallen in love with this space shirt. I mean, how cute is that?! The shirt itself is super-flattering, with a chic collar and short, loose sleeves, but it’s the galaxy print that I’m loving the most. And if space is a big theme in a Victoria Beckham collection, you can be sure that the high street will be embracing the trend!

## 2. Classic White

![Classic White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013721_2262_152x441.jpg)

Everyone should own a white shirt. They are so versatile! Whether you need something formal for an interview, want to dress up for a Christening, need to dress up jeans… a flattering, fitted white shirt is a wardrobe essential. That’s why I’m so pleased VB has made one – it looks gorgeous!

## 3. Polka Prints

![Polka Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013740_0653_148x441.jpg)

Some prints just won’t die, and polka dots are definitely one. They’ve become a classic! VB gives them an edgier look by opting for a sleeveless shirt in gray and black, and brightening it up with a white collar and stripe. It looks really cute teamed with the models’ knee-high boots and black skirt, but looks really cute worn with jeans, too.

## 4. Sequins

![Sequins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013803_1515_231x471.jpg)

Victoria Beckham rarely does sequins or glitter. Her glamor comes from the way she wears normal fabrics and that famous pout, rather than from eye-catching materials. She’s embraced sequins here, though, giving them a sports luxe feel by covering a loose jersey dress with shimmering blue. The plain black sleeves, belt and border keep it subtle and wearable.

## 5. The Tux

![The Tux](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013818_6748_230x471.jpg)

I LOVE tuxedos, and this is a great way to wear it! Victoria has created her own version of the denim dungaree trend, opting for a loose fit and teaming it with on-trend monochrome. Interestingly, VB got her model to ditch the knee-high boots here, and opted for plain black flats instead. Simple is definitely the way to own this look, the model looks totally cute.

## 6. Stripes

![Stripes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013846_3648_322x471.jpg)

Love all the retro minidresses that are around at the moment? VB gives them an almost effortless update by combining that classic shape with bold burgundy, black and navy stripes. The way the dress is striped creates some super-flattering paneling, making it perfect for occasions when you need to impress. Copy the model's look for a typically 80’s style – a blunt fringe and flat boots.

## 7. The New Midi

![The New Midi](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22013908_8940_319x471.jpg)

It wouldn’t be a Victoria Beckham collection without a midi dress! This berry-red dress is made of a really soft wool, with a comfortable and cool silk lining. The belt is placed to elongate the waist and it’s the perfect height for making you look taller – I’m not usually a midi fan, but I’d love a dress like this for those cold winter mornings.

While the prices in the Victoria Beckham collection might be a little out of reach for the time being, I’m loving the styles – and it’s a great peek into what trends will shortly be all over the high street. What are you most looking forward to?
