# 7 Super Stylish Maxi Skirts ...

Maxi Skirts can be a versatile part of any wardrobe. Depending on the style and fabric, they can be the perfect transeasonal piece. Maxi skirts are available to suit a range of different styles and trends. Simple column skirts are great for achieving a minimalist aesthetic, while floaty chiffon skirts are perfect for creating a feminine outfit. Take a look at few super stylish maxi skirts below.

## 1. Topshop Full Maxi Skirt

![Topshop Full Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/h4/bvm6fsr954c92fb9bbaa7286892767_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lwpn4E)
This teal skirt is a great way to work richer, jewel-toned colours into your outfit. It has a fitted waistband and button fastening at the side. The gathered waistband creates a full, flowing effect. Maxi skirts like this would look great in the cooler months paired with some lace up boots and a chunky knit cardigan.

## 2. T by Alexander Wang Long Silk Skirt

![T by Alexander Wang Long Silk Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/c0/k4w7gs4f54c92fba8f1b0094506752_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Column skirts are a great alternative to the more common full or pleated maxi skirts. This silk skirt has a side-zip closure and bias seam detail. This slim, straight fitting skirt is perfect for creating a lengthened silhouette. Team this skirt with a jersey tank top, collar necklace, and strappy sandals.

## 3. Textile Elizabeth and James Riley Crepe Maxi Skirt

![Textile Elizabeth and James Riley Crepe Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/pg/rbi7616t54c92fbb4522d375893434_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AiTR5c)
This navy crepe maxi skirt could easily be dressed up or down. It has casual details such as a drawstring waist, side pockets, and faux fly front. The navy fabric, however, is quite timeless. Team this with a striped tank top, straw hat, and leather sandals for a nautical inspired look.

## 4. Topshop Pleated Maxi Skirt

![Topshop Pleated Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lo/pi/xz6qa4jj54c92fbc1adbc315396458_267x399.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wo63uh)
Pleated maxi skirts are still a popular choice. This sheer navy skirt comes with a half-slip detail. Navy is an on-trend choice this season, and looks great with neutral creams and browns. This skirt would look fantastic with a sleeveless collared shirt and blush ballet flats.

## 5. Helmut Lang Draped Brushed-Jersey Maxi Skirt

![Helmut Lang Draped Brushed-Jersey Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/rv/y9beyw0j54c92fbcd60b0068723463_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $370.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/g94sfd)
The draping detail on this maxi skirt really sets it apart. It is made from a taupe brushed jersey fabric and has an elastic waistband, asymmetric draping, and a front split. The overall effect is that of a wrap skirt. Dress this skirt up by teaming it with a sequin top and stiletto sandals.

## 6. Topshop Basic Maxi Skirt

![Topshop Basic Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/ia/imnf2w8n54c92fbda8086862666186_268x399.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/2A79xn)
If you’re after a basic and affordable maxi skirt, then take a look at this version from Topshop. It is made from jersey fabric, features an elastic waistband and has a simple column-like design. It’s ideal for creating the perfectly layered outfit this fall. Team it with a denim shirt, burgundy knit, and wedge boots for an on-trend look.

## 7. Sandro Jarod Pleated Maxi Skirt

![Sandro Jarod Pleated Maxi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wp/jh/hquvm1al54c92fbe6a8a6704179670_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $335.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ns2VTA)
Pleated maxi skirts are great for working a ballet-inspired look. This elegant cream skirt is made from semi sheer pleated chiffon and is fully lined. It also has a zip and button fastening at the side. Pair this skirt with a mint green tank top and wrap around sandals for a fresh, carefree look.

Floor skimming skirts are a dramatic addition to any outfit. These maxi skirts are just an example of what is currently available online. Which of these maxi skirts suits your style best?
