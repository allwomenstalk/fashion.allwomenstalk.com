# 7 Chic White Trousers ...

White trousers may seem like a basic wardrobe item, but they are surprising hard to pull off. While they may conjure images of bad 90s fashion and a Euro-trash aesthetic, the white trouser is enjoying a new fashion lease of life. To work the contemporary white trouser into your wardrobe, choose a pair that is tailored and well-fitted. Below are a few chic white trousers that you may want to check out.

## 1. Topshop Ecru Tapered Trousers

![Topshop Ecru Tapered Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p2/z8/mh1zd52t54c8fa2457fbf940901439_267x398.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/YAaC5P)
These chic white trousers are affordable and stylish. They are a slightly off-white, ecru colour. These trousers feature a wide waistband, pleats at the thigh, and cuffed hems. The button tab detail on the waistband is a novel design aspect. These trousers would make a comfortable option for everyday dressing.

## 2. Isabel Marant Garett Tencel and Linen-Blend Pants

![Isabel Marant Garett Tencel and Linen-Blend Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kp/n0/ysii2qjo54c8fa2500c7e280906662_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $405.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YKB2Pj)
These linen-blend trousers are a great option for warm-weather dressing. The linen-blend ensures breathability while still maintaining a tailored look. These trousers have a ruched elastic waist, pleats through the front, and slanted pockets at the side. They also have a concealed hook and zip fly fastening. Complete the chic, tailored look with subtle colours and a structured blazer.

## 3. Alexander Wang Washed Satin Pant

_ **Price:** $520.00 at lagarconne.com_
These washed satin trousers are a contemporary take on a classic design. As the purveyor of ‘cool’, Alexander Wang has created a trouser that is destined to reach cult status among the fashion pack. These chic white trousers are a relaxed fit and feature a seamed gusset, rose gold hardware, and zipped cuffs. Wear them with a fitted nude tank and some statement wedges.

## 4. Sportmax Cropped Cotton Trousers

![Sportmax Cropped Cotton Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/g6/bdf0adnn54c8fa26a05b7651648262_323x387.jpg)

**Price:** $255.96 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These cropped cotton trousers are a fresh take on the trend. The cropped length makes them the ideal option for casual daywear. The white trouser provides the perfect canvas to work with an arrangement of bright colour palettes. Team these trousers with some statement ankle books to make the most of the cropped length.

## 5. Lover High-Waisted Twill Wide-Leg Pants

![Lover High-Waisted Twill Wide-Leg Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jf/no/st0ku33454c8fa274edea762794486_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $370.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/3vGNwH)
Wide legged trousers are enjoying increased popularity. The exaggerated shape works well with the contemporary 70s inspired silhouette that has been seen all over the latest runways. These high-waisted trousers feature pleats throughout the front, slanted side pockets, and a concealed zip fastening. Balance the volume on the bottom half of the body by wearing keeping the top half quite fitted.

## 6. Planet White Wide Legged Linen Trousers

![Planet White Wide Legged Linen Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/r3/v2kv81jq54c8fa2826f43973842085_400x370.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [debenhams.com](https://www.debenhams.com/)
These are another take on the wide legged trouser. While the previous Lover trousers feature voluminous pleats, these Planet trousers are streamlined and tailored. These trousers feature crisp front creases, button tab detailing, and a zip fly fastening. Incorporate a pastel colour palette when wearing these pants for a contemporary, softly tailored look.

## 7. Dorothy Perkins White Bow Pleat Leg Trousers

![Dorothy Perkins White Bow Pleat Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/db/keisg7as54c8fa28db57c299579778_295x394.jpg)

**Price:** $46.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These chic white trousers come with a contrasting self-tie bow belt. They have pleating down the front, a zip fly fastening, and are slim throughout the leg. The tan coloured bow belt is a novel way to update the white trouser trend. Limit your outfit to a neutral colour palette to really let the bow belt stand out.

There are a range of chic white trousers to suit all styles and budgets. Will you be wearing this trend this season?
