# 7 Amazing Accessories for Summer ...

Want to get amped up for the upcoming summer with super cute and trendy accessories? Nothing gets me in the summer mood like brightly colored and perfectly on-trend necklaces, rings and earrings! Start prepping for those long summer nights and daytrips with friends **with these 7 amazing accessories for summer**!

## 1. Inedible Jewelry Rainbow Snow Cone Necklace

_ **Price:** $22.00 at shanalogic.com_
Who doesn’t love this classic summer treat?! This deliciously cute summer accessory will get you in a fun and carefree mood and will remind you that summer fun is only days away! Sport this sweet treat on casual days like a movie date or coffee with girlfriends.

## 2. Crinkle in Time Scarf in Aqua

![Crinkle in Time Scarf in Aqua](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/Modcloth-Aqua-Scarf_378x514.jpg)

What makes this chic summer accessory even more amazing than your average? The beautiful soft aqua blue color will remind you of long summer days at the beach and the crinkly cotton- silk blend scarf with fringe details make it the perfect piece to throw on anytime you want to add some style and color to your ensemble.

## 3. Forever 21 Cracked Stone Necklace

_ **Price:** $7.80 at forever21.com_
Looking for a classy yet affordable summer necklace? Look no further than this irresistible cracked stone necklace. For less than $8, you can pair this cheap and chic necklace with your favorite maxi or layer it with other necklaces with your favorite summer dress and you’re ready for a casual lunch date or dinner and drinks at the beach!

## 4. Ruche Shades of Floral Black Sunglasses

![Ruche Shades of Floral Black Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/Shades-of-Floral_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $14.99 at [shopruche.com](http://shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
Designer shades are great, but the designer prices, not so much. We all need some sunnies to protect our peepers and why not choose an affordable pair that features delicate florals on a edgy, black frame? This summer accessory is not only a necessity, but they’ll go great with all of your favorite summer ensembles all while adding a touch of feminine flowers.

## 5. ASOS Colorblock Fluro Belt with Double Buckle Detail

![ASOS Colorblock Fluro Belt with Double Buckle Detail](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/Asos-Fluro-Belt_290x370.jpg)

**Price:** $14.32 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/O8kj3H)
Incorporate a splash of neon into your wardrobe with this fun and funky belt! Rock this belt with a basic T-shirt and jeans or with a dress, either way you’re sure to wow spectators with this stylish accessory for summer. This belt is a great way to play with the neon and colorblock trend without going all out 80’s and looking like a highlighter explosion.

## 6. Forever 21 Stripe Band Fedora

_ **Price:** $10.80 at forever21.com_
Another hot weather necessity is a fun and stylish fedora. Save the floppy hat for the beach and rock this cool fedora with a casual T-shirt and shorts when you’re out running errands or meeting your BFF for fro-yo. Hats are the ultimate summer accessory and can easily transform a casual outfit from blah to bam!

## 7. ASOS Pastel Jelly Watch

![ASOS Pastel Jelly Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/Asos-Pastel-Watch_290x370.jpg)

**Price:** $32.23 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/BF8XV0)
Pastels are everywhere and if you’re not into wearing delicate pale colored clothing; this watch is a fun and feminine accent without overdosing on pastels. You can wear this chic watch to work or out with friends. It’s the perfect summer accessory that is sure to add some style and feminine accents to any outfit!

Accessories are a must for a fabulous and fashionable warm-weather look! Throw on some of these summer accessories for a perfectly polished and chic getup that will be sure to make you stand out from the crowd while incorporating hot colors and trends!
