# 7 Super Stylish Jackets for Fall ...

Stylish Jackets for Fall are a great way to transition your wardrobe into the colder months. Fall jackets are versatile and can be paired with anything from dresses to jeans. As it begins getting cooler outside, you’ll want to make sure you have one of these super stylish jackets on hand to keep you warm!

## 1. Penfield Kasson Parka

![Penfield Kasson Parka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/1_penfield-kasson-parka_216x418.jpg)

**Price:** $185.00 at [madewell.com](http://www.madewell.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This stylish jacket for fall is the most beautiful shade of blue! Parkas are on trend this fall and are the perfect weight for keeping you warm, but not too warm during the cooler fall months. I love its versatility. The weather-resistant material makes it great for rainy days. Pair it with jeans and wellies to keep warm and dry this fall!

## 2. The Row Garton Jacket

**Price:** $1490.00 at _barneys.com_

Color-blocking and blazers are two major trends this fall, and this stylish jacket has both! Dress up your fall outfits with this cozy blazer. I love the rose gold buttons paired with the midnight and ash blue color of the jacket. It’s a little heavier in weight than your typical blazer since it’s made from a wool crepe jersey, so it’s great for staying warm and looking dressy.

## 3. Rick Owens Classic Metallic Leather Biker Jacket

![Rick Owens Classic Metallic Leather Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/3_rick-owens-classic-metallic-leather-biker-jacket_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $2575.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/gqkKXh)

Leather biker jackets are always in style, and this is one of the best looking ones I’ve ever seen! This metallic gold textured-leather stylish jacket for fall is eye catching for sure. I am in love with the draped collar and tailored style of this jacket. The zipper accents and ribbed paneled sleeves are the perfect embellishments for this stunning jacket.

## 4. Burberry Prorsum Puffer Jacket

![Burberry Prorsum Puffer Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/4_burberry-prorsum-puffer-jacket_600x920.jpg)

**Price:** $1995.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You can’t go wrong with a puffer jacket for fall, and this might be the cutest one I have ever seen! With its ruched belt that’s secured with an adorable bow, puffed sleeves, and peplum-style waist, this jacket is perfectly stylish for fall. I’ve never seen a more feminine, adorable puffer jacket. Wear it with dark, denim skinnies and heels for an effortlessly stylish look for fall.

## 5. Evil Twin Archaic Eyelash Jacket

![Evil Twin Archaic Eyelash Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/5_evil-twin-archaic-eyelash-jacket_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $155.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This stylish jacket for fall has major personality! The name itself is enough to draw me in. The fuzzy "eyelashes" give it a faux fur appearance without the cruelty. The beautiful jewel-toned deep blue goes with everything. Wear this jacket out on the town to add flair to your outfit!

## 6. Chloe Button down Jacket

![Chloe Button down Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/6_chloe-button-down-jacket_400x534.jpg)

**Price:** $1565.00 at [farfetch.com](http://www.farfetch.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Orange is a color that I always associate with fall. This lovely, orange button down jacket will brighten up any outfit. So many people tend to stick with the typical black or brown jackets for fall. It’s time to branch out and add a pop of color to your wardrobe!

## 7. Carven Boiled Peplum Jacket

_ **Price:** $660.00 at [farfetch.com](http://www.farfetch.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

We all know peplum is a huge trend this fall season, so this peplum jacket is a must-have. This gorgeous, bright-white jacket may not be appropriate for every occasion, but it is beautiful and super stylish. It will add posh to every outfit. I love the hidden zipper and fitted, feminine shape of this jacket.

These gorgeous jackets will keep you warm and stylish this fall! Which of these super stylish jackets for fall do you like best? What would you pair with your favorite jacket?
