# 10 Wardrobe Tips for Girls with an Apple Shaped Body ...

Wondering **how to dress for apple shaped body type**? An apple shape body means you have a body shape like an apple where you carry most of your weight in your midsection. However, most of the women who have this body shape have great legs. These few tips will help you solve this imbalance to prevent you from looking much shorter and wider. Here's **how to dress for apple shaped body type**.

## 1. Cute a-line Shifts

Loose fitting clothing hanging straight from the shoulders is a very good choice to hide a full midsection. You can choose a short dress which shows off your great legs and make your body look more balanced. That's one of my best tips for **how to dress for apple shaped body type**.

## 2. Single Button Jackets

This is a really good choice of clothing to cover your torso and look more fashionable at the same time.

## 3. Wide Legged Trousers

Most of us might find this old fashioned, but they help draw all the attention to your sexy long legs. Any pants that flare at the legs work great.

## 4. Long Fitted Shirts and Sweaters

Long fitted shirts and sweaters that fall to the hips really help in covering a full midriff.

## 5. An Empire Cut Dress

An empire cut waist that falls just below the bust with an A-line or flared skirt is your best cut dress for any event that you are attending. Show some leg and you are ready to party.

## 6. Trousers Cut with a Higher Waistband

If you want to choose trousers with a simple straight cut, make sure to choose the one with a higher waistband that helps elongate your legs. Avoid anything with a low rise.

## 7. V-neck

A v-neck creates the illusion of an elongated torso and will make you look slimmer. You can also choose a cowl neck which gives the same result as v-neck.

## 8. Choosing a Bathing Suit

A one-piece bathing suit with a plunging neckline will help to define a waist. As for bikini, choose the one with a solid halter top and printed bottoms. To add more balance to your body, you can choose ones with ruffles, bows or ties on bikini bottoms.

## 9. Choose Long Necklaces and Pearls

A long necklace helps to draw the eyes down to your legs.

## 10. Bold Coloured Bottoms

You can also try bold coloured bottoms, including denim or skirts. To make it more exciting, you can try geometric or floral prints.
