# The New Hoodie That Makes Napping Easier than Ever ...

Have you ever fallen asleep in public? Well, if you don't have a problem with taking a nap on a train or on your college campus, you're going to want to buy the new hoodie from [Hypnos](https://bit.ly/1niP2Wk).

Why? Because it comes with an inflatable hood that you can use as a pillow!

The company claims that they're "redefining the hooded sweatshirt for sleep through an engineered, patent pending pillow system."

The best part? You don't have to worry about looking like a fool, because when the hood isn't inflated, it looks just like a regular jacket. See?
