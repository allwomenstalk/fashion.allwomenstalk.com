# 8 Styling Ways to Work the Turtleneck ...

Knowing different ways to wear turtleneck tops will be the key to making the most of this season’s must-have staple. While turtleneck tops usually have a bit of a dowdy or nerdy reputation, they can look all sorts of fashionable when styled right. Wear them underneath pinafore style dresses or with crisp cigarette trousers for an on-trend look. Check out these best ways to wear turtlenecks, below.

## 1. Under Dresses

Make your warm weather dresses last that little bit longer by layering on a plain turtleneck underneath. Black, white, and neutral turtlenecks are your best bet for creating a range of versatile looks. When looking at ways to wear turtlenecks this way, stick to dresses with scooped or plunging necklines for a more flattering look.

## 2. With Tuxedo Trousers

As far as current trends go, it’s all about the tuxedo right now! Luckily, a simple fitted turtleneck looks oh so styling when teamed with a chic pair of tuxedo pants. Finish your tuxedo inspired look off with some ladylike pumps and a cute clutch and you’ve got yourself an extremely on-trend outfit.

## 3. With Blazers

For office-friendly and smart casual attire, wear your turtleneck tops with a tailored blazer. It’s a timeless combination that’s perfect for wearing during the day and night. Add a dainty pendant necklace and wear the turtleneck/blazer combination with trousers or a pencil skirt for a smart and stylish look.

## 4. Tucked into Maxi Skirts

Tuck in a fitted turtleneck top into a flowing maxi skirt for a luxe look this season. When pulling off this look, stick to gathered or pleated maxi skirts in chiffon or silky fabrics. It’ll look elegant and feminine, and can give off ballet-inspired vibes. This kind of outfit can be worn during the day or night depending on the colour palette and textural elements.

## 5. Under Vests

Turtleneck tops are the prefect layering piece come winter. And what better way to layer them than with one of the must-have garments this season - the vest? Choose a fitted style of turtleneck in a plain colour to wear under an assortment of vests this season. Whether it’s a faux fur vest, shaggy knitted vest, or good old leather vest; the vest is stylish layering option and looks great teamed with a turtleneck top.

## 6. Layered with a Statement Necklace

When it comes to ways to wear turtlenecks, use yours as a backdrop for a stunning statement necklace. Metals can be cold on the skin during winter so a turtleneck top is the perfect solution for keeping your skin warm but still being able to show off all your stunning statement jewels.

## 7. With Leather Jackets

Nothing says edgy and cool quite like a leather jacket. Make your turtleneck top look all sorts of stylish this winter by teaming it with a chic leather jacket. Try biker style jackets for an urban, off-duty look and to take away from the dowdy reputation of the turtleneck.

## 8. With Sequins

Give your turtleneck top a taste of the old razzle dazzle by teaming it with a stylish sequin skirt. This way to wear a turtleneck is perfect for working a fun evening look. Top your look off with a thin necklace, compact clutch, and strappy heels for a stylish outfit to last you through the night.

Winter is the perfect time to break out the turtleneck tops. These are just a few ways to wear turtleneck tops to try out this season. Are you on board the turtleneck bandwagon? What are your top ways to wear turtlenecks?
