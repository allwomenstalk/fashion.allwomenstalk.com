# 7 of Kate Middletons Best Fashion Looks That We Cant Get over ...

The Duchess of Cambridge is one of the most popular fashion icons and it is easy to see why after getting a look at Kate Middleton’s best fashion looks. She has such an effortless look to everything she wears, yet she always looks put together. If you are looking for a style icon who is feminine and classic, Kate Middleton is your best bet. While I could write novels about all of her amazing outfits, here are a few of my personal favorites of Kate Middleton’s best fashion looks.

## 1. Casual and Comfy

![Casual and Comfy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103614_2394_600x780.jpg)

While most of Kate Middleton’s best fashion looks are from formal events, my personal favorite is her in a causal look. Few other women could pull off such an effortless look while still looking polished and put together. Kate knows how to pair her clothing to create a uniquely-hers look that is still royally approved.

## 2. Pregnant in Pink

![Pregnant in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103614_2833_600x780.jpg)

Even thought she didn’t look as pregnant as us “common folk,” Kate Middleton looked flawless in this light pink Alexander McQueen jacket. She knew exactly what jacket would highlighter her baby bump in the best manner and paired it perfectly with a matching pink hat. It was even voted by “Hello Daily News” as her best maternity outfit.

## 3. Turquoise Gown

![Turquoise Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103614_2079_600x780.jpg)

While she usually sticks to neutral or lighter colors, Kate stepped out in this incredible Jenny Packham turquoise gown for the Our Greatest Team Rises event. Kate has always known how to dress for her body type, and this dress is no exception. It belts to show off her tiny waist and the neckline flattered her perfectly. To emphasize the dress, she put her hair into an updo and kept her makeup natural.

## 4. BAFTA Beauty

![BAFTA Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103613_2281_600x899.jpg)

During Will and Kate’s trip to America, they stopped by the BAFTA awards where Kate was a vision in this Alexander McQueen gown. Like her usual dresses, this one was belted to emphasize her incredible figure. What I love about this dress is that it is so simple yet so sexy. She looks absolutely glamorous, but she also looks like she put no effort into getting ready, which any girl knows is a huge feat.

## 5. Floral Frock

![Floral Frock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103613_0007_600x899.jpg)

Kate is notorious for wearing simple clothes and rarely wears patterns. But based on this adorable floral dress, she should look into wearing prints more often. What I love about this outfit is that she wears a print that is not too overwhelming. In her royal life, she is not allowed to be outlandish with her clothes and this dress is a good balance. She also pairs it perfectly with a neutral clutch and nude strappy heels.

## 6. Asian Inspired

![Asian Inspired](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103614_6298_600x780.jpg)

In all of Kate’s outfits, she sticks to a classic English look that is not too bold and is relatively simple. That is what makes this Asian inspired dress one of Kate Middleton’s best fashion looks. She blends her classic style with Asian influence and the outcome is perfection.

## 7. Navy Engagement

![Navy Engagement](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18103615_0496_600x899.jpg)

How could I not include this navy Alexander McQueen dress? This dress is one of the most iconic Kate Middleton dresses and for good reason. She wore this as she and Prince William announced their engagement to the world. My favorite thing about this adorable wrap dress is that she matched it to her engagement ring. The outcome: a flawless look that will remain iconic for quite some time.

It is so hard to pick just a few looks because Kate Middleton looks flawless no matter what she wears. She looks just as fabulous in jeans and a blazer as she does in a gown. What did you think of the outfits I picked as Kate Middleton’s best fashion looks? What are some other great fashion moments she has had? What is your favorite thing about Kate’s style?
