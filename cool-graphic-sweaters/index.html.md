# 7 Cool Graphic Sweaters ...

Quirky and whimsical, cool graphic sweaters are the new essential for cozy fall layering. Unabashedly loud, graphic prints, patterns and pictures make bold sweater statements perfect for creating a playful off-duty look suitable for weekend wear. Steal the spotlight this season and make cool graphic sweaters your go-to layer for fall.

## 1. Aubin & Willis Fox Motif Sweater

![Aubin & Willis Fox Motif Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_aubin-willis-fox-motif-sweater_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $215 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/FOhUkn)
Animal motifs were seen on the runways of all your favourite designers and have been a huge hit ever since. Foxy and fun, this offbeat sweater will have you embracing your wild side for a playful way to stay on-trend. The subtle graphic allows you to dress this sweater up or down to create a cute yet chic look suitable for work or weekend wear.

## 2. River Island Chelsea Girl Print Sweater

![River Island Chelsea Girl Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_river-island-chelsea-girl-print-sweater_430x430.jpg)

**Price:** $35 at [riverisland.com](http://www.riverisland.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The vintage, cross-stitched feel of this graphic sweater has a soft edge to it. Cutesy heart and rosebud details add a feminine touch and pop of colour to the all black knit sweater, which can be paired with just about anything. The cool large handwritten "girl" print makes this not just another grandma sweater.

## 3. Joe Fresh Graphic Sweater

![Joe Fresh Graphic Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_joe-fresh-graphic-sweater_359x326.jpg)

**Price:** $29 at [joefresh.com](https://www.joefresh.com/ca/404)
Striking and strong, this multi-colour sweater with vivid red tones and line display from Joe Fresh taps into the popular geometric trend for fall. The stripped pattern commands attention and directs your eyes to the most flattering places on your body for a look that slims and accentuates. With eye-catching detail, mix and match with other patterns for a high fashion statement.

## 4. French Connection Lady Owl Sequin Sweater

![French Connection Lady Owl Sequin Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_french-connection-lady-owl-sequin-sweater_272x352.jpg)

**Price:** $128 at [usa.frenchconnection.com](http://usa.frenchconnection.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Another option in the animal motif trend is this sequined owl sweater by French Connection. The simple and sweet design is a subdued way to introduce cool graphics into your wardrobe if you are print and pattern shy. Black and light rose-colored sequins allow light to reflect off this feminine embroidery, adding shimmer and shine to any outfit.

## 5. Topshop Knitted Aztec Motif Sweater

![Topshop Knitted Aztec Motif Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_topshop-knitted-aztec-motif-sweater_280x384.jpg)

**Price:** $42 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/rc5kIb)
To play up the tribal and Navajo trend this season, opt for this knit Aztec motif sweater from Topshop. The muted colours allow you to pair this jumper with almost anything, making it a cool simple way to add print and colour into your wardrobe this fall. If you loved the tribal trend this summer and aren’t ready to give it up just yet, the tribal trend has made its mark in fashion and it’s clear it’s here to stay for a while, so invest in this noteworthy piece while you still can.

## 6. H&M Merino Sweater

![H&M Merino Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_h-m-merino-sweater_384x449.jpg)

**Price:** $49.95 at [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Become a scenic beauty in this watercolor wash print with dreamlike quality. The soft and elaborate detail of this nature scene is printed on silk for a luxe and refined way to incorporate graphic print sweaters into your wardrobe. A rich jewel colour like dark emerald green makes this graphic pop at the center of attention. Pair this sweater with a pencil skirt and pointed pumps for a polished and ladylike way to wear graphics.

## 7. She inside Pop Art Sweater

_ **Price:** $38.40 at [sheinside.com](https://goo.gl/wvEIpn)
Playful pop art is super stylish and artistic. This fun graphic image makes a bold, loud statement with abstract art. Perfect for a weekend of lounging or running errands, this sweater is an easy and effortless way to stay off-duty chic. Combine this sweater with a skinny jean and ultra-trendy wedge sneaker for the ultimate urban uniform ideal for the girl on the go.

When layering up for fall opt for the offbeat trail and standout from the crowd in a cool graphic sweater. From bold lettering to geometric designs, an in your face print is a definite crowd pleaser with eye-catching designs and playful patterns that demand attention. Are you daring enough to try this seriously loud trend? How will you wear your graphic sweater?
