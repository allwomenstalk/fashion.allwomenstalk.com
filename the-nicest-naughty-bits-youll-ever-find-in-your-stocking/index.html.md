# The Nicest Naughty Bits Youll Ever Find in Your Stocking ...

Finding just the right stocking stuffers for your loved ones can be a little difficult, because everything's the same. If you want something really special for the cool girls in your life, you need to think outside the box. Maybe … maybe even start thinking in French. Remember when we introduced you to [Locher's](https://goo.gl/0r8UMz) a while ago? The provocative brand definitely sparked some interest, so in response to requests for more, more, more (and as proof that you can display a naughty sense of humor while still being both stylish and classy), we thought you might like to check out these cheeky, charming pieces – which really are the nicest naughty bits you'll ever find in a stocking!

## 1. The Secret Insult

![The Secret Insult](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/w6/pk1csyh2_374x476.jpg)

This is the perfect stocking stuffer for the bestie or loved one who has to deal with people she hates all day long, especially if she can't tell her coworkers, classmates, or roommates how badly they suck. “Vous êtes tous cons” looks like such a pretty phrase, especially surrounded by glam silver glitter and accented by two lovely little charms. In reality the wearer is announcing to everyone in the vicinity, “You are all idiots.” Don't you wish you could say that sometimes?

## 2. Classy Curses

![Classy Curses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/av/fkwj60tl_374x476.jpg)

You're going to have to translate this one for yourselves, my smart little stalkers! However, it's also an excellent choice for those days when you just want to shout at the top of your lungs – especially if your curse of choice corresponds with the phrase on this glittering gold piece.

## 3. Pain in the...

![Pain in the...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/vl/wwf4vfdt_374x476.jpg)

Oh, we all have that friend. She knows she's a little pain in the butt, and she's proud of it. Heck, maybe it's you – there's no saying you can't get a stocking stuffer for yourself! Besides, announcing your own cheek has never been so chic!

## 4. Life of the Party

_ Do you have a friend who makes the party? Someone so special that real life just seems a little dim and dull when she's not around? Let her know with this gorgeous locket! Actually, I love these Locher's pieces_ because you can choose from several different messages, all of which will delight the recipient!

## 5. Wonderbuns!

![Wonderbuns!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/vs/f1h8l89g_374x476.jpg)

There's no other way to say it! Again, however, you can choose different nicknames – Wonderbuns, Hot Mama, Psycho, Sweet Thang, Sugarlips, and more. I just love these little envelopes. You don't see jewelry like this every day. It's both vintage-inspired and gorgeously glamorous. What more could you ask for?

## 6. Pretty Punishment

_ Okay, so clothes_ aren't usually stocking stuffers, but really, they could be. If you have a friend who knows she ought to be chastised for her cheek, this is all you need. The embroidery is sweet as can be, but the message is absolutely saucy!

## 7. Good Girls Gone Bad

![Good Girls Gone Bad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/b4/sy3mw6ep_432x560.jpg)

We all have this friend too, the girl who tries so very hard to be good. She may not always succeed – maybe she's super sarcastic, a huge flirt, or the kind of awesome girl who is bluntly honest and always says what's on her mind – but why not reward her efforts?

The really great thing about Locher's is that every gift comes with a really positive affirmation that tells you just how amazing, beautiful, and unique you are. That in itself is a wonderful gift, so why not treat the epic girls in your life to something so uplifting? Remember, though, these goodies ship from Paris, so don't waste a second! Think you might like to gift your girls with a little fabulous French style?
