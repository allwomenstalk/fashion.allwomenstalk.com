# 17 Stylish Ways for Women to Wear Crop Tops This Season

Now that the weather is warming up, it’s the perfect time to break out your favorite crop tops. If you’re looking for fresh inspiration, here are a few stylish ways for women to wear them with confidence.

## 1. With a High-Waisted Skirt

You don't have to show a ton of skin to rock a crop top. Pairing it with a high-waisted skirt is a chic, balanced option that offers just the right amount of coverage while keeping the look fun and modern.

## 2. With Your Favorite Shorts

When the temperature spikes, feel free to show some skin! Crop tops paired with high-quality denim shorts are a timeless summer staple that lets you stay cool while looking put-together.

## 3. With a Matching Set

If you want an effortless outfit, go for a matching set. Many brands now sell crop tops paired with skirts or pants in identical patterns or fabrics, taking the guesswork out of styling your look.

## 4. With a Button-Down Shirt

Layering is key for transitional weather. Try wearing an unbuttoned shirt over your crop top for a laid-back, effortlessly cool vibe that works for coffee dates or casual outings.
