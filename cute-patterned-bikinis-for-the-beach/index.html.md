# 8 Cute Patterned Bikinis for the Beach ...

Patterned bikini styles are a fun option for the beach. If you’re sick of plain, block coloured bikinis then try one with a pretty pattern. There are plenty of cute and affordable patterned bikinis available at the moment. It’s just up to you to find one in a pattern that takes your fancy. Take a look at a few cute patterned bikinis, below.

## 1. Topshop Frill Underwire Bikini

![Topshop Frill Underwire Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_topshop-frill-underwire-bikini_210x400.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lbU0rG)
This patterned bikini features plenty of cute detailing. It has frill details on along the bust and hips, and features a fruity clementine print. The bikini top has underwiring with a clasp closure at the back. Accessorise this bikini with a pair of oversized white sunglasses for a retro-inspired beach look.

## 2. Dorothy Perkins Floral Patterned Bikini

![Dorothy Perkins Floral Patterned Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_dorothy-perkins-floral-patterned-bikini_339x400.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 (for matching set) at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This floral patterned bikini is such a sweet look. It has a shapely balconette style top with bow detailing at the bust and a thin halterneck strap. The bold floral pattern works quite nicely against the pink background. The bikini top is available at a price of $25 and the matching bottoms are available for $15.

## 3. Forever21 Roped Sailboat Bikini

_ **Price:** $20.60 at forever21.com_
The sailboat pattern on this bikini makes it ideal for wearing by the seaside. To complete the nautical theme, the bikini top has roped straps. The bikini and top are sold separately, but are still quite affordable when bought together.

## 4. Topshop Striped Bandeau Bikini

![Topshop Striped Bandeau Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_topshop-striped-bandeau-bikini_222x400.jpg)

**Price:** $52.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/9417PG)
Stripes are a classic look when it comes to swimwear, but this colour combination is much more on-trend. The combination of pink and blue just screams cute, and the bow details at the bust and hips sweeten the deal. The bikini top has a curved neckline and comes with an adjustable halter strap. Work a retro-inspired look by teaming this bikini with cat-eye sunglasses and a headscarf.

## 5. Forever21 Floral Cherry Bikini

_ **Price:** $20.60 at forever21.com_
This patterned bikini features a blue, red, and green cherry pattern. The bandeau bikini has a keyhole front and comes with self tie halter straps. The bikini bottoms come in a basic hipster style with elasticized trimmings and matching cherry print. The top and bottom are sold separately.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Gingham Patterned Bikini

![Dorothy Perkins Gingham Patterned Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_dorothy-perkins-gingham-patterned-bikini_367x400.jpg)

**Price:** $34.00 (for matching set) at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Retro-inspired swimwear is a big trend right now, and a pattern like gingham is a great way for working the trend. This bikini top has a gathered bust with bow detailing and has a self-tie halter strap. The bikini bottoms are a typical brief style with tie sides and are sold separately to the top.

## 7. Esther Williams Polka Dot Bikini

![Esther Williams Polka Dot Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_esther-williams-polka-dot-bikini_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $89.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you have a flair for vintage styling then take a look at this striking polka dot bikini. The patterned bikini top has a gathered bust and thick halter straps while the bottoms have low cut legs with ruching detail. This swimsuit is perfect for those who prefer a bit more coverage at the beach.

## 8. Topshop Floral Patterned Bikini

![Topshop Floral Patterned Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/8_topshop-floral-patterned-bikini_190x400.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/qnnljd)
Floral patterns go hand in hand with summer dressing. This floral patterned bikini has a push-up style bikini top with removable halter straps and a clasp fastening at the back. This playful bikini is perfect for getting around in at the beach this summer.

A patterned bikini is a fun way to ring in summer next time you’re at the beach. From pastels to primary colours, you’ll be able to find a range of cute patterned bikinis this season. Which of these patterned bikinis do you like best?
