# Top 10 Summer Street Fashion Looks of 2012 ...

Summer street fashion 2012 encompasses a range of trends. Whether you’re into bright and bold prints or sweet and subdued pastels, it’s easy to incorporate summer runway trends into your everyday outfits. Finding inspirational street fashion for summer 2012 is only a click away. Take inspiration from the following examples of summer street fashion for 2012.

## 1. Print on Print

_ life-is-a-luxury.tumblr.com_
Prints are everywhere this season. Wearing matching printed separates is a quirky interpretation of this summer street fashion 2012 look. Since prints can be quite busy, keep the rest of your look fairly simple with minimal accessories.

## 2. Retro Chic

_ ispiraremoda.tumblr.com_
Feminine, retro-inspired silhouettes were a popular feature on many designer catwalks for spring-summer 2012. Make this look work for you with a simple vintage-style dress and simple, streamlined accessories. If you want to take it up a notch, try adding some cat eye sunglasses for some extra glamour.

## 3. City Shorts

_ harribel.tumblr.com_
A chic pair of shorts is a must have when it comes to summer street fashion 2012. Work a tailored, contemporary look by teaming them with a loose blouse and crisp blazer. Lace-up Oxford shoes are also a great way of incorporating a menswear inspired twist to an outfit.

## 4. Sweet Pastels

![Sweet Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_sweet-pastels_500x750.jpg)

[afinefashionfrenzy.tumblr.com](https://afinefashionfrenzy.tumblr.com/post/25105479034)
Pastels are still as popular as ever, and a pastel dress will be a guaranteed style hit this summer. Lightweight, sheer fabrics are perfect for the season. Accessorise with metallic accessories and heels for a more polished look.

## 5. Summer Maxi Skirt

_ hola-g.tumblr.com_
Floor-sweeping hemlines are perfect for recreating some of the chicest summer street fashion 2012 looks. Take inspiration from this outfit and team a white maxi skirt with a basic gray tank top and cut-off denim vest for a youthful take on summer dressing.

## 6. Coloured Stripes

_ ispiraremoda.tumblr.com_
Stripes are timeless but this summer work a modern look by donning them in colour. A bold and bright striped garment is sure to get you noticed. The look is fresh, fun, and very surf inspired.

## 7. Slouchy Pants

_ reggiedowntherabbithole.tumblr.com_
Slouchy, lightweight pants are perfect for working a relaxed summer look. Choose a pair in a stylish print and team them with a plain tank and strappy sandals. As far as summer street fashion 2102 goes, this is the epitome of effortlessly stylish dressing.

## 8. Florals

![Florals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_florals_518x843.jpg)

Floral prints are a tried and true summer fashion favourite. If you’re feeling adventurous, try mixing two different floral prints together. Take a tip from this lovely lady and choose two different prints with a matching background colour and bring it all together with a crisp white blazer.

## 9. Colour Pop Dress

_ tuula.tumblr.com_
Nothing says summer more than a burst of colour. Add a colour pop dress to your wardrobe this summer for a fresh update. Citrus colours are a good choice, like this sunshine yellow number.

## 10. Marine Dream

![Marine Dream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/10_marine-dream_600x900.jpg)

[the-streetstyle.tumblr.com](https://the-streetstyle.tumblr.com/post/25237396977/color-drips)
The aquatic trend was a big one on the runways, and it’s set to filter down into summer street fashion for 2012. The easiest way to make this trend work for you is to base your outfit around blue hues. This dip dyed maxi dress is a luxe example of the trend.

With the wealth of fashion blogs and street style websites out there, it’s nearly impossible to cover all the best street style looks. Hopefully these examples of summer street fashion 2012 will inspire your new season outfits. If you have any more examples of great summer street fashion for 2012, feel free to share them!
