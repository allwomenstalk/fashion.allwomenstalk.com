# 7 Funky Accessories for That Festival Outfit ...

Funky accessories can be a standout point for any fashion-forward festival outfit. When styling your festival outfit, try adding a funky accessory like an embellished bag or an interesting head piece. Festival fashion is all about having fun and pushing boundaries, so feel free to take risks with your look (all while remembering that comfort is key, of course!) Take a look at a few funky festival accessories below.

## 1. Feather Fringe Headband

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_

Feather accessories and festivals go hand in hand. Work this trend into your outfit by wearing funky accessories like this feather fringe headband. The slim headband features a feather and cable chain fringe attached to one end. Wear it with your hair out so it appears that the feathers peek from in between the strands.

## 2. Bright Wrist to Ring

![Bright Wrist to Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_bright-wrist-to-ring_412x560.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/I4ruY4)

Add a bit of arm candy to your festival look in the form of this funky accessory. It’s made up of a multicoloured stretchy bracelet connected to a metal ring with different coloured strands of faceted stones. This accessory is perfect for brightening up a basic outfit!

## 3. Interchangeable Lens Sunglasses

![Interchangeable Lens Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_interchangeable-lens-sunglasses_560x350.jpg)

**Price:** $31.31 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/3Wj3Un)

One of the easiest ways to update your festival look with funky accessories is with a pair of killer shades. These round sunglasses, interestingly enough, come with interchangeable tinted lenses. Match a colour to suit your mood or colour clash with your outfit!

## 4. Tri Again Ring

**Price:** $11.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You can’t go wrong with a statement ring for bit of extra detail. This one comes in an oversized triangle shape and features a red, white, and blue zig-zag pattern. Team it with similar coloured jewellery and try working a relaxed festival look in a maxi dress and oversized sunglasses.

## 5. Baroque Bow Headband

**Price:** $16.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/OVlupV)

If you’re going to be wandering into mosh pits at festivals, things are bound to get a bit sweaty. Stay cool and keep any matted hair off your face with hair accessories like a this headband. It comes in a sweet lilac colour and features an on-trend baroque print. Simply tie or braid your hair and push your fringe back with this headband.

## 6. Eons of Neon Bag

![Eons of Neon Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_eons-of-neon-bag_342x356.jpg)

**Price:** $62.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This compact bag would make a fun addition to a summer festival outfit. It comes in a cute heart-shaped design and features a woven finish. The neon accents are also on-trend for the summer. This bag would look great teamed with a colourful, flirty sundress.

## 7. Bull’s Eye Floppy Hat

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_

Outdoor festivals can be draining in the heat of the summer months. Stay shaded with funky accessories like this floppy hat. It features an eye-catching striped pattern and is available in orange, pink, and blue coloured patterns. Wear it with a basic sundress and leather satchel for a cool, casual festival look.

Updating your festival outfit can be as simple as adding an eye-catching and funky accessory. From neon accents to tribal-inspired designs, you’ll be able to find something to suit your style. What kind of funky accessories will you be rocking this festival season?
