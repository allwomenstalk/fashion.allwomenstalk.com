# 7 Pieces of Customisable Jewellery ...

Individuality is one of the greatest markers in fashion, hence the popularity of pieces of customisable jewellry. With so many mass produced items out there, choosing something that’s completely unique is becoming harder. If you want to try something that will add a touch of individuality to your outfit, start looking at pieces of customisable jewellery. A fine piece provides an excellent opportunity to be a complete individual. The following are just a few to get you started.

## 1. “Carrie” Style Name Necklace

![“Carrie” Style Name Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fx/xv/qr7lee4j54c929fe76cf0150208654_250x152.jpg)

**Price:** $29.95 at [bestpersonalizedjewelry.com](http://www.clixgalore.com/NoRedirect.aspx?ID=14206&AfID=221066&BID=144421&ER=0&RU=)
This is easily one of the most iconic pieces of customisable jewellery. Made famous by the style savvy Sex and the City character, this piece is a must for fashionistas. While a bit ubiquitous, you’ll always feel fabulous wearing one of these necklaces. Let the whole world know exactly who you are -- even if Carrie isn't your name!

## 2. Typewriter Toggle Bracelet

![Typewriter Toggle Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rr/k7/s2tv8ayv54c929ff344c9043717831_250x224.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [uncommongoods.com](http://www.uncommongoods.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bracelet design is quite a quirky concept. It is handmade from old typewriter keys. You can also choose keys that correspond to your initials. This bracelet is quite vintage looking and perfect for someone who is after a conversation starting piece of jewellery.

## 3. Giles and Brother Pied-de-Biche Brass Cuff

_ **Price:** $125.00 at gilesandbrother.com_
This brass cuff is one striking piece of customisable jewellery. I love its hoof shaped design and antique finish. The best part about this bracelet is that you can get a customised stamp on the edge of the cuff. You can write a heart-felt message or commemorate a special occasion.

## 4. Asos Vintage Style Charm Bracelet

**Price:** $20.69 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/jlhNrO)
Charm bracelets are one of my favourite ways to add a touch of individuality to an outfit. This Asos bracelet is just one to whet your appetite. What I love most about charm bracelets is that charms can both have meaning and really say something about your personality. You can keep it simple or stack on the charms. Either way it’s still uniquely personal.

## 5. In God We Trust Brass Initial Ring

**

![In God We Trust Brass Initial Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/5_250x209.jpg)

Price:** $60.00 at [boutiques.old.refinery29.com](https://goo.gl/3prN0H)
This ring is a subtle take on the customisable trend. It’s made from solid brass and can be customised with a maximum of two characters. Stamp your initials or other meaningful characters. This ring is perfect for wearing every day.

## 6. Scosha 10-Karat Gold Braided Bracelets

**Price:** $475.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fh3nXG)
This set of four bracelets is casual luxury at its finest. The bracelets are adorned with diamonds and gold and silver charms. Customise your look by wearing them either all at once or one at a time, stacking them with other bracelets, or maybe even threading your own charms on. Add these for a bohemian touch to your outfit.

## 7. Scrabble Jewellery

**Price:** $8.00-$38.00 at [uncommongoods.com](http://www.uncommongoods.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love a good game of scrabble, so it’s no wonder I’ve fallen for these pieces of customisable jewellery. Made from scrabble tiles, you can get jewellery to spell out any kind of message you want. Pick out initials, or put together a whole word. You’re bound to impress either way!

Customised jewellry is an excellent way to express your individuality. Let me know what you think about the pieces here. Do any of these pieces of customisable jewellery catch your eye?
