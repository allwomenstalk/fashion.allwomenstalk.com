# 7 Celebrities in Monique Lhuillier \- Who Wore It Best?

This list of seven celebrities in Monique Lhuillier does not give justice to the creations of this fabulous, creative, and talented woman, who has since captured the hearts of Hollywood with her own brand of style. Monique Lhuillier, the woman who has since become a friend to the stars, defines fashion with her creations that empower women to be boldly beautiful. Here is my attempt to feature seven celebrities in Monique Lhuillier as they strut their stuff on the red carpet:

## 1. Drew Barrymore

![Drew Barrymore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/d4/zu3isnga_600x809.jpg)

It's one thing to be pregnant; it's another thing to be pregnant and wear Monique Lhuillier. Nobody but Drew Barrymore can pull off this look and this dress. Every pregnant woman knows that dressing up with a baby bump is a major challenge, but Drew made it so fun! She looks so stunning in this red, pink, and white combination with floral appliques and ruffled train. She is totally one of the celebrities in Monique Lhuillier who look gorgeous, stunning, and blooming with happiness.

## 2. Cheryl Cole

![Cheryl Cole](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jk/bj/n86zygwh_580x870.jpg)

When I saw her in this Monique Lhuillier Fall 2014 strapless black lurex jacquard gown with a rise and fall hemline, I had to ask the question, "Is that Cheryl Cole?" She was in France when she wore this masterpiece at the "Foxcatcher" Cannes Film Festival premiere, where she was reportedly talking business to some producers. Well, let's see what happens. Meanwhile, get blown away with me. Hot, hot, hot!

## 3. Jenna Dewan-Tatum

![Jenna Dewan-Tatum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/dx/sjgfwode_550x825.jpg)

I first saw her in the 2006 movie "Step Up," opposite her now husband, Channing Tatum, and she looked plain then. How this beauty transformed over the years! They are one gorgeous-looking couple, and they give the cameras beautiful shots. But for the premiere of her husband's film "22 Jump Street," Jenna just stole red carpet attention with this Monique Lhuillier 2014 Spring Collection long-sleeved, embroidered dress. No need for Mr. Tatum to look the other way!

## 4. Jena Malone

![Jena Malone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/my/lc/pvya0rt1_600x871.jpg)

She was crazy as Johanna Mason in the film, but she looked even crazier and bolder and prettier wearing this fiery red gown at the UK premiere of "The Hunger Games: Catching Fire." It goes so well with the theme of the movie and I particularly love this photo of her showing so much confidence and attitude. It is so evocative of her movie character. Really great job!

## 5. Halle Berry

![Halle Berry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i2/j8/oin0xrr4_396x594.jpg)

How she manages to strut her stuff on the red carpet with this Monique Lhuillier gown is absolutely a wonder, and a joy to behold! Halle Berry wore this the sexy, strapless red dress at the 2013 BET Honors, which recognizes outstanding African-Americans in education, media, music, entertainment, literature, and service. Well, by the looks of it, it's no wonder she earned the award!

## 6. Jennifer Garner

![Jennifer Garner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/p0/g45oucj3_600x772.jpg)

It's hard not to fall in love with Jennifer Garner, even when her 2005 movie "Elektra" was a flop. With this sighting during the 2012 premiere of her husband Ben Affleck's film "Argo," you are already forgiven, dear! Another red dress from Monique Lhuillier's fabulous creations, and this stunner just made everyone stare at Jennifer's utter magnificence. And for the record, everything's turned out so well for the new Batman ever since THIS Jennifer came into his life.

## 7. Nina Dobrev

![Nina Dobrev](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/sp/os8dk9i4_396x594.jpg)

I don't exactly like Nina Dobrev's acting but I like how she dresses up, especially when she dons Monique Lhuillier. Oh, the spirit of this girl when it comes to fashion and style is unbeatable! I love how she carried herself in this black and white gown at the 2012 Toronto International Film Festival premiere of "The Perks of Being a Wallflower." That beautiful face is her ultimate edge and that just makes her a real star.

What do you think about these Monique Lhuillier creations? Have you stumbled upon photos of other actresses wearing them? Who wore it best?
