# 7 Fruit\-Inspired Fashions You'll Want to Serve up with Your Outfits ...

Forget florals, this season is all about fruit-inspired fashions. Fruit inspired clothes have been popping up everywhere lately with a number of designers and affordable fashion brands having jumped on board this delectable bandwagon. The look is fun and quirky, and screams “I’m ready to go on vacation now, thanks”. Sound good? Then check out our pick of fruit-inspired fashions to drool over this season.

## 1. Mother of Pearl Silk-Appliquéd Mini Dress

![Mother of Pearl Silk-Appliquéd Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/c7/f9mwlbxi_600x900.jpg)

Make a statement in fruit-inspired fashions this season. This crisp white mini dress by Mother of Pearl features on-trend fruit appliqué patterns and bold monochromatic stripes. Wear it with espadrilles and oversized sunglasses for a summery daytime look, worthy of the French Riviera.

## 2. Topshop Lemon Print Bodycon Dress

![Topshop Lemon Print Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/an/kgqkwxw9_600x891.jpg)

Stripes and fruit motifs are proving to be a popular combination this season. This bodycon dress is from Topshop and is one of the more affordable ways to incorporate fruit-inspired fashions into your wardrobe. The ‘clashing’ style of prints is right on-trend and would make a bold fashion statement. Wear this dress with a cropped denim jacket and flat strappy sandals for a cool and casual look this season.

## 3. Equipment Strawberry Print Blouse

![Equipment Strawberry Print Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/r9/maje3zox_600x797.jpg)

This strawberry print blouse by Equipment would make a sweet addition to any wardrobe. It has a boxy cut with a crew neck and sleeveless design. The small strawberry print is eye-catching yet understated. You could easily team this printed blouse with anything from cut-off denim shorts to chic cigarette trousers.

## 4. Modcloth ‘Seeds the Day’ Tee

![Modcloth ‘Seeds the Day’ Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/0p/n9oogx6j_600x847.jpg)

The white t-shirt is a wardrobe staple and this one from Modcloth features a fun watermelon print patch pocket. It’s a simple touch but one that gives a humble wardrobe classic an on-trend update. You could even try creating your own version by sewing a piece of printed fabric onto a plain t-shirt. Wear it with distressed boyfriend jeans and pool sliders for a relaxed off-duty look.

## 5. Topshop Pineapple Motif Jumper

![Topshop Pineapple Motif Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/0p/sl8kjlrv_600x724.jpg)

Pineapple prints have been really popular lately and you can find them on anything from blouses to bikinis this season. Here, the pineapple can be found glamming up a basic grey jumper. This grey Topshop sweater features a large sequin pineapple detail to the front. It’s bound to add a bit of glitz and glamour to a basic everyday outfit.

## 6. Modcloth ‘Pull up a Cherry’ Dress

![Modcloth ‘Pull up a Cherry’ Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/js/vv6aqzqu_600x846.jpg)

This cherry print dress from Modcloth has a retro, rockabilly-inspired design. The fit and flare design of the dress is flirty and feminine and the cherry print is, well, the cherry on top! Let the dress speak for itself and team it with some simple wedge heels and a polished hairdo.

## 7. Dsquared2 Silk Banana Print Top

![Dsquared2 Silk Banana Print Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/gv/rvdpfmuz_600x696.jpg)

If you prefer your fashion with a bit of attitude then check out this printed silk top by Dsquared2. It features an all over banana print, complete with frowning facial features. It proves that even the humble banana deserves a high fashion turn. This top would easily lend a quirky touch to a classic outfit of skinny jeans and ballet flats.

It’s no longer good enough to just eat your fruits this season; it looks like you’ll be wanting to wear them as well! What do you think of this season’s fruity trend? How would you style these fruit inspired fashions?
