# 7 Colourful Swimsuits ...

Bold, bright swimsuits make for excellent poolside attire. Depending on your personal style preference, you may prefer busy patterns or a bold block colours. Either way, you’ll be sure to make an impact. Take a look at a few colourful swimsuits below.

## 1. Diane Von Furstenberg Bash Leopard Print Swimsuit

![Diane Von Furstenberg Bash Leopard Print Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/ch/aua9tcz154c9058590067779683523_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $220.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fWz8bo)
The plunging twisted V-neck design of this swimsuit is quite risqué. It features a multicoloured leopard print, open back, and is fully lined. The design of this swimsuit is probably better suited for those with smaller busts. When not by the pool, slip a floaty maxi skirt over the top for a relaxed resort look.

## 2. Topshop Multi Paisley Print Swimsuit

![Topshop Multi Paisley Print Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l3/wd/tlgrfwit54c90586d4c28388098247_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $66.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/hG4Lg8)
Bold patterns can be seen on a lot of colourful swimsuits this season. This Topshop swimsuit has a decidedly retro feel about it. I’ll attribute that to the blue and white paisley print. It also has frill detailing at the bust and a halterneck design. What’s interesting about this swimsuit is the cut out detail at the back which makes it look like you’re wearing a bikini.

## 3. Jets by Jessika Allen Lustre Frilled Swimsuit

![Jets by Jessika Allen Lustre Frilled Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/gs/zaktgjpt54c905880f8db109979240_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $180.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/3dlSP0)
Go for poolside glam with this luxe frilled swimsuit. It comes in a bold turquoise colour and features frill trimming at the neckline. It also has a cut out back, self tie straps, and built in bra. If you’re after a block coloured swimsuit in an interesting design, then definitely take inspiration from this particular number.

## 4. Dorothy Perkins White Floral Cut out Swimsuit

![Dorothy Perkins White Floral Cut out Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/z5/cskfo7e954c905893160e525508565_368x496.jpg)

**Price:** $33.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Floral prints will always be popular during the warmer months. They also make for colourful swimsuits. This swimsuit has an all over floral print, frill trims, and keyhole detail at the front. It also has self tie straps at the neck and back. This swimsuit is an affordable way to update your poolside look.

## 5. We Are Handsome the Kinaree Bandeau Swimsuit

![We Are Handsome the Kinaree Bandeau Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/c5/ka1ijfu554c9058a45a88132444892_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ae28Sl)
While on the expensive side, this bandeau swimsuit does do a good job of working colour into the design. This swimsuit features an abstract multicoloured print, sweetheart neckline, and small cut out detail at the bust. This garment could also be quite versatile as a bodysuit and worn underneath shorts or skirts.

## 6. Tomas Maier Diagonal Colour Block Swimsuit

![Tomas Maier Diagonal Colour Block Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r0/nw/a0rhik6g54c9058b5edda401309211_404x464.jpg)

**Price:** $352.00 at [brownsfashion.com](http://www.brownsfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This colour block swimsuit is simple yet effective. It has a v-neck, low scoop back, and features a diagonal orange and white panel design. The overall aesthetic is quite sporty. Orange is going to be a popular colour this summer, and this swimsuit would be a fuss-free way of working the trend into your poolside wardrobe.

## 7. Rosa Cha Ruched Printed Swimsuit

**Price:** $350.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XLDw06)
This definitely takes the cake as far as colourful swimsuits go. The multicoloured abstract print is made more intriguing by the all over ruched finished. This ruching adds depth and texture to the overall appearance. This swimsuit has a detachable halter strap so can be worn as a bandeau or halterneck.

If you’re going to be spending a lot of time by the water this summer, think about investing in a bright and bold swimsuit. Whether you choose a busy pattern or a block colour, there’s bound to be a swimsuit for you. Which of these colourful swimsuits do you like best?
