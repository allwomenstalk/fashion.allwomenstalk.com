# 9 Sophisticated Neon Fashions ...

**Neon fashions** are all over the place lately. You can’t escape this latest trend. While neon can come off as a tacky, rest assured that this season there are plenty of more sophisticated neon fashion items to choose from. From acid bright to faded neons, take a look at the following examples of stylish neon fashion.

## 1. Tosphop Sleeveless Lace Dress

![Tosphop Sleeveless Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_tosphop-sleeveless-lace-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Qox5d0)
Tone down any glaringly bright neon fabric by layering it underneath neutral cutouts. This Topshop dress is a great example of **stylish neon fashion**. This white lace dress features a bright lime green contrast lining that peeks through the lace. This dress would look great teamed with cream accessories.

## 2. Michael Michael Kors Tote

![Michael Michael Kors Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_michael-michael-kors-tote_420x690.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bPTFyY)
Neon accessories are an excellent way to add some colour to an outfit. This neon bag comes in an eye-catching lime green colour and features adjustable leather handles. It also has a frosted finish which is another trend that’s gaining popularity.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Pencil Skirt

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_marc-by-marc-jacobs-pencil-skirt_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $280.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9Y7wna)
Coral hues are a popular choice this season. Choose one in a neon hue to pull together a new season outfit. Pair a neon coral skirt, such as this Marc by Marc Jacobs number, with a white tank top and neutral heels. The pencil silhouette is quite classic, but the neon colour and draping at the side adds some modern touches.

## 4. ASOS Portfolio Clutch

![ASOS Portfolio Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_asos-portfolio-clutch_350x234.jpg)

**Price:** $28.65 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/Ql9URa).
Colourful, oversized clutches are an on-trend choice of accessory. This envelope style clutch has an oversized design and comes in a brilliant shade of yellow. It also has sleek metal corners and bars to the strap.

## 5. Modcloth Neon Clutch

![Modcloth Neon Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_modcloth-neon-clutch_320x274.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You might be forgiven for thinking you’re going to a rave with this fancy little clutch. It’s made from faux leather and has a neon pink body and yellow borders. Since it’s quite a bold **neon fashion** accessory, team it with something simple like a black dress and heels.

## 6. Jil Sander Leather Ballet Flats

![Jil Sander Leather Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_jil-sander-leather-ballet-flats_350x137.jpg)

**Price:** $425.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/onckDg)
Wear the **neon fashion** trend on your feet for a cool take on the trend. A simple style such as a ballet flat will easily do the trick. These Jil Sander shoes are a perfect example of the trend (albeit a tad pricey – keep an eye out for affordable chain store versions!) Try your hand at some spring colour blocking by teaming neon shoes with faded pastel clothing.

## 7. Elizabeth and James Blazer

![Elizabeth and James Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_elizabeth-and-james-blazer_350x374.jpg)

**Price:** $445.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vcVbXz)
A hot pink blazer would look great thrown over a floaty white dress this spring. This Elizabeth and James blazer is made from lightweight silk-crepe material and features three-quarter length sleeves. The three-quarter length sleeves are also perfect for showing off an arm full of stylishly stacked bracelets.

## 8. Topshop Boutique Lime Dress

![Topshop Boutique Lime Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/8_topshop-boutique-lime-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8nQ8En)
Faded **neon fashions** are a subtler take on the trend. Faded neons are perfect for those who find the impact of normal neons too harsh. This lime dress has a metallic finish and a simple shift design. It would look great worn with a silver collar necklace, silver cuffs, and strappy sandals.

## 9. Jason Wu Silk Skirt

![Jason Wu Silk Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/9_jason-wu-silk-skirt_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $990.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/dTNyFc)
If you’ve got the cash and really want to make a statement then this skirt is definitely for you. It comes in a striking neon chartreuse colour and features ruffle detailing at the back. Despite the shockingly bright colour, it’s quite an elegant skirt.

Whatever you think of the **neon fashion** trend, there’s no denying that it’s one of the hottest trends for spring and summer. Will you be wearing this trend? What do you think of these particular neon fashions?
