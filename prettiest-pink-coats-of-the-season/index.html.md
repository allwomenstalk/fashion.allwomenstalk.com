# 9 Prettiest Pink Coats of the Season ...

Chase the winter blues away with the prettiest pink coats of the season. When the temperatures begin to drop, sartorial direction tends to favor dark, prominent shades. But this fall, wardrobes are lightened and lifted with a palette of pretty pinks. For a refreshing new take on autumnal outerwear, think pink with the prettiest pink coats of the season.

## 1. Pink Boyfriend Coat

![Pink Boyfriend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/1_pink-boyfriend-coat_600x899.jpg)

Oversized coats are currently en pointe with the season’s chicest outwear for fall. A boyfriend coat with oversized proportions and boxy cuts play into the menswear-inspired vibe seen across designer collections to create gentlemanly tailoring and ladylike polish all at once. Femininity is enhanced when boyfriend coats get a makeover in blushing pinks to suit fall’s fresh vibe. An ultra-trendy boyfriend coat has refined urbanite appeal that’s chic for the downtown lady. To maintain a cool city look, pair a boyfriend coat with sleek leggings or fitted denim and ankle booties. Pink coats from $95 like this Topshop one shown above to couture creations like Balenciaga priced at $1,300 will keep you seeing pink all season long, with other chic choices at J.Crew, John Lewis and Romwe.

## 2. Pink Collarless Coat

![Pink Collarless Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/2_pink-collarless-coat_600x800.jpg)

A collarless coat has an air of easy sophistication to it and is optimal for layering cool-weather pieces like scarves and wraps. The appeal of a collarless coat is its retro and vintage quality, with a feminine twist reminiscent of first lady Jackie O. To wear your collarless coat with ladylike charm and demure stature, pair your rosy [pink coat](https://streetstyle.allwomenstalk.com/street-style-ways-to-wear-pink-coats-for-fall/) with a chic fitted pencil skirt and pointed toe pumps for a soft and sweet silhouette.

## 3. Pink Long Coat

![Pink Long Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/3_pink-long-coat_249x468.jpg)

Long coats are necessary for cool climates that require extra warmth and layers during the fall season. To maximize your cool style opt for a long, sheltering coat in a fresh rose tone to stand out in the style crowd. A long trench-inspired coat creates a sophisticated silhouette that’s chic and contemporary for use all season long.

## 4. Pink Hooded Coat

![Pink Hooded Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/4_pink-hooded-coat_320x360.jpg)

For those days that have a brisk chill, a hooded coat is the perfect option to keep you sheltered from the elements and cozy at the same time. Opt for a short hooded coat in a simple and effortless material like wool for easy sporting during the weekend or on off-duty days. Pair with slim, skinny denim and tall boots for a chic look suitable for the girl on the go.

## 5. Pink Pea Coat

![Pink Pea Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/5_pink-pea-coat_306x500.jpg)

A pea coat is a classic outwear staple and ultra-flattering on most figures. Pea coats generally come in double-breasted cuts but can sometimes be found in single breasted styles to suit the busty woman. A pea coat has a true utilitarian and equestrian feel that is instantly punched up and taken up a notch when created in a bright and bold bubblegum pink shade. Throw your pea coat over a comfy knit sweater and fitted jeans for a casual cool look.

## 6. Pink Button down Coat

![Pink Button down Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/6_pink-button-down-coat_600x800.jpg)

For an elegant outerwear option this fall, a classic button down coat is ideal for the chic city lady. A button down coat is essential to the professional, fashionable woman who maintains function with style and grace. To maintain a downtown cool aesthetic, opt for a rosy hue in a soft, muted color like pale pink to keep your look refined and polished. Throw a button down coat over feminine suits, razor thin pencil skirts and demure dresses to maximize your pink coat’s work appropriate style.

## 7. Pink Blazer Coat

![Pink Blazer Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/7_pink-blazer-coat_368x464.jpg)

A rosy pink blazer coat is the perfect alternative to formal coats for evenings out or holiday soirées. The bright punch of color creates stand out style during nights on the town and can dress up and elevate even the simplest of evening wardrobe choices, like your trusted little black dress.

## 8. Pink Trench Coat

![Pink Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/8_pink-trench-coat_330x436.jpg)

Classic trench coats are timeless investment pieces every chic fashionista should have in their closet for moments where effortless sophistication is needed. A longer trench coat style is figure flattering and ultra-feminine, keeping for an elegant and smart look that’s simple and cool all the time. The versatility of a trench coat means you can select this fall outerwear option for all occasions, day or night.

## 9. Pink Military Coat

_ Military style coats with large lapels, burnished metals, edgy studding and tough epaulettes are always popular for the fall season. For this autumn, try and work an on-trend military coat in a soft blush tone to add feminine edge to this otherwise tough, utilitarian style. Create this feminine edge by pairing your pink military coat with edgy moto boots and black leather for a look that’s strong yet feminine.

From balletic blushes to soft roses, the allure of pink takes form in the season’s coolest outwear. Banish basic blacks and neutrals to lighten up this fall with demure and ladylike pinks in your outerwear wardrobe and be tickled pink with the prettiest pink coats of the season. Will you be thinking pink this season with pretty pink coats?
