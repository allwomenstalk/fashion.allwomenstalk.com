# 9 of the Best Dressed at the 2014 Grammys ...

The women took over as the best dressed at the 2014 Grammys! Everyone looked absolutely incredible. I love award season because we get to see all these gorgeous dresses. If you watched the Grammys, then you know that everyone went all out this year. Narrowing the list to only 9 was pretty hard, but these women are my picks for the best dressed at the 2014 Grammys! Take a look and tell me if you agree.

## 1. Beyonce

![Beyonce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/se/gc/yt/ke15u4n2_600x956.jpg)

Queen Bey always looks flawless, right? She kept it sexy and sophisticated in this sheer white floral Michael Costello gown. It hugs her in all the right places and shows just enough skin. I love that she kept it simple with barely any accessories. The bold red lip is enough! She was definitely one of the best dressed at the 2014 Grammys!

## 2. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fr/cq/rn/cdehhjus_600x969.jpg)

How gorgeous is this Valentino gown?! It came straight off the runway from Paris Fashion Week. Katy was reminiscent of a ballerina in this gown that features music notes and big bows on the back. Her braided updo completed the whole princess look. It's funny that this look is the complete opposite of her "Dark Horse" performance look (which was incredible!).

## 3. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r6/br/qm/sci8xzgh_600x892.jpg)

Taylor looked fabulous in this golden Gucci gown! She sparkled and really stood out on the red carpet. She told Ryan Seacrest that the dress was "like wearing a suit of armor." I think we can all relate to sacrificing comfort for looking fashionable. Right, ladies?

## 4. Ciara

![Ciara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/yv/o2/iatq52so_460x692.jpg)

First off, how gorgeous does Ciara look with that baby bump?! This glittering gold Emilio Pucci gown hugged her body in all the right places. I love that she kept her hair and makeup simple, since the dress is already a statement maker! Her makeup looks very fresh and natural.

## 5. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ci/ae/vr/ds7tt93v_600x901.jpg)

I'm a fan of everything Anna Kendrick. She wore a strapless Azzaro design. Even with the cleavage-baring cutouts, I think this dress is still very tasteful! It's sexy, yet sophisticated. Her sideswept hair helps show off her earrings, and her makeup is fabulous!

## 6. Zendaya

![Zendaya](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/s0/ln/jzl1rixj_600x901.jpg)

The 17-year-old singer wore a funky Emanuel Ungaro dress which featured a sheer black top and polka dot aqua skirt! I love that she kept the accessories and makeup minimal and wore her hair slicked back. She's becoming quite the fashion icon on red carpets! I love the open silt on her skirt where she can show off the fab shoes. Her fashion risk is definitely a win for me!

## 7. Pink

![Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/d0/gg/k44qumy4_600x925.jpg)

Pink wore a silky strapless gown designed by Johanna Johnson and vintage Neil Lane jewels. I love that even with super short hair, she styles it fabulously. I also love that she chose to wear no necklace, it would have taken away from the dress! It's obvious that red is the perfect color for her. She looks gorgeous! Did you see her performance that night? Mind-blowing!

## 8. Ariana Grande

![Ariana Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/cj/g4/xz4nocsk_478x724.jpg)

Ariana looked beautiful in this strapless floral Dolce gown! She wore her hair up in a ponytail with curls and kept the jewelry to a minimum. I love this look! It's very sweet and retro. I admire the fact that Ariana keeps her style no matter where she goes!

## 9. Colbie Caillat

![Colbie Caillat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/w6/gv/gi7mhgbl_600x853.jpg)

Um, this dress is breathtaking! The "Bubbly" singer wore a cherry red Ezra Santos gown that featured a high neck and petal appliqués. She wore her hair up in an elegant bun and accessorized with a gold clutch! This statement making dress was paired with fresh, natural makeup. She looked great from head to toe!

Well, there you have it! These are my picks for the best dressed at the 2014 Grammys. Don't get me wrong, the men looked incredible too. But, as always, us girls looked better! Haha, which look was your favorite?
