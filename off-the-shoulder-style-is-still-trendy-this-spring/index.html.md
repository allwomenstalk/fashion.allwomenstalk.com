# Off the Shoulder Style is Still Trendy This Spring ...

One of my favourite trends we're seeing right through spring is off the shoulder styles, and I don't think I'm alone either! It seems this style is absolutely everywhere these days, am I right? Since there's no better way to incorporate this super sexy yet totally feminine style into our wardrobes, we've found some of the best off the shoulder styles for spring!

## 1. Crisp White Bodysuits

![clothing, sleeve, outerwear, brown hair, photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fk/s3/wc4tndxi5905df044689e520758161_600x750.jpg)

One of our favourite spring looks is a crisp white bodysuit paired with a super flattering pair of high waisted jeans!

## 2. Flowy Maxi Dresses

![clothing, sleeve, dress, spring, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/bb/y6lnh5kn5905df1957b8c865832312_600x742.jpg)

Another clean, crisp number that will brighten up your spring wardrobe! We're totally loving flowy maxi style dresses in a great off the shoulder style.

## 3. Charcoal Crop Sweaters

![black, clothing, lady, eyewear, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/zk/e20rqozm5905df2bc14be128109807_600x749.jpg)

Girls who love anything in a dark colour like black or charcoal will love this style! It looks best when paired with a pair of dark sunnies and a matching black hat.

## 4. Black Ruffle Tops

![hair, clothing, vacation, sitting, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/s4/vr7xa8sy5905df412a9cd409826581_600x750.jpg)

It's time to add ruffles into your spring looks and lighten it up with your fave lightwash jeans! Better yet, finish off this sleek outfit with a pair of statement ankle boots for spring.

## 5. Flare Sleeve Crops

![clothing, blue, beauty, dress, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/o0/v2ivsxfu5905df571f020031324547_600x750.jpg)

A trend within a trend! We still can't get enough of flare sleeves, so why not incorporate this amazing trend within the off the shoulder style?

## 6. Bold Red Dresses

![color, red, clothing, lady, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o6/av/gim53fss5905df8ab4255078863889_600x749.jpg)

Stop traffic in a super bold number like this red dress, complete in a lightweight fabric just made for warm days in spring!

## 7. White Lace Playsuits

![hair, dress, clothing, photograph, woman,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t4/nf/qx0xv9ik5905df9d52113661187372_600x750.jpg)

Feel girly and super feminine in a cute white playsuit like this one, featuring lace detail and a sheer layer on top! Stunning!

## 8. Embroidered Tops

![hair, blue, clothing, beauty, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/vv/t1e6l7yu5905dfb208e0c795982470_600x749.jpg)

Add a bit of interest to your off the shoulder tops with some eye-catching embroidery along the neckline!

## 9. Bubble Sleeve Crops

![person, photograph, clothing, image, blue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/b7/mzzhrdha5905e0682247d539478912_600x750.jpg)

If you want to stand out from all the flare/bell style sleeves everywhere right now, why not opt for a fun bubble sleeve instead? They're just as trendy during spring!

## 10. Denim Jackets

![denim, clothing, blue, jeans, sleeve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/u6/q91nczr55905e0ceb4c69209652347_600x749.jpg)

You're sure to be one of the few seen with this piece over spring! This super unique look is a denim jacket featuring an off the shoulder style.

## 11. White Crop Tops

![white, black, clothing, dress, footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/ps/gobn9lsc5905dfd6c2b6f705640678_600x749.jpg)

If you love anything simple yet stunning, you'll love this white crop top when paired with smart looking high waisted shorts!

## 12. Simple Straight Denim

![clothing, denim, footwear, jeans, street,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y2/g4/ha4mnpsk5905e037aa40e574080674_600x749.jpg)

Forget the bubble and flare sleeve styles, it doesn't get much more simple than a straight cut denim top in a fabulous off the shoulder style!

## 13. Vertical Stripes

![blue, vacation, season, rock, sea,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/zp/azo9e81k5905e165a2aca786435784_600x701.jpg)

Make a statement this spring with an eye-catching vertical striped dress or top like this one! It features cute tie sleeves in a loose, relaxed fit.

## 14. Fitted Layered Tops

![white, photograph, blue, image, statue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/zf/xepk75v65905e268d4dc4786126095_600x749.jpg)

If you're looking for a Disney princess inspired top in the off the shoulder style, look no further! Opt for one with super pretty layered sleeves in a sheer fabric like chiffon or lace.
