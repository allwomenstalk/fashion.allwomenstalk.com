# Relive the Best Red Carpet Fashion from the Hollywood Film Awards ...

My favorite part about award season? The fashion, of course! Now you can relive The Hollywood Film Awards' best red carpet looks, which took place on Friday (Nov. 14):

## 1. Felicity Jones

![Felicity Jones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/c3/hvrrs50v_600x969.jpg)

Felicity Jones stunned in a black sheer gown!

## 2. Benedict Cumberbatch

![Benedict Cumberbatch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/qo/xi933eki_600x969.jpg)

Sigh, Benedict's so handsome!

## 3. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/u1/jdht31gj_590x888.jpg)

Reese's black and red gown paired beautifully with her black heels!

## 4. Channing Tatum

![Channing Tatum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m4/r1/v4nuxe9n_600x969.jpg)

<i>Swoon</i>. Channing was part of the award-winning ensemble of Foxcatcher!

## 6. Hilary Swank & Gerard Butler

The P.S. I Love You co-stars reunited on the red carpet to share a couple of laughs together!

## 7. Keira Knightley

Keira's long sleeve, floor-length gown looks fabulous on her.

## 8. Kristen Stewart

Kristen Stewart looked edgy chic with white and grey ensemble.

## 9. Shailene Woodley

The Fault in Our Stars' Shailene Woodley sparkled in a silver gown embellished with stars.

## 10. Janelle Monae
