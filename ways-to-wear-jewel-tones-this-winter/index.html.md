# 7 Ways to Wear Jewel Tones This Winter ...

If you need some ways to wear jewel tones, you aren’t alone. These deep shades of red, green, blue and purple are all over the place now that the weather has cooled off. While they may look intense, they aren’t that hard to pull off and there loads of ways you can do it. Whether you want to start small or go all out, check out these awesome ways to wear jewel tones and your entire winter season will be just a little bit brighter.

## 1. Don a Coat

What could be more fun than wearing a coat that isn’t basic black or brown? It’s actually one of the easiest ways to wear jewel tones. Choosing a coat in your favorite shade can really enhance any outfit. And that includes your basic jeans and sweater look as well as your little black dress and heels. You can even use a jewel toned coat to add color to your work attire. See – easy?

## 2. Tie on a Scarf

Scarves are such an easy way to add personality and fun to any outfit. If you’re not ready for a jewel toned coat, try topping the one you have with a deeply colored scarf instead. The right scarf can also add pizazz to a sweater or blouse and make any outfit a statement that you’ll get loads of compliments on.

## 3. Buy New Shoes

If you’re the kind of girl that likes to ease into trends, especially those that involve lots of color, shoes may be the right route for you. A pair of jewel toned ballet flats are perfect for wearing with jeans when you go shopping or out to brunch. Jewel tones heels are great for pairing with a sheath dress or a pair of slacks for the office. It’s not hard to find shoes in deep, dark blue, purple, green or red so you won’t even have a hard time rocking this trend.

## 4. Lacy Skirt

A lacy skirt is ideal for a party and you might even pull it off for work if you choose one that’s a respectable length. The great thing about a skirt that is lacy is that you’ll add just the right dose of femininity to your outfit and you’ll look fabulous while you do it. Pair a jewel toned skirt with a bright white shirt for a really big impact.

## 5. Grab a Purse

You have a little bit of freedom when it comes to carrying a handbag. It doesn’t always have to match your shoes or be the same color as your outfit. By choosing a jewel toned purse, you have lots of options. You can carry it with virtually anything in your closet. Dark blue especially because it’s considered a neutral by many fashion experts. A purse is just the thing to add a pop of color to any of your favorite outfits.

## 6. A Dress

Every girl should own at least one little black dress, but if you want a little colored dress too, jewel tones are the way to go. They’re super elegant and easy to pair with all of your neutral accessories. Long or short, a jewel toned dress with breathe new life into your closet and you’ll be trying to come up with reasons to wear it all the time.

## 7. Pile on the Jewelry

Here’s another subtle way to wear jewel tones. Try a statement necklace, a couple of chunky bracelets, a cocktail ring or some dangling earrings. No matter what you choose, you’ll love the extra bit of color that it adds to your ensemble. Just don’t go overboard piling on all of the examples in every color.

What’s your favorite jewel tone? Purple is mine. I love it for all sorts of reasons. Do you have any other ideas for how we can all rock a jewel tone this winter?
