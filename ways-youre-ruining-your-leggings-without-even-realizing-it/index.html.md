# Ways You're Ruining Your Leggings without Even Realizing It ...

You don't want to ruin your leggings. That's why you have to be careful when washing them. According to [Self,](https://www.self.com/story/how-to-wash-leggings?mbid=social_twitter) here are a few mistakes you might be making with your leggings:

## 1. You’re Using Warm Water

![You’re Using Warm Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/do/u5/vu00wywl56ab822f14409971750065_500x334.gif)

The fabric that your leggings are made out of is sensitive. That means it can be damaged if you use warm water to clean them. Stick to using cool water.

## 2. You're Using the Dryer

![You're Using the Dryer](https://img.allw.mn/content/bb/o4/taxkl7mb56ab822de597d592337986_500x275.gif)

It's best to let your leggings air dry. If you need to use the dryer, try using "tumble dry on low."

## 3. You’re Using Regular Bleach

![You’re Using Regular Bleach](https://img.allw.mn/content/pv/lz/nabr3r1k56ab822d7f14f253239208_451x311.gif)

Oxygenated bleach is safe for your leggings. However, you don't want to use regular bleach on them. It could destroy the leggings you've grown to love.

## 4. You’re Not Using Fabric Softener

Don't you hate the fuzz buildup on your leggings? If you do, then fabric softener will make your life a whole lot easier, because it'll reduce the chances of that fuzz sticking.

## 5. You’re Not Washing Your Bottoms ASAP

![You’re Not Washing Your Bottoms ASAP](https://img.allw.mn/content/dw/ib/kn9bewhl56ab822ead5ec181601056_500x333.gif)

Don't leave your leggings in the hamper. As soon as you take them off, wash them. Otherwise, the dirt and sweat on them could end up soiling them.

## 6. You’re Wearing Your Leggings Too Often between Washes

![You’re Wearing Your Leggings Too Often between Washes](https://img.allw.mn/content/wk/xs/ianq51e156ab822e832ac708993954_500x274.gif)

You don't want to wear your leggings three times before washing them. You should clean them after every use.
