# 9 Colourful Tights to Brighten up Your Day ...

**Colorful Tights** are just one way to brighten up your day to day outfit. Whether you prefer faded pastels or shocking acid brights, **colorful tights** make a fun addition to any outfit. They might take some getting used to at first, but once you’ve found your groove there’s no turning back. Check out a few chic and **colorful tights** below.

## 1. Topshop 50 Denier Tangerine Tights

![Topshop 50 Denier Tangerine Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/vw/jj3svxo354c9372e6dbeb255322587_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Ra6mos)
Tangerine is set to be one of the biggest color trends of the year, so get in early with these **colorful tights**. At 50 denier, these tights fairly sheer. The faded, almost neon tangerine color would look great teamed with a navy Mod-inspired shift dress and wool pea coat.

## 2. Modcloth Tights in Rose

![Modcloth Tights in Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/xk/bw60ypfx54c9372f2ed0e597008525_280x399.jpg)

**Price:** $14.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These rose colored tights are simply darling. The dark rose colour is ideal for those who don’t want tights that are too bright. They are also a heavier style as they come at a denier of 80. They’d work wonderfully well teamed with a cream printed dress, Mary Janes, and a framed handbag.

## 3. Forever 21 Heart Print Tights

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_
Printed tights are still a popular choice, so why not try these heart print tights on for size? They come in a super sweet coral color and feature opaque hearts all over. These lightweight tights are perfect for wearing when the winter weather warms up.

## 4. Topshop Teal Dotty Heart Tights

![Topshop Teal Dotty Heart Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/n3/u7ra0w1i54c9373095fcc403983314_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/X3IW4g)
These heart tights have a bit of a fish-net effect to them. They come in a teal green color and feature vertical strips of hearts down the legs. They would look great worn with a color palette of neutrals, particularly greys.

## 5. Topshop 120 Denier Mid Green Tights

![Topshop 120 Denier Mid Green Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/j9/a71htxc154c9373161deb835518521_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $16.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/dsJWW6)
At a denier of 120, these **colorful tights** are thicker and sturdier. They come in a brilliant green color, and are perfect for anyone wanting to stand out in a crowd. Try your hand at color blocking and team these tights with an orange or blue skirt.

## 6. Betsey Johnson Colorblock Tights

![Betsey Johnson Colorblock Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/md/pvhbcl1c54c937322bfae835350002_280x395.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These **colorful tights** are also balanced out with neutral panels. They feature a horizontal striped pattern, with the rose panel being the focal color. These tights are typical of the playful and offbeat Betsey Johnson aesthetic.

## 7. Look from London Indigo Peacock Feather Tights

![Look from London Indigo Peacock Feather Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c0/x6/d7qkh7p854c9373317411584720042_280x397.jpg)

**Price:** $29.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These darker colored tights might not seem like much from the front, but a peek at the back will have you quite amazed. A peacock feather design runs along the back of each calf. These handmade tights are perfect for adding a quirky touch to an outfit.

## 8. Forever 21 Floral Garter Tights

_ **Price:** $6.80 at forever21.com_
Nothing says seductive more than a garter. These **colorful tights** come in a beautiful burgundy color and feature a floral garter at the top of each tight. Show off the garter detail by teaming these tights with a short white shift dress.

## 9. Topshop Mustard Lace Tights

![Topshop Mustard Lace Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/lc/lcklmvvd54c9373494997266531317_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/rfRgCO)
Not everyone can pull off the mustard look, but if you think you’re chances are good then try out these lace tights. These mustard colored tights feature an all-over floral lace detail. The lace is quite delicate and feminine, while the color is punchy and bold. This colour would work well with dark denim.

If you want to add a splash of color to your outfit, try on a pair of **colorful tights**. It’s a fun, youthful look that can really add some extra ‘oomph’ to your outfit. Would you ever wear a pair of **colorful tights**?
