# 7 Best Waxed Denim Pieces ...

Denim has been at the forefront of all major fashion trends this season, whether it be in pastel or neon hues, but this fashionista favourite gets a fresh new remix with 7 of the best waxed denim pieces. These wax coated and treated pieces are easily transitional for the upcoming autumn season. If you want to update your off-duty denim wardrobe, try adding in a pair of these wax-coated jeans as part of your new rotation sure to have you looking and feeling like a rockstar.

## 1. Juicy Couture Coated Skinny Jean

![Juicy Couture Coated Skinny Jean](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_juicy-182x300_182x300.jpg)

**Price:** $178 at [juicycouture.com](http://www.juicycouture.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Opt for jeans in a rich jewel tone to compliment the upcoming fall season, while still remaining on trend with coloured denim to create the perfect blend of these two styles. This pair of Juicy Couture jeans looks like leather but maintains all the comfort of your favourite pair of jeans. The red wine shade adds a subdued pop of colour sure to standout during the autumn season.

## 2. Rag & Bone Waxed Mid Rise Skinny Jean

For a super rock ‘n’ roll edgy vibe don your denim with effortlessly cool accents like these zippered skinny jeans from Rag & Bone. This pair is a versatile addition to your wardrobe and can be worn to make any outfit a little urban punk. The slick black colour makes the wax coating look even more like leather, instantly upping your style status.

## 3. Mango Waxed Effect Jeans

![Mango Waxed Effect Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/mango-214x300_214x300.jpg)

**Price:** 34.99 at [shop.mango.com](http://shop.mango.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Blue Hues are making a huge colour statement this year. This pair from Mango is a new and unconventional take on your tired and true regular blue denim. The vivid colour screams super chic even when running errands in this off-duty denim.

## 4. Tobi Wax Coated Jeans

**Price:** $25 at [tobi.com](https://www.tobi.com/product/42571-aphrodite-wax-coated-jeans?color_id=53294)
Metallic metal tones are a hot trend happening right now. Super skinny and seriously sexy, these stretch skinny jeans are the ultimate evening denim piece. The lustrous silver shade gives this denim a futuristic fantasy feel, which is completely out of this world!

## 5. Hudson Jeans "Krista" Super Skinny Jean

![Hudson Jeans "Krista" Super Skinny Jean](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/hudson-225x300_225x300.jpg)

**Price:** $198 at [piperlime.gap.com](https://www.gap.com/CookieFailure.do)
Adding a bit of shimmer and sheen is a simple way to add dimension to your outfit. The sparkle in this pair of denim can dress up an everyday office look or take you out for a night on the town. A glitzy and glamorous jean is a surefire way to play up the metallic trend this season.

## 6. DL1961 "Emma" Waxed Coated Jean

_ **Price:** $99 at shopambience.com_
Bold and sexy, these flame coloured jeans are red hot. A cropped skinny jean is ideal for those warmer temperatures in fall where you can show off your calves and ankles. Seriously sleek, this pair by DL191 comes in a four way stretch to help maximize comfort and style. Hello gorgeous!

## 7. Habitual "the Alice" Waxed Skinny Jeans

![Habitual "the Alice" Waxed Skinny Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/alice-195x300_195x300.jpg)
