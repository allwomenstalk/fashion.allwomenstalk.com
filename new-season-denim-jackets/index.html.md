# 8 New Season Denim Jackets ...

**Denim jackets** are mainstay in many a wardrobe. During spring and summer, faded denim jacket looks casually chic thrown over light sundresses. However, you don’t have to wear any old jacket this spring. There are a range of new season jackets, from slouchy cuts to bright colours, which are all intended to give your outfit an instant update. Take a look at some stylish denim jackets below.

## 1. 7 for All Mankind Stretch-Denim Jacket

![7 for All Mankind Stretch-Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_7-for-all-mankind-stretch-denim-jacket_400x445.jpg)

**Price:** $290.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XkbFbB)
Take a break from the classic blue denim jacket with this blush denim number. It has frayed trims, front welt pockets and elbow length sleeves. Work a cool urban look by teaming this jacket with a white singlet, cropped leather look pants, and simple black strappy heels.

## 2. Topshop Moto Stripe Hooded Jacket

![Topshop Moto Stripe Hooded Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_topshop-moto-stripe-hooded-jacket_400x530.jpg)

**Price:** $84.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7Qnfrs)
Relaxed, sporty silhouettes are a key trend this season. This Topshop jacket is a fun take on the denim jacket trend. It features a striped main with contrasting denim front pockets. Keep it casual by wearing this hooded jacket with colourful shorts and lace up shoes.

## 3. Christopher Kane Neon Stretch Denim Jacket

![Christopher Kane Neon Stretch Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_christopher-kane-neon-stretch-denim-jacket_400x515.jpg)

**Price:** $520.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/26ig64)
The focus on all things neon is still going strong. This **denim jacket is from the** J Brand and Christopher Kane collaboration, and is definitely one way to brighten up your new season look. It would look great teamed with a short white dress and tan strappy sandals.

## 4. J Brand Denim Slim Stretch-Denim Jacket

![J Brand Denim Slim Stretch-Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_j-brand-denim-slim-stretch-denim-jacket_400x490.jpg)

**Price:** $235.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Dw6kPt)
The faded denim jacket is a classic, and this cropped style would make a great addition to any wardrobe. It features rose gold hardware and button fastening pockets and cuffs. This slim cut jacket could easily become a year-round favourite.

## 5. Topshop Moto Crop Denim Jacket

![Topshop Moto Crop Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_topshop-moto-crop-denim-jacket_400x495.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zcMFV1)
This cropped denim jacket has a grungier, distressed finish. It features silver hardware, authentic trims, and front flap pockets. This jacket would look great paired with a bold printed dress and ankle boots.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Green Crop Jacket

![Dorothy Perkins Green Crop Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_dorothy-perkins-green-crop-jacket_400x543.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**Coloured denim jackets** are an on-trend option. They’ll freshen up any outfit. This jacket comes in a bright green denim that is sure to turn heads. It has a slim cut with classic pocket detailing. The combination of green and white is fresh for spring, so team this jacket with a white shirtdress and colourful accessories.

## 7. Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Polka-Dot Denim Blazer

![Sonia by Sonia Rykiel Polka-Dot Denim Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_sonia-by-sonia-rykiel-polka-dot-denim-blazer_400x473.jpg)

**Price:** $355.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/gGj0nX)
**If classic, cropped denim jackets** aren’t your thing then try a structured blazer. This denim blazer comes in a dark blue denim and features a quirky polka dot pattern. Wear this blazer during the day with colourful cropped pants and patent flats.

## 8. Topshop Moto Hooded Denim Jacket

![Topshop Moto Hooded Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/8_topshop-moto-hooded-denim-jacket_400x543.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/C8wRH4)
This dark denim jacket is another example of the sporty chic trend. It has a relaxed cut with authentic trims and a hood. Work an off-duty look by teaming it with a light dress, metallic jewellery, and messenger style satchel.

There are a **range of denim jackets** to choose from this season. Classic styles are a popular choice, but experiment with coloured denim and sporty silhouettes for something a bit different. What’s your preferred take on the denim jacket this season?
