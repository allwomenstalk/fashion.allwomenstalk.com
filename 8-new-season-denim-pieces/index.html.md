# 8 New Season Denim Pieces ...

Nowadays, denim is a wardrobe staple. However, each season brings with it inventive and new styles of denim to be incorporated into outfits and wardrobes the world over. It can be as simple as a new denim wash, or maybe twist on a tried-and-tested design. Below are a few new season denim pieces to take inspiration from.

## 1. Topshop Moto Pink Skinny Jamie Jeans

![Topshop Moto Pink Skinny Jamie Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jr/ry/x7a177aj54c8f985993f6870922433_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/OgzbZi)
Coloured denim is a look that can be hit or miss. These coloured jeans are definitely a ‘hit’. This dark pink colour is becoming increasingly popular, and the skinny leg jean is a stalwart on the fashion scene. Pair these jeans with light colours in the daytime, and darker colours at night.

## 2. R13 Sweat Jeans

![R13 Sweat Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hh/bz/q3aqrvim54c8f9866fcec119808784_300x428.jpg)

**Price:** $310.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
New season denim pieces usually feature an unconventional twist of some sort. Take these jeans, for example. The waistband resembles something closer to a sweat pant. It has an elastic drawstring waist and side welt pockets. It also features elastic leg cuffs. Despite all this, style them as you would usually do with normal jeans.

## 3. Topshop Bleached Denim Seventies High Waisted a-Line Skirt

![Topshop Bleached Denim Seventies High Waisted a-Line Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/qo/hi7jienv54c8f98764fd0992163615_300x315.jpg)

**Price:** $66.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lDZX8i)
Mid-length, A-line skirts are set to be popular this season. Faded denim washes are also a great option for the summer months. This skirt combines these two elements to create one stylish garment. Steer away from a washed-out pastel colour palette and try pairing this skirt with a bright orange or yellow top.

## 4. Topshop Petit Pale Blue Spot Denim Shirt Dress

![Topshop Petit Pale Blue Spot Denim Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/ck/t5za9wqm54c8f98858276201329558_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/xbCfRi)
This dress is cute and playful. This denim dress features an all-over polka dot pattern. It also has a small collar, placket detailing, and a side zip fastening. You could wear this dress as is, or layered with a contrasting shirt underneath.

## 5. See by Chloe Cherry Denim Crossbody Bag

_ **Price:** $355.00 at lagarconne.com_
New season denim pieces aren’t just restricted to clothing. Accessories such as bags can come in a range of denim designs. This stylish crossbody purse features a leather shoulder strap, front zip pockets, and leather tassel details. It would be a quirky way to add a denim touch to your outfit.

## 6. Acne Bleached Denim Jacket

![Acne Bleached Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/yb/swec2hqc54c8f98a1bd79984210428_300x416.jpg)

**Price:** $240.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Denim jackets are a great way to add edge to an outfit. Lighter washes of denim are a more popular choice than darker denim. This Acne denim jacket features a pointed collar, two patch pockets, and copper hardware. Pair this bleached denim jacket over a floral dress.

## 7. A.P.C. Denim Indigo Backpack

![A.P.C. Denim Indigo Backpack](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ub/l1/aaicqn9i54c8f98adfd7d583923042_300x417.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Something that I never thought would be fashionable was the backpack. However, lately I have been proven wrong. The back pack is almost a must have accessory these days. This indigo backpack features a classic drawstring design and brown leather trims. If you are a student, this would be a stylish option for carrying around your notebooks.

## 8. Paul & Joe Xulu Ruched Denim-Effect Dress

![Paul & Joe Xulu Ruched Denim-Effect Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/db/xc/eva1qa7i54c8f98bce4f4474658484_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $440.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/TCx1DY)
While this dress is not technically made from denim, let’s include denim-effect under the umbrella of new season denim pieces. This dress is made from lyocell, but is convincingly denim in appearance. It features ruching through the front, spaghetti straps, and a sweetheart neckline. Wear this dress as is, to show off your shoulders, or layer a cream blazer over the top for something a bit more sophisticated.

Will you be adding new season denim pieces to your wardrobe? Do any of the above pieces catch your eye?
