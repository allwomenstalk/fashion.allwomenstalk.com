# 9 Stylish Leopard Print Items on a Budget ...

Leopard print items are pretty much a wardrobe staple these days. A little bit of leopard print can go a long way in livening up an outfit. You don’t have to spend a fortune to pull off this trend either, with plenty of affordable leopard items available. Check out the following stylish leopard print items that are also easy on the wallet.

## 1. Leopard Print Sandals

![Leopard Print Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/1_leopard-print-sandals_281x202.jpg)

Leopard print items like shoes are a great way to wear the trend. During the summer, a pair of leopard print sandals can look stylish worn with anything from denim shorts to white summer dresses. These Topshop sandals are currently marked down to $30 from $90, and you can find other similar styles at stores like Macy’s and Forever 21.

## 2. Leopard Print Belt

![Leopard Print Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/2_leopard-print-belt_290x119.jpg)

A slim leopard print belt is an easy way to work subtle hints of this trend into your outfit. Thread one through your belt loops or wear one on the waist to cinch in a dress. You can easily get an affordable belt like this from Forever 21, ASOS, and Dorothy Perkins.

## 3. Leopard Print Scarf

![Leopard Print Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/3_leopard-print-scarf_600x815.jpg)

Spice up your look with a leopard print scarf. Wear one tucked into a leather jacket for a rock chick look or team it with a blazer for something more sophisticated. This ombre leopard print scarf from Dorothy Perkins is a more colourful take on the trend, but you can get more traditional tan coloured scarves from River Island, French Connection, and Bebe.

## 4. Leopard Print Earrings

![Leopard Print Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/4_leopard-print-earrings_600x643.jpg)

Earrings might not be the first thing you think of when it comes to leopard print, but they can be a cute and quirky way to wear this print. This cute set of heart earrings by River Island from ASOS features a fun leopard print pair. You can also get similar styles of earrings by Betsey Johnson and Guess that are all under the $30 mark.

## 5. Leopard Print Hat

![Leopard Print Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/5_leopard-print-hat_600x507.jpg)

Adding a hat to your outfit is an easy way of adding that extra bit of polish to your look. This leopard print hat would also easily spice up a plain outfit. At $52, this Topshop hat might stretch your budget a little bit, but it’s a great alternative to your basic black or tan hat. You could also check out Lucky Brand and ASOS for similar hats that are currently on sale.

## 6. Leopard Print Headband

![Leopard Print Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/6_leopard-print-headband_328x400.jpg)

Hair accessories are a great way of updating your hairdo. This leopard print headband from Forever 21 is a steal at just under $5. Wear it with your hair up or down - either way would look just fine! ASOS and Forever 21 both stock a range of cute leopard print hair accessories.

## 7. Leopard Print Sunglasses

![Leopard Print Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/7_leopard-print-sunglasses_600x330.jpg)

Stay cool in a pair of leopard print sunglasses. Depending on the style you choose, they could be a classic cat eye shape or maybe a more modern flat top frame. These particular sunglasses are around $20 from ASOS and would look great with a range of outfits. Also check out stores like Topshop and Forever 21 for other takes on leopard print sunglasses.

## 8. Leopard Print Watch

![Leopard Print Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/8_leopard-print-watch_600x766.jpg)

Accessories are a great way to wear leopard print. They still make a statement without being too over the top. Leopard print watches are a great way of updating your ‘arm party.’ Check out ASOS, Modcloth, and Kohl’s for a range of different leopard print watches.

## 9. Leopard Print Jacket

![Leopard Print Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/9_leopard-print-jacket_600x647.jpg)

Work a fresh and fun angle by adding a leopard print jacket to your look. Instead of a classic leopard print coat, try something more modern like a cropped jacket. This bomber jacket from Forever 21 is not only affordable, but also plays into one of this season’s hottest trends. Add this bomber jacket to your outfits to create a range of sport luxe looks.

These are just some leopard print items worth considering. Depending on the style of leopard print you go for, it could add either fun or classic elements to an outfit. How would you style these leopard print items?
