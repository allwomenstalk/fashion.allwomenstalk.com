# 7 Gothic Romance Fall Pieces ...

This season’s fashion takes a walk on the dark side with gothic romance fall pieces. Black and brooding, gothic elegance is back in style for fall with leather, lace and velvet pieces suitable for the season’s dark, moodier fare. Fierce vampy fashion and Victorian romance give this gothic trend a modern makeover swimming in lavish opulence and luxe details. Don these 7 Gothic Romance Fall Pieces for traditional gothic inspiration with Victorian edge.

## 1. Like You a Lattice Dress

![Like You a Lattice Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/Untitled-6_300x506.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
To embrace your dark side this season with a romantic twist don a delicate little lace dress for gothic inspiration that has mid century Victorian edge. With a deep berry hue, the slip underneath this French little black dress offers a lavish pop of color while still remaining opulent and moody for pure gothic glam. Pair with all black leather or velvet accessories to complete your dark side transformation.

## 2. All Saints Neely Bodysuit

This bodysuit is alternative and edgy with a seductive silhouette that features all the sexy and strappy details needed to create a spot on bondage look. For serious edge opt to wear this bodysuit under a fitted black or leather jacket.

## 3. Mango Snap Buttons Epaulettes Cape

![Mango Snap Buttons Epaulettes Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/75236776_02_B-400x560_400x560.jpg)

**Price:** $149.99 at [shop.mango.com](http://shop.mango.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Nothing screams gothic like a black cape does (Dracula anyone?), but this cape gets a modern revival with a high collar and snap epaulettes for cool cloak chic. Wear this cape buttoned up for a bold statement while dressed in head to toe black. Leather accessories complete this look for a strong and sleek style.

## 4. H&M Black Peplum Top

![H&M Black Peplum Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/hmprod_384x449.jpg)

**Price:** $29.95 at [hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sheer black blouses embellished with luxe finishings like this H&M peplum top create detail and visual interest to a basic black top. When wearing head to toe black to pull off your gothic glam look, remember to pay attention to the details since this will set you a part from anyone else trying this trend for fall. The glittery thread and textured weave in this peplum top adds a touch of sparkle and shine for true Victorian romance.

## 5. Elizabeth and James Winston Brocade Pants

![Elizabeth and James Winston Brocade Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_elizabeth-and-james-winston-brocade-pants_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $395 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/iMsbDB)
Richly romantic and luxe, brocade’s opulence stems from its super ornate patterns, metallic shimmer and textured detailing. The finishing on these Elizabeth and James metallic brocade pants creates stunning eye-catching detail that will leave no head unturned. Wear this ultra-glam pair of pants with rich dark fabrics like velvet or lace for mysterious romance.

## 6. Topshop Black Embroidered Lace Skirt

![Topshop Black Embroidered Lace Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/27K17BBLK_large-373x560_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $40 at [topshop.com](https://goo.gl/PJHyFF)
To unleash your inner Goth with dark and romantic elegance, an embroidered lace skirt is the quintessential Gothic piece. The fall runways unveiled uber-feminine ensembles mixed with a hard, strong edge for a fierce vampy look. The sheer see through design makes this skirt subtlety sexy and sweet with just enough dark power behind it to pull out a Gothic romance vibe. Pair this skirt with an inky lace top and leather bootie for a spellbinding polish.

## 7. Rich and Skinny Leather Legacy Pants

_ **Price:** $250 at eluxe.ca_
What Gothic look would be complete without an all out leather piece? The tough urban cool of these Rich and Skinny pants are so sleek and sexy they’ll have you oozing confidence. When paired with more gothic touches like all black lace and sheer details, you get a look that is the perfect blend between tough and chic, giving a sweet edge to your outfit. The leather pant is the ideal urban update for this romantic Goth trend making it wearable and contemporary for any style.

Relish in head to toe black for fall as this sleek and sophisticated style will have you going strong all season long. When paired with edgy elements and structured cuts, gothic romance is a chic way to vamp up your look with a Victorian twist. Are you daring enough to embrace your inner dark side this season?
