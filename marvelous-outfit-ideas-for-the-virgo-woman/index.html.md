# 20 Marvelous Outfit Ideas for the Virgo Woman ...

You prefer to keep it simple and sophisticated with timeless pieces and classic designs. You tend to pick and choose what trends to follow. You look fabulous in beige, tans, browns, greens, and navy blue and you LOVE your shoes! Celebrity Virgo fashionistas include Beyoncé, Blake Lively, Cameron Diaz, and Jennifer Hudson.
