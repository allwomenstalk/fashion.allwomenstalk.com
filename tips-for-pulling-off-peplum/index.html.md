# 8 Tips for Pulling off Peplum ...

**How to wear peplum** may not be a question on everyone’s lips, but it’s one worth checking out this season. If the new season collections are anything to go by then peplum is definitely the shape to watch out for. Some people may be scared off my peplum silhouettes, but it can look quite flattering. The exaggerated shape of the peplum can in fact create a slimmer silhouette by diminishing the surrounding areas. It’s all about knowing how to wear peplum well. Take a look at the following wearing peplum how-to.

## 1. What’s Your Hip Size?

The peplum silhouette can be suitable for all sizes. Knowing **how to wear peplum** all comes down to your hip size. Those with narrow hips can get away with big frills and bold patterns as this added fullness at the hips creates a curvier appearance. Those with wider hips should steer clear of contrasting colours, cut away peplum finishes, and busy patterns.

## 2. Black on Black

Ease your way into wearing peplum by sticking to a monochrome colour palette. Black on black is probably the safest way you could go. Flouncy peplum flourishes can take some getting used to, and wearing this trend in black is one way to wear them subtly.

## 3. Streamline Your Look

Peplum detailing can add fullness around your waist, so keep the rest of your outfit fairly streamlined. If you want to know **how to wear peplum** this way, think of teaming a peplum skirt with a fitted blouse or a peplum top with a pencil skirt or skinny jeans.

## 4. Peplum on Top

Avoid teaming peplum tops or jackets with voluminous separates. Like mentioned previously, it’s all about keeping your outfit streamlined. The fuller the peplum silhouette, the slimmer the skirt or pants should be.

## 5. Crop It

Since peplum flourishes sit mainly at the waist, show them off by wearing cropped jackets and blazers. Longer or oversized jackets will just drown out the shape. This tip for **how to wear peplum** is all about creating a balanced outfit.

## 6. Hip Vs. Hem

The placement of the peplum detail is important when deciding on how to style your outfit. While peplum detailing commonly occurs at the waist, it can also appear at the hem of skirts and dresses. A skirt with peplum detailing at the knee can be trickier to style. Generally, use the rule of balance and team bigger peplum silhouettes with more fitted tops.

## 7. Add a Belt

This simple **wearing peplum how-to** can make all the difference. Since peplum detailing adds a bit of emphasis to your hip area, cinch in your waist with a slim belt. This will help define your waist and enhance the silhouette.

## 8. Heel Matters

Complement the bold peplum silhouette with a strong heel. Peplum detailing can be quite feminine, so play this up with a pointed stiletto shoe. A chunky, sturdy heel can also work as they complement the bold shape of the peplum. Flat shoes can be trickier to pull off as they can create a ‘stumpy’ shape.

Peplum clothes can be a great way to fake an hourglass silhouette. It all comes down to knowing **how to wear peplum** well. What do you think of the peplum trend, and do you have any tips for how to wear peplum?
