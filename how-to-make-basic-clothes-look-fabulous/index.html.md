# Genius Video on How to Make Basic Clothes Look Fabulous ...

Want to know **how to make basic clothes look fabulous**? It’s one of the saddest facts of life, but the truth is that no matter how hard you try, you are probably never going to have as much income to spend on high-end designer fashion as you would like! We can’t all be as lucky as the likes of Kate Moss and Naomi Campbell, able to wear whatever they want without ever having to worry about the price tag.

We truly believe that a real sense of style is something that can’t be bought. A wealthy person could have all the money in the world and still not be able to dress as well as a fashion-conscious teenager who only has a weekend store job! It is all about having a great understanding of how you can start with the total basics, and build up from there to create a wardrobe that, whilst not the most expensive in the world, has the style and substance to rival the closet of any famous fashionista!

Just because you don’t have the dough, it certainly doesn’t mean that you can’t match those famous ladies in terms of sense of style and achieving a killer look; it just might be that your looks are put together with a bigger focus on a budget! This video shares a few great tips on how to make your basic clothes look fabulous. Believe me when I say that this video has the potential to completely change your outlook on your fashion options! So, here's **how to make basic clothes look fabulous**.
