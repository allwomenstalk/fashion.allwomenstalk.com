# 10 Fashion Trends to Try on a Budget ...

Working the latest fashion trends on a budget is definitely achievable. While the hottest trends may originate on the runways of New York, Milan, and Paris, there are plenty of ways to incorporate them into your everyday looks without forking out for designer duds. It is how the majority of us get by looking fashionable everyday, isn’t it? Here we’ll look at some of the hottest trends right now and how you can work these fashion trends on a budget.

## 1. Baroque

![Baroque](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/12_740x493.jpg)

Okay, so the Baroque trend might be all about opulence and excess, but you can still get away with working this fashion trend on a budget. If you can’t get your hands on intricately embroidered or embellished fabrics, then load up on the costume jewellery instead. Think crystal-look drop earrings, bauble necklaces, and chunky cocktail rings. Just keep an eye out for those bargain buys!

## 2. Military

![Military](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/22_600x900.jpg)

Military inspired looks are a big trend right now and have been all over the designer runways. On a budget? Then hit up consignment stores to pick up an authentic looking military jacket or parka, or even a pair of lace up boots.

## 3. Fashion Sneakers

![Fashion Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/32_554x831.jpg)

The sport luxe look is staying put, and one of the best ways to work the look right now is with a pair of fashionable sneakers. You don’t have to drop the dollars for a pair of *those* Isabel Marant sneakers either – a simple canvas pair can set you back as little as $10 and still look all sorts of stylish!

## 4. Oxblood

![Oxblood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/44_500x750.jpg)

All things oxblood are a must right now – clothing, shoes, and accessories. Look on-trend by incorporating this rich hue into your new season looks. Working this fashion trend on a budget is as simple as keeping a look out for anything in this bloody, berry shade.

## 5. Prints

![Prints](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/53_600x848.jpg)

Everyone seems to be mad for prints these days. Luckily you can also work these fashion trends on a budget. You’ll find plenty of affordable and on-trend pieces in places like Forever 21, Urban Outfitters, and H&M. Or, you could hit up thrift and vintage stores for unique, one-of-a-kind prints.

## 6. Leather-Look

![Leather-Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/64_600x901.jpg)

Leather clothing is having a real trending moment right now. However, real leather pieces will usually set you back a pretty penny. For a budget take on this trend, stick to leather-look pieces that not only look chic but are more animal-friendly too.

## 7. Collars

![Collars](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/77_960x640.jpg)

A statement collar is an easy way to jazz up an outfit. Simply pop one on over a plain t-shirt or a dress to update your look. You can pick up a range of stylish collars from places like Topshop and ASOS, but you could also create your own DIY version for something a bit more unique (there are plenty of tutorials on the internet!)

## 8. Graphic Sweaters

![Graphic Sweaters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/83_561x785.jpg)

One of the hottest looks this fall is the graphic sweater. It could be an all over print, pattern, or just one stand out graphic on the front. Don’t bother spending all of your hard earned cash on a designer number when you can easily work fashion trends on a budget thanks to stores like H&M and Forever 21. Or, why not dig out that old Mickey Mouse sweater? It’ll still be chic after all these years!

## 9. Oversized Clutches

![Oversized Clutches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/92_810x505.jpg)

Say buh-bye to handbags – it’s all about the clutch right now! You don’t need to fork out for them either since plenty of chain stores are stocking stylish oversized clutches. Alternatively, get crafty and multipurpose a colourful document folder or laptop pouch as a clutch!

## 10. Glitter Accessories

![Glitter Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/101_554x371.jpg)

Glitter shoes, glitter bags, glitter sunglasses – glitter is still having a fashion moment. It’ll instantly add some glitz and glamour to your outfit. Even on a budget, this is one of those budget fashion trends that can still look a million bucks!

Working fashion trends on a budget is all about being innovative and shopping smart. Money doesn’t always buy style, remember? What’s your best tip for working a fashion trend on a budget?
