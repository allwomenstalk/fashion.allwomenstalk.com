# 7 Superb Sun Hats to Keep You Shaded from the Sun ...

Being able to wear big, floppy sun hats is one of the great things about summer. Sun hats are so much fun to wear, and they also make a big fashion statement, which is one of the reasons why I love them. I also love them because they have the added benefit of shading my [fair skin](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/makeup-tricks-for-pale-skin/) from the sun. If you have fair skin, or if you just love wearing hats, you will adore all of these fabulous sun hats from Zappos. They are all stylish, and they will all shade you from the sun.

## 1. Columbia Sun Ridge Hat

![Columbia Sun Ridge Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tu/m7/oenw7th3_480x314.jpg)

The Columbia Sun Ridge Hat is one of the sun hats that combine fashion and function. This hat has an SPF of 30, which means it really will protect you from the sun’s rays. Aside from protecting you from the sun, the Columbia Sun Ridge Hat will also keep you fashionable this summer. The navy blue and white stripes are chic and perfect for wearing with a white sun dress.

## 2. Echo Design Multi Patterned Floppy Hat

![Echo Design Multi Patterned Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/u5/qkq6bqub_480x313.jpg)

This is a fantastic floppy hat! The broad brim is wide enough to keep the sun’s rays off of your face, but not so wide that you won’t be able to see where you are going. This hat comes in three fabulous colors, fuchsia, black, and blue.

## 3. Roxy by the Sea

![Roxy by the Sea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/z0/wb92jvww_480x310.jpg)

If you are looking for a neutral colored hat that has a bit of flare, this is the perfect hat for you. The Roxy by the Sea hat is a white hat that is very floppy. Walking around in this hat will make you feel very stylish, especially if you pair it with some cut off shorts and a blousy floral top!

## 4. Ralph Lauren Bright and Natural Sun Hat

![Ralph Lauren Bright and Natural Sun Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/ob/olefs2b8_480x312.jpg)

The bright blue stripes on this sun hat are perfect for summer! They remind me of a beautiful turquoise ocean. Speaking of the ocean, wouldn’t you love to wear this hat for your next beach vacation? I know I would!

## 5. Grace Hats Audrey Sun Hat

![Grace Hats Audrey Sun Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l2/kh/0hfgi2bm_480x312.jpg)

The Audrey Sun Hat is aptly named. It reminds me of hats the Hollywood stars wore in the '50s and '60s! I love the glamorous, vintage feeling of this chic hat. If I owned this hat, I would wear it with a basic shift dress to an outdoor party. Not only would I look great, but I wouldn’t have to worry about the sun!

## 6. San Diago Company Stripped Neon Floppy Hat

![San Diago Company Stripped Neon Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/k6/ek0o5iov_480x312.jpg)

Don’t you just love a hat that has a pop of color? This hat from the San Diego Company is perfect for adding interest to an outfit. The black and white stripes would have been enough by themselves, but the added hot pink stripe makes this hat really specially. If pink isn’t your color, you can also get this hat with an electric blue stripe or a neon yellow stripe.

## 7. Kate Spade New York Stripe Sun Hat

![Kate Spade New York Stripe Sun Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yi/l5/fn6b5van_480x321.jpg)

Kate Spade is such a fabulous fashion designer. All of the clothes and accessories in the Kate Spade line are fantastic, and this hat is no different. This hat comes in either pink with cream stripes or black with cream stripes, both of which are very stylish. The stripes are great, but the straw bow detail is what I love most about this hat. It looks so feminine without being cutesy, which I love.

Sun hats are an essential part of my summer wardrobe. They look great and the keep my face from becoming as red as a tomato! All of these are fantastic and would look great on anyone. Which of these hats is your favorite?
