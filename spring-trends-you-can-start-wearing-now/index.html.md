# 7 Spring 2014 Trends You Can Start Wearing Now ...

Get a head start on spring 2014 trends by slowly incorporating them into your wardrobe now. With fashion week cycles and collection previews taking place so far ahead of time, you can easily make preparations for your seasonal wardrobes well in advance. What better way to shake off the winter blues than to think about all the new fashion for the season ahead? Better yet, why not start incorporating them into your wardrobe now? These are just a few spring 2014 trends that you can start wearing now.

## 1. Fringing

![Fringing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/pn/fg/o1tk9mhi_600x896.jpg)

Fringed accents are one of the most noticeable spring 2014 trends. Start wearing this trend now by in the form of fringed jackets or skirts. Pair a fringed skirt with a cosy knit sweater and ankle boots for an urban take on boho-inspired dressing. Come spring, all you have to do is switch the sweater and boots for a chic shirt and sandals. You could also look into other clothes like fringed sweaters and jackets.

## 2. Full Skirts

![Full Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0r/ha/al/q5iqhown_600x900.jpg)

Ladylike full skirts will be a popular spring silhouette. Luckily you don’t have to wait until spring to wear them. Right now, stick to skirts made from heavier fabrics that can hold their shape. If you’re feeling a bit risqué you could try a leather-look full skirt for that mix of ladylike and street edge. Wear longer length full skirts with sweaters, ankle boots, and beanies for a stylish everyday outfit.

## 3. Crop Tops

![Crop Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/z8/oh/du1f9yl4_600x870.jpg)

Crop tops have been on our radar for some time now. Crop tops are great for balancing out high-waisted skirts and trousers so wearing a crop top right now can involve exposing little to no skin at all. Team a tailored cropped shirt with high-waisted trousers and a coat for a twist on polished dressing or wear a graphic cropped sweater with a high-waisted column skirt and sneakers for a sporty street-inspired look.

## 4. Bomber Jackets

![Bomber Jackets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gd/mn/i4/uir492l9_600x899.jpg)

Sporty silhouettes are still going strong and the bomber jacket is one of the most wearable ways to wear this spring trend. Bomber jackets come in a range of styles from floral printed numbers to varsity-inspired styles. Try wearing your bomber jackets with pencil skirts to toughen up a ladylike outfit.

## 5. Pretty Pastels

![Pretty Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/ni/yo/i7x8g74g_600x900.jpg)

Pastel colours seem to be a permanent fixture for the spring collections. While pink usually rules the roost, this spring will see colours like lavender, baby blue, and mint green take centre stage. Pop on a pastel coat or sweater for a cosy cold weather look. Alternatively, you could take advantage of all the great pastel accessories available at the moment to update a basic outfit.

## 6. Wide-Leg Trousers

![Wide-Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yx/al/cx/ywvk9akj_600x900.jpg)

Skinny-leg trousers and jeans are being given a run for their money. Wide-leg trousers were a popular trend on the spring 2014 fashion show runways and they can be easily incorporated into your everyday wardrobe. While spring’s wide-leg trousers might be of the lightweight variety, wear them now in stiffer fabrics and tailored styles.

## 7. Embellishments

![Embellishments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q4/mh/q7/wo876w6r_600x900.jpg)

Add a touch of glamour to your outfits with all things beaded and bedazzled. Embellished sweaters are a fun take on the trend and are ideal for wearing right now. Alternatively, you could also try cute accessories like embellished beanies and handbags.

These are just some spring trends that you can start bringing into your wardrobe right now. The transition will be so seamless that you’ll hardly notice the change of seasons! What spring trends are you looking forward to? What are your best tips for wearing spring trends now?
