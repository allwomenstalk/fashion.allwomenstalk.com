# 7 Red Winter Clothes Perfect for the Holidays ...

Whether you’re naughty or nice, red in all its glory ignites the holidays hottest looks and these red winter clothes are perfect for the holidays. While you can’t go wrong with a little seasonal sparkle, there’s another understated way of embracing festive fashion that goes beyond black. Red is the always-trendy tradition that sizzles in soiree outfits that make an entrance.‘Tis the season to get on Santa’s list! So get the mistletoe ready in these festive red winter clothes that are perfect for the holidays.

## 1. Little Red Dress

![Little Red Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/1_little-red-dress_600x815.jpg)

Boldly announce your arrival to the party in a red-hot number that dresses up a simple silhouette for definite drama. To don the perfect party favor frock in this festive fiery shade choose a hue to suit your skin tone. True reds compliment pale skin, while warm, deep shades work for darker tones. Get decked out with scarlet fever in this fabulous frock from Miss Selfridge priced at $45 or in other red-hot pairings from Alice & Olivia, Tibi, Meadham Kirchoff, H&M, Oasis and Steffen Schraut in price points of $60 to $1,300 for red winter clothes that are perfect for the holidays.

## 2. Red Winter Coat

![Red Winter Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/2_red-winter-coat_600x1102.jpg)

Ignite your winter wardrobe with a bold red coat to heat up the frosty chill. Channel this color of the season into cool cover-ups that make for classic show-stopping style. Invest in vibrant outerwear to turn every holiday ensemble from now to wow.

## 3. Red Party Skirt

![Red Party Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/3_red-party-skirt_600x689.jpg)

Set the night ablaze in a twirl worthy skirt fit for dancing the night away. Dazzle dance floor onlookers in a feminine full skirt offset with your favorite sleek sequined and shimmery pairings for hot holiday flair. Elevate all your fashionable fetes in this bold number and become the it girl on fire this New Year.

## 4. Red Festive Sweater

![Red Festive Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/4_red-festive-sweater_300x450.jpg)

Kick up your heels at all celebratory soirees equipped in a festively embroidered sweater that sparkles and shines. Tinsel like trimmings top off your seasonal ensemble for fun-filled festive gatherings that spread the holiday spirit in style. Ugly Christmas sweaters need not apply!

## 5. Red Trousers

![Red Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/5_red-trousers_600x702.jpg)

Spark elegant evenings with a modern twist in a pair of cheery crimson pants to salute old Saint Nick. Make the holiday rounds in trendy trousers to comfortably celebrate large family gatherings and festive feasts. You’ll be thankful for the extra room and relaxed fit when cozying up by the fire after tasty treats.

## 6. Red Blouse

![Red Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/6_red-blouse_400x534.jpg)

Soften the impact of a bold bright hue like high-spirited scarlet with romantic Victorian lace for a festive feminine touch. Try a racy red lace overlay top to complete your sophisticated soiree ensemble.

## 7. Red Blazer

![Red Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/7_red-blazer_272x392.jpg)

Get fired up, day or night, with bold work attire like a bright berry blazer to sere as the perfect accompaniment to all office appropriate occasions and after hour’s celebratory cocktails. Opt for fiery red in a satin or sheen festive finish to give your holiday pairings just the right amount of glitz and glamor for daytime or evening dressing.

Add a little festive flair to your seasonal ensembles with these 7 red winter clothes perfect for the holidays to get you on jolly Saint Nick’s list. Embrace the holiday season by stepping out in red to turn up the heat on your festive fetes. Naughty or nice, either way you’ll come out a winter winner in these merry reds set to make a cheerful Christmas statement. What’s your favorite way to red for the holidays?
