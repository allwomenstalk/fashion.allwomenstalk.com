# 7 Designer\-Inspired Affordable Printed Sweaters That Wont Break the Bank ...

Deck yourself out in some affordable printed sweaters this season. Printed sweaters are a top trend right now, with plenty of designers releasing their own take on this must-have item. However, dropping huge sums of cash on a designer sweater by Kenzo or Givenchy isn’t an option for most of us. Instead, we’ve scoured the stores to bring you some printed sweaters that are much easier on the wallet. Take a look at these affordable printed sweaters for the season ahead.

## 1. Tiger Print Sweater

![Tiger Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203054_2742_350x500.jpg)

If you can’t afford that Kenzo tiger print sweater (and let’s face it, not many of us can) then hit your favourite fashion chain stores for affordable printed sweaters in similar styles. This lightweight grey sweater from Modcloth features an eye-catching tiger face print and will set you back around $50. Wear it with skinny jeans, ankle boots, and a floppy fedora for a cool off-duty look.

## 2. Navy Heart Print Sweater

![Navy Heart Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203056_3155_599x752.jpg)

Heart print sweaters are a popular choice and designers like MSGM, Moschino, and Wildfox are all doing heart print sweaters this season. This navy sweater features a white floral heart print with a crew neck design with ribbed trims. It’s around $30 from Dorothy Perkins and you can find more affordable heart print sweaters at ASOS. Wear it over a collared shirt and with a leather-look skater skirt for an edgy take on preppy dressing.

## 3. Blush Leopard Print Sweater

![Blush Leopard Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203054_9432_341x437.jpg)

Leopard prints pop up every season and right now leopard print sweaters are a chic choice. French fashion house A.P.C. did a version but you can find yourself bargain buys from Dorothy Perkins and Mango. This crew neck sweater features blush leopard print and costs around $45 from Dorothy Perkins. It would look great teaming with boyfriend jeans and ballet flats for a casual weekend look.

## 4. Cat Print Sweater

![Cat Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203053_0550_349x490.jpg)

Let your inner cat lady shine through with a print sweater. This sequin number will set you back around $55 from Modcloth and is a fraction of the cost of versions by Alice + Olivia and Markus Lupfer. You can also check out Urban Outfitters and Dorothy Perkins for more cat print sweaters.

## 5. Landscape Print Sweater

![Landscape Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203056_0271_407x569.jpg)

Digital prints have been pretty popular with designers lately. You’ll definitely stand out from the crowd in a digital print sweater. This landscape print sweater is a bit pricier at $70 from Mango. It has a slouchy, cropped fit and would look great worn over a buttoned up white shirt dress and pointed pumps.

## 6. Polka Dot Print Sweater

![Polka Dot Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203054_3055_407x569.jpg)

Polka dot prints are everywhere right now and designers like Burberry are hopping on the spotty bandwagon. A simple black and white polka dot print sweater is an easy way to wear the trend this season. This sweater costs around $35 from Mango and you can find similar affordable sweaters at Modcloth and ASOS.

## 7. Monochrome Print Sweater

![Monochrome Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/10203056_0929_599x712.jpg)

Stand out from the crowd in a bold monochrome print. This animal-inspired printed sweater from Topshop is similar to one by MSGM. Of course, the designer one retails for around $300 but the chain store one is a fraction of the cost at $64. Wear it with leather-look pants and sneakers for a sport luxe look.

Whether you prefer traditional patterns or on-trend animal motifs, there’s no escaping the printed sweater this season. Will you be sporting a printed sweater this season? Which of these sweaters do you like best?
