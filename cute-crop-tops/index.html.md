# 7 Cute Crop Tops ...

Cute crop tops are a popular look this season. This belly baring look can probably be credited to the current 90s revival and sport-inspired trends. However, if you’re not fond of the athletic look, there are a range of cute crop tops available for pulling off a more demure look. A cute crop top would look great teamed with [high waisted skirts](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-high-waisted-skirts/) and shorts this summer. If this is a look you’re keen to try out then take a look at a few cute cropped tops below.

## 1. Topshop Geo Lace Crop Vest

![Topshop Geo Lace Crop Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-geo-lace-crop-vest_350x524.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/4zQ9fD)
Cute crop tops in pastel colours are a sure-fire winner this season. This pretty pink top features an all-over geometric lace pattern and has a simple slip on design. For a funky weekend look, try teaming this top with some faded denim boyfriend jeans and strappy flat sandals.

## 2. Topshop Petite Love Heart Crop Tee

![Topshop Petite Love Heart Crop Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_topshop-petite-love-heart-crop-tee_350x269.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/aDrbsx)
The print on this cropped tee is just too cute. The print is made up of heart-shaped candies with phrases such as “hug me” and “I love you” featured on them. This top would look great teamed with a high-waisted black skirt and strappy black heels.

## 3. Modcloth ‘Bluegrass Singer’ Top

![Modcloth ‘Bluegrass Singer’ Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_modcloth-bluegrass-singer-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Inject some free-spirited boho charm to your outfit with this cute crop top. It has a relaxed fit and features a diamond patterned bodice and lace edging. For a laidback look, pop this top on over a flowing maxi dress and team with an assortment of eclectic jewellery.

## 4. Modcloth Watermelon Print Top

![Modcloth Watermelon Print Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_modcloth-watermelon-print-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cropped top features a cute watermelon print. It also has a relaxed cut and is made from semi-sheer satiny fabric. Style this top with high waisted denim shorts, lace up shoes, and a floppy straw hat for a fun summer look.

## 5. Topshop Embellished Scallop Crop Top

![Topshop Embellished Scallop Crop Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_topshop-embellished-scallop-crop-top_350x503.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/UNbRKr)
When it comes to cute crop tops, you can’t beat floral embellishments and scallop details. This cornflower blue top combines all of these details into a stylish, wearable top. This shade of blue would not only look great teamed with white and neutral tones, but other pastel shades as well. Wear it with a full skirt and ballet pumps for a perfectly ladylike look.

## 6. Topshop Ice Cream Crop Sweat

![Topshop Ice Cream Crop Sweat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_topshop-ice-cream-crop-sweat_350x481.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/s22mNv)
Cosy up in this cropped sweater. It comes in a sweet shade of pink and features a embroidered ice cream motif on the front. Wear this cropped sweater over a full-skirted dress in a complementary colour for a cute and playful look.

## 7. Preen Cropped Cashmere Sweater

![Preen Cropped Cashmere Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_preen-cropped-cashmere-sweater_350x380.jpg)

**Price:** $890.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/F7K4D0)
As far as cute crop tops go, this one is definitely on the pricier end of things. However, you can’t deny its cute colour block design and ruffled collar. If I had this cropped sweater in my possession I’d team it with a full skirt or dress and some ladylike court shoes.

A cute crop top is one way to inject some playfulness into your wardrobe. Look for ones with cute patterns and detailing to give your outfit that sweet edge. What do you think of these cute crop tops?
