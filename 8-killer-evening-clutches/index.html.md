# 8 Killer Evening Clutches ...

**Killer Evening Clutches** can add a touch of glamour to any outfit. It’s always a treat to be able to dress up. After all, how often do you get to don your best attire, spend a borderline ridiculous amount of time grooming yourself, and spend the night feeling like a perfect ten? Selecting the perfect dress and shoes may be the source of some anxiety, but nothing rivals the stress of having to then accessorize said dress or outfit with jewelry and purse. Behold 8 **killer evening clutches** that may just streamline the process of accessorizing. Affordable, classy, and practical, these eight, gorgeous **killer evening clutches **may just exponentially cut down your prep time for your next night out.

## 1. The Boho Clutch

![The Boho Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Boho-Clutch_400x239.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/eER7Ux)Not all girls dream of the opportunity to wriggle their way into a princess gown complete with formed bodice and crinoline. In fact, four years of liberal arts college taught me that many girls prefer a much more laidback approach of evening wear. In fact, tailored boho gear has rounded the corner from purely casual to after hours chic. The good news is that there are plenty of casual killer evening clutches, too. Pair this decorated clutch with a pair of dark skinny jeans, an oversized blouse and feather hair extension for a night time look that blends class with haute hippie style.

## 2. Woven for the Weekend Clutch

![Woven for the Weekend Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Woven-for-the-Weekend-Clutch_376x400.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)Calming, relaxing weekends at the shore are what this demure woven clutch brings to mind. Fashioned from straw, this nude clutch is ideally suited for cute summer sundresses and teacup skirts. It manages to combine the lofty sophistication of a poised 1950’s housewife with a touch of liberated girlishness. Complete with tassel, this clutch could easily debut at a family get together or a picnic with a sweetheart. Match it up with something floral and girly for a sweet, dreamy look.

## 3. Portfolio Envelope Clutch

![Portfolio Envelope Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Portfolio-Envelope-Clutch_400x257.jpg)

**Price:** $36.59 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/6qMXz9)Mixing business with pleasure is not always recommended, but in this case it’s a must. This oversized portfolio doubles not only as a sleek briefcase, but its sharp edges and tasteful hardware make it an excellent choice for an evening affair that requires a bit of businesslike allure. Tote it about with your favorite blazer or spice up a neutral sheath dress with this pop of sensational color. It's just one of a new breed of functional, versatile killer evening clutches.

## 4. Ace Python Clutch

![Ace Python Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Snakeskin-Clutch_400x269.jpg)

**Price:** $69.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)Chances are that if you’ve ever dressed up to go out you’ve encountered a striking problem: when going out, sometimes we rely to heavily on neutral or dark colors. From the classic LBD to cowl-necked sweaters, many of us tend to rely on drab colors when we’re trying to appear nice or sophisticated. However, the less electrifying end of the color spectrum doesn’t always have to be a point of concern. Favoring simpler colors allows us to experiments with colorful accessories like this subtle silver evening clutch. Accented with a dark closure and hints of gold, this eye-catching piece of arm candy is the perfect addition to any classic evening outfit.

## 5. Satin Glisten Clutch

![Satin Glisten Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Satin-Glisten-Clutch_400x175.jpg)

**Price:** $11.00 at [payless.com](http://www.payless.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)Let’s face it: at times we all need a little bit of a break. Obsessing over every aspect of one’s wardrobe can, after all, be quite tiring. When you need a clutch that is simple, elegant, and goes with positively everything, check out this black satin clutch with oversized bow from Minicci. Large enough to store the essentials, this purse also comes complete with a shoulder strap for ease of use. Always remember, Payless is great for killer evening clutches that don't cost an arm and a leg.

## 6. Black Ruched and Tasselled Clutch

![Black Ruched and Tasselled Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Black-Ruched-and-Tasselled-Clutch_400x236.jpg)

**Price:** $35.75 at [debenhams.com](https://www.debenhams.com/)There’s just something about this clutch. Perhaps it’s the gentle ruching that adorns the front, or maybe it’s the ornate gold piping. Then again, it could be the braided tassel dangling daintily along the front flap. Anyway you spin it, this sexy little clutch screams seduction. Keep it in hand or wear it over your shoulder with its attached shoulder chain for comfort and practicality that’s sure to endure the night. Set off the gold accent with a pair of equally metallic heels and pair with a sweet, satin strapless for that come hither look.

## 7. Ally Vintage Wristlet by Hobo International

![Ally Vintage Wristlet by Hobo International](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/09/Hobo-International-Ally-Vintage-Clutch_400x309.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)I purchased this wristlet in lime green as an everyday clutch and I absolutely love it. Each wristlet features a detachable wrist strap, six interior credit card pockets, a change pocket, and three general storage pockets. Newer versions such as these also feature an attached keychain, which is ever so useful for a weekend night out when you’re loath to carry your keys in your pocket. Compact, durable, and stylish, this cute little billfold functions equally as a daytime wallet and evening clutch. Oh, and if this price seems a little steep, stop into your local TJ Maxx-they tend to carry Hobo for much cheaper. Even if you have a fondness for glamorous killer evening clutches, there are always ways to save a buck.

## 8. Perspex Box Clutch

Well, now Asos has managed to turn that fashion no-no into an oh-yes. Cut from clear plastic, this space age clutch exemplifies futuristic fashion. Sure, you may have to pare down what you put into it, but the Perspex Clutch has a funkiness that you just can’t pass up.
