# 8 Divinely Dazzling Belts ...

A stunning belt can make all the difference to an outfit. If you don´t believe me, take a plain dress and try it on. Then add a dazzling belt and see the change! Suddenly a basic dress becomes an amazing outfit with just a quick addition. So I´ve picked out some truly stunning belts from netaporter.com – you don´t have to spend this much, but it´ll show you what beautiful belts are out there …

## 1. Miu Miu

![Miu Miu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_miu-miu_460x330.jpg)

**Price:** $350 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/TvRpXk)

This dazzling belt caught my eye straight away – if I had $350 to spare I´d love to jazz up my Little Black Dress with it! What girl doesn´t love sparkly accessories? A glittery belt is the perfect way of adding that touch of glamour without overdoing it. I´d wear it with my short-sleeved black dress and a beret for Parisian style.

## 2. J Crew

![J Crew](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_j-crew_460x226.jpg)

**Price:** $30 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/zrMlH4)

This braided belt is more in the affordable bracket. What I love about it is the dazzling orange colour. If you don´t like bright outfits, but want to add a little color to what you´re wearing, then belts are a great way of doing this, as are bags. This one will look as good with summer outfits as with winter clothes.

## 3. Marni

![Marni](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_marni_460x388.jpg)

**Price:** $320 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/KlZTr0)

This is the less-is-more of belts. A stunning belt doesn´t have to be dramatic; sometimes it´s the quality of the materials and design that do the trick. This Marni belt seems on first glance to be just a basic black belt, but those with an eye for style will see how well the gold buckle stands out against the black background.

## 4. Emilio Pucci

**Price:** $835 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oUBjMC)

I must admit that I like the look of this belt more than I like the idea of wearing it! I don´t have a very long waist, so wide belts are too constricting for me. However, it is beautiful, isn´t it? The contrast decoration of the thin faux-belt makes it look like two belts in one.

## 5. Gucci

![Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/5_gucci_460x159.jpg)

**Price:** $880 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ihyiGs)

For a bit of bling (well, quite a lot actually), take a look at this Art Deco- inspired Gucci belt. The Swarovski crystal buckle is very 1920s Manhattan. If your budget doesn´t quite stretch that far, look for a gorgeous buckle in a flea market and attach it to a more budget belt.

## 6. Bottega Veneta

**Price:** $750 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/nw6Ykm)

Most belts save their decoration for the buckle. This Bottega Veneta belt has an interesting twist to it, with the gold trim on the belt itself, and a very plain buckle. It would look stunning cinching in a plain dress, or with a white satin shirt.

## 7. Proenza Schouler

![Proenza Schouler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/7_proenza-schouler_460x357.jpg)

**Price:** $255 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/klxj0h)

Do you like tribal chic? Then opt for a belt like this intricately woven and colourful one from Proenza Schouler. It also adds a subtle touch of color without being overwhelming. The woven pattern has a fun look about it, so is ideal for giving a young, summery, look to an outfit.

## 8. Sofia Kokosalaki

![Sofia Kokosalaki](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/8_sofia-kokosalaki_460x336.jpg)

**Price:** $2,310 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/o3IdDz)

We´re well into dream territory here, but what a dream it is! This is a truly dazzling belt in more ways than one, with more than $2k worth of Swarovski crystals. If you´re a very creative person, you could even try making your own version – wouldn´t it look stunning?

I´ve shown you some amazingly dazzling belts here – okay, few of us would pay that much.for a belt, but it´s fun to dream! Use them as inspiration, and find more affordable versions to make your outfits look special. Belts don´t just have to be practical, they can be beautiful too. How much would you pay for a truly stunning belt?
