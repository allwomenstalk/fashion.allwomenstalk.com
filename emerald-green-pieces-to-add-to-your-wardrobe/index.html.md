# 7 Stunning Emerald Green Clothes to Refresh Your Wardrobe This Season

Emerald green clothing items are a timeless wardrobe must-have. This gorgeous jewel tone looks great with every skin tone and hair color, ensuring it's flattering on everyone. If you're looking for more inspiration, check out our guide on [how to style bold colors](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-bold-colors) to make your outfits pop. It’s a perfect color for your winter wardrobe but transitions beautifully into the warmer months too. Whether you're looking for an emerald green 2 piece set or just a single statement piece, mixing and matching these emerald green clothing pieces into your wardrobe is a sure way to stay on trend and feel sophisticated.

Why we love emerald green:

- It’s universally flattering for all skin tones.

- It adds a luxurious feel to any outfit.

- It transitions easily between seasons.

## 1. Nasty Gal Disco Nights Dress

![Nasty Gal Disco Nights Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/1_nasty-gal-disco-nights-dress_340x511.jpg)

Emerald green clothes don't get much better than this stunningly sparkly dress from [Nasty Gal](https://www.nastygal.com)! This sequined velvet dress has a low-cut back and long sleeves, making it perfect for parties during the colder months. You will look and feel glamorous wearing this emerald dress! Add some heels and jewelry and you'll have the perfect emerald party outfit. If you prefer a more casual look, check out the [Cowl Neck Sweater](#2) below.

## 2. H&M Cowl Neck Sweater

![H&M Cowl Neck Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/HM-Cowl-Neck-Sweater_384x449.jpg)

This emerald green sweater from [H&M](https://www2.hm.com) will keep you warm and in style all winter long. At a budget-friendly price point, you can't go wrong with this versatile purchase for your emerald green wardrobe. This knit sweater has a ribbed cowl neck that is super cozy and fashionable. Pair it with jeans and boots for a simple, comfortable outfit that still makes a statement!

## 3. Topshop Emerald Modal Ribbed Tights

![Topshop Emerald Modal Ribbed Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/3_topshop-emerald-modal-ribbed-tights_600x899.jpg)

Add some color to your neutral wardrobe pieces and stay warm with these emerald green, ribbed tights from [Topshop at ASOS](https://www.asos.com/topshop/). Layer on some tall boots and boot socks with a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/) this winter. Adding colored tights adds variety and interest to an otherwise simple outfit. For more tips on styling accessories, see how we style the [Mango Snake Foulard](#5).

## 4. Zara Tartan Blazer

![Zara Tartan Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Zara-Tartan-Blazer_400x640.jpg)

Blazers are a staple in any modern closet, and this tartan [Zara](https://www.zara.com) blazer is no exception. The navy and emerald tartan pattern makes this blazer reminiscent of classic menswear while still remaining feminine and chic. You can dress it down with jeans or dress it up with slacks or a skirt, making it one of the most versatile emerald green clothes you can own. It's definitely one of those [wardrobe essentials](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/wardrobe-essentials-every-woman-needs) that never goes out of style.

## 5. Mango Snake Foulard

![Mango Snake Foulard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/4_zara-tartan-blazer_407x569.jpg)

This pretty decorative scarf from [Mango](https://shop.mango.com) is an easy way to dress up any outfit. The snakeskin print has hints of emerald and is edgy yet girly. The material is super soft and has gently frayed edges, perfect for adding texture. This emerald accessory looks great with casual outfits when you're out shopping or meeting friends for coffee.

## 6. Elizabeth and James Colorblock Lori Halter

![Elizabeth and James Colorblock Lori Halter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Elizabeth-And-James-Colorblock-Lori-Halter-448x560_448x560.jpg)

This mod, colorblocked halter top from [Elizabeth and James](https://www.kohls.com/catalog/womens-elizabeth-and-james.jsp?CN=Gender:Womens+Brand:Elizabeth%20and%20James) has just the right amount of emerald green for those who prefer a subtle touch. The majority of the top is black, with a white halter and emerald edging. The pretty keyhole front adds shape to this flowy chiffon top. It’s a great example of how to mix and match bold colors with neutrals for a sophisticated look.

## 7. Haute Hippie Junk Sequin Mini Skirt

![Haute Hippie Junk Sequin Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Haute-Hippie-Junk-Sequin-Mini-Skirt_394x527.jpg)

It's the ultimate showstopper for your emerald green closet.
