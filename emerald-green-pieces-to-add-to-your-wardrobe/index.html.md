# 7 Emerald Green Pieces to Add to Your Wardrobe ...

Emerald Green Clothing items are a wardrobe must have for 2013. Emerald clothing is a great way to show off Pantone's color of the year for 2013. This gorgeous jewel tone looks great with every skin tone and hair color so it's sure to be flattering on everyone! It's a great color for your winter wardrobe, but can look great in the warmer months as well. Mix and match these emerald green clothing pieces into your wardrobe to stay on trend in the new year!

## 1. Nasty Gal Disco Nights Dress

![Nasty Gal Disco Nights Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/1_nasty-gal-disco-nights-dress_340x511.jpg)

Emerald green clothing items don't get much better than this stunningly sparkly dress from Nasty Gal! This sequined velvet dress has a low cut back and long sleeves, making it perfect for parties during the colder months. You will look and feel glamorous wearing this emerald dress! Add some heels and jewelry and you'll have the perfect emerald party outfit.

## 2. H&M Cowl Neck Sweater

![H&M Cowl Neck Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/HM-Cowl-Neck-Sweater_384x449.jpg)

This emerald green sweater will keep you warm and in style this winter. At the low price of $24.95, you can't go wrong with this purchase from H&M. This emerald green knit sweater has a ribbed cowl neck that is super cozy and fashionable. Pair it with jeans and boots for a simple, comfortable outfit!

## 3. Topshop Emerald Modal Ribbed Tights

![Topshop Emerald Modal Ribbed Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/3_topshop-emerald-modal-ribbed-tights_600x899.jpg)

Add some color to your neutral wardrobe pieces and stay warm with these emerald green, ribbed tights from Topshop. Layer on some tall boots and boot socks with a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/) this winter. Adding colored tights adds variety and interest to an otherwise boring outfit, and it's a perfect excuse to wear Pantone's color of the year!

## 4. Zara Tartan Blazer

![Zara Tartan Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Zara-Tartan-Blazer_400x640.jpg)

Blazers are hugely popular right now and this tartan Zara blazer is no exception. The navy and emerald tartan pattern makes this blazer reminiscent of menswear while still remaining feminine. You can dress it down with jeans, or dress it up with slacks or a skirt, making it very versatile!

## 5. Mango Snake Foulard

![Mango Snake Foulard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/01/4_zara-tartan-blazer_407x569.jpg)

This pretty little decorative scarf is an easy way to dress up an outfit. The pretty snakeskin print has hints of emerald and is edgy yet girly. The material is super soft and has gently frayed edges. This emerald accessory looks great with casual outfits when you're out shopping!

## 6. Elizabeth and James Colorblock Lori Halter

![Elizabeth and James Colorblock Lori Halter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Elizabeth-And-James-Colorblock-Lori-Halter-448x560_448x560.jpg)

This mod, colorblocked halter top has just the right amount of emerald green. The majority of the top is black, with a white halter and emerald edging. The pretty keyhole front adds shape to this flowy chiffon top. At $265, this Elizabeth and James halter is one of the pricier items on this list, but it's worth saving your money for!

## 7. Haute Hippie Junk Sequin Mini Skirt

![Haute Hippie Junk Sequin Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/Haute-Hippie-Junk-Sequin-Mini-Skirt_394x527.jpg)

Everyone deserves a sequined mini skirt and this is the perfect one for 2013! Haute Hippie's Junk Sequin Mini Skirt in emerald is sparkly and stylish. It's perfect for the weekend. Pair it with heels and a simple top to let this skirt be the star of your outfit!

Emerald green clothing is a staple for your 2013 wardrobe! Add a few of these emerald clothing items to your closet to update your look for the new year. At affordable prices, you can't go wrong with these stylish emerald pieces! What is your favorite emerald green clothing item?
