# 7 Modern Pencil Skirts for a Stylish Look ...

**Pencil Skirt** outfits present a great way to incorporate this season’s streamlined silhouette into your wardrobe. The **pencil skirt** is suitable for both work and play. Understated designs work well for more modest outfits while skirts in bright fabrics and interesting textures are a great way to mix things up. Why not try making the **pencil skirt** the statement piece of your next outfit?

## 1. Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Philosophy Wool Blend Pencil Skirt

![Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Philosophy Wool Blend Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f0/gj/ohg9f0tp54c92f7e86467059420419_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Bwbmra)
This wool-blend skirt can function as both work wear and evening wear. It has a fitted waistband, asymmetric back hem, and draping detail at the side. It also features a concealed zip fastening at the side. Team this with a blouse and enclosed heels for work, and wear it with a slinky tank top and stiletto heels for a night out.

## 2. Moto Black Denim Seamed High Waisted Skirt

![Moto Black Denim Seamed High Waisted Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/p6/ioq42bkt54c92f7f8b593461782888_300x446.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/eKOhRV)
A denim pencil skirt is a great modern interpretation of the look. The denim adds a tough edge to what is a classically feminine design. This skirt has a high-waisted design, seamed pockets, and button and fly fastening at the front. For a rock chic look, team it with a loose-fitting band t-shirt and some statement earrings.

## 3. Topshop Jersey Pencil Skirt

![Topshop Jersey Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/z7/erjakc1254c92f807158a591501641_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/i72yEV)
Cobalt blue is a colour that is enjoying renewed popularity this season. Inject some colour into your wardrobe in the form of this jersey skirt. It has a fairly minimal design and reaches approximately mid-calf. As this skirt has the potential to be quite figure hugging, it might pay to invest in some shapewear!

## 4. Topshop Burnt Orange Panel Bodycon Pencil Skirt

![Topshop Burnt Orange Panel Bodycon Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/yr/utmyq9gr54c92f8145de1665499681_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ixbhgV)
Get ready for fall in this burnt orange pencil skirt. It has a figure-hugging bodycon design. It also features panel detailing at hits at the knee. Pair this skirt with a loose blouse and trench coat for a fail-safe look.

## 5. Alexander Wang Neoprene Pencil Skirt

![Alexander Wang Neoprene Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qf/vd/zjwgk76754c92f8283762113420231_300x431.jpg)

**Price:** $445.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The neoprene fabric is what makes this skirt oh-so-modern. Alexander Wang is always at the forefront of modern yet understated design. This dense knit skirt has a fitted waistband and shirttail hem. Slip this skirt on over a loose tank top and pair with leather wedge ankle boots.

## 6. Topshop Caramel Faux Leather Trim Pencil Skirt

![Topshop Caramel Faux Leather Trim Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/cf/dhqfevj754c92f83866e0665727526_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $36.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/b95SbZ)
This caramel pencil skirt has a uniquely modern design. It features a faux leather trim at the waistband, exposed zip pockets, and front split detail. It also has a fly front zip. Wear it with a cream top and patent ballet flats for an effortlessly chic look.

## 7. Calvin Klein Collection Mayfair Sequin Embellished Pencil Skirt

![Calvin Klein Collection Mayfair Sequin Embellished Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/08/Calvin-Klein-Collection-Mayfair-Sequin-Embellished-Pencil-Skirt_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $178.50 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This sequin embellished skirt is an excellent choice of evening wear. It has a concealed zip and hook fastening and is fully lined. When styling this skirt, keep the rest of your outfit fuss-free. Wear it with a pale gold top and peep toe heels for a stylish look.

Balance out the **pencil skirt** by adding some volume on the top half – think loose fitting t-shirts or blouses. **Pencil skirts** also look great worn with heeled shoes. Will you be trying out the **pencil skirt** this season?
