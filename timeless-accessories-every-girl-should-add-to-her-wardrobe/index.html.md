# Totally Timeless Accessories Every Girl Should Add to Her Wardrobe ...

There are some classic and timeless accessories every girl should add to her wardrobe and that will give you another great excuse to go out shopping. Like you needed an excuse! Tastes and fashions can change over the years but there are certain items that are classic and will never date. Accessories can make an outfit and these timeless accessories are no exception.

## 1. Simple Studs

![Simple Studs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fu/xc/hwomhruo_600x900.jpg)

I love statement earrings and they can add that much needed glamour to your look. However, simple studs are another one of the timeless accessories every girl should invest in. Whether they're a pair of diamond, gold or pearl studs, they can make your ears shimmer and sparkle. They're also more appropriate for everyday wear and great to add a little glamour to office attire.

## 2. Statement Necklace

![Statement Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/kr/w1i6ms1a_600x1109.jpg)

A statement necklace is another timeless accessory and will add some glitz and glamour to an otherwise simple outfit. You may want to invest in both a gold and silver statement necklace so that you can add them to a variety of outfits.

## 3. Simple Necklace

![Simple Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fd/y9/aiizvqs3_600x900.jpg)

I went through a phase when I wanted Carrie's name necklace but my name is a little long so I settled for the simple initial instead. Although a statement necklace can add some glamour to an otherwise plain and simple outfit, a simple necklace can go with everything and adds a little class and simplicity to any outfit. Whether it's a simple heart or initial necklace, a necklace can add a little understated glamour to your look.

## 4. Cross Body

![Cross Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mb/fd/yfj0bumt_600x900.jpg)

A cross body bag is another timeless accessory that will go with every outfit. I love my cross body bag and I feel more secure with it when I'm out shopping. I'm less likely to put it down and forget it in a store and people can't snatch it from me either! Invest in a leather cross body that will go with every outfit and is great for both shopping and traveling.

## 5. Big Bag

![Big Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/pk/rp0p5izp_600x818.jpg)

Every girl needs a big bag that goes with every outfit. A big plain bag is likely to go with everything and is an investment piece. There are so many types of bags on the high street to suit every style and although we can't all afford a beautiful Birkin, there are many more that will cost a lot less but will still look glamorous.

## 6. Classic Clutch

![Classic Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/wz/hgudfjsp_500x750.jpg)

Every girl needs a clutch bag for a night out. They come in various shapes and sizes but it's a good idea to invest in one that will go with a variety of outfits.

## 7. Statement Ring

![Statement Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cs/e6/i8v3gy3y_600x807.jpg)

A statement ring is another timeless accessory that is going to add some glamour to your outfit. It depends on what statement you want to make as you can get statement rings that are literally the size of volley balls! Whatever the size or style, a classic statement ring is another great accessory investment.

## 8. Sexy Scarf

![Sexy Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/li/nj/z9b08qtm_600x712.jpg)

A scarf is another classic and timeless accessory to add to your wardrobe. A leopard print scarf is a great addition to your wardrobe and looks great with a plain shirt, pants and heels. You could also opt for a bold color to go with any outfit. It's an inexpensive way to add a little glamour to your outfit.

## 9. Beautiful Bracelet

![Beautiful Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bp/ki/nsrfd13x_600x906.jpg)

We all need a little arm candy and a bracelet will add some interest to your look. There are so many different [types of bracelets](https://jewelry.allwomenstalk.com/types-of-bracelets/) to invest in. Whether it's a charm bracelet, an edgy leather bracelet or bangle, ladies are literally spoilt for choice!

These are my classic and timeless accessories every girl should add to their wardrobe. Can you think of any others?
