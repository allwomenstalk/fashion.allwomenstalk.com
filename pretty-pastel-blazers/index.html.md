# 7 Pretty Pastel Blazers ...

**Pastel blazer** styles are a fantastic addition to any wardrobe. Think about adding one to your wardrobe this spring-summer season. A pastel blazer can create a polished, sophisticated look all while maintaining the lightness of a spring wardrobe. The following are just a few pretty pastel blazers you might want to consider adding to your wardrobe this season.

## 1. Topshop Pale Blue Boyfriend Blazer

![Topshop Pale Blue Boyfriend Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/1_topshop-pale-blue-boyfriend-blazer_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/xf3qMo)
This boyfriend blazer is a great everyday option. Unlike pastel pinks, which can sometimes be hard to style, this pale blue colour is quite versatile. **Wear this pastel blazer** with tan cropped pants and a white tank top for an effortlessly chic look.

## 2. Forever21 Longline Woven Blazer

_ **Price:** $32.80 at forever21.com_
A longline blazer works well worn over dresses or pants. This blazer comes in a wonderful blush colour and features a notched collar, two button fastenings at the front, and slightly padded shoulders. This pastel blazer would look great worn on the weekends over a breezy sundress and with leather sandals.

## 3. Topshop Ponte Shrunken Blazer

![Topshop Ponte Shrunken Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_topshop-ponte-shrunken-blazer_324x400.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/5rB1wL)
This pastel blazer comes in a lovely lemon colour. It has a boxy, shrunken fit and features a popper fastening at the front. It also features turned up cuffs and slit pockets at the front. Wear this lemon yellow blazer with a pair of printed pants, a white tank top, and strappy sandals.

## 4. Topshop Co-ord Fluid Blazer

![Topshop Co-ord Fluid Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_topshop-co-ord-fluid-blazer_311x400.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/tJt55N)
This pale pink blazer has a classic double breasted design. It would look great worn over a fitted mini dress and with pair of pumps. A pair of matching shorts is also available, so you can work this season’s hot short suits look.

## 5. Forever21 Essential Linen Blazer

_ **Price:** $19.80 at forever21.com_
With a lot of focus on relaxed, boyfriend style pastel blazers, be sure not to overlook the cropped blazer. A shorter, cropped blazer is perfect for cinching in and accentuating the waist. This linen-blend peach blazer has a single button fastening, 3/4 sleeves, and decorative flap pockets. Wear it with a shirt dress and pumps for a classically feminine look.

## 6. Forever21 3/4 Sleeve Jacket

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
This lightweight jacket is perfect for creating a slouchy silhouette. It comes in a light blue colour and has a notched collar and 3/4 sleeves. This blazer would look great worn with a pair of printed shorts and a loose t-shirt for a casually sophisticated look.

## 7. Forever21 Modal Blend Jacket

_ **Price:** $32.80 at forever21.com_
If you’re after something a bit brighter than your ordinary pastel blazer, try this coral number on for size. It has a classic cut and has a slight sheen finish. It’s perfect for creating a polished spring-summer look. Wear it with some cuffed cream chinos, a simple tank top, and patent pumps.

When looking at a pastel blazer for the warmer months, choose one made from lighter and more breathable fabrics. A pastel blazer teamed with a floaty sundress creates a cool, polished look. Which of these pastel blazers do you like best?
