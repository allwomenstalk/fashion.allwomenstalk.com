# Spring Fashion for Your Zodiac Sign ...

We all know that as the seasons change, our fashion should too, but it can be difficult to find the inspiration to make the changes sometimes, especially if you aren’t in a super fashiony mood! At time like this, it can be really helpful to just look to someone else for some advice, and luckily for you I am here to do just that for this season! Here is the spring fashion you should be getting into this year, according to your zodiac sign!

## 1. Aries

![White, Clothing, Dress, Fashion, Beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/tl/tkdrqojt5cae4affac9eb911164828_600x750.jpg)

You are fearless, independent and a real trailblazer, so when it comes to spring fashion for an Aries, you should be making bold choices like animal print, statement jewellery, and vivid colours!

## 2. Taurus

![Clothing, Dress, Fashion model, Shoulder, Fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/tg/itxchw275cae4b2bac95f117802992_600x751.jpg)

You are the understated type, so black is always going to be a good colour for you in any season. You can add some personality and style to any look by paying more attention to gold jewellery.

## 3. Gemini

![Clothing, Pink, Street fashion, Fashion, Eyewear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/jz/fcubqi855cae4b42aed23328376944_600x750.jpg)

You are the kind of person who likes to follow all of the latest trends to say on top of current fashion, so to get your inspiration you just have to look at all of the more popular online influencers!

## 4. Cancer

![Clothing, White, Jeans, Turquoise, Denim,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/fw/mdy6uc025cae4b78ef0c0620902034_600x750.jpg)

You are much happier when your style leans on the casual side of things, so the one item that you should be looking to attain for spring is a really good quality pair of jeans that will get you through to summer.

## 5. Leo

![Street fashion, Yellow, Orange, Clothing, Fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/i0/xir9v6m25cae4bb1aaa1c154984977_600x750.jpg)

You love to be the center of attention, so it’s big, bold colours and patterns all the way for a Leo! Don’t be afraid to wear things that other people wouldn’t dream of!

## 6. Virgo

![Clothing, Street fashion, Waist, Fashion, Shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/rr/gw2to0025cae4befe2895116280081_600x688.jpg)

You are a perfectionist, so it is best to stick to clean lines and classic silhouettes. Spring can be one of the classiest seasons to dress for because the fair temperatures lend themselves to classy layering.

## 7. Libra

![Clothing, Pink, Street fashion, Beauty, Fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/as/nlztke1q5cae4c1871fb2263313932_600x750.jpg)

You are a very classic beauty, so things like soft pastels and flattering silhouettes are perfect for the spring.

## 8. Scorpio

![Jeans, Clothing, Denim, White, Shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/cm/scxrlyob5cae4c3daa74a553457713_600x750.jpg)

You are a real creature of habit, so your style tends to be the same pretty much all year around. You have your key colours and patterns that you stick to, and as long as you know your shapes and shades, you will look great 365 days of the year.

## 9. Sagittarius

![Yellow, Clothing, Street fashion, Outerwear, Jacket,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/ee/b48xu9ff5cae4c91e6b1d274915586_600x750.jpg)

You are often too busy to even have time to worry about your fashion, but the kicker is that you look great in everything anyway! If you need advice, then stick to nice and simple garments that you can pair with interesting accessories.

## 10. Capricorn

![Jeans, Denim, Snapshot, Jacket, Fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/nc/sfg2sgv65cae4ca831624778846447_600x750.jpg)

Denim, denim, denim! Not everyone can pull of the double denim look, but there is nothing that suits a Capricorn more at this time of the year!

## 11. Aquarius

![Pink, Clothing, Photograph, Dress, Shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/qg/aiagx1325cae4cd931cf0599998317_600x750.jpg)

Fun colours and breezy fabrics are the order of the season for you. You want to promote as much fun in your outfits as you have in your natural personality.

## 12. Pisces

![Clothing, White, Dress, Day dress, Sleeve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/zk/g8jrj6nu5cae4cf6ae758355315687_600x750.jpg)

Lots of flowing fabrics are what you should be wearing this spring. Beautiful airy maxi dresses that are just as flowy and gorgeous as the water that represents your sign.
