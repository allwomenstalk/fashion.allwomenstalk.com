# 8 Divine Wine Coloured Winter Accessories ...

Winter Accessories and what’s fashionable varies each year. When it comes to on-trend winter colours, they usually revolve around rich, warm hues. This winter, you can’t go past wine coloured winter accessories. Think of deep burgundy and purple-red hues. Get inspired with a few of the wine coloured winter accessories below.

## 1. Topshop Metallic Skinny Belt

![Topshop Metallic Skinny Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oi/as/i9qk1vy154c934f3cc9b3740653976_400x178.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/bfVRSD)
Belts are excellent winter accessories for updating an outfit. This stylish belt has a shimmer metallic finish and can be worn on the waist or on the hip. It also has a contrasting black buckle and comes in three sizes. Wear a wine coloured belt threaded through a pair of black jeans, and with a slouchy blouse and ankle boots for a stylish everyday look.

## 2. Aurelie Bidermann Bracelet

![Aurelie Bidermann Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/c8/upjuwe5254c934f4c59f3277909562_400x398.jpg)

**Price:** $380.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/NLuyb4)

It also has a paperclip fastening with tassel details at the ends. This bracelet is quite versatile and would look great with a similarly toned outfit.

## 3. Topshop Velour Floppy Fedora

![Topshop Velour Floppy Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/oe/jydun98n54c934f5c0075252690402_400x314.jpg)

For a seriously cool daytime look, you can’t go past the floppy fedora. This one from Topshop is made from premium wool and comes in a rich red colour. Team it with indigo jeans, a t-shirt, and a slouchly black coat for a casually chic look. While this particular hat is a bit pricey, stick to felt styles for something more affordable.

## 4. Topshop Spot Pussybow Scarf

![Topshop Spot Pussybow Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/tb/rxw25m3d54c934f698998859397621_220x400.jpg)

This spotted scarf is an example of fun and quirky winter accessories. It is made from a woven burgundy fabric and features a bold contrasting spot print. The large pussybow design is the focal feature of this scarf. For a seriously feminine look, team it with a white silk blouse, grey wool pencil skirt, and almond toe pumps.

## 5. Comme Des Garcon Luxury Long Zip Wallet

![Comme Des Garcon Luxury Long Zip Wallet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/j3/bg6rv4ha54c934f7924c6665461601_243x400.jpg)

The interior contains card and bill slots as well as a snap coin pocket. This luxe wallet would make a stylish addition to any handbag.

## 6. Solange Azagury-Partridge Hot Lips Ring

![Solange Azagury-Partridge Hot Lips Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/qi/nurxuj4354c934f87c240201343150_400x318.jpg)

## 7. Oliver Peoples Sunglasses

![Oliver Peoples Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dh/lp/ujhn9bwx54c934f949595746467547_400x168.jpg)

## 8. Topshop Metal Corner Bag

![Topshop Metal Corner Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a0/z7/o2zsk8uv54c934fa11aa6383266784_400x251.jpg)
