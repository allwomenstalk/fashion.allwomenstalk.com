# Affordable 🤑Brands to Shop for Girls Looking for a Bargain ...

No one should have to spend a fortune when they're looking to save money, and I'm sure we all know by now how easy it is to spend more than what you originally planned to! 💸

Save yourself from falling into this trap by shopping these top affordable brands that will guarantee you save money in the long-run as well as getting really good quality purchases! ✨

Everyone has a different idea of what's cheap, affordable and expensive so it's totally okay if you don't agree with some of the brands on this list - just bear in mind that others might so they're still worth a mention! ❤️

## 1. ASOS

![clothing, sleeve, t shirt, dress, spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/fg/ybxwdwrv579c23f931232073368082_600x432.jpg)

ASOS is a great website to look at if you're looking to save money on your purchases, simply due to the fact that there's so many different brands on their website that you can choose from! The wide range means there's a really good chance of finding the cheap to affordable ones and grabbing a few good deals!
[asos.com](https://goo.gl/4Pu56N)

## 2. H&M

![clothing, little black dress, dress, sleeve, t shirt,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/bi/r6nblz58579c24dfb4b03245684623_600x397.jpg)

H&M is pretty much a fashion giant these days because they sell heaps of things for women, men and even kids! This means that there's something for everyone at H&M so you're guaranteed to find something you will totally love! Plus the cheap to affordable prices means it's easy to save money while shopping with them.
[hm.com](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Brandy Melville

_ Brandy has basically everything you could ever ask for when it comes to fashion! Not only do they have super affordable clothes that are to die for but they even have a cool line of graphics t-shirts and sell heaps of accessories too!
brandymelvilleusa.com_

## 4. Windsor

![clothing, gown, dress, day dress, formal wear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/il/h9fme01n579c237fdd959769642201_600x430.jpg)

Windsor is another great choice when it comes to saving money while still getting good quality purchases! Keep an eye on their website and grab the discount and promo codes they advertise - perfect for anyone wanting to pay a little bit less off their total purchase!
[windsorstore.com](https://www.windsorstore.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Forever 21

![clothing, hood, plaid, jacket, hoodie,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/h9/jtu83wpv579c238ace4fc538194281_600x430.jpg)

Forever 21 is your go-to brand for those basic, staple pieces that every woman should have in her wardrobe! It's one of the most inexpensive brands on this list so it's definitely worth a look if you're after a few more super reasonably priced pieces!
[forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Topshop

![clothing, pattern, costume, outerwear, has,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/vk/d2zpyvd6579c23a53f293128100863_600x296.jpg)

No matter where you live in the world you must have heard of Topshop by now! They offer everything from clothes, shoes, accessories and even a handy makeup line that are all pretty affordable, so it's only right that you should take a look at their amazing website and grab a few cheap products!
[us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/i53WQe)

## 7. Minkpink

_ Minkpink is a super affordable brand selling all things women love: clothing, sunglasses and swimwear! On top of that, the high quality of their products are totally worth snapping up if you want clothes that will be sure to last you a long time!
