# A Sequin Jumpsuit and Other Eye\-Catching Looks for Girls Who Want to Be the Center of Attention ...

Sometimes we just crave the feeling of walking into a room and having all eyes on us, and that's totally okay! There's nothing wrong with wanting to be noticed, so go right ahead and have all eyes on you, ladies! What's your favorite tip below which always works for you?

## 1. Glamourous Sequin Outfit 👚

Anyone who loves to look glitzy and glamourous will love the idea of wearing a sequin outfit! The sparkle that it brings alone is enough to have all eyes on you! If, however, sequins and glitziness isn't really your thing, you might want to opt for something sleek and sophisticated like a jumpsuit or something with a plunge neckline if you're more daring!

## 2. Classic Red Lippy 💄

A classic red lipstick is the perfect way to have all eyes on you! Choose one that is a great match with your skin-tone in particular, but also that works well with your hair color and eye color. Remember to bring your lippy with you so that you can continue to reapply throughout the night while you're sipping on martinis!

## 3. Soft, Romantic Waves ❤️

You may find yourself with the dilemma of what you should do with your hair and how you can make it work perfectly with your outfit...sound familiar? If ever in doubt, opt for soft waves as these will give your hair volume and lusciousness!

## 4. Sexy Stilettos 👠

If you're a girl who loves her stilettos you'll know that this is a sure way to have all eyes on you! Not only will a pair of stilettos add an oomph to your height, but it will give you a major presence in the room and therefore have those heads turning from the moment you walk in!

## 5. Statement Jewelry 💍

If there's one thing that jewelry is totally good at doing it's making a statement, am I right? Choose a pair of dangly earrings, a chunky necklace or a set of matching bracelets and you'll draw jealously as well as their undivided attention!

## 6. Dramatic Eye Makeup ✨

If you're not a fan of the classic red lippy idea and prefer a natural or neutral lip, you totally have the ability to have an amazing smoky eye which is sure to have all eyes on you! You can always use the services of a professional makeup artist if you're not too confident about doing your own dramatic eye makeup!

## 7. Elegant Pair of Pumps 👠

An elegant pair of pumps is a great alternative for anyone who doesn't feel totally comfortable wearing stilettos (or just doesn't like them)! You can't go wrong with a classic pair of black pumps, so don't be afraid to give these a go with your outfit if you have them!

So... Which one on the list is jumping out at you? Do you like the sequin jumpsuit idea, or the simple yet bold stilettos?
