# 9 Basic Clothing Items to Have in Your Closet ...

Regardless of your tastes in fashion, there are certain basic clothing items that you should have in your closet. The key component of being style-savvy is wearing items that are versatile. Fashionistas always know how to spot a great deal! Whether you are into the grunge scene or skate more on the bohemian side, these basic clothing items are sure to complete your look!

## 1. Little Black Dress

![Little Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_little-black-dress_600x904.jpg)

In the world of fashion, this is a no-brainer when it comes to basic clothing items. Having a little black dress can come in handy for those nights when you want something simple yet elegant without all the fuss of a specific color scheme. The right dress will accentuate all of your beautiful curves and features!

## 2. Little Black Something

![Little Black Something](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_little-black-something_600x799.jpg)

Conversely, if you're not into dresses, I'd say that having a black tank top or a pair of nicely fitted black pants at hand works just the same. The overall message is that the color black is the ultimate slimmer. Fashion shouldn't discriminate!

## 3. Ballet Flats

![Ballet Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_ballet-flats_533x800.jpg)

Sticking a pair of flats in your bag before you head out for a night on the town will save you a lot of trouble in the end. You will breathe a sigh of relief once you feel your heels bearing the brunt of your night! The best part about flats is that they come in different shades; there is bound to be a pair that matches your style and preferences perfectly!

## 4. A-line Skirt

![A-line Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_a-line-skirt_600x900.jpg)

I am obsessed with wearing a-line skirts; I love the fullness they give to any look. I pair my a-line skirt (purchased for only $6.80 at Forever 21) with anything and everything - I even buy shirts with my skirt in mind! This is arguably my best buy because it is so versatile!

## 5. I've Got a Black Belt in Fashion

![I've Got a Black Belt in Fashion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_ive-got-a-black-belt-in-fashion_400x690.jpg)

One of my favorite go-to items is a skinny black belt. Adding a belt to certain outfits can cinch in your waist and give you an hour-glass definition! I usually go for neutral tones when it comes to belts, depending on the outfit. On the other hand, because I always end up wearing something black in my color scheme for the day, adding a colored belt makes it that much easier to stand out!

## 6. It Was All Red

![It Was All Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_it-was-all-red_600x792.jpg)

Wearing red makes for a bold fashion statement. With the hues ranging from burgundy to cherry, I've found that red is one of the primary colors that complements most shades of skin! Whether it's a belt, headband or in the form of lipstick, owning a red item is essential!

## 7. Cardigan

![Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_cardigan_600x900.jpg)

I am the type of person that would rather be over-prepared than under-prepared. A cardigan is not only useful in the winter; it can be used for every season! Having one within your reach will do wonders when you're catching a summer flick and the temperature in the theatre becomes synonymous to a freezer.

## 8. Captain Underpants

![Captain Underpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/8_captain-underpants_600x900.jpg)

I know firsthand that one of life's many woes is finding a bra that actually comes in your size without fearing being poked by underwire. It may be worth it to spend a bit extra on a few pieces and then rotate them with those already in your collection. Ladies, wearing a bra that fits will surely make all the difference!

## 9. Turns to White

![Turns to White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/9_turns-to-white_597x897.jpg)

Whether I've worn it for five minutes or an hour, I always manage to either accidentally spill or rub something on my white shirts. Because I am a bit clumsy, I sometimes have a fear of wearing white. That being said, I've noticed that the color white generally tends to look dashing on other people! If you are more careful than I am, wearing white can actually accent your greatest assets. Start off with a white button-down shirt with black trimming to take advantage of the contrasting look!

Combining these basic clothing items with other articles of clothes is sure to leave a lasting impression on anyone you meet. The key to feeling like a fashionista is making sure that you are comfortable and confident in what you are wearing! Are there any other basic clothing items that you feel are necessary to have in your closet?
