# Here Are the Best Dressed Celebrities at the 2015 Golden Globes ...

So, the Golden Globes happened yesterday and before anything else I just want to say, Amy Poehler and Tina Fey ROCK! A big congratulations to all the winners, and a huge thank you to Tina and Amy for never failing to make us laugh. And now let's take a look at the fashion winners aka the best dressed celebrities at the 2015 Golden Globes.

## 1. Lupita Nyong'o

![Lupita Nyong'o](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/bc/j8t87yr4_600x834.jpg)

How does she do it every single time? Lupita looked like a dream in this lilac Giambattista Valli gown. Even her makeup was perfect! You've got to love how she's never afraid to take risks on the red carpet.

## 2. Anna Kendrick

![Anna Kendrick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f0/xn/vfq2p58k_600x903.jpg)

I LOVE this girl and I LOVE this Monique Lhuillier gown on her! She really does look like a modern day Cinderella, doesn't she?

## 3. Kate Hudson

![Kate Hudson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/h2/hegfj38f_600x969.jpg)

Kate Hudson showed off her banging body in this sexy Versace gown. It's not the easiest dress to pull off but Kate did a stellar job!

## 4. Jennifer Lopez

![Jennifer Lopez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/sc/mpriurr3_600x902.jpg)

JLo certainly made heads turn in this Zuhair Murad gown. Now THAT is what I want to look like when I'm 45!

## 5. Emma Stone

![Emma Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/q0/d4fwzxdi_600x857.jpg)

Emma Stone looked darling as always in this Lanvin jumpsuit. I'm loving the big ol' bow and her hair!

## 6. Matt Bomer

![Matt Bomer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/ws/xu5xijmg_600x901.jpg)

We couldn't leave out the boys now, could we? Matt Bomer looked rather dashing in this classic Ralph Lauren tux. Of course, this man could make a potato sack look good!

## 7. Benedict Cumberbatch

![Benedict Cumberbatch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fq/om/ka7m5mvq_600x969.jpg)

Okay, I may be horribly biased here but that tux fits him like a dream! Of course he's also totally amazing (and hilarious) for doing this to Meryl Streep and Margaret Cho:

![Benedict Cumberbatch](https://img.allw.mn/content/zh/qc/kk7x6rkp_636x358.gif)

Spill the beans ladies. Who was your favorite celebrity at the 2015 Golden Globes?
