# 9 Stylish Wide Brim Hats You'll Want to Wear Right Now ...

Wide brim hats are having a bit of a fashion moment right now. We’ve spotted the wide brim hat, in various forms, on several designer runways lately. It’s the ideal way of updating your outfit for the season ahead. You can’t beat a classic fedora, but straw sunhats are also a stylish choice for the sunny days ahead. These are a few wide brim hats worth checking out right now.

## 1. ASOS Wide Brim Felt Fedora Hat

![ASOS Wide Brim Felt Fedora Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/ra/cu5lwnqa_600x282.jpg)

Wide brim hats like this black fedora would make a stylish addition to any outfit this season. It has a classic pinched crown design with a wide brim and trim band. It’s made from pure wool and has a felt finish. Wear it with anything from sheer blouses and pleated skirts to chunky sweaters and skinny jeans. This ASOS number will set you back around $40 but you can check out Forever 21 for a similar version under $20.

## 2. River Island Shaker Hat

![River Island Shaker Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/sz/v2f8ruw1_600x262.jpg)

Structured shaker hats, like this one from River Island, are another popular silhouette. The stiff, structured design is perfect for working this season’s austere menswear-inspired trends. This stylish blue hat is made from 100% wool and has a soft felt finish with a wide cut and shaped brim. Team this hat with a white collared shirt and grey cigarette trousers for an on-trend menswear-inspired look.

## 3. ASOS Felt Fedora Hat

![ASOS Felt Fedora Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ty/uv/cne2ju5m_600x392.jpg)

This light grey fedora from ASOS is perfect for teaming with spring’s pastel colour palettes. This hat is made from felt and has a pinched crown, wide brim, and contrasting ribbon band. Wear it with a pastel sweater, faded jeans, and blush coloured shoes for a relaxed weekend outfit.

## 4. Topshop Big Felt Floppy Hat

![Topshop Big Felt Floppy Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/ia/lifr6m26_600x277.jpg)

Work a more relaxed vibe with a wide brimmed floppy hat. This one from Topshop comes in a neutral taupe colour with a floppy circular brim and slim contrasting ribbon band. Work a bohemian look and team it with a breezy white sundress and tan sandals.

## 5. Catarzi Wide Brim Straw Hat

![Catarzi Wide Brim Straw Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/ao/un7abcsh_600x291.jpg)

Wide brim straw hats are also an excellent choice right now. They’re great for wearing during the day and are a lightweight alternative to heavy wool and felt materials. This Cararzi straw hat is exclusive to ASOS and is made from woven straw. It features a grosgrain ribbon trim, domed crown, and wide brim. Wear it with a striped t-shirt, cut-off denim shorts, and canvas trainers for a relaxed nautical-inspired look.

## 6. Lack of Color Wide Brim Fedora Hay

![Lack of Color Wide Brim Fedora Hay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/gs/pgvr7iz9_600x278.jpg)

There is no escaping the pastel colour palette right now. This wide brim hat by Lack of Color would make a playful addition to your wardrobe. It is made from 100% wool and comes in a pale pink colour. Work a polished look and team it with a white shift dress, structured handbag, and patent ballet flats.

## 7. Brixton ‘Gia’ Wide Brim Hat

![Brixton ‘Gia’ Wide Brim Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/zo/jqnxcsyl_600x299.jpg)

Tan wide brim hats are a versatile everyday option. This Brixton hat has a floppy wide brim with a braided leather band and fringed tassel. For a cool and casual everyday look, team this tan hat with a denim shirt and printed shorts.

## 8. Eugenia Kim Wide Brim Fedora

![Eugenia Kim Wide Brim Fedora](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/iv/wfaqp1gx_600x377.jpg)

Make a statement in a brightly coloured hat. This Eugenia Kim fedora comes in a striking red colour. It has a pinched crown, stiff wide brim, and a wide ribbon trim. Wear it with floor-sweeping maxi dresses for a truly statement-making look.

## 9. French Connection ‘Linn’ Hat

![French Connection ‘Linn’ Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/me/b8iz8ae5_600x202.jpg)

This French Connection straw hat comes in a cool monochrome design. It has a woven design with a dipped crown and wide cut floppy brim. Stick with the monochrome theme and team it with a breezy black dress and ballet flats for a chic daytime outfit.

Add some panache to your look with a wide brim hat this season. Styles vary from structured shaker hats to floppy straw numbers. Which of these hats do you like best?
