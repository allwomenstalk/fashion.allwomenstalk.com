# 8 Chunky Knit Socks to Wear around the House This Winter ...

**Knit Socks** are cute, comfortable and very, very practical. When the temperature drops, **knit socks** are a must have. Chunky **knits socks** are perfect for wearing around the house in the winter time. Thicker socks will not only keep you warmer, but they can also be more durable and longer-wearing than thinner knit styles. Take a look at my pick of **knit socks** below.

## 1. Urban Knit Mix Bobble Socks

![Urban Knit Mix Bobble Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/qd/c7x2p4wh54c93618a01a8923250348_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/J6FDTS)
These colourful knit socks have a fun and playful design. They come in a chunky teal knit and feature multicoloured bobble details throughout. They also feature a designer stamped button. These oversized socks are ideal for wearing on a cold winter’s night.

## 2. Hunter Balmoral Knitted Socks

![Hunter Balmoral Knitted Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/ee/vugh2mgm54c93619690be436003161_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pPX2Xy)
These socks are perfect for wearing at home or outdoors with your Wellington boots. They come in a hot pink colour combination, with a bubble-gum pink main and fuchsia trim. They have a thigh-high design and fold over at the top. These socks have a very sporty appearance so would also be well suited to a range of outdoor activities.

## 3. Urban Knit Fairisle Booties

![Urban Knit Fairisle Booties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/tg/xfe9dxxp54c9361a5b368067996403_267x368.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vJ8ir6)
These knit socks have a short ankle-length design. They feature a cream and red Fairisle pattern and oversized pom-pom detailing at the side. These festive booties are perfect for wearing in the winter, and would also make cute gifts or stocking fillers.

## 4. Topshop Eli Lurex Slipper Socks

![Topshop Eli Lurex Slipper Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/ni/q05y57y554c9361b7e83b213457451_267x397.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/P8Qshd)
These black knitted slipper socks feature circular embellishments to the heels. The black knit is accentuated with lurex thread. The weave is quite open, so these socks would be best left to warmer nights. They also come packaged with a black velvet bow, which is a wonderfully delicate touch.

## 5. Forever 21 Ribbed Melange Socks

_ **Price:** $3.90 at forever21.com_
These ribbed melange socks are so cheap, you’d be tempted to stock up on more than one pair! They feature a solid heel and toe, and are mid-calf length. These light-weight knit socks come in a range of colours from blue to light brown.

## 6. Topshop Cable Knit Ankle Socks

![Topshop Cable Knit Ankle Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/g0/mix144th54c9361d60481632741929_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/fPmYJE)
These cable knit ankle socks come in a wonderfully warm orange colour. They feature cable-knit detailing and a contrasting charcoal trim top. Wear these to bed or around the house, and team them with your favourite pyjamas.

## 7. Urban Knit Texture Boot Socks

![Urban Knit Texture Boot Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/by/xjfu529v54c9361e32062256699064_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HAAcOk)
These bootie styled socks are quite expensive, but they had a sturdier design. They feature a chunky knit and flat, shoe-like sole. They come in an army green colour and also feature a designer-stamped button at the ankle. These are ideal for wearing around the house, on both wooden and carpeted floors.

## 8. Topshop off White Aztec Chunky Socks

![Topshop off White Aztec Chunky Socks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pk/pa/o89f0tzz54c9361ef042b693717660_267x399.jpg)

**Price:** $10.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Dg1EYc)
Aztec prints are still quite popular, so why not try out the print in a pair of socks? These off white knit socks feature a black Aztec design. They’re of a chunkier knit which is perfect for keeping your toes warm in winter. As they’re a lighter colour they are more prone to get dirty, so it might be wise to keep them as bed socks only.

There’s no better feeling that being rugged up in layers and **knit socks **on a cold winter’s day. You can lose a lot of heat from your extremities, so making sure your feet are looked after should be a priority. **Knit socks** are the perfect remedy to this. Which of these **knit socks** would you want to be wearing?
