# 7 Adorable and Cozy Cashmere Sweaters ...

I love autumn, so when I think of cozy cashmere sweaters, cooler weather is on my mind. But these lightweight sweaters are also perfect for cool summer evenings and end of summer days, when an unexpected cold front sweeps in. I think of long walks in cozy cashmere sweaters, or evenings spent indoors by the fireplace with great friends, as comfortable as my sweater.

## 1. Jewel Embellished Cashmere Sweater by Halogen

![Jewel Embellished Cashmere Sweater by Halogen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ya/x7/vgb2mmi2_600x920.jpg)

If you’re shopping for cozy cashmere sweaters for yourself or a gift, check out this very special style embellished with glittery hearts and eyes. This sweater, seen at Nordstrom, is available in black or white cashmere as well as the featured grey. This looks as if it tends to run a little on the short side, which makes it just perfect for a cute shirt to peek out from the bottom.

## 2. Embellished Cashmere Sweater by Halogen

![Embellished Cashmere Sweater by Halogen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/vb/fd542x6q_600x920.jpg)

This is adorable just the way it’s styled, but it would look equally terrific with a pair of jeans. I think cashmere tends to be a little scratchy, so I’ll wear something underneath to cure that, rather than pass up a great looking sweater. Another plus about wearing a shirt underneath your cashmere sweater is cutting down on the need to dry clean so often. Find this cutie at Nordstrom.

## 3. Drape Neck Cashmere Sweater by Nordstrom Signature

![Drape Neck Cashmere Sweater by Nordstrom Signature](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/up/mn2jibld_600x920.jpg)

I’m a huge fan of the draped neck sweater and this one has the perfect amount of drape. Too much and you’re into cowl neck territory. I see this as a very casual sweater and I think a pair of grey jeans would be a perfect pairing. I wouldn’t wear a necklace to compete with the neckline, so a pair of dangly earrings that aren’t too large would be perfect. Let the draping be the star!

## 4. Drape Front Cashmere Cardigan by Aqua

![Drape Front Cashmere Cardigan by Aqua](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/i3/syf1g789_550x688.jpg)

This drape front sweater is so incredible looking that I want it in every color, and Bloomingdale’s agrees by offering it in this blue/black twist, along with oatmeal, light grey, heather brown, black & white twist, and solid black. This is an essential piece in your wardrobe and one that’s worth getting in a couple of colors.

## 5. Moto Cashmere Jacket by C by Bloomingdale's

![Moto Cashmere Jacket by C by Bloomingdale's](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/l0/l8o0g1nz_550x688.jpg)

What a cute little charcoal grey jacket! I love the nipped in waist and fold-over collar. Did you happen to notice the zip closure was asymmetrical? This also comes in black, midnight blue and heather. I’d prefer this with a camisole that was visible from the neckline, and probably with an inch or more showing on the bottom as well.

## 6. Shimmer Split Back High/Low Sweater by Caslon

![Shimmer Split Back High/Low Sweater by Caslon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/vb/ek22nz0d_600x920.jpg)

I want this right now! I can’t decide which is cuter; the front or the split back. I love the tiny pocket on the front, and the way this sweater drapes with such ease. It’s perfect with your favorite jeans. With so much interest in both the back and front, you’ll look awesome coming and going! Available at Nordstrom.

## 7. Studded Split Back High/Low Sweater by Caslon

![Studded Split Back High/Low Sweater by Caslon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xo/ky/fwyvlvue_600x920.jpg)

While this studded sweater also comes in magenta, with auburn hair, I’ll opt for the black every time. It’s great with jeans and there’s enough detail on the front with the studs, to keep it from being boring. Because of the studs, I’d do minimal jewelry to keep the sweater the focal point. See this at Nordstrom.

With so many choices , there should be enough to keep everybody happy. I love the shorter ones with a cute shirt underneath, and the longer ones look great with skinny jeans. I love neutral colors, so cashmere sweaters are a natural for me. Do you wear cashmere sweaters? Which of these do you see yourself wearing?
