# Trend Alert: White Suits Are All the Rage This Season ...

Back up, guys... girls are proving that they can wear the suit WAY better! Some of Hollywood's leading ladies have put their own twist on this mens-inspired fashion take and I'm absolutely loving it! How would you wear this trend?

## 1. Dakota Fanning

![Dakota Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/zp/u2trarfj_440x660.jpg)

Dakota wore a suit jacket over a white tank and cream-colored thigh-high slit skirt. It's the perfect balance between sophisticated and sexy!

## 2. Rosario Dawson

![Rosario Dawson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/p3/aetmkchg_600x789.jpg)

Rosario Dawson added a sexy edge to the look by wearing a white suit sans shirt. If you got it, flaunt it, right?

## 3. Jada Pinkett Smith

![Jada Pinkett Smith](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/z6/s8pv2t1q_600x902.jpg)

Like Dakota, Jada paired her white pieces with a beige one! It's great because it doesn't let you look too "drowned out" in all that white.

## 4. Britney Spears

![Britney Spears](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/j9/ygcfyzus_600x799.jpg)

Britney showed off her brand new bob cut while promoting her lingerie line int Germany. She rocked a matching white suit set with a sheer lace top underneath.

What do you think of menswear-inspired trend?
