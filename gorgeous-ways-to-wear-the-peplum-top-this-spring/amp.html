Frequently Asked Questions

How can I style a peplum top for a casual look?
You can totally rock a peplum top casually! Pair it with your favorite skinny jeans or distressed denim. Throw on some cute flats or comfy sneakers, and you're good to go. It’s all about keeping it relaxed but chic.
What types of bottoms work best with a peplum top?
Peplum tops look amazing with slim-fit bottoms since they balance out the volume of the top. Think skinny jeans, tailored trousers, or pencil skirts. If you're feeling adventurous, a high-waisted maxi skirt can also look fantastic!
Can you wear a peplum top for a formal event?
Absolutely! For a formal look, opt for a peplum top with luxe fabrics like silk or satin. Pair it with a sleek pencil skirt or tailored pants, and add some classy heels. Finish off with elegant jewelry, and you're all set to impress!
Which colors are trending for peplum tops this spring?
This spring, bold colors like magenta, teal, and mustard are super trendy. If you prefer something softer, pastels like blush pink and lavender are also in. Don’t be afraid to experiment with patterns and prints as well!
How do I choose the right peplum top for my body type?
It’s all about proportions! If you have an hourglass figure, embrace the peplum to highlight your waist. For pear shapes, go for a peplum that flares out at the right spot to balance your hips. If you’re more apple-shaped, choose a peplum top that isn’t too flouncy and focuses more on creating a streamlined look.
