# 25 Gorgeous Ways to Wear the Peplum Top This Spring ...

Do you still love the peplum top trend or are you just now working up the courage to try one on for yourself? No matter where you are in your love for peplum tops, I'm sure that you'll find some gorgeous fashion inspiration for your peplum top looks on this list!

![clothing,thigh,leg,footwear,dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/b8/vgths_clothing_thigh_leg_footwear_dress_259x475.jpg)

![clothing,footwear,sleeve,tights,trousers,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/qc/m12gp_clothing_footwear_sleeve_tights_trousers_303x503.jpg)

![color,clothing,lady,photography,girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/ig/eqkfb_color_clothing_lady_photography_girl_550x825.jpg)

![clothing,lady,beauty,dress,fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/su/j67rz_clothing_lady_beauty_dress_fashion_428x640.jpg)
