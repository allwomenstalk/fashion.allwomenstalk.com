# 7 Colors to Brighten Your Wardrobe for Spring ...

Who wouldn't want new spring colours to brighten up your wardrobe! Spring is always fun, because it’s when you finally get to step out of your winter clothes, and put something fun and a lot less heavy on your body! Plus, it’s so much fun to shop for spring colours because there’s more options to choose from. So here are 7 colours to brighten up your wardrobe for Spring.

## 1. White, White, White

![White, White, White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/gt/tt8n13we_538x720.jpg)

White is one of the best colours to brighten up your wardrobe, literally because it’s one of the brightest colours on the spectrum! Spring is all about new things coming to life, and having white in your wardrobe will reflect the spring awakening we all want. Just make sure that you don’t spill anything on it!

## 2. Mint Green

![Mint Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/yx/cadr3v82_600x766.jpg)

I love, love, love this colour, I think it’s absolutely gorgeous. It’s a perfect breath for fresh air for the spring, and definitely one of the best colours to brighten up your wardrobe. If you’re new to the colour game, then start with something simple, like your nails or a belt, then slowly work up to a brighter colour.

## 3. Coral

![Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lw/ci/ql1ctyww_600x901.jpg)

This is one of those colours that looks a little different depending on what item of clothing you pick with it. How it looks as a shirt may work better for you than how it looks as a dress, but regardless, one of the best colours to brighten up your wardrobe for spring is coral. I reminds me of a strawberry sorbet!

## 4. Light Blue

![Light Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/bp/dxh4kpt0_403x600.jpg)

This is such a soft colour, and so beautiful yet striking. I think everyone needs to have something in this colour! And what better way to make that happen than to get something in a light blue as one of the prettiest colours to brighten up your wardrobe? I’m still looking for a nice denim shirt - that’s going to be my addition!

## 5. Sunny Yellow

![Sunny Yellow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/h9/hp3axrf4_600x780.jpg)

When I think of a sunny yellow, my mind instantly goes to DVF wrap dresses in a deep, sunny, happy yellow! This is definitely one of the best colours to brighten up your wardrobe for spring. Just looking at it will instantly make you smile.

## 6. Orange

![Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v1/ye/hfrdzho8_600x546.jpg)

Orange is strong colour, but whether or not it’s a bright orange or a lighter shade, this is definitely one of the colours to brighten up your wardrobe. I have a nice light sweater in this colour, and normally I never would’ve picked this colour, but luckily I decided to go outside my regular colour choices of black and white.

## 7. Ombre

![Ombre](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/w7/xqxytm01_600x927.jpg)

The great thing about ombre is that it comes in all different colours and shades and patterns. This is one of the best ways to use colours to brighten up your wardrobe for spring, because it’s fun and flirty. There are so many different options, you’re bound to find one that works for you.

I’m so excited when spring comes and I am always excited to dive in to my spring wardrobe! Living in Canada, we definitely have 4 completely different seasons, and it’s so nice to be able to wear something lighter, and a little more fun to celebrate spring. So tell me, what colours would you add to your spring wardrobe?
