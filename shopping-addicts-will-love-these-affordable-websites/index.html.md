# Shopping Addicts Will Love These Affordable Websites ...

There's nothing more satisfying than discovering a new, amazing website stocked with all of your favourite pieces! Shopping addicts must take a look at these top clothing websites (asap!) and buy knowing you're getting heaps for your money!

Have you brought from any of these stores and what's your ultimate favourite that you just can't get enough of?

## 1. [missguided](https://ui2.awin.com/closedmerchant.php?mid=6882&aid=85386)

Missguided has such flattering and sexy pieces that you will be lusting over their entire website! They have great deals for an affordable price as well as a generous student discount for all those of you studying to grab! Delivery is usually pretty quick and problem-free which is always a bonus, right?

## 2. [Boohoo](http://www.boohoo.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Boohoo is stocked with all your favourite basics - most with a sexy twist so that you're sure to stand out from the crowd! Enjoy their generous deals and discounts which are offered regularly on the website as well as cheap international shipping prices to lighten your load a little bit more!

## 3. [Tobi](https://www.tobi.com/)

Tobi is full of super girly and feminine pieces in mostly light, pastel shades. It's the perfect place to stock up on your summer essentials for the hot days ahead! Grab the best deal around with a massive 50% off your entire order (first time only), so why not add a few extra items to your bag while you're there?

## 4. _brandy melville_

Brandy Melville is packed with affordable basic pieces such as singles, crop tops and simple cardigans. Even better - most of their clothes are between $20 - $50! They also have a wide selection of cool graphics tees and accessores such as bags, hats, jewellery, socks and belts!

## 5. _lulus_

Lulu*s is perfect for anyone who likes basic pieces but with a little bit more sophistication and glam about them! It has a huge selection of both shoes and dresses with tonnes of different styles to choose from. And can you believe their line of amazing accessories start at only $10?

## 6. _Windsor_

Windsor literally have hundreds of gorgeous tops to choose from, the cheapest starting at $3.43! That's crazy cheap, right? They also have cute jewellery pieces for you to finish your order such as rings, earrings, necklaces, watches and bracelets! And while you're there check out their range of shoes!

## 7. [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Forever 21 is your go-to place for basics and essential items! They have so many great deals and discounts for you to steal as well. Many of their tops and dresses are between $10 and $20 so it's easy get heaps of things for a really cheap and affordable price all up! They also have a plus size range to accommodate for voluptuous women.

## 8. [charlotterusse](http://www.charlotterusse.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

When it comes to trendy fashion, this is the place to go. Their dresses and shoes are a sort of specialty here so you know to go for them. The website also has a fantastic plus size section as well as a load of sale merchandise. Everything you find is going to be your new favorite and you won't go broke while you're shopping.

## 9. [gojane](https://goo.gl/0MvQVE)

Your first stop here is the What's New section, which is packed with all the latest trends. Shoes, heels, dresses and tons of other clothing round out the affordable selection of awesome finds to be had here. If you want to save even more money, be sure to check out the sale section too. You won't be sorry!
