# 8 SpringSummer Blazers ...

Blazers are an excellent way to add polish to an outfit. However, come summertime, blazers tend to be a tricky item to incorporate into a wardrobe. Especially if they’re made with heavy winter fabrics. To overcome this, choose blazers made of lightweight fabrics such silks and linens. Take a look at a few spring/summer blazers below.

## 1. Topshop Lilac Co-ordinating Linen Blazer

![Topshop Lilac Co-ordinating Linen Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/ze/leskfm8c54c8fa0e27127065229306_267x377.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zztwYA)
Embrace spring with this lilac coloured blazer. It is made from linen and features a shawl collar and contrast binding. This blazer could be incorporated into both a work and play wardrobe. Pair it with a cotton t-shirt and denim shorts for a casual affair, and tailored pants and a blouse for the office.

## 2. DKNY Stretch Linen Blazer

![DKNY Stretch Linen Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/cm/m79esh1n54c8fa0f073e4833548095_291x392.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jrHZL1)
This stretch linen blazer is classic and understated. It is without a doubt a wardrobe mainstay. This blazer has notched lapels, button-fastening cuffs, and a breast pocket. It is partially lined for comfort. This particular blazer can be purchased with a pair of matching shorts, which is a clear indication that this is designed for warm weather appeal.

## 3. Rachel Comey Club Jacket

![Rachel Comey Club Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/uw/avj3li8t54c8fa0fe73e7423286775_280x400.jpg)

Longer length spring/summer blazers can be a smart outfit option. The relaxed fit of this blazer would work well in a warm weather environment. It features a lapel collar, slightly padded shoulders, and a vent at the back hem. Experiment with different silhouettes when styling this blazer.

## 4. Acne Blacksweet Denim Effect Blazer

![Acne Blacksweet Denim Effect Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/qk/kzkg29ai54c8fa10d9f4c280158079_317x396.jpg)

**Price:** $570.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/iapjh8)
Nothing says spring/summer more than light coloured denim. Denim, however, can be quite a heavy material. Acne brings us the best of both worlds with this cool denim effect blazer. Work a palette of spring colours and pair this blazer with mint or pink pastels.

## 5. Topshop Shrunken 3/4 Sleeve Blazer

![Topshop Shrunken 3/4 Sleeve Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/s0/k9lj2w7954c8fa11d46b9932442560_341x397.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ops4oW)
This blazer would add a touch of tailored sophistication to any outfit. It has a shrunken design and features a single button closure, 3/4 sleeves and two front slit pockets. It comes in a dark rose colour which would work well with a neutral colour palette. This blazer would look great worn with floaty summer dresses in a range of different lengths.

## 6. Uniqlo Premium Linen Long Tailored Jacket

![Uniqlo Premium Linen Long Tailored Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/fx/tw3tv59l54c8fa12d3461466321024_471x391.jpg)

Spring/summer blazers often come in pale, neutral colours. This lightweight white blazer is a simple and sophisticated option for your summer wardrobe. It features a single button closure and two front patch pockets. It could easily be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

## 7. Richard Chai Love Striped Linen Blazer

![Richard Chai Love Striped Linen Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/o8/uk8huv0454c8fa13e2b06555580909_280x383.jpg)

The fabric has a slightly crinkled appearance, which gives it a laidback summer vibe. This blazer would look great paired with slim fitting tailored trousers and a floaty blouse.

## 8. Theyskens’ Theory Jixi Slub Linen and Silk-Blend Blazer
