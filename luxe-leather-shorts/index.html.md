# 7 Luxe Leather Shorts ...

Leather shorts are an edgy take on summer dressing. To keep the look fresh, choose shorts with a looser fit around the thighs. Looser, tailored styles are also more flattering and comfortable to wear. Below are a few luxe leather shorts that you can currently find online.

## 1. Topshop Black Leather Zip High Waisted Shorts

![Topshop Black Leather Zip High Waisted Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/eu/cuz39zvv54c92bce25dd3531005708_300x287.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Je2cMI)
These luxe leather shorts are a cool choice of play wear. They have a button fastening zip fly and feature zipper details at the sides. They seem to be quite fitted so it may be best to go up a size when purchasing. These leather zip shorts would look great paired with an oversized grey knit sweater and a large colourful clutch.

## 2. Kelly Bergin Leather Shorts

![Kelly Bergin Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/so/fonh8mhw54c92bcf060c5576026101_300x250.jpg)

**Price:** $430.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4sxgUH)
These leather shorts have a clean, sleek design. They feature pleats at the front, slant pockets, and a concealed zip and hook fastening at the waist. These shorts also come fully lined. For a polished take on the trend, wear these shorts with a camel coloured silk blouse and patent ballet flats.

## 3. IRO Leather Shorts

![IRO Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/fw/oktv9u2j54c92bcfadf72831457246_244x296.jpg)

**Price:** $408.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Leather shorts help create quite a contemporary look. These hot-pant style shorts have pleating at the front, side pockets, and concealed zip and hook fastening. They also have a small pocket at the back. If these shorts are just a bit too risque, you can always wear opaque tights underneath.

## 4. Topshop Premium Berry Suede a-Line Shorts

![Topshop Premium Berry Suede a-Line Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/hx/ye3ykfyq54c92bd08a32b818447451_282x300.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1TS64f)
These suede leather shorts are great alternative to the more ubiquitous black leather shorts. These shorts come in a rich berry colour and have an A-line silhouette. They feature minimal details and come with a concealed side zip fastening. Wear these with a sleeveless cream blouse, brogues, and a framed handbag for a vintage inspired look.

## 5. Theory Pepin Crinkled-Leather Shorts

![Theory Pepin Crinkled-Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/ms/uct0q0x654c92bd184244332037599_300x280.jpg)

**Price:** $455.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/aMl6hA)
These luxe leather shorts have a casually crinkled finish. They are cut high at the waist with the aim being to create the appearance of lengthened legs. They have side slant pockets, back welt pockets, and a concealed zip fastening at the front. Experiment with contrasting textures and pair these shorts with a sheer blouse and patent accessories.

## 6. Chloe Sevigny for O.C. Cut-off Leather Shorts

![Chloe Sevigny for O.C. Cut-off Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/cl/qrzlpw1454c92bd2695fd797687286_300x212.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These soft leather shorts have a straight cut design and feature classic five pocket styling.

They also have a raw hem and distressed copper hardware. They sit at the hips and are designed for a slim fit. Team these leather shorts with a bold patterned blouse and wedge ankle boots for an edgy 'It girl' look.

## 7. 3.1 Phillip Lim Drawstring Leather Shorts

![3.1 Phillip Lim Drawstring Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wk/yo/xx5dqalg54c92bd34029d806089148_300x230.jpg)

**Price:** $675.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jPKWlK)
These luxe leather shorts have a casual drawstring design. They are made of a grey-green leather which is a great alternative to the more commonly seen black leather shorts. These shorts have gentle pleating at the front, a beige drawstring tie, and slant pockets. Work a sporty look and team these leather shorts with a soft sweater and lace up shoes.
