# 9 Lightweight Spring Cover Ups ...

Top off your outfits this season with chic spring cover ups. A lightweight cover up not only looks great but can help stave off any light breezes or unwanted spring chills. Keep it seasonal by choosing a spring cover up in a statement pattern or a light and bright hue. Take a look at a few lightweight spring cover ups, below.

## 1. Lace Biker Jacket

![Lace Biker Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_lace-biker-jacket_600x737.jpg)

A lace jacket would make a fine addition to your outfits this spring. A lace biker jacket is a mix of delicate and tough, and would look great worn with anything from spring dresses to jeans and a t-shirt. As far as spring cover ups go, this Simone Rocha jacket is extremely expensive at over $1000. Luckily, though, you can find much more affordable versions by Mango and Forever 21.

## 2. Patterned Bomber Jacket

![Patterned Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_patterned-bomber-jacket_600x743.jpg)

Bomber jackets are perfect for working a sport luxe look this spring. Choose one made from a lightweight fabric and in an eye-catching print. Team it with a plain dress and accessories for a street smart look. This jacket is from Topshop and will set you back around $90. Check out Forever 21 and ASOS for similar patterned jackets.

## 3. White Tailored Jacket

![White Tailored Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_white-tailored-jacket_600x688.jpg)

Work a polished look this spring by donning a sleek tailored jacket. For an on-trend look, choose one in a cropped style. This Topshop jacket is pretty pricey at $110 but you can find similar, more affordable versions at ASOS and JC Penney. Team it with a plain tank top, loose printed pants, and patent ballet flats for a smart everyday look.

## 4. Floral Blazer

![Floral Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_floral-blazer_600x657.jpg)

Florals are a spring favourite. Instead of wearing a classic floral dress, try on a floral jacket instead. Depending on the style of jacket you choose, wear it over a pretty dress or team it with something edgier like leather-look pants. This silk floral blazer from By Malene Birger would set you back around $500 but you can find similar styles on a budget at Macy’s and Piperlime.

## 5. Chambray Utility Jacket

![Chambray Utility Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_chambray-utility-jacket_600x732.jpg)

A chambray jacket is a great way of working a cool and casual look this spring. Pop one on over a printed dress or team it with skinny jeans and sneakers for a more street-smart look. This jacket from Forever 21 will only set you back around $30 and features some jewel embellishments on the pockets.

## 6. Polka Dot Blazer

![Polka Dot Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_polka-dot-blazer_600x815.jpg)

Polka dots are still trending. One way to wear them this spring is in the form of a cute polka dot jacket. This polka dot blazer costs $75 from Dorothy Perkins and is a fun way to wear black and white this season. You can find similar, more affordable styles at Urban Outfitters and ASOS.

## 7. Short Knitted Cardigan

![Short Knitted Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_short-knitted-cardigan_600x769.jpg)

Add a cosy element to your spring outfits with a sweet knitted cardigan. This cream cardigan is from Topshop but the sky’s the limit when it comes to colours and cuts this spring – the choice is entirely up to you. Pastel cardigans are great for working a sweet spring look while bold colours are perfect for making a statement. Check out ASOS and Forever 21 for a range of lightweight cardigans for spring.

## 8. Hooded Utility Jacket

![Hooded Utility Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/8_hooded-utility-jacket_600x732.jpg)

A utility jacket is great for working a casual, downtown look. This Forever 21 jacket comes in a light olive colour and will cost you around $35. It would look great teamed with anything from denim shorts to pretty floral dresses this spring.

## 9. Embroidered Kimono

![Embroidered Kimono](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/9_embroidered-kimono_600x900.jpg)

So an embroidered kimono won’t necessarily protect your from chilly weather, but it sure does look fancy. Asian influences are popular with designers right now, and a kimono jacket is one way of working the trend. This embroidered kimono jacket from Topshop is pretty pricey at $150 but you could try Nasty Gal and Modcloth for more affordable buys.

These are just a few lightweight cover ups worth checking out this season. From classic blazers to more eclectic kimonos, there’s something for everyone this spring. Which of these cover ups do you like best?
