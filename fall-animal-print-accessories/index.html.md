# 8 Fall Animal Print Accessories ...

Unleash your natural instincts to stylize fall animal print accessories into your wardrobe this season. Exotic animal skins and safari inspired styles are pieces that will never go out of style leaving you reigning king (or queen) of the high fashion jungle. Wild accents in neutral palettes are fabulous for fall and add a graphic playful pattern to any outfit. While there aren’t any lions, and tigers, and bears, (oh my!) there are definitely these 8 fall animal print accessories for a little magnetic charm this season.

## 1. Calvin Klein Handbag

![Calvin Klein Handbag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_calvin-klein-handbag_320x312.jpg)

**Price:** $248 at [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Take a walk on the wild side in true ladylike fashion this fall with a demure feminine satchel. This everyday accessory is spot on when it comes to mixing just the right amount of print with all black leather. The sheer animal magnetism of this satchel makes for a subtle yet standout statement that will have you reaching for this bag for seasons to come.

## 2. Agent Provocateur Leopard Print Gloves

![Agent Provocateur Leopard Print Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_agent-provocateur-leopard-print-gloves_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $500 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/XWOuJh)
Vivid red adds a serious pop to these leopard print calf-hair gloves. With a nod to old school glamour these retro inspired driving gloves are a cool way to keep warm this season. The eye-catching impact of these gloves makes for a stand-alone statement and should be paired with an outfit that won’t compete for attention.

## 3. Mango Animal Print Calf-Hair Belt

![Mango Animal Print Calf-Hair Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_mango-animal-print-calf-hair-belt_378x72.jpg)

**Price:** $39.99 at [shop.mango.com](https://shop.mango.com/preHome.faces?tduid=30a44eba02f70e27809516a6a91fdacc&ts=1658447677189)
A simple and classic black and white zebra print belt in calf-hair makes the perfect high fashion statement when paired with a monochromatic outfit. Combined with leather, the calf-hair detail adds a richer luxe feel to this understated belt that is ideal for all season long. Whatever the occasion incorporate this zebra print into your outfit for a wild edge.

## 4. Reiss Nelia Snake Print Scarf

![Reiss Nelia Snake Print Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_reiss-nelia-snake-print-scarf_487x558.jpg)

**Price:** $85 at [reiss.com](http://www.reiss.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A large wrap scarf is a definite necessity during the chillier seasons and a snakeskin print one will add some wild charm to your fall wardrobe this season. The luxe length of this scarf makes it flawless when it comes to wrapping, twisting and knotting for your favourite scarf styles. Scarves are great layering tools and handy travelling accessories, which also makes them timeless investment pieces.

## 5. J.Crew Wild Side Wide Bangle

![J.Crew Wild Side Wide Bangle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_j-crew-wild-side-wide-bangle_312x216.jpg)

**Price:** $82 at [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/KJ2DLo)
So cool and super chic is this wild side wide bangle from J.Crew. If you were wondering how else you could incorporate animal print and calf-hair into your look for fall, this is an updated way to sport an animal accent. Dress up your weekend look or off-duty denim with this chic bangle for an instant style pick-me-up.

## 6. Paul Smith Croc Cross Body Bag

![Paul Smith Croc Cross Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_paul-smith-croc-cross-body-bag_600x341.jpg)

**Price:** $388 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/MVrSmm)
If subtle is more your style and you still want to work in an animal print accessory for fall, opt for a crocodile print bag in supple leather. Neutral in color, this Paul Smith bag can be worked into a variety of outfits regardless of personal style. The cross body design allows for a totally utilitarian and functional accessory for a woman on the move.

## 7. La Mer Collections Layered Wrap Watch

_ **Price:** $84 at endless.com_
A wrap watch is the perfect option for the girl who doesn’t like to over accessorize. The lengthy thin straps on this La Mer Collections watch allows for never ending wrapping, giving the illusion of an accessorized wrist while still remaining simple and elegant. The trend factor on this watch is turned up a notch with its silver studded details and snake print finishing.

## 8. Anthony Peto Safari Leopard Ombre Hat

_ **Price:** $121 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Receive your call to the wild loud and clear with this safari inspired leopard hat by Anthony Peto. A penchant for animal accents will have you sporting this hat all season long and with true exotic flair pair this top accessory with an oversized coat for a complete smart look.

Walking on the wild side this season is as simple and effortless as adding some of these 8 fall animal print accessories into your fall wardrobe.
