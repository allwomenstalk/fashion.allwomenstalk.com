# 7 Playful Logo Sweaters to Wear Right Now ...

Statement sweaters have been a big trend for some time and logo sweaters are a must-have right now. Sweaters have been given the ultimate high fashion makeover and these days you’ll find them in a range of on-trend colour palettes and featuring an array of eye-catching logos and prints. From designer numbers to high street buys, there’s no shortage of logo sweaters to choose from. Check out these playful logo sweaters you’ll want to cosy up in.

## 1. Carven Appliquéd Sweater

![Carven Appliquéd Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/qg/o64lgc6r_600x732.jpg)

Designer labels have really embraced logo sweaters lately. This tropics-inspired sweater from Carven would make a sweet addition to any wardrobe. It features a cute banana appliqué with a cursive yellow Carven logo. Keep the quirky tropics-inspired theme going and team this sweater with palm print pants and patent lace-up shoes.

## 2. Christopher Kane Floral Embroidered Sweater

![Christopher Kane Floral Embroidered Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/qf/mynmhlos_600x589.jpg)

This Christopher Kane sweater is a quirky take on floral prints. It features a cross-section of a colourful sequined flower and looks like something you would find in a science textbook. Try dressing up a slouchy sweater like this by teaming it with a fitted pencil skirt and heels.

## 3. Topshop ‘Kiss Me’ Sweater

![Topshop ‘Kiss Me’ Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/ss/w0ykv31b_600x781.jpg)

If you’re not graced with a designer budget (and let’s face it, not many of us are) then this Topshop sweater is an affordable and on-trend option. It comes in a sweet pastel hue and features a Kiss Me slogan and lip motif. It’s cute and playful, and would look great teamed with anything from white tailored shorts to distressed boyfriend jeans.

## 4. Forever 21 Mickey Mouse Sweater

![Forever 21 Mickey Mouse Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/hg/u5o746u9_600x638.jpg)

Mickey Mouse is one of the most easily recognisable Disney icons. This officially licensed sweatshirt from Forever 21 would make a playful addition to any outfit. Wear it with leather-look shorts and sneakers for an athletic-inspired look or style it up with skinny jeans and heeled ankle boots for something more contemporary.

## 5. Markus Lupfer Letter Sequined Sweater

![Markus Lupfer Letter Sequined Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hk/qu/pn5q6ep7_600x729.jpg)

This Markus Lupfer sweater is perfect for adding a preppy touch to your outfit. The relaxed grey sweater is embellished with a bright yellow and navy sequined letter. The grey sweater is a wardrobe staple and the addition of the sequined letter takes it up a style notch or two. Balance out the loose fit of the sweater by wearing it with skinny jeans and fitted skirts.

## 6. Karl Lagerfeld Printed Sweater

![Karl Lagerfeld Printed Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/09/rgbv3z8q_600x727.jpg)

This black and white printed sweater is a subtle nod to iconic designer Karl Lagerfeld. The sweater features the designer’s easily recognisable profile set in ‘K’ shaped stud print. The design is simple but so effective. Work a classic off-duty look by teaming this sweater with dark denim jeans and patent ballet flats.

## 7. Sincerely Jules ‘Right Meow’ Sweater

![Sincerely Jules ‘Right Meow’ Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rs/pv/fety9ye9_336x596.jpg)

Channel your inner cat lady with this playful printed sweater by Sincerely Jules. The sweater comes in a classic crew design and features a fun ‘Meow’ print set in among cat ears and whiskers. Wear this quirky sweater with a collared shirt peeking out from underneath and a flippy skater skirt for a preppy daytime look.

Sweaters reign as the ultimate in casual dressing and look super stylish in seasonal colour palettes and featuring playful logos, motifs, or slogans. A logo sweater is a great way of adding a playful touch to a casual outfit. Which of these logo sweaters do you like best and how would you style them?
