# Tips for Feeling Comfortable and Even Confident in Shorts ...

It’s summertime, which means that it’s officially shorts season. However, if you’re not one for shorts, this can be a tricky time of the year for you. Do you sweat it out the whole summer in a pair of jeans? Avoid that this year with these confidence-boosting tips. They’re sure to make wearing shorts an easy transition for you, no matter why you don’t feel comfortable in shorts. It can feel daunting to wear shorts in the summer, but once you gain the confidence, it will feel like a blip on your radar when you put them on every morning!

## 1. Start by Wearing Them around the House

![Start by Wearing Them around the House](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/ar/m1f1294p5568ca7909ccd224124802_500x667.jpg)

When you’re trying to get comfortable wearing shorts again, start wearing them at home first. If you’re not comfortable wearing them at all, wearing them at home is a great way to introduce them back into your wardrobe and gain some confidence while doing so!

## 2. Take Care of Your Legs

![Take Care of Your Legs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/vz/dgn820605568ca8453fda043037146_468x702.jpg)

One of the most important parts of caring for your legs is to take good care of them. Exfoliate and shave every day, and then be sure to wear lotion. The more time you spend loving and taking care of your legs, the more confident you’ll feel in your shorts!

## 3. Find Your Perfect Pair of Shorts

![Find Your Perfect Pair of Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/s5/y84sytz95568ca8f1d7ac445528775_600x900.jpg)

It’s amazing what the perfect pair of shorts can do for your confidence. If you don’t feel confident wearing shorts, it may just be because you haven’t found that perfect pair of shorts yet. Spend a day going shopping at all of your favorite stores looking for the best pair of shorts for you!

## 4. Try for a Longer Pair of Pants and Transition

![Try for a Longer Pair of Pants and Transition](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/bl/qkj63mn85568ca9de98b6880674347_600x802.jpg)

If you really don’t feel comfortable in shorts in any capacity, whether you’re at home or not, start with longer pants. Wear capris for a week, and then transition into Bermuda shorts, and then transition into your perfect pair of shorts. Transitioning can make the process easier.

## 5. Find the Right Length for You

![Find the Right Length for You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lq/s6/cxawysoq5568caa3acbe6218416357_600x905.jpg)

Everyone is going to have a different “perfect length” when it comes to a pair of shorts. Figure out what yours is, whether you feel comfortable enough in cutoffs, like a few inches of fabric, or you’re a Bermuda shorts girl. Whatever length you choose, you have to feel comfortable in it, and once you find that length, shopping for shorts will become so much easier!

## 6. Start Working out

![Start Working out](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/xn/crx3c1xg5568cab3d68e4351914706_600x900.jpg)

I’m not saying that you need to work out, lose a bunch of weight, or gain a lot of definition to feel comfortable in shorts. However, if you do start working out, you’ll feel physically stronger, and as a result you’ll feel more confident in your shorts, no matter how well defined your legs are.

## 7. Highlight Your Favorite Body Parts

![Highlight Your Favorite Body Parts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e7/xz/r9ke7bsh5568cac16d2f0963124033_533x800.jpg)

Everyone has his or her favorite body parts. If you don’t feel very comfortable in shorts, try wearing something else that accentuates that favorite body part. It’s a quick and easy way to pull attention from your shorts and focus it on something you love about yourself!

How are you trying to gain confidence in shorts? Have you already gained confidence when wearing shorts? How did you do it? Go ahead and offer support and tips to girls who still struggle with it in the comments!
