# 8 Fresh Takes on Classic Wayfarer Sunglasses ...

Wayfarer sunglasses are a classic. They’re an extremely popular style of eyewear that has stood the test of time. You’d be hard pressed to find someone who hasn’t owned at least one pair of wayfarer style sunglasses in their life. While black and tortoiseshell are the most popular kinds of frames, there are more and more fresh takes on the wayfarer popping up. Take a look at a few fresh and stylish wayfarer sunglasses below.

## 1. Models Own Lilac Sunglasses

![Models Own Lilac Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_models-own-lilac-sunglasses_560x249.jpg)

**Price:** $26.09 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Un4at9)

These wayfarer sunglasses have a more rounded shape than the traditional version. They also come in a sweet and on-trend lilac colour. Pastels are still as popular as ever, so work it into your look by popping on a pair of pastel sunglasses like these ones.

## 2. Betsey Johnson Bow Sunglasses

**Price:** $75.00 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

These wayfarer style sunglasses come in a fresh white colour and feature cute rhinestone-adorned bow details at the edges. They also feature a purple bow detail on the tip of one arm. Pop these one with an outfit of a denim shirt dress and strappy sandals for a cool and cute look.

## 3. Mango Wayfarer Sunglasses

**Price:** $31.31 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/St0jb8)

These sunglasses have a retro design and feature transparent frames with stud detailing to the sides. They’re a more oversized style and also have polarised lenses. These would work well to complement a classic outfit of a Breton top and cropped black pants.

## 4. Checkered Wayfarer Sunglasses

**Price:** $5.80 at _forever21.com_

These sunglasses feature a cute blue and white checkered frame. The look is very gingham-inspired and perfect for working the preppy trend. Wear these sunglasses with a white dress for a fresh look.

## 5. Ray-Ban Pattern Wayfarer Sunglasses

![Ray-Ban Pattern Wayfarer Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_ray-ban-pattern-wayfarer-sunglasses_560x278.jpg)

**Price:** $244.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/dF0yq0)

Make more of a statement with these bold patterned sunglasses. They’re made by iconic sunglasses brand Ray-Ban and feature the classic wayfarer styling. The geometric pattern is, however, a fresh take on the classic style.

## 6. Espresso Yourself Sunglasses

![Espresso Yourself Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_espresso-yourself-sunglasses_350x154.jpg)

**Price:** $11.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

These are a more contemporary take on the wayfarer style of sunglasses. They have a bolder design with thicker frames and a matte black finish. Team these with a basic tee and deconstructed denim for an off-duty look.

## 7. Floral Wayfarer Sunglasses

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_

Florals seem to be everywhere these days, and it’s even the case when it comes to sunglasses. These sunglasses come in a traditional wayfarer style and feature contrasting floral prints on the front and sides. Wear them with a fairly minimal outfit to really let them pop.

## 8. Flip-up Wayfarer Sunglasses

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_

These fun wayfarers have a quirky flip-up design. The have clear lenses underneath with shaded flip-up lenses on top. The frames come in a neon yellow finish which is perfect for working this season’s neon trend into your look.

When it comes to wayfarer sunglasses, there are plenty of fresh takes on the classic design. Keep an eye out for fun and funky styles of wayfarers next time you’re purchasing a pair of sunglasses. Which of these fresh wayfarer style sunglasses do you like best?
