# 7 Beautifully Breezy Summer Dresses under 50 to Add to Your Wardrobe ...

Deck your new season wardrobe out with some breezy summer dresses under $50. For summer, there are plenty of trends that you could try out. Floral print dresses are a forever favourite while dresses with cut out and drop waist details make for some on-trend additions. Check out this collection of stylish summer dresses under $50.

## 1. Floaty Floral Dress

![Floaty Floral Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/1_floaty-floral-dress_350x500.jpg)

First up on our list of summer dresses under $50 is this floral number from Modcloth. It features a summery floral print and has floaty draped sleeves. Style it casually with flat sandals and a floppy straw hat or dress it up with heels and a compact clutch. This particular dress will cost you $45 and you can find similar buys at Zara and ASOS.

## 2. Cut out Maxi Dress

![Cut out Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/2_cut-out-maxi-dress_600x732.jpg)

Work a luxe look in a floaty maxi dress this summer. Choose one in a relaxed fit for maximum breeziness. This sea green maxi dress costs $33 from Forever 21 and would be great for wearing to garden parties and dressy outdoor events. Team it with a statement necklace and wedge heels for a stylish summer look. Also check out Topshop and ASOS for more luxe maxi dresses.

## 3. Printed Wrap Dress

![Printed Wrap Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/3_printed-wrap-dress_407x569.jpg)

For a more tailored look, choose a dress with a bit more structure. This Mango dress has a wrap detail top with a structured skirt. It also features a colourful light blue print. Team it with neutral shoes and accessories for a casual daytime look. You can currently grab this dress on sale for $29, down from $49. Also check out Zara for wrap dresses in a range of summery colours.

## 4. Strappy Maxi Dress

![Strappy Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/4_strappy-maxi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Dressing head-to-toe in white is a popular trend this season. An easy way to work this look in the summer is by donning a clean white maxi dress. This strappy maxi dress is from Topshop and will cost you $36. Keep it simple with an outfit of white sandals and maybe a statement necklace or lip colour. If a white dress isn’t your cup of tea Topshop also have this dress in plenty of other colours.

## 5. Drop Waist Lace Dress

![Drop Waist Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/5_drop-waist-lace-dress_600x732.jpg)

Take inspiration from The Great Gatsby and choose a dress with a 1920s twist. This lace dress features a drop waist design and is perfect for channeling your inner Daisy Buchanan. Team it with a long chain necklace and ballet flats or lace-up shoes. Finger-waved hair is optional! This dress will set you back $25 from Forever 21 and you can find similar style dresses at Charlotte Russe.

## 6. Pastel Frill Dress

![Pastel Frill Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/6_pastel-frill-dress_350x500.jpg)

Work a feminine look in pastel and sorbet shades. This light pink dress is ideal for everyday summer dressing. It features a flirty frill detail around the chest and simply slips on. It costs $48 from Modcloth and would look great worn with a neutral bag and studded or embellished sandals. You could also style it up a bit with a white blazer or light denim jacket.

## 7. Gathered Sleeve Dress

![Gathered Sleeve Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/7_gathered-sleeve-dress_600x919.jpg)

Stand out from the crowd in a hot pink dress, like this one from Zara. It has a relaxed design, with floaty short sleeves and a lightly gathered waist. Wear it during the day with neutral accessories or glam it up at night with some statement jewellery and embellished sandals. This statement-making dress will cost you around $40 from Zara.

Stay cool as a cucumber in bright and breezy dresses this season. These are just a few summery dresses that won’t break the bank. Where do you go to buy stylish yet affordable summer dresses?
