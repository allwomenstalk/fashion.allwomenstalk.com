# 8 Most Fabulous Christian Louboutin Shoes ...

Christian Louboutin Shoes are some of the most covetable in the world. With the trademark red sole, they are somewhat of a status symbol. These days, saving up and buying a pair of Christian Louboutin shoes is almost like a rite of passage among the fashion savvy. Christian Louboutin shoes come in a range of different designs, and below are just a few of the more popular styles around.

## 1. Sexy Strass

![Sexy Strass](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/a1/bwg780zc54c9367342f29507004944_400x378.jpg)

**Price:** $3295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wktJfI)
These Christian Louboutin shoes are super expensive, but when something is encrusted with Swarovski crystals you have to expect it to break the budget. These shoes have a classic design with a slim stiletto heel and peep-toe front. The crystal encrusted leather is a stand out feature of the design. These shoes would do well to dress up a plain party dress.

## 2. Lady Peep

_ **Price:** $945.00 at barneys.com_
These stiletto heels just ooze sex appeal. They have a peep-toe design and feature a platform heel. This particular pair comes in a patent-leather finish. The sky high stiletto heels on these shoes give a timeless design a daring twist.

## 3. Very Prive

_ **Price:** $795.00 at barneys.com_
The Very Prive is one of the more iconic Christian Louboutin designs. These shoes come in an on-trend gold metallic finish and feature a concealed platform and 120mm heel. These particular shoes are a glamorous choice and would look great paired with a range of cocktail dresses.

## 4. Creve Coeur

![Creve Coeur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/pv/v8le6ztr54c93675e9839104512866_400x296.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/7MdPkD)
If you’re after Christian Louboutin shoes with a shorter heel, then these leather sandals are a stylish option. They have sleek cut-out design and come in a patent leather finish. These particular shoes would look great paired with a dressed down outfit of jeans and a blouse.

## 5. 8 Mignons

_ **Price:** $1095.00 at barneys.com_
These strappy sandals are perfect for complementing an evening outfit. These black suede shoes feature knotted strap detailing with gold trims and a glitter platform. I could imagine these looking extremely fabulous when worn with a one-shouldered black dress and matching gold accessories.

## 6. Aranea

_ **Price:** $1795.00 at mytheresa.com_
The crystal-embellished ‘spiderweb’ on the front of these shoes is truly spectacular. These shoes have a slim stiletto heel, woven orange Lurex back, and black crystal embellishments to the toe. These shoes are a head-turner and would look great paired with a jewel-toned dress.

## 7. Madame Butterfly

_ **Price:** $945.00 at barneys.com_
The Madame Butterfly pump is a popular choice of shoe. With its satin-covered main, platform pump, and bow detailing, the shoe is both elegant and sexy. Work a feminine silhouette and team these with a full skirt and fitted top.

## 8. Patent New Simple

**Price:** $775.00 at _barneys.com_
These shoes are fabulous in their simplicity. Nude shoes are considered a wardrobe staple, and these Christian Louboutin shoes are indeed an investment piece. They can easily be dressed up or down, and can be worn to work or for a night out.

As you can see, Christian Louboutin shoes are by no means cheap. Quite a bit of saving needs to be done before a purchase (well, for most people anyway!) If you’re thinking of investing in a pair of Louboutins, keep an eye out for end of season sales and stick to classic styles that you can wear year in and year out. Which of these Christian Louboutin shoes take your fancy?
