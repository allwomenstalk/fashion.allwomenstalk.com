# Of Beards and Christmas Baubles!

First, [it was flowers in your beard](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/this-one-is-for-girls-who-love-beards/), and now, it's time for Christmas baubles!

Seems like bauble beards are the newest bizarre trend this holiday season! What started off as London ad agency, Grey's Christmas card to its clients and friends, has now grown to be so much more. After an overwhelming response, the company decided to sell these ornaments to the public and they actually sold out in a matter of days! I bet designers Mike Kennedy and Pauline Ashford didn't see that one coming. It seems like beard adornment is here to stay!

The best part is that all proceeds go to "Beard Season", an Australian charity that's raising awareness about melanoma. Here are some pictures of the trend:
