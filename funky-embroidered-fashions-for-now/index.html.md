# 7 Funky Embroidered Fashions for Now ...

Embroidered fashion is a great way to add some embellishment to your outfit. When thinking of embroidery, however, you may at first think up images of crafts and boho-style peasant blouses. However, there are a range of funky embroidered fashions at the moment that are perfect for working a contemporary look. You may just be surprised at what’s out there. Take a look at a few stylish embroidered fashion pieces below.

## 1. Embroidered Georgette Top

_ **Price:** $32.90 at forever21.com_
This sheer embroidered top is a great way to get on top of the trend. It comes in a lush purple color and features floral embroidery and beaded details. The fabric is quite see-through so it’s probably best to wear a slip or crop top underneath. Work a cool night time look by teaming this embroidered fashion top with a full leather skirt, strappy heels, and a patent clutch.

## 2. Daily Jaunt Shoe

![Daily Jaunt Shoe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_daily-jaunt-shoe_320x184.jpg)

**Price:** $69.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whether you love them or hate them, slipper shoes are still popular choice of shoe right now. These black ones are given a luxe twist in the form of embroidered gold tassels. Work a timeless look by teaming these shoes with cropped trousers and a striped top.

## 3. Embroidered Organza Dress

![Embroidered Organza Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_embroidered-organza-dress_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/rOPRZT)
This embroidered fashion dress has a classically feminine design but comes in a very on-trend lime colour. It also features intricate all-over embroidered detailing. This sleeveless dress has a round neckline, nipped in waist, and full skirt. Wear it in the daytime by teaming it with a faded denim jacket and flats or dress it up at night with heels, drop earrings, and a compact clutch.

## 4. Embroidered Fringe Earrings

**Price:** $5.80 at _forever21.com_
If you’re not sold on embroidered clothing, why not look to jewellery to get your fix of embroidered fashion? These earrings have a bit of an antique look to them and feature embroidered detailing as well as faux pearl and rhinestone accents. Wear them with a cream one-shouldered dress for a chic party look.

## 5. Embroidered Neon Hotpants

![Embroidered Neon Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_embroidered-neon-hotpants_300x270.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/E5F4ZK)
Hit up summer festivals in style with these tribal-inspired shorts. They come in basic black but feature a neon embroidered pattern at the front. These shorts have a high waisted style with pockets at the front and back. Wear them with a loose blouse and ankle boots for a summer festival look.

## 6. Grow Your Own Way Bag

![Grow Your Own Way Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_grow-your-own-way-bag_300x392.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bag is a bit more what you’d typically expect from embroidered fashion. It’s definitely more boho-inspired with its colorful floral embroidery. Work a retro-inspired look by teaming it with high-waisted flares and a basic tank top.

## 7. Floral Embroidered Knit Romper

_ **Price:** $11.83 at forever21.com_
Work a fresh and funky look with this bright pink romper. It has a v-neckline and features a floral embroidered bib at the front. It also has adjustable crossover straps, which are almost a necessity when it comes to rompers. Wear this embroidered fashion as is, or dress it up with a cream blazer and patent shoes.

When it comes to embroidered fashions, you can either go for subtle touches or something more obvious. The choice is entirely up to you. This season, there’s a range of embroidered fashion that leans to the more contemporary side of things. Which of these embroidered looks do you like best?
