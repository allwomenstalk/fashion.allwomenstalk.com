# 9 Playful Pleated Skirts ...

With the warm weather months right around the corner, I’ve started my search for spring-perfect pleated skirts. Not only can they work in almost any setting, but they’re also girly-chic and totally cozy! Whether you’re preparing for a morning at the office or a night on the town with your beau, you can rock one of these nine trendy pleated skirts. So what are you waiting for?

## 1. Gabriella Rocha Hilly Pleated Skirt

![Gabriella Rocha Hilly Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Gabriella-Rocha-Hilly-Pleated-Skirt_480x560.jpg)

Sure, there are tons of pleated skirts on the market, but this pick from Gabriella Rocha is one of my favorites. I own this skirt in red and grey (I simply couldn’t help myself!). Its stretch waist makes it easy to slip on and off and the length is flirty but won’t break office dress code. I like to pair mine with a patterned tank, cardigan sweater, oversize tote, and black wedges for a day in the cubicle. Pick this skirt up now on 6pm for just $14.

## 2. Pleated High-Low Skirt

![Pleated High-Low Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Pleated-High-Low-Skirt-459x560_459x560.jpg)

High-low skirts that flutter in the breeze top my list for spring! Wear this sleek pick with your beau’s white V-neck tee, open-toe flats, and a slouchy hobo bag for a casual lunch date. Top it all off with chunky accessories for a perfectly playful look. Find this skirt now on Forever21 for $15.50.

## 3. See You round Skirt

![See You round Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/See-You-round-Skirt_350x500.jpg)

A waist-cinching A-line skirt is just what a girl needs for a charming first date look. Pair this pretty polka-dotted skirt with a yellow bow blouse and your go-to nude kitten heels. Pick it up (before he comes to pick YOU up) on ModCloth now for $42.99.

## 4. Full Skater Skirt in Leather

![Full Skater Skirt in Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Full-Skater-Skirt-in-Leather-438x560_438x560.jpg)

If you own one too many silky blouses, a daring leather skirt will add some edge to your wardrobe. For a little something extra, throw a set of spiked bracelets and a pair of wedge booties into the mix. You can scoop up this pick now on Asos for $149.09.

## 5. Fierce Feline Skirt

![Fierce Feline Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Fierce-Feline-Skirt-373x560_373x560.jpg)

Rawr! You’ll look and feel fierce in this pleated leopard print skirt. Wear it with black platform pumps, a cutout tank, and a sexy red lip for a night spent on the dance floor. How can the cuties at the club resist the queen of the jungle (Hint: They can’t!)? Buy this piece now on Joa + Closet for $24.

## 6. Wild Rose Pleated Skirt

![Wild Rose Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Wild-Rose-Pleated-Skirt-392x560_392x560.jpg)

Don’t underestimate the power of a good floral print! This mini will take your tired old tees from meh to marvelous. I’d pair it with a solid-colored tee, cream-colored flats, and a neon pink satchel bag. Scoop this skirt up now on ShopRuche for $38.99.

## 7. Beige Pleated Skirt

![Beige Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Beige-Pleated-Skirt-412x560_412x560.jpg)

Scouring the ‘Net for a simple skirt to go with your standout shirts? The search ends here! This simple beige pick will go with almost any patterned top in your closet. Finish off your look with haute wedge heels and oversize shades. Find this skirt now on Dorothy Perkins for $39.

## 8. Pins and Needles Daisy Pleated Skirt

![Pins and Needles Daisy Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/Pins-And-Needles-Daisy-Pleated-Skirt_338x507.jpg)

This fabulous floral-embroidered skirt will get any tights and tank combo ready for date night! Finish off your sexy-but-sweet look with a pair of suede wedges. You can buy this skirt now on Urban Outfitters for $59.

## 9. DKNYC plus Size Pleated Skirt

![DKNYC plus Size Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/DKNYC-plus-Size-Pleated-Skirt_480x560.jpg)

With sizes ranging from 14W to 24W, plus size gals can look and feel beautiful in this bright and bold skirt. Wear it with chunky neon accessories and wedge sandals for a stroll around town. Pick this flouncy skirt up now on 6pm for $34.99.
