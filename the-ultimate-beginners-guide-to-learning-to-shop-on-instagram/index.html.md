# The Ultimate Beginners Guide to Learning to Shop on Instagram ...

For those of you who are getting a wee bit bored of online shopping, no fear! I'm sure you've heard by now that Instagram is *the* place to shop these days! Designers have made it super easy for you to get your hands on some great bargains, all the while enjoying a great customer experience. ‌﻿[refinery29](https://www.refinery29.com/instagram-shops#slide‌)﻿‌﻿ has given us a fab list of pieces you simply must pick up via Insta shopping!

## 1. Vintage Loafers

![footwear, shoe, leg, sneakers, outdoor shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/ue/o1ed329u58ff54d40c072267624009_529x530.jpg)

[@kaleidosvintage](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Ever since I scored these amazing vintage loafers, I haven't turned back. This store is still fairly new, but I'll be checking back religiously for new gems.

## 2. Vintage Shoes

![clothing, costume, spring, dress, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/ht/pdc8yv6y58ff54d44e5e8483553094_536x534.jpg)

[@goodvintagenyc](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This Insta-shop knows a good vintage shoe (or jacket, or dress, or top) when it sees one - meaning the selection is out of this world. What we love most, though, is the pieces are shown on-body, so we can see how they actually fit. And, its offering is just as good as its name promises.

## 3. Unique Accessories

![jeans, denim, clothing, pocket, abdomen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/v0/rmsbwrkk58ff54d470185391020358_474x481.jpg)

[@shopmoonlightmile](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
What we love about this shop is that it's curated and selective. Instead of throwing a million items in your face per day, it takes things a bit slower, making the pieces feel more special. Though it sells all types of things, it's particularly strong for tops, earrings, and unique accessories.

## 4. Colourful Slides

![footwear, shoe, leg, sandal, outdoor shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/pi/yxbs2l6m58ff54d4ab678250733316_575x572.jpg)

[@peopleof2morrow](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
A favorite to shop both on Instagram and IRL, People of 2morrow is a gold mine for vintage and name-brand goodies alike, and the prices can't be beat. Just comment "It's a wrap!" along with your last name to purchase straight from the feed. Come on, Rachel Comey shoes for under $100? Sign us up.

## 5. Basket Bags

![clothing, outerwear, jacket, pattern, costume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/zp/w05mqb8t58ff54d4ded88194825392_512x593.jpg)

[@lisasaysgah](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Aside from selling some of our favorite indie and contemporary brands, S.F.-based Lisa Says Gah also boasts a pretty sick vintage section - and updates its Instagram following with the best sunglasses, basket bags, and more on the regular, with links to shop in the bio.

## 6. Sportswear

![clothing, blue, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l8/dc/ie19o3ga58ff54d52a96d483325181_594x592.jpg)

[@ltrainvintage](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Tap into one of New York City's coolest vintage stores from wherever in the world you are by following its constantly-updated feed. It sells select items on its website, and is pretty good about posting on Instagram every time something hits the store (but, you've got to move quick, because the best pieces never last long).

## 7. Aesthetically Pleasing Objects

_ [@thedrivenewyork](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
For equal parts inspiration and shopping, follow The Drive New York_, which is consistently stocked with an array of aesthetically pleasing clothing and objects, with complimentary imagery to match.

## 8. Open Toe Prints

![footwear, shoe, slipper, leg, flip flops,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mw/qo/jw0d9x7158ff54d770c3a072307658_593x593.jpg)

[@thefrankieshop](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This is one of the best-kept shopping secrets in New York City for fashion girls, and we're practically counting down the days until Frankie gets its goodies online. In the meantime, we're following its feed for perfectly minimal pictures and updates on new arrivals, sales, and more. Plus, you can call and order anything you see on Insta if you're not based in the area.

## 9. Embroidered Tees

![hair, eyewear, white, clothing, glasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/wv/ourgtiao58ff54d77ecbf571003871_476x593.jpg)

[@theacademynewyork](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Embroidered T-shirts, dad hats - what more could you want? Stay up-to-date on this brand's latest drops and get all the grungy photos you need along the way.

## 10. Minimalist Dresses

![human positions, sitting, photograph, woman, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/sk/g3hekz8058ff54d7c1613052782881_595x592.jpg)

[@twoson.co](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
A store founded by the coolest Insta-famous [mom](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser), ever? Talk about a must-follow. It's got an amazing selection of minimalist dresses, tops, shoes, and more, and an online shop and a phone number for easy purchasing.

## 11. Ruffled Pieces

![clothing, dress, pink, gown, sleeve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/i6/b98k73mw58ff54d82f8e6159473125_472x591.jpg)

[@shareendotcom](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
If you're in the market for an amazing vintage dress for a party or event, look no further than Shareen. There's something here for everyone.

## 12. Designer Bodysuits

![clothing, boutique, room, outerwear, shopping,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/s4/pnbup3pk58ff54d83289b818235858_514x596.jpg)

[@procell](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
For a super-curated collection of vintage pieces you won't find anywhere else, you'll want to keep tabs on Procell, a tiny Lower East Side vintage shop with treasures for days. Keep an eye on Insta for some serious gems.

## 13. Frayed Edges

![clothing, blue, sleeve, fashion accessory, textile,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/dr/gl61uxoa58ff54da42093069581794_472x594.jpg)

[@moreislove](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Remember when we told you about all the talent coming out of [Tbilisi, Georgia](https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/2016/05/110583/mercedes-benz-fashion-week-tbilisi-georgia-fashion-designers) (yes, the country)? Shop at this e-shop and keep tabs on all the new labels you should know.

## 14. Casual Jumpsuits

![clothing, coat, trench coat, outerwear, overcoat,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ip/cx/ou9ztaxi58ff54da9abe0383914905_598x592.jpg)

[@of.an.era](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Only the cool kids know about this New York-based thrift shop that stocks duds that look like you'd find them at Opening Ceremony. Just comment with your email and location to claim the piece you want.

## 15. Matching Sets

![clothing, pattern, dress, design, active undergarment,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/he/sih57tgw58ff54dace246388704305_592x596.jpg)

[@ellen123ludlow](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Move over, Reformation, because you can get matching sets like this one and more at this treasure chest of a store. Feel free to call or e-mail to purchase what you see.

## 16. Trendy Handbags

![Trendy Handbags](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/v4/npj8ma9w58ff574a8c38e086476163_597x523.jpg)

[@foxandfawn](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Shop affordable vintage from the comfort of your own couch on Fox & Fawn. Here's a refresher on how to purchase from its feed. Better make moves quick!

## 17. Collared Dresses

![tree, plant, spring, flower,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/jo/h4v7ui5a58ff54db885b5106971120_591x393.jpg)

[@namesakevintage](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Don't get too distracted by Namesake Vintage's perfectly curated Instagram feed that you forget to head to its online shop to actually score the beautiful vintage pieces you see.

## 18. Floral Printed Maxis

![clothing, dress, pink, pattern, design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/ew/aoqrgc1k58ff54dbb8a52611355009_597x597.jpg)

[@dustyrosevintage](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Score floral dresses, vintage tees, and some sick denim pieces via this Brooklyn-based brand's Instagram. Though you can shop items on the store's Etsy shop, for of-the-moment updates, Instagram is where you'll find new arrivals as soon as they come in.

## 19. Designer Jumpers

![pink, clothing, long sleeved t shirt, sleeve, hoodie,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a5/uo/v2api78s58ff54dd315c6837619875_495x496.jpg)

[@shopmarkt](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
We're still upset we didn't make moves on this $22 "Gucci" sweatshirt. Find gems like this (as well as mint-condition duds from brands you know and love). All you have to do to buy is comment with your e-mail address.

## 20. Luxurious Slips

![wedding dress, clothing, dress, bridal clothing, gown,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/pl/b6h7yu2358ff54dd78308658496957_577x595.jpg)

[@shop_passenger](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Minimalists will love this dreamy L.A.-based shop's selection of luxury basics, slip dresses, denim, and more.

## 21. Semi Sheer Dresses

![clothing, dress, lady, fashion, spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w4/g3/lzu7b4ga58ff54ddc4340406161692_596x590.jpg)

[@forartistsonly](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This Australian boutique gets an A+ for styling its product. And if you want everything, you're in luck. You can shop all you see on its website.

## 22. Sneakers and Sportswear

![clothing, sleeve, t shirt, outerwear, pocket,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/gm/frubnjxe58ff54de9161e539397498_597x595.jpg)

[@nakedcph](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Sneakerheads, you'll want to follow this account ASAP. Not only does it boast some major eye candy, but it's our go-to for sneakers that are sold out in the U.S.

## 23. Lace Feature Pieces

![clothing, dress, interior design, textile, pattern,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/r8/xyt3ksw958ff54dedb34b564360419_477x595.jpg)

[@wonder_wander_shop](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Ruffles, lace, and prints, oh my! This Moscow-based luxury Instagram shop has all your needs covered with its worldwide delivery.

## 24. Silky Shirts

![pink, clothing, sleeve, peach, dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yr/wd/ejjmo4nw58ff54dec8c16416489135_425x535.jpg)

[@the_vintage_edit](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This page takes all the best stuff from Instagram's seemingly endless shops and puts it in one place. So if you only want to follow one account, this should be it.

## 25. Turtlenecks and Jeans

![clothing, yellow, denim, electric blue, jeans,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/i8/lvngkt8x58ff54e03abed726560396_425x529.jpg)

[@shoplittlegood](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Vintage Wranglers, silver hoop earrings, and perfectly pink turtlenecks are just a few of the gems you can find on this fairly-new Instagram shop. They've only posted 70 times, and we're already eyeing a solid handful of items.

## 26. Statement Earrings

![hair, face, nose, beauty, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/w1/ufbqsz6258ff54e074ac3545757221_532x532.jpg)

[@persephonevint](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
The on-point styling on this sleek shop seals the deal on all of its lust-worthy finds.

## 27. Bold and Bright Pants

![clothing, pink, red, maroon, magenta,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wb/qf/r3upklof58ff54e0b8085889988245_531x531.jpg)
