# 8 Draped Dresses ...

Finding the right statement dress can take some time and effort. There are a range of different design details to take into account. One design aspect that is both timeless and seemingly effortless is draping. Draping detail on a dress can really enhance its overall appearance. Take a look at a few fashionable draped dresses below.

## 1. Juicy Couture Draped Velour Mini Dress

![Juicy Couture Draped Velour Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/pl/h6laysx954c92b8460a33955617841_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/spm1tk)
This velour mini dress is a youthful interpretation of the draped dresses trend. It features diagonal draping from one shoulder. It has a slash neck, bracelet-length sleeves, and is fully lined. Accessorise this dress with some statement jewellery, opaque tights, and contrasting shoes.

## 2. 3.1 Phillip Lim Printed Drape Front Dress

![3.1 Phillip Lim Printed Drape Front Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/ly/mreusill54c92b855d719431313980_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This striped printed dress is a directional take on daytime dressing. The focal point of this dress is the draped overlay at the front left shoulder. This draping also creates the appearance of an asymmetric hemline. This dress would work well paired with tan shoes and a felt camel-coloured fedora.

## 3. Maje Avoine Draped Wrap-Effect Dress

![Maje Avoine Draped Wrap-Effect Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/to/t6/eq5mgphx54c92b8630bcc820855262_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $280.00a t [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/5TTNfr)
Draped dresses can work well in a day-to-day setting. This Maje wrap-effect dress is stylish and understated. It has an asymmetric cowl neck, cropped sleeves, and pleating throughout the skirt. Pair this dress with neutral accessories for an classic look, or eye-popping reds for something a bit alternative.

## 4. Topshop Limited Edition Draped Side Dress

![Topshop Limited Edition Draped Side Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/g7/tab2dxet54c92b8706273756218434_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $350.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/58vagc)
Draped dresses can come in a range of shapes and designs. This pastel pink dress would be well suited for a cocktail event. It features a bodycon underlay, and has draped detailing along the sides. The voluminous nature of the dress is cinched in by a wide waistband. For a smart look, pair with a white tuxedo blazer and patent heels.

## 5. Lipsy Flower Gem Dress

![Lipsy Flower Gem Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/eg/v8zr8c3j54c92b87c7b23997191110_305x400.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [bankfashion.co.uk](https://goo.gl/KveK3l)
This would make quite the party dress. It features detachable sequin embellishments at the shoulders, cross-over chiffon draping, and an elasticised waist. The soft draping effect of the chiffon contrasts nicely with the figure-hugging silhouette. Pair this dress with complementary metallic accents to really bring the look together.

## 6. Warehouse Cape Back Shirt Dress

![Warehouse Cape Back Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/on/hh/nlhlwtwo54c92b88a2be1055733799_267x370.jpg)

**Price:** $97.00 at [warehouse.co.uk](https://www.warehousefashion.com/womens/dresses/shirt-dresses)
This shirtdress is a great ‘every day’ dress. The navy colour is a wardrobe classic. The dress features draping along the back which creates a ‘caped’ effect. It also features gold-tone buttons down the front and a cinched waist.

## 7. All Saints Aeson Dress

![All Saints Aeson Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f1/vz/ki9eh93i54c92b8999ef2378755543_344x355.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.allsaints.com](https://www.us.allsaints.com/women/dresses/style,any/colour,any/size,any/)
This cotton blend devore dress would make an excellent layering piece. The burn out cotton creates a sheer texture to the material. This dress has a v-neck, features a herringbone tie at the waist, and has draping along the sides. Work a monochromatic black outfit with opaque tights, boots, and leather jacket.

## 8. Alexander Wang Tailored Blouson Dress

![Alexander Wang Tailored Blouson Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zi/gz/a4dqzqpz54c92b8a81fba851772719_280x400.jpg)
