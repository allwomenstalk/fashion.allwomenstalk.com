# 7 Ways to Wear High Waist Denim Shorts like a Diva ...

Summer fashion is all about simple, sexy style, such as the many ways to wear high waist denim shorts this year. Denim shorts can be boring or brilliant, depending on what you wear them with, and how you wear them. To get the cutest outfit possible, while still wearing shorts that flatter your figure, check out these cute ways to wear high waist denim shorts. You’ll feel like a dignified diva the minute you put these ensembles together, plus they’re all wallet-friendly too, which make them even more fabulous in my book!

## 1. With a White Blouse

![With a White Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/bn/i1tcr45a54c939c233508930800196_600x900.jpg)

One of the best ways to wear high waist denim shorts is with a simple white blouse. Collared, cotton varieties are some of the best options. Tuck them in to achieve a sleek, sexy and fashionable look that also looks smart and in style. My favorite kind of a white blouse to wear is a button-down long sleeved, or quarter- length sleeve blouse, made of 100% cotton that also has a prominent collar. When you tuck this in, you’ve got a simple outfit that's also a classic!

## 2. With a Colorful Tank

![With a Colorful Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_with-a-colorful-tank_600x900.jpg)

Maybe you’re looking to dress down with your denim shorts. I’ve got the perfect wardrobe ensemble for you! Pair your high waist denim shorts with a colorful, casual and fitting tank. This adds a nice colorful pop in contrast to the basic denim in the shorts, but also creates a nice balance between the two. Having a colorful top is a great way to draw a person’s eyes upward, which helps to elongate the legs and enhance your figure. A colorful tank is a great way to do that, and you can go as simple as you want, or even pick a dressy tank if you’re going somewhere special.

## 3. Wear the Wedges

![Wear the Wedges](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_wear-the-wedges_600x840.jpg)

Wedges stole my heart the moment I put on my first pair, and these classic shoes are a great accessory to high waist denim shorts. Pair a 2-4 inch pair of wedges on with high waist denim shorts and your legs will instantly achieve length and dimension. High waist shorts automatically create an illusion of longer legs, but when you add a wedge into the mix, you’re creating even more definition and length. I love neutral colored wedges, which go well with any color and don’t cause too much focus on my feet. Wearing a neutral color also helps to enhance the length of your legs, while solid colors like black, or bright colors, can cause you to look stocky instead of long and lean.

## 4. With a Cute Belt

![With a Cute Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/4_with-a-cute-belt_600x770.jpg)

When you put on a pair of high waist denim shorts, throw on a cute little slim belt too. A cute belt is a great way to accessorize high waist denim shorts. High waist denim shorts are usually worn with a shirt tucked in, so wearing a belt is almost a must in my opinion. Pick a neutral color such as tan or beige, or even a vibrant color like tangerine or turquoise! The options are limitless and a girl can never have too many belts or pairs of shoes in my opinion!

## 5. With a High Neckline

![With a High Neckline](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_with-a-high-neckline_500x749.jpg)

Wear your cutest pair of high waist denim shorts with a high neckline if you can. A low neckline plus a high waist can create a short upper body effect, which can make you look stocky or out of proportion. Instead, wear your high waist shorts with a high neckline, which will elongate your upper body and make you look taller and leaner without you even trying. This can be achieved by buttoning up a collared shirt just before the final button, which you can leave unbuttoned. Or, pair it with a cute halter sleeveless style top.

## 6. With a Tunic

![With a Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_with-a-tunic_600x899.jpg)

I adore tunic style shirts. The long, flowing sleeves and airy feel are perfect for summer when we don’t want to wear a tank top or short sleeve shirt. Long tunics also pair excellent with high waist denim shorts. Though you won’t tuck a tunic in, you’ll still be able to see the bottom part of the shorts, which makes your legs look incredibly long and lean. Pair these with some Boho style jewelry and some cute flats or even wedge heels, and you’ve got one sexy, yet classy ensemble everyone will remember. My favorite part is that tunics are also comfortable too, and they need nothing under them except a simple nude tank.

## 7. Add Some Cute Jewelry

![Add Some Cute Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_add-some-cute-jewelry_600x900.jpg)

When wearing high waist denim shorts, be sure you wear some cute jewelry, preferably a longer necklace and accessorizing earrings. Bangle bracelets and classic gold watches are another great accessory to high waist denim shorts. Since the waistline is the focus of the shorts, you’ll want to add balance to the rest of your outfit, which can be achieved through these snazzy little pieces of jewelry. You’ll still look classy, but also look stylish.

High waist shorts are so in right now, and denim is my favorite material of all to wear them in. Do you like the new high waist denim styles? If so, what’s your favorite wear to wear high waist denim shorts?
