# 11 Times Ariana Grande Proved She's a Fashion Queen ...

As Ariana's music evolves, so does her style! The talented performer has gone through a preppy phase, followed by a retro-chic phase, and is now acing the sultry/sophisticated look. Here are 11 times that Ariana Grande proved she's a major fashionista:

## 1. 2014 MTV EMAs

![2014 MTV EMAs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/lx/x2fma14i_600x900.jpg)

Ariana walked the red carpet at the MTV EMAs last weekend wearing a gorgeous navy cut-out gown.

## 2. 2014 MTV EMAs

![2014 MTV EMAs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fo/a4/pu9ogsva_600x900.jpg)

After performing on the EMAs stage, Ariana changed into a black bustier and matching lace skirt. She wore her hair down and completed the look with some black pumps and a colorful clutch!

## 3. London Airport

![London Airport](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/so/nx23svrx_600x900.jpg)

Ariana even keeps it chic at the airport! She was spotted in a white furry coat over an all-black ensemble while arriving at Heathrow airport back in October in London, England.

## 4. 2014 IHeartRadio Music Festival

![2014 IHeartRadio Music Festival](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zx/rq/mvhrnbiu_480x720.jpg)

Ariana hit the stage during the 2014 iHeartRadio Music Festival wearing black sequins and knee-high boots.

## 5. IHeartRadio after Party

![IHeartRadio after Party](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ih/ee/u40x1n4f_600x900.jpg)

Ariana arrived at the 2014 iHeartRadio after party looking stunning in a white lace dress! Seriously, I need it.

## 6. Sirius Xm Studios

![Sirius Xm Studios](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/pv/dawusfyc_480x720.jpg)

Ariana paired a black long-sleeved crop top with some plaid high-waisted shorts at the Morning Mash-Up Broadcast at SiriusXM Studios last summer. She completed the look with black pumps and a cat ear headband!

## 7. 2014 Teen Choice Awards

![2014 Teen Choice Awards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/n5/tvguy9bx_480x720.jpg)

Ariana opted for a white mini with nude pumps at the 2014 Teen Choice Awards.

## 8. Birthday Celebration at DISNEY WORLD

![Birthday Celebration at DISNEY WORLD](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/oi/qt9tez40_480x720.jpg)

Ariana celebrated her 21st birthday at Walt Disney World back in June and wore a gorgeous polka-dot midi skirt paired with a white crop top.

## 9. IHeartradio Music Awards

![IHeartradio Music Awards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/jp/l6fbxatj_480x722.jpg)

Ariana posed with Rihanna backstage at the iHeartRadio Music Awards in L.A. back in May. She wore her hair in her signature pony, an oversized sweater-dress, and some incredible white boots!

## 10. KISS FM's Jingle Ball

![KISS FM's Jingle Ball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mi/f2/sw17ly8j_480x728.jpg)

Ariana looked angelic in all white at KISS FM's Jingle Ball on Dec. 2, 2013!

## 11. American Music Awards

Ariana stunned (and won) at the AMAs last year wearing this gorgeous red sequined gown!
