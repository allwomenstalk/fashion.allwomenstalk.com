# How to Remove Wine Stains from Your Favorite Clothing ...

Don't you hate when you spill wine on your gorgeous clothing? Well, you don't have to worry about stains anymore. [Self](https://www.self.com/story/how-to-remove-wine-stains-on-clothes?mbid=social_twitter) has some tips that'll keep you looking clean, even after an incident:

## 1. Rinse with Cool Water

![Rinse with Cool Water](https://img.allw.mn/content/ao/e6/b0dvnvyl5666f3db7aebe240927312_500x280.gif)

Don't hesitate to run to the bathroom. The quicker you can put cold water on the stain, the better.

## 2. Use a Dishcloth or Towel, Not a Cocktail Napkin

![Use a Dishcloth or Towel, Not a Cocktail Napkin](https://img.allw.mn/content/xs/nb/rz64xe195666f3de65df0876167564_479x209.gif)

You don't want any lint or paper to rub off on you. That's why you should stick to using clean towels to wipe yourself off.

## 3. Blot and Press, but Never Rub

![Blot and Press, but Never Rub](https://img.allw.mn/content/zt/k7/htx3xbl35666f3db8bbac833158633_500x271.gif)

If you rub, it'll either make the stain larger or cause your fabric to rip.

## 4. Or, Wait It out. It’ll All Come out in the Wash

![Or, Wait It out. It’ll All Come out in the Wash](https://img.allw.mn/content/o2/j2/s6eog7525666f3dbd176a369048889_500x272.gif)

If the stain isn't too embarrassing, you can wait to wash it out in the washing machine back at your house. It'll still come out a day later.
