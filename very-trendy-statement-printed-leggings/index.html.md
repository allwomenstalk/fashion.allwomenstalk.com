# 9 Very Trendy Statement Printed Leggings ...

**Printed leggings are sure to** make a statement. It’s all about the graphic print these days, so add an interesting twist to your look with a pair of print leggings. Team them with a longline top or shirt dress, and pair with anything from ankle boots to sandals. For a truly funky look, take a look at the following statement printed leggings.

## 1. Topshop Space Print Leggings

![Topshop Space Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_topshop-space-print-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/iJBSts)
These fun **printed leggings feature an** abstract space print. The colours palette revolves mostly around blue shades, with yellow and purple splashes thrown in for good measure. The ‘stars’ are dotted throughout, but aren’t entirely visible. Team these leggings with a sleeveless white shirtdress and metallic sandals for a fresh and funky look.

## 2. Annika Wilmont Floral Print Leggings

![Annika Wilmont Floral Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_annika-wilmont-floral-print-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $405.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/piiYBD)
These leggings will set you back a pretty penny, but they’re definitely a more sophisticated take on the floral printed leggings. These semi sheer jersey leggings feature a striking digital floral print. The grey, muted colour palette is also quite understated. These leggings would look great with a slouchy oversized knit and black ankle boots.

## 3. Topshop Aztec Print Leggings

![Topshop Aztec Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_topshop-aztec-print-leggings_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/SM57xF)
The Aztec/tribal trend is still going strong. These leggings feature a predominantly black and white print, with splashes of red and yellow throughout. Team these with a denim button-up shirt and strappy sandals for a casual weekend look.

## 4. Dorothy Perkins Purple Rose Print Leggings

![Dorothy Perkins Purple Rose Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_dorothy-perkins-purple-rose-print-leggings_412x560.jpg)

**Price:** $17.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These floral leggings have a certain 90s inspired, grunge vibe to them. They feature a pink and purple rose pattern set against a black background. Keep the grunge vibe going **by teaming these printed leggings** with a loose singlet and lace up ankle boots like Dr Martens.

## 5. Topshop Black Aztec Print Leggings

![Topshop Black Aztec Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_topshop-black-aztec-print-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/W1xkUq)
Stick to a monochrome colour palette by teaming these Aztec print leggings with a black or white shirt dress. Pair them with some simple black ballet pumps and accessorise with silver metallic jewellery.

## 6. Alexander McQueen Lace Print Leggings

![Alexander McQueen Lace Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_alexander-mcqueen-lace-print-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $635.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4BR8ot)
You can’t help but marvel at the sheer brilliance of these leggings. Designers manage to reinvent the lace trend each season, and these lace leggings are a great look. At over $600, however, they’re way out of many of our budgets.

## 7. Topshop Geo Stripe Leggings

![Topshop Geo Stripe Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_topshop-geo-stripe-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LqwSVe)
Geometric prints are a key trend. Here, they appear in the form of a blue and white stripe pattern on these black leggings. Try this season’s sporty chic look and team them with high top sneakers.

## 8. Just Cavalli Snake Print Stretch Leggings

![Just Cavalli Snake Print Stretch Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/8_just-cavalli-snake-print-stretch-leggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $210.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/r697sV)
Animal prints can be a fun way to add some attitude to your outfit. These snake print leggings would make quite the statement. Team them with a slouchy black sweater, metallic sandals, and a messenger bag for a chic down-town look.

## 9. Dorothy Perkings White Floral Print Leggings

![Dorothy Perkings White Floral Print Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/9_dorothy-perkings-white-floral-print-leggings_412x560.jpg)

**Price:** $11.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Big, bold floral prints are where it’s at this season. Work this trend into your look with these affordable multicoloured printed leggings. Incorporate another one of this season’s hottest trends into your look by pairing them with a loose peplum top.

They may no be everyone’s cup of tea, but there’s no doubt that everyone from fashion designers to chain stores are hopping on the printed leggings bandwagon. Abstract space-inspired prints are popular right now, whereas lave an animal prints have a bit more staying power. What do you think of these print leggings?
