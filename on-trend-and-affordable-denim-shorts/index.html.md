# 8 on\-Trend and Affordable Denim Shorts ...

Trendy denim shorts are just one way to work a cool and casual summer look. As far as denim short trends go this season, keep an eye out for coloured or printed denim. Basic blue denim shorts can be given the trendy denim short treatment with the addition of a few embellishments. Take a look at a few stylish and affordable denim shorts, below.

## 1. High Rise Polka Dot Denim Shorts

_ **Price:** $14.80 at forever21.com_
If you haven’t already jumped on the polka dot bandwagon then now is the time to do so. These trendy denim shorts have a high rise design and feature an all-over polka dot pattern. They’re a fun choice for the summertime. Simply slip on a loose singlet and pop on some colourful shoes for an outfit worthy of bicycle riding or ice cream eating!

## 2. Moto Rose High Waist Hotpants

![Moto Rose High Waist Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_moto-rose-high-waist-hotpants_600x552.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/kvGk34)
These coated denim shorts come in a beautiful rose colour. They have a high waisted design with authentic trims. Work a retro inspired look by teaming these shorts with a sleeveless collared shirt, tied at the waist, and white canvas sneakers.

## 3. Colored Denim Shorts W/ Belt

![Colored Denim Shorts W/ Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_colored-denim-shorts-w-belt_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $17.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These trendy denim shorts are a sleek and stylish option. They are available in red, khaki, and olive shades and come with a braided leather-look belt. For a more polished and sophisticated look, pair these shorts with a silky blouse, patent flats, and structured handbag.

## 4. Floral to Ceiling Fabulous Shorts

![Floral to Ceiling Fabulous Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_floral-to-ceiling-fabulous-shorts_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Floral printed denim is perfect for wearing during the summer months! These denim shorts come in black and feature an orange and pink floral pattern. Wear them with similar orange or pink tops and with neutral Oxford shoes for a preppy summertime look.

## 5. Moto Neon Hotpants

![Moto Neon Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_moto-neon-hotpants_600x470.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IZUnrg)
If you’re like me and have been loving all things neon lately, then you might want to check out these trendy denim shorts. They come in a neon orange colour and have five pocket detailing and frayed hems. The relaxed style of these shorts makes them perfect for wearing at the beach.

## 6. Moto Washed Stripe Hotpants

![Moto Washed Stripe Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_moto-washed-stripe-hotpants_600x586.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/6OwC5k)
Dare to be different in these trendy denim shorts. They have a high waisted design with authentic trims and cuffed hems. Work a monochrome look by teaming these shorts with a black top, draped jacket, and ankle boots.

## 7. Tie-Dye Denim Cutoffs

**Price:** $19.80 at _forever21.com_
Add a splash of colour to your look with these tie-dye denim shorts. They are available in coral or turquoise colours and unlike the typically psychedelic look of tie-dye, these have a much more street smart design. Pair these shorts with a chambray shirt, wayfarer sunglasses, and strappy sandals for a cool off-duty look.

## 8. Moto Bleach Crochet Hotpants

![Moto Bleach Crochet Hotpants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_moto-bleach-crochet-hotpants_600x556.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zYmDzA)
The crochet panel one these faded shorts seemingly breathes new life into a humble wardrobe staple. In addition to the white crochet panel, these trendy denim shorts feature distressed detailing and frayed trims. Team them with a pastel top and neutral shoes for a relaxed summer outfit.

As far as denim short trends go, there’s something for everyone. Whether you prefer neon colours or polka dot patterns, you can’t deny that these trendy denim shorts would make a fun addition to your summer wardrobe. Which of these styles of denim shorts do you prefer?
