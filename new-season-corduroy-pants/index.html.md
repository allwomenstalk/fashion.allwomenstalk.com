# 7 New Season Corduroy Pants ...

Corduroy Pants are making a comeback. Take a look at any new season designer collection or in any high street store and you’ll notice that corduroy is creeping back onto the shelves. Replace your denim jeans this fall with some cool corduroy pants. Rust or tan colours are fitting for fall, while coloured corduroy pants are also a fashionable option. Take a look at some of this season’s newest corduroy pants below.

They have a mid-rise design and feature classic five pocket detailing. Wear these jeans with a patterned sweater and patent ballet flats for a chic transeasonal look.

## 2. Stella McCartney 7/8 Corduroy Pants

_ **Price:** $315.00 at mytheresa.com_
Bright hues are popular this season. Create a wonderfully colourful outfit in a pair of corduroy pants – it’s an easy way to incorporate two of this season’s hottest trends. These brick red pants have a 7/8 design and feature two front and two back pockets. Wear them with an equally colourful top to create a chic colour-blocked outfit.

## 5. Current/Elliott the Boyfriend Corduroy Jeans

## 7. MIH Jeans Tokyo Mid-Rise Corduroy Jeans

![MIH Jeans Tokyo Mid-Rise Corduroy Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n4/te/b14muws654c9318708b3d535583668_267x400.jpg)

There are corduroy pants to suit all occasions. Baggier boyfriend styles are perfect for creating a casual weekend look, while skinnier jeans create a sleeker silhouette. Corduroy pants come in a range of colours and are a great alternative to the humble jeans. Will you be wearing corduroy pants this season?
