# How to Create a Wardrobe That Makes You Feel Amazing ...

When it comes to confidence, self-worth, and just feeling good about yourself in general, there are a lot of factors involved in creating a happy and content person. From your love life to your career to your social life and relationship with your family, everything should ideally be as harmonious and pleasant as possible. Something that can play a part in all of those different areas is your wardrobe! If you don’t think you that you look good, then you aren’t going to feel good, it can be as simple as that! Here is **how to create a wardrobe that makes you feel amazing**.

## 1. Get in Shape

![shoulder, fashion model, joint, textile, material,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d3/au/p0v1aj675b8ecaf44f090346712134_600x750.jpg)

Let’s get real right off the bat. Clothes can only go so far in helping you to look and feel great. If you are working with a healthy and fit body as your base, then the clothes that you end up choosing are always going to sit and look better. Not only does a healthy body raise self confidence, but it also put you in a mindset to make more adventurous and interesting choices with your fashion. You got something to show off, so make sure you pick the correct clothes to do it!

## 2. Bold Colours

![gown, dress, shoulder, lady, photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/az/iv2ybils5b8ecb2303cd2274474759_600x750.jpg)

One of the easiest ways to achieve a ‘power’ look is to opt for pieces that present big, bold, solid colours. Patterns are fun, but they aren’t necessarily powerful, so if you are looking to build a wardrobe that gives you confidence and evokes strength, then solid, bold colours are definitely the way to go.

## 3. Sense of Fun

![girl, winter, tree, recreation, fun,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/jn/j9zfekx65b8ecbd6aee16279325388_600x750.jpg)

If you are having a good time in life, then you are a happier person, and this mentality should filter down to your clothing choices. When you aren’t going for the powerful, head bitch in charge look, you should be trying to express yourself in more fun and interesting ways. Don’t be afraid to do things like combine problem patterns and rock unconventional lengths in order to stand out and feel free.

## 4. Be Creative

![dress, fashion model, gown, girl, peach,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/kw/w6fy2d275b8ecc84c56d7435872630_600x750.jpg)

Try not to just be one of those girls who looks to the mainstream magazines and websites to find your fashion inspiration. Be deliberate in going against the grain and not just buying things because someone has told you do. Let your creative side out and go for garments that speak to you on a personal level, because those are the pieces of clothing that are going to bring you the most joy.
