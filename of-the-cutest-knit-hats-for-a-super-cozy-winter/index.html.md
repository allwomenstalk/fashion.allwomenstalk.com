# 7 of the Cutest Knit Hats for a Super\-cozy Winter ...

Nothing says super-cozy more than gorgeous knit hats. Whether you opt for a chic beret or slouchy beanie, they’ll keep you warm and looking stylish all winter long! I have quite a collection of hats that I love wearing when the weather gets cold outside, and don’t forget that knit hats are a great way to cover a bad hair day – there’s no need for any styling! Here are a few of the prettiest hats around this season that are sure to keep you warm and snuggly no matter how cold it gets outside.

## 1. Beignet or Nay Hat in Grey

![Beignet or Nay Hat in Grey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30083902_3578_349x491.jpg)

This cozy, chic knit hat from Modcloth channels a little Parisian style into your everyday look with its chunky knit and subtle grey color. Knit hats in a slouchy style are a popular choice this season, and you can easily dress this hat up with a smart retro coat and long boots or go for a more casual look with a toggle coat and ankle boots. Whichever look you choose, you’ll be rockin’ a cozy, stylish look all winter long in this cute hat!

## 2. Purr Kitty Beanie

![Purr Kitty Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30083902_1096_319x297.jpg)

Accessorize have some really great hats in stock every winter, whether you’re looking for a cute cloche, slouchy beanie or stylish beret. This super-cute kitty beanie has little ears and a ‘Purr’ logo – great for cat lovers everywhere! It’s nude pale pink coloring means it looks great with any outfit and is ideal for teaming with a cozy jumper dress, tights and boots for an adorable look. I love it!

## 3. Asymmetric Cloche Bow Hat

![Asymmetric Cloche Bow Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30083902_0881_319x300.jpg)

Love them or hate them, cloche hats add a little ‘je ne sais quoi’ to any look and are perfect teamed with a ladylike, vintage coat for the autumn and winter months. This bright red cloche from Accessorize would look stunning with a grey or black coat and is a bold choice for hat lovers. It’s not technically a knit hat, but I couldn’t resist including it as it’s just so adorable!

## 4. Stage Door Hat in Sage

![Stage Door Hat in Sage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30083902_7583_349x483.jpg)

ModCloth’s range of hats is beautiful this season and I love the Stage Door Hat in sage green. It’s a muted green that would look great on any skintone, but particularly stunning on redheads. This knit hat features a side detail flap with vintage wooden button, which is a cute touch and helps the hat to stand out. For something a bit different to black and grey winter hats, this hat is just the ticket!

## 5. Wool Beret with Corsage

![Wool Beret with Corsage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06221025_2641_300x300.jpg)

This super-chic wool beret from Accessorize is a classic style that will never go out of fashion, and it’s available in black too, so it will go with anything and everything! The cream wool flowers are a beautiful touch and help to make this hat a bit more unusual than your average beret. It’s details like this which make this season’s knit hats extra special!

## 6. French Immersion Hat

![French Immersion Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/30083902_9756_350x500.jpg)

I’m still not sure if I would be brave enough to wear this ModCloth hat myself – its bold leopard print definitely isn’t for the shy and retiring! It looks so stunning on the website that you can almost imagine yourself drinking coffee in a chic European pavement café with your wool coat and ladylike leather gloves. It’s gorgeous!

## 7. Angora Corsage Baggy Beanie

![Angora Corsage Baggy Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/06221045_7302_299x291.jpg)

Super-soft Angora makes for a cozy and comfortable hat that will go with everything you own, and this pretty baggy beanie from Accessorize features a corsage detail that will add a feminine touch to any outfit! Its nude coloring makes it the perfect choice for autumn and winter and it will keep you warm in style.

Just writing about these super-cute hats has made me want to go on a shopping spree! I love having an array of hats for cold wintry days, and they’re a great way to avoid bad hair days or ‘can’t be bothered styling my hair’ days. What are your favorite types of hats – are you a cloche girl, slouchy beanie lady or beret chick?
