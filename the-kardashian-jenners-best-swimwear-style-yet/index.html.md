# The Kardashian\-Jenner's Best Swimwear Style Yet ...

Everyone knows that the Kardashians and Jenners have great style no matter what they’re wearing, but when it comes to swimwear, they really know what’s up. Growing up and living in California, if anyone knows swimwear, it’s going to be the Kardashian family. If you’re looking for some bikini inspiration from your favorite famous family, look no further than the Kardashian-Jenner clan!

## 1. Kendall’s Orange Swimwear

![Kendall’s Orange Swimwear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yz/u0/twec3bax55b8e41487a92559669196_600x600.jpg)

Kendall recently took Instagram by storm when she posted a picture of herself in a gorgeous orange bikini. It’s hard to pull off orange, especially when your hair is as dark as Kendall’s, and she wears it so well she’s making the world covet orange swimsuits. I especially love the orange reflective wayfarers she’s wearing as well!

## 2. Kylie’s One Piece

![Kylie’s One Piece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/jw/jaofxkae55b8e415aaae7297170638_600x900.jpg)

Leave it to Kylie to rock a one-piece better than anyone else. A few weeks ago, back when her hair was still that gorgeous ice blue shade that almost broke the Internet, she posted a selfie of herself wearing a one-piece that had faux rips down the front and the sides. She looked so amazing in it that other stars have been seen wearing it since!

## 3. Caitlyn’s Swimsuit Debut

![Caitlyn’s Swimsuit Debut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/um/kg/ypahxae255b8e414afa00949046053_600x361.jpg)

While we’ve seen Caitlyn Jenner in some skimpy pieces since her Vanity Fair debut, there was one clip in an I Am Cait teaser that really impressed me, and that was when she was wearing a swimsuit for a pool day with her friends. She wore a one-shoulder one-piece that looked so amazing on her. I personally thought she looked twenty years younger in that outfit!

## 4. Khloe on the Cover of Complex

![Khloe on the Cover of Complex](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/l7/ib1woz4k55bc3db7cffbf033829376_500x500.jpg)

Sure, her sister Kim is the one who’s usually known for her ability to break the Internet, but Khloe came pretty close herself when photographs from her Complex Magazine shoot were released a few days ago. On the cover, she’s wearing a bathing suit that could only be described as iconic, and it’s one that I’m sure will become a classic piece.

## 5. Kim’s White and Gold One-Piece

![Kim’s White and Gold One-Piece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/gs/twi9vv2h55bc3d6c1f026621065073_470x705.jpg)

This white and gold one-piece was the original white and gold piece that drew the eyes of millions. This is one of the rare one-pieces that I think is simple yet so chic and glamorous. Kim rocks this swimsuit perfectly, if only we could all make one-pieces look this good!

## 6. Kendall’s Grecian Glow

![Kendall’s Grecian Glow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/bp/ed8kkgix55b8e41567883357213567_513x640.jpg)

When the Kardashian-Jenner clan got on a plane to Greece, fans waited with baited breath for their amazing Mediterranean-inspired beachwear. They didn’t disappoint, especially Kendall, who wore this absolutely stunning outfit on a day in Greece. She paired blue and white bottoms with a white peasant top for a look that looks like it came right of Mamma Mia!

## 7. Kourtney’s Stripes and Cat Eye Sunglasses

![Kourtney’s Stripes and Cat Eye Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rh/zs/dyrk0wo255b8e415df2e1402320637_236x314.jpg)

Kourtney created an entire fashion trend when she wore those sunglasses for the first time on the Kardashian-Jenner family trip to Bora Bora. Suddenly everyone was on the hunt for dramatic cat-eye sunglasses like the ones she wore on their trip. Although the sunglasses were the focal point of most of her outfits, the striped suit she wore here looked so great and classic when paired against the sunglasses!

What is your favorite Kardashian-Jenner beach look? Let me know whose beach style you’ll be emulating on the beach this summer in the comments!
