# 30 Iconic Male Models Who Are Redefining the Fashion Industry

A little eye candy never hurt anybody, and in the world of high fashion, these men are the absolute gold standard. Whether they are fronting major campaigns or dominating the runway, these icons have redefined what it means to be a top male model. Here are 30 incredibly good-looking men to brighten your day and perhaps inspire your next style obsession. Happy gawking!

## 1. David Gandy

![David Gandy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wm/uu/ic86miao55194a03b85e5928043133_600x798.jpg)

David Gandy is the epitome of the classic male supermodel. Best known for his long-standing partnership with [Dolce & Gabbana](https://www.dolcegabbana.com/), particularly the iconic "Light Blue" fragrance ads, he remains a powerhouse in the industry.

## 2. André Hamann

![André Hamann](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/mo/t5qt51dh55194a07c6fd1695360100_600x856.jpg)

With his signature tattoos and rugged charm, André Hamann has captured hearts worldwide. He's not just a model; he's also a successful entrepreneur with his own jewelry line.

## 3. Matthew Holt

![Matthew Holt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mh/bh/yp4aufvl55194a03c19bd685583267_400x600.jpg)

Matthew Holt's sharp features have made him a favorite for high-fashion editorials. He often brings a sophisticated edge to every shoot he participates in.

## 4. Nathan Owens

![Nathan Owens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m9/tz/zoqhktb155194a045ed04693191442_540x720.jpg)

Nathan Owens transitioned from a top-tier modeling career to acting, proving that he has both the looks and the talent to succeed in multiple arenas.

## 5. Ben Dahlhaus

![Ben Dahlhaus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k1/m4/y13dxs0455194a04c373e138519374_600x886.jpg)

Known as the "Brad Pitt of Germany," Ben Dahlhaus is famous for his melancholic gaze and incredibly lush hair, making him a standout in the fashion world.

## 6. Broderick Hunter

![Broderick Hunter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/bz/y704p20a55194a07db69c788609443_600x813.jpg)

Broderick Hunter is celebrated for his striking skin tone and athletic build. He is a prominent voice for diversity in the modeling industry.

## 7. Piero Mendez

![Piero Mendez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/a4/i7btcoz555194a05a1d40968443294_499x744.jpg)

Piero Mendez's unique look and curly hair helped him land major shows for brands like Prada, solidifying his status as a rising star.

## 8. Scott Eastwood

![Scott Eastwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/s2/jtv3t5yy55194a08125b4068194742_600x900.jpg)

While he's now a major Hollywood actor, Scott Eastwood's modeling roots are undeniable. He carries the classic ruggedness of his father with a modern twist.

## 9. Chad White

![Chad White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/n0/qe6bu6q555194a07896e8831708000_600x903.jpg)

Chad White is a veteran in the industry, known for his versatility and ability to look equally good in high fashion and commercial campaigns.

## 10. Jon Kortajarena

![Jon Kortajarena](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/as/wr/hwqe6r1r55194a076bbd7735006136_400x600.jpg)

Spanish sensation Jon Kortajarena is a true icon. His chiseled jawline and piercing eyes have made him a favorite for designers like Tom Ford. ## 11. Sang Woo Kim

![Sang Woo Kim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/l3/cu7nign255194a08eda74022828470_498x750.jpg)

Sang Woo Kim made history as one of the first Korean models to achieve global stardom, appearing in major campaigns for [Burberry](https://www.burberry.com/).

## 12. Johannes Huebl

![Johannes Huebl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/ta/z11kntb055194a096d50d053804987_500x764.jpg)

Johannes Huebl is the definition of a gentleman. His impeccable personal style and modeling career have made him a staple in the fashion world.

## 13. Connor Hill

![Connor Hill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/jv/mqxsi9zx55194a09d9573351321720_600x750.jpg)

Connor Hill's boyish charm and sharp features have helped him navigate both the commercial and high-fashion markets with ease.

## 14. Marlon Teixeira

![Marlon Teixeira](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/pf/qrk931w155194a0c72dec268076157_600x719.jpg)

Brazilian model Marlon Teixeira is known for his rugged, beachy look and has worked with some of the biggest names in the industry.

## 15. Benjamin Eidem

![BENJAMIN EIDEM](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ua/yct5coj755194a0aaa40f799486738_600x972.jpg)

Benjamin Eidem's classic Scandinavian looks have made him a favorite for brands like [Prada](https://www.prada.com/) and Louis Vuitton.

## 16. Graham Scaife

![Graham Scaife](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vk/h6/nc2cz3ms55194a0a71509503588569_500x700.jpg)

Graham Scaife's versatility as a model allows him to seamlessly transition between different styles and aesthetics, making him highly sought after.

## 17. Jon Kortajarena (Again!)

![Jon Kortajarena](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/ux/erh3wrun55194a0bea688277210858_600x900.jpg)

Because one mention simply isn't enough, Jon Kortajarena continues to dominate the scene with his timeless appeal and charismatic presence.

## 18. Rob Evans

![Rob Evans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wk/t0/mljk087755194a0c17587489777355_500x750.jpg)

Rob Evans, known for his work on *America's Next Top Model*, has an incredible physique and a powerful presence on and off the runway.

## 19. Matthew Terry

![Matthew Terry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/ff/xqxnmoii55194a0e3c523879382609_600x899.jpg)

Matthew Terry became an overnight sensation as the face of [Calvin Klein](https://www.calvinklein.com/), showcasing an athletic build that redefined the brand's aesthetic.

## 20. Nick Bateman

![Nick Bateman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/l9/s8odirhu55194a0c80857929904562_470x827.jpg)

Nick Bateman combines his martial arts background with a successful modeling and acting career, garnering a massive social media following in the process.

## 21. Sean O'Pry

![Sean O'pry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d5/nu/cy2r00fl55194a0e1a8ac250541369_600x752.jpg)

Sean O'Pry has consistently been one of the highest-earning male models in the world, known for his sharp jawline and piercing blue eyes.

## 22. Simon Nessman

![Simon Nessman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/y5/j8qdqrsw55194a1002003031279645_600x816.jpg)

Simon Nessman's youthful energy and curly hair have made him a favorite for major designers like Giorgio Armani.

## 23. Ronald Epps

![Ronald Epps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w8/lj/okcy1thz55194a1032771082195774_600x900.jpg)

Ronald Epps is known for his athletic physique and has worked with a variety of brands ranging from high fashion to commercial sportswear.

## 24. Silviu Tolu

![Silviu Tolu](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/ce/ash2k0vl55194a0f9f1bc323880072_595x842.jpg)

Silviu Tolu's unique look and artistic background bring a fresh perspective to his modeling work, making him a standout choice for creative campaigns.

## 25. David Alexander Flinn

![David Alexander Flinn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v5/kp/grgmc99f55194a0fcb56b766272867_517x775.jpg)

With his striking tattoos and edgy look, David Alexander Flinn has become a favorite for brands looking for a more alternative aesthetic.

## 26. Hao Yun Xiang

![Hao Yun Xiang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/oi/rw34agoz55194a0fd4e39574187439_500x667.jpg)

Hao Yun Xiang is one of China's most successful male models, known for his elegant features and strong runway presence.

## 27. Henrik Fallenius

![Henrik Fallenius](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lu/gi/flvqwsc655194a11e4fc0571662892_600x887.jpg)

Henrik Fallenius brings a sophisticated, European charm to his work, often appearing in high-end fashion editorials and campaigns.

## 28. Ryan Barrett

![Ryan Barrett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/y1/phxkus6y55194b6ec7152710853316_600x903.jpg)

Ryan Barrett's rugged good looks and versatility have made him a mainstay in the modeling world for years.

## 29. Conrad Bromfield

![Conrad Bromfield](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/n1/zpk9n8sg55194a125d682792203857_600x900.jpg)

Conrad Bromfield's striking features and confident energy have helped him land major campaigns and runway spots worldwide.

## 30. Matt Trethe

![Matt Trethe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/br/m469453055194a13186cb005214033_500x650.jpg)

Closing out our list is Matt Trethe, whose classic good looks and professional demeanor make him a favorite for brands across the board.
