# AmfAR LA Inspiration Gala 2014: Best Dressed Celebs ...

At the 2014 amfAR LA Inspiration Gala, Tom Ford ruled the night. Inside the incredible event, Gwyneth Paltrow hosted, Justin Timberlake and Coldplay put on amazing performances, and stars like Miley Cyrus and Rihanna honored the American designer with moving speeches. Some of those stars decided the best way to honor Tom Ford was by wearing his creations. Check out the best dressed:

## 1. Lea Michele

![Lea Michele](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/ge/c56qys5p_600x920.jpg)

Lea's sequined nude gown definitely turned heads!
