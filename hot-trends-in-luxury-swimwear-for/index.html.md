# 9 Hot Trends in Luxury Swimwear for 2012 ...

Now that we've shown you great trends for flip-flops we want to show you what's hot for **luxury swimwear** in 2012. There are bright colors, fun prints, and cool designs that everyone will love for this summer. Today we have [Aqua Di Lara](http://www.aquadilara.com/)showing us some of their favorites, here are **9 Hot Trends in Luxury Swimwear for 2012**...
Luxury Swimwear Trends For Everyone...

## 1. Monokinis...

![Monokinis...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/Monokinis_480x720.jpg)

Although these sexy styles are not a new trend they are a trend that is remaining strong. The monokini shows no sign of slowing down. Bringing back the glamorous 70s aesthetic, cutout pieces are a symbol of sleek, classic style. Monokinis look great and can create hour glass shapes and are an original twist to the conventional bikini. Monokini cutouts are one piece swimsuits as well as bikinis, a sort of fusion of both styles. [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) has designer styles that will make heads swing, such as this bestseller Diamante.

## 2. Intricately Detailed One Pieces...

![Intricately Detailed One Pieces...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/onepiece_481x720.jpg)

Perfect for the fashion forward sun lover, detailed one pieces make you stand out from the crowd. You will value the intricate details, chic accessories, and overall beautiful craftsmanship which add elegance and charm to such suits. [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) has one of a kind pieces that are stunningly detailed and breathtaking like this net suit Padma which is available for 2012.

## 3. Fringes and Frills...

![Fringes and Frills...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/Anita-top-Qiss-Qiss_484x727.jpg)

From frills to fringe, this season it's all about the accents on your bathing suits. Look for fringe, bows, wooden rings and accouterments, ruffles and other embellishments! [Qiss Qiss](https://www.facebook.com/QissQiss?sk=info) does it here with this beautiful frilled Anita top!

## 4. Hard Large Accessories...

![Hard Large Accessories...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/accessories_480x720.jpg)

Embellishments for this year invoke pure glamour; in term of hardware gold is the standard for 2012 swimwear. Hard large accessories custom built right into the swimsuit make a great statement. Pieces include a simple O-shaped ring between the triangles of a bikini top, elaborately woven pieces on both sides of a bikini bottom or as seen in this [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) suit a beautiful gold Anchor piece dead center of the bikini top.

## 5. Flowing and Sheer Coverups...

![Flowing and Sheer Coverups...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/coverup_478x706.jpg)

Sheer, sometimes gauzy, coverups take to the beach in a big way for 2012. They come in all typical coverup styles, including the big shirt, skirt, maxi, and short dress. These gossamer goodies are fabulous for just a touch of sassy cover at the beach or poolside. Make a bold statement in this sheer and flowy cover up by [Aqua Di Lara.](https://www.facebook.com/AquaDiLara?ref=tn_tnmn)

## 6. Serpent Skin Prints...

![Serpent Skin Prints...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/suit_484x375.jpg)

Slither into one of this season’s hottest trends: multi-colored animal print. Animal print swimsuits have a tribal, jungle flair that is sexy and fashionable. Although animal-inspired prints occur every year in swimwear, this year we’re seeing a new take on the trend. The “new” animal prints are done in youthful, often retro styling, such as the [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) Valencia Thyra suite shown here as well as the Vipers Qiss bikini from [Qiss Qiss.](https://www.facebook.com/QissQiss)

## 7. Asymmetrical Silhouettes...

![Asymmetrical Silhouettes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/swimsuit_480x720.jpg)

Bold designs, block patterns and directional cuts asymmetrical offerings will make you a walking work of art. Check out this showstopper in the [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) 2012 Miami runway show.

## 8. High-Waisted Bikinis/Monokinis...

![High-Waisted Bikinis/Monokinis...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/12/highwaist_480x720.jpg)

Retro chic high waisted bikinis are a hot summer bathing suit trend. They have become all the rage in the last couple of years with the comeback of pinup flair. High waisted bikini bottoms have now become a staple of fashion when it comes to beachwear. It has the runway appeal that the trendy shopper craves, plus the extra coverage that our contemporary customer appreciates. [Aqua Di Lara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) nailed the trend here with their Opelle Izaro Swimsuit.

## 9. Ruched Bikini Bums...

A new trend in the swimsuit world, having a ruched bikini bottom makes your bum look cute and compact. The bikini bottom is gathered at the rear to lift and accentuate the curves of the cheeks. It highlights while hiding flaws and just enhances zee curves.
