# Whats  the Deal with Leggings ?!

**What's the deal with leggings?** Yes, we love them. They are comfortable. They fit nice and tight. They keep our legs warm. They are great for workouts. They look cute.

But what is the big hype about whether it is okay for a woman to wear leggings or not?

Well, it’s clear, since they hug so tight, they show every detail. That’s right. Everything is on display when you wear a pair of tight leggings. Here’s the deal, though. Leggings don’t make the woman. So **what's the deal with leggings?**

Leggings have become the “in” thing. It's entirely normal that a woman has several pairs of leggings in her closet.

Women are expected to wear leggings instead of sweatpants.
Leggings don’t make the woman. No. They may be what we are expected to wear, but that doesn’t mean that we can’t be a woman if we wear sweatpants when we want. We are women.

Those leggings look good on us. Let’s admit it. With a cute shirt and some polka dot leggings, we are a sight for sore eyes.

Don’t ever forget that no matter how cute you look, how beautiful they make you feel, or how much you think you should wear them, leggings are leggings. They don't define you.
