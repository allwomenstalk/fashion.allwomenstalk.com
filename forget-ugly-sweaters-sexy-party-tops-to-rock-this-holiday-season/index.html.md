Who needs ugly sweater parties when you can shop for sexy party tops? Don't get me wrong; there's nothing I love more than dressing up in ridiculously quirky pullovers (especially if they feature clever quotes from the shows that I can't help binge-watching every weekend) and throwing mock fashion shows with my best pals. After all, the holiday season is all about having fun with the people you love the most. But if you're in need of a last-minute outfit for that holiday party, sexy party tops may be more in tune with the occasion. So get ready to put your best foot forward in style!
