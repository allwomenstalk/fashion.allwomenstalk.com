# 7 Smart Reasons to Shop Online and Avoid Stores ...

I personally love to shop, and I definitely have my reasons to shop online rather than in the store. When I’ve shopped recently I’ve noticed that I never really need anything, I just want something. When pondering all the wonderful reasons to shop online, I came across the fact that shopping online is just smart. Here are just a few reasons that I recommend shopping online.

## 1. No Long Line

One of my first reasons to shop online rather than in the store is because there are no lines. Whenever I go into the mall, I have to wait in line just for a lady to tell me that they are out of stock or that they don’t carry a particular size. Online, however, I don’t have to wait much and most of the time when they’re out of stock, they know when an item will be restocked. Another plus is that they normally have my size!

## 2. Coupon Codes

Coupons are another one of my reasons to shop online. Many websites already have discounts, plus they have their online exclusive items, and finally they have additional deals. Coupon codes work wonders for my budget and are obviously a smart tool to use when shopping.

## 3. Free Shipping

One of my favorite reasons to shop online is because they have free shipping. When shopping online sometimes buying more is better than buying less. Most websites ship free for a purchase over $40 or $50. That means I have more money to spend shopping instead of paying for shipping, which can be outrageous at times.

## 4. Delivered

When I shop online, all those awkward furniture pieces and heavy dishes are delivered to my house, which is another one of my favorite reasons to shop online. Shopping online allows someone to do the hard work for you; it’s just smarter to shop online sometimes.

## 5. Pre-Shop

Another one of my reasons to shop online is because I can pre-shop. I’m very indecisive when I shop, so shopping online just allows me to put things in my shopping cart and then revisit them later. This allows me to save time and money.

## 6. Online Exclusives

I’m a lady who loves to wear things that not many people have so one of my favorite reasons to shop online is for online exclusives. I like the fact that when I shop online there are some things that really are exclusive. That means that no one else can get them unless they’re shopping online as well. Everyone wants to be exclusive sometimes right?

## 7. Shop Anywhere

I know I’m not the only lady out here who likes to shop at different places and the fact that I can shop anywhere, wherever is just another one of my reasons to shop online. Let’s be honest with ourselves, sometimes there is just not enough time in the day to actually go into a store and shop. Sometimes one store we want is at one mall and the other store we want isn’t even in the mall. Online shopping allows us to kill two birds with one stone. Ladies let’s just take a moment and thank the Internet for being awesome. Thank you Internet!

These are just 7 of my personal reasons for shopping online. I honestly find that sometimes shopping online is smarter than shopping in stores and more convenient. Shopping online can be cheaper, exclusive, and less work. Ladies, let’s just sit back, kick our feet up, grab a cup o’ Joe, and shop! What stores do you shop online at and who has the best deals? Please share!
