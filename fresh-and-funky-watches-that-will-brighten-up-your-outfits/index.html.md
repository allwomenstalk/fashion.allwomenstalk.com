# 9 Fresh and Funky Watches That Will Brighten up Your Outfits ...

Funky watches are making their way back into fashion. From sporty silhouettes to more classic styles, we’re seeing more and more designers giving the humble watch a fresh makeover. Forget your classic metal or black leather strap watches, for now’s the time to have fun with fashion, even if it is in the form of this nifty little accessory. You’ll always be on time with these fresh and funky watches.

## 1. Adidas Originals ‘Santiago’ Watch

![Adidas Originals ‘Santiago’ Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/uk/jgc0dmf2_600x881.jpg)

Funky watches like this Adidas Originals number are great for pulling off a sporty look (which is totally in!). It has a retro design with a round dial and comes in a bright red colour. Wear it with an athletic-inspired outfit of a tank dress, bomber jacket, and leather-look cap.

## 2. Kate Spade ‘Tiny Metro’ Leather Strap Watch

![Kate Spade ‘Tiny Metro’ Leather Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/m6/lp1bmh0k_600x894.jpg)

This Kate Spade watch comes in a classic, elegant design. The sunny yellow strap, however, makes it a fresh and funky choice, especially for spring. It has a slim design with gold-tone accents and a mother-of-pearl dial display. This yellow watch would look great set against denim clothing.

## 3. New Look Blue Sprayed Watch

![New Look Blue Sprayed Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zx/lr/ky1xy0mw_600x736.jpg)

Chunky watches have been on-trend for some time now. This New Look number comes in a fun mint colour. It has a faux chronograph design with a round dial and diamante embellishments. Wear this watch with your everyday outfits to give them a fresh update.

## 4. Komono 80s Leopard Print Watch

![Komono 80s Leopard Print Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/07/un5zq5t6_600x533.jpg)

This watch by Komono comes in a simple and sleek design and features a multicoloured leopard print strap. The leopard print design is a fresh take on a classic and would no doubt add a playful element to any outfit.

## 5. New Look Silicon Chunky Chain Watch

![New Look Silicon Chunky Chain Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/ke/qu3uepm5_600x737.jpg)

At $15, this colourful watch from New Look is a cheap and cheerful buy. It has a round dial with a chunky curb chain strap and comes in an easy pull on style. Wear it with a range of colourful bracelets to create a cool stacked look.

## 6. Nixon ‘the Small Time Teller’ round Watch

![Nixon ‘the Small Time Teller’ round Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/u6/og8h0xd0_600x891.jpg)

This flexible and lightweight watch by Nixon comes in a striking violet colour. It has a mineral-crystal face with a triple hand display. It would be a great way to add a subtle splash of colour to a basic outfit.

## 7. ASOS Pineapple Watch

![ASOS Pineapple Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bs/t0/nqhgi7qr_600x734.jpg)

Add a tropical touch to your outfit with this fun watch from ASOS. It has a round face with triple hand display and features a kooky pineapple print plastic strap. Wear it with a casual outfit of denim shorts and a t-shirt for a relaxed daytime look.

## 8. Modcloth ‘Scents’ of Time Watch

![Modcloth ‘Scents’ of Time Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xy/y1/ua85jvrk_600x857.jpg)

Sticking with a fruity theme, this watch from Modcloth is quite unique as it is actually apple-scented. Yep, you read correctly. Scented! It has a chunky design and comes in a bright red colour to match the subtle apple scent.

## 9. La Mer ‘Saturn’ Leather Wrap Watch

![La Mer ‘Saturn’ Leather Wrap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0a/gd/itfdmb0p_600x375.jpg)

This turquoise colouerd watch by La Mer comes in a cool wrap around design. The gold-tone accents on the watch complement the turquoise strap quite nicely, and would make it easy to team with other pieces of gold jewellery.

Brighten up your outfit with any one of these cheery watches. Do you wear a watch on a daily basis? Which of these colourful watches would you want to adorn your wrists with?
