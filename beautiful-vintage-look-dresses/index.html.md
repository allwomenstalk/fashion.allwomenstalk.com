# 7 Beautiful Vintage\-Look Dresses ...

**Vintage-Look Dresses** are an ideal way to work a vintage inspired outfit. Vintage is a big buzz word at the moment, and it’s influencing our wardrobes both directly and indirectly. While we may not at first realise it, there are a range of vintage-inspired designs in stores. A vintage-look dress is a simple way to wear the trend. If you can’t wear real vintage, then vintage-look dresses are the next best things.

## 1. Artisan Gallery Shirt Dress

![Artisan Gallery Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/1_artisan-gallery-shirt-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $42.99 at [shopruche.com](http://shopruche.com/maintenance.html)
A mid-length shirt dress has a bit of a retro vibe about it. This beige chiffon shirt dress features a fun polka dot pattern, with three quarter length sleeves and a drawstring waist. It would make a great everyday dress, and look effortlessly stylish worn with patent black flats and a framed handbag.

## 2. Modcloth Rosé Bubbly Dress

![Modcloth Rosé Bubbly Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/2_modcloth-ros-eacute-bubbly-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $157.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after something a bit more glamorous when it comes to **vintage-look dresses**, then take a look at this stunning number. It has a 1950s inspired full mesh skirt, high neckline, and V-shaped back. The rose coloured fabric is also delightfully feminine.

## 3. Anna Suit Floral Crochet Maxi Dress

![Anna Suit Floral Crochet Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/3_anna-suit-floral-crochet-maxi-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oYqiX8)
This **vintage-look dress** is a bit more bohemian in style. The crochet fabric is reminiscent of 1970s fashion, and the maxi length is perfect for creating a carefree aesthetic. This dress would look great worn with strappy leather sandals and a slouchy bucket bag.

## 4. Emily and Fin Blue Check Dress

![Emily and Fin Blue Check Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/4_emily-and-fin-blue-check-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $79.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This crisp blue and white dress has a demure vintage-inspired design. Plaid and gingham fabrics will be on-trend this season, but the trend has very retro roots. This dress has a sleeveless design with a full gathered skirt. This dress would be perfect for wearing to a [summer picnic](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/essential-foods-for-a-summer-picnic/) (just make sure you don’t match the picnic blanket!)

## 5. Topshop Wisteria Shirt Dress

![Topshop Wisteria Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/5_topshop-wisteria-shirt-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/32W0Oe)
The woven fabric of this dress gives it a vintage twist, alongside the full-skirted shirt dress design. It has a button-up collar, short sleeves, and fitted waist. **Vintage-look dresses** like this would look great teames with Mary Jane shoes, short gloves, and pearl earrings for a ladylike look.

## 6. Modcloth Demure and Simple Dress

![Modcloth Demure and Simple Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/6_modcloth-demure-and-simple-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Floral prints are always a fun choice. This 50s inspired **vintage-look dress** features a bold watercolour print. It has an A-line design and the skirt features large, crisp pleats. Team this vintage-look dress with nude ballet flats and a matching red framed clutch.

## 7. Lela Rose Fringed Jacquard Dress

![Lela Rose Fringed Jacquard Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/7_lela-rose-fringed-jacquard-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $2295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/5Vk8MF)
As far as **vintage-look dresses** go, this dress is quite extravagant. But you can’t deny its uniquely beautiful design. It has a fitted bodice and full skirt, which is typical of most vintage-inspired designs, but also features all-over fringing. This dress would look perfect teamed with simple metallic heels and a slim clutch.

**Vintage-look dresses** may not be for everyone, but if you do like the look then hopefully these dresses have given you some inspiration. What do you think of these vintage-look dresses?
