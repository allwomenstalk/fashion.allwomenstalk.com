# 9 Floral Sweatshirts to Make a Statement in ...

Ring in spring with this season’s must have floral sweatshirts. The statement printed sweatshirt has been the go-to piece of clothing for fashionistas lately and they’re set to stick around for some time yet. Well, until we get into the depths of summer, at least. While Kenzo’s tiger motif might have previously ruled the roost, right now it’s time for floral sweatshirts to take centre stage. Take a look at our picks of floral sweatshirts to make a statement in.

## 1. Moto Sequin Floral Sweatshirt

![Moto Sequin Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/dp/jr/tgbm4351_599x682.jpg)

Floral sweatshirts like this one from Topshop are great for working a casual everyday look. It comes in an indigo-wash soft denim and features a floral sequin front panel. Team it with black jeans and canvas sneakers for a relaxed off-duty outfit.

## 2. Peter Pilotto for Target Floral Sweatshirt

![Peter Pilotto for Target Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/r3/y1/efcv5a5m_599x751.jpg)

Bold, graphic prints are on-trend and this sweatshirt by Peter Pilotto for Target is a stylish and affordable option. The blue and white floral print is offset by a graphic diamond pattern. If you’re a fan of print clashing then this sweatshirt should be right up your alley. Wear it with a pencil skirt and heels for a more sophisticated approach to sweatshirt dressing.

## 3. Christopher Kane Printed Floral Sweatshirt

![Christopher Kane Printed Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/qd/0y/pq63qle0_599x763.jpg)

This floral sweatshirt comes in a chic monochrome design. Unlike your usually flourishing floral prints, this sweatshirt features a sleek black and white computerized digital floral print. It comes in a relaxed fit and is lined with fleece, making it equally comfortable and stylish. It would look great teamed with flared skirt and ballet flats for a smart daytime outfit.

## 4. Mango Floral Print Quilted Sweatshirt

![Mango Floral Print Quilted Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/ep/e5/nnwnuyi4_406x376.jpg)

This sweatshirt from Mango features a more classic floral print. It also has a quilted design with a round neck and dropped shoulder seams. Work a casual weekend look and team this floral sweatshirt with denim jeans and a pair of luxe slippers.

## 5. J.Crew Cutout Floral Sweatshirt

![J.Crew Cutout Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/nz/bf/cj1zbcb7_599x712.jpg)

This J.Crew number is a subtler take on this season’s statement floral sweatshirt. It comes in a navy colour and features a navy floral cut-out panel to the front. The cut-out panel allows for hints of skin to peek through the neckline of the sweatshirt. Work a contemporary look and team it with a leather skirt and ankle boots.

## 6. Mossimo Long-Sleeved Sweatshirt

![Mossimo Long-Sleeved Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hi/b8/i5/ruqhjpdj_599x532.jpg)

Statement sweatshirts don’t have to break the bank. This Mossimo sweatshirt from Target features an on-trend black and white abstract floral print. It’s also a very budget-friendly buy at under $20. Wear it with white tailored shorts and heels for a sleek and stylish look.

## 7. Mango Distressed Floral Sweatshirt

![Mango Distressed Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/xe/np/xo2j2oks_406x395.jpg)

This floral sweatshirt by Mango has a more vintage vibe to it. It’s a lightweight sweatshirt that has a relaxed fit and almost tapestry-inspired design. Stop it from bordering on grandma-chic by teaming it with edgy leather-look trousers and ankle boots.

## 8. Erdem ‘Zea’ Floral Print Sweatshirt

![Erdem ‘Zea’ Floral Print Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/iv/b8/z8xrts0u_599x764.jpg)

Designer brand Erdem is well known for their take on florals. This statement sweatshirt is a casual take on their usually much dressier signature florals. It features a bold multi-coloured floral print set against a fresh white background. This sweatshirt would look great worn with anything from black jeans to pastel mini skirts.

## 9. McQ Alexander McQueen Floral Sweatshirt

![McQ Alexander McQueen Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/vp/yh/srr84vb2_599x693.jpg)

The grey sweatshirt is a popular off-duty option. While it might conjure up images of less-than-stylish gym attire, this boxy sweatshirt by McQ Alexander McQueen has been given a whimsical floral touch. The front of the sweatshirt features embroidered mirrored rose motifs. It’s a sweet design that would work well worn with ladylike full skirts and patent shoes.

These are just a few floral sweatshirts to take stock of this season. Are you a fan of the floral sweatshirt? How would you style one this season?
