# 9 Stylish Floral Sweatshirts to Refresh Your Spring Wardrobe

Ring in spring with this season's must-have floral sweatshirts. The statement printed sweatshirt has been the go-to piece of clothing for fashionistas lately, and they're set to stick around for some time yet. While Kenzo's tiger motif might have previously ruled the roost, right now it's time for floral sweatshirts to take center stage. These versatile pieces perfectly blend comfort with high-fashion flair, making them a wardrobe essential for anyone looking to refresh their look. Take a look at our picks of floral sweatshirts to make a statement in.

| Feature | Best For | Styling Tip |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Graphic Prints | Bold Looks | Pair with solid colors |
| Textured/Quilted | Cozy Days | Layer with denim |
| Designer Motifs | Chic Outfits | Wear with heels |

## 1. Moto Sequin Floral Sweatshirt

![Moto Sequin Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/dp/jr/tgbm4351_599x682.jpg)

Floral sweatshirts like this one from [Topshop](https://www.topshop.com) are great for working a casual everyday look. It comes in an indigo-wash soft denim and features a floral sequin front panel. Team it with black jeans and canvas sneakers for a relaxed off-duty outfit. This piece is a great example of how to [mix textures](#4) for a more dynamic ensemble.

## 2. Peter Pilotto for Target Floral Sweatshirt

![Peter Pilotto for Target Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/r3/y1/efcv5a5m_599x751.jpg)

Bold, graphic prints are on-trend, and this sweatshirt by [Peter Pilotto for Target](https://www.target.com) is a stylish and affordable option. The blue and white floral print is offset by a graphic diamond pattern. If you're a fan of print clashing, then this sweatshirt should be right up your alley. Wear it with a pencil skirt and heels for a more sophisticated approach to sweatshirt dressing.

## 3. Christopher Kane Printed Floral Sweatshirt

![Christopher Kane Printed Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/qd/0y/pq63qle0_599x763.jpg)

This floral sweatshirt comes in a chic monochrome design from [Christopher Kane](https://www.christopherkane.com). Unlike your usually flourishing floral prints, this sweatshirt features a sleek black and white computerized digital floral print. It comes in a relaxed fit and is lined with fleece, making it equally comfortable and stylish. It would look great teamed with a flared skirt and ballet flats for a smart daytime outfit.

## 4. Mango Floral Print Quilted Sweatshirt

![Mango Floral Print Quilted Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/ep/e5/nnwnuyi4_406x376.jpg)

This quilted sweatshirt from [Mango](https://shop.mango.com) features a more classic floral print. It also has a unique design with a round neck and dropped shoulder seams. Work a casual weekend look and team this floral sweatshirt with denim jeans and a pair of luxe slippers. This quilted texture is perfect for those cooler spring mornings when you still want to look [effortlessly stylish](#1).

## 5. J.Crew Cutout Floral Sweatshirt

![J.Crew Cutout Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/nz/bf/cj1zbcb7_599x712.jpg)

This [J.Crew](https://www.jcrew.com) number is a subtler take on this season's statement floral sweatshirt. It comes in a navy color and features a navy floral cut-out panel to the front. The cut-out panel allows for hints of skin to peek through the neckline of the sweatshirt. Work a contemporary look and team it with a leather skirt and ankle boots.

- Mix florals with stripes for a bold pattern clash.

- Use a floral sweatshirt to dress down a formal skirt.

- Accessorize with minimal jewelry to let the print shine.

## 6. Mossimo Long-Sleeved Sweatshirt

![Mossimo Long-Sleeved Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hi/b8/i5/ruqhjpdj_599x532.jpg)

Statement sweatshirts don't have to break the bank. This Mossimo sweatshirt from [Target](https://www.target.com) features an on-trend black and white abstract floral print. It's also a very budget-friendly buy that looks much more expensive than its price tag. Wear it with white tailored shorts and heels for a sleek and stylish look.

## 7. Mango Distressed Floral Sweatshirt

![Mango Distressed Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/xe/np/xo2j2oks_406x395.jpg)

This floral sweatshirt by [Mango](https://shop.mango.com) has a more vintage vibe to it. It's a lightweight sweatshirt that has a relaxed fit and almost tapestry-inspired design. Stop it from bordering on grandma-chic by teaming it with edgy leather-look trousers and ankle boots. The distressed look adds a bit of [modern edge](#9) to the traditional floral pattern.

## 8. Erdem 'Zea' Floral Print Sweatshirt

![Erdem 'Zea' Floral Print Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kc/iv/b8/z8xrts0u_599x764.jpg)

Designer brand [Erdem](https://www.erdem.com) is well known for their take on florals. This statement sweatshirt is a casual take on their usually much dressier signature florals. It features a bold multi-colored floral print set against a fresh white background. This sweatshirt would look great worn with anything from black jeans to pastel mini skirts.

## 9. McQ Alexander McQueen Floral Sweatshirt

![McQ Alexander McQueen Floral Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ff/vp/yh/srr84vb2_599x693.jpg)

The grey sweatshirt is a popular off-duty option. While it might conjure up images of less-than-stylish gym attire, this boxy sweatshirt by [McQ Alexander McQueen](https://www.alexandermcqueen.com) has been given a whimsical floral touch. The front of the sweatshirt features embroidered mirrored rose motifs. It's a sweet design that would work well worn with ladylike full skirts and patent shoes.

These are just a few floral sweatshirts to take stock of this season. Whether you're looking for something bold and graphic or soft and vintage-inspired, there's a floral sweatshirt out there for every style. Are you a fan of the floral sweatshirt? How would you style one this season?
