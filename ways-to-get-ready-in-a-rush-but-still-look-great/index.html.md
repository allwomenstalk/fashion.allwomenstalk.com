# 7 Ways to Get Ready in a Rush, but Still Look Great ...

If you can't stop hitting snooze before class or work, you'll need ways to get ready in a rush. You don't want to throw on a random outfit and skip brushing your hair, because you still care about your appearance. However, when you're running on limited time, you have to figure out a way to make the most of it. Here are some ways to get ready in a rush that'll leave you looking as pretty as always:

## 1. Back up Plans

It always helps to plan ahead. Before you go to bed, put together an outfit so that you can just throw it on in the morning. If you forget to plan ahead, grab a simple black shirt and jeans from your closet. You can't go wrong with them, but one of the ways to get ready in a rush is to just prepare the night before.

## 2. Get Organized

Put all of your make-up together in one convenient spot. That way, you won't have to rustle through all of your products in order to find what you need. If you don't have time to apply your make-up, have a little bag handy so that you can throw everything inside. That way, you can put your make-up on once you get to your destination.

## 3. Practice Makes Perfect

If your hair is too messy to leave down or you don't have enough time to straighten it, then you have to throw it up. Practice your favorite updo as much as possible, so that you can create the look quickly when you need to do so. When all else fails, make sure your closet is filled with cute hats to cover up your head.

## 4. Mirror Magic

This one sounds simple, but always look in the mirror before you leave. No matter how little time you have before you need to leave the house, you can sacrifice a few seconds. You could find out that your shirt is stained, or that you put it on backward. Checking yourself over quickly can save you a whole lot of embarrassment.

## 5. Pick What's Important

What part of your body do you deem the most important? If you're going to be sitting behind a desk all day, then no one is going to be looking at your shoes, so don't spend hours picking them out. If you're going to be outside in the cold, then your jacket is more important than what's underneath it.

## 6. Less is More

Don't spend too much time applying eyeliner, because it'll leave you with no time to curl your hair. Spend short amounts of time working on whatever you need to do. Don't fixate on one thing for too long, or you'll end up neglecting everything else.

## 7. Rock It

Even if you feel like a mess, walk with confidence. If you're smiling while strutting your stuff, no one will notice that your outfit is mismatched. It's all about the way you act.

Even if it normally takes you an hour to get dressed, you can get ready in under twenty minutes if you really try. After a little bit of practice, you'll be a pro at looking pretty in a flash. What do you usually end up wearing when you have to get ready in a rush?
