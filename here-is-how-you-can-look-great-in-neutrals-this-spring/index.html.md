# Here is How You Can Look Great in Neutrals This Spring ...

The springtime is a big time for pastels and bright colors. However if you’re not a fan of bright colors, fashion can be a tough time for you. Rather than drudge through the springtime waiting for your glorious neutrals to come back, rock your neutrals with pride. Beige, white, and navy are gorgeous colors to wear in the spring. Especially if you have a tan, they’ll pop beautifully against your skin tone! Neutrals don’t have to be reserved for the cold months, and when you wear neutrals like this you’ll prove everyone wrong!

## 1. A Beige Coat

![A Beige Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mb/lm/v5vprbor5502a677a78ad725872737_549x800.jpg)

A nice beige pea coat is the perfect way to top off any neutral look. It completes even the most haphazard outfits, and if you really don’t love your outfit, your perfect beige pea coat will hide all. I also love beige trench coats for spring, because they’re light enough but the beige color is also the perfect neutral for the springtime.

## 2. Pick a Base Color

![Pick a Base Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/hy/lh9juakw5502a7453ab0b049344498_600x969.jpg)

Pick a base color that you’re sure you absolutely love. Try a few out, whether they are white, beige, navy, or black and then pick the one that looks best on you that you love as well. From there, create your entire spring wardrobe around that neutral color you love. You’ll become a pro at finding all of the different colors that complement your look perfectly!

## 3. Go Navy

![Go Navy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/i6/w00welt05502a898e39b5893545975_500x700.jpg)

I think that navy is a strong neutral to stick with when the weather gets warmer. A lot of people think it should be reserved for the winter months, but I think that it’s even better in the summer. You can easily pull off a navy look in the summer, because it looks nautical and fun, especially if you live by the water!

## 4. Wear White

![Wear White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/mq/zz4w3udt5502a919ce9d3610393335_600x900.jpg)

White is a springtime staple. If you want to rock the latest spring trends but aren’t a fan of bright colors, stick with white. It can easily be paired with any of your other favorite neutrals, but if you are looking to branch out a little bit with bright colors, white matches those too!

## 5. Try Some Pastels on for Size

![Try Some Pastels on for Size](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tf/jb/us4fnq725502a8f7e23f4432050314_600x900.jpg)

If you’re looking to rock neutrals in the spring, you don’t have to say goodbye to color altogether. Instead, try pastels. They’re much more muted, making it easier to add some color to your wardrobe, especially in the spring. Plus, they’ll pair well with the base neutral that you already picked.

## 6. Invest in Jewelry

![Invest in Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/vk/ho6wkmtt5502a9c03bc2d253101628_600x900.jpg)

If you’re looking for other ways to make a statement without going for the bright and loud colors, try jewelry. Even if you just go for simple gold jewelry, it will still make a statement and pop against your neutral wardrobe, and it will match with all of your favorite outfits!

## 7. Let Your Makeup Reflect Your Look

![Let Your Makeup Reflect Your Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/qm/ed0gst8u5502a9ff39000368684481_474x711.jpg)

Makeup is a really great way to play up your neutral wardrobe. Add some shimmer to your usual look, especially in the spring, to get your entire look to pop just a little bit more than usual. You’ll look effervescent in your neutrals and shimmery makeup!

What are your favorite ways to wear neutrals in the spring? Have you found a base color yet for your spring wardrobe? It can be tough, especially because spring is such a big time for bright colors. Let me know your tips and tricks for wearing neutrals in the spring in the comments!
