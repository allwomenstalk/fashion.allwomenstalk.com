# 7 Stylish Scallop\-Edged Clothes ...

Scallop-edged clothes are an easy way to update your look. This trend can be found on a range of items from clothes to shoes. A scallop trim is a guaranteed way to add interest to any item of clothing. Below are just a few of my favourites.

## 1. Etoile Isabel Marant Arthel Broderie Anglaise Cotton Top

![Etoile Isabel Marant Arthel Broderie Anglaise Cotton Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xf/oz/jvnuqml154c8f4cced968313892917_400x539.jpg)

**Price:** $385.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/8njT0d)
This broderie anglaise top is a sleek and chic take on the bohemian trend. My favourite design feature has to be the cut-out detail at the shoulders. The scallop edges are sweet and delicate. This versatile top can also be worn off the shoulder. Pair it with dark denim or tan leather.

## 2. Topshop Scallop Button Shirt

![Topshop Scallop Button Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/as/kao71a1i54c8f4ce2e128897628418_400x440.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/GUZT58)
This peter-pan collared shirt is a ray of sunshine! It’s a sure fire way to add a burst of colour to your outfit. I love the pale yellow and white contrast collar. The scallop edges complement the overall aesthetic. Wear it over a high-waisted denim skirt for a playful, pretty look.

## 3. Vanessa Bruno Striped Scalloped Blouse

![Vanessa Bruno Striped Scalloped Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xn/de/wki4qm6i54c8f4cf5b21f545664686_400x482.jpg)

**Price:** $428.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This top is all about understated style. The subtle stripes on this blouse give it a wonderful texture. It features a scalloped placket and concealed button down front. I think this would look perfect with a pair of white loose, tailored shorts.

## 4. Topshop Black Linen Scallop Hem Shift Dress

![Topshop Black Linen Scallop Hem Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v1/tr/v7ttb9hk54c8f4d176f6a798080204_400x593.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/4TDF2r)
The scallop edging on this dress adds extra interest to an otherwise ordinary little black dress. The dress is made from linen and features a back zip fastening. The simple sleeveless shift design means that it can be easily dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. I think this dress would look great paired with a leather jacket and sheer polka-dot tights.

## 5. Topshop Basic Scallop Edged Vest

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/QrMZR0)
This basic vest is the perfect item for layering. I love the all-over scallop edging. The dark blush colour is extremely fashionable. Keep it casual with jeans or dress it up with leather-look trousers. Gold accents would complete the look really well.

## 6. Vanessa Bruno Lace Trimmed Crepe Dress

**Price:** $640.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ATD2Y2)
This long-sleeved dress is the perfect piece to take you from day to night. The combination of crepe and lace makes for one delicate looking dress. I love the midnight-blue colour. It’s such a nice change from basic black. Wear this dress with a long necklace and patent accessories.

## 7. See by Chloe Scalloped Cotton and Silk Blend Jersey Dress

![See by Chloe Scalloped Cotton and Silk Blend Jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rt/c6/hnc2hczb54c8f4d49a306341511106_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $230.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/LBuWHM)
This dress is probably one of my favourites. Words cannot describe how much I love the tiered scallop detailing down the front of this dress. The combination of black and nude colours is also very on-trend. This dress would look great paired with black suede wedges.

Has the scallop trend infiltrated your wardrobe yet? Which of the above items do you like most?
