# 9 Funky Denim Vests for Now ...

Denim vests are a stylish alternative to the denim jacket. Their sleeveless style also means that they’re much more practical for the warmer months. Simply layer a denim vest over a summer dress for a cool and casual look. The following are just a few examples of stylish denim vests you might want to be wearing right now.

## 1. Colored Denim Vest

_ **Price:** $22.90 at forever21.com_
Lately it’s all been about colored denim. This affordable denim vest from Forever 21 has a classic design and comes in three colours: red, French vanilla and blossom pink. It’s perfect for wearing with a simple tank dress and flats.

## 2. Stone Crop Denim Vest

![Stone Crop Denim Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_stone-crop-denim-vest_300x407.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Denim vests are great for working a cool, off-duty look. This one comes in a stone colored wash with faded detailing at the front. Work a contemporary look by teaming it with a slouchy tank top, jeans, and lace-up boots.

## 3. Leopard Crop Denim Vest

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
For something really funky, you can’t go past printed denim vests. This vest comes in a biker-style design and features a black and grey leopard print. To really make a statement, wear this vest over a neon-bright dress.

## 4. Moto Sleeveless Denim Jacket

![Moto Sleeveless Denim Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_moto-sleeveless-denim-jacket_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Zws1L7)
Keep things a bit more basic with this denim vest. It has a classic design with authentic trims. For a look that’s right now, team it with a crisp white dress and color pop accessories.

## 5. Alexander Wang Foil Appliqué Denim Vest

![Alexander Wang Foil Appliqué Denim Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_alexander-wang-foil-appliqu-denim-vest_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $275.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Denim vests with foil appliqué? Why not? That must have been what Alexander Wang asked himself when designing this vest. Work a fresh, contemporary look by teaming this vest with an [all white outfit](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-wear-an-all-white-outfit/).

## 6. Cropped Rose Denim Vest

_ **Price:** $22.90 at forever21.com_
Floral prints are always a popular choice, and the standout feature of this cropped denim vest is its blooming rose print. It’s a fun and unexpected way to work the floral trend. Tie in the color of the roses by teaming this denim vest with a red dress.

## 7. Blue Dark Wash Denim Vest

![Blue Dark Wash Denim Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_blue-dark-wash-denim-vest_300x407.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after something more versatile and timeless when it comes to denim vests, choose one in a dark wash like this one. This one would look great teamed with a yellow printed dress, leather sandals, and slouchy side bag.

## 8. Color Blocked Denim Vest

_ **Price:** $18.90 at forever21.com_
Color blocking is certainly having a fashion moment right now. Color blocked denim is a fun way to wear this trend. This vest features features cream and light blue denim panels with contrasting patch pockets. Let the vest do the talking and keep the rest of your outfit simple when styling it.

## 9. White Cropped Denim Vest

![White Cropped Denim Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/9_white-cropped-denim-vest_300x407.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If there’s a time to wear right denim, it’s right now. If you can’t quite accept white denim jeans, then go for a vest or jacket instead. White cropped denim vests such as this one would look great teamed with colourful printed dresses this summer.

As you can see, there are a range of denim vests to choose from at the moment. Whether you prefer simple styles or something more boisterous in design, a denim vest is a cool way of layering up an outfit. What do you think of these denim vests?
