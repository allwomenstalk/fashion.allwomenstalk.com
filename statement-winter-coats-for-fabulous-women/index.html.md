# 8 Statement Winter Coats for Fabulous Women ...

Winter Coats For Women come in various cuts and designs. A good coat is a foundation piece for any functional winter wardrobe. From designer labels to affordable chain store brands, there are winter coats for women to suit a range of styles and needs. While coats in classic cuts and colourways are staples, a statement coat can really take your winter outfits up a notch. Below are a few stylish and statement-making winter coats for women.

## 1. Topshop Red Bound Seam Duffel Coat

![Topshop Red Bound Seam Duffel Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/gm/lr2jrahr54c932283dc16842082131_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $178.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/oPL86M)
The red coat is the ideal statement making piece. This duffle coat has a relaxed design and would make a great casual choice of clothing. It is made from a wool blend and comes with toggle fastenings and a detachable hood. Wear it with jeans, thigh high boots, and a knit beanie for a relaxed winter look.

## 2. Anna Sui Abstract-Pattern Knitted Coat

![Anna Sui Abstract-Pattern Knitted Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g9/m9/fpv4g6am54c9322920f4d393101261_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $625.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/sRJtO9)
As far as winter coats for women go, this one ticks both the timeless and statement-making boxes. This knitted coat comes in a black and white colourway and features a prominent abstract pattern. It has notched lapels, long sleeves, and turned up cuffs. This coat is an oversized style, so cinch it in using the waist belt to create shape.

## 3. Topshop Tweed Faux Fur Collar Boyfriend Coat

![Topshop Tweed Faux Fur Collar Boyfriend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t8/ao/xr9yfo4f54c9322a148e8370075124_334x498.jpg)

**Price:** $245.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/MMkGNV)
This grey tweed coat features a large detachable faux fur collar. It has a fairly boxy design and is made from wool-blend fabric. The combination of tweed and faux fur creates an interesting textural contrast. As the cut of this coat is fairly boxy, keep the rest of your outfit fairly streamlined.

## 4. Topshop Multistitch Wool Trench Coat

![Topshop Multistitch Wool Trench Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c7/pc/ngsck56x54c9322af336a051565793_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $240.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Eq3ZgR)
The tobacco colour of this coat gives it a certain vintage feel. This calf length trench coat has a single breasted design. It features multistitch details on the pockets and collar. Wear this coat with skinny jeans and brogues, and top it off with a floppy felt fedora.

## 5. Diane Von Furstenberg Harrington Brushed Coat

![Diane Von Furstenberg Harrington Brushed Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/kr/czmrhdef54c9322bc6e28358566236_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $695.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/k1yHMu)
This blue coat is great for making the pastel trend work in winter. It has an oversized collar and lapels, bracelet sleeves, and a sash belt. It also has pouch pockets and snap fastenings at the front. Experiment with some muted colour blocking and team this coat with tobacco coloured trousers.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Orange Scallop Minimal Coat

![Dorothy Perkins Orange Scallop Minimal Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/xd/qf0ohrdh54c9322ca3f50836949255_368x496.jpg)

**Price:** $108.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This orange coat has a fairly minimal design. When it comes to winter coats for women this season, you can’t go past a bold block colour. This coat is collarless and has a concealed front placket. It also features scallop detailing along the front. Dress this coat up by accessorising with a collar necklace and embellished bags.

## 7. 3.1 Phillip Lim Scarf Collar Peacoat

![3.1 Phillip Lim Scarf Collar Peacoat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/m3/m42vstp654c9322d8a2b6453327959_350x488.jpg)

**Price:** $800.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This peacoat has many interesting design features. It is made from a combination of tweed and neoprene. This coat features an oversized collar detail which can be worn out or as a scarf. The neoprene sleeves are also detachable. You could try removing the sleeves and wearing this coat over a knitted long sleeved shirt.

## 8. Dorothy Perkins Tobacco Colour Block Coat

![Dorothy Perkins Tobacco Colour Block Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jf/hk/pqmohadh54c9322e72908928712772_368x496.jpg)

**Price:** $125.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This half-and-half coat definitely makes a statement. The double breasted cut is a classic one for winter coats for women, but the colour blocked design is an on-trend touch. This is a shorter length swing coat and would look great worn over full-skirted dresses.

A statement winter coat is great for pulling together an outfit. Come winter, there is always a wide selection of winter coats for women. What are your thoughts on the statement coat? Which of these winter coats for women do you like best?
