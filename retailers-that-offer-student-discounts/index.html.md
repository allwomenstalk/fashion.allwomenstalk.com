# 7 Retailers That Offer Student Discounts ...

Retailers that Offer Student Discounts are vital for college students who love to shop but are on a tight budget! Thanks to these retailers that offer student discounts, you can still get your fashion fix even if you're tight on cash! Take advantage of your student ID while you have it. Shopping on a budget without sacrificing quality products is possible!

## 1. Urban Outfitters

Retailers that offer student discounts can be hard to come by, so I was thrilled to learn that one of my favorite stores has a discount program. Urban Outfitters offers 10% off in stores on select dates to students who have a current student identification card. Not only can you enhance your current wardrobe with this student discount, you can also turn your dorm or apartment into a hip and comfortable place to live. Saving 10% on home decor and clothing is a great way to start the school year!

## 2. Topshop

Even though most of us don't have a Topshop in the city we live in, we can all still take advantage of their student discount. Topshop offers a 10% discount in stores and online for current students. Satisfy your craving for European style by looking chic in Topshop for less!

## 3. J.Crew

J.Crew's preppy and effortless style is perfect to pair with your Sperry's for your jaunts across campus each day. Lucky for you, J.Crew gives 15% off of regular priced items to students who shop in their stores. Now that cashmere sweater or ultra-chic dress that seemed way out of reach can finally be yours!

## 4. Madewell

Receiving 15% off in store purchases at Madewell makes this already desirable retailer a must have! Bring your student identification card to the store and present it upon checking out. Madewell may be known for their denim, but their entire clothing line is stylish and wearable, perfect for a day spent on campus.

## 5. Steve Madden

Steve Madden makes some of the cutest and trendiest shoes! I can't go out in public anymore without seeing a girl rocking a variation of Steve Madden combat boots. I have the black studded Tarnney boots in my closet. Get your pair for 10% off when you show your student identification in Steve Madden stores!

## 6. Banana Republic

Banana Republic is known for their modern and stylish clothing and accessories. Their simple, yet fashionable clothing items are great for the office and can transition easily into evening wear. Did I mention they are getting ready to release a limited edition Mad Men collection?! Students receive 15% off in stores with a valid student ID.

## 7. Charlotte Russe

The already low prices at Charlotte Russe coupled with their 10% off in stores student discount makes for huge savings when it comes to wardrobe shopping! Get your basic layering items or even the latest fashions at extremely affordable prices. Shopping at inexpensive stores that feature trendy items, such as Charlotte Russe, is a great way to save money when buying items that may be out of season quickly!

Save money while you're living off your student loans by taking advantage of the amazing discounts these retailers offer to current students! It is possible to dress stylishly while living on a student budget. Make good use of your student identification card while you have the chance! What's the best deal you've ever scored with your student ID?
