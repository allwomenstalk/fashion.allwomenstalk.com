# 9 Stunning Statement Accessories ...

**Statement accessories** are guaranteed to take your outfit to the next level. A stunning statement accessory is a simple yet effective way to update a basic outfit. Accessorising with trend-based pieces is nothing new, but this season is all about making an impact. The more an accessory makes a statement, the better. Take inspiration from the following accessories that make a statement.

## 1. KARL Sequined Poplin Collar

![KARL Sequined Poplin Collar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_karl-sequined-poplin-collar_460x233.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DuLKEm)
Detachable collars are still a popular choice of accessory. Designed to be worn in place of a necklace, this sequined collar is sure to add a bit of a ‘wow’ factor to your outfit. Add a bit of glitz and glamour to a plain t-shirt by popping on this sequined collar over the top.

## 2. Christian Louboutin Bell-Embellished Shoulder Bag

![Christian Louboutin Bell-Embellished Shoulder Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_christian-louboutin-bell-embellished-shoulder-bag_304x560.jpg)

**Price:** $1795.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/HQYAL9)
This seemingly simple shoulder bag is given a statement-making hit thanks to an embellished shoulder strap. The great thing about this **statement accessory** is that the shoulder strap is detachable, making it quite versatile. While this version is quite pricey, you could probably get away with DIY-ing your own version at home.

## 3. Lara Bohinc Large Plait Bangle

_ **Price:** $485.00 at mytheresa.com_
Nothing makes a statement more than a bit of bling. This large plaited bangle comes in a yellow gold-plated finish. The mesh design adds an interesting texture to the finish. The bracelet simply slips on, as the design allows for some stretch.

## 4. Bijoux Heart Swarovski Crystal Ring

![Bijoux Heart Swarovski Crystal Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_bijoux-heart-swarovski-crystal-ring_460x527.jpg)

**Price:** $340.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/OUdklJ)
When it comes to **accessories that make a statement**, you can’t go past a good cocktail ring. This particular ring is 24-karat gold-plated and features an array of coloured Swarovski crystals. Wear it alone to really let it shine, or team it with simpler gold jewellery.

## 5. Thierry Lasry Sunglasses

![Thierry Lasry Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_thierry-lasry-sunglasses_460x238.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/VWvIRw)
Statement sunglasses are an easy way to update your look. Keep an eye out for thick frames in bold colours and patterns. While this pair costs several hundreds of dollars, you’ll be bound to find several cheaper alternatives at stores such as Topshop and Forever 21.

## 6. Yves Saint Laurent Arty Ring

![Yves Saint Laurent Arty Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_yves-saint-laurent-arty-ring_460x402.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ak2pHZ)
The YSL Arty ring is probably the most iconic cult **statement accessory** in recent times. The design is instantly recognisable. It is also available in a range of colours and finishes, so you can choose one that best suits your style.

## 7. Kenneth Jay Lane Gold-Plated Resin Necklace

![Kenneth Jay Lane Gold-Plated Resin Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/7_kenneth-jay-lane-gold-plated-resin-necklace_460x464.jpg)

**Price:** $300.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/qvgXah)
It’s all about colour right now, and there’s no easier way to work this trend into your everyday wardrobe than with some **statement accessories**. This particular necklace has a fun, modern design. The spiked design also works well with the currently popular tribal trend.

## 8. Topshop Seed Bead Drape Necklace

![Topshop Seed Bead Drape Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/8_topshop-seed-bead-drape-necklace_230x560.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/XehMSq)
**Statement accessories** vary depending on different fashion trends. If you’re after something a bit more bohemian or tribal-inspired, then this beaded necklace could work for you. The draped strands create a fringed effect and the multicoloured beads would look great set against a neutral outfit.

## 9. Christopher Kane Aqua Gel-Filled PVC Clutch

![Christopher Kane Aqua Gel-Filled PVC Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/9_christopher-kane-aqua-gel-filled-pvc-clutch_460x352.jpg)

**Price:** $720.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/m7hkS1)
Christopher Kane is well known for his fun, fashion-forward accessories. This gel-filled clutch is definitely a statement-maker. It really does speak for itself, and is guaranteed to set you apart from the crowd.

**Statement accessories** come in all shapes and sizes. From bags to jewellery, adding a statement accessory to your look is an effective way to update your outfit. What do you think of these statement accessories?
