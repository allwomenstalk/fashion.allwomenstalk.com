# 7 Cool Pieces of Crochet Clothing ...

While the term ‘crochet’ doesn’t initially evoke ideas of high fashion and style, I think that this is soon about to change. Nowadays there seems to be an abundance of crochet style clothes. From designer brands to high street stores, the crochet trend is really taking off. Below are some of my top picks.

## 1. Topshop All over Crochet Shift Dress

![Topshop All over Crochet Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/ef/wssg0lqf54c8f4d808cb6189820221_400x566.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/eldA3n)
This cream shift dress is a modern take on the 70s hippie aesthetic. The crochet design is sweet and delicate. I love the slight frill features at the sleeve and hem. It is 100% cotton and features a long zipper at the back. Pair this dress with tan accessories to make the most of that hippie vibe.

## 2. Missoni Mare Braden Crochet Knit Mini Dress

![Missoni Mare Braden Crochet Knit Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/yq/xrg9o6by54c8f4d98f0b0427523191_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $625.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/i3QwYO)
The Missoni brand is well known for its crochet designs. This colour mini dress is simply stunning. It’s a body-con design and is perfect for both day and night. Wear it with neutral accessories and sandals during the day, and patent heels and sparkling jewels at night.

## 3. Topshop Hand Crochet Flower Kimono

![Topshop Hand Crochet Flower Kimono](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l7/g1/fk6yynm254c8f4daee41f284407827_400x470.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/8DvOTi)
This kimono is perfect for layering over your outfit. The crochet is hand stitched and features a flower pattern. I love the scallop trim along the edges and at the sleeves. Layer it over a floaty dress during summer or over jeans and sweaters in winter.

## 4. Topshop Apricot Crochet Zip Back Top

![Topshop Apricot Crochet Zip Back Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rx/nk/h3b3c4px54c8f4dc7baa3865696584_400x575.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Zaws0d)
I love the burnt orange shade of this top. The exposed zip top is an edgy design feature. This top is the ultimate in summer festival chic. I’d pair this faded denim shorts, a floppy felt hat, and big tortoiseshell sunglasses.

## 5. Burberry Prorsum Crocheted Cotton Dress

![Burberry Prorsum Crocheted Cotton Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e2/bk/p93h6po754c8f4dda0746038599385_400x600.jpg)

**Price:** $2592.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/IlWMwv)
If you like your clothes high-end and designer, then this crochet option is just for you. This taupe cotton dress abounds with sophistication. I love the addition of the mesh panels. I think this dress would look great paired with subtle metallic accents.

## 6. Topshop Multicoloured Crochet Shorts

![Topshop Multicoloured Crochet Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vf/h9/ji8a9xw554c8f4dedb03d245485739_400x264.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/HyHjnP)
These cute shorts would make a fun addition to any wardrobe. I love the multicoloured design. They are 100% cotton and feature a self-tie waist. If you’re after a cheeky pair of shorts for the summer, then these are definitely it!

## 7. Topshop Ivory Crochet Back Waistcoat

![Topshop Ivory Crochet Back Waistcoat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/zy/opq5n34154c8f4dfe09f0384414696_400x591.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/mHj4OK)
This waistcoat is a subtle, sophisticated approach to the crochet trend. The ruffle detailing on the front is elegantly on-trend. The crochet back is definitely the statement feature. Pair this with jeans, a neutral top, and sky high heels.
