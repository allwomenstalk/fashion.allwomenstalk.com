# 8 Pretty Patchwork Pieces ...

Patchwork Pieces are a great way to work the current craft trend into your wardrobe. Taking a cue from the craft movement, anything with a slightly homespun feel will be key to creating this season’s latest look. It’s all about working patchwork pieces into your wardrobe in a way that you feel comfortable with. To keep things more chic and less homely, look for slick pieces in sharp designs. Take inspiration from the following patchwork pieces.

## 1. J.Crew Madras Checked Cotton Shirt

![J.Crew Madras Checked Cotton Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/w6/or8cgecr54c9340995308893250608_382x500.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/a642Qk)
An easy way to work some patchwork into your wardrobe is with a simple shirt, such as this button up J.Crew number. It is made from cotton fabric and features multicoloured check patchwork pieces. The look is equal parts preppy and casual. Wear this shirt with denim jeans, tan shoes, and a slouchy side bag.

## 2. Cacharel Patchwork Floral Dress

_ **Price:** $337.50 at lagarconne.com_
This is a more contemporary take on the patchwork trend. This pretty dress features multicoloured floral print patterns. It has a V-neck front and back, an empire waist, and patchwork panels throughout. The bright colours are sure to make this dress a standout piece in your wardrobe.

## 3. Bandhani Patchwork Scarf

_ **Price:** $450.00 at barneys.com_
If you’re after a subtle way to incorporate patchwork into your wardrobe, a scarf is a simple yet effective idea. This silk blend scarf has a horizontal stripe patchwork detail. The panels feature a distinctive starburst pattern and come in warm autumn hues. Wear this with a cream sweater and neutral skirt.

## 4. Marni Suede and Patchwork Watersnake Tote

**Price:** $1490.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/KiV9yL)
Featuring a grey suede body and a multicoloured patchwork face, this bag is a directional way to incorporate patchwork pieces into your wardrobe. This bag is a compact size and ideal for adding that little extra detail to an outfit. It has black leather trims, detachable top handle, and adjustable shoulder strap.

## 5. Nina Ricci Patchwork Bag

_ **Price:** $1900.00 at barneys.com_
This beautiful bag is not your typical patchwork inspired item. It is made from lilac fabric and features raw patchwork detailing. This bag has a metal fold over closure, zip pocket, and chain link handle. It’s a delicate accessory that would work well with [feminine outfits](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/super-simple-tips-to-look-more-feminine/).

## 6. Tory Burch Fair Isle Skirt

![Tory Burch Fair Isle Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rg/jy/jlex55mi54c934140b52a443220543_290x399.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YCKUZQ)
Craft inspired pieces are a key trend this season. This wool skirt features a traditional Fair Isle patchwork pattern and is typical of the patchwork trend. It has a fitted waistband, slant pockets, and an A-line design. This skirt would look great with a blush blouse and neutral pumps.

## 7. Missoni Praga Patchwork Wrap Dress

**Price:** $1185.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wtWvqP)
Missoni are renowned for their colourful take on knit dressing. This crochet-knit wrap dress has a colourful patchwork pattern and is perfect for working a fresh summer look. Alternatively, wear this dress in cooler weather by pairing it with opaque tights and wedge ankle boots.

## 8. Jeffrey Campbell Mix and Patch Wedge

**Price:** $159.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re after quirky patchwork pieces, you can’t go past these wedge booties. They feature taupe, ivory, mauve, and powder blue patches with chevron stitching. They have a concealed platform wedge and feature a zip fastening at the back. These would look great worn with skinny jeans, an oversized sweater, and cute scarf headband.

There are a variety of patchwork pieces available. Working this trend into your wardrobe can be as simple as adding a new bag to your outfit. Which of these patchwork pieces do you like best, and would you be willing to try this trend this season?
