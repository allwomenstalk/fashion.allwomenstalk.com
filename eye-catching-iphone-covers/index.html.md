# 7 Eye Catching IPhone Covers ...

iPhone Covers not only protect your device, but they can also add a sense of personality to it. Whether you prefer novelty cases or designer ones, there’s no doubt that there’s a seemingly endless range of iPhone covers available. If you’re bored of basic black or clear covers, you can always try choosing a brightly coloured or patterned cover. Make your iPhone stand out from the crowd with these eye catching iPhone covers.

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs Panther IPhone 4G Case

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Panther IPhone 4G Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t8/cc/d31pcly854c931bb42d43495122473_176x350.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Bkmmqk)
This black and white case features an all-over panther pattern. It’s a tongue-in-cheek way to work the animal print trend that is popular this season. This cover is designed for the iPhone 4, and features curved edges and a designer stamp at the back and interior. This cover only provides protection for the back, so it would be wise to also purchase a scratch-resistant screen protector.

## 2. Jonathon Adler Surfing Style IPhone 4 Case

![Jonathon Adler Surfing Style IPhone 4 Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h8/sr/y9f2h0li54c931bce67e7968073329_185x343.jpg)

**Price:** $27.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Take the colour blocking trend to the next level with multicoloured iPhone covers such as this one. Designed by Jonathon Adler, this iPhone 4 case features a cascading chevron pattern in an assortment of rainbow colours. It is made from plastic and has a selection of cutouts as well as a breakaway bottom. This case is perfect for complementing a beachy lifestyle.

## 3. Jimmy Choo Metallic Leather IPhone Sleeve

![Jimmy Choo Metallic Leather IPhone Sleeve](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nd/ob/iftmajaq54c931bdd2b63586576113_187x350.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DpglQI)
This metallic leather sleeve is a stylish way to carry your iPhone. It is made from silver leather and features a gold-tipped white grosgrain tab ribbon. This handy ribbon detail allows quick access to your iPhone. This iPhone sleeve is also fully lined.

## 4. Juicy Couture Glitter IPhone 4 Hard Case

![Juicy Couture Glitter IPhone 4 Hard Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/hy/avf0jiyr54c931beba03b770452289_186x350.jpg)

**Price:** $57.51 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/uMw1QD)
Hard iPhone covers are a popular choice. This one has a structured design with clip on fastening. It also features an eye catching metallic glitter design. It has a contrast logo at the back as well as cutout details. This case is available in gold and silver glitter designs.

## 5. Petunia Pickle Bottom IPhone 4 Case

![Petunia Pickle Bottom IPhone 4 Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/w4/erprua4h54c931bfb0b7a175920555_162x347.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Black and white doesn’t always mean bland when it comes to design. This case features a chic black and white swirl pattern. It is made from plastic and is designed for the iPhone 4. This cover is a backing only.

## 6. Fred Standing Ovation IPhone 4 Case

![Fred Standing Ovation IPhone 4 Case](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/jh/vt4ucwan54c931c0adc12307079878_212x350.jpg)

**Price:** $24.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For lovers of novelty iPhone covers, or those after something a bit retro, then check out this cassette tape case. This case is made from silicone and also comes with a ‘jewel case’ display stand. It’s a quirky way to keep your iPhone 4 safe and sound.

## 7. Forever21 Safari Wild IPhone Cover

_ **Price:** $9.80 at forever21.com_
Animal prints and patterns are popular choices when it comes to accessories. This is not different when it comes to iPhone covers. This backing cover is rhinestone encrusted and has a snap on fastening. This cover is available in gold and brown or silver and black colourways.

These are only a few eye catching iPhone covers that are currently available online. From simple leather sleeves to novelty retro iPhone covers, the range is seemingly endless. If you’re an iPhone owner, how do you protect your phone? Do any of these iPhone covers take your fancy?
