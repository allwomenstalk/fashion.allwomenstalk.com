# 9 Travel Inspired Fashion Items ...

Travel inspired fashion is an interesting concept. When looking at travel inspired fashions, I’m talking about things like map prints or digitally screened photographs of far-off destinations. You can find these on anything from bags to shoes to shirts. I guess the idea is that if you can’t go to the places you’ve always dreamed of, you can always wear them instead! Take a look at a few bits of fashion inspired by travel, below.

## 1. Topshop London Map Purse

![Topshop London Map Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/1_topshop-london-map-purse_350x188.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7cX0k5)
Take a little bit of London with you wherever you go thanks to this map print purse. It features a hand drawn design of the city of London, with all the major localities labelled for good measure. When it comes to travel inspired fashion, I guess this purse could also come in handy if you’re ever in London and in need of a map!

## 2. Eva Franco Cartography Dress

![Eva Franco Cartography Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_eva-franco-cartography-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $394.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This lovely silk dress features an eye-catching map illustration. It depicts a Bermuda-inspired scene with island outlines, sail boats, and floral prints. This is a unique dress and would look great dressed up with simple strappy heels and a medium-sized clutch.

## 3. Carven Printed Silk-Habotai Top

![Carven Printed Silk-Habotai Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_carven-printed-silk-habotai-top_350x379.jpg)

**Price:** $365.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/xLy9bd)
This printed silk-habotai top will have you dreaming of snow-capped mountains. Graphic prints are a popular trend and it’s something that works really well in the form of travel inspired fashion. Balance out the harsh terrain depicted on this top by teaming it with a [pastel skirt](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/pretty-pastel-midi-skirts-to-wear-for-spring/) and neutral sandals.

## 4. Modcloth Met Your Map Bag

![Modcloth Met Your Map Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_modcloth-met-your-map-bag_324x360.jpg)

**Price:** $45.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This large tote bag features a printed map of the NYC subway system. Even if you’ve never been to New York, you’ll become well acquainted with its subway system once you own this bag.This bag is big and roomy so would be ideal as a school bag, gym bag, beach bag, or even an overnight bag.

## 5. Modcloth Worldly Sandals

![Modcloth Worldly Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/5_modcloth-worldly-sandals_350x202.jpg)

**Price:** $32.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These printed sandals are something special. They have a peep toe design with a slim adjustable strap at the ankle. The real talking point of these sandals, however, is the old-world map print. These sandals would look great worn with a denim shirt dress and messenger bag.

## 6. Tsumori Chisato Costa Rica Jacket

![Tsumori Chisato Costa Rica Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_tsumori-chisato-costa-rica-jacket_350x310.jpg)

**Price:** $681.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For a really fashion-forward take on travel inspired fashion, look no further than this Tsumori Chisato jacket. It has a relaxed boxy design with short sleeves and double breasted front. The tropical inspired print features palm trees, volcanoes, and even the odd gecko. Team this jacket with a white tank top and jeans for a fun off-duty look.

## 7. Topshop Blue Rio Swimsuit

![Topshop Blue Rio Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_topshop-blue-rio-swimsuit_300x630.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TTpYih)
Pretend you’re in Rio de Janeiro next time you’re at your local beach with this printed swimsuit. It has a halterneck design with a cutout detail at the back. The front of the swimsuit shows a sweeping landscape of beaches, high rises, and blue skies. It’s the perfect swimsuit for a holiday getaway, when you’re not on holiday.

If you’ve got the travel bug but can’t take a trip just yet, look at some travel inspired fashion to satisfy a case of wanderlust. It can be something as simple as a bag to something more striking, like a printed dress. What do you think of these travel inspired fashions?
