By Lyndsie

Breasts come in all shapes and sizes, so you'd think it would be easier to find bras in all shapes and sizes, too, right? Yeah, not so much. If you're a busty girl, finding a comfortable, supportive bra that doesn't look like something your great-grandma would wear is difficult, to say the least. You either end up with quadri-boob – that is, your cups overflow – or you end up spilling side boob. You have even end up having the back and straps dig in painfully. Trying to fit large breasts into a bra that's even an inch too small is just a torturous nightmare. So, eff that. For all my busty, bigger breasted sisters, get the support you need. I got this.

Cosabella is all kinds of awesome. They've got tons of bras for big busted women, but I especially love the Sweetie Soft Bra – the lace is soft rather than scratchy, plus it's the perfect pick for anyone who hates underwire.

Balconette bras are so cute! This one, as you can see, is also cute, plus it offers full coverage and support.

Like, this bra makes me get a total ladyboner for my wife.
