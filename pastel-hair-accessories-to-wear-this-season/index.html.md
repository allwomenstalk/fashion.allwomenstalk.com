# 8 Pastel Hair Accessories to Wear This Season ...

**Pastel hair accessories** are a perfect way to keep your hair looking great and experiment with spring trends without having to spend a lot of cash on multiple wardrobe updates! Isn’t that fun? Well, take a look at these trendy pastel pieces then, and you’ll agree that “fun” is just one of many words to describe them:

## 1. Leatherette Rosette Headband

_ **Price:** $3.80 at forever21.com_
The first on my list of those tasty **pastel hair accessories you might want** to doll yourself up with this season is this cute, oh-so-romantic headband by Forever 21! Now, I can sure picture it paired up with a few basics I’m sure you have, color-blocked or even worn with other pastel pieces! How versatile is that!

## 2. Eric Javits Straw Cap

![Eric Javits Straw Cap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/2_eric-javits-straw-cap_350x242.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Pastels and neturals walk hand in hand this season, helping you to create a perfect daytime look! And since hot, sunny days are just a month away, you have at least one reason to allow spring trends into your wardrobe by purchasing something that goes great with just any summer outfit. Wear it with pastels in case you’re a true fashion savvy diva or combine with your favorite colors for a chic, unique look.

## 3. Juicy Couture Charm Elastic Bands

![Juicy Couture Charm Elastic Bands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/3_juicy-couture-charm-elastic-bands_350x182.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 at [zappos.com](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**These three trendy pastel pieces** will turn even the most basic ponytail into a super-stylish updo and enable you to complete the look of choice literally head to toe! Tiny golden charms give these sweet colored bands that “something-something”, enabling you to wear them on your wrist as well without the risk of ruining a perfect outfit. Now, I don’t know about you but I certainly appreciate that!

## 4. Blooming Floral Hair Clip

![Blooming Floral Hair Clip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/4_blooming-floral-hair-clip_350x354.jpg)

**Price:** $1.50 at [forever21.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.forever21.com/)
This lovely floral clip is one of the most budget-friendly **pastel hair accessories ever and** in case you’ve been considering adding mint to your list of must-wear colors, you can definitely start with this one. Combine it with a pastel belt of choice or a set of eye-catching bangles to freshen up your neutrals and be the belle of every ball.

## 5. Eugenia Kim Chiara Silk Headband

![Eugenia Kim Chiara Silk Headband](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/5_eugenia-kim-chiara-silk-headband_350x307.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/x1ze0K)
Oh-la-la! Now here’s something for all of you upscale fashionistas! But, seriously, isn’t this the **most posh-looking pastel hair accessory** you’ve seen? So Sex and The City 2 and, yes, I love the whole posh-me-up-in-tropics look! Pair it up with classy, white wide leg pants, a matching silk blouse and a few chunky pieces of jewelry and you’ll look like million bucks!

## 6. Triple Heart Slides

![Triple Heart Slides](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/6_triple-heart-slides_350x312.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/uQrvBA)
Tiny, budget-friendly, cute and absolutely trendy –this practical set will help you deal with your rebellious bangs, prevent hairstyle malfunctions and look fabulous this spring! Keep them in your bag in case of an emergency or work them into your look, pinning your bangs to create a simple yet sweet casual hairstyle.

## 7. Oversized Bow Clip

![Oversized Bow Clip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/7_oversized-bow-clip_350x117.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/D0rLOZ)
Oh my Gosh! I want this and I want it bad! The funny thing is that I actually have shoes to match and I bought them last year, before I even knew that having trendy pastel pieces will be a must. Matching shoes or not, I know you’ll love this eye-catching lilac bow so drop me a comment below and tell me what would you wear it with.

## 8. Plastic Bow Clip

![Plastic Bow Clip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/04/8_plastic-bow-clip_350x135.jpg)

**Price:** $12.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EpYZAy)
Speaking about pastel hair accessories – do you feel hungry? Because this adorable mint bow looks good enough to eat! I’d definitely wear it and I’m sure some of you ladies will like it as well! And if you feel like experimenting with such girly-girl colors, this is definitely a fool-proof way to accessorize your outfit and look like you’ve just hopped out of a magazine cover!

Which one of these pastel hair accessories do you love and can imagine yourself wearing? What about pastel in general? I do have to admit this is one of my favorite spring trends although I’m not really planning on buying a lot of big or pricy pieces. A couple of accessories, maybe a blouse – that’s it.
