# Chloe and Augustinus Bader Redefine Runway Beauty at Paris Fashion Week

**_March 6, 2025-A Collaboration of Science, Style, and Sophistication_**

Against the backdrop of **Paris Fashion Week**, where couture and innovation collide, **Chloé unveiled its Winter 2025 Ready-to-Wear collection**, radiating an air of effortless sophistication. This season’s presentation was elevated by a remarkable partnership-one that fused cutting-edge skincare with high fashion.

As the official skincare partner, **Augustinus Bader**, the award-winning brand celebrated for its scientific approach to skin, body, and hair care, took center stage backstage. Through a meticulously curated skincare regimen, Augustinus Bader ensured that every model walked the runway with **luminous, perfectly prepped skin**-the ultimate canvas for natural, **glowing beauty**.

But this was more than just a beauty ritual. It was a **harmonious collaboration between three pioneering brands**:

Together, they created a moment that transcended fashion-a **celebration of authenticity, self-care, and timeless radiance.**
