# 9 Furry Accessories to Add Texture to Your Outfit ...

Now is the time to don some season-appropriate furry accessories. They’re great for adding some texture to your outfits, as well as the added bonus of some extra warmth for these all too chilly months ahead. From faux fur collars to cosy Cossack hats, there are a number of furry options to check out. Take a look at these furry accessories that will add texture to your outfits this season.

## 1. Faux Fur Collar

![Faux Fur Collar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200934_1654_599x652.jpg)

Furry accessories like a faux fur collar are an easy way to add some texture to your outfits this season. Just pop one on over any jacket or coat to jazz up an ordinary collar. This short faux fur collar from Topshop will set you back around $50 and you can also find other versions available online at ASOS.

## 2. Faux Fur Bag

![Faux Fur Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200932_8653_406x298.jpg)

A furry bag or clutch is another way you can add some textural elements do your outfit. It’s still part of your overall look, but you don’t necessarily have to wear it on your body for the entire day. This black bag from Mango has a faux fur covering and comes with top handles and a detachable longer strap. If you aren’t too keen on the idea of an oversized tote then opt for a compact clutch or side bag instead. You can find a great range at Aldo, ASOS and Nasty Gal.

## 3. Faux Fur Cossack Hat

![Faux Fur Cossack Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200938_0805_599x425.jpg)

Top off your outfits with a faux fur Cossack hat. A blush colour would be great for adding a hint of pastel to your outfits. Wear it with an outfit of a long wool coat, skinny jeans, and mannish loafers for a chic winter outfit. You can grab one of these hats from Topshop for $44.

## 4. Faux Fur Pom Pom Scarf

![Faux Fur Pom Pom Scarf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200931_2794_599x870.jpg)

A chunky scarf with faux fur details is just another way you can add some texture to your outfits this season. Look out for cable knit scarves with faux fur details, like this grey pom pom scarf. Just pop it on over your favourite cold weather outfits for an instant update. This particular scarf is from Topshop but you can also check out Old Navy for other options.

## 5. Faux Fur Snood

![Faux Fur Snood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200934_5111_599x573.jpg)

Snoods are a pretty popular phenomenon and are great for cosying up in when the temperature drops. Wear a faux fur snood over a cardigan or knit sweater with jeans and a floppy fedora for a cool and casual daytime look. This black faux fur snood costs around $20 from Dorothy Perkins and you should be able to find other versions at department and fashion chain stores.

## 6. Faux Fur Pom Pom Beanie

![Faux Fur Pom Pom Beanie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/22043651_5035_597x559.jpg)

Keep your head nice and warm in a furry pom pom beanie. You’ll find them at a range of different retailers this season in all sorts of different styles and colours. This neutral beanie would be a versatile option that would work with anything from jeans and a sweater to a preppy skirt and coat. Check out ASOS and Topshop for a range of affordable faux fur pom pom beanies.

## 7. Faux Fur Hand Warmer

![Faux Fur Hand Warmer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200937_8104_599x720.jpg)

A furry hand warmer would be a practical accessory for winter. Slip it on over your neck and then use it to bury your hands in when they start to feel the chill. This faux fur hand warmer costs a cool $30 from Topshop and could easily become a wardrobe essential if you live in very cold climates.

## 8. Faux Fur Gloves

![Faux Fur Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200938_5459_599x349.jpg)

These faux fur gloves are made from a chunky cable knit and feature a glossy faux fur finish. They have a fingerless design (so you’re free to still use your touch screen phone) and come in an on-trend burgundy colour. Wear them peeking out from your coat sleeves for a subtle textural touch.

## 9. Faux Fur Ear Muffs

![Faux Fur Ear Muffs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/17200938_6971_599x714.jpg)

Keep your ears protected from the elements with furry ear muffs. These feature a slim head band and shaggy faux fur ear muffs. Just pop them on whenever you feel your ears might need protecting from the cold.

Furry accessories will not only add some textural elements to your outfit but they’ll also help stave off the cold. What do you think of these faux fur accessories? Would you wear any of these this winter?
