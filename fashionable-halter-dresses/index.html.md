# 9 Fashionable Halter Dresses ...

Halter dresses are usually associated with retro glam looks or more questionable eighties and nineties fashion. However, there are plenty of ways to wear halter dresses that will have you looking on-trend and fashionable right now. Whether you prefer glamorous gowns or sleek contemporary cuts, there’s a halter dress to suit all styles. Check out the following list of fashionable halter dresses.

## 1. Little Black Halter Dress

![Little Black Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/1_little-black-halter-dress_600x900.jpg)

Every girl needs a little black dress and this halter dress is a stylish option. Black halter dresses can be styled up or down, which makes them a versatile addition to any wardrobe. This Halston Heritage dress is quite expensive and will set you back a couple of hundred dollars but you can pick up more affordable dresses at Victoria’s Secret and ASOS.

## 2. Graphic Maxi Dress

![Graphic Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/2_graphic-maxi-dress_350x500.jpg)

A printed maxi dress is a great way to work luxe summer looks. This season choose one with a bright and bold graphic pattern. This graphic maxi dress from Modcloth would look great worn with metallic sandals, chunky cocktail rings, and a sleek clutch. At $175, however, it is on the pricey side. Luckily you can pick up equally stylish printed dresses at stores like Forever 21.

## 3. Leather Halter Dress

![Leather Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/3_leather-halter-dress_600x900.jpg)

Right now leather everything is in fashion. Stay on top of trends in a leather halter dress like this one by Alexander Wang. You could dress it up with heels and metallic cuffs or down with ankle boots and a slouchy jacket. Unfortunately, even on sale at The Outnet, it still fetches a price tag of $500. Check out ASOS for more affordable buys.

## 4. White Halter Dress

![White Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/4_white-halter-dress_350x500.jpg)

Channel your inner Marilyn Monroe in a white halter dress. This one from Modcloth features a sheer halter top and full lace skirt. It would look great teamed with some metallic heels and delicate jewellery. Peroxide blonde curls and beauty mole are optional! You could also check out J.Crew and Ruche for more styles of white halter dresses.

## 5. Check Halter Dress

![Check Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/5_check-halter-dress_452x645.jpg)

Check prints are on-trend right now. A breezy check print halter dress is a great way to wear this trend during the summer months. This Steven Alan dress has a relaxed fit and would be perfect to wear to a picnic. Also check out Modcloth for a range of cute check print halter dresses.

## 6. Retro Halter Dress

![Retro Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/6_retro-halter-dress_350x500.jpg)

Halter dresses can have a very retro feel to them. Take this retro looking dress from Modcloth, for example. The fitted bodice, thick straps, and full waist makes this dress look like it was plucked straight out of the 1950s. Wear it with T-bar shoes, a structured handbag, and a cropped cardigan to complete the retro look.

## 7. Halter Gown

![Halter Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/7_halter-gown_600x900.jpg)

For more formal occasions, try a halter neck gown. They’re a great way to work a glamorous look. Team them with statement drop earrings and an embellished clutch for a drop dead gorgeous look. You don’t have to spend a fortune on designer dresses like this silk Issa gown, either, with stores like ASOS stocking a range of stylish halter gowns at more affordable prices.

## 8. Pink Halter Dress

![Pink Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/8_pink-halter-dress_350x500.jpg)

All shades of pink are in fashion for the season. Step out in a pink halter dress to create a feminine look. Instead of a plunging halter neck, this Modcloth dress features a chic halter collar. I would look great teamed with cream shoes and cat eye sunglasses.

## 9. Fitted Halter Dress

![Fitted Halter Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/03/9_fitted-halter-dress_600x900.jpg)

If you’ve got the confidence, try a fitted halter dress on for size. Wear it for a night out on the town and accessorise with statement jewels and killer heels. Your typical Hervé Léger bandage dress will set you back hundreds of dollars but you should be able to find more affordable options from French Connection.

These are just some halter dresses worth checking out right now. You’ve pretty much got all bases covered with dresses ranging from retro-inspired looks to more contemporary trends. Which of these halter dresses do you like best?
