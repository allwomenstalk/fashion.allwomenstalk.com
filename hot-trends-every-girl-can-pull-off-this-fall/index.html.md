# 7 Hot Trends Every Girl Can Pull off This Fall ...

Every girl has her fall staples. Whether yours are plaid and scarves or ripped denim and graphic tees, you probably tend to follow a fall wardrobe uniform. I know personally that I do. This fall, though, there are so many trends that are taking over the fall fashion universe that it’s tough not to get caught up in some of them. If you’re looking for fall fashion trends you just can’t ignore, this is the list for you! It’s sure to provide you with a ton of fall style inspiration!

## 1. The 1970s

![The 1970s](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ft/k1/zzodyj1u55f916473e5bb583970329_600x900.jpg)

It seems like every season ushers in another decade for style, and this season is no different. This fall, the 1970s are a huge trend that is coming back this year. If you love the style of the 1970s, you’re in for a great treat this fall, because all of the 1970s fashion is back. If you love the earthy tones and different textures that embodied 1970s fashion, you’ll be so fashionable this fall!

## 2. Suede Mini Skirt

![Suede Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/wo/bvgkln1e55f91670ebd5b579383144_600x753.jpg)

Is it just me, or are suede mini skirts popping up in nearly every single Instagram lately? Taylor Swift has already put a picture up wearing one, which means that it’s only a matter of time before the entire world is wearing suede mini skirts. This is a fall trend that you simply cannot miss out on this fall!

## 3. Faux Fur Coat

![Faux Fur Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/e4/ox0e747l55f916998ebdc151720747_600x896.jpg)

It’s never trendy to wear real fur, but faux fur is a trend that you must rock this fall. Whether you’re wearing natural colors or outlandish colors, faux fur coats are dramatic and fun to wear, especially during the fall months when the weather can be pretty unpredictable.

## 4. Overalls

![Overalls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/d3/qz0xpomq55f916aded19d039729198_600x817.jpg)

Overalls were a huge trend this summer, and they’ve undoubtedly become a trend that shows no sign of slowing down as it becomes fall. Even though you may not be able to pair your overalls with your favorite crop top anymore, this is a still a trend that you can bring with you through fall.

## 5. Flare Jeans

![Flare Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/ek/wd0i9de055f916c1a9d13750605269_600x900.jpg)

I think we’re all pretty skeptical of the flare jeans trend. We’ve lived through and want to have no part in it again. We’re all Team Skinny Jeans now (yes, it’s a thing). That being said, flare jeans are back this year along with the 1970s trend, and if you can get over your Team Skinny Jeans allegiance even just a little, this is a trend that you’ll want to be rocking this fall.

## 6. Denim Skirts

![Denim Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/lv/p7lz937j55f916d845b77314282619_600x904.jpg)

Mini skirts are a huge trend this fall, if that wasn’t apparent from the sudden influx of suede mini skirts on social media. However, denim mini skirts are just as huge of a trend. Denim skirts are arguably one of the biggest fall trends this year, and one that we should all be flocking towards!

## 7. Leggings

![Leggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/p9/equymyqo55f9170182aac640519534_600x899.jpg)

Are you really a woman if you don’t love a good pair of leggings in the fall? Leggings are basically every woman’s favorite trend (it’s like basically wearing pajamas all day) but they’re especially a huge trend in the fall. Wear them to the cider mill for a comfortable and stylish day out this autumn!

Which trends are you obsessing over this fall? Have you tried any of these yet? Let me know in the comments!
