# 7 Versatile Dresses to Take You Effortlessly from Day to Night

Day to night dresses are a versatile addition to any wardrobe. Finding dresses that can transition from day to night is a smart way to get more bang for your buck. When looking at day to night dresses, stick to polished and pared down styles. Think simple sheath dresses or modern shifts. Making the transition from day to night should be as simple as switching your accessories or adding a dab of lipstick. Take a look at our pick of dresses that will take you from day to night.

| Feature | Day Look | Night Look |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Accessories | Minimalist jewelry | Statement necklace |
| Footwear | Flat shoes or sneakers | Strappy heels |
| Outerwear | Denim jacket or blazer | Sleek clutch |

## 1. The Plain Black Dress

![The Plain Black Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/1_the-plain-black-dress_600x900.jpg)

You don’t get much more versatile than the plain black dress. Wear your black day to night dresses with denim jackets and patent shoes during the day and dress them up at night with a statement necklace and bright lipstick. This [Topshop](https://www.topshop.com/) dress is a great starting point, offering a much more affordable option than what you’d be paying for similar dresses by designers like Theory. It's a staple for any [capsule wardrobe](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/style-tips-on-how-to-wear-the-little-black-dress/).

## 2. The Print Dress

![The Print Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/2_the-print-dress_600x815.jpg)

A bold black and white print is a versatile option for both day and night. This dress features an on-trend Deco-inspired print and comes in a simple shift design. Wear it during the day with a slouchy white blazer and gold jewelry. Then, at night, ditch the blazer and let the dress shine. Check out similar styles at [Miss Selfridge](https://www.missselfridge.com/) for affordable and chic options.

## 3. The Little White Dress

![The Little White Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/3_the-little-white-dress_600x900.jpg)

Another option for taking your outfit from day to night is with a structured little white dress. During the day it can look quite neat and polished, but add a bit of bold color with your accessories at night and you’ve got a whole new look. A white dress is perfect for showing off [summer trends](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/chic-ways-to-wear-white-this-summer/). Look for quality fabrics at retailers like [Zara](https://www.zara.com/) or [Mango](https://www.mango.com/).

## 4. The Lace Dress

![The Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/4_the-lace-dress_600x900.jpg)

Lace dresses often have a romantic feel to them. For the day, pair a lace dress with a military jacket or a leather biker jacket to toughen up the look. At night, let the lace do all the talking and keep your accessories minimal. This feminine style is always in fashion and can be found at [Anthropologie](https://www.anthropologie.com/).

## 5. The Midi Dress

![The Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/5_the-midi-dress_600x840.jpg)

The midi dress is a sophisticated choice for transitioning from the office to dinner. Its longer length makes it appropriate for professional settings when paired with a blazer, while the silhouette remains elegant for evening events. Try adding a [waist-cinching belt](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-wear-a-midi-dress/) to change the vibe. You can find beautiful midi options at [Nordstrom](https://www.nordstrom.com/).

## 6. The Maxi Dress

![The Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/6_the-maxi-dress_600x900.jpg)

Don't discount the maxi dress for day-to-night transitions. A jersey maxi dress is comfortable for running errands, but when paired with sparkly sandals and a clutch, it becomes an effortless evening ensemble. It's the ultimate in [boho-chic fashion](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/boho-chic-style-tips/). Visit [Free People](https://www.freepeople.com/) for more inspiration.

## 7. The Shirt Dress

![The Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/7_the-shirt-dress_600x900.jpg)

The shirt dress is perhaps the most practical of all day-to-night dresses. Wear it buttoned up with flats for a smart daytime look, and then unbutton the neckline and add some statement earrings for a night out. It's a truly timeless piece. Here are a few ways to style it:

- Pair with a tailored blazer for meetings.

- Swap flats for pointed-toe heels for dinner.

- Add a silk scarf for a touch of elegance.

Finding the right day-to-night dress is all about versatility and comfort. Which of these styles is your favorite?
