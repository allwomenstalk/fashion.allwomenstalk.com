# 7 Outstanding Printed Pants ...

Tailored trousers are great wardrobe staple. A pair of good fitting pants can take you through a range of occasions. If you want to change things up, try looking at pants with interesting prints. They’re a great way to add a unique twist to an outfit. Take inspiration from the following selection of outstanding printed pants.

## 1. Paul and Joe Poum Cat-Print Silk-Crepe Pants

![Paul and Joe Poum Cat-Print Silk-Crepe Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/a4/y82d6ua154c8f66e3acb9010433244_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $340.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/li68So)
These cat-print pants are such a bold design. From afar, it looks like your average abstract print, but closer inspection reveals numerous cat faces printed all over these pants. They are a tapered design and feature a textured silk-satin finish. Pair these pants with splashes of colour and faded denim for a street-smart look.

## 2. Topshop Blue Tulip Print Wide Leg Trousers

![Topshop Blue Tulip Print Wide Leg Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/db/h769mkes54c8f66f89df5616717160_334x498.jpg)

**Price:** $92.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/eGtAu1)
These floaty trousers are great for creating a sophisticated bohemian look. They have a wide leg design, fitted waist, and back zip fastening. The blue tulip print is dainty and not too fussy. Wear these pants with a simple tank top, floppy wide brimmed hat, and strappy wooden platforms.

## 3. IRO Arina Printed Crepe Pants

![IRO Arina Printed Crepe Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/rn/xo1m5lry54c8f670a0334652948952_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/MoHn5R)
These IRO pants are a sleek and sophisticated take on this trend. They are a relaxed fit and feature a pleated waistband, side slit pockets, and a concealed zip fastening at the side. They can easily be dressed up or down. Roll the cuffs up during the day for a casual look, and pair with some statement accessories and heels at night.

## 4. Emma Cook Floral Print Silk Crepe De Chine Pants

![Emma Cook Floral Print Silk Crepe De Chine Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/i9/nsbeqy7b54c8f671f326e086278405_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $405.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/gvCVn6)
These printed pants are a great way to work the floral trend. They are a nice change from all the floral dresses and skirts currently available. They have a tapered design and elasticised drawstring waist. Pair these pants with a floppy felt hat and loose fitting singlet during the day, and a slouchy blazer and sky high heels at night.

## 5. Sass & Bide the Kind Man Silk-Crepe Pants

![Sass & Bide the Kind Man Silk-Crepe Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/xb/o2f7cfy654c8f6732889d686667960_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $360.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/tyoANh)
What makes these pants a real stand out is the freestyle, hand-painted effect of the checked print. These textured silk-crepe pants features an elasticised waistband, elasticised cuffs, and patch pockets with a ruched trim. You can make the most of the monochromatic look by pairing the pants with black and white accessories, or go a bit wild with colourful accents.

## 6. Topshop Unique Black Silk Etchings Crop Trousers

![Topshop Unique Black Silk Etchings Crop Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/ck/u235lvlm54c8f674a37ac385154877_334x497.jpg)

**Price:** $245.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/17S2M3)
These silk trousers are quite luxe. That said, they may not be to everyone’s taste. They feature a black silk etching print, which is quite attention-grabbing. There is also a button and concealed zip on the left hand side. Due to the wide legged design, be sure to keep things fitted on top.

## 7. Topshop Snake Printed Tie-Waist Trousers

![Topshop Snake Printed Tie-Waist Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/co/evi4gjhj54c8f675d2f1d765711998_334x498.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/iUMWj4)
The snake print design of these pants is quite wild. These are quite a trend-based pair of pants. They are loose fitting and have a tapered design, ribbon waist tie, and contrast pockets. Pair these with a black low-back body suit, suede wedges, and metallic accessories.

Which of these pants do you think best exemplify the printed pant trend? If you’ve seen any interesting and outstanding printed pants lately, please feel free to share!
