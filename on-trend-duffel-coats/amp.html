8 on-Trend Duffel Coats ...

Miu Miu Wool Duffel Coat • Topshop Red Bound Seamed Duffel Coat • Vanessa Bruno Athe Chevron Duffel Coat • Sonia by Sonia Rykel Cashmere-Blend Duffle Coat • Topshop Wool Toggle Duffel Coat • More ...

By Sophia

Duffel Coats are a fashionable choice for the winter months. They are also sure to keep you warm when the temperature drops. From timeless navy to bright yellow colours, there’s a duffel coat to suit all tastes. Hooded duffel coats are an excellent choice for everyday outfits while those with faux fur trims and embellishments are suited for dressier, evening outfits. Take inspiration from a few on-trend duffel coats below.

1. Miu Miu Wool Duffel Coat

Price: $1809.00 at mytheresa.com
This wool duffel coat has a petite, elegant design. It comes in a light brown colour and features brown leather and toggle fastenings. It also has a hooded design. This designer coat will break the budget, but keep an eye out for more affordable versions in chain stores like Topshop and Urban outfitters.

2. Topshop Red Bound Seamed Duffel Coat

Price: $178.00 at us.topshop.com
A red coat is a great statement-maker. Red duffel coats are a great way of playing down the intensity of the hue. This duffel coat has a very relaxed design and features a detachable hood, toggle fastenings, and front flap pockets. Wear this thigh length coat with a cute shift dress, tights, lace-up shoes and a chunky knitted beanie.

3. Vanessa Bruno Athe Chevron Duffel Coat

Price: $660.00 at lagarconne.com
This coat is perfect for complementing an understated outfit. It has a funnel neck with hood and snap closure, which is essential for keeping warm. This coat has a slim, straight fit. It would look great worn with black skinny jeans and knee-high boots.

4. Sonia by Sonia Rykel Cashmere-Blend Duffle Coat

Price: $860.00 at net-a-porter.com
A bright yellow coat is a fun way to work some colour into your winter outfits. This preppy number has a curved collar, bracelet sleeves, and side slit pockets. It also has a button tab across the neck to keep the collar secure. Team this coat with indigo denim jeans, tan loafers and side satchel for a casually chic look.

5. Topshop Wool Toggle Duffel Coat

Price: $150.00 at us.topshop.com
Navy duffel coats are timeless. This Topshop coat features three wooden toggle fastenings, button closures at the neck, and front patch pockets. It also features a hood. This coat hits at the thigh and would make a great everyday coat.

6. Dorothy Perkins Navy Fur Hood Buckle Duffel Coat

Price: $111.00 at dorothyperkins.com
This navy duffel coat features faux fur trims on the hood. It comes in a classic navy colour and features buckle fastenings at the front. It also has front patch pockets. Wear this coat with a vintage inspired dress, tights, and knee-high boots.

7. Burberry Brit Hooded Duffel Coat

Price: $895.00 at net-a-porter.com
The Burberry brand is well known for their stylish take on outerwear. This short coat has fuses the best of classic and contemporary design. This hooded coat is made from black wool-felt, has leather toggles at the front, and is lined in the iconic Burberry check. Wear this coat with this season’s must-have coloured jeans, button-up shirt, and brogues.

8. Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Wool-Blend Duffel Coat

Price: $895.00 at net-a-porter.com
Duffel coats can also come in more sophisticated designs. This coat comes in a punchy red hue and features a draped handkerchief hem. It also features black leather tabs, a hood, and side pockets. Wear this over a LBD and with tights and heeled boots for a smart and sophisticated look.

Duffel coats are a tried and trusted choice of outerwear. A neutral or navy coat is great for accompanying a range of outfits, while brightly coloured duffel coats are ideal for making an impact. What do you think of these duffel coats?
