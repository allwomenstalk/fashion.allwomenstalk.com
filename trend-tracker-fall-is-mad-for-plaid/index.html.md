# Trend Tracker: Fall is Mad for Plaid ...

The awesome thing about plaid is that it can be worn as a "grunge" look or, when paired correctly, it can make for a polished and chic look. This year, celebrities are mad for plaid! Check out who's been wearing the trend lately:

## 1. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/sx/rkhyz3xv_600x900.jpg)

Emma paired a fit and flare plaid dress with a leather jacket, making her look super rocker-chic.

## 2. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pb/p5/pwohgafa_600x969.jpg)

Taylor perfects the preppy-chic look by pairing a plaid skirt with a long sleeve shirt and knee high socks!

## 3. Jessica Simpson

![Jessica Simpson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/gm/mwr02uby_600x799.jpg)

Jessica took a fashion risk by mixing and matching two different plaid patterns.

## 4. Chloe Moretz

![Chloe Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ak/js/a7lqhdth_585x921.jpg)

Chloe's plaid jacket is the perfect addition to any fall outfit! It's a must-have statement piece.

## 5. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ze/k3/tmavqc5i_550x765.jpg)

Grunge meets girly with Hailee's take on the plaid trend. She added some edge to her little black dress by tying a plaid shirt around her waist!
