# Instagram Accounts You Need to Follow during NYFW ...

NYFW is here and social media is buzzing. Out of all social media networks, though, Instagram is buzzing the most. We’re seeing pictures of some of the best designs, videos of some of the best runway shows, and even the best street style that came to play on the streets of NYC. If you’re looking to get your Fashion Week fix, look no further than the Instagram accounts on this list. There’s a little bit of everything on this list to make sure that you’re covered at this year’s NYFW!

## 1. Man Repeller

![Man Repeller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zc/bc/mk3pt2vf55f9f5d422d7e087122827_600x600.jpg)

[@manrepeller](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Leandra Medine made a name for herself with her blog, Man Repeller, and has since published a book and has been so successful that she’s up close with all of your favorite designers at NYFW. If you want a look at some of the best shows at NYFW, her Instagram is your ticket inside of them.

## 2. Anna Dello Russo

![Anna Dello Russo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/ls/bh5twxv655f9f5e98caae917207401_571x595.jpg)

[@anna_dello_russo](https://www.instagram.com/anna_dello_russo/)
Not only does Anna Dello Russo capture her own unique NYFW style, but she also does an amazing job capturing the runway trends. She even posts a ton of videos showing different models as they walk down the runway in the season’s hottest trends. She definitely has an account you can’t miss!

## 3. Patrick Janelle

![Patrick Janelle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/fc/y204fx7i55f9f61098f47592242111_600x600.jpg)

[@aguynamedpatrick](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
This guy has seemingly done almost everything, including winning Fashion Instagrammer of the Year at the CFDAs. His Instagram count reflects that, including shots from his regular, everyday life, but the NYFW shots are what will stop you in your tracks. He has such a unique way of catching everything that you’ll want to follow him well after Fashion Week.

## 4. Alexander Wang

![Alexander Wang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c7/kz/osuzhiyd55f9f62a1874d282844886_600x600.jpg)

[@alexanderwangny](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
If you want to see just how glamorous NYFW gets, Alexander Wang is the account to follow. His collection is easily one of the most anticipated collections of Fashion Week and this Instagram account does not disappoint. From who’s sitting on the sidelines watching the show (Nicki Minaj, anyone?) to the crazy after parties and performances (Lil Wayne may or may not have made an appearance) this is the Instagram account of everyone’s lavish NYFW dreams.

## 5. We Wore What

![We Wore What](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/nq/v3p9f20855f9f64218eff831115322_600x600.jpg)

[@weworewhat](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Danielle of We Wore What captures some of the coolest shots and videos from NYFW. My favorite? I couldn’t help but love the video from Tommy Hilfiger’s show that features all of the models walking around an island and then into a pool of water. It just doesn’t get cooler than that and Danielle is there to capture it!

## 6. Refinery 29 Fashion

![Refinery 29 Fashion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/rf/cs0ldyed55f9f661407c7633734121_600x600.jpg)

[@r29fashion](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
Refinery 29 is probably already your destination for just about everything, but their NYFW coverage on their fashion Instagram account makes it a can’t-miss. Refinery 29’s fashion account has the inside scoop on everything going on behind-the-scenes of Fashion Week, including a few snaps of some of your favorite models getting ready!

## 7. Tyler Joe

![Tyler Joe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/zm/wmy3npb955f9f68f3314c173682738_600x600.jpg)

[@nyavgjoe](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)
It’s really not every day that you see a guy photograph street style so well, but Tyler Joe does and his Instagram is perfect if you’re looking for some street style inspiration. He captures all of the most amazing looks that grace the streets of New York, especially during NYFW, when fashion completely takes over the city.

What Instagram accounts are you sure to follow during NYFW? Give me some suggestions in the comments!
