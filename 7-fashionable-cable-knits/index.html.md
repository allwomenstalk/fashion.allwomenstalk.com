# 7 Fashionable Cable Knits ...

Cable knits are enjoying a modern revival. While they may be at the height of “granny chic” that doesn’t necessarily mean that you have to style them that way. Pair a slouchy knit jumper with distressed denim shorts for a casual looks, or wear them with slim-fitting tailored trousers and heels for something more sophisticated. Take a look at some very fashionable cable knits below.

## 1. Topshop Knitted Apricot Cotton Cable Jumper

![Topshop Knitted Apricot Cotton Cable Jumper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xv/k3/dtdwa05454c8f9fbd7fbc284537955_327x392.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/N2Xcau)
This knitted cable jumper is a fresh take on a wardrobe classic. While classic fashionable cable knits tend to revolve around a neutral colour palette, this apricot knit is a pleasant alternative. One of the fun features of this knit are the baubles lined down the front. Take a preppy-chic approach to dressing and pair this jumper with tailored white shorts and tan loafers.

## 2. Doo.Ri Exaggerated Cable Cardigan

![Doo.Ri Exaggerated Cable Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sw/tc/uttysnru54c8f9fd185d4665933039_280x383.jpg)

**Price:** $895.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The exaggerated design of this cable knit cardigan makes it stand out from the crowd. The twisted cable knit detail down the front is the focal point of the cardigan. It also features a shawl collar, tortoiseshell buttons, and rib banding at the wrists and hip. This cardigan is design to be a relaxed fit, so balance it out with skinny jeans.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Sinead Cable Knit Cotton Sweater

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Sinead Cable Knit Cotton Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/vb/l0sgc5ut54c8f9fe35f4d357276684_287x393.jpg)

**Price:** $270.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/6dfhFE)
This cable knit sweater is as close to a classic design as you can get. It features a scoop neck, dolman sleeves, and ribbed trims. The cream colour can easily be teamed with a range of outfits. Experiment with textures and pair this sweater with a sheer burgundy maxi skirt, and top off the look with a floppy felt hat.

## 4. Ralph Lauren Black Label Cable Knit Cashmere Sweater

![Ralph Lauren Black Label Cable Knit Cashmere Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/w7/ktxexiyd54c8f9ff27707839171091_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $400.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Nsp59J)
Ralph Lauren gives the classic cable knit a modern twist this season. This vibrant orange sweater features a round neck, longs sleeves, and ribbed trims. It is made from 100% cashmere so will feel soft to the touch. This brightly coloured sweater will definitely give classic fashionable cable knits a run for their money.

## 5. Topshop Knitted Cable Yoke Crop Top

![Topshop Knitted Cable Yoke Crop Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/hb/vysgwnls54c8fa0030ec7858843106_369x392.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lycNXA)
This top is a different take on the cable knit trend. It is fitted and, I’m assuming, designed to be worn as a normal top. It features short sleeves, a wide neckline, and cable stitch detailing at the yoke. Wear this with an A-line denim skirt and layer on some long necklaces.

## 6. M.Patmos Cable Knit Neck Warmer

![M.Patmos Cable Knit Neck Warmer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/dy/prz7318v54c8fa014336a892153022_370x384.jpg)

**Price:** $82.50 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Fashionable cable knits are resigned to simple sweaters and cardigans. Take this knitted neck warmer, for example. This neck warmer has button fastenings throughout the front and comes in the sweetest pink colour. It would be perfect for layering over spring outfits, in case there’s an afternoon chill about.

## 7. Alexander Wang Burnout Cable Knit Tank

**Price:** $138.00 at [theoutnet.com](http://www.theoutnet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This burnout cable knit lace tank is a great option for the warmer months. I like that it’s a directional take on a classic. It features a scoop neck, racer back, and raw hems. It is quite sheer so wear a slip or bandeau underneath for modesty. Tuck this singlet into a full skirt and pair with a bright coloured blazer.

Cable knits come in a range of designs and can be styled in a myriad of ways. These were just an example of a few fashionable cable knits. Which of the above do you like best?
