# 9 Ways to Work the Lip Print Trend into Your Wardrobe ...

Designers this season are getting lippy and have been championing the lip print trend. It’s a quirky take on feminine dressing and one that’s proving to be popular. From flirty dresses to laid-back loafers, designers have been adorning a range of the latest fashions with luscious lip details. Get ready to pucker up with these lip print trend pieces.

## 1. Stella McCartney Appliqué Cotton Sweatshirt

![Stella McCartney Appliqué Cotton Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fd/aj/x1/owd8wtzj_599x706.jpg)

One of the most notable designers to embrace the lip print trend has been Stella McCartney. The latest collection from the designer consists of a range of tops and dresses that feature lip motifs. This sweatshirt is a simple yet effective take on the trend. It comes in a fresh white colour and features appliquéd embroidered lips. It has a relaxed style which makes it perfect for pairing with denim and flat shoes.

## 2. Monki Smile Lips Clutch

![Monki Smile Lips Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cl/m6/yy/zzvwljw9_599x348.jpg)

Sometimes one statement accessory is all you need to stay on-trend. This quirky clutch by Monki features a large lip design to the front. It is a generous sized clutch, which makes it an excellent choice for pairing with your casual daytime outfits.

## 3. Diane Von Furstenberg Lip Print Blazer

![Diane Von Furstenberg Lip Print Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vr/pi/sr/wwsif6cv_599x699.jpg)

This blazer by Diane von Furstenberg is a smart and stylish take on the lip print trend. It has a chic tailored cut and comes in an understated black and white lip print. You could wear it to smarten up a laid-back jeans and t-shirt combo or pair it with a fitted dress and heels for something more stylish.

## 4. Modcloth ‘First Kiss’ Dress

![Modcloth ‘First Kiss’ Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y3/re/gk/crbh0mcu_599x852.jpg)

This Modcloth dress comes in a sweet vintage-inspired design, complete with a Peter Pan collar. It comes in a standout red colour with a contrasting collar and all-over lip print. Play up the preppy, vintage feel of the dress and team it with a cropped cardigan and Oxford shoes.

## 5. Markus Lupfer Lips Stud Shoes

![Markus Lupfer Lips Stud Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/f5/wv/ha2tkgup_599x777.jpg)

Markus Lupfer’s latest collection to hit stores features a range of lip print pieces, from sweaters to slouchy track pants. These patent leather slip on shoes, however, will have you putting your best foot forward. They’re available in beige and black colours and feature fun 3D lip embellishments. Add a playful touch to a menswear-inspired look by teaming these shoes with a collared shirt and cigarette trousers.

## 6. The Limited Lip Print Blouse

![The Limited Lip Print Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/f4/b6/fh5ww9zm_600x783.jpg)

Many designer lip print pieces will set you back a pretty penny but this blouse from The Limited is a budget-friendly buy. It features an all-over graphic lip print and comes in a sheer style. Wear it with a leather-look skirt and ankle boots for a contemporary take on the trend.

## 7. Sonia Rykiel All over Lip Trouser

![Sonia Rykiel All over Lip Trouser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j1/im/hn/io4wyfw4_599x883.jpg)

Printed pants are still a popular trend and these Sonia Rykiel babies feature an all-over lip print. These silk trousers have a tailored design and come in a black and blue colourway. They’re chic and understated and would look great teamed with a plain white t-shirt and patent heels for a smart everyday look.

## 8. Charlotte Olympia Kiss Me Lips Pouch

![Charlotte Olympia Kiss Me Lips Pouch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/n8/zc/uqgiuqt0_599x419.jpg)

Charlotte Olympia is well known for her fun and frivolous designs. This pouch is no different and features pink lip appliqués on a striking red background. If you’re willing to splash the cash then this pouch would make a playful addition to any outfit.

## 9. Alice + Olivia Pout Stretch-Cotton and Tulle Dress

![Alice + Olivia Pout Stretch-Cotton and Tulle Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jm/so/rq/09903yx5_600x900.jpg)

This Alice + Olivia dress comes in a quirky, whimsical design. It features a fitted red bodice and full tulle skirt. Embroidered lip appliqués can be found at collar and all throughout the tulle skirt. Since it’s quite a busy dress, team it with simple accessories that won’t detract from the playful design.

These are just some lippy pieces that you could incorporate into your wardrobes. What do you think of the lip print trend? Will you be adding lippy details to your outfits this season?
