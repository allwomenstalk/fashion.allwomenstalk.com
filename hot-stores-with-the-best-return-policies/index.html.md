# Hot Stores with the Best Return Policies ...

You should be careful about what you buy. After all, you might want to return it. That's why you need to shop at stores with amazing return policies. According to [Seventeen](https://www.seventeen.com/fashion/a36837/stores-with-the-best-return-policies/), you should try these:

## 1. Nordstrom

![Nordstrom](https://img.allw.mn/content/lh/rn/e3ytsm4156829ed6e4614509852141_456x257.gif)

On [Nordstrom](https://www.nordstrom.com/browse/customer-service/return-policy), you don't need to return items in their original packaging. You don't even need a receipt and there's no time limit!

## 2. JC Penney

![black, white, black and white, photograph, image,](https://img.allw.mn/content/uf/mx/vix0ahkx5889780980863141768315_500x255.gif)

You do need the original packaging at [JC Penney](http://www.jcpenney.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), but you still don't need a receipt.

## 3. Zappos

![Zappos](https://img.allw.mn/content/wr/dg/l7bbdnmp56829ed885fb1350106687_500x273.gif)

You have an entire year to return items to [Zappos](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and online return shipping is free.

## 4. Macy's

![hair, hairstyle, singing, singer,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cv/y5/d1ujfvq458897809e8583561622370_500x251.gif)

You might end up getting store credit instead of cash, but [Macys](https://bit.ly/1KdAFr9) still has a great return policy.

## 5. Kohls

![eyewear, black, white, black and white, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ma/s7/c3cem2w05889780a06cda959352006_500x240.gif)

You need the original packaging at [Kohls](https://cs.kohls.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), but you don't need a receipt.

## 6. Target

![Target](https://img.allw.mn/content/yc/xm/e05turdz56829ed724046123157173_500x281.gif)

[Target](https://help.target.com/help/SubCategoryArticle?childcat=Return+policy&parentcat=Returns+%26+Exchanges) only gives you 90 days, but online return shipping is free.

## 7. Sephora

![Sephora](https://img.allw.mn/content/v4/hi/h143w95l56829ed76ded4526215232_487x280.gif)

[Sephora](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) has a 60 day return policy where you don't need a receipt or the original packaging.

## 8. Walmart

![color, pink, clothing, red, dress,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x8/im/rrcf7kut588b9c478a7d3194713444_500x281.gif)

[Walmart](https://www.Walmart.com) offers a generous 90 day return policy, and although it needs to be returned in its original packaging you don't need to bring a receipt with you. Online returning shipping is not free.

## 9. Amazon

_ Amazon_ has a 30 day return policy. Like Walmart, you must return it in its original packaging, however you won't need to bring your receipt. Online returning shipping is not free.

## 10. T.J. Maxx

![human action, hair, person, hairstyle, finger,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u9/sf/f6kq1x7d588b9dd38948d516313290_244x244.gif)

[TJMaxx](https://tjmaxx.tjx.com/store/index.jsp) offers a 30 day return policy where don't need a receipt but must the original packaging is required. It may also be worth noting that online returning shipping is not free.
