# Summer Fashion: Invest in a Vest ...

A vest is a versatile fashion piece. You can wear it for many occasions and it’s one of the great pieces for transitioning between the seasons. A vest is ideal for summer – perfect for when you don’t want the full coverage of a blazer. Whether you choose denim or a different fabric, you might need some ideas for ways to style and wear your vest. Ready for some ideas?
