# 7 Gorgeously Lovely Grey Cardigans for Fall ...

Grey cardigans have become a staple in my wardrobe this fall, sneakily replacing my black ones from last fall before I really even realized it. They’re so versatile, and can be worn with so many other colors! If you’re looking for a way to update your fall wardrobe with a few pretty grey cardigans, keep reading! Here are 7 gorgeously lovely grey cardigans for fall…

## 1. Empyreal Cardigan

![Empyreal Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/in/nv8n612k54c932b92ada1265542036_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $148.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I can’t say enough about how much I love this soft, lush-looking grey cardigan by Moth. Doesn’t it just look so incredibly soft? It’s made of wool and angora with a single button at the collar, off to one side. I’d wear this with tweedy shorts, brown tights, and chunky ankle boots.

## 2. Time Signature Cardigan

**Price:** $148.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This grey cardigan by Leifsdottir is kind of a two-for-one, with an open-knit scarf attached as the collar. I love the rich violet-grey color and the fact that it’s so light-weight - some of our fall days are still warm, so we don’t want to wear bulky sweaters all the time!

## 3. Tetragon Cardigan

![Tetragon Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tf/rg/rvm5va6654c932bb3e6c2236916334_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $79.95 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If I had the time or the know-how, I’d knit one of these myself, all made of tiny squares. But the reduced price is right, and the hand-stitching looks spot-on! Wouldn’t this look pretty with a creamy top and a pair of dark skinny jeans?

## 4. On the Ropes Cardigan

![On the Ropes Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/m0/lwrrqao854c932bc4a2ad849590568_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $88.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This drapey grey cardigan by Tiny is so sweet and ladylike I can’t wait to wear it with some of my fall dresses! The cable-knit detail on the collar adds a fall touch, and the soft dove-grey color is versatile enough to wear with almost anything in your closet (though I’m thinking pink!).

## 5. Felted Colorbar Cardigan

![Felted Colorbar Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/qk/w4yw67si54c932bd3e75e136270603_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $118.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love the combination of yellow and grey in this cardigan! I also love the shape, and the little pockets on the front. The way they show it worn, over a dress, is darling… such inspiration!

## 6. Loose Stripes Cardigan

![Loose Stripes Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/cl/bew36wib54c932be3855b502085059_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $79.95 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cardigan looks just as gorgeous in ivory and dark grey as it does in rusty orange and light grey - I can’t decide which I like better! Maybe the orange and grey, since it does mimic the fall leaves we all love…

## 7. Heathered Hillside Cardigan

![Heathered Hillside Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/y4/zx57y1m354c932bf5395d669631321_335x500.jpg)

**Price:** $248.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Is it a grey jacket or a grey cardigan, or somehow is it both? It reminds me of the sweater my mother used to wear all throughout fall when I was little, with the same cozy shawl collar, button front, and graphic motif.

With so many gorgeous grey cardigans to choose from this fall, it’s not going to be easy to pick just one… but try… or at least narrow it down to two or three! I especially adore the orange and grey striped one… the colors are so gorgeous! Which one of these grey cardigans do you like best, and how would you wear it? Please tell!
