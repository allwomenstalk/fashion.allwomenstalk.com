# 7 Chic Skater Dresses ...

Skater Dresses have such a great shape to them. Skater dresses are typically fitted throughout the top and have a flared skirt. The best part about this trend this season is that it is open for interpretation. You can easily choose a dress that best suits your personal style - be it with colour, patterns, or textures. These styles of dresses are usually shorter in length, so wear them with opaque tights if needed. Take a look at a few chic skater dresses below.

## 1. Thakoon High Collar Dress

_ **Price:** $890.00 at barneys.com_
This little black dress would make a great party number. It has a high collar, cinched waist, and pleated nylon skirt. It also has a sheer back panel. The overall effect of the high collar is quite dramatic. Wear this dress with black opaque tights and wedge heels for a modern, streamlined look.

## 2. Tibi Textured-Felt Panelled Dress

![Tibi Textured-Felt Panelled Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/ei/cfapg1vu54c9311d9d32a017650677_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $485.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/TJExK7)
This textured-felt dress is perfect for fall. It comes in a rich burgundy colour and has a warm textured finish. It features panelling through the front and back and has a full skirt. It also has a V-shaped back. Let the burgundy colour speak for itself and team this dress with fairly neutral accessories.

## 3. Topshop Structured Sleeveless Skater Dress

![Topshop Structured Sleeveless Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/od/oc/ij3l66on54c9311e89fcc631325103_334x497.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/elH7r4)
If colour is what you’re after this season then look for brightly coloured skater dresses. This violet coloured dress has a fairly structured silhouette. It has a high neckline, heavy pleated skirt, and back zip fastening. Try your hand at colour blocking and team this dress with jade coloured shoes or accessories.

## 4. Giulietta Silk-Blend Crepe Dress

![Giulietta Silk-Blend Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/gm/jtufyx3454c9311f64a68704432332_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1415.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/WpYZWQ)
A red dress is a must this season. This silk-blend dress is an elegant way to incorporate the trend into your wardrobe. It has a stand collar, cut-out neck, and three-quarter length sleeves. Unlike other shorter skater dresses, this dress falls to the knee. Wear it with closed-in pumps and a framed bag for a completely feminine look.

## 5. Dorothy Perkins Mustard Crepe Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Mustard Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tl/k3/a2yhdtig54c9312057994665231473_368x500.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s fair to say that mustard is not a colour for everyone. If you think that you can rock the mustard dress this season, then this dress is one to try out. To offset the shockingly bright colour, this dress has a demure design. It has a high boatneck, three quarter sleeves, and full skirt. It also has an exposed zip fastening at the back.

## 6. Topshop Fit and Flare Tunic

![Topshop Fit and Flare Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/z5/oncg5m1854c9312135d2f632707234_334x497.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/AJXxt0)
Simple skater dresses such as this one make great layering pieces. This dress has a scoop neck and back, fitted bodice, and flared skirt. It comes in a rich ‘oxblood’ colour which is perfect for fall. Try teaming this dress with an open denim shirt, chunky knit cardigan, textured tights and ankle boots.

## 7. Dorothy Perkins Dark Red Lace Skater Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Dark Red Lace Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/mv/bm8am0jv54c9312237e28334953449_368x500.jpg)

**Price:** $47.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The lace overlay on this dress adds a textural twist to a traditionally streamlined look. This red lace dress is a great alternative to those skater dresses with a more fuss-free finish. This dress has a high round neckline, panelled front, and full skirt. For an elegant evening look, wear this dress with a cream blazer and Mary-Jane style heels.

Skater dresses can be found in a range of styles and colours. This season, team your skater dresses with cropped jackets for a chic, understated look. Simple skater dresses can be styled in a multitude of ways, while statement-making dresses speak for themselves. Which of these skater dresses best suits your style?
