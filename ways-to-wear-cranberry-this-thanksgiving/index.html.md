# 17 Ways to Wear Cranberry This Thanksgiving ...

Thanksgiving is a great time to bust out the statement pieces and wear them to dinner. What better way to make a statement than with cranberry colored fashion pieces?! Check out these unique ways to wear cranberry this Thanksgiving!

## 1. CRANBERRY EYESHADOW

Eyes speak volumes, so why not accentuate your eyes with a pop of cranberry color?!

## 2. CRANBERRY BOOTS

Boots were made for walking, but these bad boys are show-stopping!

## 3. CRANBERRY NAILS

Bring some flair to the table by sporting cranberry inspired nails! Perfect for any holiday!

## 4. CRANBERRY BRACELETS

Cranberry colored statement bracelets are a great way to tie together the overall Thanksgiving look!

## 5. CRANBERRY DRESS

Make a statement by wearing a bold cranberry dress and partnering it with nude/white accessories.

## 6. CRANBERRY LIPS

Another great way to wear cranberry is via the lips! Tie in your overall look with luscious lips!

## 7. CRANBERRY NECKLACE

Why not go big and wear a gorgeous cranberry necklace statement piece during Thanksgiving?!

## 8. CRANBERRY EARRINGS

Make heads turn by wearing gleaming, cranberry colored earrings!

## 9. CRANBERRY HAIR

Nothing screams Thanksgiving louder than cranberry inspired hair!

## 10. CRANBERRY HEELS

Rock these cranberry colored heels and it will look like you've just stepped off the runway!

## 11. CRANBERRY PURSE

If you're wearing cranberry colored jewelry, what better way to tie in the look than with a cranberry colored purse!

## 12. CRANBERRY GLASSES

If you have glasses like I do, it kind of makes it hard to tie a look together. But not for long- why not sport cranberry colored rims?!

## 13. CRANBERRY SUIT

Your arm candy or dinner date has to be matching too! Make sure your date is rocking a cranberry inspired outfit!

## 14. CRANBERRY SOCKS

A great way to make a color pop is by wearing it with black, white, or brown.

## 15. CRANBERRY HAIR CLIP

Amp up your hairstyle by adding a cranberry inspired hair clip for pizzazz!

## 16. CRANBERRY SCARF

Stay warm and fashionable with a cranberry colored scarf! Tie it into any outfit for the season!

## 17. CRANBERRY FEDORA

Fedoras give a great accent to an overall outfit, so what's better than rocking it in a cranberry hue?!
