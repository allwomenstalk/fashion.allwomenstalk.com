# 8 Worst  Fashion Trends That Need to Go in 2017 ...

These are the trends that I personally have never liked and they shouldn't be in existence anymore. It's time to move forward and maybe then we can come up with trends for 2017 that actually work and that deserve to stick around.

## 1. OVERALLS

![jeans, denim, clothing, blue, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/dh/ca72i2am596e64048d4aa822315665_600x900.jpg)

Ladies we are not in the 90's anymore. There's a reason this wasn't brought back until now. Now we gotta kill this awful trend once and for all.

## 2. SCRUNCHIES

![hair, beauty, hairstyle, human hair color, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vr/u6/fpmcidsg596ede9f68f88091516055_600x900.jpg)

Scrunchies are more 80's but they did exist in the 90's and early 2000's. I haven't worn a scrunchie since I was 5. Ladies it's not cute anymore. It's only cute if you're 5!

## 3. JESUS SANDaLS

![jeans, denim, clothing, footwear, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/sg/ldsswd3c596e6637decd0520123560_600x955.jpg)

It is beyond me to why people think these sandals are cute. Jesus shoes are a no no!

## 4. BODYSUITS

![clothing, blazer, jeans, shoulder, snapshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/k4/ujicp6ab596e64bc4eda4418538272_600x900.jpg)

Oh hell no! These look like button ups for babies. Also they look like leotards and one piece swimsuits. Just no!

## 5. NON PreSCRIPTION GLASSES

![hair, eyewear, human hair color, hairstyle, sunglasses,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hm/mg/dtq2b3jx596e652ab82cb859088819_600x640.jpg)

The only reason you should be wearing glasses is if you actually need them. Just stop okay.

## 6. BOWLING SHOES

![red, coat, fashion, fashion model, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/k6/a5jfaqzh596e666a9f98c997744738_600x900.jpg)

I hate the whole fashionable bowling shoe look. Bowling shoes are meant for bowling and shouldn't be worn as an every day thing. That's just my opinion.

## 7. TIGHTS as PANTS

![leggings, tights, shoulder, fashion model, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b9/vd/u8gccpbs596e65894be89536663275_427x640.jpg)

Tights aren't meant to be worn all by themselves. Buy yourself some jeans, hon.

## 8. HAMMER PANTS

![photograph, clothing, standing, outerwear, suit,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/ya/ucfaejbk596edf497eeb6306949641_600x900.jpg)

It's not "Hammer Time" anymore. Hammer pants aren't cute and it makes you look like you're wearing a diaper. Just wear regular sweatpants that actually look good.
