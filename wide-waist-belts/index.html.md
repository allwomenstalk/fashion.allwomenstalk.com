# 8 Wide Waist Belts ...

Waist belts have become somewhat of a wardrobe staple. They’re perfect for cinching in a range of dresses and voluminous cardigans. Even though the recent trend has been gravitating towards skinny belts, there’s still a place for wide waist belts. Below are a few wide waist belts to take inspiration from.

**Price:** $64.63 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/9by4qU)
This wide waist belt comes in a soft pastel pink colour. It is made from leather and features a large brushed gold metal buckle at the centre. It is available in two sizes: S/M and M/L. This belt would look great worn with a neutral coloured sheer, pleated skirt.

## 2. Moschino Polka-Dot Silk and Leather Waist Belt

![Moschino Polka-Dot Silk and Leather Waist Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/zi/tsr3dyb954c905c2776cb098008118_318x372.jpg)

**Price:** $345.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4omwzn)
This wide belt features a playful polka-dot pattern. It is made from leather and features a silk overlay. The colour combination of navy and cream is timeless. This belt has a belt loop, gold rivets, and an adjustable buckle fastening. Take a cue from the runways and experiment with some pattern clashing by teaming this belt with a striped shirt dress.

## 3. Tan Leather Wide Double Buckle Belt

![Tan Leather Wide Double Buckle Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/bs/y237bxen54c905c35f700075395885_400x137.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/QEjQDE)
Tan leather wide waist belts are somewhat of a wardrobe staple. They work with a range of colour palettes and can dress an outfit up or down. This one features unique looking cross-over straps at the front. It has a double buckle fastening and comes in small, medium, and large sizes.

## 4. Forever21 Open Knit Belt

![Forever21 Open Knit Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/uw/ips8w6h054c905c4550db187189613_400x172.jpg)

## 5. Topshop Cream Leather Eyelet Belt

![Topshop Cream Leather Eyelet Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/f9/wxe4dg0a54c905c53a5c1111189994_400x174.jpg)

This belt has a certain gladiatorial feel to it. It curves at the front so the widest point of the belt is at the centre. It has an eyelet studded leather trim, stud press fastening, and elastic back. Use this belt to cinch in a draped, Grecian-style maxi dress.

## 6. Sophie Hulme Armoured Wide Belt

![Sophie Hulme Armoured Wide Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/lg/nonw9s0854c905c6474d7896010398_400x120.jpg)

![Pieces Virabello Wide Buckle Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/ni/hi38lvqi54c905c7137b6782258148_400x152.jpg)
