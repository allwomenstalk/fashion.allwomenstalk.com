# 8 Magnificent Mint Green Fashions ...

**Mint green** has to be one of the hottest colours right now. The look is perfectly fresh for spring, but it also can brighten up a basic winter outfit. Mint green clothes such as Acne’s cable knit sweater have already become cult favourites. If mint green clothes aren’t your thing, you can always work the look with a range of wonderful mint green accessories. Take a look at just some examples of this season’s must have mint green fashions below.

## 1. Acne Mint Green Cable Knit Sweater

![Acne Mint Green Cable Knit Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_acne-mint-green-cable-knit-sweater_306x400.jpg)

**Price:** $320.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/FEpcdy)
A chunky mint knit is just one way to wear this trend now. This Acne number has proven to be quite popular. While this one is quite pricey, you should be able to find more affordable options from places like Topshop and ASOS. A mint sweater would look great teamed with neutral cigarette style pants and cutaway pumps.

## 2. Ray Ban Mint Sunglasses

_ **Price:** $159.00 at mytheresa.com_
Coloured sunglasses are a fun addition to any outfit. These Ray Ban sunglasses have a pale mint frame and green gradient lenses. They also have silver toned metal handles and feature a designer logo on one of the lenses.

## 3. Topshop Mint Green Holdall

![Topshop Mint Green Holdall](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_topshop-mint-green-holdall_350x375.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pgOPOq)
**Mint green** accessories such as this bag make for stylish trend-based pieces. It has a simple rectangular design with grab-top handles, and comes in a pale mint green colour. It has a zip top fastening and single interior compartment. Work a fun, casual look and team this bag with peach jeans and a bold polka-dot sweater.

## 4. Miu Miu Suede Pumps

![Miu Miu Suede Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_miu-miu-suede-pumps_350x300.jpg)

**Price:** $580.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/pbDVir)
These Miu Miu pumps would make a stylish addition to any wardrobe. They have a classic cut and come in an on-trend mint hue. These shoes would look great with either a neutral colour palette or some colourful prints.

## 5. Acne Mint Crepe Shorts

![Acne Mint Crepe Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_acne-mint-crepe-shorts_350x304.jpg)

**Price:** $210.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PBVFS2)
**Mint green** seems to be a big part of Acne’s latest offerings this season. These mint shorts would be perfect come summer. They have an elastic waistband, pockets at the front and back, and have a relaxed fit. These would look simply stylish paired with a plain white t-shirt.

## 6. ASOS across Body Bag

![ASOS across Body Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_asos-across-body-bag_350x259.jpg)

**Price:** $35.81 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/ipCTBT).
This **mint green** bag is a stylish and affordable option. Bird motifs are another key trend this season, and this bag features a gold tone swallow motif at the front. This bag would look great worn with a white sundress and pastel pink blazer.

## 7. Topshop Mint Green Ballet Pumps

![Topshop Mint Green Ballet Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/7_topshop-mint-green-ballet-pumps_350x182.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/H5jSJD)
**Mint green** clothes aren’t the only way to wear this colour. These mint ballet flats are great for incorporating into your everyday outfit. Team them with a neutral pleated skirt and slouchy knit to complete the ballet-inspired aesthetic.

## 8. ASOS Flat Leather Sandals

![ASOS Flat Leather Sandals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/8_asos-flat-leather-sandals_350x205.jpg)

**Price:** $57.30 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/O3sRqP)
If you only want to add a hint of **mint green** to your outfit, then try these leather sandals come summer. They have mint green straps at the toes, and a mint green panel at the heel. They also feature contrasting elastic straps at the ankles. These would look great worn with a pair of coloured jeans.

There are a range of **mint green** fashions to choose from this season. Whether you opt for a must-have cable knit or more subtle mint green accessories such as shoes and bags, you’ve got to try out this colour. Will you be wearing mint green this season?
