# 7 Amazing Pairs of over the Knee Boots for Fall\-Winter 2012 ...

Over the knee boots are a great addition to your fall-winter 2012 wardrobes. Over the knee boots look great with pants or dresses, they can be casual or dressy, and they are a great alternative to your traditional tall fall-winter boots! Here are my picks for the 7 most amazing pairs of over the knee boots for fall and winter 2012.

## 1. Jeffrey Campbell Studded Joe Tall Boot

![Jeffrey Campbell Studded Joe Tall Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/1_jeffrey-campbell-studded-joe-tall-boot_356x475.jpg)

**Price:** $398.00 at [freepeople.com](http://www.freepeople.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Jeffrey Campbell always has the most amazing shoes, and these over the knee boots are no exception! This black, distressed leather boot has spiked studs, an inside zipper, laces up the front, and a wooden heel. These are perfect to pair with a mid-length skirt with leggings for fall-winter 2012.

## 2. Bed Stu Ankle Strap Boot

![Bed Stu Ankle Strap Boot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/2_bed-stu-ankle-strap-boot_600x824.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [urbanoutfitters.com](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These gorgeous chocolate riding boots are very versatile. They are slightly taller in the front to cover the knee and have a buckled ankle strap harness detail. The low heel makes them comfortable and great for days when you have to walk a long way but still want to look cute. Pair them with skinny jeans and a jacket for a cute and casual look this season.

## 3. Lateral Zip Riding Boots

![Lateral Zip Riding Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/3_lateral-zip-riding-boots_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $268.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These sleek, black leather riding boots can be dressed up or down to go with any outfit. The 3 inch leather wrapped heal adds just enough height without making them uncomfortable. These over the knee boots are understated but classic.

## 4. Emi Thigh High Boot in Wine Velvet

![Emi Thigh High Boot in Wine Velvet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/4_emi-thigh-high-boot-in-wine-velvet_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $198.00 at [nastygal.com](http://www.nastygal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This pair of '90s-inspired, wine colored, velvet boots are thigh high and definitely statement-making! They have a black platform heel and a side zipper to make them easier to put on. Wear these gorgeous boots with a black mini dress for a night out on the town!

## 5. Christian Louboutin Sea.NN Girl Boot

_ **Price:** $3,795.00 at barneys.com_
These over the knee boots don't just look good, they have a purpose! They are equipped with a side pocket with a snap enclosure, 3 card slots on the inside, 3 pen holders, and a slot for lipstick. This show stopping boot has a 4 inch heel, adjustable buckle straps at the knee, and Louboutin’s iconic red leather sole.

## 6. Manolo Blahnik Brunchileehi Boot

_ **Price:** $1,695.00 at barneys.com_
I’m always a fan of a slouchy pair of boots, and this pair just might be my favorite! This pair of gorgeous red, suede boots has a pointed toe and covered heels. The beautiful red will pair perfectly with a [simple black dress](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/how-to-style-your-little-black-dress-different-ways/) to add some color to your look.

## 7. Pura Lopez Wedge Knee High Booties

_ **Price:** $695.00 at intermixonline.com_
These over the knee boots are the perfect combination of wedges and boots! The platform, wedge heel will lengthen your legs and make you stand tall. They come in a beautiful gray suede that is ideal for fall-winter 2012. I love the tone on tone gray suede with gray stitching for a monochromatic look.
