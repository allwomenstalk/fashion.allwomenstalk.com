# 17 Fab Fashion Hacks for Girls with a Big Bust ...

I have big boobs. They are hot, they get in my way and yet, I still love them. They truly are breathtaking but dressing the girls up? It can be hard! I can't be the only one that is suffering from too much boob right? Well, for all you big-chested girls, I”ve got the best tips in the world to dress you and the girls up for a night on the town – or even just a night crossing over campus.

## 1. Get the Right Fit

![Get the Right Fit](https://img.allw.mn/content/mi/re/sg2lnhmp5775d49c5def1700707368_500x281.gif)

This is so, so important! Seriously girls, you can't not have the right fit, it'll make the girls sad.

## 2. No Turtlenecks for You!

![No Turtlenecks for You!](https://img.allw.mn/content/k5/l0/mmv4gy0y5775d49c8f3f4201819553_500x280.gif)

Yes, it's sad, but your boobs will thank you. And you'll look fab and stylish too.

## 3. Avoid Polo Necks Too

![Avoid Polo Necks Too](https://img.allw.mn/content/c8/xt/qb4jp3pj5775d49cbae00684278531_500x261.gif)

So, let's talk about polo necks – those are not your friends either.

## 4. Go for Lower Necklines

![Go for Lower Necklines](https://img.allw.mn/content/bd/ky/satw20825775d49dab251885774401_500x281.gif)

Good news? Lower necklines are better, v-necks are your friends especially!

## 5. Avoid Chunky Knits

![clothing, model,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hf/kw/mlvc7g4j5775d49e8ced4449863847_500x269.gif)

They just make your boobs look even bigger than they are.

## 6. … and Heavy Sweaters

![… and Heavy Sweaters](https://img.allw.mn/content/re/mr/o2ounzfo5775d49f92478744431137_500x260.gif)

Ditto for heavy sweaters.

## 7. Clever Tailoring Makes Such a Difference

![clothing, dancer, showgirl, headgear, She,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ly/uz/nczc499c5775d4a142efb217012155_500x268.gif)

So not only do you have to have the right fit, but you've got to get it all tailored. Expensive but worth it.

## 8. Little Belts around the Highest Part of Your Waist Look Awesome

![Little Belts around the Highest Part of Your Waist Look Awesome](https://img.allw.mn/content/wn/hf/si87ybie5775d4a1ba641478534996_500x281.gif)

Oh yes, do a skinny belt right around the upper part of your waist for an instant hourglass figure.

## 9. Stay Away from Elaborate Detailing

![Stay Away from Elaborate Detailing](https://img.allw.mn/content/od/g7/lljza63l5775d4a296db7009492077_500x252.gif)

You don't need it! Especially if the elaborate detailing is around your cleavage.

## 10. Skinny Jeans + Heels Look Amazing

![Skinny Jeans + Heels Look Amazing](https://img.allw.mn/content/r3/qo/ln0s12gp5775d4a41e07a120522699_540x308.gif)

Going out? Toss on a pair of skinny jeans and some heels!

## 11. High-waisted Jeans Don't Really Work

![High-waisted Jeans Don't Really Work](https://img.allw.mn/content/r5/mc/mv3b1mmo5775d4a4afc71087226053_500x281.gif)

Yep! Draw attention away from your boobs.

## 12. Classic Shirts Are a Wardrobe Staple

![Classic Shirts Are a Wardrobe Staple](https://img.allw.mn/content/t1/v7/mc0uxfu75775d4a6d4d15820299149_480x319.gif)

Oh, I love classic shirts!

## 13. Make Sure Your Bra Fits Well

![Make Sure Your Bra Fits Well](https://img.allw.mn/content/pv/bn/nkorh7775775d4a6c0edd555879582_500x266.gif)

The bra fitting is important too! Almost more so than any other fitting.

## 14. Shift Dresses Are Incredible

![Shift Dresses Are Incredible](https://img.allw.mn/content/z5/eo/r8m6r4rf5775d4a52b062632038568_500x240.gif)

Loveeee these dresses! So fun and everyone sells them!

## 15. Wrap It up

![Wrap It up](https://img.allw.mn/content/j3/go/smu1w3nr5775d4a636e82586283546_480x263.gif)

Not a shift dress person? That's okay, wrap dresses are breathtaking.

## 16. No Halters for You

![No Halters for You](https://img.allw.mn/content/yw/w4/hycvim245775d4a699059954817248_500x250.gif)

Yep, sorry, you've got to avoid these ones too.

## 17. Sleeve Length Matters

![Sleeve Length Matters](https://img.allw.mn/content/go/d4/jp8am73s5775d4a683cac713244353_500x281.gif)

Finally, the sleeve length is something that really matters when you are dressing up the girls.
