# Turn Your Ugly Christmas Sweater into an Adorable One ...

It’s finally Ugly Christmas Sweater season again! If you’re into ugly Christmas sweaters, whether you’re going to a party or just wearing them out and about, this is the season that’s all about your favorite style! A lot of people worry that when they’re wearing ugly Christmas sweaters, though, that their style looks a lot less cute and chic. That can easily be combatted with these simple tips and tricks. It seems daunting to try to style a piece that’s purposely ugly, but don’t worry, these tips will be simple and completely turn an ugly outfit into a cute one!

## 1. Go All out with the Ugly

If you’re going to wear an ugly Christmas sweater, you really have to go all out with it. The uglier it is, the more intentional the look appears. Think about it, you don’t want to wear an ugly sweater that people think is actually your taste! Talk about a fashion faux pas! Don’t be afraid to go for it!

## 2. Pair It with Leggings

You really can’t go wrong with an ugly Christmas sweater when you’re wearing it with a pair of leggings. The leggings add a more modern touch to the look, and you’ll be super comfortable! No matter what you’re going to or what you’re wearing your ugly Christmas sweater, you can’t go wrong with a pair of leggings.

## 3. Wear It with a Sequined Skirt

A lot of people are worried when going to an Ugly Christmas Sweater Party that they won’t be dressed up for a holiday party. That can easily be fixed by wearing a sequined skirt. It instantly makes your Christmas sweater perfect for any party you may have to go to!

## 4. Or Just Wear It as a Dress

If your ugly Christmas sweater is oversized or just really long, wear it as a dress with a pair of knee socks or even just a pair of boots. It’s perfect for a party where you’re allowed to get a little crazier! It’s such an easy look, too! It doesn’t take much to make an ugly Christmas sweater adorable!

## 5. Personalize It to Your Style

If you’re a grunge girl, don’t be afraid to wear grungier accessories with it. If you prefer to be a little preppier, there’s no reason you can’t pair your favorite gold necklace with it. There are so many different ways you could style an ugly Christmas sweater so that it fits your personal style and is especially cute!

## 6. Accessorize Well

Sometimes it’s really just in the accessories! If you’re trying to figure out how to dress up an ugly Christmas sweater, play around with some accessories. You’d be surprised just how many a pair of combat boots can completely change your look around! Accessorize well for the perfect look!

## 7. Wear It with Confidence

When it really comes down to it, though, it’s all about wearing clothes that make you feel confident. No matter what, if you don’t feel confident in what you’re wearing, you’re never going to like how you look! Wear your ugly Christmas sweater with confidence and you’ll feel like the most stylish person in the room!

What’s your favorite way to style an ugly Christmas sweater? Where are you wearing ugly Christmas sweaters this year? Let me know what you think in the comments!
