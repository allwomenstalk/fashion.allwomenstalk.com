# 7 Chic and Stylish Vests to Be Seen in ...

Stylish vests are an easy way to work a layered look this summer season. From lightweight tailored styles to leather bikers, a stylish vest is all you need to update your new season look. Simply pop a vest on over a dress and you’re good to go. Take a look at a few fashionable and stylish vests, below.

## 1. Ivory Sleeveless Blazer

![Ivory Sleeveless Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_ivory-sleeveless-blazer_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Nin432)
Work a distinctly tailored look in this stylish vest. It comes in an ivory colour and has a double breasted design with shoulder tab details. It’s basically a sleeveless blazer, but better suited to the warmer months. Pop it on over a colourful printed dress for a casually cool look.

## 2. Longline Shawl Collar Vest

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
This lightweight vest comes in a vibrant coral hue. It has a relaxed design with a shawl collar and open front. This vest would look great worn with a sleeveless collared blouse and tailored shorts.

## 3. Yves Saint Laurent Peplum Vest

![Yves Saint Laurent Peplum Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_yves-saint-laurent-peplum-vest_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $1245.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/4fTtia)
If you’re one to drop ridiculous amounts of money on a single item of clothing (i.e. the rich and famous) then this pink vest is a stylish option. It has a sweet peplum waist with a concealed fastening. Work a runway inspired look by teaming these with tailored pants and strappy heels.

## 4. Tailored Vest

_ **Price:** $27.80 at forever21.com_
This tailored vest comes in classic black. It has a timeless design and would make a versatile addition to any wardrobe. It features a peaked lapel collar, single button closure and decorative pockets. Work a fairly neutral look by teaming these with tan pants and a cream top.

## 5. Sleeveless PU Biker

![Sleeveless PU Biker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_sleeveless-pu-biker_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $84.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Qhh2hs)
For an edgier take on stylish vests, choose one in a biker style like this Topshop number. This one comes in white and features antique trims. Wear this vest open over a grey singlet dress and with ankle boots for a cool off-duty look.

## 6. Embellished Vest

_ **Price:** $27.57 at forever21.com_
Adding embellishments is one way to work some extra detail into your outfit. This vest features a heavily embellished front with a shawl collar and open front design. Work a feminine look by wearing this stylish vest over a pale pink dress and with ballet pumps.

## 7. Karl Denim Biker Vest

![Karl Denim Biker Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_karl-denim-biker-vest_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $324.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AlONUG)
This pale pink denim vest comes in tough biker style with black PVC coated panels. It has snap fastening lapels and double zip details. Wear this stylish vest open over a slouchy jersey dress and with wedge shoes for a contemporary look.

Stylish vests are in plentiful supply this season; it’s just up to you to choose one that’s right for your look. Whether you’re going for a tailored look or something more casual, a stylish vest is perfect for layering over your summer outfits. Which of these stylish vests would you want to be seen in?
