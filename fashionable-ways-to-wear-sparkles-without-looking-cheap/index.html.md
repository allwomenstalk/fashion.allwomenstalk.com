# 8 Fashionable Ways to Wear Sparkles without Looking Cheap ...

How To Wear Sparkles is valuable knowledge to have these days. Glittery, sparkly pieces are dominating all the designer collections and chain store shelves this season. If you want to pull this look off, then you need to know the best tips on how to wear sparkles. Wearing glitter or sequin pieces of clothing can be tricky to pull off, and you want the final look to be glamorous and tasteful, not tacky. The following is a bit of a guide to how to wear sparkles, without looking cheap.

## 1. Glitter Shoes

Glitter shoes are one of the hottest trends around right now. Everywhere you look, you’ll see sparkling, shiny, toe-tapping goods. When it comes to how to wear sparkles this way, stick to classic styles of shoes. Glitter pumps (a la Miu Miu) are great for a night out, while glitter brogues or ballet flats are an easy way to wear sparkles during the day.

## 2. Sequin Dresses

A metallic dress in gold or silver is a chic choice, as is one in a matte black colour. Black is great for playing down the sequin aspect and ‘tackiness’ of a dress. When choosing sequin dresses, choose one in a fairly basic cut. This season, you can’t go past the sequin shift dress. As sequin dresses can be quite eye-catching, be sure to keep the rest of your accessories fairly minimal.

## 3. Bejewelled Clothing

Bejewelled clothing is one such way to add a bit of sparkle to your outfit. Keep an eye out for clothes with jewel collars or cuffs. Alternatively, hit a craft store and pick up some materials for your own DIY version. Don’t go overboard; keep it to a line of large jewels and smaller accents.

## 4. Glitter Clutches

Accessories are some of the easiest ways to add sparkling touches to an outfit. A hard-case clutch with a glitter finish is a chic approach to the trend. Metallic gold and silver clutches are always tasteful, but this party season look for glitter clutches in midnight blue or jewel tone colours.

## 5. Glitter Nails

Glitter nail polish is a fun way to add a bit of sparkle to your look. From subtle shimmers to chunks of glitter, the possibilities are endless when it comes to wearing glitter polish. A tip for how to wear sparkles this way: instead of buying up many bottles of [glitter nail polish](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/fabulous-ways-to-wear-glitter-nail-polish/), try buying one clear polish with glitter pieces and layering it over your already existing coloured nail polish.

## 6. Encrusted Earrings

Earrings encrusted with jewels and crystals are another way to sparkle this season. Choose dangly drop earrings or chandelier styles to wear during the evening. Show the earrings off by teaming them with a swept back hairstyle. Coloured jewels are fun touch, whereas clear crystal styles are a classic.

## 7. Glitter Belts

Add an offbeat touch to an outfit with a glitter belt. Choose a skinny belt in a glitter finish and use it to cinch in a loose-fitting party dress. When it comes to how to wear sparkles like this, you’ll find that black or silver belts are the most versatile. For a glamorous touch, you can’t go past a gold glitter belt.

## 8. Glitter Eyeshadows

Stay away from tacky finishes and go for a more dewy effect when it comes to wearing glitter eyeshadows this season. Stick to eyeshadows that have more of a shimmer finish. If you’re choosing brighter coloured eyeshadows, keep them the focal point by keeping the rest of your makeup fairly minimal.

The key to how to wear sparkles is balance. Stick to one sparkly focal point per outfit, and choose your accessories accordingly. If you’re going to wear something sequined, choose one with a tasteful matte finish. What are your best tips for how to wear sparkles?
