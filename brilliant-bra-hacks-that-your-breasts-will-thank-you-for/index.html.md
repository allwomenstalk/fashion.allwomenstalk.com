## 13. The Humble Salad Spinner

![The Humble Salad Spinner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/mg/zz0iktt0_540x434.jpg)

Hand wash your bras in a salad spinner. According to bustygirlcomics.com, all you need is enough water and soap (baby shampoo or lingerie soap) to soak your bra in. Soak it, spin the salad spinner, dump the soapy water, replace with clean water and spin again. Dump the water and spin once more until almost dry and then, hang them until completely dry.
