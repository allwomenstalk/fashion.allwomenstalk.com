# 7 Gorgeous Dresses for £20 or Less ...

When was the last time you found amazing dresses for £20? Never? You could be missing out! I was totally skeptical when a friend challenged us to find a great dress for £20 or less, and I was sure that we’d all end up with something unflattering, uncomfortable and quite possibly itchy. That totally wasn’t the case, though. Here are my fave dresses for £20!

## 1. Sexy LBD

![Sexy LBD](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fy/b2/jj/qq8j3ap9_290x370.jpg)

When it comes to dresses for £20, you can’t beat a cute LDB. I found a whole selection from simple cami dresses to casual dresses like this adorable ASOS design, and even black dresses featuring lace cutout panels. Take inspiration from Lordes and goth up the look by teaming it with a leather jacket and dark lipstick, or go for a more everyday look by adding gold jewellery and heels. Either way, you’ll look totally gorgeous.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/9Y1Uyg)

## 2. Floral Fancy

![Floral Fancy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/vb/j0/pjngeurj_290x370.jpg)

This New Look dress is perfect for capturing that fun spring look. It’s perfect for the warmer weather, being made from stretch cotton jersey, and the fabric is oh-so-soft. I love the boat neckline and flare shape, too! For something more glamorous, opt for a slim-fitting dress that ends just below your knees. F&F at Tesco have a stunning racerback neck design, and it’s perfect for looking prim and gorgeous! Just add heels.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/HGqD9Z)

## 3. Bring Me Sunshine

![Bring Me Sunshine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/ri/za/w971nzkp_289x368.jpg)

You just won’t be able to stop smiling wearing a dress in this yellow! Not only will it give you a spring in your step, but it’ll look gorgeous throughout spring and summer, and for seasons afterwards. The dress itself is easy to wear and look after, it being made from a soft, easy-care jersey, and the deep neckline is just perfect for teaming with a statement necklace. Try adding some pink bracelets and sandals for all-day chic.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/9d07EY)

## 4. Risque Red

![Risque Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qt/l6/df/ns9qdk17_290x370.jpg)

Nothing will make you feel hotter than bright red – and this tunic is utterly gorgeous. It has that comfortable soft-touch jersey feel, but it’s given more structure with a crew neckline and cap sleeves. Wear it with a messy up-do and flat black sneakers, or dress it up with heels and a casual hairstyle. Just don’t forget your red lippie – you’ll look amazing.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/yqQCX6)

## 5. Daisy Daisy

![Daisy Daisy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jn/k8/xq/056s9wq3_290x370.jpg)

Love the retro look? Swing dresses will be your next big look. This New Look design features a round neckline, cap sleeves and that amazing swing shape, which is sure to make you feel amazing. You won’t need many accessories, either – the bold daisy print does all the talking.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/ekGNm3)

## 6. Skater Style

![Skater Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cn/kk/q9/wjb2pxxl_290x370.jpg)

I’m loving this red skater dress, too. For just £12.99, it’s pretty, versatile and sure to put you in a good mood. Who doesn’t feel gorgeous in red? Team it with thick black sandals and loose hair for a dressed down look, but it looks just as chic worn with a belt and a messy up-do, or teamed with super-cute wedges and wooden bangles. You can pretty much wear it with anything. Within a month, it’ll be your go-to dress, guaranteed.

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/9VCZin)

## 7. Glamorous

![Glamorous](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/r4/id/m81zp2jt_290x370.jpg)

Want something more eye-catching and out there? Paprika have some amazing designs, including this super-flattering bodycon dress. It’s flexible enough to be comfortable but it offers enough support to slim the tummy and hips, which is ideal if you’re going out to eat but want to look amazing. The cross-back style is so on trend and stunning, and I love the contrast between the black skirt and top. The frill is perfect for highlighting your bust, too! Team it with a show-off belt, black court shoes and loose hair. A choker is the perfect finishing touch. Who would have thought that dresses for £20 would make you look amazing?

[asos.com](https://goo.gl/rvBdxV)

There’s plenty of other dresses for £20, too. A white shirt style swing dress is perfect for rocking the monochrome trend and staying cool at the same time, and there are loads of gorgeous dresses covered in cute prints. Whatever your size or style, you don’t need to be spending a fortune on dresses right now – you’ll feel twice as good if you look amazing in dresses for £20, and just think of what you can spend the rest of your money on! What’s your best buy for under £20?
