# 7 Versatile Pieces You Need in Your Wardrobe for Fall ...

Clothes you need for fall can range from the trend-based to the timeless. To get the most out of your wardrobe, it pays to invest in versatile pieces. These are the things that will work well with a range of different outfits. Read on for a few on-trend examples of the kind of versatile clothing you need for fall.

## 1. Peplum Tops

![Peplum Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/18_800x533.jpg)

A plain black or white peplum top is a must have for fall. The peplum silhouette is still as popular as ever for this season, and a neutral colour will make it easier to pair with the majority of your wardrobe. Pair a peplum top with blue jeans for something casual, or printed pants for something a little bit more ‘fashion’.

## 2. Pencil Skirts

![Pencil Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/28_486x730.jpg)

It’s all about sleek silhouettes when it comes to clothes you need for fall. This season, you can’t beat the classic pencil skirt. Black and grey are timeless but a berry, burnt orange, or printed skirt are all favourites for fall. They can also be much more versatile than you may think!

## 3. Trench Coats

![Trench Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/38_680x1020.jpg)

The ultimate in fall outerwear, the trench coast is a classic piece of clothing. You can’t beat a Burberry-esque beige trench to pair with all your fall outfits. However, navy and khaki are also quite versatile and on-trend options for the season.

## 4. Denim Shirts

![Denim Shirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/410_500x749.jpg)

When it comes to clothes you need for fall, the denim shirt is right up there! It’s perfect for working a cool, off-duty look. Team them with anything from black trousers to printed skirts – the denim shirt is by far a favourite for fall.

## 5. Tuxedo Trousers

![Tuxedo Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/59_1067x1600.jpg)

Slim, sleek trousers wills see you through a range of occasions this fall. Whether it be for polished daytime dressing or an on-trend take on evening wear, the tuxedo style trouser will ensure you’re always on the best dressed list this season.

## 6. Knit Sweaters

![Knit Sweaters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/610_633x640.jpg)

The clothes you need for fall will also need to keep you warm. A chunky cable knit sweater is a firm favourite for the fashion set, but a printed or patterned sweater can look great as well. When choosing something that will be versatile, stick to muted and neutral colours.

## 7. Leather Skirts

![Leather Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/713_633x950.jpg)

When it comes to clothes you need for fall, the leather (or leather look) skirt is a must have. Designers are having a love affair with all things leather at the moment and a leather skirt is an easy way to work this trend into your wardrobe. Whether it’s pleated, A-line, or pencil, a black leather skirt would make a most versatile addition to your fall wardrobe.

These are just a few ideas of clothes you need for fall. When you’re creating a capsule wardrobe this season, keep these items in mind. What tops your list of versatile clothing you need for fall?
