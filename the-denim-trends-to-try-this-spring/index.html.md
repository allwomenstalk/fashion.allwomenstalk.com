# The 7 DENIM TRENDS to TRY THIS SPRING ...

Unless you've been out of the loop when it comes to trends you'll know that denim is making a major comeback, we can't wait to get back into wearing it and pairing it with all our fave outfits!

From lace-up to vintage inspired to fab frayed styles, we've put together all the denim trends you need to jump on the bandwagon for this spring! Don't forget to comment how you love to wear all your favourite denim pieces!
