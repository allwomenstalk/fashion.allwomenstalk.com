# Awesome Tips on How to Score Cheap Clothes ...

Want to learn **how to score cheap clothes**? It’s one of the greatest challenges that any fashion forward girl has to face on a day to day basis, the desire to be as stylish as possible but also try to achieve great looks for a relatively small price! As much as we would love to, we can’t all afford Chanel, Gucci and any of the other high fashion, high price names you’d care to imagine, but that does not mean that you can’t find great clothes for a fraction of the cost. If you are looking to stock up your wardrobe with some great essentials but don’t necessarily have the cash to splash, then take a look at these tips for **how to score cheap clothes**.

## 1. Go Straight to the Back

When you enter a clothes store, always go straight to the back and start your browsing there. Businesses always put their sale items at the back of the store in hopes that customers will have already found things that they want to buy on the regular price racks nearer to the entrance. By starting out in the sale section, you are already giving yourself the best chance of picking up items that have a great discount applied to them. This is one of the best tips for **how to score cheap clothes**!

## 2. Coupons

Turn yourself into one of those people who scouts the internet and local papers for coupons! Couponing can get a bit extreme, but what those shoppers trade for a few looks in the store is a whole bunch of great savings! You can get really clever with coupons, combining as many as you can together to get mega discounts.

## 3. Reward Programmes

If you have a handful of favourite stores that you always end up buying clothes from, then it’s worth checking out if they have any kinds of rewards programmes for loyal and regular customers. These schemes usually give you a regular discount on all items in the store, and you will probably also be given access to even more coupons and offers in the form of an email newsletter.

## 4. Low to High

When you are shopping online, a great tip is to always filter your search results on any store from low to high in terms of overall price. This will help you to see all of the cheapest items first, and will prevent your eye from doing too much wandering across the super expensive and out of budget options! If you know that you don’t want to spend more than a certain amount on a new sweater, then it makes sense to set the filters in such a way that you aren’t constantly distracted by garments twice or triple that price!

## 5. Thrift Stores

There is absolutely nothing wrong with shopping at a thrift store. You would be surprised by just how many hidden gems you can find if you know where to look! You would also be surprised by just how many brand new, tag still on items of clothing are donated every single day. Just because you are shopping at a thrift store, it doesn’t mean that you are going to be searching through used, moth-bitten items all the time!
