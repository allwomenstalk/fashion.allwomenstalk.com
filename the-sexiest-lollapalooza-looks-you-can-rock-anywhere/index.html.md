# The Sexiest Lollapalooza Looks You Can Rock Anywhere ...

Lollapalooza is one of the biggest music festivals of the summer if you live in the United States, but unfortunately, most of us can’t make it to Chicago for the actual festival. It’s also one of the biggest places to get inspired for some much-needed style inspiration, especially if your style is boho, rocker, or grunge. If you couldn’t make it to the festival but still want to dress like you did, this list is sure to give you tons of style inspiration, whether you’re going to a music festival or you’re simply running some errands, you can look like you just walked into Lollapalooza all of the time!

## 1. A Kimono Look

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/dy/kct3hmkr55cd6bcbcb5d1808073275_500x694.jpg)

Kimonos are such an easy festival look, and they’re an even easier look when you’re not going to a festival. Kimonos are starting to become expected at music festivals, but they’re so easy to pull off, whether you’re walking down the street or actually going to a music festival. Simply pair your favorite pair of denim shorts with a tank top and throw a kimono over it. You can wear the simplest outfit, but when you wear a kimono, it looks like an outfit that you spent a ton of time putting together!

## 2. A Shapeless Sundress

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/ow/ld1qj3oq55cd6bf4c7ef2169246920_600x900.jpg)

When it comes to music festival style, most people try to go with flowy looks. Whether you’re wearing a tank top or a dress, the flowier, the better. If you want to wear a sundress, wear something that’s loose and not very fitted. The loose look will make you look relaxed and carefree. It’s such a classic music festival look!

## 3. Crop Top and Maxi Skirt

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/xu/ibhuc8lr55cd6c0ada723520758286_500x750.jpg)

Whether the crop top is form-fitting or loose, when paired with a maxi skirt, this outfit is the perfect music festival look. It was an outfit that was seen all over Lollapalooza, and one that can easily be worn on a regular day too. While it’s not necessarily a work outfit, it’s definitely one that you can rock on a weekend out with friends!

## 4. Acid Wash Tie-Dye

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/dn/ogue65gf55cd6c252ac0f932844288_600x587.jpg)

Acid wash and tie-dye are two trends that are seen everywhere at Lollapalooza. However, when paired together, these are two trends you simply cannot ignore. Wear a tie-dye dress that isn’t colorful, but rather tie-dyed acid wash. The two effects will, without a doubt, turn eyes and create a gorgeous street style look!

## 5. Ripped Denim

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/cm/xk0xgj5755cd6c3bbbced015687585_600x900.jpg)

I’m pretty sure that if Lollapalooza had a dress code, ripped denim would be a requirement. If you really want to nail the Lollapalooza look, wear any amount of ripped denim that you’re comfortable with and turn heads!

## 6. A Simple Jumpsuit

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/c6/hrds1edi55cd6c8a59509441350261_600x928.jpg)

Jumpsuits are a huge trend this summer, whether your style is more polished or bohemian. No matter what your style looks like, jumpsuits are so easy to wear because it takes mere seconds to put your outfit together. They’re a staple at Lollapalooza, and should be a staple in your wardrobe!

## 7. Your Favorite Maxi Dress

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/q5/yrhs3ojd55cd6ca3a3581231479264_600x900.jpg)

Maxi dresses are such a fun and carefree look, but they can get pretty difficult to wear to outdoor music festivals because they can get hot, sweaty, and dirty very quickly. However, when you’re not at a music festival, they can be worn without all of those consequences, making it a look that is perfect for when you want to look like you’re going to a music festival but you’re not!

What is your favorite music festival look, even when you’re not going to a music festival? Let me know in the comments!
