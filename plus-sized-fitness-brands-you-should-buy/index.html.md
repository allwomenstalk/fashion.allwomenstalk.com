# 7 plus\-Sized Fitness Brands You Should Buy ...

If you want to work out, you need workout clothes you can feel comfortable in. Of course, they can be hard to find if you don't know where to look. According to [Self,](https://www.self.com/gallery/plus-size-workout-clothes?mbid=social_twitter) here are some of the best brands to buy:

![NOLA by Addition Elle](https://img.allw.mn/content/sf/jk/zsvxds5k56b8a11b3e06d827927251_400x225.gif)
