# 7 Fabulous Pairs of Jeggings ...

**Jeggings** can divide the masses. Somewhere between jeans and leggings, the jegging lives in hazy fashion territory. Simply uttering the word can elicit a range of responses – mostly negative. While they can be regarded as a fashion faux pas, there are a range of designers that are giving the jegging a better reputation. There are also a range of more affordable chain-store options that aren’t completely tacky. The following are just a few of the more stylish jeggings around.

## 1. Gold Sign Leggings-Style Jeans

![Gold Sign Leggings-Style Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_gold-sign-leggings-style-jeans_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $240.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YwePdK)
Notice how they avoid the ‘**jeggings**’ tag by naming them ‘leggings-style jeans’? Well, never fear, these still belong in jeggings territory and are a very smart and stylish option at that. They look exactly like a pair of super skinny jeans, complete with zip and pocket detailing, but are made from super stretchy fabric.

## 2. Topshop Pastel Turn up Jeggings

![Topshop Pastel Turn up Jeggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_topshop-pastel-turn-up-jeggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/PGFTzb)
Pastels are every so popular for spring. Work these mint green **jeggings** into your wardrobe by teaming them with a longline white blouse and lace up brogue shoes. Complete the look with neutral accessories for a fresh spring look.

## 3. Topshop Jeggings

![Topshop Jeggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_topshop-jeggings_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/NM7GRj)
If you’re after **stylish jeggings** then definitely go for one in a darker colour. Since jeggings are made from stretchy material, they can be very unforgiving. A darker coloured jegging is much more slimming and hides imperfections better. Team these jeggings with an oversized shirt or sweater.

## 4. Topshop Bleached Denim Jeggings

![Topshop Bleached Denim Jeggings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_topshop-bleached-denim-jeggings_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $44.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Zbiv9z)
Spring usually calls for lighter denim, and I guess that goes for **jeggings** as well. However, as mentioned previously, lighter coloured denim can be quite unforgiving so it’s important to wear it well. These bleached denim jeggings would look great teamed with a longline collared shirt in either a cream colour or something brighter like a coral pink hue.

## 5. Jil Sander Leggings-Style Pants

![Jil Sander Leggings-Style Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/5_jil-sander-leggings-style-pants_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $560.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/SLxOhv)
Once again, the use of the word ‘**jegging**’ has been artfully avoided in this title. This pair is made from a stretch jersey fabric and, unlike most styles that cling right around the ankle, are looser at the opening. This means that they don’t create such a top-heavy effect and appear much more like a normal pair of pants.

## 6. Karl Sequined Leggings-Style Pants

![Karl Sequined Leggings-Style Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/6_karl-sequined-leggings-stoptyle-pants_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $335.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/YvB7Ir)
Up the ante next time you go out with these sequined **jeggings**. They have a mid-rise design and feature zip details at the ankles. Wear these at night with a longline black tank and heels, or work a quirky look during the day by teaming them with a sporty sweater and sneakers.

## 7. Mason by Michelle Mason Leggings-Style Pants

![Mason by Michelle Mason Leggings-Style Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/7_mason-by-michelle-mason-leggings-style-pants_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $910.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/3ub6tL)
So, I don’t know anyone who’d spend $900 on a pair of **jeggings**, but let’s have a look at this pair anyway. These rockstar red leather jeggings could be tacky but when teamed with a simple white shirt, black blazer, and ballet flats, it can all look quite stylish. What do you think?

So, there you have it. Not all **jeggings** are evil. When styled well, the jegging can look fabulous! What do you think of these jeggings?
