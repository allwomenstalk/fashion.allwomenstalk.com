# 8 Stylish Silk Blouses ...

Silk Blouses can be incorporated into a range of stylish new season outfits. Silk blouses are quite versatile as they can be worn with both business-style outfits and more casual ones. While silk blouses may seem like a bit of a luxury buy, this is doesn’t always have to be the case. You can pick some up at a range of chain stores for relatively affordable prices. Below are a few silk blouses to take inspiration from.

## 1. Equipment Brett Washed-Silk Shirt

![Equipment Brett Washed-Silk Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/yl/byz2vbby54c932b01639e943331584_267x383.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/j4gXtK)
This silk blouse comes in an on-trend peach colour. It is made from a semi-sheer washed silk and features a classic button-up design. It has long sleeves, curved hems, and button-fastening cuffs. Tuck this skirt into a knee-length leather pencil skirt and team with almond toe pumps for a contemporary take on business dressing.

## 2. J.Crew Natasha Ruffled Silk Crepe Blouse

![J.Crew Natasha Ruffled Silk Crepe Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/dj/m4m6kljf54c932b0e6fad134306569_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AZsHgd)
This ruffled silk blouse has a Victorian-era feel to it. It comes in a pale peach colour and features a high neck, ruffled trims, and button fastening placket at the front. Wear this top with some slim 7/8 length trousers, patent ballet pumps, and a knee length coat for a stylish everyday look.

## 3. Topshop Boutique Cream Silk Blouse

![Topshop Boutique Cream Silk Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/h4/opx3bni654c932b1cf19f880215541_267x354.jpg)

**Price:** $116.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EljH3J)
This short sleeved silk blouse is well suited to a corporate setting. It has a modest neckline, stitch detailing on the sleeves, and a button fastening at the back. The gathering at the neckline is also a nice detail. Silk blouses such as this one would look great tucked into grey pencil skirts or with wide-legged trousers.

## 4. Topshop Washed Silk Slim Shirt

![Topshop Washed Silk Slim Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/ec/ji63vbq454c932b2ac7e1646461887_267x352.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/635w7p)
Bright coloured silk blouses are a great way to stand out from the crowd. This particular shirt has a button-up collar, front breast pocket, and long sleeves with turn-up detailing. For a casual weekend look, team this shirt with black or dark denim jeans, tan loafers, and a slouchy bucket-style bag.

## 5. Richard Chai Love Printed Georgette Blouse

![Richard Chai Love Printed Georgette Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hf/q7/yvm075jd54c932b394938921945028_280x381.jpg)

**Price:** $109.50 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This blouse is perfect for those who prefer a quirky take on modern classics. It features an all over abstract print, pointed collar, and button-down front. It is a slim fit and has a slightly sheer finish. At 70% off retail price, this is a great piece to snap up.

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Simone Silk Shirt

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Simone Silk Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/ux/alwdr10c54c932b490687062317690_267x327.jpg)

**Price:** $280.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PmgR3Q)
This silk shirt has a bit of a military inspired feel to it. The military trend is still a quite popular one, and silk blouses like this one are a great way to work the trend. This shirt features two button fastening breast pockets and tab detailing at the sleeves. The sleeves can be worn long or rolled up and secured with the tab fastenings. This shirt would look great worn with a full wool skirt.

## 7. J.Crew Silk Crepe De Chine Blouse

![J.Crew Silk Crepe De Chine Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/hf/iivkuj9354c932b55d060943437087_267x352.jpg)

**Price:** $88.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/O35rsq)
A short sleeved blouse is a stylish choice this season. This silk blouse comes in a light cream colour and has gold button fastenings through the front. Cream silk blouses are great for teaming with brighter colours. Wear this blouse with wide-legged pants and a sharp coat.

## 8. Cacharel Open Back Silk Blouse

![Cacharel Open Back Silk Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/w2/g8rqpu5o54c932b62245c417994297_280x371.jpg)

**Price:** $282.50 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While it looks quite austere from the front, this silk blouse has a wonderful open design at the back. This shirt has a concealed button-down front, long sleeves with pleated cuffs, and a draped self-tie at the back. This blouse has a relaxed fit and the blush colour is perfect for this season’s take on feminine dressing.

There’s nothing more chic than a silk blouse worn with slim jeans and ballet flats. As silk blouses are a classic piece, also keep an eye out for discounted designer brands in cuts that won’t date. There are a range of silk blouses available, from dressy sleeveless numbers to classic collared styles. Which of these silk blouses do you like most?
