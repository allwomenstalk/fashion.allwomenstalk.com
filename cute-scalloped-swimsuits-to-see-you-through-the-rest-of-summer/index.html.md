# 7 Cute Scalloped Swimsuits to See You through the Rest of Summer ...

Next time you plan on dipping your toes into the ocean or clocking some pool time, do it in style in sweet scalloped swimsuits. Scalloped details are still very much on-trend and you can spot them on anything from skirts to shorts to swimsuits this season. A scalloped swimsuit will give your poolside look a feminine, flirty twist. The following are just a few scalloped swimsuits that will see you through the rest of the summer months.

## 1. Colourblock Scalloped Swimsuit

![Colourblock Scalloped Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_colourblock-scalloped-swimsuit_600x900.jpg)

Scalloped swimsuits like this Topshop number are perfect for working a retro-inspired look. It has a colour blocked design which accentuates your waist line. It also has scallop trim details and comes with removable straps. Wrap a headscarf around your ‘do and pop on some oversized sunglasses for a glam poolside look.

## 2. Scalloped Underwire Swimsuit

![Scalloped Underwire Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_scalloped-underwire-swimsuit_294x600.jpg)

Scalloped details give this basic black swimsuit feminine twist. It has a fairly simple design with underwire cups and halterneck straps. This swimsuit is by J.Crew and while it originally retailed for around $100, you can currently grab it on sale for $50. It’s also available in a range of other colours.

## 3. Scalloped Texture Bikini

![Scalloped Texture Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_scalloped-texture-bikini_600x900.jpg)

If you prefer a two-piece then check out this Topshop bikini. It has an underwire bra with halterneck straps and tie-waist bottoms. The material has a textured feel to it and the cups feature scalloped edging. Buying the set will set you back around $60 but you can also buy pieces separately to mix and match. If a skimpy bikini bottom isn’t your thing then try a high waisted bottom for a retro twist.

## 4. Scalloped Trim Swimsuit

![Scalloped Trim Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_scalloped-trim-swimsuit_336x596.jpg)

Printed swimsuits are always a fun choice. This Tory Burch swimsuit features a blue and green flamingo print. It also has subtle scallop trims around the bust and leg openings. It’s pretty pricey, originally retailing at over $200 and even on sale at Shopbop it will still set you back around $160.

## 5. Scalloped Hem Swimsuit

![Scalloped Hem Swimsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_scalloped-hem-swimsuit_350x500.jpg)

This red and white polka dot swimsuit has a bit of a Minnie Mouse feel to it. The design is very retro-inspired, with its frilly trims and scalloped hem. This swimsuit also offers up a bit more coverage around the bum and thighs. It’s by Bettie Page and you can grab yourself one of these swimsuits from Modcloth for around $90.

## 6. Scalloped Bandeau Bikini

![Scalloped Bandeau Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_scalloped-bandeau-bikini_336x596.jpg)

You’ll look oh so fashionable in this scalloped bikini. It’s by Marysia Swim and currently available from Shopbop. A black bikini is timeless and this one is made from textured material and features on-trend scalloped edges. The bikini top also has boning on the ruched cups, which helps it keep its shape. Wear this bikini with an open kaftan-style shirt and oversized sunglasses for a glam poolside look.

## 7. Scalloped Hem Bikini

![Scalloped Hem Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_scalloped-hem-bikini_330x404.jpg)

This scalloped swimsuit is all sorts of playful. The bikini top features a loose frill with scalloped edges and spaghetti straps. The bikini bottoms also feature scalloped edges. All this swimsuit needs is a floppy straw hat for a fun summertime look. This swim set is by Roxy and you can buy the top and bottom separately from Macy’s.

Scalloped swimsuits would make a sweet addition to your summer swimwear wardrobe. These are just a few chic scalloped swimsuits for you to try out. Which of these swimsuits do you like best?
