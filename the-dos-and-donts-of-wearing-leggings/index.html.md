# The dos and Donts of Wearing Leggings on the Regular ...

It's hard to survive without a good pair of leggings. After all, they can turn any bland outfit into a bold one. Of course, there are some unspoken rules about the trend that [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/outfits-ideas-for-leggings) has put into words. Here are the [dos and don'ts](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/dos-and-donts-of-life/) of wearing leggings:

## 1. Do Buy Them in Leather or Suede

![Do Buy Them in Leather or Suede](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zo/hi/f2dlc7t05628efa56f960582623148_482x724.jpg)

This can make you look sexy, while still seeming professional.

## 2. Don't Wear Neon Colors

![Don't Wear Neon Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jg/z8/ycm9puie5628f003e3791823576138_400x600.jpg)

This is a fun outfit idea for a party, but you shouldn't wear neon leggings to work.

## 3. Do Layer Them under a Dress or Skirt

![Do Layer Them under a Dress or Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p1/bh/uhjifl9o5628efa57e71d202348528_482x724.jpg)

This can turn any plain dress into a stylish one!

## 4. Don't Wear Them with a Crop Top

![Don't Wear Them with a Crop Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/vh/bvfb73305628f005a8528793313509_600x800.jpg)

You've heard people complain about women wearing leggings as pants, haven't you?

## 5. Do Style Them with an Oversized Sweater

![Do Style Them with an Oversized Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/i8/ppxf62vn5628efa565519645080270_482x724.jpg)

If you own an oversized sweater that'll cover your crotch and booty, then don't be afraid to wear leggings underneath it all.

## 6. Don't Throw on a T-shirt and Call It a Day

![Don't Throw on a T-shirt and Call It a Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iu/gs/ouluawe05628f005274b8435112270_600x841.jpg)

Unless you're going to the gym, try to spice up your outfit a little bit more than this.
