# 9 Pretty Pastel Midi Skirts to Wear for Spring ...

Update your look this spring with ladylike pastel midi skirts. Pastels are a tried and true favourite for spring and there will be no shortage of sweet sorbet hues in stores if seasonal trends are anything to go by. Play up the pastel colour palette by accessorising with crisp whites and neutrals or add an edgy twist with distressed leather and denim. Whichever way you decide to go, step into spring with these pretty pastel midi skirts.

## 1. Dorothy Perkins Blush Textured Midi Skirt

![Dorothy Perkins Blush Textured Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/v6/s2605h6q_600x797.jpg)

Full, pleated pastel midi skirts are some of the most popular silhouettes right now. They’re a little bit retro and perfect for working a ladylike look. This Dorothy Perkins midi skirt is an affordable option and comes in a beautiful blush colour with a textured finish. Offset the pastel nature of this skirt by teaming it with a tough leather jacket and low ankle boots for a contemporary take on the trend.

## 2. Topshop Textured Gathered Midi Skirt

![Topshop Textured Gathered Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x0/hr/qagwusw9_600x635.jpg)

This Topshop midi skirt has a full silhouette with its gathered waist and softly textured finish. It comes in a pale blue colour that would work well with neutral accents. Wear it with a cream cable knit sweater and blush ballet flats for a simple and understated look this season.

## 3. Topshop Pink Tweed Pencil Skirt

![Topshop Pink Tweed Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w1/vn/ma8dy5na_600x876.jpg)

If full skirts aren’t your thing, try a more fitted pencil skirt instead. This Topshop tweed skirt comes in a pretty pastel pink colour. It sits high on the waist and has a zip fastening at the back. Tuck in a collared shirt for an office-friendly outfit or team it with a striped t-shirt and sneakers for a casual everyday look.

## 4. Derek Lam Mesh Flared Midi Skirt

![Derek Lam Mesh Flared Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qy/mo/lkg03ukn_600x702.jpg)

This midi skirt by designer Derek Lam has a rendered open knit mesh overlay. The skirt is quite fitted throughout the top and flares out towards the hem, creating quite a striking silhouette. The mint colour is right on trend and this skirt would look great worn with a fresh white t-shirt and ladylike court shoes.

## 5. River Island Box Pleat Midi Skirt

![River Island Box Pleat Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iw/xr/oxksnwq4_600x839.jpg)

This pale pink midi skirt from River Island features bold box pleats. It has a contrasting black waistband, but you can easily disguise that by layering cropped shirts and sweaters over the top. Alternatively, you could work an edgy angle by teaming this skirt with a black bustier top and strappy heels.

## 6. Boohoo Organza Overlay Midi Skirt

![Boohoo Organza Overlay Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0u/o5/ag767ss4_600x742.jpg)

This midi skirt by Boohoo combines two of the hottest spring trends – sheer fabrics and pastels. It comes in a cool mint colour and has a full sheer overlay and matching slip skirt underneath. This style of skirt is perfect for working a playful spring look. Wear it with a slouchy tank top and strappy flat shoes for a breezy everyday outfit.

## 7. The Whitepepper Floral Print Midi Skirt

![The Whitepepper Floral Print Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/rv/idabhgdd_600x874.jpg)

Floral prints are another spring favourite and this pale pink skirt from The Whitepepper features a dainty white floral print. The skirt has a relaxed design with a swing silhouette and button through design. Team it with cropped t-shirt and lace-up shoes for a slightly nineties-inspired take on spring dressing.

## 8. Vivetta Floral Jacquard Midi Skirt

![Vivetta Floral Jacquard Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/cm/jd6k9f8x_460x608.jpg)

This Vivetta skirt has a modest midi silhouette. It comes in a baby blue colour and features an ornate jacquard floral print. Keep things modest by teaming it with a tucked-in collared blouse or dress it down with a slinky singlet and ankle strap shoes.

## 9. Love Moschino Pleated Skirt

![Love Moschino Pleated Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wg/mo/iidg6fgn_600x753.jpg)

Pleated skirts are another great option for spring. This pleated skirt by Love Moschino comes in a lovely lilac colour. Team it with a fitted singlet, cropped denim jacket, and blush coloured shoes for a relaxed springtime outfit.

Whether you opt for a feminine full skirt or a sleek pencil silhouette, the midi skirt is a stylish option this season. These are just a few pastel midi skirts to make your mark in for spring. How would you style a pastel midi skirt for spring?
