# 7 Beautiful Tanks to Wear This Summer ...

The hot summer months are the perfect opportunity to show off your beautiful shoulders and neck with a pretty tank top. Whether you want something classic and professional for the office, or a look that is fun and flirty for a date night, there is a tank top that will work for the occasion. Here are 7 beautiful tanks to wear this summer.

## 1. The Beaded Motif Tank

![The Beaded Motif Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/tank1_282x408.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [armaniexchange.com](https://goo.gl/NIz0N9)
This beautiful tank has delicate gold beading on the front. It has a rounded hemline which gives a romantic, soft feel to the look. Dress it up with a pencil skirt or dress it down with casual jeans. You could wear it a dozen different ways and never exhaust the options it gives you.

## 2. Sweet Pink Crochet Tank

![Sweet Pink Crochet Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/tank2_520x693.jpg)

**Price:** $19.94 at [oldnavy.gap.com](http://oldnavy.gap.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This pretty scoop neck tank is ruched in the front to hide any problems with your midsection. The crochet back will show off your beautiful shoulders and a racerback cut is a nice way to elongate your figure. It is casual enough to wear with your favorite shorts, but you could also pair it with a denim skirt for an instant go-to look for a day of shopping.

## 3. Billabong Sea Saw Tank Top

![Billabong Sea Saw Tank Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/mn/msmj32p854c92757af328383300073_327x400.jpg)

**Price:** $34.50 at [buckle.com](http://www.buckle.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With a fun southwestern print, this tank will add a punch of cool to your summer wardrobe. The highlight of this tank is the fringed hemline which is a flirty touch when paired with your favorite jeans for a fun Saturday date.

## 4. Tiered Tie Waist Tank

![Tiered Tie Waist Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/s2/jmalnzqq54c92758acb6f447812596_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $17.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With delicate tiers of white, this tank is flirty and sophisticated. It’s way more than just a basic tank, but the price would fool you! This is a bargain worth adding to every girl’s wardrobe.

## 5. Twisted Stripes Tank

![Twisted Stripes Tank](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/qj/h913d52o54c92759939aa173818789_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $13.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Forever 21 gets it right with this pretty tank top in classic black and white. The twisted pattern gives it a modern, graphic feel that is youthful and fun. This top would be perfect with skinny jeans or a black mini skirt.

## 6. Striped Tunic Top

![Striped Tunic Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/re/xo7cwz3354c9275a8c56f532199604_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $9.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With red and white stripes, this casual cotton tank has a nautical feel perfect for summer. The back features a racerback cut to show off your shoulders and keep you cool. For less than $10, this is a must have!

## 7. Floral Lace Knit Top

![Floral Lace Knit Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/de/g3/q57q4cty54c9275b8a283035935041_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $9.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
No summer wardrobe would be complete without a little floral touch to complete it. This lace tank is both feminine and sophisticated. It adds an elegant touch to any look without being so formal that it feels stuffy. Add this sweet tank to your favorite summer Capri’s or wear it with a flirty skirt for the perfect outfit.

These are just 7 beautiful tanks to wear this summer, but there are many more out there. Take a quick inventory of your wardrobe and see what colors and prints would add an extra punch of summer style. Where is your favorite place to buy tank tops?
