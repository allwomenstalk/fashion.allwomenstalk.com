# 9 Stylish Celebrities under 20 Who Have Style beyond Their Years ...

It seems that every celebrity is a fashion icons these days, including these stylish celebrities under 20. They dress with a sophistication that is way beyond their years, but still incorporate their youthfulness into their wardrobes. My favorite thing about all of these stylish celebrities under 20 is that they dress age appropriate and do not show too much skin or dress too scandalous. From ladylike to funky, they have such a wide variety of personal style. No matter how old you are or what your wardrobe is like, you can take inspiration from these awesome and young stylish celebrities.

## 1. Chloe Grace Moretz

![Chloe Grace Moretz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08125125_1496_593x831.jpg)

I am starting off this list of top stylish celebrities under 20 with one of the most popular up and coming young stars. She has starred in Kick-Ass and Carrie, but she is just as incredible in her off-screen fashion as she is in her roles. She has an edgy style and she loves to take risks with her clothing. What I love about her is that she sticks with clothes that are age appropriate and that any young fashionistas can buy. She even did a campaign with Aeropostale because she wanted her fans to be able to wear the same clothes as her.

## 2. Hailee Steinfeld

![Hailee Steinfeld](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08125359_4330_527x790.jpg)

Although Haliee Steinfield is 2nd on this list, she is just as incredible a fashion icon as Chloe. She has such a classic and feminine style around her and she always looks confident in what she is wearing. She has the most incredible long brown hair that she always uses to enhance her outfits. She is a fan of short dresses to show off her incredible legs, but always makes sure she is age appropriate. She even was named the face of Miu Miu in 2011.

## 3. Willow Smith

![Willow Smith](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08125713_7653_459x709.jpg)

Willow Smith likes to march to the beat of her own drum and does not let anyone tell her how to dress. She has a more tomboy feel to her wardrobe and can been seen rocking baggy pants and funky t-shirts. She is constantly mixing colors and prints and loves to stand out from the classic girly feel that so many young stylish celebrities under 20 tend to gravitate towards.

## 4. Elle Fanning

![Elle Fanning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08125745_0328_500x616.jpg)

If Dakota Fanning was under 20, she would be up here right next to her stylish sister. But unfortunately Elle is left sister-less on this list of stylish celebrities under 20. Elle has such an innocent and vintage feel to everything she wears. She balances her pale skin and blonde hair with feminine dresses that tend to have a hippie feel to them. She loves soft colored clothes and takes tons of inspiration from the '60s and '70s.

## 5. Lourdes Leon

![Lourdes Leon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08130001_2349_524x759.jpg)

You're bound to be incredibly fashionable when your mother is Madonna. Lourdes has such an edgy and funky vibe to her wardrobe and she dresses way beyond her age. She even gets to work as a creative director for her mother’s fashion line, Material Girl. So if you love her edgy awesome fashion, you can check out that line and take a page from Lourdes' edgy wardrobe.

## 6. AnnaSophia Robb

![AnnaSophia Robb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20112514_4017_600x876.jpg)

She has incredible clothes in her show, The Carrie Diaries, but she looks just as fabulous off screen as on screen. She loves fun prints and you can always see her in adorable A-line dresses. You can tell that Robb dresses for comfort and she feels confident in whatever she wears. She dresses like an adult while keeping a youthful vibe to her outfits.

## 7. Kendall & Kylie Jenner

![Kendall & Kylie Jenner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20112745_7600_570x760.jpg)

When your older sisters are Kim, Khloe, and Kourtney Kardashian, you know you are going to inherit great style. They have been guest stylists for Teen Vogue and even designed their own line for Pac Sun, all before reaching 18 years old. But just because they design together doesn’t mean they have the exact same style. Kendall tends to have a tomboy vibe to her clothes while Kylie loves to rock edgier clothes. They both have such simple style that is easy for any young girl to take inspiration from.

## 8. Saoirse Ronan

![Saoirse Ronan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/08130806_0783_600x789.jpg)

She has wowed us in movies like The Host and Hanna, and she has wowed us on the red carpet. Her clothing tends to have classic silhouettes with high necklines and she keeps her accessories to a minimum. She has been seen in clothing straight off of the runway. Glamour even named her as one of the top 25 fashionistas to watch in 2012.

## 9. Suri Cruise

_ She might be the youngest on this list, but she definitely deserves a spot as one of the stylish celebrities under 20. At the young age of 7, she is already a style icon for children everywhere. She has such a strong sense of style for someone so young. She loves to wear girly dresses with jackets and can almost always been seen with hair accessories like headbands. Some mothers were upset when she was seen wearing high heels a few years ago, but that just reinforces how much Suri loves fashion and is ready to dress with a sophistication years beyond her age.

I wish I were this fashionable when I was younger. Rather than rocking jeans and a t-shirt like most other kids and teens, these celebrities love to embrace fashion and show off their personality. They dress like adults while still having an age-appropriate wardrobe that they feel confident in. We could all take a page from their stylebook.
