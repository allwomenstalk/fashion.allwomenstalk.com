# 7 Kimono Inspired Clothes ...

This season, inject some oriental flavour into your wardrobe with a kimono inspired piece of clothing. Work a kimono blouse with a pair of jeans, or really stand out in a kimono inspired dress. Look to kimono inspired garments in on-trend pastel and neutral colours for a subdued take on oriental dressing. Spice up your wardrobe with the following kimono inspired clothes.

## 1. Elizabeth and James Anita Belted Kimono Dress

![Elizabeth and James Anita Belted Kimono Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gm/dc/dn0alfjw54c9272fc6537986748910_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $365.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/zLX4ZV)
This blush kimono dress is a chic way to wear the trend. It has a v-neck, draped bodice, and short kimono sleeves. It also has a gathered waist with detachable sash. Work a chic cocktail look and team this dress with sparkly drop earrings, strappy sandals, and a sleek metallic clutch.

## 2. Michael Michael Kors Kimono-Sleeved Silk Blouse

![Michael Michael Kors Kimono-Sleeved Silk Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zc/nl/coas9vqz54c92730a2e5d008285664_411x496.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/eFsfk7)
This silk blouse would be great for incorporating into your everyday wardrobe. The short kimono sleeves provides a basic silk shirt with an oriental twist. This shirt features a wide neck, stud detailing along the chest, and ruched drawstring ties at the sides. Wear this blouse with blue jeans, strappy leather sandals and an oversized clutch.

## 3. Topshop Brick Co-ordinating Cutwork Kimono

![Topshop Brick Co-ordinating Cutwork Kimono](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/bk/o3bxi7zp54c927319ed09129103820_373x500.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ce2x4C)
Kimono inspired clothes often take the form of robes and cover ups. This kimono sleeve cover up would be great for layering over outfits. It features cutwork detailing along the edges. It has elbow-length sleeves and an open draped front. This kimono top could easily dress up an outfit of black jeans and a white tank top. Team with long layered necklaces and patent heels for a chic night time look.

## 4. Topshop Cream Kimono Sleeve Maxi Cover up

![Topshop Cream Kimono Sleeve Maxi Cover up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/oh/lo8ddom354c92732982bc673247024_334x498.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/vldvpL)
Maxi dresses are an easy choice for summer dressing. This printed maxi dress features kimono style sleeves and drawstring tie under the bust. The all-over print consists of abstract swirls of colour. This maxi dress would look great teamed with dangly earrings and strappy sandals.

## 5. Dorothy Perkins Orange Kimono Dress

**Price:** $64.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This vibrant orange dress would make a fun evening outfit. It has an asymmetric design with a single kimono-inspired sleeve. It has a draped appearance and comes with a self-tie waist belt. Wear this dress with turquoise drop earrings, stacked bangles, and strappy sandals.

## 6. Topshop Black Embroidered Kimono

![Topshop Black Embroidered Kimono](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/y1/p5r90v8y54c92733d8c5d490550717_414x500.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/7Gj2R7)
This black embroidered kimono is probably the most traditional of the kimono inspired clothes on this list. It features a striking butterfly floral embossed detail at the back. It has long kimono sleeves and a self-tie waist. This kimono would look great paired with a cream top and leather leggings.

## 7. Issa Printed Silk-Chiffon Kimono-Style Dress

![Issa Printed Silk-Chiffon Kimono-Style Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/so/mrv3c0bt54c92734de283108188830_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $725.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZkXCqI)
Kimono inspired clothes are flattering in that they can create a wonderfully draped silhouette. This dress has a low v-neck, draped sleeves, and self-tie waist. It features a vivid purple and red abstract print. This dress would make quite the statement evening dress.

A kimono inspired garment in a basic block colour is a great way to incorporate the look into your everyday attire. Flowing, silky fabrics are also a relaxed way to wear the trend. Which of these kimono inspired clothes do you like best?
