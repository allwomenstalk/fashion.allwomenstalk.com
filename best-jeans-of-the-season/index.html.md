# 8 Best Jeans of the Season ...

Best jeans… you know you want them, but where to find them? I can help! I’ve scoured all of my favorite shops, and tried on every brand and fit you can think of to compile this list of the 8 best jeans of the season. So whether you’re in the market for a new pair of jeans for back to school, or you just want to add a pop of unexpected color to your wardrobe, keep reading! Here are my picks for the best new jeans.

## 1. AG Stevie Ankle Jeans

![AG Stevie Ankle Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/nf/l00mk6wd54c92f989b438197773338_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $158.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Classic five-pocket styling meets slight cropping and gorgeous hues - that’s the sartorial equation behind the Stevie cut, and why the land at the top of m list of the best jeans. Even if you’re not fond of the pale plum color, no worries. They come in a range of other hues, too.

## 2. J Brand 29” Skinny Jeans

![J Brand 29” Skinny Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/ei/dxrz8nga54c92f996b906533602862_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $176.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Available in size 25 to 32, these bright blue jeans are super-skinny but oh-so-comfortable. They’re made of a soft cotton/Lycra blend, so they hug your curves in all the right places. This color looks marvelous with bright whites or sleek blacks.

## 3. Pilcro Stet Slim Straight Jeans

![Pilcro Stet Slim Straight Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/du/xl49eslz54c92f9a3e36f199752408_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These are the best jeans for layering with botanical print tunics and long sweaters. Their moss color is dark but neutral, with a hint of color when everyone else will be wearing blue or black this fall. The fit is flattering and comfortable, and looks great with ankle boots.

## 4. Citizens of Humanity Dita Petite

![Citizens of Humanity Dita Petite](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/yb/rd7zy7ys54c92f9b145fe601305133_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $186.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
It’s not easy to find jeans that fit well on a petite frame, but these are divine. The fabric is thin, but not too lightweight, and the distressing and color are wonderful. Best of all, they’re made to fit a petite girl well, with appropriate proportions in the hips, waist, thighs, and bootie. Nice!

## 5. J Brand High-Waist Bette Flares

**Price:** $218.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Looking for a pair of jeans that make the transition from office wear to date wear in a snap? These may well be the pair! They’re a gorgeous dark indigo wash, with a high waist and adorable patch pockets on the front. They look stunning with a beaded tunic for date night, a graphic tee and long-line vest for casual, or a button-down blouse and heels for the office. They’re the best jeans for all three!

## 6. Level 99 Lily Straight Leg Jeans

![Level 99 Lily Straight Leg Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/el/ekc7vnnx54c92f9cd55e0215206916_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Looking for a pair of skinnies with a little more give in the fabric? Give these a go! They’re a perfect flattering skinny fit, with classic five-pocket styling of your old favorite jeans. I just love the stretchiness of the denim!

## 7. AG Tomboy Crops

![AG Tomboy Crops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cx/en/i0ap2dra54c92f9de8e21761497484_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $168.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These may well be my pick for the absolute best jeans of the entire season, made to be worn cuffed and casual, with wedges or platforms or clogs. Or, for that matter, skimmers or sneakers, too! I can think of about twenty different ways to wear them… how would you wear them?

## 8. Doted Denim Trousers

**Price:** $128.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How darling! I love the pale wash, and the fitted flare shape of these jeans. They look great as a base for all sorts of print tops, and I love how they look with wedges! The trouser cut makes them versatile, too, since you can dress them up for the office or down for a night out with the girls.

With so many cute new jeans to choose from, it’s going to be impossible to say which are the best jeans, or to pick just one pair! I do love that last pair, though, and the lavender ones are so pretty, too! Which of these jeans do you like best, and how would you wear them this season?
