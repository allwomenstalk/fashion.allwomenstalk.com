# 7 Flawless Long Sleeve Dresses That Are Great for Winter ...

Because of their practicality (when paired with the right clothing finds), I've become completely addicted to wearing long sleeve dresses this winter. I used to fear buying these type of dresses because I was not sure how flattering they would be. After seeing so many people rock this trend flawlessly, I decided to give it a try and am ecstatic at how amazing they are! I love that you can execute wearing a dress during this cold season and still manage to stay warm by pairing it with thick tights or leggings (there's no shame in layers). Check out these lovely long sleeve dresses that are perfect for winter!

## 1. Forever 21 Coming up Roses a-Line Dress

![Forever 21 Coming up Roses a-Line Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130853_1330_600x881.jpg)

From its striking red and pink printed roses to the slimming silhouette, this has become one of my favorite long sleeve dresses. I'm obsessed with A-line dresses and skirts because of how they accentuate your waist and skim over everything else. I fell in love with the print to the point that I purchased this despite not being a major fan of turtle necklines.

## 2. Old Navy Women's Marled Sweater Dress

![Old Navy Women's Marled Sweater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130852_8140_417x600.jpg)

I'm not exaggerating when I say that this is the warmest thing that I have ever gotten my hands on. I definitely recommend this knit dress as a basic for everyone's winter wardrobe. Pairing it with bold belts will make your outfit pop! Because of its moderate length, it can be worn on many occasions.

## 3. Modcloth a Stunning Start

![Modcloth a Stunning Start](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130853_2037_599x836.jpg)

The name of this dress certainly does it justice; the deep royal blue hue is absolutely stunning. Because it runs on the short side, I always pair it with thick tights and leggings underneath (better safe than sorry) to look cute while staying nice and toasty.

## 4. ASOS New Look Inspire Spot Print Long Sleeve Dress

![ASOS New Look Inspire Spot Print Long Sleeve Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130853_9270_600x766.jpg)

Whenever I rock this with my navy blue pea coat, I pretend that I'm walking down the runway. This silky polka dot dress by New Look Inspire is the perfect fit. It has an elasticated empire waist that makes it really easy to get in and out of. Whenever I wear this to the office or on a night out with pals, I always get compliments.

## 5. ASOS Skater Dress in Nepi with Long Sleeves

![ASOS Skater Dress in Nepi with Long Sleeves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130854_5138_600x766.jpg)

This next dress is incredibly soft and flattering. Its stretchy material is what sold me on this dress as it is sometimes hard for me to find tops that accentuate without being suffocating. I like pairing this with my charcoal grey boyfriend cardigan for a contrasting look.

## 6. Modcloth Brand New Lace Dress

![Modcloth Brand New Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/11130855_3217_599x844.jpg)

I love how sophisticated this lace dress is! I'm a total sucker for anything with collars and this dress does not disappoint. The design is truly elegant;. Wearing this with trendy oxfords or fabulous ankle boots will instantly boost your style game!

## 7. Forever 21 Poetic Shift Dress with Belt

![Forever 21 Poetic Shift Dress with Belt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/24171905_6637_409x600.jpg)

Lastly, the eclectic print featured on this boho shift dress is phenomenal! Forever 21 is my go-to place for inexpensive finds. If you look hard enough, you can find a bunch of gems that are completely versatile in nature.

With so many great choices, I am confident that this style will stick around for a long time. Where do you shop for long sleeve dresses? What is your favorite color to rock in this chilly season?
