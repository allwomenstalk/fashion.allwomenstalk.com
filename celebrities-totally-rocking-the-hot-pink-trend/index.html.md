# 7 Celebrities Totally Rocking the Hot Pink Trend ...

Are you a fan of the hot pink trend? It seems pink is all over the catwalk this season, and the high street isn’t far behind. From that sunglasses-at-the-ready neon pink to a slightly-less-garish blush pink, hot pink is definitely the color of the season, and it’s set to be around for a while yet. Need some style inspiration? Here’s how the celebs are rocking the hot pink trend…

## 1. Victoria Beckham…

![Victoria Beckham…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24191155_4734_500x821.jpg)

VB definitely knows how to make the hot pink trend her own. While she’s shied away from anything too garish, she’s been snapped in a variety of pink outfits this year, and every one has been chic and stylish. My personal favorite? Her tailored pink shift dress. Steal her style by wearing one with nude heels and a snuggly knit.

## 2. Selena Gomez…

![Selena Gomez…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24191128_8133_533x800.jpg)

If you’re a fan of true hot pink, you’ll love Selena’s style. Her wardrobe seems to be utterly packed with neon shades, from printed prom dresses to cut-out mini dresses and super-cute jumpers. The key to wearing such a bright color is to make sure your complexion is looking flawless, and keep the rest of your look effortless. Lipgloss and natural hair? Check.

## 3. Taylor Swift…

![Taylor Swift…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24191056_1531_500x689.jpg)

I love Taylor’s style. She switches effortlessly between vintage dresses and off-duty chic, and most of her outfits would look just as good at the supermarket as they do on the red carpet. Taylor was papped in a bright pink floral dress this season (kudos to her stylist!) and it would look great teamed with a white cardigan and pink lips. Definitely one to try.

## 4. Miley Cyrus…

![Miley Cyrus…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24190326_7970_600x1261.jpg)

Okay, so Miley’s style can be hit and miss, and it’s usually a little more miss than hit. She does have an AMAZING dress collection, though. Channel her casual chic look by buying an oversized hot pink dress and teaming it with nude makeup and an updo. Double points for layered hemlines!

## 5. Miranda Kerr…

![Miranda Kerr…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24144850_5831_500x626.jpg)

Looking for playsuit inspiration, or need a new way to wear shorts? Miranda Kerr has tropical outfits down. Her neon jumper, ankle boots and denim cut-offs look set a trend around the country, and it’s still one of my go-to looks. Hot pink and denim look great together, whatever the season!

## 6. Emma Watson…

![Emma Watson…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24144850_4388_500x688.jpg)

If you’ve got a pale complexion, Emma Watson should be your inspiration when it comes to wearing color. She’s great at pulling off a bold lip, and she doesn’t shy away from bold colors. She owned the hot pink trend with that striking high-neck silk dress, and I loved her matching lips and nails! If you’re looking for a smart outfit this season, this could be the perfect stand-out choice.

## 7. Jennifer Lawrence…

![Jennifer Lawrence…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24144850_4461_500x554.jpg)

Just want general inspiration on the hot pink trend? Check out Jennifer Lawrence. She has such a fun sense of style, and I love the way she mixes trends! Try opting for a hot pink dress with cut-out sections, for example, or a hot pink leather jacket. If Jen can look great, so can we!

If the fash-pack is right, the hot pink trend will be around until this time next year at the earliest, and it’s covering everything from dresses to coats and handbags and boots. Knowing how to wear it might take some learning, but finding a celeb whose style you love is a great way to get some inspiration! Are you rocking the hot pink trend? What looks are you loving?
