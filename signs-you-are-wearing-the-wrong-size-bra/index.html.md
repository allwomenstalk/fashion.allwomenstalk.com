# 10 Signs That Say Your Bra is the Wrong Size ...

Did you know that there are several **signs you are wearing the wrong size bra**? Your bra should feel comfortable but it should also support you. Too often, women wear theirs too loosely while other women wear theirs too tightly. Here are ten **signs you are wearing the wrong size bra**.

## 1. You Wear Your Bra on the Last Hook

This is one of the top **signs you are wearing the wrong size bra**. Over time it’ll loosen in the wash. If it’s new or fairly new and you’re already wearing it on the last hook it won’t last as long. The suggestion is to buy a smaller band size.

## 2. Your Straps Keep Falling down

If you have tightened your straps, which you will need to do now and then, and they still fall off of your shoulders then you need a smaller band size.

## 3. The Cup Bunches or Wrinkles

Go down one cup size.

## 4. The Underwire Pokes You

Your bra is too small.

## 5. Bra Back Bulge

The bra back bulge, or bbb, is not caused by being too chunky. It’s caused by your band size being too small.

## 6. The Underwire Rises when You Lift Your Arms

Your bra is too big.

## 7. The Straps Are Digging into Your Shoulders

This actually means that the straps are doing all of the work. You need a smaller band size.

## 8. Your Flopping All over when You Play Lacrosse

You need to wear a sports bra.

## 9. Your Neck Hurts

Your bra may not be giving you support.

## 10. You Boobs Fall out

Oopsie! It happens to us all, although it’s better if it happens in a dark theatre. It’s time to go up a cup size.
