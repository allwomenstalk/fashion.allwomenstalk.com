# 7 Super Cute Fall Fashion Finds to Be Excited about Now ...

I don’t know about you, but I’m crazy about fall, and this year, fall fashion trends have got me more excited than EVER! I love the coziness fall brings, when you can walk outside and bask in the glowing sun, with the wind gently whispering through your hair, and the golden hues of the leaves on the ground just seem to kiss your feet. Preferably, your feet are covered in a cute pair of fall boots like rider boots, or a sexy high heel boot, which are my personal favorites! This year, just like every fall, boots are totally in, as are oh-so feminine sweaters, and tons of stylish, yet sexy jewelry. I’m more excited than ever about this year’s fall finds and just can’t wait to see what fashion does to inspire us this season. Join me in celebrating fall a little early with these fall fashion trends you have to look forward to!

## 1. Ruby Toned Ensembles

![Ruby Toned Ensembles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/1_ruby-toned-ensembles_427x640.jpg)

This year, one of the biggest fall fashion trends is ruby- colored hues paired with more neutral, earthy tones, like this cute little dress ensemble paired with boots. I think this slightly burgundy, ruby dress would be perfect with any skin tone or hair color. I can imagine this dress would be stunning with a denim jacket and even a cute pair of leggings and ballet flats, if you wanted to go a different route as well. I’m more of a boots girl, but the possibilities are endless with this super sweet hue!

## 2. Antique Style Jewelry

![Antique Style Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_antique-style-jewelry_600x733.jpg)

This fall, jewelry is taking a few steps back in time with classy little antique pieces that are popping up everywhere in fashion magazines. Think long necklaces, pearls, lockets and the like. I love pieces of jewelry that look super feminine, while also looking classy and sophisticated. These fall fashion pieces definitely fit the bill!

## 3. Mustard and Navy Pairings

![Mustard and Navy Pairings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_mustard-and-navy-pairings_494x750.jpg)

Mustard yellow is one of my absolute favorite fall fashion colors, and no, it isn’t because I happen to love the condiment mustard too, if you’re wondering! Mustard yellow seems to bring out the best hues in most skin tones and hair colors. It is being paired with navy in this year’s fashion trends, and appeared in some fashion pieces the same way last year. I love this ensemble, which is totally random with a navy, striped shirt, a printed scarf, and a classic pair of earthy brown shoes.

## 4. Motorcycle Jackets

![Motorcycle Jackets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/4_motorcycle-jackets_600x827.jpg)

Motorcycle jackets have been in style for a few years, and they aren’t going out of style anytime soon. I love this ensemble, which features a classic, black jacket paired with a yellow skirt looks incredible! It looks totally laid back, while also super stylish too. Wear this with your hair up or down to show off this outfit’s killer neckline, and you’ll look like a fall fashion runway model in minutes!

## 5. Bold Belts

![Bold Belts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_bold-belts_522x800.jpg)

What girl doesn’t love a good belt to make her feel put together? This year, just like last year, big belts are totally in. I’m seeing more of them paired with classic,softer feminine pieces like this sweet and soft black dress. I love how down to earth this outfit feels, yet it would be so cute wearing on a night out too! These fall boots are really cute for those of us girls who live in the south, but girls from up north and the Midwest would look oh so cute in these as well!

## 6. Plaid is Back

![Plaid is Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_plaid-is-back_600x915.jpg)

Can I be honest? I’m not really a big plaid kind of girl, however this dress has me sold. Plaid can make or break an outfit, but if chosen correctly, such as this bright piece which is anything but drab, plaid can look so fashionable! This fall, try experimenting with plaid and enjoy the relaxed look and feel this style brings.

## 7. Safari Shirtdress

![Safari Shirtdress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_safari-shirtdress_485x750.jpg)

This safari style shirtdress would be perfect for fall! You could pair it with some knee high boots, a classy pair of stilettos, or a cute pair of flats, depending on the occasion. I adore how the earthy tone of this dress celebrates fall in one of its best colors. I can just picture this paired with a scarf and boots for a crisp, fall night’s breeze!

Fall fashion excites me and I love seeing the new styles released in magazines each year! What’s your favorite fall fashion find you’re looking forward to wearing this year?**
