# Forget Black: the 7 Best Leather Jacket Colors ...

Everybody loves leather, but it's boring to own the same exact jacket as everyone else. That's why you should follow [Womanitely's](https://womanitely.com/leather-jacket-colors-fall/) advice and buy a leather jacket in a different color than black. Here are a few of the trendiest ones:

## 1. White

![White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/hg/d72s5x03564e11b5378c3212319136_600x932.jpg)

This will strike the perfect balance between tough and innocent.

## 2. Purple

![Purple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m4/ez/x2nndann564e125a0a37d405890746_499x750.jpg)

You'll stand out in a jacket this bright!

## 3. Brown

![Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ck/yz/acghn7n0564e129768693364518241_600x911.jpg)

It's still a dark color, which is why it's perfect for this time of year.

## 4. Gray

![Gray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x3/vh/xf4otyzy564e130a5edcc769547839_500x749.jpg)

This is about as close to black as you can get.

## 5. Red

![Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/yq/r8q50gjz564e13d6a46d6395420753_600x875.jpg)

You'll look sexy and fierce in one of these babies!

## 6. Yellow

![Yellow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zg/iv/g81bdksw564e140ebb37c878711172_500x768.jpg)

If you want to brighten up your wardrobe, this will do the trick.

## 7. Dark Blue

![Dark Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/p0/txwv71u4564e144153de7376014890_600x1048.jpg)

Now that's what I call fashionable!
