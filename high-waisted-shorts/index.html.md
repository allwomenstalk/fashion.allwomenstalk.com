# 8 High Waisted Shorts ...

A pair of high waisted shorts is a wardrobe staple these days. Look for shorts that sit at your natural waist and that come in tailored styles. These shorts can easily be styled up or down and are versatile pieces to have in your wardrobe. Take a look at a few styles of high waisted shorts below.

These lavender wool-crepe shorts are elegantly tailored. They are a smart-casual choice for a day at the office, and are a chic choice of off-duty attire. These shorts have a fitted waistband, concealed zip fastening, and turned-up cuffs. They would look great paired with a silky black blouse and patent pumps.

## 2. Thread Social Jacquard Bow-Belt Shorts

![Thread Social Jacquard Bow-Belt Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/c1/u11lndkq54c9278c94f93298620381_400x424.jpg)

These jacquard shorts come in a luxe black and gold colourway. They are a high waisted style and feature ann exaggerated bow belt at the waist. They have two side slit pockets, concealed zip fastenings at the sides, and curved hems. Team these high waisted shorts with a fitted scoop-back top and black ankle boots for a fun nighttime look.

## 3. Topshop Peach Pleated Waistband Shorts

![Topshop Peach Pleated Waistband Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/y4/w6oideys54c9278dce490057638480_400x416.jpg)

These peach cupro shorts feature a distinctive horizontally pleated waistband. These shorts also feature subtle pleating from the waistband and side pockets. They are loose throughout the thigh and have a fairly flattering cut. The peach colour would co-ordinate well with an outfit consisting of this season's neutral colour pallete.

## 4. Topshop Black Shadow Floral Print Shorts

![Topshop Black Shadow Floral Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/06/36H29YBLK_large1_460x333.jpg)

## 5. Marc Jacobs High Waisted Cotton-Poplin Shorts

![Marc Jacobs High Waisted Cotton-Poplin Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/xd/l6x63n9v54c9278ea9f12430892833_400x355.jpg)

A pair of black high waisted shorts are a wardrobe staple. They are a great starting point for a range of basic day to day outfits. They have pleating at the front, side and back pockets, and a concealed zip fastening. These shorts also come with a detachable buckle-fastening belt. If these particular shorts are out of your price range, look for similar tailored styles within your budget.

## 6. Marc Jacobs Floret Short

_ **Price:** $495.00 at barneys.com_
These silk floret shorts are a playful number. They have a slight paperbag-style waist with self-tie belt. They also have front slash and rear welt pockets. These printed shorts are casually chic. Team them with a white top, light denim jacket, and lace up shoes for a fun day to day look.

## 7. Topshop Apricot Cotton Shorts

![Topshop Apricot Cotton Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rp/w3/dvmdtzig54c9279103716528395535_400x384.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/EbKCtp)

They also have pleat details and a button and zip fastening at the front. Channel a retro inspired look and pair these shorts with a loose tank top, wrap sandals, and floppy sun hat.

## 8. Diane Von Fursternberg Simca Stretch-Canvas Shorts
