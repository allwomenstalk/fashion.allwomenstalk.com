# 7 Printed Shorts ...

Add a fun twist to your summer outfit by wearing a pair of printed shorts. They look great paired with a basic t-shirt or singlet. From bold tribal-inspired prints to subtle florals, printed shorts can encompass a range of different trends. It doesn’t matter what fabric or style, as long as it’s printed! Take a look at a few fun printed shorts below.

## 1. Topshop Multi Pansy Waterfall Print Shorts

![Topshop Multi Pansy Waterfall Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tt/zy/jer6p1ms54c90680202eb591450900_300x302.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/bGB649)
These pansy floral printed shorts feature waterfall draping at the front. The draping adds depth to the overall design. The print is bright and bold, and perfect for summer. Team these shorts with a dark blue t-shirt, tan satchel, and strappy leather shoes.

## 2. Cacharel Printed Drawstring Shorts

**Price:** $320.00 at _lagarconne.com_
These shorts feature a colourful abstract print. They have an elastic drawstring waistband, faux fly, and side slit pockets. They sit at the hips and fit true to size. Dress these up by pairing them with a loose-fitting blouse and mid-heel ankle boots.

## 3. Diane Von Furstenberg Miggy Printed Cotton-Poplin Shorts

**Price:** $200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/lx2Ycm)
The graphic black and white print on these shorts is quite eye-catching. These shorts are quite fitted and sit at the natural waist. They have a concealed zip fly, gold button fastening, and slant pockets at the sides. Work a fashion-forward look and pair these shorts with a bright colour palette.

## 4. Moto Scatter Floral Hotpants

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/xmy2hh)
These denim printed shorts are a sweet summer option. They feature a scattered floral print and come in a classic denim short silhouette. They have a five pocket detail and come with authentic trims. Pair these shorts with a light coloured top and leather sandals for a casually chic look.

## 5. Giambattista Valli Giraffe-Print Rami Shorts

![Giambattista Valli Giraffe-Print Rami Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/re/zqd9v7e354c906845dbb5438407489_300x292.jpg)

**Price:** $860.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AyTZXG)
These giraffe print shorts are a quirky take on the animal print trend. They come in a black and white colourway and have a grosgrain trim at the waistband and slant pockets. They also feature pleating at the front, concealed zip fastening, and welt pocket at the back. Work a monochrome colour palette and pair these with a black singlet and white blazer. The look is distinctively urban.

## 6. Topshop Pink Lily Print Shorts

![Topshop Pink Lily Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ou/tn/wdxkpia454c9068566851969626071_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $66.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ZfYC9K)
These lily printed shorts have a tropical vibe about them. The have a zip fly and button fastening, fitted waistband, and side slit pockets. The pink colour is feminine; however the bold floral pattern ensures that these shorts aren’t saccharine sweet. These shorts would look great paired with a silky blouse and nude-coloured ballet flats.

## 7. Topshop Black Daisy Spot Print Shorts

![Topshop Black Daisy Spot Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iq/gm/m83i67l554c906868be29517853768_300x317.jpg)

**Price:** $72.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1BEU8K)
These black shorts are a more tailored take on the printed shorts trend. They feature a stylised daisy print and have a high-waisted design. They also have pleats at the front, a button waist detail, and side zip fastening. Pair these shorts with a basic blouse, lace up dress shoes, and leather shoulder bag for a casually chic look.

Printed shorts are great for adding extra interest to an outfit. They range from casual to dressy styles and can be worn in a variety of ways. Which of these printed shorts are your favourites?
