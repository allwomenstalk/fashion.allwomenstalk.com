# Simple Fashion Tricks to Make You Look Slimmer ...

You're here because you want some **fashion tricks to make you look slimmer**, right? Some might not like to admit it, but it would be fair to say that all girls, no matter what size or shape they are, find themselves concerned with how slimming their outfit choices are.

Wanting to look slimmer in a certain outfit doesn’t always have to stem from a place of insecurity or unhappiness, it can simply be a fun fashion trick to try out to see how effectively you can achieve certain looks within your own wardrobe. If you are interested in experimenting with slimming fashion and don’t know exactly where to start, then here are a few **fashion tricks to make you look slimmer**.

## 1. Avoid Sacks

Not wearing something that looks like a sack is one of the best **fashion tricks to make you look slimmer**. Sometimes a hidden figure can look even bigger than one that is being held tightly by material! I advocate somewhere in between for the best slimming look. Don’t go too baggy because it means that all of your proportions become hidden. The aim instead should be to find clothing that accentuates your best parts and delicately helps you hide your least favourite parts!

## 2. Go Vertical

Any horizontal seam, stripe, piping or deep V-neck will all work to stretch a bigger body out to a potentially unflattering degree, regardless of colour. For the best slimming results, make a note to opt for vertical patterns and styles every single time.

## 3. Equal Proportions

You can work your own optical illusion magic by learning how to make the most out of your proportions. For example, if you are a pear shape then offset your larger lower half by incorporating details that broaden your upper half, like flamboyant sleeves or boat necks. Alternatively, if you have a large, disproportionate bust, you can wear simple tops to even out the attention.

## 4. Disguise Your Thighs

The best way to even out your thighs in a slimming way is to aim for skirts that provide a pleasing mid-knee length. It’s at the knee where your leg starts to thicken out, so a mid-knee length shows off all the parts that can be most appealing.

## 5. Emphasize Your Waist

Emphasize your waist by making the most of belts in your outfits, because cinching in will help to create a more even balance between your upper half and lower half. Other things that help with distraction include colours and patterns. Vibrant maxi dresses with a belted waist are perfect.

## 6. Avoid Flimsy Materials

Flimsy, cheap fabrics tend to cling to a person’s body in all of the wrong places, so make sure that you invest in high-quality clothing that emphasizes and rests on all of the body parts that the design intends.

## 7. Shapewear

At the end of the day, there is only so much you can do with clothing alone, so you shouldn’t underestimate the amazing aesthetic impact that a perfectly fitted piece of shapewear can have on your overall look. Shapewear is a convenient and perfectly designed base from which to create a more slimming look.
