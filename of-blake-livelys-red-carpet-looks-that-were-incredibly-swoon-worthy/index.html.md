# 7 of Blake Lively's Red Carpet Looks That Were Incredibly Swoon\-worthy ...

Blake Lively's red carpet looks have proven that the talented actress is well on her way to becoming a style icon. She's looked amazing at every event this year - I'm always so eager to see what she's wearing! She's definitely a role model and inspiration for young fashionistas. Check out some of Blake Lively's red carpet looks and let me know which ones your favorites are. P.S the first three looks are all from the Cannes Film Festival and yes, they ALL deserve a spot on this list!

## 1. Cannes Film Festival

![Cannes Film Festival](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/wa/awr1nw93_600x902.jpg)

Blake wore a burgundy colored Gucci gown on day 1 of the Cannes Film Festival. It looks like this dress was tailor-made just for her, it fits so perfectly! I love that it's sophisticated and sexy at the same time. She kept the accessories to a minimum with some chunky earrings and a bracelet. That leg slit is the perfect way to show off those amazing shoes! This is just one of Blake Lively's red carpet looks.

## 2. Cannes Film Festival

![Cannes Film Festival](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sw/s9/iamkgm6j_594x808.jpg)

At the premiere of 'Mr. Turner', Blake rocked a sheer Chanel dress and looked fantastic. Personally, I don't think a lot of people could have pulled off this dress as effortlessly as Blake. I love that she kept her hair looking natural with some [loose curls](https://hair.allwomenstalk.com/summer-hair-loose-waves-and-curls-are-causing-a-stir/). What do you think of the dress? P.S is it just me or is it kind of awesome that photographers/press dress up in suits as well?!

## 3. Cannes Film Festival

![Cannes Film Festival](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ca/oq/u11l00dc_600x900.jpg)

If you ask me, Blake wins 'Best Dressed' at this year's Cannes Film Festival. Every single one of her looks was phenomenal. Blake always looks elegant and classy - I love this look because black and white is timeless. I like that she kept the accessories to a minimum; that dress is definitely enough of a fashion statement. I can't get enough of it!

## 4. Met Gala

![Met Gala](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/ok/stcxypej_600x900.jpg)

Blake looked absolutely stunning at this year's Met Gala. Her champagne colored Gucci gown was to die for! Of course, how could she look bad when she's got her hubby, Ryan Reynolds, by her side? The two are such a power couple. I love that Blake went for the old 'Hollywood-glam' look, rocking a bright red lipstick and tight curls.

## 5. 'savages' Premiere

!['savages' Premiere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/qb/gksnklgx_400x600.jpg)

For the premiere of 'Savages', Blake channeled Marilyn Monroe when picking her dress. She's great at pulling off the old "Hollywood-Glam" look. I'm obsessed with this nude dress. The black stripes complete the look and the red nails matching the bold red lip is everything. Wouldn't this make the perfect prom dress?

## 6. Met Gala 2013

![Met Gala 2013](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/d1/kll2xg39_600x824.jpg)

Blake always impresses at the Met Gala. Last year, she showed up in this gown and wowed everybody. I love when she wears her hair in a high ponytail because it's such a cool change from wearing her hair in loose curls! Do you prefer her hair up or down?

## 7. 'savage Beauty' Premiere

!['savage Beauty' Premiere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jn/pe/q5qfzth7_523x726.jpg)

Blake channeled her inner 'greek goddess' with this dress at the "Savage Beauty" premiere back in 2011. I'm absolutely in love with the way the dress drapes across her body. The color is stunning and looks great with her complexion!

Well, there you have it! These are just seven out of MANY swoon-worthy red carpet looks from Blake Lively. She's always been a fashionista (i.e. her role as Serena Van Der Woodsen) and has become a major style icon. I mean, the girl can do no wrong! Of the list, which look was your favorite?
