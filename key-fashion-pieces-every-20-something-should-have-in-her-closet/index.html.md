# 6 Key Fashion Pieces for Women in Their 20s ...

Wondering about the **key fashion pieces every 20-something should have in her closet**? All of these pieces will come in handy at several points in your life. These are key pieces that, despite your style, you are going to need in life. In your twenties, you may want to dress like your favourite fashion icon but there is a high chance that you are not there financially. Take note that these **key fashion pieces every 20-something should have in her closet** will suit women of all ages.

## 1. Black Blazer

![clothing, blazer, shoulder, jeans, fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/wh/l6gwmlrt5ac3e3817a1e6884648894_600x750.jpg)

"You cannot climb the ladder of success dressed in the costume of failure," Zig Ziglar once said. This will come in handy when you have a presentation at work or you are going for a job interview. They always say dress for your next job. Whether you're in a corporate field or not, a blazer will help you look like a professional and is one of the most important **key fashion pieces every 20-something should have in her closet**.

## 2. Little Black Dress

![fashion model, model, beauty, photo shoot, purple,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/hh/fz0xgxjn5ac3e3a432a86411528574_600x750.jpg)

Every woman needs a little black dress. You can never go wrong with one of these. Don't worry about length. Many people tend to move away from these because they think that it is not decent enough. But there are so many dresses for those who are conservative. You can dress it up and dress it down.

## 3. A White T-shirt

![car, motor vehicle, vehicle, mode of transport, vehicle door,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t7/hj/h7udj0av5ac3e3ceeabe4607137601_600x750.jpg)

It's simple and neat. It is very accessible for all women of all ages. There are so many ways to style it. When buying a white t-shirt, get one that is fitted. It shouldn't be too tight or too loose. A good fitting plain white tee goes with anything, fancy or casual.

## 4. Black Leather Jacket

![white, clothing, footwear, eyewear, shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/wh/xqud750k5ac3e4281b5d4027359818_600x617.jpg)

This is a great investment piece. Leather jackets make you stand out. They are versatile and can be worn all year long, regardless of the weather. This piece will serve you for years if you take care of it.

## 5. Scarf

![clothing, footwear, snapshot, jeans, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o5/p2/mapbihi05ac3e531366b6185231599_600x749.jpg)

It's a great accessory. It gives that final touch that takes your outfit from sixty percent to a hundred percent. You can decorate your bag with it or even wear it as a headpiece on a bad hair day.

## 6. A Pair of Black Sunglasses

![eyewear, sunglasses, vision care, glasses, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yb/xm/fadereif5ac3e5653b096781373745_600x750.jpg)

No one has ever looked bad with a pair of black sunglasses on their face. They attract the right kind of attention. They are cool so that cool factor will be transferred to you.
