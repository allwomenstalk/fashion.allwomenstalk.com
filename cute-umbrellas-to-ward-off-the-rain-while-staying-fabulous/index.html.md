# 7 Cute Umbrellas to Ward off the Rain While Staying Fabulous ...

When the weather gets rough, you’ll need some cute umbrellas to protect you from the rain. You wouldn’t want your favorite outfit to get ruined from a downpour. Mother Nature can be fickle, so it’s best to be prepared for any situation. Why not buy one of these cute umbrellas to keep fashionable while staying dry?

## 1. Animal Umbrellas

![Animal Umbrellas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/1_animal-umbrellas_300x276.jpg)

These cute umbrellas come in the shape of any animal you can think of. There are bears, ladybugs, and even bumblebees that can shield you from the rain. It might seem like something a child would use, but why not release your youthful side? These umbrellas are fun and should make passersby jealous. Check out [aliexpress.com](http://www.aliexpress.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) if you’re interested. If it's not something that you'd personally use, you can always buy one as a gift for a younger cousin or child.

## 2. Transparent Domes

![Transparent Domes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_transparent-domes_600x505.jpg)

These umbrellas are becoming more and more popular. As silly as they look, they're actually quite useful. Their sides come down further than a typical umbrella, which keeps you dryer. It also makes it so that your view isn't obstructed. Try searching [umbrellaheaven.com](https://www.umbrellaheaven.com/) for the perfect fit for you.

## 3. Ruffled Umbrella

![Ruffled Umbrella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_ruffled-umbrella_300x300.jpg)

What's better than something frilly and cute? This umbrella is perfect for anyone who likes to stay stylish. You can find it on Amazon.com in any color that you'd like.The best way to ward off the rain is with something as adorable as this, so why not purchase one? You'll be the talk of the town the next time it rains.

It'll make you stand out from all of the people with boring, black umbrellas. Why not make yourself seen?

## 5. Sword Umbrella

![Sword Umbrella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/5_sword-umbrella_590x339.jpg)

## 6. Flower Umbrellas

![Flower Umbrellas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_flower-umbrellas_250x250.jpg)

Bring out your girly side with one of these gorgeous umbrellas. They're strong against the wind, but have a dainty appearance. Search through [polyvore.com](https://goo.gl/OKn5R6) to find the one you think is prettiest. There's a large selection, so you might just have to buy two. Once you're done shopping, you'll be hoping for rain just so you can try out your new toys.

## 7. Lightsaber Umbrella

![Lightsaber Umbrella](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_lightsaber-umbrella_300x300.jpg)
