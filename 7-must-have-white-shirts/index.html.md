# 7 Must Have White Shirts ...

A white shirt is a wardrobe staple. When buying a white shirt, it’s important to find a cut and shape that suits your figure. It’s worth spending a bit extra for a well-crafted item of clothing. The cost-per-wear will make it the perfect investment piece!

## 1. Rag & Bone the Classic Shirt

![Rag & Bone the Classic Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/mu/xg4mtmy854c8f2d8cd518118271578_300x376.jpg)

**Price:** $185.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
There’s a reason this is called a classic shirt. The design is elegant and timeless. This one by Rag & Bone is made from soft 2-ply cotton gauze. It features a single front pocket, button down front, and button cuffs. This shirt is perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

## 2. Alexander Wang Essential Cotton Poplin Shirt

![Alexander Wang Essential Cotton Poplin Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/y7/qn7vx6f054c8f2d9c559b095801510_300x398.jpg)

**Price:** $265.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Alexander Wang is the purveyor of fashion cool. His wares are popular with models and fashionistas alike. What I love most about this classic white shirt is the concealed button down front. It really gives it a modern edge. Get in on the action with this essential shirt!

## 3. Max Azria Silk-chiffon Paneled Cotton-blend Shirt

![Max Azria Silk-chiffon Paneled Cotton-blend Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/nw/z284j25054c8f2da8994c558800572_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $255.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bQisAn)
This short sleeved shirt is a more casual take on the classic white shirt. I’m a big fan of anything oversized, and this shirt has won me over. The asymmetric panels also give it that unexpected, modern twist. It’s the perfect piece to take you through the summer months.

## 4. DKNY Pleated Cotton-blend Shirt

![DKNY Pleated Cotton-blend Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/np/mb/pzkwyr4254c8f2db72314331736221_300x438.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/0IHtID)
This crisp tuxedo-style shirt is a more formal version of the white shirt. I love the pleated detailing on the front. This cotton blend shirt is breathable and allows for stretch – making it perfectly wearable! Dress it up with tailored pants, or dress it down with jeans or a printed skirt.

## 5. Topshop Sleeveless Silk Shirt

![Topshop Sleeveless Silk Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/od/tt/bvetx9xb54c8f2dc54d7d657081527_300x446.jpg)

**Price:** $70.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Nzo9GZ)
This sleeveless silk shirt is all about casual luxury. It features a split-back hem and front pocket. This 100% silk blouse will feel absolutely luxurious against your skin. Pair it with high-waisted shorts or a pastel coloured skirt. It’s the perfect play shirt.

## 6. Aubin & Wills Sunnydale Silk-georgette Shirt

![Aubin & Wills Sunnydale Silk-georgette Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/qy/tb7gc7ix54c8f2dd2452b073259025_300x406.jpg)

**Price:** $165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/6VLhL1)
I absolutely adore this 100% silk shirt. It features some lovely gathering details at the sleeves and back of yoke. I love the addition of two front pockets. It gives it a bit more of a casual and military edge. This is one versatile shirt, and would make the perfect white staple shirt!

## 7. Charles Anastase Popeline Blouse

![Charles Anastase Popeline Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/gg/r7sj0yrw54c8f2de0431b450552884_300x379.jpg)

**Price:** $568.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a more dramatic take on the white shirt. The addition of the removable bib is quite an eye catching detail. The pearl buttons also add a delicate, refined touch. There’s just so much to love about this shirt!
