# 8 Incredibly Awesome Ways to Style Your Lovely Sheer Tops for Winter ...

Sheer tops were a big trend this past summer. When it comes to transitional dressing, however, your once favourite sheer tops may be a bit harder to style. When looking to style your sheer tops this winter, play up textural contrasts and team your sheer chiffons and silks with distressed leather, faux fur, or heavy wools. Depending on the design of your sheer top, there are a range of ways to style them.

## 1. Peeking out

![Peeking out](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/blouse-sweater-02_540x390.jpg)

Work one of the chicest looks this season and wear a collared sheer top underneath a warm sweater. Just make sure the collar is peeking out the top. This look works best with a sweater that has a high round neckline. If your sheer top has long sleeves, also think about letting them poke out from beneath your sweater cuffs too.

## 2. Buttoned up

![Buttoned up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/buttoned-up_500x360.jpg)

Man-style dressing is a hot trend this season, and one way to work this look is by buttoning your collared shirts all the way up. While the summer months may have been too warm to pull of this look, winter is the perfect time to try it out. I think this look works especially well with sheer tops as the soft fabric is still quite feminine.

## 3. With a Faux Fur Vest

![With a Faux Fur Vest](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/faux-fur_500x375.jpg)

The combination of sheer fabric and faux fur is the perfect play off of textures. Team your favourite long sleeved sheer top with a shaggy faux fur vest this winter. The sheer sleeves sticking out from the vest will create an interesting contrast.

## 4. Layered

![Layered](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/sheer-over-dress1_1000x748.jpg)

One way to stay warm this winter is to wear your sheer tops with a warm knit underneath. Also think about wearing different coloured tops underneath to create interesting colour contrasts. Alternatively, wear a loose-fitting sheer top over a figure-hugging knit dress and teamed with a cropped jacket for an artfully layered look.

## 5. With Leather

![With Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/leather-over-sheer_578x435.jpg)

Leather is the ultimate staple for transitional dressing. A sheer top paired with a leather jacket is the ultimate stand-by outfit. Tucking your long-sleeved sheer tops into elbow-length leather gloves is another fashion-forward option this winter.

## 6. Mod-Style

![Mod-Style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/A-line_520x693.jpg)

Take a sheer blouse with a pussy-bow neck and team it with an A-line tweed skirt and knee high boots for a modern take on Mod dressing. A wool swing coat will finish the look of perfectly.

## 7. Sheer on Sheer

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/fashion/2011/10/BlackBeige-detail_640x480.jpeg)
Work not one, but two of your favourite sheer pieces this winter by layering them over each other. Try a fitted, lacy long-sleeved top underneath a flouncy short-sleeved chiffon top. Clashing prints will be a big trend this winter, so take this into account when matching your sheers.

## 8. With Knits

![With Knits](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2011/10/cardigan1_629x453.jpg)

One sure-fire way to stay warm in winter is to team your sheer tops with an array of woolly garments. An oversized cable knit cardigan, circle scarf, and gloves are all guaranteed to keep you cozy warm.

As you can see, there are a variety of ways to style your sheer tops for winter. A bit of experimentation can see your favourite summer pieces transition into the cooler months. What will you be doing with your sheer tops come winter?
