# 8 Sweet Holiday Themed Sweaters ...

Holiday themed sweaters usually get a bad rap. Snowman knits? Reindeer motifs? They’re not what you’d usually associate with fashion and style. However, the recent trend towards novelty sweaters this season makes the holiday themed sweater a bit more acceptable. Try a simple printed motif or a stylish pattern to make this trend work. Embrace the holiday season with these stylish holiday themed sweaters.

## 1. Star Sweater

![Star Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/1_star-sweater_230x327.jpg)

Choosing a loosely themed festive sweater like on with a star print will make it much more wearable. When it’s not topping your Christmas tree, make sure it’s on your clothing for a fun take on holiday themed sweaters. Get your star print fix with sweaters by Richard Nicoll, Wildfox, or BDG at Urban Outfitters.

## 2. Crystal Embellished Sweater

![Crystal Embellished Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/2_crystal-embellished-sweater_600x776.jpg)

Add a bit of sparkle to your look with an embellished sweater this holiday season. It doesn’t have to be over the top embellishment either, like this Topshop sweater shows with its sparse mixed crystal details. One the higher end of the price spectrum you can expect to shell out anywhere from $100-$500 for version by Topshop or Meadham Kirchoff. You can, however, find similar, more affordable styles from Urban Outfitters and Dorothy Perkins.

## 3. Fair Isle Knit Sweater

![Fair Isle Knit Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/3_fair-isle-knit-sweater_600x776.jpg)

The Fair Isle print is pretty typical of festive and holiday themed sweaters. They’re also a great way to incorporate prints and patterns into your winter outfits. From a couple of hundred dollars for a Proenza Schouler number to more affordable versions from Old Navy and Topshop, there’s a Fair Isle sweater to suit all budgets.

## 4. Reindeer Print Sweater

![Reindeer Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/4_reindeer-print-sweater_230x345.jpg)

The reindeer is the animal of the season come the holidays so what better way to wear the trend than with a sweet reindeer print? While this Chinti and Parker reindeer sweater costs a whopping $560, rest assured you can find more budget friendly reindeer sweaters from stores like Modcloth and ASOS.

## 5. Gingerbread Print Sweater

![Gingerbread Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/5_gingerbread-print-sweater_230x345.jpg)

A gingerbread print is a fun take on holiday themed sweaters. This sweet sweater by Moschino costs close to $600 but you can find a very similar and more affordable version at Modcloth for a fraction of the cost.

## 6. Holly Sweater

![Holly Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/6_holly-sweater_600x833.jpg)

Really get into the swing of the holiday season with a Christmas holly print sweater. This one from Topshop is pretty pricey at $100, but I’m sure you could find similar styles by raiding thrift stores or your grandma’s wardrobe. Make it less dowdy and more stylish by teaming it with skinny jeans or leather skirts.

## 7. Motto Sweater

![Motto Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/7_motto-sweater_230x312.jpg)

Not a fan of showy motifs or prints? Then try a motto sweater instead. As far as holiday themed sweaters go, this ‘Ho! Ho! Ho!’ sweater by Lot87 is a quirky way of channelling your inner Santa Claus.

## 8. Snowflake Sweater

![Snowflake Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/8_snowflake-sweater_230x312.jpg)

Snowflakes are another popular motif for holiday themed sweaters. Stick to sweaters in simple colourways to make this look more wearable. This sweater by Banjo & Matilda will set you back a couple of hundred dollars but you can find similar, more affordable takes on this trend at Madewell and Land’s End.

These are just a few fun and quirky holiday themed sweaters to get you into the festive spirit. What do you think of these holiday themed sweaters? Will you be wearing a holiday themed sweater this season?
