# 7 Embroidered Clothes to Give Your Outfits Boho Flair ...

Embroidered clothes, previously thought of as kitschy and dated, are enjoying a comeback of sorts. The resurgence of embroidered clothes has a lot to do with the vintage revival and the popularity of bohemian-inspired dressing. Embroidered clothes are eye-catching and can be a great way of adding some flair to your outfit. Take a look at these embroidered clothes that will give your contemporary outfits some boho flair.

## 1. Modcloth ‘Judy Blue Skies’ Dress

![Modcloth ‘Judy Blue Skies’ Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/ag/fg/cfow99k4_350x500.jpg)

Embroidered clothes can be quite eye-catching, like this Modcloth dress. The navy dress comes in a demure design with a fitted bodice and softly gathered skirt. The pink and red embroidery features along the bust and hemline, and really pops out against the navy blue. Wear this dress with a light denim jacket and ankle boots for a relaxed, festival-inspired look.

## 2. Topshop Flower Embroidery Tee

![Topshop Flower Embroidery Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cu/mk/mb/j8kssfg0_599x673.jpg)

For an understated take on this season’s embroidered trends, you can’t go past this Topshop tee. It has a simple design with a round neck, short sleeves, and relaxed fit. It comes in a neutral cream colour with striking floral embroidery on the front. Team this tee with cigarette trousers, heels, a compact clutch, and drop earrings for a casually elegant evening outfit.

## 3. Alice + Olivia Embroidered Jacket

![Alice + Olivia Embroidered Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0o/j2/vw/bi58ou3c_600x750.jpg)

Add some boho flair to your outfit with this embroidered jacket from Alice + Olivia. It has a draped open front with long sleeves and an all-over paisley embroidered pattern. Wear this jacket during the day over a slouchy tank top and jeans or at night over a chic little black dress.

## 4. Topshop Embroidered Midi Skirt

![Topshop Embroidered Midi Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eq/e7/k6/l7userj3_599x880.jpg)

The embroidery on this Topshop skirt is subtle but would still manage to add a boho touch to your look. The skirt is made from sheer grey fabric and comes with a short slip underneath. Floral embroidery is scattered throughout the skirt. Sheer skirts are really popular right now and you could wear this skirt with a tucked in tank top, biker jacket, and heeled ankle boots for a more urban take on boho dressing.

## 5. Melissa Odabash Embroidered Kaftan

![Melissa Odabash Embroidered Kaftan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/hq/jh/h1xaxipn_599x734.jpg)

This embroidered kaftan by Melissa Odabash is probably what you’d expect of hippie, boho embroidered trends. It has a breezy, relaxed fit and features red and black embroidery throughout. Wear it over your swimsuit at the beach in summer or layer it up with chunky cardigans, jeans, and slouchy boots in winter.

## 6. RVCA Embroidered Flare Pants

![RVCA Embroidered Flare Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/im/op/jeajl9ks_233x650.jpg)

The retro revival is alive and well if these embroidered pants by RVCA are any indication. The low rise pants come in a flared cut and feature a smocked waistband that allows you to easily pull them on. They feature Moroccan embroidered detailing to the front of the legs and come in both black and rust colourways. Channel your inner flower child and team these pants with a peasant blouse, flat sandals, and floppy felt hat.

## 7. Nanette Lepore Embroidered Mini Skirt

![Nanette Lepore Embroidered Mini Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/v7/e5/fj3qncnq_600x750.jpg)

This embroidered mini skirt by Nanette Lepore has a more tribal-inspired element to the design. It comes in a sleek A-line design and features a bold multi-coloured embroidered pattern. You could try wearing it with a tucked-in chambray shirt and ballet flats for a polished daytime look.

Whether you go for subtle embroidered elements or full-blown boho styling, embroidered clothes are a guaranteed way of adding some flair to your outfits. Are you a fan of embroidery and would you wear any of these embroidered clothes?
