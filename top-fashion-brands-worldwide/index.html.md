# Top Fashion Brands Worldwide

The fashion industry is dominated by huge brands that have revolutionized fashion over the years. Some brands focus on making only luxury brands within reach of the elite class, others such as Nike, make products within reach for most people.

Other upcoming fashion brands deserve worldwide recognition. If you would like to know more about these fashion brands, you should keep a keen eye on fashion events such as the [Fashion Brand in NYC](https://www.nililotan.com/) or Paris fashion week and so on.

If you love fashion and style, the following are the top fashion brands you should know about.

## 1. Nike

Nike is an American fashion giant that is widely known for its high-ranking sportswear. It has worn world cups by sponsoring different soccer teams. It is involved in different charitable activities. Recently, the company donated over $15 million for Covid-19 response.

Nike is one of the top fashion brands involved in the development, design, manufacturing, marketing, and sales of its products. The Nike brand deals with shoes, accessories, apparel, equipment, and services.

## 2. Balenciaga

Balenciaga is a Spanish brand founded by Cristobal Balenciaga in 1917. The company operates under its parent company, Kering. Balenciaga continues to uphold the high-level fashion status and is among the top fashion luxury brand recognized globally.

## 3. Burberry

Burberry is a British fashion house established in 1856 by Thomas Burberry. The 150 years' old brand is still making waves in the industry not only with its iconic trench coat but other ready-to-wear apparel and accessories. The Burberry's chief creative officer is Riccardo Tisci, whose drive is to lead the brand's initiatives forward.

## 4. Gucci

Gucci is an Italian fashion luxury brand established in 1921. The brand started by making luxury travel accessories and goods for wealthy Italians. The parent company for Gucci is the French Kering, which owns another luxury brand such as Balenciaga. Gucci generates revenue of approximately $13.66 billion, and it is famously recognized for its extravagant styles and accessories.

## 5. Louis Vuitton

In recent years, the luxury fashion brand Louis Vuitton has scaled to heights where no other brands can reach. It is one of the highly valued brands today in the world of fashion. The brand is famous for making luxury luggage bags, accessories, apparel, and shoes. The Louis Vuitton Monogram LV stands out in this luxury brand.

## 6. Hermes

Hermes is a French Luxury brand with worldwide recognition in top fashions. The brand deals with home furnishings, fashion accessories, perfume, jewelry, watches, ready-to-wear clothes, handbags, and travel luggage bags.

## 7. Prada

Prada is an Italian top fashion brand that enjoys worldwide recognition. The brand prides itself on creating high-quality products to facilitate freedom of expression. The Prada brand specializes in making luxury leather bags, handbags, accessories, perfumes, shoes, travel accessories, and ready to wear outfits.

Conclusion

The discussed above are among the top fashion brands worldwide. Some of these brands are quite old and have worked on their brands to become the world's re-known brands. These fashion brands have it all when it comes to fashion accessories, ready-to-wear outfits, shoes, travel bags, and handbags.
