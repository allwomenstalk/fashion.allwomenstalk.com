# 7 Cocktail Dresses to Wear for the Holidays ...

Become the life of the party in these cocktail dresses to wear for the holidays. No holiday soiree or festivity is complete without the gorgeous glamour of a chic cocktail dress. It’s time to get your frock on and slip into one of these cocktail dresses to wear for the holidays.

## 1. Lace Cocktail Dress

![Lace Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_lace-cocktail-dress_500x500.jpg)

Shimmy into lace when looking for the perfect party frocks and cocktail dresses to wear for the holidays. A simple, sexy, sleek lace number is an effortlessly versatile piece that can take you from meetings in the office to after hours cocktails. Opt for lace dresses with contrasted overlays like black lace against a nude slip to amp up the sex appeal during the holidays, like the H&M cocktail dress above priced at $56. For additional chic cocktail options, check Topshop, Alice + Olivia, Haute Hippie, John Lewis, Notte by Marchesa and Rag & Bone with dresses in the $100 to $900 range.

## 2. Sequin Cocktail Dress

![Sequin Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_sequin-cocktail-dress_600x900.jpg)

Turn up the sparkle in a sequin cocktail dress for the holidays. Show off your holiday spirit in a dazzling dress that sparkles and shines all the time. Enchant the crowd and become the life of the party with a shimmering sequin cocktail dress that’s fun and flirty to spread holiday cheer for all.

## 3. Ruffled Cocktail Dress

![Ruffled Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_ruffled-cocktail-dress_600x960.jpg)

Deck your wardrobe with tinsel-inspired trimmings like ruffles for a luxe textured cocktail dress. For a frock that’s both feminine and flirty, a ruffled cocktail dress adds romantic drama to your soirees this season. Luxe, voluminous layers create lively feminine flair and high-spirited fun for the ultimate party girl. Pair a ruffled cocktail dress with minimal accessories to hit all the right notes during the holidays.

## 4. Draped Cocktail Dress

![Draped Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_draped-cocktail-dress_230x345.jpg)

Step out in a draped cocktail dress that’ll stun them coming and going with sexy skin. A draped cocktail dress is super sleek and feminine with its figure hugging displays and breezy, flowy materials. This silhouette is your go-to dress for effortless elegance during the holiday season. Elevate your holiday dressing with sparkling accents to play up your draped cocktail dress’ “it” factor.

## 5. Flared Cocktail Dress

![Flared Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_flared-cocktail-dress_600x800.jpg)

Dance the night away in a flared cocktail dress during the holidays. For extra volume to amp-up your holiday look, a flared cocktail dress is a sweetly feminine option that enhances ladylike curves and sophistication. The full skirt on a flared cocktail dress is reminiscent of the trendy retro dressing seen on the runway this past year and is an elegant option for maximum movement. Display your soft side in a richly luxurious texture like plush velvet in a festive berry tone for true holiday style.

## 6. One Shoulder Cocktail Dress

![One Shoulder Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_one-shoulder-cocktail-dress_230x345.jpg)

Forget the little black dress, a red one-shouldered number boldly announces your arrival. Amplify evening glamour with black accessories like strappy heels and a leather clutch to let this fiery statement dress speak for itself. No need for the cold shoulder as you can turn heads after dark in a racy red number that bares and dares with its asymmetrical detail.

## 7. Metallic Cocktail Dress

![Metallic Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_metallic-cocktail-dress_230x345.jpg)

Fast-forward into the holiday season with a metallic cocktail dress that’s all flash and fabulous. Get your shine on in a sweet, short frock with a gilded finish that’ll dazzle in any light. For when a little sparkle goes a long way, opt for a metallic cocktail dress in regal gold for a festive take on the new season.
