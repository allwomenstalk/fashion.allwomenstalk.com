7 Fabulous Holiday Fashion Tips to Shine like a Star This Season ...

A Crisp White Button down Shirt • A Black Sweater • Leopard Print Pants • Red Heels • A Sequin Scarf • More ...

By Chelsie

During the holiday season there are always so many parties and functions to attend, and it is always nice to have some holiday fashion tips so you can show up to any get-together looking amazing. When I think about holiday fashion, I think about basic pieces that I can mix and match together, but I want them to have just a little something extra. It is amazing how the right accessory or piece of clothing can make you look as bright as the holiday lights. I love getting together with people over the holidays, but because the season is so busy I like having some simple holiday fashion tips that will have me looking great in no time.

1. A Crisp White Button down Shirt

Never underestimate the power of a great white button down shirt. They may seem rather plain, but in reality they are very classy. Having one on hand is one of the best holiday fashion tips. It amazes me how pairing a white button down shirt with a pair of jeans and some heels can have me looking ready for an impromptu holiday lunch with friends in minutes. I always make sure to iron my shirt before putting it on, because the crispness of the shirt is part of its magic. Another great aspect of the white shirt is that you can easily add your own spin, such as popping the collar or rolling up the sleeves. It is your choice how to style it, but a crisp white button down shirt will have you looking great no matter what!

2. A Black Sweater

Like the white shirt, a black sweater can update any outfit and have you looking holiday ready in no time. A good black sweater can be paired with anything from leggings to a pencil skirt and have you looking fabulous. You can choose to wear all black and look really chic for the holidays, or you can wear a black sweater with colored jeans for a more relaxed holiday vibe. I love that black sweaters look great on all skin tones, and I also don’t mind the slimming effect they have. Cookies and eggnog are abundant during the holidays, and who doesn’t like to indulge!

3. Leopard Print Pants

I know that leopard print pants are much more daring than a white shirt or a black sweater, but they are great pants for the holidays. They have a notice me quality that is perfect during the holiday season. Just be sure to keep the leopard print pants the only article of clothing that has a print. This ensures you will stand out in an amazing way, and not look like you are trying too hard. I will admit that I am a quiet girl, but even I love to wear a great pair of leopard print pants to a holiday party!

4. Red Heels

What girl doesn’t love a great pair of red heels? They can take any outfit up three notches in an instant, and they are especially festive for the holidays. They can dress up jeans for a tree decorating party or add that little something extra to a holiday party dress. I have to admit, my red heels hold a special place in my wardrobe; I just avoid wearing them with green because I feel that can be a little tacky!

5. A Sequin Scarf

Scarfs are a great way to add interest to an outfit anytime of the year, but a sequin scarf is an especially great accessory during the holidays. It is reminiscent of the holiday lights, and directs light toward your face. A sequin scarf looks great peeking out from a coat, or loosely tied around your neck and paired with a simple sweater. Perhaps you might consider pairing it with that black sweater I mentioned earlier? I love the festive quality of a sequin scarf, and I also love that it keeps my neck warm during the holiday season.

6. Chandelier Earrings

There is something so special about a pair of sparkly, chandelier earrings. They have this amazing ability to make anything look special. While I don’t recommend going to your office holiday party in sweat pants, I swear they even make sweat pants look better. You could, however, wear chandelier earrings with a basic black dress at your holiday office party and look spectacular. You can even keep your makeup simple because the earrings add enough sparkle to your face that you don’t need much else!

7. Brooches

I used to think of brooches as an accessory my grandma wore on her wool coat. Now, however, I know that they can add interest to even the most basic of outfits, and they can make you look ready for a holiday get-together in under 60 seconds. Placing a diamond shaped brooch at the tip of the V on a V-neck shirt can look really great, or you could place two small matching brooches on your shirt collar to make it look a little more festive for the holidays. Feel free to experiment and raid your grandma’s jewelry box. I have on more than one occasion!

The holiday season is so fun and joyful, and there are so many parties to attend. If you’re like me, you want to look great for the holidays without spending a lot of time getting ready. I have found that these fashion tips keep me looking great during the holidays and are easy enough that I am not getting dressed for hours. What is your favorite holiday outfit?
