# 8 Striped Pieces to Wear This Spring ...

Stay on top of the trends this season and pick up some striped pieces for spring. There’s no such thing as too many stripes when it comes to your new season wardrobe. This season the focus is on bold stripes in mainly monochrome colour palettes. Get your new season wardrobe sorted and check out these striped pieces for spring.

## 1. Striped Shell Top

![Striped Shell Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/1_striped-shell-top_600x900.jpg)

As far as striped pieces for spring go, you can’t go past a striped top. Instead of a classic Breton stripe, try a bold stripe like the one on this sleeveless cropped top from Topshop. Team it with high waisted jeans, pants, or even a cute full skirt. Check out Nasty Gal and ASOS for similar striped tops.

## 2. Vertical Striped Shirt

![Vertical Striped Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/2_vertical-striped-shirt_600x732.jpg)

If you’re going to pick up a new striped top, try a vertical stripe instead of your classic horizontal design. Vertical stripes are a fresh way to wear your stripes this season. From bargain buys at Forever 21 and Urban Outfitters to designer options from Marc Jacobs, there’s a vertical striped shirt for all budgets. Wear them loosely tucked into jeans, skirts, and shorts for a casual spring look.

## 3. Striped Dress

![Striped Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/3_striped-dress_600x900.jpg)

A dress is an easy way to make a statement in stripes this season. This dress from Topshop features bold horizontal and vertical stripes and a cheeky cut out back. The best part is it won’t break the bank. Check out ASOS, Modcloth, and Gap for plenty of stylish and affordable striped dresses.

## 4. Striped Skirt

![Striped Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/4_striped-skirt_600x800.jpg)

Make a striped skirt the hero piece of your wardrobe this spring. You can easily pull off a nautical inspired outfit just by teaming your striped skirt with a plain white t-shirt. This particular striped skirt by Jil Sander costs a small fortune at $990. While plenty of high end designers feature striped skirts in their new season collections, you can find a range of affordable and equally stylish striped skirts online at ASOS.

## 5. Striped Jeans

![Striped Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/5_striped-jeans_600x900.jpg)

Add some striped jeans to your transitional spring wardrobe. Statement pants are all the rage nowadays and these jeans from Topshop are funky way to wear the striped trend. Team them with a denim shirt, flat sandals, and a messenger bag for a chic, off-duty look.

## 6. Striped T-Shirt

![Striped T-Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/6_striped-t-shirt_452x645.jpg)

The striped t-shirt is almost a must-have in any wardrobe. It’s comfortable, easy to style, and always in fashion. Since the sport luxe trend is still going strong, try a wide striped t-shirt like this Tsumori Chisato one (although, you might prefer to opt for a cheaper version than this $234 one). Wear your t-shirts relaxed and oversized, and roll up the sleeves for extra swagger.

## 7. Striped Jacket

![Striped Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/7_striped-jacket_600x810.jpg)

Throwing on a striped jacket is an easy way to freshen up your look. Classic styles like the blazer look smart and will easily dress up an outfit. Keep an eye out for other styles like bomber and biker jackets for a fresh take on the stripe trend. While designers like Lanvin and Marc Jacobs are out of many of our budgets, stores like Topshop, Gap, and Old Navy are all great places to find a striped jacket on the cheap. Wear your striped jackets with anything from a pretty sundress to chic tuxedo pants and heels.

## 8. Striped Shoes

![Striped Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/02/8_striped-shoes_345x320.jpg)

Striped shoes may not always be the most versatile kind of shoe, but they sure are fun to look at! Wear them to liven up a basic outfit. These American Apparel lace-ups cost just under $100 but check out Lucky Brand and Dolce Vita for similar, more affordable styles.

Be daring with your stripes this spring. Wear two types of stripe at once or choose a really bold, wide striped pattern. How would you style these striped pieces for spring?
