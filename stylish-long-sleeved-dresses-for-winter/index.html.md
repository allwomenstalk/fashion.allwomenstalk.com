# 7 Stylish Long\-Sleeved Dresses for Winter ...

If you’re looking for long-sleeved dresses for winter, you’re in luck. This winter there are plenty of styles of dress to choose from. From slinky figure-hugging slips to full-skirted skater dresses, there’s a style and cut to suit all tastes. Whether they’re for layering or making a statement, check out the following long-sleeved dresses for winter. They’ll keep you looking stylish and warm all season long – what’s not to love?

## 1. Skater Dress

![Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/1_skater-dress_328x400.jpg)

When looking at long-sleeved dresses for winter, a simple skater dress will work for most occasions. Team it with a wool blazer, tights, and ballet flats for a smart daytime look or dress it up with a faux fur stole and statement heels. There are a range of dresses to suit all tastes and budgets, starting at $20 for this Forever 21 number to slightly more expensive versions at ASOS and Topshop.

## 2. Bodycon Dress

![Bodycon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/2_bodycon-dress_600x900.jpg)

A bodycon long-sleeved dress is the perfect layering piece for winter dressing. Wear them underneath vests, cardigans, blazers, jackets – you name it, you can layer it. You don’t have to break the bank with a designer Hervé Léger number either, with stores like Forever 21, Reiss, and Topshop all selling a range of plain and printed bodycon dresses.

## 3. Lace Dress

![Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/3_lace-dress_350x500.jpg)

Lace dresses never go out of style. A ladylike lace dress is great option for this winter. Try a coloured lace dress instead of your basic black for something a little bit different. Wear them with opaque tights and pumps, and accessorise with ladylike jewels and compact handbags. This dress from Modcloth will set you back around $70 and you can find other lovely lace dresses at ASOS and Shopbop too.

## 4. Sweater Dress

![Sweater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/4_sweater-dress_230x345.jpg)

Winter is the time to rug up in cosy clothes. So why not keep warm in a sweater dress? While they can come across as shapeless, you can create shape by cinching them in, choosing a more fitted style, or wearing a structured jacket over the top. Choose one in a style that suits you and team them with tights and ankle boots for a chic off-duty look. Since a $600 Chinti and Parker dress is out of the question for many of us, try American Apparel and Free People for some more affordable options.

## 5. Cocktail Dress

![Cocktail Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/5_cocktail-dress_230x345.jpg)

For more formal occasions, a long-sleeved cocktail dress can be a stylish option. Look out for long-sleeved dresses in slinky fabrics with plunging necklines. You can’t go wrong with a basic black dress but if you’re after something a bit more daring then a dress in a bold colour is the way to go. Spend as much or as little as you’d like with dresses by Issa and L’Agence or ASOS and Forever 21.

## 6. Shirt Dress

![Shirt Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/6_shirt-dress_600x900.jpg)

For an everyday dress, try a loose shirt dress like this one from Topshop. Create a cool down-town look by teaming it with leather-look leggings, ankle boots, and a slouchy beanie. If loose fitting styles are your thing, try a dress with a full pleated skirt for a more feminine look – stores like Modcloth and Forever 21 have some cute styles available at the moment.

## 7. Maxi Dress

![Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/7_maxi-dress_350x500.jpg)

Winter is the perfect time to break out the long-sleeved maxi dress. Stick to heavy knitted styles with a column-like shape rather than lightweight, flowy dresses. For an edgy winter look, wear such maxi dresses with leather jackets and boots. This one costs $100 at Modcloth but you can get similar, more affordable styles at Topshop and ASOS.

When it comes to the long-sleeved dress there are plenty of styles to choose from. Plain dresses in figure-hugging styles are great for wearing under winter layers while lace and skater styles make for perfect party dresses. How would you style these long-sleeved dresses for winter?
