# 8 of the Best Stores for a Quick Fashion Fix ...

**Fashion Stores** vary in both the stock they carry and their purpose. Some **fashion stores** are more suited to high-end investment pieces, while others are great for last minute fashionable buys. There is a huge emphasis these days on creating a capsule wardrobe, and incorporating seasonal trends in the form of shoes and accessories. However, as we all know, getting dressed can become a costly exercise. If you're after a quick fashion fix, the following are just a few **fashion stores** that you can't go past.

## 1. [Urban Outfitters](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Urban Outfitters stocks a range of both men's and women's clothing, as well as electronics and homewares. The store caters to a more youth-oriented crowd, stocking a range of affordable and niche designers. Stores can be found through the U.S.A. and Europe, with an online store catering to international customers as well.

## 2. [Topshop](http://www.urbanoutfitters.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

With fashion stores dotted all around the world, Topshop has become synonymous with fast fashion. New season trends can be found in-store as fast as they come off the catwalk. With a range of collections inspired by the high-end designer brands, Topshop is your one-stop-shop for new trends at a reasonable price point.

## 3. [Forever 21](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Forever 21 fashion stores are perfect for seeking out affordable fashion. While there may seem to be a lot of 'clutter', with a bit of searching and a keen eye for new trends, you're guaranteed to find key pieces to update your new season wardrobe with.

## 4. [Zara](http://www.zara.com/)

The Zara brand is renowned for its fast rate of manufacturing. Key trends can be found in stores within weeks of appearing on the designer runways. Infamous for imitating many designer fashions, this chain store is popular with customers in its ability to bring designer-inspired fashion to the masses.

## 5. [H&M](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

H&M is a Swedish retailing company that offers clothing for men, women, and children. Much like the other chain stores on this list, H&M fashion stores cater towards fast fashion at budget prices. This store stocks a range of items from seasonal pieces to more classic styles and basics.

## 6. [ASOS](http://www.hm.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Asos has quickly become a fast-fashion mecca, despite being a solely online store. Stocking its house brand along with a range of other designers, this site caters to nearly every fashion genre. If you're in the UK, you're in luck because they also offer next day delivery.

## 7. _Net-a-Porter_

If it's high-end designer fashion that you're after, then this is your one-stop-shop. One of the first online stores dedicated solely to designer wares, Net-A-Porter has cemented itself as one of the leading online shopping destinations. You're bound to find all the newest designer collections here. They also offer fast international shipping.

## 8. [Target](https://www.target.com/)

Now, Target may seem a bit dowdy compared to the rest of the fashion stores on this list, but it's one worth taking note of. If you care to look, you'll find a fair few trend-based pieces and basics in stores. Target also collaborates with a range of designers to create capsule collections that are definitely worth checking out.

While the turnover rates and sustainability of some **fashion stores** can be questioned, there's no doubt that they're a saviour for many fashionistas on a budget. Even if you've got a bit more money to splurge, online **fashion stores** such as Net-A-Porter are great for designer buys that get delivered quickly to your door. What **fashion stores** would you recommend for a quick fashion fix?
