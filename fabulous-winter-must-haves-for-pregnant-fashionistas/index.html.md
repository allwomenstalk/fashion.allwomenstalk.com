# 7 Fabulous Winter Must\-Haves for Pregnant Fashionistas ...

Firstly, many congratulations on your little bundle of joy! I know it may seem hard to stick to your fashionista tag when you’re pregnant but fashionable clothes for pregnant women are easier to find than you think. All you need are a few classics, a willingness to experiment and an open mind. Armed with these fashionable clothes for pregnant women, winter will never be boring again!

## 1. A Colorful Cocoon Coat

![A Colorful Cocoon Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_a-colorful-cocoon-coat_484x860.jpg)

‘Tis the season for a chic and comfortable cocoon coat in an eye-catching color. Doesn't the word cocoon itself conjure up images of toasty nights cuddling by the fire with a cup of hot cocoa? Ah! Cocoon coats are certainly unconventional but they look fabulous on pregnant women and let me tell you, there’s no better way to keep your little one snug this winter.

## 2. Stylish Swing Wool Coat

![Stylish Swing Wool Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_stylish-swing-wool-coat_484x860.jpg)

If a cocoon coat is not quite your style, you can never go wrong with the feminine and flattering swing coat. Pick a neutral coat, add a little color and you’re all set to brave the winter in style!

## 3. Comfortable Classic Ugg Boots

![Comfortable Classic Ugg Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_comfortable-classic-ugg-boots_550x613.jpg)

I cannot stress enough the importance of comfortable footwear when you’re pregnant and does it get any more comfy than a pair of classic Ugg boots? I don’t think so. Uggs are extremely versatile and there’s a reason why they never go out of style. This is one winter must-have every pregnant woman should say yes to.

## 4. A White Cotton Tunic

![A White Cotton Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_a-white-cotton-tunic_484x860.jpg)

[us.burberry.com](http://us.burberry.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Now here’s a basic that shouldn't just be on the list of fashionable clothes for pregnant women but on every woman’s list. There are a thousand different ways to wear a white cotton tunic on any occasion and they’re great for working those layers in the winter. Don’t forget your chunky knit scarf and a quirky handbag!

## 5. Classic Chanel Flats

![Classic Chanel Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_classic-chanel-flats_500x334.jpg)

Just because you have to stick to flats doesn't mean your flats have to be boring. This nude and black pair is classy, elegant and perfect to add a little pizzazz in your winter wardrobe.

## 6. Designer Dark Denim Jeans

![Designer Dark Denim Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_designer-dark-denim-jeans_450x590.jpg)

I know it’s tempting to lounge around in your comfortable pajamas all day long when you’re pregnant but sometimes all it takes is the perfect pair of maternity jeans to find that balance between comfort and va va voom. Opt for darker denim, ensure that the belly band fits right and get ready to show off your legs! This is one fashionable piece of clothing that every pregnant woman needs!

## 7. A Cozy Oversized Cardigan

![A Cozy Oversized Cardigan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_a-cozy-oversized-cardigan_484x860.jpg)

Your winter wardrobe is absolutely incomplete without a couple of cozy oversized neutral and colorful cardigans. The best part is you can continue to wear these even after your pregnancy. By the way, these would work wonderfully with your maternity jeans and ballet flats.

I hope this list has given you a basic idea of fashionable clothes for pregnant women this winter. Take a cue from celebrities like Megan Fox, Jessica Alba, Natalie Portman and show off that adorable baby bump! Add your own personal touches and get ready to be the most fashionable new mommy on the block. So, what fabulous winter must-haves are you rocking this season?
