# 7 Feminine 50s Inspired Fashions for Fall ...

Designer’s retro mood has returned to revive feminine 50s inspired fashions for fall. This bourgeois style is reminiscent of old Hollywood glamour and ultra-feminine ladylike refinement. Pay homage to the 50s woman this season with perfectly chic pairings and serious sophistication for a look that rings true to retro repertoire style. The elegant re-emergence of this oh la lady look for fall is classically vintage while channeling today’s modern day woman. Work feminine 50s inspired fashions for fall into your wardrobe this season for a look that is sophisticatedly polished and put together.

## 1. Tea Length Dress

![Tea Length Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/1_tea-length-dress_280x456.jpg)

Dresses that fall just below the knee are considered tea worthy and should be worn with high style to all affairs. Accessorize your little tea length dress with pointed toe kitten heels and a top handle bag for a look fit for a queen.

## 2. Pencil Skirt

![Pencil Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/2_pencil-skirt_300x450.jpg)

A razor thin pencil skirt that hits below the knee is seriously sleek and sexy in romantic crush velvet. The lethal combination of a super tight silhouette and plush fabric make for a femme fatal look that is reminiscent of the 50s. A form fitted pencil skirt shows off all the right curves in all the right places and gives the illusion of an hourglass shape with a small-defined waist for a figure flattering style.

## 3. Top Handle Bag

![Top Handle Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/3_top-handle-bag_600x653.jpg)

A top handle bag or satchel is very ladylike and sophisticated, perfect for toting around for all occasions. The shape of a top handle bag is spacious and roomy enough to store all of a refined lady’s unmentionables and more, making it the go to bag to use all season long. For a romantic and elegant finish, opt for a top handle bag in a luxe fabric or material like lace or high polished leather to complete your oh la lady look.

## 4. Three-Quarter Coat

![Three-Quarter Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/4_three-quarter-coat_600x900.jpg)

Boxy cuts and oversized shapes with a distinct vintage menswear inspired yet ladylike edge give way to modern three-quarter coat designs this season. A three-quarter coat is the ideal shape for fall you can throw on overtop anything this season and still remain polished and put together. The effortless ease of a three-quarter coat makes this cool weather must-have a versatile option for fall.

## 5. Bow Tie Blouse

![Bow Tie Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/5_bow-tie-blouse_306x400.jpg)

Demure, yet playful is the bow tie blouse that features a large necktie at the collar. When selected in a luxe fabric like silk the end result is a refined rich finish. Wear a bow tie blouse with a sleek, form fitting pencil skirt for office sophistication or with skinny dark wash denim for a casual look that is still put together.

## 6. Fur Wrap

![Fur Wrap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/6_fur-wrap_344x400.jpg)

Get wrapped up in a fur wrap that is simply lavish and divine for the cool weather of fall. Whether real or faux, select a fur wrap that is soft and plush to give an added air of elegance to it, which ups ladylike sophistication. Pair with ease over clean cut cocktail dresses for evening that channel true vintage inspiration.

## 7. Elbow Length Gloves

_ Elbow length gloves are the ultimate touch of posh elegance and sophistication. For a modern and contemporary chic update on elbow length gloves select a pair in an unexpected material like leather for an edgy new way to wear. Pair with a three-quarter-length coat for a look that is luxuriously lavish and refined and suitable for the chill in day or night.

To become your own Hollywood heroine this season, channel the dignified lady of the 50s with these 7 feminine 50s inspired fashions for fall. To achieve true ladylike refinement with ease and grace, make sure to create a look that seems effortless yet still polished from head to toe. Will you embrace the retro return of the 50s this fall?
