# 7 on\-Trend Ways to Style a Varsity Jacket ...

How to style a varsity jacket can range from classic to fashion-forward looks. No matter what your style preference is, what we do know is that varsity jackets are all the rage right now. Everyone from fashion editors to models have adopted the varsity (or letterman – whatever you want to call it) jacket as this season’s must-have form of outerwear. Keep your look less high school and more high fashion with these tips on how to style varsity jackets this season.

## 1. Keep It Classic

![Keep It Classic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/15_620x932.jpg)

If you’re a traditionalist, choose a varsity jacket in a classic style. Black, white, navy, red, and cream colours are all timeless and versatile. Personally, I prefer this season’s varsity jackets minus any lettering and in fuss-free cuts. When looking at how to style a varisty jacket, team them with indigo denim, cropped trousers, or pencil skirts for a classic look.

## 2. Stay Sporty

![Stay Sporty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/25_800x533.jpg)

All things sporty are in fashion right now, which is probably why varsity jackets are so popular. They’re an easy way to work the trend without looking like you’ve just come from the gym. Accessorise a basic outfit of a white tee, jeans, and a varsity jacket with other athletic touches like canvas sneakers, a baseball cap, and a sporty watch.

## 3. Make It Feminine

![Make It Feminine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/35_700x1062.jpg)

Work a fashionable contrast when it comes to how to style a varsity jacket by teaming yours with a mostly feminine outfit. Wear your varsity jackets with flirty dresses for something girly or sleek pencil skirts for something more streamlined. Prevent anything too cheerleader inspired by swapping short hemlines for midi and maxi ones.

## 4. Toughen up

![Toughen up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/47_600x900.jpg)

Varsity jackets are traditionally quite preppy. Toughen them up with a good dose of black leather (or leather-look materials). Either choose a style with prominent leather detailing or team your jackets with this season’s must have leather skirt or pants.

## 5. Go Monochrome

![Go Monochrome](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/56_527x790.jpg)

When looking at how to style a varsity jacket, you might find that one in a single colour will be much more wearable. For example, a black varsity jacket with black leather sleeves is probably a lot more versatile than one with contrasting coloured sleeves.

## 6. Do It like a Boy

![Do It like a Boy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/67_980x1473.jpg)

Masculine and androgynous dressing is always present in some form or another in the fashion world. The varsity jacket is just one way to work a boyish angle right now. Team them with boyfriend jeans and flat lace up shoes for an effortlessly on-trend look.

## 7. Try a Print

![Try a Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/710_383x586.jpg)

Are the classic varsity jackets just a bit too ‘boring’ for your style? Take it up a notch by choosing a varsity jacket in a bold print or pattern. When it comes to how to style a varsity jacket, this is a much more modern take on the trend and perfect if you like your outfits to stand out from the crowd.

Varsity jackets are without a doubt one of the hottest trends around right now. You don’t have to drop a whole lot of dollars on one either as plenty of affordable fashion retailers have taken a shine to this trend. Do you have any tips for how to style a varsity jacket this season?
