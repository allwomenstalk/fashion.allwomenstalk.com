# 8 Fabulous Sporty Bomber Jackets ...

**Sporty bomber jackets** are must haves this season. A sporty bomber jacket thrown over a sun dress is a great transitional look for spring. All things sport inspired are trending right now, and wearing a sporty bomber jacket is an easy way to incorporate this trend into your outfit. Below are just a few stylish sporty bomber jackets that you may want to check out.

## 1. Topshop Soft Seamed Bomber Jacket

![Topshop Soft Seamed Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/1_topshop-soft-seamed-bomber-jacket_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $84.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TcF7Gr)
If you’re just dipping your toes into this trend, look for **sporty bomber jackets** in neutral colours. A simple black bomber or something equally as neutral will be easier to pair with the existing clothes in your wardrobe. This stone coloured bomber jacket is made from a soft fabric and features an exposed zip and elastic trims which are typical for this style of jacket.

## 2. 3.1 Phillip Lim Cotton-Blend Twill Jacket

![3.1 Phillip Lim Cotton-Blend Twill Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/2_3-1-phillip-lim-cotton-blend-twill-jacket_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $575.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/l7Vqwp)
Bright coloured garments will be everywhere this spring/summer season, and a coloured bomber jacket is a chic option. This bomber by 3.1 Phillip Lim is a more relaxed take on the sporty cut, and features a drawstring tie at the waist. Wear this jacket open, over a cream shift dress and with tan flat sandals for a fun spring outfit.

## 3. Topshop Textured Drawstring Bomber Jacket

![Topshop Textured Drawstring Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/3_topshop-textured-drawstring-bomber-jacket_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $56.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/gEDgw5)
Neon colours are here to stay, for the next couple of months anyway. This **sporty bomber jacket** has a bit of a 1980s retro appeal. However, you can keep your outfit looking very contemporary by teaming this jacket with a flirty white dress and neutral accessories.

## 4. Topshop Cupro Bomber Jacket

![Topshop Cupro Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/4_topshop-cupro-bomber-jacket_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $110.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/bL1P1v)
This charcoal bomber jacket is perfect for dressing down a night time outfit. It has an exposed zipper and a black jersey collar and cuffs. This style of jacket would look great worn over a slinky party dress and with metallic jewellery and accessories. You could also quite easily wear it during the day with a plain t-shirt and colourful printed shorts.

## 5. Joseph Leather Hooded Bomber Jacket

![Joseph Leather Hooded Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/5_joseph-leather-hooded-bomber-jacket_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $1290.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Ynh2Rk)
This **sporty bomber jacket** will pretty much only ever exist in my dreams, but it’s a cool take on a classic cut. It’s made from a chunky-knit wool, to keep you extra warm, and comes with a detachable leather hood. This type of jacket would look great teamed with coloured jeans, a slouchy t-shirt, and lace up shoes.

## 6. Topshop Boutique Graphic Bomber

![Topshop Boutique Graphic Bomber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/6_topshop-boutique-graphic-bomber_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $260.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/e5yVXL)
Graphic prints are already showing themselves to be a popular trend this year. This particular bomber jacket has a bit of a 1960s Mod vibe, thanks to it’s black and white check print. Keep this retro vibe going by teaming it with a colourful full skirt, collared top, and beehive hairdo and winged eyeliner.

## 7. Topshop Hawaiian Floral Bomber Jacket

![Topshop Hawaiian Floral Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/7_topshop-hawaiian-floral-bomber-jacket_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/1ijqGY)
Admittedly, a few of these **sporty bomber jackets** won’t be to everyone’s tastes. This Hawaiian print jacket is an example of that. However, I’ve noticed that right now there’s a ‘so bad it’s good’ appeal to certain prints. You could easily get away with wearing this jacket by teaming it with a black dress or jeans, and similarly neutral accessories.

## 8. Topshop Jersey Sleeved PU Bomber Jacket

![Topshop Jersey Sleeved PU Bomber Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/02/8_topshop-jersey-sleeved-pu-bomber-jacket_374x560.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/e8Nuap)
If you’re look is more off-duty and utilitarian, then this sporty leather-look bomber jacket may just be for you. It features jersey contrast sleeves, ribbed trims, and zip pockets. Style it as you would a normal black leather jacket – with everything from jeans to your favourite party dress.

**Sporty bomber jackets** come in all kinds of colours and cuts this season. From subtle, classic cuts to the more in-your-face prints, there’s a style of bomber jacket to suit most tastes. What do you think of the sporty bomber jacket this season?
