# 7 Reasons the Color Nude is the New Black This Year ...

In case you haven’t noticed, the color nude is the new black this year in terms of being the go-to color for almost anything! There is an array of nude colors shades everywhere, for choices in fashion, cosmetics, and even home design. The bare, sleek appearance of nude displays certain qualities that make every woman fashionable, feminine, and fabulous! Check out why nude is the new black this year and pick up a few more nude clothing items, cosmetic choices, and even décor for your home to use this stylish color in your life!

## 1. Simple

One reason nude is the new black this year is because it’s simple. There’s almost nothing you can’t do with it and there’s nothing too fancy about it. Remember, fashion doesn't always have to be hard, so make it simple!

## 2. Versatile

A great pair of nude heels is one of the most versatile pieces of fashion items you can own. Not to mention nude nail colors go with everything, as does nude lip color. Using more nude here and there is a great way to add versatility and simplicity to your life, all in one lovely shade!

## 3. Classic

One of the reasons so many women love black is because it’s a classic color, and nude offers that same chic safety that black does. It’s a timeless classic that’s not too harsh, yet it makes a statement all on its own without the need for too much fuss.

## 4. Sophisticated

Sure, bright colors may be trendy, but when it comes to looking sophisticated, a woman’s options are a bit limited in the color department. Nude is one of the most powerful colors for showing you’ve got sophistication, class, and style, all rolled up into one lovely color choice. Nude nails, lip color, nude heels, a nude dress, and even a nude handbag all make great choices.

## 5. Attractive

Nude is a highly attractive color in terms of cosmetic and clothing choices. A creamy nude top or a fresh coat of nude nail polish is appealing to both women and to men. Not to mention it’s so easy to use, so it’s effortless attractiveness!

## 6. Sleek

Fashion is all about what’s fresh, sleek, and trendy, and nude is every single one of those- with a special emphasis in the sleek department. Something about a nude dress, a nude pair of heels, or even a nude lip color offers a true, sleek appeal that’s both high-fashion and highly feminine.

## 7. Effortless

Hey, we’re all busy women, and one thing we all love about black is that we don’t have to think about it- it’s effortless. Well, good news, because so is the color nude, and it’s one of the main reasons it’s the new black this year. Nude is one of the easiest color choices you can make. If it matches almost anything, looks good on everyone, and it’s appealing to everyone, what more could you ask for?

This season, try adding a few more nude items to your cosmetic choices, your wardrobe, and even to your home. Nude colors of paint, bedspreads, and throw pillows are all great options. With nude being the new black, there’s just no reason not to use it however you can. Do you like the color nude or have you bought anything nude this year?
