# 8 Clothes with Cool Clashing Prints That Are Unique and Fun ...

Clashing prints is a fun trend to try out this season. If mixing and matching separates seems a bit too difficult for you to try on your own, why not leave it up to the designers? There are plenty of clothes out there at the moment that come equipped with their own clashing prints. If you’re a fan of clashing prints or are just keen to try out this trend, then check out the following clothes with cool clashing prints.

## 1. Mixed Print Dress

![Mixed Print Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/1_mixed-print-dress_350x500.jpg)

One of the simplest ways to pull off clashing prints is with a statement-making dress. Just add some neutral shoes and accessories and you’re good to go. Polka dots and florals are popular prints and this Modcloth dress is a cute take on the mixed prints trend. It’ll set you back around $50 and would be perfect for wearing on a lunch date with the girls. You can also find more mixed print dresses from Rachel Roy, River Island, and ASOS.

## 2. Mixed Print Shorts

![Mixed Print Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/2_mixed-print-shorts_600x585.jpg)

Printed shorts are a cool option this season. Wear them with a plain t-shirt for a casual look or dress them up with a collared blouse and blazer. These mixed print shorts are an eye-catching option and will cost you around $60 from Topshop. Check out Forever 21 and J.C. Penney for more cute printed shorts.

## 3. Mixed Print Sweater

![Mixed Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/3_mixed-print-sweater_600x639.jpg)

If you’re not ready to make a statement in bright colours, try picking prints with a black and white colour palette. This Topshop sweater pairs a vintage-inspired floral print with a plain striped pattern. Team it with dark denim jeans, black patent shoes and a compact clutch for an effortlessly stylish look.

## 4. Mixed Print Tee

![Mixed Print Tee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/4_mixed-print-tee_600x800.jpg)

Ditch your plain old tee for a fun printed variety this summer. This Marc by Marc Jacobs tee features a mix of abstract check prints and would look great worn with a colourful skater skirt and ladylike flat shoes. It is pretty pricey but you can find similar, more affordable styles at stores like Dorothy Perkins and Urban Outfitters.

## 5. Mixed Print Pants

![Mixed Print Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/5_mixed-print-pants_600x766.jpg)

Right now, no wardrobe is complete without a cool pair of printed pants. These ASOS pants will set you back around $60 and feature a blue retro print with clashing panels at the sides and waistband. They’d look great teamed with a slouchy t-shirt, tan sandals, and a straw fedora for a relaxed summertime outfit. Also check out Lucca Couture and Clover Canyon for more cool mixed print pants.

## 6. Mixed Print Skirt

![Mixed Print Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/6_mixed-print-skirt_600x600.jpg)

A mixed print skirt is a stylish way to incorporate some bright and bold prints into your look. This Rachel Roy skirt has a simple A-line design and features panels of tribal-inspired prints. It would look great teamed with a sleeveless chambray top and lace-up shoes.

## 7. Mixed Print Blouse

Put a fun twist on corporate wear with a mixed print blouse. Tuck it into a pencil skirt or team it with some cropped tuxedo pants. This Michael van der Ham blouse is extremely expensive, even on sale, at around $360. Luckily you can find more budget-friendly buys at ASOS and J.C. Penney.

## 8. Mixed Print Boho Tunic

![Mixed Print Boho Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/06/8_mixed-print-boho-tunic_465x585.jpg)

A printed tunic would be the perfect beach cover up. This Clover Canyon blouse has a real boho vibe about it and you could easily imagine it worn with a floppy hat and bejewelled sandals for a glam vacation-ready look.

Clashing, mixed prints are a great way to add some personality to an outfit. These are a few clothes with eye-catching mixed prints. What do you think of these clothes with clashing prints? How would you style them?
