8 Wall Decals to Decorate Your Home with ...

Silhoutte Tree Wall Decal • Floral Chain Wall Decal • World Map Decal • Damask Wall Decal • Olivia Headboard Wall Decal • More ...

By Sophia

Wall decals are a great way to liven up a room. The decals are easily transferrable and removable. They’re perfect for the indecisive decorator! There are a range of designs available, and below are just a few options.

1. Silhoutte Tree Wall Decal

Price: $28.00 at urbanoutfitters.com
This decal design features a tree silhouette with nesting birds. This vinyl decal comes packaged as four compact sheets and is easy to assemble. Complete instructions are provided. Place this decal in an area full of light, and let it be the talking point of the room!

2. Floral Chain Wall Decal

Price: $14.99 at urbanoutfitters.com
This design comes in a total of 18 pieces, so you can arrange them to suit your own personal taste. The vinyl material means you can simply wipe them clean. I love the intricate designs on the flower petals. These are sure to breathe life into a tired room.

3. World Map Decal

Price: $59.00 at urbanoutfitters.com
How would you like the whole world in your room? Well now you can with this wall decal. You can choose between a black or bright blue colour. The decal is made from a soft vinyl with a matte finish. This is perfect for brushing up on your geography!

4. Damask Wall Decal

Price: $9.99 at urbanoutfitters.com
These decals would make a stylish addition to any room. They come in 29 pieces so you can place them all on one wall or spread them out through several rooms. I love the damask inspired designs. These are a great way to add old-world charm to a modern setting.

5. Olivia Headboard Wall Decal

Price: $55.00 at urbanoutfitters.com
If you don’t have a headboard for your bed, try a decal instead! This decal is made from removable vinyl and is self-adhesive. It was created by designer Mina Javid and would suit most queen or twin beds. This will definitely add a bit of charm to your bedroom.

6. Antique Mirrored Birdcage Wall Details

Price: $17.99 at urbanoutfitters.com
These reflective acrylic decals are so quirky! I love the antique birdcage design, complete with a bird and chain links. They come in a set of 19 pieces and come with application supplies and instructions. If you can’t have a pet bird, this might just be the next best thing!

7. Chalkboard Wall Decal

Price: $24.00 at urbanoutfitters.com
This chalkboard decal is such an excellent idea! Simply transfer onto a smooth wall surface and get writing! If you want to do something bigger, simply place multiple chalk decals side by side. I think these would be perfect for a kitchen or play room setting.

8. USA Map Chalkboard Wall Decal

Price: $24.99 at urbanoutfitters.com
This is another chalkboard style decal, but this time in the shape of the U.S.A. Teach your kids some basic geography, plan a road trip, or simply mark all the places you have visited. Alternatively, you could always just use it to write notes and reminders! It’s sure to add interest to any room.

Which of these do you like best? Would you ever decorate with wall decals?
