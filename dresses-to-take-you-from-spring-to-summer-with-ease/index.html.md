# 7 Dresses to Take You from Spring to Summer with Ease ...

Every girl needs a good collection of pretty dresses, and dresses to take you from spring to summer are a great way to streamline your wardrobe - they can be worn until fall arrives! Here is my pick of 7 dresses to take you from spring to summer; you're sure to find something here to tempt you.

## 1. Missguided Nikola Halterneck Shift Dress

![Missguided Nikola Halterneck Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/n8/dxvlwqch_355x520.jpg)

Shift dresses are in fashion this spring/summer and cobalt blue is so on-trend! With this cool halterneck style and floaty skirt you have one of the best dresses to take you from spring to summer. This pretty dress from Missguided is sure to become a wardobe staple and would look as good with flats during the day as it will with heels and a clutch bag for a party!

## 2. Missguided Masumi Spaghetti Strap Mini Dress

![Missguided Masumi Spaghetti Strap Mini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pf/c7/mcqjcpj8_355x520.jpg)

This nude mini dress with delicate spaghetti straps is perfect for those spring and summer nights out. The shimmery stretch fabric would look gorgeous teamed with a long silver or gold necklace and heels, and it's one of my favourite dresses around this season!

## 3. Modcloth Swoop in and See Me Dress

![Modcloth Swoop in and See Me Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x1/ex/r5bl2rzq_600x845.jpg)

This gorgeous tank dress features a full, flirty skirt and pretty swallow print in hues of aqua and peach. This would look great dressed up with heels for a spring/summer wedding or party or dressed down with gladiator sandals or ballerina pumps for a cool everyday look.

## 4. Topshop Photo Print Daisy Tunic

![Topshop Photo Print Daisy Tunic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0t/qs/g1il2myx_280x416.jpg)

I'm in love with this gorgeous strappy Topshop dress with daisy photo print, spaghetti straps and pretty lace detail to the bust area. It would look great in the spring with leggings, pumps or wedges and a cardigan but equally great in the hot summer months with a pair of sandals.

## 5. Topshop Open Back Bodycon Tunic Dress

![Topshop Open Back Bodycon Tunic Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bd/wn/0qtht76h_280x416.jpg)

This stylish jersey tunic dress from Topshop is figure-flattering and a great wardrobe basic for spring and summer. Team it with a pair of pumps and oversized cardigan for chillier days or gladiator sandals when the weather starts to get hotter!

## 6. Louche Kia Dress

![Louche Kia Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nb/mx/qe3b8kws_600x896.jpg)

This dress from Louche (available from Joy) is a more sophisticated spring and summer style staple - it would be perfect for spring or summer weddings, garden parties or BBQs and I love its full skirt and slightly retro style. The sweetheart neckline and pretty Delft-inspired floral print add a vintage feel - make sure you wear it with your favourite retro sunglasses.

## 7. Louche Gretta Dress

![Louche Gretta Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/0c/y65dkljc_600x877.jpg)

This fully-lined striped sundress in yellow chevron stripes is a perfect dress for the warmer months. It's cute without being overly girly and could be worn with a variety of accessories - I would probably choose pumps or wedges and a cute cardigan for cooler spring days. This dress has a slightly retro feel which could be enhanced by pairing it with a wide waist-cinching belt, if you like!

So that's my pick of dresses which will take you from spring through summer with ease - which one was your favorite? Do you like to wear the same dresses throughout the spring and summer or is it too cold where you are for summer styles? Here in London it's usually warm from April until September, so I like to have a wardrobe filled with multi-tasking dresses that will last throughout the spring and summer months!
