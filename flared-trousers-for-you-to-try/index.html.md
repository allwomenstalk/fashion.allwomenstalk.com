# 7 Flared Trousers for You to Try ...

**Flared trousers may seem** a little bit retro, but haven’t you heard? All things retro are making a comeback. With skinny leg jeans and trousers dominating our wardrobes and the department store shelves, the flared trouser may take some getting used to. However, when worn well it can look exceptionally contemporary and stylish. Take a look at some stylish flared trousers and jeans below.

## 1. BB Dakota Flared Trousers

![BB Dakota Flared Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/1_bb-dakota-flared-trousers_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $58.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Work a polished look in these tan flared trousers. These pants have a flattering high rise, and come with a matching brown belt. The sleek design makes them ideal for wearing to the office. Tuck in a silk blouse and pop on a pair of peep toe pumps for a perfectly polished look.

## 2. MIH Jeans Chambray Jeans

![MIH Jeans Chambray Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/2_mih-jeans-chambray-jeans_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $205.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/mk37po)
These flared chambray jeans are perfect for cutting a spring silhouette. Chambray is a warm weather favourite, and these trousers just ooze laidback chic. Wear them with a striped t-shirt, leather sandals, and a floppy hat for a casual weekend look.

## 3. Blank NYC Kickball Club Jeans

![Blank NYC Kickball Club Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/3_blank-nyc-kickball-club-jeans_280x400.jpg)

**Price:** $87.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
For a more classically retro **take on the flared trouser**, you can’t go past these blue denim jeans. These jeans have a high rise, double front zipper closure, and classic bellbottom flares. Work a 70s inspired look and team these jeans with a floaty blouse, platform shoes, and a tooled leather handbag.

## 4. Burberry London Flared Wool Twill Pants

![Burberry London Flared Wool Twill Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/4_burberry-london-flared-wool-twill-pants_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $395.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/uGdzgt)
If you’re willing to make a bit of an investment, then these designer flares are worth a look. The black colour means they’re never go out of style, and the flared silhouette is subtle enough to not look dated. These are a very flattering and versatile pair of trousers. Wear them with basics during the day, and add some bling and embellishment at night.

## 5. Forever21 Flare Leg Pants

_ **Price:** $24.80 at forever21.com_
If you’re on a budget than these flared trousers may be just the ticket. They come in a light beige colour and have a wide waistband, front zipper, and crease accents down the legs.Wear these trousers with a printed top and strappy sandals for a cheerful, on-trend look.

## 6. Theyskens’ Theory Pepper Flared Cord Trousers

![Theyskens’ Theory Pepper Flared Cord Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/03/TheyskensTheory_392x523.jpg)

**Price:** $171.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These cord flares come in a luxe dark-red colour. They have a mid-rise design, classic five pocket detailing, and a wide flared leg. These trousers would look great teamed with a heather grey singlet and platform sandals.

## 7. BB Dakota Fair is Flared Pants

_ **Price:** $67.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Black pants are a wardrobe staple, so why not try making a flared pair your go-to option? These particular black flares have a simple, clean design and are made from flex-knit fabric. They have an elasticised waist for extra comfort. These pants would make a great foundation for a range of versatile outfits.

**A flared trouser** can dramatically change your look. If you’re looking for an alternative to the skinny leg, try the flare on for size. What do you think of these flared trousers? Are you a fan of this look or not?
