# Hottest Instagram Looks to Steal for This Spring ...

Admit it. When it comes to scoping out the hottest street style looks on Instagram, your creep game is strong. Being that I pretty much live on Pinterest and Instagram, I'm definitely right behind you on that one! Don't get me wrong. I love following all of the up and coming fashion trends featured on the runway and red carpet. That being said, there's just something so inherently genuine and raw about street style that leaves me feeling inspired. If you're ready to kill the spring fashion game, you need to see these trendy Instagram looks.

## 1. Luscious Purple Lips

![Luscious Purple Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/lj/flbhs7oh55277cfad4a7b962527085_600x600.jpg)

Source: [Cara Delevingne](https://pro.iconosquare.com/p/954301363432235480_3255807)

Not only is Cara Delevingne the queen of the mauve lip, she she is a total pro at getting the hottest trends to take off. Purple is such a flattering, not to mention incredibly sexy, color. It's also versatile and compliments all skin tones. I recently moved away from my fire engine red lipstick to test a dark purple shade for the first time. My mind was completely blown at the difference it made!

## 2. The Little Black Jumpsuit

![The Little Black Jumpsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/vz/dlsen3xs551a383a66c42430687558_600x600.jpg)

Source: [Utibe Abasi](https://websta.me/)

If spring is in need of a fashion find that perfectly represents the style heavy season, this sexy jumpsuit is it. The flirty silhouette alone makes this a must-have. Even her shoe game is strong! If you made last minute plans but have nothing to wear, you can never go wrong with an all black ensemble. I'm pretty sure that is a fashion law somewhere.

## 3. Très Chic Thigh High Boots

![Très Chic Thigh High Boots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d9/gs/c5qbd1ol55277cfb34f11445454036_600x600.jpg)

Source: [Rosie Londoner](https://pro.iconosquare.com/viewer.php)

From her flowing A-line dress to her killer accessories, everything about this look is a total win. One of the best aspects of wearing thigh high boots is that they elongate your legs, making them appear slimmer. In case you need a bigger excuse to grab a pair, monochromatic color combinations will always be in style regardless of the season. If that's not enough of an incentive, I don't know what is.

## 4. Floral Suit

![Floral Suit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/ke/y1objb3j55278fa0ccc4a396646445_600x600.jpg)

Source: [She's a Fashion Lover](https://pro.iconosquare.com/viewer.php)

Floral print has become synonymous with spring. Why not show off your style with a flirty two piece? I love that fashion blogger Jelinda tones down the bright pink hues with a sleek black top. Her high waisted skirt looks absolutely gorgeous when paired with those killer heels. Also, never underestimate the power of a good clutch.

## 5. The Smokey Eye

![The Smokey Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/ml/gznx3bgz55278fa0600fa436621896_600x583.jpg)

Source: [Vanessa White](https://www.digitalspy.com/showbiz/)

In my opinion, choosing the right makeup to compliment your already cute look automatically sets the tone for the day. From the expert blend of dark colors in her eye shadow to her long lashes, her makeup is totally on point. Honestly, I don't think the smokey eye trend has ever looked this good! The contrast between her purple eye shadow and her red lips is stunning.

## 6. Flirty Lace Dress

![Flirty Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/ow/glsxvt0x55278fa07aa74521503694_600x600.jpg)

Source: [Sarah KJP](https://pro.iconosquare.com/viewer.php)

It's a given that lace dresses can work for any occasion. That's why having at least one little number that you can rely on is sound idea. In fact, you can even shy away from the conventional white dress and take a red haute piece for a quick test run.

## 7. High Waisted Shorts

_ Source: Adrienne Bailon_

I still can't get over how sexy Adrienne Bailon's shorts are! If you're uncomfortable with the length, I'd definitely pair these with a longer cardigan to balance your outfit. I think replacing the bandeau with a gorgeous flowy top would add more definition to this hot look.

What are some of your go-to looks for spring? Are there any fashion accounts that you love following on Instagram? Let us know in the comments below!
