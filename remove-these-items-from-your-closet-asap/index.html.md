# Remove These 9 Items from Your Closet ASAP ...

You shouldn't leave your closet cluttered. There are plenty of things that it's time for you to throw out. According to [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/things-clean-out-of-closet), you should ditch these items ASAP:

## 1. Broken Hangers

![Broken Hangers](https://img.allw.mn/content/w5/bo/e5chby615681326f86845044990044_400x224.gif)

You don't need to keep them around for sentimental reasons, so why do you still have them? Toss them in the trash.

## 2. Anything Damaged beyond Repair

![Anything Damaged beyond Repair](https://img.allw.mn/content/n0/t9/qjo5irqu56813273478a4450746546_400x250.gif)

It doesn't matter how much you love that old skirt. If it's filled with holes you won't end up sewing, then throw it out.

## 3. The Small Boxes Jewelry Comes in

![The Small Boxes Jewelry Comes in](https://img.allw.mn/content/km/l0/nsrkh3lg5681327026933440840238_500x281.gif)

You know you're never actually going to use them, so why keep them around?

## 4. Empty Shoe Boxes

[https://media.giphy.com/media/p6N1ZitLPK8jC/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/p6N1ZitLPK8jC/giphy.gif)
Again, you're never going to use them.

## 5. Broken Jewelry

![Broken Jewelry](https://img.allw.mn/content/ul/tt/a3pyw8wk56813270b59fe899111950_500x261.gif)

If you can't wear it, you don't need to own it.

## 6. T-shirts with Underarm Stains

![T-shirts with Underarm Stains](https://img.allw.mn/content/uv/hg/stuo4fbg5681327053763578206267_415x198.gif)

If you can't wash those stains out, then you wouldn't be caught dead wearing them anyway.

## 7. Single Socks, Solo Earrings, Anything That's Mate-less

![Single Socks, Solo Earrings, Anything That's Mate-less](https://img.allw.mn/content/wh/mo/a8kwqxuk568132716429c991225233_400x227.gif)

You can't wear them without a match.

## 8. Shoes Too Beat up to See Another Day

[https://media.giphy.com/media/rcL35Aht9RuoM/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/rcL35Aht9RuoM/giphy.gif)
Sometimes, you need to know when you've gotten all of the use that you can out of an item.

## 9. Shoes That Ache the Minute You Put Them on

![Shoes That Ache the Minute You Put Them on](https://img.allw.mn/content/lx/ps/rmc1j837568132728a09e852492356_640x360.gif)

Don't torture your poor feet.

Do you own any of these items?
