# 7 Outstanding Bathing Suits for Busty Gals for a Fabulous Summer ...

I know how frustrating it can be to find perfect bathing suits for busty gals. It seems that all stores offer is flimsy unpadded fabric that offers almost no support. But with these adorable bathing suits for busty gals, you will be sure to look fierce, fabulous, and adorable anytime you hit the beach or pool.

## 1. Bow Detail Bikini

![Bow Detail Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_bow-detail-bikini_600x920.jpg)

If you are looking for some adorable bathing suits for busty gals, this Ted Baker bikini from Nordstrom is the way to go. It has underwire to make sure that all of your goods stay in place and are supported. But it also has an adorable light blue color and bow for the most adorable feminine bathing suit. Not to mention the adjustable straps for girls with busts of all sizes.

## 2. Retro Anchor Top

![Retro Anchor Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_retro-anchor-top_40x57.jpg)

This Modcloth bustier inspirited top is perfect for those with a vintage style. It has a modest top, which is perfect to keep everything in place while still looking adorable. Yet the halter straps will lift your bust in all of the right places so you look fabulous. Add in some sunglasses and a headscarf for a retro look, perfect for some fun in the sun.

## 3. Bandeau Top

![Bandeau Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_bandeau-top_100x150.jpg)

As a busty girl you might think that strapless bandeau tops are out of the question. But with this underwire bandeau top from Everything But Water, you are sure to be busty and beautiful. It has underwire to support you and it even has removable straps in case you want to switch things up a bit.

## 4. Blue Halter Top

![Blue Halter Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_blue-halter-top_196x245.jpg)

What is sexier than showing some cleavage at the beach? With this Juicy Couture halter-top, you will channel your inner Marilyn Monroe by the pool. It has wide straps to give you the support you need while still letting you show some skin.

## 5. Butterfly Halter

![Butterfly Halter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_butterfly-halter_418x418.jpg)

Get ready to fly all over the pool scene with this adorable J. Crew butterfly halter-top. It has the perfect cups to support your busty chest and has the halter straps to lift you in all of the right places. Pair this bathing suit with a sheer cover up and a floppy hat and you are ready to look adorable in the sun all day long.

## 6. White One-Piece

![White One-Piece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_white-one-piece_90x90.jpg)

Do you think you are bold enough to wear white to the beach? With this frilly white one-piece from Zimmerman, all eyes will be on you. It has underwire cups to keep your bust lifted and in place but the frills on the side will emphasize your outrageous curves.

## 7. Polka Dot Bandeau Bikini

![Polka Dot Bandeau Bikini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_polka-dot-bandeau-bikini_290x370.jpg)

Few things are more adorable than a polka dot bandeau bikini like this one from ASOS. Its stretchy fabric will not pull and look (or feel) uncomfortable, and it has underwire support that is necessary for larger busts. The adorable color and polka dot design are just the icing on top of the cake with this bathing suit.

Having a fuller bust is no reason to not find the perfect bathing suit you love. There are so many options out there in almost any style. All you have to do is know what you are looking for.
