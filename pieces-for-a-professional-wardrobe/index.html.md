# 7 Pieces for a Professional Wardrobe ...

**Professional Wardrobe** can be tricky to put together. You want to outfits to be suitable for the workplace, but still maintain a sense of personal style. There are a few key pieces that you need in your **professional wardrobe**. These pieces will form the basis of many of your outfits and, if thoughtfully chosen, can be mixed and matched to create a range of looks. When adding personal touches to your **professional wardrobe**; look to jewellery and accessories to add definition. Take a look at a few pieces for a **professional wardrobe** below.

## 1. Topshop Premium Tux Blazer

![Topshop Premium Tux Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/1_topshop-premium-tux-blazer_334x500.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/W3Ntwf)
The cream coloured blazer is a great alternative to the somewhat harsher black blazer. Cream works well with a neutral colour palette, which is what most **professional wardrobes** are made up of. You can also incorporate it into your weekend wardrobe by teaming it with a cute printed dress.

## 2. DKNY Crepe Dress

![DKNY Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/2_dkny-crepe-dress_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/viGnQU)
This black dress will not only become an essential part of your **professional wardrobe**, but your off-duty wardrobe as well. It’s a classic cut with a demure round neckline and box-pleat shoulders. Wear it with a silk scarf, cropped jacket and Mary-Jane pumps at work, and with a statement collar necklace and strappy heels for a chic night time look.

## 3. Topshop Short Sleeve Pussy Bow Blouse

![Topshop Short Sleeve Pussy Bow Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/3_topshop-short-sleeve-pussy-bow-blouse_364x500.jpg)

**Price:** $64.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/ucIoPz)
The pussy bow shirt has a very secretarial feel to it. It’s also a timeless piece of any wardrobe. This blouse has a v-shaped neck and short, banded sleeves. Team it with a high waisted grey pencil skirt and pumps for effortless office attire.

## 4. Jimmy Choo Gilbert Leather Pumps

![Jimmy Choo Gilbert Leather Pumps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/4_jimmy-choo-gilbert-leather-pumps_460x370.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/H3Z15y)
Nude pumps are an essential part of the modern **professional wardrobe**. They’re a great alternative to black pumps and also have leg lengthening effects. These particular pumps come in a classic design with a mid-height heel and rounded toe. They’d look great worn with wide legged trousers and a silk blouse.

## 5. Topshop Boucle Jacket

![Topshop Boucle Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/5_topshop-boucle-jacket_477x500.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IXPZvq)
This jacket has a very timeless, Chanel-inspired feel to the design. This boucle jacket has a cropped design and features contrasting black binding. Wear it with a simple top, high-waisted skirt, and pumps. Accessorise with some classic pearl jewellery for that extra feminine touch.

## 6. Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Striped Skirt

![Vivienne Westwood Anglomania Striped Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/6_vivienne-westwood-anglomania-striped-skirt_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $390.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/gWKZnH)
Nothing hints to a **professional wardrobe** more than pinstripes. This skirt however, has a much more contemporary feel to it. It has abstract pleating from the waist and features an asymmetric hemline. Wear it with a crisp white blouse, black pumps, and rose gold men’s style watch for a chic day to day look.

## 7. Warehouse Hinged Shopper Bag

![Warehouse Hinged Shopper Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/01/7_warehouse-hinged-shopper-bag_246x400.jpg)

**Price:** $81.81 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/7XlKar)
Every **professional wardrobe** needs a functional handbag. This leather-look bag has a sophisticated, elegant design, minus the hefty price tag. It has a structured design and features grab top handles as well as an adjustable shoulder strap.

These pieces for a **professional wardrobe** can also hopefully translate to your off-duty style as well. Once you’ve got the basics of a **professional wardrobe**, you can add your own sense of style through a range of accessories. What are your tips for putting together a **professional wardrobe**?
