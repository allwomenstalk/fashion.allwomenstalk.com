# ASOS Curve Looks Were Obsessed with ...

ASOS is one of my all-time favorite online stores, and they, without a doubt, have one of the best lines out there for curvier women. If you’re a curvier woman and sick of the lack of options for flattering pieces of your body type, I want to introduce you to ASOS Curve. This line will fix nearly all of your biggest fashion problems, making fashion fun again!

## 1. Patterned Shorts

![Patterned Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/cv/t2fr1eym55d49884e2217607766486_600x766.jpg)

I’m a huge fan of patterned shorts, however, I often hear from curvy women that they don’t like the way that patterned shorts look on them, and that they fear that they look like pajamas rather than a fashion statement. I think that these shorts couldn’t be farther from those fears, though, so if you’re a curvier girl who wants to rock patterned shorts, these are the pair for you.

## 2. Floral off-the-Shoulder Dress

![Floral off-the-Shoulder Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/x1/chf10vct55d4988590f82342678846_600x766.jpg)

I’m a sucker for an off-the-shoulder dress, and this beautiful floral number is no exception. Whether you have a wedding to attend or simply a work function that you need to dress up for, this dress is so pretty that you’ll never want to take it off, especially if you pair them with the stunning strappy heels the model is wearing!

## 3. Waterfall Coat

![Waterfall Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/id/rm1vtw1l55d49885ef0d6525505759_600x766.jpg)

If you’re looking to add a bit of drama to your fall wardrobe, do it with this waterfall coat. It’s the perfect fall staple, and its beige color makes it a coat that you can wear with nearly your entire fall wardrobe! If you want to add some texture to any look, pair it with this coat for the perfect fall outfit!

## 4. Black Velvet Shorts

![Black Velvet Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p9/cu/mxo6tws755d4988675fe5851671036_600x766.jpg)

Although I especially love the entire outfit that seems to look like a co-ordinate outfit, these shorts are really what seal the deal in my opinion. I don’t know about you, but I have a butt. I love high-waisted black shorts but it’s nearly impossible to find a pair that doesn’t make my butt look weird and misshapen. That’s why this ASOS Curve look is so amazing-the shorts are specifically made for women with butts who simply want to look cute in a pair of black shorts!

## 5. Black Wrap Playsuit

![Black Wrap Playsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/y0/ak44ojp355d498867a54e382237347_600x766.jpg)

This one’s for all of the hippie girls out there! If your style leans toward the more bohemian side of things and you’re a curvy girl, it may often feel like you’re living in a world of clothes that just weren’t made for you. That ends with ASOS Curve, though, because this black playsuit is the perfect boho look for any curvy girl!

## 6. Embellished White Dress

![Embellished White Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zr/ws/dfd7ekcj55d49886d69ff907337930_600x766.jpg)

I’m a sucker for any piece of clothing that has beautiful detailing, and this dress is no exception. The embellishment on this white dress is absolutely stunning! I think it’d be a great dress for your wedding rehearsal dinner if you’re the bride, or it’d be great to wear to a wedding if you’re a guest (and don’t care about forgoing the “only the bride can wear white” rule). No matter what occasion you wear this dress to, it’s a look that you simply can’t pass up!

## 7. Mini Body Con Dress

![Mini Body Con Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/o5/z58t5dj955d4988705625542091922_600x766.jpg)

Another common complaint that I often hear from curvy women is that it’s nearly impossible to find a body con dress that’s flattering, or even more than that, a dress that doesn’t force them to show much more of their body than they’re comfortable showing. This dress fixes both of those problems. This black mini body con dress is both tasteful and figure flattering, making it a win for all of the curvy girls out there!
