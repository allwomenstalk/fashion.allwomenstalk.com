# 6 Gorgeous Jewelry Pieces from Barbara Michelle Jacobs ...

[Barbara Michelle Jacobs](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)**Etsy shop** is filled with **jewelry** that is Eco-friendly, socially responsible, and inspired by nature. I was excited to see that I won her pretty [peridot bracelet](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)from the giveaway she hosted on my friend Diana's blog [Express-O](http://diana212m.blogspot.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) and couldn't wait to show it off! **Nature** inspires me every day when I look out the window and see the beautiful trees in my yard and the colorful birds that fly from branch to branch. Do you have **jewelry pieces** that are inspired by things you love? Perhaps you will find inspiration in these **6 Gorgeous Jewelry Pieces from Barbara Michelle Jacobs's collection**...
