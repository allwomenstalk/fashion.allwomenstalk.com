# 9 Quirky Cape Coats ...

Cape coats are a great option of outerwear in the cooler months. Most come in thick wool or wool blends, but why not think outside of the square this season and look to cape coats in different fabrics and with interesting details? As most cape coats don’t provide much warmth in the arms, it’s also worth investing in some elbow length gloves. The resulting look is quite ladylike. Add a twist to your look with the following cape coats below.

## 1. Junya Watanabe Trapeze-Sleeved Leather Jacket

![Junya Watanabe Trapeze-Sleeved Leather Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/cz/unfiwwkz54c93044eb817264305809_400x402.jpg)

**Price:** $2810.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Bgrxju)
Work a fashion-forward look in this leather cape-inspired jacket. It has a rounded collar, trapeze sleeves, and a sculpted waist. It also has a pleated cape style back with a satin underlay. Keep it ladylike and team this edgy leather jacket with a full wool skirt.

## 2. Michael Kors Hooded Wool Poncho

![Michael Kors Hooded Wool Poncho](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/jw/t2vmnxs054c93045e4f95640489823_400x502.jpg)

**Price:** $2995.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/qscGbq)
Contemporary cape coats make a great alternative to the more classic styles of coats. This particular cape is made from wool and has a hooded design. It also has an open front with a drape effect design. Team it with a sleek, streamlined outfit for a really dramatic look.

## 3. Rodarte for Opening Ceremony Fringe Cape

_ **Price:** $432.50 at lagarconne.com_
This cape is quite eye catching. It features an opulent floral tapestry body with fringe trimming. It has winged sleeves that overlap at the back, a rounded hem, and is fully lined. As the cape itself is quite bold, it would be best to keep the rest of your outfit fairly simple.

## 4. Azzaro Feather-Hooded Cashmere-Blend Cape

This cape coat is quite glamorous. It features a feather lined hood, silke-crepe bow fastening, and velvet trims. It’s ideal for evening wear. Team it with a demure cocktail dress and finish the look off with some opulent jewellery for added dazzle.

## 5. Topshop Animal Print Capelet

![Topshop Animal Print Capelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/s6/clia0qha54c9304962ebc287792947_400x397.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/CGuQDK)
Cropped cape coats are great for wearing over dresses. This particular cape coat comes with an animal-print twist. It features a brown animal print, borg collar, and faux leather trims. It also has toggle front fastenings. Wear it with a full skirted dress and elbow length leather gloves for a decidedly demure look.

## 6. Burberry Prorsum Patent Leather-Trimmed Cape

![Burberry Prorsum Patent Leather-Trimmed Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/i8/szzxeu4354c9304a76c4f233001061_400x376.jpg)

**Price:** $1295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/MLzRTY)
This transparent cape has one of the quirkiest designs around. This cape has black patent leather trims, storm flaps, and bow detailing at the front. It also has side slits for the arms. Simply slip this on over any outfit when the weather is looking a bit worse for wear.

## 7. United Bamboo Cable Knit Jacket with Cape

![United Bamboo Cable Knit Jacket with Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/qn/ft7xlzro54c9304b8788a151875061_400x491.jpg)

**Price:** $863.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This knit jacket features its own built-in cape. It has a chunky knit body and a removable cape with leather trims and buckled straps under the arms. The combination of the charcoal and army knit lends to a military inspired design. Team this knit cape coat with black skinny jeans and biker boots for an off-duty look.

## 8. Rag & Bone Cape

_ **Price:** $535.00 at barneys.com_
This cape has an inspired design. It seems to combine elements of a blazer with the classic cape design. It comes in an on-trend tan colour and features winged lapels, two front pockets, and a single button closure. Wear it with tailored trousers and a silk blouse for a contemporary office look.

## 9. Topshop Oatmeal Borg Collar Cape

![Topshop Oatmeal Borg Collar Cape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/e9/v90ho8ap54c9304da2990128961741_400x517.jpg)
