# How to Steal Claire Underwoods Style on a Budget ...

I've been guilty of binge-watching Netflix's House of Cards not only for its amazing storyline but to also fangirl over Claire Underwood's ace wardrobe. She's singlehandedly redefined work attire for women everywhere with her elegant but carefully curated outfits. If you've been wondering how you can copy her signature style without it costing you, great news! Here's how you can emulate this style queen without breaking the bank.

## 1. If There's One Thing That Claire Underwood is Known for, It's for Her Love of Tailored Dresses

![If There's One Thing That Claire Underwood is Known for, It's for Her Love of Tailored Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/yp/ch21krse55cc0a280a2dc855541979_600x848.jpg)

Get Modcloth's Enterprising Entrepreneur Dress here: [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

While we see her experimenting more with flirty A-line cuts in the second season, Claire Underwood's go-to style has always been the dress that's tailored to perfection. Everything she wears fits her shape. As a start, try scoring structured dresses that outline your silhouette while still being work appropriate.

## 2. Structured Button-down Shirts Are Workwear Essentials

_ Get Merona's button-down shirt here: target.com_

Claire has proved time and again that you can never have too many button-down shirts. One of the major benefits of wearing these shirts is that they are very versatile in nature. To get that polished look, you can layer them with a smart blazer.

## 3. Tuck Your Blouse into a Pencil Skirt or Tailored Trousers

![Tuck Your Blouse into a Pencil Skirt or Tailored Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/wq/o503lwp955cc0a75f046d259891030_327x400.jpg)

Get Style&co's Pull-On Ponte-Knit Pencil Skirt here: [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Pencil skirts are always in season, making them perfect for any occasion. They also look great (not to mention incredibly flattering) when paired with blazers and cardigans. Their smooth finish makes them a great fashion find to keep in your work arsenal.

## 4. Her Color Palette Usually Revolves around Work Friendly Hues Such as Navy, Black, White, Grey and Beige

![Her Color Palette Usually Revolves around Work Friendly Hues Such as Navy, Black, White, Grey and Beige](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/c2/zk0u7jmy55cc0adeb7837552976017_600x857.jpg)

Get Modcloth's I Think I Can Dress here: [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I love that Claire is a minimalist at heart. Everything she wears is expertly cut but still manages to look super comfortable. On those days when you don't know what to rock, channel Claire by grabbing a shirt or dress in one of these flattering shades.

## 5. Faux Leather Totes = Your New Best Friend

![Faux Leather Totes = Your New Best Friend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/tq/a38eauvd55cc0b352c951848170270_479x503.jpg)

Get London Fog's Paley Faux Saffiano Leather Tote here: [saksoff5th.com](http://www.saksoff5th.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you have to splurge, buying a sexy bag can honestly benefit you in the long run. Perfect for those brunch and dinner dates, totes are steadily becoming the hottest bags of the season. A black tote or satchel totally screams Claire. The red lining in this bag is a plus!

## 6. Don't Ever Underestimate the Power of a Good Skirt Suit or Pantsuit

![Don't Ever Underestimate the Power of a Good Skirt Suit or Pantsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/bc/hbjyfbmh55cc0bef542af899508822_400x489.jpg)

Get Tahari ASL's Pearl-Trim Snap-Front Skirt Suit here: [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Who said a power suit couldn't be a little flirty? Whether you buy separates or a full suit, remember that exact measurements are crucial to making this look work. Just think of the pearls on this skirt suit as a delightful bonus.

## 7. Pumps Are Always a Good Idea

![Pumps Are Always a Good Idea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/zf/nowrq8w555cc0c271390b661688695_534x367.jpg)

Get Michael Antonio's Love Me Platform Pump here: [dsw.com](https://goo.gl/al3oTc)

Let's be real. Is there a better shoe option than fierce pumps? I absolutely adore their sleek black color and the hidden platform.
