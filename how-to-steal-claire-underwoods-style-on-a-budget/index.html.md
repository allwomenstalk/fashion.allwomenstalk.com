# Where to Buy Claire Underwood Clothes: Steal Her Fashion on a Budget

I have been guilty of binge-watching Netflix's House of Cards not only for its amazing storyline but also to admire Claire Underwood fashion. She has singlehandedly redefined work attire for women everywhere with her elegant, carefully curated outfits. If you have been wondering where to buy claire underwood clothes and how you can copy her signature style without it costing you a fortune, I have great news! Here is how you can emulate this style queen and recreate those iconic claire underwood outfits without breaking the bank.

When analyzing her wardrobe, a few key elements stand out:

- Structured silhouettes that command attention.

- A strict adherence to neutral, professional colors.

- Minimalist accessories, like her signature claire underwood glasses.

## 1. If There's One Thing That Claire Underwood is Known for, It's for Her Love of Tailored Dresses

![If There's One Thing That Claire Underwood is Known for, It's for Her Love of Tailored Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/yp/ch21krse55cc0a280a2dc855541979_600x848.jpg)

Find a great alternative to the classic dress claire underwood wears here: [modcloth.com](https://www.modcloth.com/)

While we see her experimenting more with flirty A-line cuts in the second season, the go-to style for claire underwood dresses has always been a garment tailored to perfection. Everything she wears fits her shape flawlessly. As a start, try scoring structured dresses that outline your silhouette while still being work appropriate.

## 2. Structured Button-down Shirts Are Workwear Essentials

![Structured Button-down Shirts Are Workwear Essentials](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sl/wh/xpwplvnu55cc0a5191959640817254_480x471.jpg)

Find a structured button-down shirt here: [target.com](https://www.target.com/)

Claire has proved time and again that you can never have too many button-down shirts. One of the major benefits of wearing these shirts is that they are very versatile in nature. To get that polished look, you can layer them with a smart blazer, which perfectly complements her [neutral color palette](#4).

## 3. Tuck Your Blouse into a Pencil Skirt or Tailored Trousers

![Tuck Your Blouse into a Pencil Skirt or Tailored Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zn/wq/o503lwp955cc0a75f046d259891030_327x400.jpg)

Find a beautiful Ponte-Knit Pencil Skirt here: [macys.com](https://www.macys.com/)

Pencil skirts are always in season, making them perfect for any occasion. They also look great, not to mention incredibly flattering, when paired with blazers and cardigans. Their smooth finish makes them a great fashion find to keep in your work arsenal.

## 4. Her Color Palette Usually Revolves around Work Friendly Hues Such as Navy, Black, White, Grey and Beige

![Her Color Palette Usually Revolves around Work Friendly Hues Such as Navy, Black, White, Grey and Beige](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/c2/zk0u7jmy55cc0adeb7837552976017_600x857.jpg)

Explore more neutral tone options here: [modcloth.com](https://www.modcloth.com/)

I love that Claire is a minimalist at heart. Everything she wears is expertly cut but still manages to look super comfortable. On those days when you do not know what to rock, channel Claire by grabbing a shirt or dress in one of these flattering shades. This [neutral styling approach](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-wear-neutral-colors/) is universally flattering.

## 5. Faux Leather Totes = Your New Best Friend

![Faux Leather Totes = Your New Best Friend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lt/tq/a38eauvd55cc0b352c951848170270_479x503.jpg)

Check out a sleek Faux Saffiano Leather Tote here: [saksoff5th.com](https://www.saksoff5th.com/)

If you have to splurge, buying a sexy bag can honestly benefit you in the long run. Perfect for those brunch and dinner dates, totes are steadily becoming the hottest bags of the season. A black tote or satchel totally screams Claire. The red lining in this bag is a wonderful detail!

## 6. Don't Ever Underestimate the Power of a Good Skirt Suit or Pantsuit

![Don't Ever Underestimate the Power of a Good Skirt Suit or Pantsuit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ds/bc/hbjyfbmh55cc0bef542af899508822_400x489.jpg)

Who said a power suit could not be a little flirty? Whether you buy separates or a full suit, remember that exact measurements are crucial to making this look work. Just think of the pearls on this skirt suit as a delightful bonus, adding a touch of budget underwood elegance.
