# 8 Hottest Fashion Bloggers to Follow for Girls Who Need Serious Style Inspo ...

Sometimes we just need a bit of fashion inspiration to make us super motivated when it comes to styling our next outfit, and what better way to do that than follow all the top fashion bloggers?! Here are just a few amazing bloggers that you need to follow asap! 👠 💋 ❤️ ✨

## 1. Rumi

_ fashiontoast.com_
Not only has Rumi started her own fashion label called 'Are You Am I' but she travels all over the world wearing amazing clothes in every city you can imagine, all of which is captured by her very talented photographer! Follow Rumi as she moves around cities and browse 'Are You Am I' for heaps of inspiration!

## 2. Lydia

![clothing, sleeve, outerwear, coat, overcoat,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/vr/ox1k2j3257e34a7cd7452932519964_600x612.jpg)

[lydiaelisemillen.com](http://lydiaelisemillen.com/category/fashion/)
Lydia is just one of the bloggers you need to start following asap! She has heaps of amazing content on her blog such as how to style to 'shirt dress' in a modern way, denim that will flatter your figure and how to wear the classic blazer!

## 3. Victoria

![white, clothing, sleeve, outerwear, spring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/q7/x0w4vrms57e34a671d741699135490_600x616.jpg)

[inthefrow.com](https://www.inthefrow.com/category/fashion)
Are you a fan of popular online shopping brands like New Look, ASOS and Topshop or do you prefer more luxury designer brands like Burberry, Dior, Ted Baker, Chanel and Celine? Either way, Victoria covers it all on her blog with her list of at least 30 different brands to search by as well as many fashion themes to browse for all the inspiration you need!

## 4. Kalyn

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/gy/k8nmls1457e34cf5af7a9105428848_600x600.jpg)

[kalynnicholson.com](https://www.kalynnicholson.com/search/label/fashion)
Kayln is an amazing blogger who writes about all things fashion such as spring outfit ideas, date night outfit inspiration, what to pack for a trip away! You can even follow her popular DIY fashion videos and make a few amazing pieces yourself!

## 5. Freddy

![clothing, fur clothing, sleeve, coat, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/dj/qg14jznc57e34a4e5c6b4782208737_600x601.jpg)

[freddymylove.co.uk](https://freddymylove.co.uk/)
Follow Freddy's blog to see all the latest pieces that she's picked up as well as heaps of her fashion favourites as she models them around the streets of London! Steal her style by opting for similar bright and light pieces for serious fashion inspiration!

## 6. Josie

![clothing, hairstyle, leg, spring, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/gf/sa2i9orj57e34a3a825a3895315177_600x638.jpg)

[fashionmumblr.com](http://www.fashionmumblr.com/category/fashion)
Just a few of Josie's amazing fashion posts are around how to redefine your style, London Fashion Week outfit diary including do's and don'ts, amazing accessories and must-have autumnal shoes!

## 7. Steph

_ cocochicblog.co.uk_
It's pretty clear that Steph has a passion for fashion so she's definitely one to follow for loads of inspiration! Check out her Fashion Week survival guide as well as her amazing posts on summer must-haves and DIY add accessories to all your favourite bikinis!

## 8. Evelina

![clothing, road, street, footwear, winter,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/dl/kf1qyl4357e34a143db62377570387_600x613.jpg)

[evelinasfashioncafe.com](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=evelinasfashioncafe.com)
You simply must follow Evelina's stylish fashion blog, full of her go-to style for both New York, Paris and even Italy! As if this wasn't enough, she also has heaps of posts on great ways to style certain pieces such as faux fur, suede, lace and high tops!
