# 8 Ways to Work Equestrian Style in to Your Wardrobe ...

**Equestrian style** is timeless, chic and sophisticated. Once exclusive to the world of horse-back riding this glamorous attire has been urbanized by the world’s most prominent [fashion houses](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/top-fashion-houses-and-how-to-tell-them-apart/). It is now available in luxurious downtown-appropriate designs. No experience at the stables? No problem! Follow my tips for flawless ways to work **equestrian style** in to your wardrobe.

## 1. The Riding Boot

A wardrobe must-have, the classic riding boot is a wonderful way to integrate **equestrian style** in to your wardrobe. Pair them with leggings and a chunky sweater for high-style casual wear or dress them up with dark skinny jeans, a silk blouse, and a fabulous leather bag to transition seamlessly from day to evening.

## 2. A Classic White Shirt

The classic white shirt is a true staple for this type of dressing. Traditional cuts are versatile and functional, and many labels now feature equestrian inspired designs with ruffled collars and interesting cuff detailing, perfect for tucking in to denim, skirts or dress pants.

## 3. Silk Scarves

Available in tones and prints specifically inspired by the equestrian world, silk scarves are a lovely, feminine accessory for women. Tie a scarf around your neck to draw instant attention to your face, or wrap one around the handle of your leather bag for an unexpected fashion accent.

## 4. The Riding Pant

Sleek and comfortable the riding pant is one of the most recognizable nods to **equestrian style**. Best designed in dark, neutral hues with leather insets on the inside of the knee, this style pant is a great alternative to denim and ideal for creating a fresh, classic weekend look.

## 5. Sleek, Leather Belts

A conservative, mid-sized leather belt is one of the most subtle ways to work **equestrian style** in to your wardrobe. Define your waist by adding one over a sweater, or take a style cue from equestrians themselves by pairing your belt with riding pants, boots and a crisp shirt or polo.

## 6. A Quilted Jacket

Long popular with the equestrian set, quilted jackets are warm, comfortable and fashionable. The most flattering styles are fitted to the body, available in dark, rich colors and ideal for transitioning your wardrobe through the winter months.

## 7. The Tweed Blazer

Also referred to as the hacking jacket, tweed blazers are an **equestrian style** favorite. Fashionable with your basics, this type of jacket is also a fabulous way to have fun with your wardrobe - add it over a feminine dress, a chic skirt, or an elegant dress pant for a creative, equestrian inspired look totally your own.

## 8. The Horse-bit

Made fashionably famous by designers like Gucci and Ralph Lauren, the horse-bit is synonymous with **equestrian style** and heritage. Featured on handbags, shoes and accessories, horse-bit detailing is an elegant, understated way to add just a touch of **equestrian style** to your look.

Perfect for fashionistas focused on timeless, versatile pieces, **equestrian style** is a stunning, sophisticated way to take your casual look to the next level. Integrate a few of these items in to your wardrobe for subtle style impact or mix and match them together for a true equestrian look. Which are your favorite equestrian inspired pieces?
