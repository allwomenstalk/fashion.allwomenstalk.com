# 7 of Kate Middletons Best Looks as the Royal Baby is on Its Way ...

The news the whole world was waiting for is finally here – Kate Middleton is in labor! It’s reported that Kate and Wills spent a relaxing weekend at home, before being driven to the hospital at around 6 a.m. this morning, and the whole world has been keeping watch since. Whether she has a boy or a girl, one thing is certain – it’ll be all eyes on Kate for the foreseeable future. It seems pregnancy suited the new Duchess of Cambridge – while you are waiting for news, check out these amazing maternity looks!

## 1. Chic Wool Coats

![Chic Wool Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/1_chic-wool-coats_600x792.jpg)

For her last appearance before her due date, Kate Middleton went for a simple, classic look. Her custom Alexander McQueen light wool crepe coat featured an oversized collar, three-quarter sleeves and slimming side darts, and she rocked it along with a matching hat by Jane Corbett and a very dapper looking William in his regal dress. Well, it was Queen Elizabeth’s birthday parade!

## 2. Animal Print

![Animal Print](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/2_animal-print_360x599.jpg)

Kate even made animal print look chic. She attended the Princess Cruises ship-naming ceremony (yep, really) on June 13th, and did her Royal duty by smashing a bottle of Champagne against the ships hull. Despite being heavily pregnant, Kate kept cool in a linen and rayon blend Dalmatian print coat from Hobbs – get thee to the High Street!

## 3. Vintage

![Vintage](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/3_vintage_344x599.jpg)

Kate went for a very different look for the 60th anniversary of Queen Elizabeth II’s coronation. Her blush lace dress has a high neck, empire waist and fringe trim, giving it a very vintage feel, and Kate teamed it with a Jenny Peckham coat with three-quarter length sleeves, a blush clutch and some sparkly jewelry. That pillar-box hat finishes it off perfectly. Want to steal some of Kate’s style this summer? Go for shades of peach or blush for those formal get-togethers.

## 4. Neon

![Neon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/4_neon_401x599.jpg)

Kate made sure she stayed on trend at Buckingham Palace’s annual garden party! This neon yellow coat was designed by Emilia Wickstead, and sold for around $1,826. It was quick to sell out after the Duchess was snapped in it, but there are a few for sale on eBay, although you should expect to pay around double the initial value. Kate completed the look with some cute pearl earrings, a Russell and Bromley Park Avenue clutch, nude heels and a cream hat. Glam.

## 5. Pastels

![Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/5_pastels_600x901.jpg)

Everyone was after the first glimpse of Kate’s bump, and it came in this striking mint Mulberry coat on April 21st. The frayed cotton coat sold for $3000, but again sold out soon after Kate was seen in it – if nothing else, Kate’s pregnancy has given the fashion industry an amazing boost! Kate paired the dress with nude pumps and bold green Amethyst earrings, along with a gold clutch. Accessorizing at its very best.

## 6. Modern

![Modern](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/6_modern_367x600.jpg)

Back in December, Kate was due to present the Lifetime Achievement Award at the BBC’s Sports Personality of the Year awards. It was doubtful whether she’d make it, after being hospitalized with severe sickness, but all eyes were sure to be on the Duchess after her early pregnancy was announced – and boy did she look great! Kate ditched her usual style for a glamorous floor-length Alexander McQueen gown, featuring peplum waist detailing and a thigh-high split. Stunning!

## 7. Silk

![Silk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/07/7_silk_590x700.jpg)

Kate Middleton really managed to hide her baby bump here! She attended the National Portrait Gallery on January 11th to unveil her portrait, and her outfit choice was simply divine. This Whistles Sofie Rae dress is from the high street, and the burgundy silk really showed off Kate’s coloring. She kept the rest of her look simple, with black pumps and a black clutch. I love this look, maternity or not!

There’s no denying that Kate Middleton has done maternity fashion well! Despite hundreds of cameras following her since the pregnancy was first announced, Kate has looked dignified and styled in every photo, and remained unfazed by the attention. What was your favorite maternity look? I think mine has to be the ballgown…
