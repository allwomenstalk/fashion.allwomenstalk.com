# Genius Tips on How to Look Great by Dressing for Your Body Type ...

Are you wondering **how to select the perfect clothes for your body shape**? To look your best you have to know what clothing accentuates your features best. Not completely sure what you should highlight? Continue reading because I have come up with a no-fail list of what you should look for, no matter your size and shape. Here's **how to select the perfect clothes for your body shape**.

## 1. Rectangle

A rectangle body shape is characterized by a similar width throughout your body from shoulders to waist. To dress this body shape you need to work on creating curves. Off-the-shoulder style or V-neck tops will create a figure that flatters your silhouette. Choose more form fitting bottoms, which will create the illusion of curves. Pencil and trumpet skirts will create a shape. Wrap dress work well for this body shape as they create a waist.

## 2. Inverted Triangle

An inverted triangle body shape is characterized by broad shoulders, shapely legs, and slim hips. TO dress this body shape you will need to make sure you balance your broader should with your narrower lower body to make the illusion of an hourglass shape. V-neck and wrap style tops look best. Try going for A-line skirts, which enhance the lower body and balance the upper. Flare and fit dresses look great for this shape.

## 3. Pear

A pear body shape is characterized by your hips being the widest part of your body. To dress this body shape you should balance things out by accentuating the top half. Flare and fit dresses work great for this shape. An off the shoulder flare and fit dress is a great trendy option. Also, work to highlight the smallest part of your waist. A-line skirts and structured jackets also flatter this shape.

## 4. Hourglass

An hourglass body shape is characterized by a smaller waist and a curvy body. To dress this body shape you want to highlight your curves. Don’t try to hide them. Buy clothes that accentuate your body. Jumpsuits are a great option. Tailored jackets and blazers and pencil skirts work well. When it comes to dresses, wrap dresses highlight the area between your hips and waist well. Wide leg pants are great because they help define your shape.

## 5. Apple

An apple body shape is characterized by a slim lower body carrying weight around the middle. To dress this body shape you would to focus on highlighting the shoulders and legs. Stay away from form-fitting tops and turn toward flowy styles. Low rise, straight leg pants are also great. Shift dresses should be your go to.
