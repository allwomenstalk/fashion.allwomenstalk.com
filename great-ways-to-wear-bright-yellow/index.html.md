# 7 Great Ways to Wear Bright Yellow ...

We definitely need more ways to wear yellow. It’s such a bold, bright color…you can’t walk past something yellow without taking a look, and it’s almost certain to boost your mood. Whether it’s daffodils or someone’s amazing shoes, yellow is a color that we all need more of. Here are my favorite ways to wear yellow; try to add it to your wardrobe…

## 1. Bold Cardigans…

![Bold Cardigans…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/1_bold-cardigans_236x321.jpg)

This is what I’m wearing today. It’s bold and beautiful, and I’ve had so many compliments! It didn’t take much planning, either. You can get denim shorts and casual black vests from anywhere, and my necklace is rather understated. It’s the bright yellow cardigan that gets everyone’s attention! Coordinate it by using bold colors in your make-up, too, and keeping hair natural and casual. This is one of the easiest ways to wear yellow!

## 2. Two Shades…

![Two Shades…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/2_two-shades_336x775.jpg)

Feeling bold? Wearing two shades of yellow like this is perfect for giving your work wear a modern, bright update. Opt for a pastel yellow pencil skirt, and a jumper in a brighter yellow. A yellow bangle and yellow shoes coordinate the rest of the look, and a red lip adds a touch of old-school glamor…this is a look that is certain to get plenty of compliments. Wear it with confidence!

## 3. Vintage…

![Vintage…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/3_vintage_250x373.jpg)

There’s plenty of vintage-inspired shapes and pastel colors around at the moment, and this outfit is no exception. Opt for a pastel yellow skirt with plenty of texture, and team with a mint jumper and a cute handbag. It’s effortless and timeless, and very easy to wear.

## 4. Yellow Skinnies…

![Yellow Skinnies…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/4_yellow-skinnies_563x844.jpg)

There are yellow jeans everywhere, but knowing what to team them with can be daunting. Keep the look simple, and you’ll easily radiate confidence. A striped top and blazer dress this look up, and the casual shoes and bag keep it everyday. It’s the perfect way to add a touch of color to those early spring days!

## 5. Sundress…

![Sundress…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/5_sundress_384x576.jpg)

One of the most straightforward ways to wear yellow is by opting for a cute fitted sundress. I love the puffed sleeves on this one! Keep it chic by adding a cute bag and a vintage belt, and keeping the rest of your look casual.

## 6. Camis…

![Camis…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/6_camis_200x333.jpg)

I love how this cute yellow textured cami brightens up the whole look. Nude heels and tight jeans are a classic combination, and a geometric jacket adds interest. It’s a look that could easily be taken from day to night effortlessly, and it really looks great. I’m definitely going to hunt down a yellow cami!

## 7. Shoes…

![Shoes…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/04/7_shoes_400x557.jpg)

If you’ve already got your outfit sorted, there are plenty of ways to wear yellow using accessories, too. These heels are just perfect for adding a splash of neon to jeans or dresses, and yellow bracelets and earring are equally as easy to find. Even a yellow belt can be enough to give your outfit a fresh new feel. And if you fancy accessorizing your hair, try a jeweled hair clip, or yellow feathers placed into a bun.

With all these ways to wear yellow there is really no excuse for having a dull wardrobe! I’ve been adding a few neon pieces here and there, and I can’t wait to wear them all. It’s such a happy color! What are your favorite ways to wear yellow? Let me know!
