# 8 Wardrobe Must Haves 2012 ...

Wardrobe must-haves 2012: there’s an abundance of trends at the moment and knowing which ones to incorporate into our wardrobes can be tricky. You want items that are trend-based but will last more than one season. If you haven’t already, learn what your wardrobe must-haves for 2012 are with the help of this simple list.

## 1. Button up Shirt

![Button up Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_button-up-shirt_554x369.jpg)

This wardrobe must-have for 2012 can be classic or trend-based, depending on what style you choose. White, cream and black colours are timeless and will no doubt last you years to come. If you want something more on-trend choose shirts in pastel or neon colours, a statement print, or made from sheer fabrics.

## 2. Statement Necklace

![Statement Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_statement-necklace_500x334.jpg)

Big, bright, and bold jewellery is where it’s at this year. We’ve seen it on the runways and now it’s time for it to take place on the ‘real way’. It’s also one of the easiest ways to work new season trends into your outfit. From fun neon numbers to tribal inspired pieces to costume jewels, make sure your necklace makes a bold statement.

## 3. Retro Sunglasses

![Retro Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_retro-sunglasses_500x333.jpg)

These wardrobe must-haves 2012 are instant outfit updaters. A pair of retro sunglasses is all you need to take your outfit from drab to fab. Go glam in retro cat eye frames or try something quirkier with a steam punk inspired design.

## 4. Street Smart Sneakers

![Street Smart Sneakers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_street-smart-sneakers_500x323.jpg)

Work a fashionable look in extreme comfort with a pair of stylish sneakers. The look is much more high-end designer than trackside work outs. Sneakers with wedge heels are having a major trending moment, but if this look isn’t for you then go with a retro pair of high tops or sneakers with metallic finishes. You’ll be mastering the art of off-duty dressing in no time!

## 5. Oversized Watch

![Oversized Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_oversized-watch_500x349.jpg)

Oversized watches make for chic wardrobe must-haves 2012. When choosing a watch, opt for those with a more menswear inspired design. Rose gold colours are popular right now, but you also can’t go wrong with yellow gold or silver finishes.

## 6. Minimalist Bag

![Minimalist Bag](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_minimalist-bag_554x370.jpg)

Thanks to designer brands like Celine, minimalist bags are at the forefront of fashion once more. The best part about simple, fuss-free bags is that they have longevity. Some bags date quite easily but clean and classic designs are timeless.

## 7. Cropped Jeans

![Cropped Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_cropped-jeans_554x341.jpg)

These wardrobe must-haves for 2012 are a simple update on a classic. This year, opt for skinny jeans in cropped styles that show off your ankles. Coloured jeans are on trend, but you can’t go past classic black for Audrey Hepburn inspired glamour.

## 8. Printed Pants

![Printed Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_printed-pants_550x433.jpg)

2012 is all about the prints, and pants are no exception. Give boring pants the boot and embrace a world of prints. Floral and scarf print pants are the most popular choices and as far as wardrobe must-haves 2012 go, this one is easily open to interpretation.

These are just some of the more popular wardrobe must-haves for 2012. If you haven’t already, now’s the time to start incorporating them into your wardrobe. What are your wardrobe must-haves 2012?
