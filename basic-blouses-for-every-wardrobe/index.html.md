# 8 Basic Blouses for Every Wardrobe ...

Basic blouses are essential pieces for every wardrobe. A basic blouse is versatile and when worn in a neutral colour can be dressed up or down to suit a range of occasions. You don’t have to spend a lot on the perfect basic blouse, but know that choosing one in quality materials and with a quality cut can make all the difference. The following are a few stylish basic blouses you may want to add to your collection.

## 1. Longline Sleeveless Button up

_ **Price:** $11.80 at forever21.com_

Basic blouses in sleeveless styles are not only ideal for wearing during the summer months but also for layering when the temperature drops. This cheap and cheerful option is made from cotton and has a longline design. Wear it with coloured jeans for a casual look or tuck it into tailored pants for something more refined.

## 2. J.Crew Silk Blouse

![J.Crew Silk Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/2_j-crew-silk-blouse_302x400.jpg)

**Price:** $100.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ZSMZcG)

Treat yourself by investing in a luxe silk blouse. This one is made from off-white semi-sheer silk. It has a classic design with a concealed front placket and two front pockets. Give a basic blouse a more contemporary edge by teaming it with cutting edge materials like neoprene and patent leather.

## 3. Sleeveless Silk Shirt

![Sleeveless Silk Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/3_sleeveless-silk-shirt_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/DbI2q3)

Black basic blouses are just as much of a wardrobe necessity as white or neutral ones. This sleeveless blouse is made from 100% silk and has a button-up design with a single breast pocket. Work it into a cool night time look by teaming it with a black leather skirt, ankle boots, and metallic jewellery.

## 4. White Boxy Shirt

![White Boxy Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/4_white-boxy-shirt_295x400.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This boxy blouse is a more contemporary take on the wardrobe classic. It has a button-up front, breast pocket, and cap-style sleeves. Wear it over printed pants and with pointed heels for an on-trend look.

## 5. Crisp Button-up Shirt

_ **Price:** $16.80 at forever21.com_

Basic blouses like this crisp button-up blouse are perfect for working a menswear inspired look. This blouse has a basic collar design and is made from a cotton blend. It also has darts to give it a more feminine silhouette. Wear it with cropped pants and brogues for a slightly androgynous look.

## 6. Day Birger Et Mikkelson Georgette Blouse

![Day Birger Et Mikkelson Georgette Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/6_day-birger-et-mikkelson-georgette-blouse_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/1Bdyub)

This blush blouse is great for creating a soft look. It has a longer length design with a curved hem and concealed button fastenings. The longer length of this blouse makes it suitable for wearing over leggings as well as tucking into skirts.

## 7. A Simple Rose Top

![A Simple Rose Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/05/7_a-simple-rose-top_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $44.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This blush coloured basic blouse is much more affordable than the previous option. It has a fairly basic design with concealed button fastenings and flap chest pockets. Tuck it into a floral skirt and pop on some Mary-Jane shoes for a sweet, romantic look.

## 8. Silk Military Style Shirt

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/M07lov)

A military style shirt can give your outfit a bit of an off-duty edge. This basic blouse is made from silk and comes in a light sage colour. The button-fastening front pockets give it that military inspired twist. Loosely tuck the front of this blouse into a pair of denim shorts for a casual weekend look.

Whether you’re after an investment piece or something more affordable, these are just a few basic blouses for you to try out. The basic blouse will stand the test of time, fashion-wise, and is essential to any wardrobe. How would you style your basic blouses?
