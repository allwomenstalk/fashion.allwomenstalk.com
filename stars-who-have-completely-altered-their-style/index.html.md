# 7 Stars Who Have Completely Altered Their Style ...

There are so many stars who have altered their style this year, especially considering the amount of child stars who are back in the spotlight and impressing everyone with their gorgeous sense of style. Take Demi Lovato and Selena Gomez for example. They started out on the Disney Channel wearing skinny jeans and Converse high tops, and today they’re some of the most influential stars who have altered their style. That being said, all of these women are absolutely killing the fashion world these days!

## 1. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rg/o2/x783h0kd_585x1170.jpg)

I don’t think I need to remind everyone of the days of Taylor Swift and her cowboy boots. While her style has constantly changed over the years, she has recently become a style icon, and she made that clear in her music video for Blank Space. In fact, she even has a song called “Style” on her new album, and when you listen to it, you’ll probably wish you never went of “Style” either! Especially this year, Taylor Swift is one of the biggest stars who have altered their style in the spotlight!

## 2. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/sd/bym0y2f2_600x825.jpg)

It goes without saying that Miley Cyrus has experienced quite the fashion transformation over the course of her career. In 2008, she was Hannah Montana, and today, she is anything but. Whether you preferred her style in 2007 or today, I t think we all can agree that her style is one of the most drastic changes she’s made during her career!

## 3. Lauren Conrad

![Lauren Conrad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/cl/iusm6b5q_600x900.jpg)

You probably remember your first introduction to Lauren Conrad during her Laguna Beach years. It’s probably safe to say that you didn’t expect her to become the person she is today: a lifestyle and fashion media mogul with an eye for class that the world hasn’t seen since Martha Stewart hit the scene. Sometimes, it’s easy to forget that Lauren Conrad even had her start on reality TV!

## 4. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/j2/xfxw52q5_405x600.jpg)

Everyone remembers Katy Perry’s break out onto the scene with 2008’s “I Kissed a Girl.” From there, she transformed from a slightly punk-pop chick, to a bubblegum sweet pop singer. Today, she’s still just as over the top in the best way possible, but she continues to out-do herself over and over again!

## 5. Demi Lovato

![Demi Lovato](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/v8/ursktgyi_399x594.jpg)

Demi Lovato got her start on the Disney Channel during an interesting time. She was one of the last breakout stars of the Disney Channel era in 2008, and her style made that clear. However, over the years, she has blossomed into one of the most sophisticated yet fun style icons for young women in Hollywood today!

## 6. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/my/gx/ibfnj49o_333x500.jpg)

Being Demi Lovato’s best friend at the beginning of their Hollywood careers, their style was pretty similar when they started out together. However, a lot of people don’t know that Selena Gomez was a total tomboy. She hated wearing dresses and loved wearing Converse high-tops whenever she could. Today, that couldn’t be further from the truth, and Selena Gomez is definitely a certified style icon!

## 7. Emma Watson

![Emma Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/gg/hk2cl3f7_600x969.jpg)

The world was introduced to Emma Watson at a young age when she made her debut in the Harry Potter movies. Everyone has seen her transformation from the first Harry Potter premiere and the last, and I think we can all agree in saying that we all covet Emma Watson’s gorgeous style.

Who’s your favorite style icon? What’s the craziest celebrity style transformation, in your opinion? Let me know in the comments!
