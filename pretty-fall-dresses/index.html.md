# 8 Pretty Fall Dresses ...

Fall Dresses are one of my very favorite things to shop for this time of year! My linen summer dresses aren’t quite right for the falling temperatures, but m winter dresses are still too warm, so I’ve been looking for something in-between, and my favorite shop, Anthropologie, has loads of them! If you’re looking for fall dresses, keep reading! I’ve made a list of my favorite fall dresses, and here they are.

## 1. Tangerine Flicker Dress

![Tangerine Flicker Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y7/pb/sizjaif154c92f0a0db9d814073608_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $358.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
What better way to celebrate the season than with this gorgeous tangerine-colored fall dress? It’s made by Tracy Reese, and features delicate contrasting embroidery for visual interest. Add emerald green or black accessories, and you’re set for the office or date night (or both!).

## 2. Ottoman Poppies Dress

![Ottoman Poppies Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/uu/jstg5ebb54c92f0ad1b75103536996_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $298.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This gorgeous silk dress needs one thing to be ready to wear: a pair of tall dark brown riding boots. Notice the dainty cut-outs along the bodice, and the lush poppy print. Add a cropped cardi for cooler days and nights, and with those afore-mentioned boots, this dress is ideal for fall!

## 3. Noon & Night Dress

![Noon & Night Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nn/io/l8olu74354c92f0bbb553031157489_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $158.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This vintage-inspired slubby knit a-line dress is gorgeous, flattering to every figure. The deep ruby-red color is flattering to every complexion, too, so feel free to add cut-out booties, patterned tights, and a chunky conversation-starter necklace, and rock the red fall dress!

## 4. Mark Making Dress

![Mark Making Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/ny/dd728rnw54c92f0ca9ce4038421154_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $248.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Not every fall dress is made for a night on the town, though, with the right accessories, this belted beauty can go from casual to date-ready in moments. I love the subtle pattern and the loose, drapey fit. Gorgeous!

## 5. Cloak of Quills Dress

![Cloak of Quills Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/ef/q6s27nyn54c92f0d6bc1a868237624_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $228.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How marvelously unique! This wrap dress by Rutzou features a feather pattern I love, and a figure-flattering drape that I haven’t seen anywhere else. How elegant and interesting… how would you wear this fall dress?

## 6. Different Degrees Maxi Dress

![Different Degrees Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/uk/pa3lg0vr54c92f0e3f6d8828645608_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $178.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I absolutely must have this maxi dress! It’s softer than clouds, and the color combination stripes make it fun. Id’ wear it with flats for the office, then add a chunky bracelet and ankle booties for night-time wear. It’s available in a range of sizes, too, so you’re sure to get a perfect fit.

## 7. Wavering Grid Dress

![Wavering Grid Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/q0/qyetcc7u54c92f0f2fa77971711907_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $148.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Show off your tin waist, or just add to the illusion of one with this gorgeous cross-hatch printed dress. Add pale-colored hose and classic taupe heels, or layer with a cardigan and boots for warmer fall wear.

## 8. Ihrin Shirtdress

![Ihrin Shirtdress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/yb/bkgpeqrn54c92f1000276646346437_268x400.jpg)

**Price:** $148.00 at [anthropologie.com](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Add the classic look of a shirt-dress to your wardrobe this fall, and you won’t regret it! This one is ideal, with two lush colors to choose from (green or dark purple). It’s nipped at the waist, and has cuffed sleeves, a flattering self belt, loads of pleats on the skirt, and even side pockets (perfect for stashing a lippie).

With so many beautiful fall dresses to choose from, there’s no way I’ll be able to choose only one of them! I especially adore the first one and the last one, but which of these pretty fall dresses strikes your fancy? How would you wear it? Do tell!
