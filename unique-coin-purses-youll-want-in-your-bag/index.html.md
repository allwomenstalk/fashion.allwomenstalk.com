# 9 Unique Coin Purses You'll Want in Your Bag ...

Coin purses come in super handy whether you need easy access to change for parking meters, the laundromat, public transportation or just to sort out your money. If you use a coin purse on a regular basis, why not use one that is fun to look at and carry around?! I found nine incredibly cute coin purses that I think you’re going to fall in love with and want in your bag immediately if not sooner!

## 1. Scented Macaroon Coin Purse

![Scented Macaroon Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204232_5117_500x374.jpg)

I have to say that this is one of the most unique coin purses I’ve ever seen! I love the design, color and it’s scented-does it get any better than that?! This chic coin purse measures 2.75” x 2.25” so it’s on the small side, but the attention to detail and the fact that it’s deliciously scented make it a must-have for any foodie or macaroon lover! Available at FredFlare.com for $14.

## 2. Diana Eng Fortune Cookie Coin Purse

![Diana Eng Fortune Cookie Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204210_4525_400x300.jpg)

This is another food-themed coin purse that’s totally original and delightful! Measuring 4” x 4” x 2”, this coin purse won’t hold a ton of change but it’s perfect for holding loose change or small items like earrings that you want to keep close by. Each coin purse is made from recycled leather and has a unique number so you can track its origins! Available at DianaEng.com for $65.

## 3. Kate Spade Cinema City Coin Purse

![Kate Spade Cinema City Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22105003_1175_522x345.jpg)

If you’re a movie buff, this coin purse was made for you! This cute coin purse is designed to resemble 3D glasses and will fit your change, tickets or any loose items you don’t want getting lost in your bag. This measures 6"W x 2"H x 1/4"D and would fit perfectly in any bag! Available at Nordstrom for $98.

## 4. Longchamp ‘Le Pliage’ Coin Purse

![Longchamp ‘Le Pliage’ Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204211_1460_600x399.jpg)

If you’re one of the many women who bought the full size ‘Le Pliage’ bag from Longchamp, this cute coin purse belongs in your bag! I have this bag, but had no idea there was a miniature coin purse version. But even if you don’t own the bag, this 3 1/2"W x 2 3/4"H x 2"D coin purse is compact and ideal for carrying your change and tiny treasures. Available at Nordstrom for $45.

## 5. Candy Store Silicone Coin Purse

![Candy Store Silicone Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204321_6960_600x401.jpg)

Silicone coin purses are just as functional as they are cute! The material makes them easy-to-clean, durable and they’re quite stylish, don’t you think? This is a great coin purse to throw in your purse, gym bag or by itself to keep your personal items on hand! It measures 3.5''W x 3.25''H x 1.5''D and is available at Ulta for $4.99 in seven different colors.

## 6. ‘Huge Bosca-Old Leather’ Coin Purse

![‘Huge Bosca-Old Leather’ Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204340_7153_349x344.jpg)

This coin purse is simple yet chic and although it’s considered a men’s product, I think it’s perfect for both sexes! It measures 3.25"W x 3.25"H x 0.5"D and makes the ideal coin purse for your pocket or purse. Made of leather with a snap closure, this coin purse will keep all your loose change in order so you’re always prepared.

## 7. Blue Q Boss Lady Coin Purse

![Blue Q Boss Lady Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204211_9392_600x420.jpg)

Show everyone who’s boss with this coin purse made of 95% recycled post-consumer material. It has a cool vintage-y feel, which makes it perfect to carry around by itself or in your tote. Carry your dollar bills, change, hair ties, lip balm and more in this 4.3"W x 3.2"H pouch. Available at BlueQ.com for $3.99.

## 8. LOUNGEFLY OWL with HEART EYES COIN BAG

![LOUNGEFLY OWL with HEART EYES COIN BAG](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204210_9840_291x252.jpg)

Owl lovers will adore this cute coin purse from Loungefly! This comes in faux leather and measures 4.5" x 5", so it’s one of the larger coin purses of the bunch. It’s got a cute cream and brown heart pattern inside and it’s roomy enough to carry your ID, credit cards and your money. Available at Loungefly.com for $21.25.

## 9. Blue Q Shoe Money Coin Purse

![Blue Q Shoe Money Coin Purse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/09204211_7182_600x422.jpg)

Another cool coin purse from Blue Q! I had to include this one in the list because what girl doesn’t want a coin purse that encourages her shoe habit?! This is also made from 95% post-consumer material and measures 4.3"W x 3.2"H. Available at BlueQ.com for $3.99.

Any of these stylish coin purses can help keep you organized and on top of all your belongings. If you currently own a bulky wallet, why not drop some change on a coin purse so you can keep your bag neat and clean? Would you buy any of these coin purses?
