# Stop Doing These Things That Make You Look Less Classy ...

Your clothes can say a lot about you. That's why you should be careful with the way you walk around town. Listen to [Cosmopolitan's](https://www.cosmopolitan.com/style-beauty/advice/a47107/things-that-make-you-look-shabby/) advice and stop doing these things that make you look less classy:

## 1. Your Underwear is Showing

![Your Underwear is Showing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i2/ol/fra2i7ob560e92fb1b303081715293_600x572.jpg)

If your skirt is short enough that your underwear is visible, it's time to change.

## 2. Your Shoelaces Are a Mess

![Your Shoelaces Are a Mess](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/pj/f5aac9vt560e92fbd289e220145706_600x449.jpg)

It doesn't take long to fix them, so make sure they're tied correctly.

## 3. You're Carrying Two Bags at Once

![You're Carrying Two Bags at Once](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/jj/zzro36av560e92fc80559937913752_600x446.jpg)

If you can afford an expensive bag, only carry that one around.

## 4. You're Always Adjusting Your Clothes

![You're Always Adjusting Your Clothes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/sa/gekvvcho560e92fc4de9b607192250_600x465.jpg)

If they fit you right, you wouldn't have to adjust them all day long.

## 5. Your Clothes Are Covered in Pet Hair

![Your Clothes Are Covered in Pet Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/vb/e692409q560e92fca102d457574556_600x465.jpg)

It's hard to get cat hair off of black jeans, but try to do it before you leave the house.

## 6. Your Pants Hit the Ground

![Your Pants Hit the Ground](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vu/d1/bgd8l5xt560e92fcdb7e3182583375_600x572.jpg)

You don't want to get them dirty, do you?

## 7. Your Top is Only Half Tucked

![Your Top is Only Half Tucked](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/y9/cvsvzcfg560e92fd913ad970269529_600x572.jpg)

If you're going to tuck your shirt in, make sure it stays tucked.

Now that you know what fashion mistakes make you look less classy, you can avoid making them. Do you ever make any of these fashion mistakes?
