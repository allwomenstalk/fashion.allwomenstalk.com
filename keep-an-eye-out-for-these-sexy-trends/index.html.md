# Keep an Eye out for These Sexy 2016 Trends ...

Don’t expect too many trends to end with 2015 just yet! We had so many new trends in 2015, and most of them just aren’t ready to die out yet! However, we’re also welcoming so many new trends in 2016. A lot of these trends are extensions of some of your favorite 2015 trends, and others are completely unlike anything you’ve seen before, making this a list you simply cannot miss!

## 1. Faux Leopard Print Fur

![Faux Leopard Print Fur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/k4/fcd9xsm15680412c9d470800495108_600x898.jpg)

Faux fur was one of the biggest trends of 2015, and that’s not expected to go away just yet in the new year. However, leopard print is sure to be the faux fur of choice for most ladies out there. If you’re looking for a faux fur trend that feels fresh, new, and on point, you really can’t go wrong with leopard print faux fur!

## 2. Unique Necklines

![Unique Necklines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hi/pj/ja0y528f56804157eb256855801381_600x800.jpg)

Unique necklines are going to be all the rage in 2016. If you’re looking to go a little bit crazier than usual with your fashion choices in 2016, start with crazy necklines. They’re such an easy way to add some whimsy to any plain old outfit!

## 3. Suede

If you were paying attention to fashion closely in 2015, you noticed just how big the 1970s were this year. Everywhere you turned, runways seemed like they were traveling back in time to the 1970s with their fashion choices. That’s not expected to change in 2016, and in fact, one of the biggest 1970s trends, suede, is going to be one of the absolutely biggest trends in 2016 as well!

## 4. Pastels

I’m all about those bright colors, but in 2016, pastels are going to be huge. Not even in just in the spring, but year round, women are going to be flocking for neutrals and pastels that are sure to turn heads and catch the attention of other fashion loving ladies out there!

## 5. X-Shaped Halter Top

This new twist on a halter top is going to be one of the biggest trends in 2016. The halter top neckline is made to look like it’s in the shape of an X, which is so fun and unique while also being incredibly stylish and chic. If you’re looking for a fun new way to bring halter tops back, try this great twist on them!

## 6. Flats

![Flats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/xg/faz5gglp568041bc5cc1b704787807_600x600.jpg)

Every girl loves a good pair of heels, but in 2016, your feet will thank you immensely, because flats are going to be in style in a huge way. These gender-neutral flats are going to completely change the fashion game. Your feet will look chic no matter what you’re wearing or where you’re going!

## 7. Neoprene

Scuba material is never really a mainstream trend, it’s mostly one that we reserve for the runways, but in 2016, it’s going to be everywhere. Embrace it, because before you know it, you’ll want to wear it everywhere. It looks chic but it’s also super comfortable, making it a trend that you’re going to love wearing this summer!

What’s your favorite soon-to-be 2016 fashion trend? Will you be rocking any of these trends or are you sticking to your favorite 2015 trends? Or do you think you’ll try something else entirely that didn’t make this list? Let me know what you’re looking forward to wearing in the comments!
