# 7 Sheer Panel Outfits That Took over the Red Carpet ...

Sheer panel outfits have become a huge trend within the stars and on the red carpet. This is a great way to show off your killer body while still looking classy (in some cases!). The look can be taken overboard very easily, and needs to be done in small portions. As I say that, some of these stars didn’t get that memo. Here are some of the most statement making sheer panel outfits.

## 1. Jaimie Alexander

![Jaimie Alexander](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mx/hs/d1/wcfktzt5_600x948.jpg)

This is the most daring outfit I may have ever seen, and that is why it tops our sheer panel outfits list. Jaimie stepped out at the Thor: The Dark World premiere rocking this sheer panel dress showing off her amazing body. This is still a dress the media cannot stop talking about. Not only is this a risqué dress but also eyes all over were just waiting for a wardrobe malfunction. The black floor-length cutaway gown featured a sheer panel that started at her neckline and plunged down to her toes. Not only the front, but the back also featured this sheer panel look. Wowza.

## 2. Paris Hilton

![Paris Hilton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l0/rf/au/sti4whmw_600x969.jpg)

When Paris stepped onto this year’s Grammy red carpet, heads turned. The blond bombshell showed off her toned body in this white high neck number while walking the carpet. While the front of the turtleneck design appears classy and covered up, it's an entirely different story once you catch a peek at Hilton's bare backside.

## 3. Gwyneth Paltrow

![Gwyneth Paltrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uw/f6/go/q36soitz_600x824.jpg)

Jaws dropped when Paltrow stepped out at the Iron Man 3 premiere in a very risky sheer paneled dress. This Antonio Berardi floor-length gown featured sheer panels running down the sides, and bell like sleeves. This isn’t the first time the Coldplay wifey has rocked this look, but I have to admit that I like it!

## 4. Rihanna

![Rihanna](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/px/ti/mh/iu6zuo5f_600x900.jpg)

The Grammy 2011 red carpet got a lot hotter as this red haired babe strutted her stuff in this beautiful white and sheer dress. The off-the-shoulder dress looked amazing on the singer; it accentuated her curves and elongated her figure with the mermaid flare. I love this look on RiRi!

## 5. Kelly Rowland

![Kelly Rowland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/o1/ht/cb4uwc5r_500x730.jpg)

Now this is how you do sheer panels! Kelly looked absolutely gorgeous in this Georges Chakra black dress when she walked the 2013 Grammy red carpet. The small cut outs in the long gown as well as the plain black material give this dress a classier look. Without showing it all, Kelly gives little peeps of her rock hard body through the slim sheer panels.

## 6. Eva Longoria

![Eva Longoria](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/pd/xh/voa7iaxv_583x875.jpg)

Eva hit the Global Gift Gala red carpet in a Yanina Haute Couture midnight blue, sequinned sheer panel dress. While the actress does wear sheer panel dresses a lot, this takes the cake. With this dress not only featuring sheer panels, it also featured leopard like design around the chest and legs. Thankfully, she finished look with simple navy shoes. Thoughts?

## 7. Katy Perry

![Katy Perry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mn/uw/kr/zomfjznd_417x600.jpg)

Katy rocked a gothic glam look while attending the NARM Music Biz Awards in California. Still sporting her metallic-tinged purple locks, Katy wore a floor-length sheer dress with black panels covering her modesty. The long sleeve adds a touch of a unique feature while Katy’s curvy body shines through. This look really works on the singer!

While we love sheer panels, we can’t decide if any of these should be repeated. What are you thoughts? Will you or have you ever rocked this look?
