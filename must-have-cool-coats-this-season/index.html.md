# 7 Must\-Have Cool Coats for This Season ...

A classic coat is a true investment for the cooler months. Whether it is a timeless trench or super trendy oversized coat, these styles are worthy of a spot in your cool coat collection. Turn up the heat for fall with 7 must-have cool coats for this season.

## 1. Trench Coats

![Trench Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/130_675x1012.jpg)

A timeless classic, the trench coat is effortlessly chic. Season after season we see the return of the trench coat with a new modern makeover. This fall we’re seeing longer trench coats that hit at the knee or just below for a sleek [feminine look](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/super-simple-tips-to-look-more-feminine/). A trench coat is a wardrobe staple every fashionista must own that serves function and purpose with just as much style.

## 2. Leather Jackets

![Leather Jackets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/222_600x900.jpg)

A leather jacket is also a trend that is always being reinvented. If you pick up a leather jacket without so many trend influenced details it is sure to last you a lifetime while still remaining current and chic. Sleek, sexy and easy to wear, a leather jacket can dress up a casual outfit or add some tough edge to a sweet feminine look. For a seriously trendy take on the leather jacket this fall opt for pieces with lots of structural design details like zips, pockets and patchwork.

## 3. Oversized Coats

![Oversized Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/319_565x880.jpg)

The reigning style of the season is the oversized coat. This supersized outwear piece is a must-have staple trend for fall. Comfortable and chic, oversized coats and maxi jackets dominated the runways with roomy silhouettes and box cuts. Creating a vintage inspired and boyish shape, these voluminous coats are ideal for wearing over top layered outfits in the cold temperatures.

## 4. Pea Coats

![Pea Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/420_464x800.jpg)

A tailored pea coat is versatile when it comes to projecting a certain style and trend. Added embellishments and adornments can easily take this simple structured pea coat to a militia stance. You can also take it back to a sophisticated suiting with button down designs and front pockets for menswear inspired dressing. This fall prepare to battle the harsh seasonal elements in an army worthy pea coat with urban utilitarian cool.

## 5. Fur Coats

![Fur Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/521_600x969.jpg)

Whether it’s real or faux, fur is always an elegant and luxe statement. Instead of opting for an all over fur coat, which can make you look bulky and not to mention extremely hot, try choosing outerwear with chic accents like a fur collar or stole. The combination of the two different fabrics, fur and wool or anything else, creates a rich plush look.

## 6. Asymmetrical Coats

![Asymmetrical Coats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/09/620_1200x1500.jpg)

Asymmetrical tailoring and bias cuts have a graphic cool edge that can only be achieved with precision style. The layered draping creates various attention, drawing lines that adds visual interest with striking silhouettes. Experiment with asymmetrical designs to play up the layering trend this season and stay a cut above the rest for fall.

## 7. Blanket Coats

_ Wrap yourself up in fall’s big cozy blanket coat and stay warm all season long. For blanket coats think cowboy style with a sweeping gaucho and nomadic vibe. Over-sized and super long, blanket coats give you the ability to play with texture and mix prints and patterns to create that travelling bohemian feel. Even heavy capes and fringed ponchos worked their way into this blanket coat trend. Keep the rest of your look sleek and slim to balance out the sheer volume of this cool coat trend.

When the temperatures drop a cool coat is a necessity for the fall season that can be easily carried over to the winter. Heat up the season with these coats that are sure to have you cool, cozy and comfortable for fall. Which must-have cool coats will you be working into your wardrobe this season?
