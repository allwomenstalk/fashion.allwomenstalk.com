# Wear a Matching Two\-piece Combo with Some Celeb Inspo ...

Matching two-piece sets have been super popular this summer and you can easily transition them into the fall. If you're wearing a crop top + skirt, layer a cardigan over it and maybe add some tights underneath. Check out the different looks you can create with some inspiration from celebs!

## 1. Selena Gomez

![Selena Gomez](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/ra/xqn7801x_417x560.jpg)

Selena's maroon long sleeved crop top looks fabulous with the matching high waisted skirt. Nude pumps complete the look!

## 2. Reese Witherspoon

![Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hx/v9/c2f34t03_600x827.jpg)

Reese's all-white top and skirt combo is the perfect balance between girly and sophisticated. Adding a pop of color with a purse is the perfect touch.

## 3. Florence Welch

![Florence Welch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lr/i0/emnvfl91_419x594.jpg)

Florence's street style is unreal. I'm obsessed with this patterned black and white set. The long pants, booties, and floppy hat is perfect for the fall!

## 4. Bella Thorne

![Bella Thorne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/uu/bi9jtta2_600x938.jpg)

If you're not afraid of having all eyes on you, Bella's pop-art matching set is for you.

## 5. Victoria Justice

I'm a big fan of all-black ensembles but who knew a dark navy color would look just as good? The mesh is a really cool style!

## 6. Taylor Swift

![Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/lb/lcviimbp_600x864.jpg)

Taylor's been looking better than ever! She moved to New York City and has been spotted wearing the cutest matching crop top + skirt combos. The pleated skirt and textured fabric gives the outfit some dimension.

## 7. Ariana Grande

![Ariana Grande](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/yx/dzn0kspl_600x900.jpg)

Ariana Grande's got a signature style - girly, fun, and chic. I loved this hot pink two-piece set; it makes quite the statement!

Who had your favorite look?
