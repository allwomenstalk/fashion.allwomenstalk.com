# 8 on\-Trend Orange Garments ...

Bright, bold colours have been featuring heavily in many recent runway collections. One of the more popular colours in the limelight is orange. Citrus colours in general are proving popular, but orange is the stand out of the season. Take a look at a few on-trend orange garments below.

## 1. Chinti and Parker Woven Cotton Shirt

![Chinti and Parker Woven Cotton Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ga/sm/u0hu94x954c8ff11ae7b8201730654_300x400.jpg)

**Price:** $140.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/bTSmiP)
This bright orange shirt is exclusive to Net-A-Porter. This semi-sheer shirt has a Peter Pan collar, button-down front, and button fastening cuffs. As this shirt is so bright, make sure the rest of your outfit is fairly neutral. Try a tan leather skirt and wrap sandals for a cool off-duty look.

## 2. Topshop Coral Lace Trim Shorts

![Topshop Coral Lace Trim Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z6/x7/snuhfy3u54c8ff1285b0d469309792_300x293.jpg)

**Price:** $76.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/MFG52I)
Orange colours can be quite harsh, so if you want to ease your way into the trend, try a softer coral colour. These coral shorts would make a fine choice for the summer months. They have a button and zip fly fastening, pleating from the waist, and a lace trim. They are fairly loose around the legs so would be flattering for most figures.

## 3. Tibi Basketweave Cotton-Blend a-line Dress

![Tibi Basketweave Cotton-Blend a-line Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/ck/loh2652e54c8ff13668af609038438_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $355.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/dWobp2)
On-trend orange garments will always make a statement. This A-line dress is a guaranteed head turning garment. This dress has a round neck, V-shaped back, and narrow cuts at the bodice. Try your hand at colour blocking and pair this dress with brightly coloured accessories.

## 4. Dorothy Perkins Orange Fit and Flare Dress

![Dorothy Perkins Orange Fit and Flare Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/g2/ltj026jw54c8ff14438a6981677806_300x407.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If the last dress is out of your price range, then take a look at this affordable alternative. This dress has quite a classic silhouette. It has three quarter length sleeves, a fitted top, and flared skirt. It also has a zip fastening at the back. This dress would look great paired with cream ballet flats and neutral accessories.

## 5. Acne Niki Cutout Stretch-Crepe Dress

![Acne Niki Cutout Stretch-Crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/fj/fu1a71l154c8ff1530025573615084_300x450.jpg)

**Price:** $290.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/EJscH6)
From the front, this dress looks like a normal t-shirt dress, but the back reveals something quite unexpected. The back of the dress reveals a quite intricate looking draped cut out detail. On-trend orange garments such as this dress make great outfit centrepieces. Show off the draped detailing at the back and wear this dress as is, with statement wedge ankle boots.

## 6. Cacharel Pleated Silk Skirt

**Price:** $709.05 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bright orange silk skirt is quite luxurious. It is high waisted and falls to mid-calf. It also has a slightly longer back hem. Pair this skit with a cropped cable knit sweater, strappy sandals, and a slim clutch for a casually chic look.

## 7. Topshop Orange Chino Trousers

![Topshop Orange Chino Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x5/hw/hsv6de5154c8ff16a5449458883973_300x448.jpg)

**Price:** $75.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/VH7h7B)
Work the chino trend with these orange trousers. They are a darker red-orange colour and feature a button and zip fly fastening, twisted front seams, and turned up cuffs. Wear these with a simple white tank top and loafer-style shoes for a casual weekend look.

## 8. Steven Alan Renada Double-Breasted Silk Blazer

**Price:** $385.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/wRVzMb)
On-trend orange garments can come in quite ‘sensible’ forms, such as this double breasted blazer. This silk blazer has black button fastenings, front flap pockets, and is fully lined. This bright yellow-orange would look great against crisp white fabrics. Wear it open or closed, depending on the look you are after.

There are quite a number of on-trend orange garments available at the moment. The trick is to pick the right orange for your skin tone. Which of these orange garments do you like the look of most?
