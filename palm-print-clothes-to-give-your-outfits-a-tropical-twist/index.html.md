# 9 Palm Print Clothes to Give Your Outfits a Tropical Twist ...

Palm print clothes might have you dreaming of island vacations and beachside destinations. However, if what we’re seeing in stores is anything to go by, then this tropical trend is one that you can wear all year round. Yes, even in the middle of the city in the dead of winter. Right now you’ll find palm prints on everything from sweaters to skorts. Take a look at these palm print clothes that will inject a little bit of the tropics into your everyday outfit.

## 1. Topshop Palm Print Skater Dress

![Topshop Palm Print Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g3/xu/ro/jsikq75d_600x900.jpg)

Channel those balmy summer evenings with this palm print dress from Topshop. It comes in a cool coral colour with a black all over palm tree print. Like most palm print clothes, it has a very vacation inspired feel to it. However, the structured design and cut out details at the sides makes this a more fashion forward option. Wear it as is during the summer months or team it with a leather jacket, tights, and ankle boots for a more street-inspired look.

## 2. Kenzo Palm Print Shirt

![Kenzo Palm Print Shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/er/eu/l6/awjmmn1u_599x692.jpg)

This Kenzo palm print shirt is a preppy take on the tropical trend. It comes in a classic collared design in mint, white, and black colors. The hand drawn palm tree prints are a bit more abstract and blend into the striped design. Wear this smart shirt with tailored pants and heels to work or with a skater skirt and ballet flats for something more playful.

## 3. Dezso Palm Print Sweatshirt

![Dezso Palm Print Sweatshirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/ey/wl/tn6fbv2e_599x727.jpg)

This palm print sweatshirt by Dezso has an urban flavor to it. It comes in a not-so-tropical monochrome colourway with a photographic palm tree print to the front and contrasting black sleeves and trims. This kind of sweatshirt is great for working an understated look and you could easily get away with wearing it with jeans and sneakers for an entirely urban look.

## 4. Topshop Tropical Palm Print Skort

![Topshop Tropical Palm Print Skort](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g6/u8/iq/wc00fr7a_599x630.jpg)

This jacquard print skort from Topshop features a striking burnt orange and black palm print design. While it appears to be a wrap skirt from the front, they’re actually easy-to-wear shorts, hence the ‘skort’ tag. Wear these skorts with opaque stockings and a chunky knit sweater during the cooler months or with a plain black t-shirt and sandals in the summer time.

## 5. Equipment Palm Print Blouse

![Equipment Palm Print Blouse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/gq/cp/ux7j7skq_599x890.jpg)

There’s nothing quite like the feeling of a luxe silky blouse and this Equipment blouse comes in a fresh palm print pattern. The grey and green palm print pattern pops against the white background. It’s perfect for adding just the right amount of colour to a basic outfit. Wear it with dark denim jeans or tucked into a high-waist skirt for a smart casual look.

## 6. Topshop Palm Print Romper

![Topshop Palm Print Romper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/tc/k3/mypyrq84_599x838.jpg)

This Topshop romper has a bit of a retro vibe to it. It comes in a relaxed fit with long sleeves, draped lapels, and a silky satin finish. The subdued colours of the palm print pattern also add to the retro feel of the design. Wear this luxe romper at night with wedge heels, statement earrings, and a compact clutch. During the day, dress it down with a floppy fedora and tan lace-up shoes.

## 7. Modcloth Palm Print Maxi Dress

![Modcloth Palm Print Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/yt/kd/ncz5twnc_599x846.jpg)

The maxi dress is here to stay and this one from Modcloth features a repetitive palm print pattern. It has an easy-to-wear strapless design making it great for summer. You can also layer it up during winter months with a cropped sweater, coat, scarf, and ankle boots.

## 8. Mango Palm Print T-shirt

![Mango Palm Print T-shirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/cu/at/d1ngz79u_406x398.jpg)

This t-shirt by Mango features an almost psychedelic palm print design. The faded design is more typical of your peachy palm print pieces. It’s a fun t-shirt that would look great worn with cut-off denim shorts, perfect for checking out the surf or skateboarding along the sidewalk during summer.

## 9. Topshop Palm Print Sweater

![Topshop Palm Print Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/fo/uh/sjyms1t8_599x730.jpg)

Inject some tropical vibes into a winter outfit with this Topshop palm print sweater. The green palm frond pattern is complemented with all-over flower details and would look great with a range of casual daytime looks.

There’s no shortage of palm print clothes this season. Whether you go all out with the tropical vibes or stick to something more subdued is entirely up to you. What do you think of these palm print pieces?
