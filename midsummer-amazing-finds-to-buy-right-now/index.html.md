# 7 Midsummer Amazing Finds to Buy Right Now ...

It's summer time, and who doesn’t love finding out amazing finds to buy? Well I sure do! This is the time to buy that summer dress you just weren’t too sure about, or that bright pink lipstick you have been waiting to find. Whatever it is that you have been dreaming of all winter long, go buy it and rock it this summer. I narrowed down some amazing finds to buy right now!

## 1. Jacob Lipstick

![Jacob Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/xb/i52pma7y54c939beb732e856003963_520x751.jpg)

First of the amazing finds to buy is some colorful lipstick. I was so happy to find out that Jacob has lipsticks, as they are amazing and only $10! This is your chance to buy that bright color you have been waiting to rock, as the summer is the best time for the bright colors. This is a huge trend right now in Hollywood as well as on the streets all over the city.

## 2. Ardene Swimwear

![Ardene Swimwear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/2_ardene-swimwear_600x764.jpg)

I live off of these pieces in the summer time as the suits are a great price while still being adorable and trendy. You can now grab two tops for $15! Why not save some money while looking fierce? This is a great way to wear the latest trends in fabric and design while not feeling guilty, and if you are tired of it next summer, simply donate it.

## 3. Old Navy Flip-flops

![Old Navy Flip-flops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/3_old-navy-flip-flops1_520x693.jpg)

You absolutely cannot go wrong with two pairs of sandals for only $6! They are actually comfortable and perfect for that go-to throw-on pair and are perfect for the beach. Also, go in on an extra 30% off day to save even more!

## 4. Zara Sale

This is my favorite store of all time (I’m addicted!). The prices can definitely range from high to low, but each summer they have a sale with up to 50% off some items. If there is a dress you have been eyeing up, go buy it now! Dresses can start at $29.99 and you can grab a cute top for only $15. Amazing, right?

## 5. EMBROIDERED BEADS DRESS by Mango

![EMBROIDERED BEADS DRESS by Mango](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/18190052_9459_599x597.jpg)

This is an adorable dress that can be worn through the summer as well as the winter with some black pantyhose. Best part? What was $130 is now $60! This is a great deal for something that can be worn all year round but will look adorable at a summer bash.

## 6. Aldo Sale

![Aldo Sale](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/6_aldo-sale_600x600.jpg)

The wedge sneaker trend is still in full force, and what better way to buy them than on sale. This is a trend that might not be around next year, so try and find these sort of things on sale. With an extra 30% off, your sneaker of choice will be a great deal.

## 7. Walmart Sun Hats

![Walmart Sun Hats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/08/7_walmart-sun-hats_500x500.jpg)

What every girl needs for those beach days is a cute sun hat. With a full wall of choices at Walmart, you can nab the cutest one for only $30. The wide brimmed hats with colorful ribbons are to be a sure hit on the beach while you still look super trendy and chic.

This really is the time to do some shopping ladies. These stores are trying to get rid of their summer stock in order to bring in the winter (brr) so it's time to hit the mall and shop till you drop. If you find something full price, try to make sure it can be worn throughout the fall and winter. Buying something now full price for the summer isn’t the smartest move, as you only have a little while left and you might not like it next year. Shop smart ladies!
