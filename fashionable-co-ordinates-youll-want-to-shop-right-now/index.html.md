# 7 Fashionable Co\-Ordinates Youll Want to Shop Right Now ...

It’s all about head-to-toe matching looks these days and it’s easier than ever to work the trend thanks to the availability of fashionable co-ordinates. A range of designers and fashion brands are creating coordinating separates that you can pair together to create on-trend looks. Whether it’s a corporate tailored suit or a playful printed outfit, you can make this season’s fashionable co-ordinates work for a range of occasions. Get matching with these sets of fashionable co-ordinates.

## 1. Topshop Shell Top and Shorts

![Topshop Shell Top and Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/mc/zls0aft4_600x900.jpg)

Some of the most fashionable co-ordinates are the simplest. This Topshop set, made up of a tailored shell top and shorts, is perfect for working a modern, minimalist look. The fresh white separates are perfect for the summer months. They can be worn during the day with tan sandals and a floppy hat, and dressed up at night with some costume jewellery and heels.

## 2. Zara Cardigan and Bermuda Shorts

![Zara Cardigan and Bermuda Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sv/ib/ys6xsz8s_600x743.jpg)

These chic co-ordinates will definitely brighten up your day. Consisting of a boxy cardigan and tailored shorts, these sunshine yellow co-ordinates from Zara are a modern update on classic pieces. Wear a crisp white shirt underneath to break up the bright colours and pop on a pair of pool slides to complete the contemporary look.

## 3. New Look Pixelated Jacket and Skirt

![New Look Pixelated Jacket and Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/y9/iutru7gn_600x766.jpg)

Fashion-forward prints are guaranteed to make a statement. This jacket and skirt set by New Look features an eye-catching, oversized pixelated pattern. It's great for working a contemporary, polished look. Worn together, these co-ordinates are at the height of contemporary chic dressing.

## 4. Topshop Suit Blazer and Trousers

![Topshop Suit Blazer and Trousers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/03/te/x65xvu80_600x900.jpg)

Work a polished look in these matching separates from Topshop. The tailored peach suit blazer and cigarette trousers are a distinctly feminine take on a typically masculine look. Wear the blazer and trousers together for a modern take on power dressing or wear them separately to add tailored touches to your everyday outfits.

## 5. Gat Rimon Leather Tank and Shorts

![Gat Rimon Leather Tank and Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c6/pn/vwaem0zj_600x900.jpg)

Graphic prints are still a firm favourite and these fashionable co-ordinates by Gat Rimon feature a striking all-over marble print. The boxy leather tank top and slouchy shorts are on-trend for the summer months and perfect for working a casual off-duty look. Let the print stand on its own and keep the rest of your look simple with minimal accessories and jewellery.

## 6. Topshop Denim Stripe Tee and Skirt

![Topshop Denim Stripe Tee and Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0z/bw/jfmgm3m5_600x900.jpg)

Wear your denim with a twist with this striped top and skirt set from Topshop. The vertical stripes on the top and skirt create a streamlined look and are a great alternative to your typical horizontal striped patterns. Let the stripes speak for themselves and keep the rest of your outfit fairly minimal with simple sandals and a neutral coloured bag.

## 7. Zara Floral Peplum Top and Flared Skirt

![Zara Floral Peplum Top and Flared Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l8/tt/0xxxpibn_600x744.jpg)

Work a feminine floral look in these fashionable co-ordinates from Zara. The light green floral separates are a fresh take on the trend and are perfect for working a demure, ladylike look. This floral printed outfit is ideal for a garden party or wedding, but could also be dressed down with a cropped denim jacket and slide sandals.

Wearing matching separates is an easy way to trick people into thinking that your outfit is completely pulled together, even if you did happen to throw it on at the last minute. Are you a fan of the matching outfit trend? How would you style these fashionable co-ordinates?
