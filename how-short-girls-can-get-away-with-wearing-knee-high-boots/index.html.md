# How Short Girls Can Get Away with Wearing Knee High Boots ...

Knee high boots can be a little intimidating for short girls. A lot of people like to reserve knee high boots for girls with long, super model-esque legs, but that should never be the case! If you’re a short girl, you can still rock knee high boots! There are just a few extra steps and precautions to take if you’re a short girl. For one, you don’t want too much material bulking up your gorgeous outfit, and more than anything, you need to wear them with confidence. These tips will make wearing tall boots a breeze for even the shortest girls!

## 1. Wear a Short Dress, Skirt, or Shorts

![clothing, footwear, leg, tights, thigh,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ag/up/n8si0wrx586c111484606195491561_600x600.jpg)

When you’re short and wearing knee high boots, you want your legs to look as long as possible. They won’t look as long as possible when you’re wearing layers over your knee-high boots. In fact, that may seem to shorten your legs. Make sure that when wearing knee high boots, you get keep your skirt, dress, or shorts fairly short to create the illusion of supermodel long legs!

## 2. Make It a Heel

![clothing, footwear, denim, street, outerwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/bg/hr1wxkic586c115b691de568352016_600x724.jpg)

If you’re going to wear knee high boots, make sure you pick out a pair that has a pretty good heel (this depends on what height you’re comfortable walking in). A heel will elongate your legs and make them look extra long next to the extra long leg of the boots. Plus, it will give your butt a nice lift that I think no one can complain about!

## 3. Go High-Waisted

![clothing, footwear, fashion, dress, tights,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/i0/y0ep7a0e586c123f9443d895531429_500x492.jpg)

If you’re often self-conscious that your legs are too short, especially when you’re wearing super tall boots, try wearing high-waisted pants or a skirt. That instantly makes your legs look way longer than they may feel, and that is exactly the look you want to create!

## 4. Wear Matching Pants

![white, black, clothing, outerwear, footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/m5/g76vn0jz586c1148d30c4563412424_600x723.jpg)

If you’re not ready to make a huge fashion statement yet, wear matching pants with your knee-high boots. For example, if your boots are black, find a pair of matching black pants to wear with them. It will create a seamless transition from boots to pants and make your look subtler if you’re not ready to rock the dramatic knee-high boots look just yet!

## 5. If You’re Showing Skin, Show a Lot of It

![clothing, footwear, road, snapshot, street,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/vh/v399q1v8586c122fcc4b8806351213_500x492.jpg)

If you’re committing to showing skin, be sure that you’re showing a good chunk. You don’t want your skirt or dress to overlap with your boots. It may not look like you’re wearing and instead it will look like you’re wearing suede or leather leggings, and that’s not a look that anyone wants to create, right?

## 6. Go Bold with Your Accessories

![clothing, spring, fashion, leg, dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bb/q8/k4ra35uq586c127d1204d140480071_600x722.jpg)

Again, if you’re not ready to show off your dramatic boots just yet, go for a dark outfit, but pair it with bold accessories to bring the eye elsewhere. Plus, when pairing the two bold choices together, you’ll look so fashionable and stylish! It’s a win-win!

## 7. Wear Them with Confidence!

![road, clothing, street, snapshot, footwear,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e4/xr/icwgfrkc586c1251a37bb250509836_500x750.jpg)

The absolute most important part of wearing over the knee or knee-high boots is to wear them with confidence. Keep your shoulders back, your head held high, and walk like you own the room! You’ll feel so confident in your fashion choices and people will take notice!
