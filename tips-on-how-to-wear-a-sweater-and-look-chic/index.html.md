# 7 Tips on How to Wear a Sweater and Look Chic ...

How To Wear A Sweater may not be something that you think about often, but there are a range of ways to style this humble garment. With winter just around the corner, look to variations on how to wear a sweater to update your look. By simply adding statement jewellery or embellishments, you can take your run-of-the-mill sweater from drab to fab. Below are just a few pointers on how to wear a sweater this season and still look chic.

## 1. Play It up with Jewellery

The addition of statement making jewellery is a great way to dress up a basic sweater. This season, a chunky collar necklace is your best bet. If you are looking at how to wear a sweater this way, make sure that the neckline on your sweater is fairly high. This way the necklace doesn’t rub against your skin and sits flat against the sweater.

## 2. Add a Sharp Jacket

Dress up your sweater by teaming it with a sharp jacket. For something edgy, pair it with a leather biker-style jacket. For something a bit more refined, team your sweater with a structured blazer. A basic grey sweater will look great with both neutral and coloured jackets.

## 3. Choose the Right Fit

The right style of sweater can make or break an outfit. A fitted sweater is great for creating a dressed-up, streamlined look while slouchy sweaters are ideal as casual weekend attire. If you’re afraid of being ‘swallowed up’ by a slouchy sweater, then go for more fitted styles. Cropped sweaters are also a chic choice this season.

## 4. Add a Collar

Add a detachable collar to your sweater for an on-trend look. Detachable items are a great option because you can switch and change looks depending on trends. Lace collars were a popular choice, but this season look to leather or metallic-tipped collars. Wear a leather Peter Pan-style collar against a grey sweater for a funky and on-trend look.

## 5. Add Embellishments

Transform the humble sweater by adding stylish embellishments. Leather elbow patches are an ironic nod to geek-chic dressing, while studded shoulder embellishments are a directional way to update your look. If you are wondering how to wear a sweater this way, they key is to keep the rest of your outfit fairly fuss-free and let the embellishments to all the talking.

## 6. Tuck It in

Wear your sweater tucked into a high waisted skirt or pair of trousers. This is great for dressing up a sweater and making it appear less ‘slouchy’. This look works best with thinner, more fitted sweaters so keep this in mind. Tuck a sweater into a full circle skirt and team with Mary-Jane heels for some ladylike glamour.

## 7. Choose a V-Shaped Neckline

Sweaters with higher, rounded necklines can sometimes be less flattering to those with bigger busts. A sweater with a V-shaped neckline may be a much more flattering choice. Keep this in mind when thinking about how to wear a sweater this season. A V-neck is also great for showing some skin.

As you can see, there are a range of ways to make the humble sweater look chic and on-trend. If you’ve ever wondered how to wear a sweater and still look chic, then hopefully these tips will help you out. What are your best tips on how to wear a sweater?
