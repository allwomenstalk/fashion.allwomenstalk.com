# 7 Genius Fashion Tips for Women Who Feel Insecure about Their Body ...

Feeling insecure about your body is something that most (if not every) woman will feel at some point during their lives, but that shouldn't stop them from enjoying fashion and taking on new trends! Keep reading for all the latest and greatest fashion tips and comment which ones work for you! ❤️ 👙 👠 🕶 👚

## 1. Opt for Loose Clothing to Hide Insecurities 👗

Loose clothing is a great solution for women who feel insecure about their body! It will allow you to strategically hide any part of your body that you may be insecure about. So for example, hide your hips by wearing shift dresses and non-fitted clothes. This will draw attention away from the area that you're feeling insecure about and help you to feel heaps more confident!

## 2. Follow Your Fashion Comforts 👙

Everyone has those favourite pieces that they love to wear and feel totally comfortable in, so play it safe by sticking to what you love to wear! With fashion, it's important that you feel totally comfortable in your clothes as this instantly gives us more confidence! Do you love to wear jeans with Converses when you go out or are you fan of crop jackets and heels? It's all about following your fashion comforts!

## 3. Take a Fashion Risk Every so Often 👠

Playing it safe with fashion is good for anyone feeling insecure about their body, but it's equally as good to mix it up a little by taking a fashion risk every now and again! This doesn't mean you have to go super crazy when trying something new but start with making little, minor changes to your outfit such as swapping Converses with a pair of cute kitten heels or swap that coat for a stylish crop jacket!

## 4. Shop for Clothes Strategically 👚

Although it's super fun to buy clothes just because we feel like it, it's also great to buy clothes for a certain purpose. So if you feel self-conscious about your legs, for example, keep your eyes peeled for cute maxi skirts and/or dresses, smart black pants or opaque stockings which you can buy knowing that they will serve a purpose and help you to feel less insecure about certain parts of your body!

## 5. Introduce Yourself to Online Shopping 🕶

So you've probably already done this by now, but it's a super great way for insecure women to go about shopping! It's easy to feel insecure or self-conscious about your body when you're shopping in a store and trying clothes on in the changing rooms, right? The solution: online shopping! There's so many amazing online places you can shop now and I guarantee there will be one (or several) out there that you will love! Online shopping saves us the hassle of having to try clothes on and possibly feeling worse about our body and our insecurities.

## 6. Ask Your Friends for Advice 👛

Chances are your friends will have their own insecurities about their bodies as well, so sometimes if you're feeling really stuck it can help to talk to them about it. They may not be insecure about the same things as you when it comes to your body but they might be able to give you some helpful advice and fashion tips on how you can feel less insecure while still looking amazing and feeling confident in the clothes you have!

## 7. Stay True to Yourself 👢

We all have insecurities about our bodies so the thing to remember is that although it might make you feel bad, it's a totally normal thing so you're not alone! Keep in mind that you don't have anything to prove to anybody else, so stay true to yourself and don't feel pressured to try any fashion trends that you're not comfortable with or don't feel confident doing. Stay true to yourself and find non-compromising ways to make it work for you!
