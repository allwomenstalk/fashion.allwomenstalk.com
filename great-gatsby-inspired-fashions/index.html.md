# 8 Great Gatsby Inspired Fashions ...

Great Gatsby inspired fashion is only going to get more popular as the release date to the much hyped movie remake nears. Embrace the roaring twenties with flapper dresses, sequins, pearls, and all things glitz and glamour. The designer runways have been full of 1920s inspired fashion, and these trends are filtering down into chain and high street stores. So, make like Daisy Buchanan in these Great Gatsby inspired fashions.

## 1. Waterfall Ruffle Midi Dress

![Waterfall Ruffle Midi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_waterfall-ruffle-midi-dress_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $120.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/FOjEl2)
When looking at Great Gatsby inspired fashion, you can’t go past a Daisy Buchanan inspired dress. You’ll be a vision this in delicate ruffled midi dress. While it may seem fairly basic and free of embellishments, you can easily style it up with a few well placed statement jewels. Pop on a crystal necklace, slip on some metallic shoes, and finish the look off with an embellished clutch bag.

## 2. Seychelles Petunia Heel

![Seychelles Petunia Heel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_seychelles-petunia-heel_320x299.jpg)

**Price:** $99.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Put your best foot forward in these vintage inspired heels. They have a noir toe, tapered heel, and feature round cutout front. The metallic bands that twist around the shoes give them that extra glamorous edge. These would look great worn with a flapper style dress and metallic accessories.

## 3. Woven Cloche

_ **Price:** $14.80 at forever21.com_
Don’t forget to put on this woven cloche hat for a touch of Great Gatsby inspired fashion for a day out in the garden. It has a basketwoven design with grosgrain ribbon trim and double bow detail. This cloche would look great teamed with a pastel sundress and draped pearl necklaces.

## 4. Joseph Hand Painted Silk Maxi Dress

![Joseph Hand Painted Silk Maxi Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_joseph-hand-painted-silk-maxi-dress_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $397.50 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/C6z1B3)
This floral dress is truly lovely. It has a body skimming cut and is made from 100% silk. It comes in a blush colour and features a hand painted floral print. Wear it with a light coloured cardigan and compact side bag for a romantic summer look.

## 5. Adorned Mesh Headwrap

_ **Price:** $6.80 at forever21.com_
Add a touch of Great Gatsby inspired fashion to your look with this sweet headwrap. It has a mesh band and is adorned with rhinestones and pearlescent beads. Complete the look with finger waved hair, a smokey eye, and a matte lip.

## 6. Miu Miu Lizard Trimmed Sequined Clutch

![Miu Miu Lizard Trimmed Sequined Clutch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_miu-miu-lizard-trimmed-sequined-clutch_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $1810.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/9uyBv1)
Add a bit of razzle dazzle to your look with this sequined clutch. It has a vintage inspired design and features copper sequins and lizard trims. At a price of nearly $2000, however, I’ll purely be using this product as inspiration until I can find a more affordable alternative.

## 7. Teardrop Deco Necklace

_ **Price:** $5.80 at forever21.com_
You don’t have to spend a fortune to incorporate some Great Gatsby inspired fashions into your outfit. This necklace has a rhinestone teardrop pendant with a stylish art deco pattern. This cheap and cheerful necklace would look great worn with a slinky drop waist dress and vintage-look heels.

## 8. Lulu Frost Crystal and Resin Spike Earrings

![Lulu Frost Crystal and Resin Spike Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/8_lulu-frost-crystal-and-resin-spike-earrings_460x690.jpg)

**Price:** $190.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/PHzmBm)
These earrings are an exercise in modern/vintage styling. They feature faceted clear crystals set in a teal resin. Wear them with an embellished cream coloured evening dress for a wonderfully Great Gatsby inspired look.

Whether you’re after a head-to-toe Great Gatsby inspired fashion look or subtle touches, this is a trend worthy of trying out this year. Add luxe touches to your look in the form of sequins, embellishments, and metallics. What do you think of these Great Gatsby inspired fashions?
