# 17 Vivacious \#OOTD 'Grams That Showcase Spring Style ...

There are so very many things to love about spring – flowers and trees in full bloom, blossoming back to life; the weather; the sunshine; the smell of freshly turned earth, green grass, and life itself; and, I'll admit it, the style. After a long winter, it's finally time to break out the skirts, the short sleeves, and the bright colors. Take some stunning style inspo from these outrageously lovely #OOTD looks celebrating the long-awaited return of spring!

## 1. Brighter than the Sun

This gorgeous fashionista is brighter than the springtime sun. That yellow lace is gorgeous, but I gotta tell you, I'm really in love with that precious polka dot cardigan.

## 2. Fun with Floral

Remember, florals are on trend for the season. This print is subtle but it still screams springtime. It's an excellent reminder that you don't have to wear pastels.

## 3. Love That Lace

I want this to be my #OOTD! I die for that dress, I adore that style, and the boots are perfect for it.

## 4. Timeless Simplicity

Of course, just because it's spring, that doesn't mean you have to change your style status quo. A tee shirt and a cool jacket are always on point.

## 5. Or Fierce Simplicity

Then again, you can steal this style and give your timeless staples a colorful springtime update. Splash out in tee shirts and jeans, just embrace vivid hues when you do so!

## 6. Light but Lovely Layers

You can still layer in spring, as well, just make sure you choose lightweight layers that won't weigh you down or make you feel too hot. Everything about this look is on point – I especially love the way those gorgeous goldenrod trousers pair with those fierce, strappy shoes!

## 7. Mix Your Prints

This is gorgeous, absolutely gorgeous! Not only is it another exquisite example of springtime layering, but it's also a perfect guideline for how to mix spring-friendly prints. See, the key is to choose subtle, smaller patterns and to create cohesion. With this #OOTD, the cohesion comes from the way every article of clothing contains that bold, beautiful shade of blue.

## 8. Crochet Shorts

These epitomize the idea of Boho chic, don't you think?

## 9. Stunning Solids

Bright solid colors usher in the springtime vibe, too. That skirt is everything – love the color!

## 10. Bright Pants, Graphic Shirt

Everything about this is perfection. As a matter of fact, I'm pretty sure I need that shirt to be truly happy.

## 11. Pastels Done Right

Oh, those pants, those pearls, those sunglasses! Try wearing a look like this to the office.

## 12. Off the Shoulder

And feel free to bare your shoulders to the sunshine. Seriously, peasant-style tops are all the rage this season.

## 13. Make It Match

Yes to the crop top, yes to the full skirt, and yes to the shoes!

## 14. White-party Ready

I myself cannot wear white because I am a mess, but an all-over white outfit can certainly be the thing for spring.

## 15. Fab in Floral

Told you floral was a big deal this season. This print is big, bright, and fun – perfect for spring! Love that fuzzy cardi, too.

## 16. Watercolor Goddess

Oh, the colors in this dress are stunning! This is the kind of piece that will easily transition to summer.

## 17. Black and White

Because you can't go wrong with black and white.
