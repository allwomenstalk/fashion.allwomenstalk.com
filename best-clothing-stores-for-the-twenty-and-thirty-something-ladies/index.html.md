# 7 Best Clothing Stores for the Twenty and Thirty Something Ladies ...

I would love to share some of the best clothing stores for the twenty and thirty something ladies. When you get into your upper twenties and lower thirties, you usually find that your style changes a bit, if not quite drastically. This list is made up of some of my favorite stores. These are the best clothing stores I have found for ladies our age.

## 1. Maurices

This is probably my favorite on this list of the best clothing stores for women in their twenties and thirties. Maurices is geared more toward a woman in her twenties, but I have found there are many things I am still comfortable wearing in my early thirties. The main things I purchase from this store are tops. I also buy some shoes and accessories from there. I get a lot of compliments on things I purchase from this store.
[maurices.com](http://www.maurices.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. American Eagle

I feel like I have outgrown a lot of clothing items at American Eagle, however, one item I do not feel I am too old to wear is their jeans. Every pair of jeans I wear is from this store. They are cute, fit well and are age appropriate. I see women at least ten to fifteen years older than I am still rocking American Eagle jeans. My favorite cuts are the boyfriend and skinny jeans.
[ae.com](http://www.ae.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Dressbarn

Dressbarn is a store that, in my opinion, is geared more toward women aged at least thirty and upwards. I still feel like a lot of their things are too old for me. However, I love their dresses and most of my dresses do come from this store. I also got a lovely pair of riding boots there for around $40 in the fall. They have beautiful accessories, too.
[dressbarn.com](http://www.dressbarn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Loft

Who doesn’t love Loft? If you have never checked this store out, then you are certainly missing out. I don’t have a lot of clothing from this store, but I do love the things that I do have from there. Loft is pricey so if I shop there, I almost always shop clearance. You can still find a lot of cute stuff on clearance, though.
[loft.com](https://goo.gl/6UsSWq)

## 5. Old Navy

Old Navy is not my favorite store for fashion. But I do love them for basics. When it comes to tees and tanks, there is no better place to go. They have so many different choices and their prices are stellar. I also love that they often offer coupon codes to save even more.
[oldnavy.com](https://oldnavy.gap.com/CookieFailure.do)

## 6. Kohls

Kohls has beautiful clothes and there is so much variety. I always find something when I go to Kohls. They seem to carry their own unique style of clothing. I have bought a lot of different things from Kohls, from basics to fashion statement pieces. I also love that I can shop for the whole family there.
[kohls.com](http://www.kohls.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Dots

Have you ever shopped at Dots? I shop there, but only online because there is not a Dots near my home. Dots is a great little store that is priced very economically, which I certainly appreciate. I scored some gorgeous scarves a couple months ago for under three dollars. And yes, I did say three dollars, believe it or not.
[dots.com](https://goo.gl/tW5Je4)
