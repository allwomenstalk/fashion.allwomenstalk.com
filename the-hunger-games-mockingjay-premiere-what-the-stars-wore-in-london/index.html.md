# The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Premiere \- What the Stars Wore in London ...

Mockingjay's world premiere took place in London this week! The entire cast was there to celebrate and the red carpet fashion was, as always, flawless. Check out what the cast wore at the London premiere:

## 1. Jennifer Lawrence

![Jennifer Lawrence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/ey/dpq2ymzy_600x969.jpg)

The Hunger Games' leading lady stunned in a high-low white Dior gown. The black floral embroidery is such a nice touch!

## 2. ELIZABETH BANKS

![ELIZABETH BANKS](https://img.allw.mn/content/e7/zv/urel7hid_600x800.gif)

Elizabeth wore a watercolor-print Elie Saab and looked nothing short of exceptional!

## 3. Liam Hemsworth

![Liam Hemsworth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/be/sq0ctczq_600x969.jpg)

The hunky actor kept it classic in a black tux.

## 4. Josh Hutcherson

![Josh Hutcherson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j6/he/yxssf4gz_600x969.jpg)

Josh opted for a navy Ralph Lauren Black Label suit. He looks so handsome!

## 5. Julianne Moore

![Julianne Moore](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/oa/atclq6ds_600x969.jpg)

Julianne wore a Balenciaga design.

## 6. NATALIE DORMER

![NATALIE DORMER](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/p0/vi7e7ydw_600x969.jpg)

Natalie's Nicholas Oakwell gown is the perfect balance between girly and edgy.

## 7. Jena Malone

![Jena Malone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/ff/widpsupj_600x969.jpg)

Jena wore a white-and-yellow Emilia Wickstead gown at the London premiere.

## 8. Lorde

![Lorde](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/ol/sshkdph1_600x969.jpg)

Lorde - who curated the movie's soundtrack - wore black parachute pants, white blouse, a thin ribbon bow-tie, and a long blazer!

## 9. Sam Claflin

![Sam Claflin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/ed/ls9h4096_386x612.jpg)

Sam mix-and-matched colors and it looked great!

These photos have only made me MORE excited for the movie's release. Who do you think had the best look?
