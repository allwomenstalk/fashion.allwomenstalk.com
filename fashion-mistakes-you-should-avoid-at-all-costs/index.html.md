# 7 Fashion Mistakes You Should Avoid at All Costs ...

Whether you’re going to a party or the supermarket, there are fashion mistakes you should avoid. You always want to look fabulous, dont you? In order to look your best, you need to keep a few things in mind. Here are some fashion mistakes you should avoid making at all costs.

## 1. Falling Fashion

![Falling Fashion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24024928_8151_600x900.jpg)

Before you leave the house, jump up and down a few times. Make sure that your outfit remains in place. The worst thing that can happen is your outfit falling out of place. You don’t want to reveal more than you intend to. One of the fashion mistakes you should avoid is wearing something too loose or tight. Make sure that your clothing fits properly.

## 2. Clashing Colors

![Clashing Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24025000_3607_567x850.jpg)

Wearing random colors can look fabulous, as long as they go together nicely. You want to avoid wearing different colors or patterns that don’t make sense together. You want others to admire your outfits, not to be confused by them. Your clothes should be alluring, not distracting. When you step in front of a mirror, make sure that your color scheme is pleasing to the eye.

## 3. Wrong Footwear

![Wrong Footwear](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24025018_9741_589x837.jpg)

Don’t wear heels when you’re planning on walking around the city all day. You might look sexy, but you’ll feel horrible. You need to make sure that your shoes match the activity that you’re performing. Fashion is important, but comfort is also crucial. If you’re in pain from your shoes, you’re not going to look your greatest.

## 4. Avoiding Accessories

![Avoiding Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24025113_0066_600x900.jpg)

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so don’t forget to accessorize with jewelry, hats, and belts. It can turn your outfit from bland to glorious in seconds. Why wouldn’t you want to enhance your wardrobe? With accessories, you can also wear the same outfit multiple times and look like you’re switching it up. There’s no reason to leave the house without accessories.

## 5. Same Situation

![Same Situation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24025148_2192_600x824.jpg)

Take a quick look at your wardrobe. Is it mostly composed of the same color, or the same fabric? Try to buy a variety of different things. Even if black is your favorite color, it doesn’t mean that you have to wear it all of the time. You can branch out and look just as great as you always do. Plus, people will notice that you’re changing things up, and compliments will follow.

## 6. Wrong for Weather

The gorgeous dress that you wore in summer doesn’t have to be thrown into a drawer during winter. However, you can’t wear it in the same exact way you did two seasons ago. Pair it with a cute pair of boots and leggings. If you have a fashionable jacket or belt to match, throw that on as well. As long as your outfit looks and feels appropriate for the current weather, you’re good to go.

## 7. Careful Concealing

![Careful Concealing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/24025307_6141_600x764.jpg)

Avoid wearing a bright bra with a white shirt if you want it to be concealed. Just because it is underneath your clothing doesn’t mean that it’s hidden. You have to be careful with the underwear you choose to wear. Every outfit requires something different. Take your outer clothing into consideration before you put on your undergarments.

Everyone approaches fashion in a different way. However, you should keep a few key things in mind when you’re putting your outfits together. What’s your biggest fashion rule?
