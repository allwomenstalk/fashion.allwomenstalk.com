# 8 Inspiring Street Fashion Looks ...

**Street Fashion** has long been a source of wardrobe inspiration. While runway looks are still desirable, **street fashion** is more appealing as it comes across as more accessible. If your wardrobe is in need of some new season inspiration, why not sit down at the computer and take a few tips from popular street style sites? There are a range of websites that cater to documenting **street fashion** around the world. The following are just a few **street fashion** looks sourced from the popular image inspiration website Pinterest.com.

## 1. The 70s Silhouette

![The 70s Silhouette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qi/ui/q3wde7pv54c932847bac8597079572_300x429.jpg)

Flared jeans, a floaty blouse, and a floppy felt hat – it’s a simple combination that works wonders, especially in terms of street fashion. The high waisted jeans have a lengthening effect, and the floaty blouse gives off a free-spirited vibe. The maroon felt hat and long wavy hair complete the 70s-inspired look.

## 2. Casual Colour

This colourful dress is a show stopper. I like how it’s been anchored by neutral accessories, such as the sandals and belt. The small satchel is also the perfect size for carrying the essentials – a bigger bag would have weighed down the ‘floaty’ aspect of this look.

## 3. Dressed down Gown

![Dressed down Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ws/at/s92fwq7854c93286a8a8b757761119_300x450.jpg)

Pleated dresses and skirts have the potential to appear quite dressy. Here, the pleated maxi has been given a casual twist that works well with street fashion. The basic grey sweater is an unexpected choice, but it creates a great contrast. The collar necklace is an on-trend choice of accessory, and the red lipstick and high bun complete the fresh-faced look.

## 4. The Printed Dress

![The Printed Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/mz/xvu31pwy54c93287c68d6701815643_300x451.jpg)

You can’t go past the statement-making printed dress. This dress features a bold animal print in a neutral colourway. The addition of the cream belt breaks up the print quite nicely. The black heeled sandals and small clutch complement the dress, without detracting focus from the print.

## 5. The Belted Coat

![The Belted Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/lm/gu3t4tpa54c93288d44cf105399143_300x450.jpg)

Street fashion is great for finding inspiring everyday looks. The belted coat is an unexpected detail, but one that works quite well here. The yellow dress is a stylish choice and works well with the tan coat. The black stockings and shoes created a streamlined look and, combined with the bag, really anchor this look.

## 6. Ladylike Dressing

![Ladylike Dressing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/ry/c6zn2qgj54c93289d9934196025804_300x408.jpg)

Street fashion doesn’t always have to be about casual dressing. This woman works an elegant, ladylike look in a pink lace dress. The white underlay is a fresh choice. The pink shoes and delicate clutch bag complete the look. I also like how she’s accessories with statement-making gold earrings.

## 7. Colour Blocking

![Colour Blocking](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ae/ar/wwhp138e54c9328ae0c85287573381_300x450.jpg)

Colour blocking is a trend that’s still sticking around. It’s a look that can be a bit hit and miss, so while you’re still experimenting you can always take inspiration from street style. This example of colour blocking is bound to put a smile on your face. The royal blue pants are an on-trend choice, and the pink blazer and mint sweater complement each other perfectly. The buttoned-up collar peeking out the top is also a nice touch.

## 8. Layered Textures

![Layered Textures](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ww/yv/ej8vx9o254c9328bebf62800912491_300x451.jpg)

This look is great for picking up some tips when it comes to layering textures. The textured metallic jacket looks so chic paired with the cable knit sweater and sheer skirt. The neutral shoes keep the attention on the top half of the body. Keeping all the textures in the same colour scheme is also a great way to play down the contrasting textures.

Runways looks set the trends, but it’s **street fashion** that really shows how attainable these trends are. **Street fashion** websites are a great source of inspiration, especially if you’re stuck in a wardrobe rut. Which of these **street fashion** looks do you like best?
