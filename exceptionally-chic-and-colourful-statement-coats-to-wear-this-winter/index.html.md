# 7 Exceptionally Chic and Colourful Statement Coats to Wear This Winter ...

Colorful Coats are an instant outfit updater this winter. Whether you prefer your colours acid bright or a little more subdued, there are colorful coats to suit all tastes. Pair a colored coat with a neutral outfit for a statement making look this season. You’ll also find that adding colorful coats to your winter outfits is a helpful way of brightening up the day. Check out a few chic and colorful coats below.

## 1. Rebecca Taylor Wool-Blend Coat

![Rebecca Taylor Wool-Blend Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/br/n47fiz8a54c934ebca010129764268_250x375.jpg)

**Price:** $495.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Urb4T3)
This bright blue coat is a timelessly elegant take on the colorful coats trend. This cobalt wool-blend coat has puff shoulders, front flap pockets, and pewter button fastenings. It hits mid thigh and is also fully lined. For a contemporary look, wear it with patterned pants and patent heels.

## 2. Topshop Short Green Coat

![Topshop Short Green Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m7/jr/iov2q9ye54c934ecb225e351887062_250x375.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/lHjTaV)
This green coat has a bit of a retro feel to it. It has a double breasted design, with three-quarter sleeves and a grosgrain tie at the neck. The bottle green color is bound to help you stand out in a crowd. Work a vintage-inspired look and wear this coat with a monochrome shift dress, thick tights, and Mary-Jane style shoes.

## 3. Issa Flared Wool Princess Coat

![Issa Flared Wool Princess Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kz/j1/pbics1wh54c934ed80a77772929420_250x375.jpg)

**Price:** $850.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/hC0jsC)
For truly statement-making color, you can’t go past a bright red hue. This wonderful red coat has a princess design with a flattering flared silhouette. It has a double breasted design with concealed fastenings, notched lapels, and front flap pockets. A red coat would look great paired with hounds tooth patterns this winter.

## 4. Topshop Metal Clasp Hooded Coat

![Topshop Metal Clasp Hooded Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/yd/mt08d2g254c934ee572e1039267813_250x347.jpg)

**Price:** $170.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/Yyn3Pv)
For colorful coats with a more casual feel, look to those with duffel and hooded designs. This thigh-length pink coats features contrast piping and metal clasp fastenings to the front. It also has a detachable hood. This coat would look great teamed with a casual outfit of blue jeans and flat knee-length boots.

## 5. Weekend by MaxMara Herringbone Coat

![Weekend by MaxMara Herringbone Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/w5/mzrrr40m54c934ef4f6de075759263_250x376.jpg)

**Price:** $512.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This orange herringbone coat would make a colorful addition to any winter wardrobe. It has a double breasted design with single button fastening and comes with a detachable tie at the waist. It also has buttoned epaulets and front patch pockets. Work a high-fashion look and wear this coat with a pair of wide-legged trousers and pointed shoes.

## 6. Dorothy Perkins Red Stormflap Slimline Coat

![Dorothy Perkins Red Stormflap Slimline Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kk/dj/ezq3gjxh54c934f02a91d960861268_250x336.jpg)

**Price:** $107.00 at [dorothyperkins.com](http://www.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This red slimline coat has a chic, understated design. It has a concealed placket, front pockets, and stormflaps. This coat has a slight flared design and hits mid-thigh. Wear it with skinny jeans and animal-print boots for a fun and fashionable take on winter dressing.

## 7. Diane Von Furstenberg Milford Coat

![Diane Von Furstenberg Milford Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/qn/kvp3fvjo54c934f0de522659468353_250x227.jpg)

**Price:** $646.00 at [matchesfashion.com](http://www.matchesfashion.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You can also make a statement this winter in shorter-length colorful coats. This turquoise coat has a small round neck, three-quarter length sleeves, and a box-cut design. It also has a concealed front fastening and an elasticated hem. Wear it with white and caramel coloured dresses and skirts for a stylish contrast.

These are just a few examples of colorful coats to get you inspired for winter. Colorful coats are great for making a statement, and the right coat can last you year in and year out. Which of these colorful coats do you like best?
