# 7 Cute Holiday Dresses ...

One of the best things about the holiday season is all of the cute holiday dresses you can wear! From lace to sparkles, the options for cute holiday dresses are endless. So amidst the tons of dresses you can try, here are a few of my favorites for this holiday season. And don’t forget to accessorize.

## 1. ASOS Lace Skater Dress

![ASOS Lace Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/1_asos-lace-skater-dress_600x766.jpg)

This dress, which is $87.95 at ASOS, has all of my qualifications for cute holiday dresses. Cute collar, some lace, and of course a little bit of sparkle. This is the perfect dress to wear if you are feeling fun and flirty. Pair it with some gold accessories and a pair of nude heels. And don’t forget to put your hair in an updo to show off that rockin’ collar.

## 2. MINKPINK Outrageous Fortune Dress

![MINKPINK Outrageous Fortune Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/2_minkpink-outrageous-fortune-dress_600x900.jpg)

Embrace that ever so hot baroque trend this season with this adorable dress from Urban Outfitters. Put an edgy spin on the trend with a zipper going down the front of the dress and a cutout in the back. Add to the edginess with a pair of black studded heels and a thick black leather bracelet. This is a great dress to bring out your inner badass.

## 3. Blazing Beauty Dress

![Blazing Beauty Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/3_blazing-beauty-dress_350x500.jpg)

Look red hot in the snow with this long sleeve lace getup, available at ModCloth for $177.99. It is perfect to stand out at any holiday party without feeling like you are going overboard. This dress would be perfect with big vintage curls and some understated accessories.

## 4. Ribbed Peplum Pencil Dress

![Ribbed Peplum Pencil Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/4_ribbed-peplum-pencil-dress_600x900.jpg)

Who says you can’t wear white in winter? Embrace the ever so popular peplum trend in this white peplum cocktail dress from TopShop. This cute holiday dress is the perfect pick for any girl who loves to express herself through accessories and shoes. Because it is such a simple dress, feel free to dress it up anyway you want with some crazy accessories.

## 5. Stardust Sequin Dress

![Stardust Sequin Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/5_stardust-sequin-dress_600x900.jpg)

If you want more adventurous cute holiday dresses, try shining in this hot sparkly party dress from NastyGal. It is just what you need to feel flirty at any holiday party. Pair it with some gold accessories and voluminous hair to really add to your shine. And don’t forget to add in some gold sky-high heels.

## 6. Maalika Long Sleeve Velvet Skater Dress

![Maalika Long Sleeve Velvet Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/6_maalika-long-sleeve-velvet-skater-dress_355x520.jpg)

What is so great about this burgundy velvet skater dress is that you can go glam or edgy with it. Plus, since Missguided has it for just $30.96, it's affordable and versatile. Dress it up with some sparkly bling accessories and over the top hair. Or you can add a military jacket and studded boots for a more causal, laid back look. No matter what, you will look smoking hot this holiday season.

## 7. Brocade Bow Tube Dress

![Brocade Bow Tube Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/12/7_brocade-bow-tube-dress_328x400.jpg)

This may be my obsession with bows, but I love this gold bow dress from Forever 21. Go for a girly feel and style your hair in a cute updo. Pair that with dainty jewelry and patterned black tights and black pumps. You will be sure to turn heads in this feminine frock.
