# 17 Sexy Punk Rock Styles to Wear to Your Prom ...

I may be long past my prom days, but that doesn't mean that I don't still appreciate all the fun and excitement of prom season – mainly because the dresses are so fierce compared to what they were in the way-back days of the long ago time. I wore an unfortunate, offensively purple dresses to my prom. It was all sparkled overlay. I am not proud. If I could do it all over again, I'd go for a grungy punk princess prom look – like one of these epic ensembles.

## 1. Combat Boots and Tulle

![Combat Boots and Tulle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/kv/ohtr77ha56fdd755e1a5a894386293_600x600.jpg)

Is there a better combination? Honestly, I think combat boots and tulle are ideal for day-to-day wear. Love this look!

## 2. Lace and Fishnets

![Lace and Fishnets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/kk/an8i3w8356fdd756b6c25426821417_500x754.jpg)

This is a show-stopper. Who's thinking about wearing something like this to prom? 'cause you should definitely do it.

## 3. The Corset Dress

![The Corset Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/pw/y1km58qt56fdd75726e2e812619758_576x1024.jpg)

Comfortable? No. Flattering? Universally.

## 4. The Darkest Fairy

![The Darkest Fairy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/ec/kw44ln2g56fdd7567a1b0227023150_600x900.jpg)

I have fallen in love with a dress. Actually, the whole look is gorgeous, the makeup and hair complement the dress perfectly, but I am all about the dress.

## 5. Denim and Tulle

![Denim and Tulle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/v4/l9700nzj56fdd75696d98240703914_468x805.jpg)

## 6. Mad for Plaid

![Mad for Plaid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pu/kp/g4yk9h2h5700364678e8e938345021_500x1194.jpg)

## 7. A Little Victorian

![A Little Victorian](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/oa/b7uzjcej56fdd757f30b6469168414_600x572.jpg)

The dress, the sleeves, the shoes, the knee socks! Love, love, love it!

## 8. Gothic Punk Rock

![Gothic Punk Rock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/ix/cxis5r3v56fdd7579bb36874386726_450x597.jpg)

This dress is actually a work of art. Pure props to the designer. The bodice, the skirt, the accents – all flawless.

## 9. Those Tights

![Those Tights](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/ru/qznjedjw56fdd757d7ebe677823838_419x626.jpg)

## 10. Ruffles on Ruffles on Ruffles

![Ruffles on Ruffles on Ruffles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ru/ug/hvf5wlgt56fdd757e8d99077216335_480x720.jpg)

Admittedly, this is more goth than punk, but who cares? She is serving and we can all have a slice.

## 11. Blazer and Tulle

![Blazer and Tulle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/n1/x5zlj0n756fdd7584094b187737465_574x853.jpg)

Clearly, pairing tulle with something unexpected is a winning idea here. This reminds me of early '90s Courtney Love.

## 12. Simple but Not Plain

![Simple but Not Plain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/lx/svzz2jgf56fdd75837881973210385_456x683.jpg)

This is chic, sleek, and sophisticated, but although it's just a little black dress, there's nothing plain about it. Absolutely nothing.

## 13. Go for Some Glitter

![Go for Some Glitter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/qu/rb5qwwk856fdd75aec9aa756057530_600x600.jpg)

Not a lot, you don't want to overwhelm your look with lots of glitter and sequins, but something like this with the right jacket and boots? That could work.

## 15. Striped Perfection

## 16. Sassy Ombre
