# 7 Luxe Leather Pieces for Fall ...

This season, luxe leather pieces for fall go beyond traditional coats to cover every inch of your new fall wardrobe in this supple and sexy material. From sultry dresses to streamlined pants, wearing luxe leather pieces for fall from head to toe is the way to go. Structured and tailored pieces with clean lines and minimal designs make leather contemporary chic and effortlessly cool for the modern lady this season.

## 1. Peplum Leather Top

![Peplum Leather Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/1_peplum-leather-top_600x644.jpg)

Peplums are in full swing and are the silhouette of the season with shapes popping up in all designs from skirts and dresses to jackets and tops for an elegant and feminine finish. This sophisticated trend is taken up a few notches when created in luxe leather, which adds an edge to all looks. When worn in a standout colour like a red-hot hue, peplums are a super sexy and sultry way to wear leather that releases every fashionista’s inner vixen.

## 2. Leather Moto Jacket

![Leather Moto Jacket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/2_leather-moto-jacket_339x386.jpg)

A cool moto jacket is an edgy cool coat that should be a part of everyone’s wardrobe for fall. A traditional moto jacket with lots of zippers, buckles, studs and snaps are a super edgy way to wear this luxe leather piece. For an added cool touch opt for pieces that differentiate from the standard moto jacket like pieces with fur trim or shearling that you can cozy up to for fall.

## 3. Leather Dress

![Leather Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/3_leather-dress_230x345.jpg)

Leather is always a seductive finish, but a sleek and sexy form fitted dress is a super tempting way to wear leather for fall. Opt for a minimalistic approach when picking out the perfect leather dress as to show off your figure and curves in a tempting way. Pair a sleek leather dress with a ladylike top handle bag and pointed toe pumps for a nod to feminine dressing that speaks volumes without being loud.

## 4. Pleated Leather Skirt

![Pleated Leather Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/4_pleated-leather-skirt_300x300.jpg)

Leather gets a fun and flirty update when created in a pleated skirt. When created in luxe leather, ultra-feminine pleated skirts are toughened up for a cool edge that’s still soft and demure. Loose fitting pleats skim the body for a universally flattering shape that adds movement and volume without bulk. Pair a pleated leather skirt with tall boots or platform pumps depending on if you want to make your look a little edgier or ladylike.

## 5. Oversized Leather Coat

![Oversized Leather Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/5_oversized-leather-coat_600x900.jpg)

Oversized coats are the shape of the season when it comes to cool outerwear for fall. An oversized coat’s exaggerated shape is relaxed and comfortable allowing you to bundle up in this playful layering piece. Make sure to tailor the rest of your look when wearing an oversized coat, as you don’t want to look bulky or add extra volume. An oversized coat creates impact and drama so keep the rest of your silhouette form fitted and figure flattering in order to show off your shape.

## 6. Leather Shorts

![Leather Shorts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/6_leather-shorts_600x579.jpg)

Shorts are the perfect layering item for fall. When done in an uber-luxe material like leather, shorts standout as a fashionable option rather than just a beach bum cover up. For the cooler days of fall, pair your leather shorts with printed and patterned or coloured tights with tall boots to keep covered and cozy while remaining just as chic.

## 7. Leather Pants

![Leather Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/10/7_leather-pants_230x345.jpg)

Classic leather pants are a fail-proof piece for fall. A pair of skinny leather pants is a chic rock and roll way to incorporate this luxe material into your wardrobe. Leather pants can be easily datable so beware of the finishes and details you choose when selecting your favorite pair. For a cool way to wear leather pants opt for a cropped pair you can cuff for a relaxed and comfortable fit. Rock your leather pants with pointed toe pumps or sneakers for a look that’s effortlessly versatile and workable into your everyday life.

Luxe leather pieces for fall offer a touch of effortless ease and sleek sophistication to all looks and styles this season. The flawless finish of a luxe leather piece is tempting and totally irresistible so be prepared to sport this sexy and seductive material all season long. What’s your go-to leather piece for fall? Which of these luxe leather pieces are you lusting over?
