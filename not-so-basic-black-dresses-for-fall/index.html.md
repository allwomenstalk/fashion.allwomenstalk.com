# 9 Not\-so\-Basic Black Dresses for Fall ...

Black dresses are pretty much a wardrobe staple. We’ve all been told how the little black dress (LBD) is a must-have for all seasons. But what if you want your black dress to have a bit more of a ‘wow’ factor to it? Sure, it might not be the most versatile, but it’ll be much more memorable than your usual plain black dress. If the idea of the not-so-basic black dress has taken your fancy, be sure to check out the following stylish black dresses.

## 1. Dot a Hold on Me Dress

![Dot a Hold on Me Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/1_dot-a-hold-on-me-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $97.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cute as a button dress would be a wonderful addition to your fall wardrobe. Polka dots, bold collars, sheer sleeves – it hits all the right notes for this season! Team this black dress with T-bar shoes, a framed handbag, and pearl earrings for a vintage inspired look.

## 2. Bold Geo Print Shift Dress

![Bold Geo Print Shift Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/2_bold-geo-print-shift-dress_328x400.jpg)

**Price:** $24.80 at [forever21.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.forever21.com/)
Bold, geometric prints are everywhere these days. While some can be quite busy, especially in colour, a black printed dress is a smart way to pull off the trend. This stylish dress comes in a relaxed shift cut and would look great teamed with a faded denim jacket and lace up shoes for a casual daytime look.

## 3. 50s Crochet Skater Dress

![50s Crochet Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/3_50s-crochet-skater-dress_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $130.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/pgyFiQ)
One tip for choosing not-so-basic black dresses is to keep an eye out for interesting fabrics and textures. This black dress is made from eye catching black crochet lace with a contrasting nude lining. Style it up at night by teaming it with some peep toe heels, a cute box clutch, and chandelier drop earrings.

## 4. Black Vintage Lace Dress

![Black Vintage Lace Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/4_black-vintage-lace-dress_600x815.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re looking for a black dress to wear for special occasions, take a look at this little beauty. It has a classic sweetheart necklace and features an embroidered mesh overlay. It also has daring a deep V-shaped sheer back. When the weather gets cooler, slip on sheer stockings underneath and layer with a cute capelet for traveling between destinations.

## 5. Black/Ivory Peplum Dress

![Black/Ivory Peplum Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/5_black-ivory-peplum-dress_600x815.jpg)

**Price:** $69.00 at [us.dorothyperkins.com](http://us.dorothyperkins.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Who knew that a few peplum details and some strategically placed stripes could make the basic black dress that little bit more special? This fitted bandeau dress features a cool twist in the form of chevron striped panels down the centre. Team it with metallic accessories, pumps, and a cropped blazer for a chic night time look.

## 6. Traveling Cupcake Truck Dress

![Traveling Cupcake Truck Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/6_traveling-cupcake-truck-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $59.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Embrace all things retro in this glam polka dot dress. It has a bustier bodice with thick halterneck straps and a full skirt. Channel your inner vintage vixen and team this dress with patent heels, a cropped cardigan, red lippy, and a killer quiff hairstyle.

## 7. Tiger Face Skater Dress

![Tiger Face Skater Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/7_tiger-face-skater-dress_600x899.jpg)

**Price:** $52.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/r08uuL)
Feeling the call of the wild? Answer it in this daring tiger print dress! Perfect for those who like their outfits on the edgier side, this dress would look great worn with a leather-look biker jacket and ankle boots

## 8. Sequin Strappy Prom Dress

![Sequin Strappy Prom Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/08/8_sequin-strappy-prom-dress_600x900.jpg)

**Price:** $160.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/K3JiR4)
If you’re a fan of glitz and glamour, then have I got the dress for you! This prom-style dress is covered in sequins and would be the perfect party dress. Wear it with cute heels and a sleek blazer, but don’t worry too much about accessories since the dress is all the dazzle your outfit will need.

## 9. Hound Sleuth Dress

_ **Price:** $79.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This isn’t your typical houndstooth print! Look closely and you’ll notice that this black dress features an all-over dachshund dog print. Team it with ballet flats and a coloured cardigan for an easy everyday look.

As far as black dresses go, these are anything but plain. Whether you prefer prints or sequins, there are plenty of ways to go when it comes to choosing a not-so-basic black dress. Which of these dresses do you like best?
