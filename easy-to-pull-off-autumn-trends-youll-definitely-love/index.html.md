# 7 Easy to Pull off Autumn Trends You'll Definitely Love ...

Autumn trends this year range from uber posh to goth, promising an interesting season for all, regardless of what their personal style is leaning towards the most. As great as they all are, I can’t help but favor a few, all because they’re so easy to emulate and require little to no wardrobe updates. Let’s make the upcoming rainy days as stylish as possible, shall we? Check my favorite autumn trends out, choose a favorite fall trend of your own and walk into a new season feeling like a diva:

## 1. Total Look

![Total Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02214029_2680_600x900.jpg)

If mixing and matching isn’t your strong side, you’ll definitely enjoy at least one of this year’s autumn trends! Yup, you’ve guessed it – total look is what I have in mind. Isn’t this a perfect opportunity to dress yourself head to toe in your favorite color or pattern? One thing is for sure, being stylish this fall isn’t only easy but budget-friendly to boot and it will definitely make dressing up in the morning a tad bit easier.

## 2. Plaid

![Plaid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02214100_4974_400x600.jpg)

Not being able to time-travel and snag yourself a hunky Highlander doesn’t mean you don’t get to act on your favorite romantic fantasies. Start digging for that plaid skirt I’m sure you have - tartans have been forgotten for quite some time, which is why you ought to grab them while they’re hot and enjoy the moment! What a way to feel like a romance novel heroine without having to give up all those little 21st century perks that make life sweeter (and a good deal safer, not to mention considerably longer)!

## 3. Cool Hat

![Cool Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02214148_0389_427x640.jpg)

Pulling a plug on frizz, keeping warm and dealing with bad hair days is a package deal this fall, all thanks to this next, must-do autumn trend. All of those hats you fashionistas have worked (and spent) hard on obtaining last year will come in rather handy now, allowing you to rock the trend straight off the runway and enjoy all of those aforementioned perks as soon as…well, now!

## 4. Houndstooth and Tweed

![Houndstooth and Tweed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02220110_8405_400x600.jpg)

These gorgeous classic prints are all the rage this season, and may I be so free to point out that it was about darn time! I’m pretty sure all of you fans of those super feminine vintage pieces would jump at a chance to wear them more often, and let’s not forget all of those chic, contemporary designs that will surely convince even the youngest of fashionistas to give a refined Chanel-esque look a shot.

## 5. Peplum

![Peplum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02220154_2673_400x600.jpg)

Getting to recycle various items of clothing and still look like you’ve just stepped off the runway is always a good bargain! And since designers still don’t have the heart to move peplum out of the picture, neither should you. Find a brand new way to mix and match your favorite pieces, put a gorgeous last year’s look up for a much-needed re-run or consider adding a few new accessories. Chic, feminine silhouettes are obviously here to stay and you certainly won’t hear me complaining about it any time soon!

## 6. Whimsical Pieces

![Whimsical Pieces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02220515_9347_388x587.jpg)

Hear ye, hear ye – the ban on wearing Disney prints after a certain age has been lifted! And that’s not nearly the half of it! Every single cute or interesting print you might find is A-ok, be it hearts, flowers or even Smurfs. Let your inner child, girly girl or geeky chic out to play because you don’t only get to flaunt your Daffy Duck obsession, you can also mix and match so that your fancier pieces turn just a bit more whimsical.

## 7. Big Fluffy Sweaters

![Big Fluffy Sweaters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/02220456_4619_400x600.jpg)

We might be up for a long, cold winter this year which, in turn, makes this fashion trend a necessity. A nice one, to be sure, but a necessity nevertheless. Get those fluffy, dreamy, incredibly warm mohair sweaters out and ready therefore and, should you realize you have none, you’re more than welcome to go digging through your mom’s closet. Oversized is a word to describe a lot of trends this year, so why should anyone pass on a perfect opportunity to snuggle into something warm, comfy and cloud-soft and call it fashion?

Which of these cool fall trends do you find yourself curiously eager to try on for size?
