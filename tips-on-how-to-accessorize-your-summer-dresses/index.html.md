# 7 Tips on How to Accessorize Your Summer Dresses ...

With days of fun under the sun ahead, you’ll need to know how to accessorize a summer dress. Charming summer dresses are a must-have for almost every girl’s wardrobe. But when you’re practically living in them, you need to find creative ways to spruce things up. These 7 tips will take your plain frock from drab to fab!

## 1. Go for the Gold

![Go for the Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/1_go-for-the-gold_600x400.jpg)

There’s just something about gold baubles that make summer dresses shine. When the sunlight strikes, you’ll shimmer in these jewels –and they work so well with a tan! So, save the silver for winter and bask in your golden glow.

## 2. Invest in Shoes

![Invest in Shoes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/2_invest-in-shoes_600x376.jpg)

While you can usually buy trendy dresses on the cheap, you shouldn’t skimp when it comes to the most important accessory in your closet: shoes! A standout pair is key when it comes to accessorizing a summer dress. Because if your feet are aching in cheap, worn-out kicks, you won’t look or feel your best.

## 3. Polish up

![Polish up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/3_polish-up_600x399.jpg)

Nail polish is an easy and super affordable way to spice up a summer dress. Maybe it’s not a jewel or pretty pair of shoes, but it’s just as important to your overall look. When I’m heading out in an all-white getup, a neon mani/pedi usually makes all the difference. The devil’s in the details, ladies!

## 4. Belt It

![Belt It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/4_belt-it_600x400.jpg)

I can’t get enough of billowing tunics and maxi dresses, but without a good belt, curves can get lost under all that fabric. When I’m going nautical, I tend to rummage through my drawers for a braided belt. But if I’m doing the dressy look, I head straight for belts in timeless animal prints or classic neutral.

## 5. Wear a Haute Hat

![Wear a Haute Hat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/5_wear-a-haute-hat_600x375.jpg)

This is an easy, breezy way to accessorize your summer dresses. A straw floppy hat is stylish and stops the sun from damaging your lovely locks. I especially love to don a cute hat on the beach – it’s the perfect way to help me look put together even when I’m walking around with sand between my toes.

## 6. Don’t Forget Your Sunnies

![Don’t Forget Your Sunnies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/6_dont-forget-your-sunnies_593x395.jpg)

If you’re wondering how to accessorize your summer dress, simply open your pretty peepers. Sunglasses will protect your eyes from the sun’s harmful rays and give last year’s dress a cool update. If you’re splurging on a new pair, always be sure to try them on before you take them home. I know from experience (sadly) that sunglasses that look amazing on a model can look vastly different on your own face.

## 7. Top It off with a Tote

![Top It off with a Tote](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/06/7_top-it-off-with-a-tote_600x376.jpg)

If you’re wondering how to accessorize your summer dress AND carry around tubes of lip gloss, sunscreen, and half the drugstore, a tote is your answer. Tote bags are perfect for a casual day at the office, an afternoon with the girls, or even a laid back date. I can usually be spotted toting around a brightly hued canvas bag for an effortless outfit addition.

With these tips, you’ll know how to accessorize just about any summer dress. Which ones are you planning to try? What are some of your own tips on accessorizing a flowing summer frock?
