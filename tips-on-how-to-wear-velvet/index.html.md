# 8 Tips on How to Wear Velvet ...

How To Wear Velvet – it can be a bit of a tricky situation. With the velvet trend still going strong this season, now’s the time to incorporate velvet touches to your outfits. There is a range of ways to wear the trend, from clothing through to accessories. If you’re unsure of how to wear velvet, you might want to start off with subtle accessories rather than dramatic velvet clothing. Below are just a few tips on how to wear velvet.

## 1. Velvet Dresses

If you are unsure of how to wear velvet, a dress is one of the easiest ways to wear the trend. Bodycon dresses and skater style dresses are popular designs. The most popular colours for velvet dresses are black, maroon, and midnight blue. Offset the textured nature of the velvet dress by teaming it with sleek patent accessories.

## 2. Velvet Leggings

Velvet leggings are a popular choice. Their thickness and texture makes them ideal for the cooler months. Work a rock chic look by pairing them with an oversized shirt, leather jacket, and lace-up boots.

## 3. Velvet Jewellery

Add subtle touches of velvet to your look with some velvet jewellery. A leather cuff with velvet bow embellishments, velvet covered earrings, or velvet brooches are all ways you can wear the trend.

## 4. Velvet Collars

When it comes to how to wear velvet, collar attachments are a great way to work the trend. The detachable nature of a collar also means you can incorporate the trend into your outfit without making a commitment. Make a chic statement and wear a velvet collar with a silk blouse, high waisted jeans, and almond toe pumps.

## 5. Velvet Hair Accessories

Headbands and hair clips are a quirky way to incorporate velvet into your outfit. Velvet bow headbands are a popular choice but if you’re after something a bit more edgy then try accessorising with a studded velvet headband. Velvet hair clips are also an easy way to wear the trend – simply clip your hair back with one and go!

## 6. Velvet Shoes

Velvet shoes may seem like an odd choice, but it seems like they’re going to be an on-trend option this season. Chain store Topshop has introduced a range of velvet shoes ranging from ballet flats to ankle boots. If you want to know how to wear velvet shoes, simply choose a style that best suits you.

## 7. Velvet Jackets

If you’re going to wear a velvet jacket, make sure it is well cut and choose a design that is fuss-free. Anything overly fussy or embellished may come across as a bit dowdy. A sleek black velvet blazer could easily be incorporated into a range of outfits.

## 8. Velvet Embellishments

Add velvet touches to your outfit in subtle forms. Think velvet covered buttons or velvet trims. If you’re feeling crafty, you could try DIY-ing some velvet trims into your outfit. A velvet scarf tied on to your handbag is another way you could wear the trend.

Hopefully these tips on how to wear velvet have helped you out. Velvet can add drama to an outfit so depending on how much of a statement you want to make, you can wear a little or a lot. Do you have any tips on how to wear velvet?
