# 8 Winter White Fashions to Wear This Season ...

Challenge summer’s “no white after Labor Day rule” with these winter white fashions to wear this season. Although white tends to be thought of as a summer color, designers recreated a dreamy winter wonderland on the runway in angelic hues like ivory, eggshell and cream. Brighten and freshen up your seasonal wardrobe with these crisp and clean winter white fashions to wear this season and let your inner snow bunny shine!

## 1. Winter White Coat

![Winter White Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/1_winter-white-coat_600x816.jpg)

When the cool weather hits, throw on a wonderful wintry coat in a chic shade of white. Coats make excellent winter white fashions to wear this season. Select a white coat in a feminine silhouette like an A-line cut to define your shape, as unfortunately lighter colors tend to make figures appear larger than they are. Pairing your coat with a monochromatic belt will help to create and enhance your natural waist, giving you some semblance of curves underneath your warm outerwear. Cozy up to the winter white trend with the Miss Selfridge coat above at $135 while other winter white designer and retail creations like Erin Fetherston, Mango, IRO, Marni, Topshop and H&M can be found in the $20 to $600 range.

## 2. Winter White Dress

![Winter White Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/2_winter-white-dress_400x400.jpg)

Turn up the heat in an angelic winter white dress. For all your holiday festivities and soirees, take the route less traveled and opt for a simple yet classic cream dress to make an understated statement this season. Slip on a pair of black tights underneath your dress for a lovely contrast that’s elegant and sophisticated.

## 3. Winter White Skirt

![Winter White Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/3_winter-white-skirt_407x569.jpg)

Don a winter white maxi skirt for all your fashion fetes during the holidays to give off an air of slinky, seductive evening glamour Opt for a maxi skirt in rich silk or satin to amp up the luxe factor for dazzling drama. Pair with gilded and glittering accessories to shine with festive flair.

## 4. Winter White Blazer

![Winter White Blazer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/4_winter-white-blazer_600x714.jpg)

Keep your look crisp in winter with a white blazer. For a versatile piece you can transition easily from day to night, a white tuxedo style jacket can take you effortlessly from meetings in the office to cocktail drinks with the girls. Although a menswear classic, the tuxedo jacket works best on the feminine silhouette with short, flirty party skirts and sleek trousers for sexy suiting.

## 5. Winter White Sweater

![Winter White Sweater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/5_winter-white-sweater_407x448.jpg)

To keep your winter whites on the cool and casual side, a traditional cable knit sweater is the perfect seasonal addition to your winter wardrobe. Cozy up to this winter white cable knit sweater while running errands or during relaxing days in front of a fire. When out and about, pair your sweater with slim jeans, tall boots and a cool chapeau for effortless seasonal style.

## 6. Winter White Pants

![Winter White Pants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/6_winter-white-pants_600x600.jpg)

Although white pants are usually reserved for the scorching summer season to deflect light, during the winter white pants are a classically chic and crisp way to wear this seasonal trend. Pair with contrast colored pieces like a black sweater and statement jewelry for high-impact results that add drama and glamour during the holidays.

## 7. Winter White Top

![Winter White Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/7_winter-white-top_600x899.jpg)

For a safe, foolproof way to wear winter white this season, opt for a winter white top you can layer underneath cool seasonal pieces to brighten and freshen up your wardrobe for winter. Layer a white top under other wintry white pieces like a jacket or wear with snowy white pants for a fresh and dreamy monochromatic look that’ll make you queen of a wintry wonderland.

## 8. Winter White Fur

![Winter White Fur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2012/11/8_winter-white-fur_600x702.jpg)

Your snow bunny style wouldn’t be complete without luxe winter white fur. Whether worn in real or faux, a fur vest in a snowy hue is a glamorous and elegant option for cool outerwear during winter. Fur is such an easy piece to wear which is why this fabulous fur vest can take you chicly from day to night when layered over casual denim or a festive cocktail frock.

Wear white and say bye bye to the winter blues with these 8 winter white fashions to wear this season. From snowy hues to frothy vanillas, white is the color commanding glamour and elegance in the crisp winter season. Would you wear white during winter? What do you think about the no-white-after-Labor-Day rule?
