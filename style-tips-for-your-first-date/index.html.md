# 7 Style Tips for Your First Date ...

If we had a penny for every time we've wondered what to wear on a first date, we'd be very, very rich! Here are a few tips by guest contributor Nousha on how to look your very best on a first date and leave a lasting impression.

The first date is all about a good first impression, right? Because of this fact, it is important to look and dress your best when going out with someone on a first date. Here are 7 fabulous outfit sets for date nights:

## 1. Dress for the Occasion

![Dress for the Occasion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/1_dress-for-the-occasion_320x408.jpg)

Your dress formality should be based on where you'll be going. This outfit set for example is perfect for an upscale dinner but would be OTT for something more casual. Pick this set on Mango.com

## 2. Confidence is the Key

![Confidence is the Key](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/2_confidence-is-the-key_312x408.jpg)

Wear something that you're comfotable in. If it doesn’t fit right or it just isn’t for you then it won’t work. Pick this set on Zara.com

## 3. Flaunt Your Best Features

![Flaunt Your Best Features](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/3_flaunt-your-best-features_320x392.jpg)

Pay close attention to the fit of your outfit. It should not be too tight or too baggy. It should highlight your body’s best features and ideally, pick one feature at a time. Too much leg and too much cleavage at the same time is a big no no! Pick this set on MadCloth.com

## 4. Make Sure It’s Not Overdone

![Make Sure It’s Not Overdone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/4_make-sure-its-not-overdone_440x416.jpg)

You should look beautiful of course but don’t go overboard. An excessively revealing outfit can be distracting and too much make up is scary! This outfit set is sweet yet sexy...perfect for a first date! Pick this set on Express.com

## 5. Show Your Personality

![Show Your Personality](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/5_show-your-personality_504x376.jpg)

Let your personality shine through your outfit. Wear something boring and chances are, you will come across as boring. Before you choose your outfit for the night, think about what aspect of your personality you want to show off. Add color or a vibrant print and you're sure to catch his attention.

## 6. Finish up Your Outfit with Accessories

![Finish up Your Outfit with Accessories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/6_finish-up-your-outfit-with-accessories_304x384.jpg)

Never underestimate the power of accessories. They add personality and uniqueness to your outfit. You can’t go wrong with one piece of bold jewelry or even something delicate if that's your style. Pick this set on Asos.com

## 7. Find Colors That Work for You

![Find Colors That Work for You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fashion/2013/05/7_find-colors-that-work-for-you_384x384.jpg)

When you're picking out an outfit, choose shades that flatter your skin tone. To highlight dark skin tone, try cool colors. To highlight light skin tone, try warm colors. Pick this set on Nordstrom.com
