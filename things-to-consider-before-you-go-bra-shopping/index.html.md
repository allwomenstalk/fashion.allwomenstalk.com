# 7 Things to Consider before You Go Bra Shopping ...

Before you go bra shopping, it’s good to consider what you want out of a new bra. Women usually need several different types of bras. Bra shopping can be fun if you do some thinking about the type of bra you need first. These are 7 things to consider before you go bra shopping.

## 1. The Colors You Need

One thing to consider before you go bra shopping is the color of bras that you need. Most women need the basic colors of black, white and beige. One of those colors will usually work under any color top you might wear. If you already have the basic colors, you might want to think about other colors you want to purchase. Shopping for colored and print bras is usually more fun than shopping for basic neutrals.

## 2. The Fabric and Texture You’d like

Considering the fabric and texture you want in a bra is a good idea. You may want a plain cotton bra for a sleek look under t-shirts. You might want a satin bra with lace for a sexier look. There’re many different fabrics and textures that bras are available in. The more you know about what kind of bra you’re shopping for, the easier shopping will be.

## 3. Having a Bra Fitting

Another point to consider before you go bra shopping is when was the last time you had a bra fitting. If you haven’t had a bra fitting in a year or so, it might be a good idea to have one. This is especially true if your weight has changed by more than five pounds or you’ve had a baby. Your breasts can change after pregnancy even if you lose all of your baby weight. If you’ve never had a bra fitting, they’re very helpful in teaching you what size to shop for.

## 4. Your Budget

Of course you want to consider what your budget for bra shopping is before you go. You don’t want to be looking at sixty dollar bras if a thirty dollar bra is what you can afford. There’re ways to stretch your budget, though. You can watch for sales. You may even be able to find a coupon to use. I have used coupons to purchase bras several times.

## 5. The Styles You Are Shopping for

Knowing what style of bra you’re shopping for is helpful. There’re so many different options to choose from. There’re strapless, racerback and sport bras as well as regular bras that have different cuts such as a demi bra or a full coverage bra. It’s good to know what style you’re looking for. It will save you from getting confused when you shop.

## 6. The Level of Padding You Want

Women have very individual preferences on the level of padding they want. Some women prefer a plain t-shirt bra with no padding. Others prefer the highest level of padding available. Many women like a mixture of different padding levels for different tops and occasions. It’s all about what you feel most comfortable in.

## 7. A Good Time to Bra Shop

It’s good to think about the best time to bra shop. You want to shop when you can take your time and not feel rushed. This will allow you to be sure you’re getting exactly what you want. Bra shopping can be a lot of fun. New bras always boost your confidence level, too.

These are some things to consider before you go bra shopping. Now let’s talk girl talk. What’s your go-to bra style?
